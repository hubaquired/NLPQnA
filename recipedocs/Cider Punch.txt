Cider Punch recipe. Into a punch bowl containing the block of ice, pour the cider, club soda, brandy, and the orange-flavored liqueur. Combine the mixture well and garnish the punch with twists of orange peel. Cider Punch has 449.0 calories. Cider Punch has 1.0 grams of fat. Cider Punch has 48.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cider Punch are Block of ice, 10 cups apple cider, 5 cups club soda, 2 1/2 cups brandy, 1 1/4 cups orange-flavored liqueur, Garnish: twists of orange peel.

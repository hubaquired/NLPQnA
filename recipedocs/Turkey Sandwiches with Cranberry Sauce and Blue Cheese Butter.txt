Turkey Sandwiches with Cranberry Sauce and Blue Cheese Butter recipe. Mash blue cheese with butter and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Spread on bread and make sandwiches with turkey, cranberry sauce, and arugula The ingredients of Turkey Sandwiches with Cranberry Sauce and Blue Cheese Butter are 2 ounces blue cheese (1/3 cup) at room temperature, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened, 4 slices whole-wheat or multi-grain bread, 6 to 8 ounces sliced cooked turkey, 2/3 cup cranberry sauce, 3 ounces arugula (2 cups).

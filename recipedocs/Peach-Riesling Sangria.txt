Peach-Riesling Sangria recipe. Mix first 5 ingredients in large pitcher. Scrape in seeds from vanilla bean; add bean and stir to blend. Mix in fruit. Cover and chill overnight. Mix in ice and serve. Peach-Riesling Sangria has 159.0 calories. Peach-Riesling Sangria has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peach-Riesling Sangria are 750-ml bottle dry Riesling, 1 1/2 cups white cranberry-peach drink, 1/2 cup peach schnapps, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1/2 vanilla bean, split lengthwise, 2 1/2-inch-thick lemon slices, 2 1/2-inch-thick orange slices, 2 peaches, cut into wedges, 10 fresh raspberries, 3 cups ice cubes.A lovely white sangria with great balance.

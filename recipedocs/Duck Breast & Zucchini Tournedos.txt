Duck Breast & Zucchini Tournedos recipe.  For the mushroom powder, process the dried cep mushrooms until finely ground, combine them with the pepper and herbes de Provençe. Cut the duck breasts lengthways into 3 mm ⅛ inch) wide strips. Lay out the strips of duck two by two on top of each other, alternating layers of fat and lean. Cut the zucchini into strips using a vegetable peeler. Lay the strips of zucchini on top of the duck, season with the mushroom powder and season with salt. Roll up the strips tightly to make tournedos and tie with string. Make 6 tournedos in this way. Cook over gentle heat 5 minutes each side. Duck Breast & Zucchini Tournedos has 129.0 calories. Duck Breast & Zucchini Tournedos has 18.0 grams of protein. Duck Breast & Zucchini Tournedos has 4.0 grams of fat. Duck Breast & Zucchini Tournedos has 505.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Duck Breast & Zucchini Tournedos are 3 duck breasts, 3 young zucchini, salt, 1 3/4 oz dried cep mushrooms ('champagne cork' or bouchon) or porcini, 2 tablespoons coarsely ground black pepper, 1 tablespoon herbes de Provençe (usually marjoram, oregano, rosemary, thyme).With the little one's football team.

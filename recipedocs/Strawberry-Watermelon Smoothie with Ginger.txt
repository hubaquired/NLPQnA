Strawberry-Watermelon Smoothie with Ginger recipe. Combine all ingredients in blender and process until smooth. Divide smoothie between 2 glasses and serve. The ingredients of Strawberry-Watermelon Smoothie with Ginger are 3 cups 1-inch cubes seeded watermelon, 2 cups frozen quartered hulled strawberries, 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated peeled fresh ginger, 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lime juice.Fresh ginger gives this a zesty kick.

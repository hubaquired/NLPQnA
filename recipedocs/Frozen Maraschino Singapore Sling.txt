Frozen Maraschino Singapore Sling recipe.  In a blender, blend together gin, kirsch, Benedictine, cognac, cherries, cherry juice, lime juice, sugar, club soda and 2 cups cracked ice, scraping down the sides occasionally, until the mixture is smooth but still frozen. Divide the mixture between 2 stemmed glasses, and spoon the Port over the drinks. Frozen Maraschino Singapore Sling has 165.0 calories. Frozen Maraschino Singapore Sling has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Frozen Maraschino Singapore Sling are 2 ponies (2 ounces) gin, 1 pony (1 ounce) kirsch, 1 teaspoon Benedictine, 1 teaspoon cognac, 3 maraschino cherries, 2 tablespoons maraschino cherry juice, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 cup club soda or seltzer, 2 teaspoons chilled ruby Port.

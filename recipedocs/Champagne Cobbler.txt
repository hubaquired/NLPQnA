Champagne Cobbler recipe.  Fill a 10-ounce goblet two-thirds full with crushed ice. Add lemon juice and curaçao, stir, and add the orange slice and pineapple stick. Fill champagne. The ingredients of Champagne Cobbler are Crushed ice, 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon curaçao, Thin slice orange, Small pineapple stick, 4 ounces chilled champagne.

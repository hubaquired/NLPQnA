Endive, Arugula and Orange Salad recipe.  Using sharp knife, cut away all peel and white pith from oranges. Cut oranges crosswise into 1/4-inch-thick slices. Cut each slice in half. Set aside. Whisk juice, olive oil, vinegar, walnut oil and orange peel in medium bowl to blend. Season with salt and pepper. Combine endive, arugula and orange slices in large bowl. Add vinaigrette and toss to coat. Sprinkle with walnuts. Endive, Arugula and Orange Salad has 105.0 calories. Endive, Arugula and Orange Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Endive, Arugula and Orange Salad has 8.0 grams of fat. Endive, Arugula and Orange Salad has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Endive, Arugula and Orange Salad are 2 medium-size seedless oranges, 2 tablespoons orange juice, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar, 2 teaspoons walnut oil or olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon grated orange peel, 4 medium-size heads Belgian endive, separated into leaves, 1 large bunch arugula, ends trimmed, 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted, chopped.The peppery flavor of arugula is nice with the slightly bitter endive and sweet orange.

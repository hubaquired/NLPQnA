Spinach with Bamboo Shoots recipe.  Wash spinach leaves thoroughly under cold running water, drain well. Heat the oil in a wok or skillet. Using a medium-high flame, cook the bamboo shoots in the oil approximately 45 seconds, stirring constantly. Add spinach and stir until wilted. Add salt and sugar, and cook, stirring, about 1 1/2 to 2 minutes longer. Transfer to a hot platter, but do not add the liquid from the pan. Spinach with Bamboo Shoots has 45.0 calories. Spinach with Bamboo Shoots has 4.0 grams of protein. Spinach with Bamboo Shoots has 1.0 grams of fat. Spinach with Bamboo Shoots has 699.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spinach with Bamboo Shoots are 1 lb. fresh spinach, 1/2 cup peanut, vegetable, or corn oil, 1/4 cup finely shredded bamboo shoots, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 2 teaspoons sugar.

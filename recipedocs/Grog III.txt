Grog III recipe.  For each drink, pour rum into a mug or highball glass. Stir in lemon juice and sugar syrup. Fill the glass with hot tea. Serve immediately, garnished with a cinnamon stick or twist of lemon. The ingredients of Grog III are 1 jigger heavy-bodied rum, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 teaspoon sugar syrup, Hot tea, Garnish: Cinnamon stick or a twist of lemon. Rum Tea Alcoholic Lemon Winter Gourmet Drink.

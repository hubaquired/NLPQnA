Ginger Cream recipe.  Combine 1/4 cup water, ginger, and sugar in heavy small saucepan. Stir over medium heat until sugar dissolves. Simmer until mixture is syrupy, about 5 minutes. Cool completely. Using electric mixer, beat cream in medium bowl until peaks form. Fold in ginger mixture. Can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover and chill.) Ginger Cream has 231.0 calories. Ginger Cream has 2.0 grams of protein. Ginger Cream has 24.0 grams of fat. Ginger Cream has 21.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger Cream are 1/4 cup water, 3 tablespoons minced peeled ginger, 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar, 1 cup chilled whipping cream.This recipe is an accompaniment for Pear and Fig Strudels with Ginger Cream.

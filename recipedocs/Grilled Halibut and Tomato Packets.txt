Grilled Halibut and Tomato Packets recipe. Cut six 12-inch squares of foil. Place each fish fillet in center of 1 square. Drizzle each fillet with 2 teaspoons oil; turn fish to coat. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Spread each with 2 teaspoons tapenade. Top each with 4 tomato wedges, 1/3 cup fennel, 1 teaspoon tarragon, 1 teaspoon lemon peel, and 2 teaspoons vermouth. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Fold up foil and seal edges to enclose completely. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Place packets on grill rack; cover barbecue with lid. Cook until fish is just opaque in center, about 8 minutes. Divide packets among plates and serve. Grilled Halibut and Tomato Packets has 535.0 calories. Grilled Halibut and Tomato Packets has 86.0 grams of protein. Grilled Halibut and Tomato Packets has 16.0 grams of fat. Grilled Halibut and Tomato Packets has 409.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Halibut and Tomato Packets are 6 3/4-inch-thick halibut fillets, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup olive tapenade, 3 medium heirloom tomatoes, each cut into 8 wedges, 1 medium fennel bulb, halved, cored, thinly sliced (about 2 cups), 2 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon, 2 tablespoons grated lemon peel, 1/4 cup dry vermouth.

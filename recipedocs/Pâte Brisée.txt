Pâte Brisée recipe. In a large bowl blend the flour, the butter, the vegetable shortening, and the salt until the mixture resembles meal. Add 3 tablespoons ice water, toss the mixture until the water is incorporated, and form the dough into a ball. Knead the dough lightly with the heel of the hand against a smooth surface for a few seconds to distribute the fat evenly and re-form it into a ball. Dust the dough with flour and chill it, wrapped in wax paper, for 1 hour. Pâte Brisée has 234.0 calories. Pâte Brisée has 3.0 grams of protein. Pâte Brisée has 16.0 grams of fat. Pâte Brisée has 99.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pâte Brisée are 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, 3/4 stick (6 tablespoons) cold unsalted butter, cut into bits, 2 tablespoons cold vegetable shortening, 1/4 teaspoon salt.

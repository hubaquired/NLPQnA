Chocolate Frosting recipe.  In a work bowl with an electric mixer, cream the butter. Sift the sugar and cocoa powder together and gradually add to the butter, beating well after each addition. Add the cream and vanilla and beat until well blended and fluffy. Chocolate Frosting has 245.0 calories. Chocolate Frosting has 1.0 grams of protein. Chocolate Frosting has 12.0 grams of fat. Chocolate Frosting has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chocolate Frosting are 1/2 cup unsalted butter at room temperature, 3 cups powdered sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa powder, 4 tablespoons heavy cream, 1 teaspoon vanilla.Editor's note: This recipe is from Michele Adams's and Gia Russo's book Wedding Showers: Ideas & Recipes for the Perfect Party.

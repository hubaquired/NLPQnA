Radish Slaw recipe.  In a bowl toss together the radishes, the cabbage, the carrots, the onion, the lemon juice, the sugar, the oil, the coriander, and salt and pepper to taste. Radish Slaw has 103.0 calories. Radish Slaw has 2.0 grams of protein. Radish Slaw has 7.0 grams of fat. Radish Slaw has 52.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Radish Slaw are 1/2 pound radishes, trimmed and grated coarse (about 2 cups), 3 cups finely shredded cabbage, 1 cup coarsely grated carrots, 1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh coriander, mint, or parsley leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

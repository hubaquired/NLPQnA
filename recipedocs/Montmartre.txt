Montmartre recipe. Combine all ingredients, except the cherry, in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass; add cherry. The ingredients of Montmartre are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1/2 ounce sweet vermouth, 1/2 oounce Triple Sec, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Maraschino cherry.

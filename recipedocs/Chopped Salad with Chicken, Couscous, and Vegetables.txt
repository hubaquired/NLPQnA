Chopped Salad with Chicken, Couscous, and Vegetables recipe.  Blend basil leaves, mayonnaise, and shallot in processor until smooth. Gradually blend in buttermilk and lemon juice. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Mix Asiago cheese, currants, and pumpkin seeds in medium bowl. Using 1/6 of couscous, 1/6 of arugula, 1/6 of tomatoes, 1/6 of chicken, 1/6 of corn, and 1/6 of Asiago cheese mixture, form rows on each of 6 plates. Serve, passing dressing separately.

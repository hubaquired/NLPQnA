Pousse l'Amour recipe. Pour the maraschino in a straight-sided 2-ounce liqueur glass and carefully slide in the unbroken egg yolk. Gently pour in the Benedictine, letting it trickle down the side of the slightly tilted glass, or pouring it over the back of a teaspoon held just above the glass. Add the brandy in the same way, making sure that the ingredients don't mix. Pousse l'Amour has 123.0 calories. Pousse l'Amour has 2.0 grams of protein. Pousse l'Amour has 4.0 grams of fat. Pousse l'Amour has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pousse l'Amour are 1/3 ounce maraschino liqueur, 1 egg yolk, 1/3 ounce Benedictine, 1/3 ounce brandy.

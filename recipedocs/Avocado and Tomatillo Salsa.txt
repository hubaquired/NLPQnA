Avocado and Tomatillo Salsa recipe.  Purée tomatillos and jalapeño with salt in a blender until smooth, then transfer to a bowl. Quarter, pit, and peel avocados and cut into 1/4-inch dice. Add to tomatillo purée and stir in onion. Avocado and Tomatillo Salsa has 203.0 calories. Avocado and Tomatillo Salsa has 3.0 grams of protein. Avocado and Tomatillo Salsa has 17.0 grams of fat. Avocado and Tomatillo Salsa has 299.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Avocado and Tomatillo Salsa are 1/2 lb fresh tomatillos (8), husked, rinsed, and quartered, 1 to 2 teaspoons chopped fresh jalapeño chile, including seeds, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 firm-ripe small California avocados (1 lb total), 1/4 cup finely chopped white onion.Active time: 10 min Start to finish: 10 min.

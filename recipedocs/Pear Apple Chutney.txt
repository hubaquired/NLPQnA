Pear Apple Chutney recipe.  Halve and core pears and apples. Cut 2 pear halves and 2 apple halves into 1/4-inch-thick slices and chop remaining pears and apples. In a saucepan combine sliced and chopped pears and apples with remaining ingredients and bring to a simmer, stirring gently. Simmer chutney, covered, stirring occasionally, until fruit is just tender, 10 to 15 minutes, and cool. Chutney may be made 1 day ahead and chilled, covered. Serve chutney chilled or at room temperature. Pear Apple Chutney has 248.0 calories. Pear Apple Chutney has 2.0 grams of protein. Pear Apple Chutney has 1.0 grams of fat. Pear Apple Chutney has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pear Apple Chutney are 2 firm-ripe red Bartlett pears, 2 Granny Smith apples, 1 cup golden raisins, 1/2 cup rice vinegar (not seasoned), 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon finely chopped peeled fresh gingerroot, 1 teaspoon mustard seeds, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

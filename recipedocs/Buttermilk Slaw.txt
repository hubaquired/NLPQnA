Buttermilk Slaw recipe.  Mix both cabbages, carrot, red onion and parsley in large bowl. Whisk buttermilk, mayonnaise, mustard, sugar and celery seeds in medium bowl to blend. Pour over cabbage mixture and toss to blend. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill.) Buttermilk Slaw has 186.0 calories. Buttermilk Slaw has 3.0 grams of protein. Buttermilk Slaw has 15.0 grams of fat. Buttermilk Slaw has 206.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Buttermilk Slaw are 8 cups thinly sliced green cabbage, 2 cups thinly sliced red cabbage, 3/4 cup grated peeled carrot, 1/4 cup minced red onion, 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley, 2/3 cup chilled buttermilk, 1/2 cup regular or low-fat mayonnaise, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon celery seeds.Try this with sausages or roast chicken.

Cider Cup recipe. Pour the cider, sherry and brandy into a large pitcher. Add chunks of ice or 12 to 16 ice cubes and the apple slices. Add club soda and stir gently. Serve in 4-ounce punch glasses or wine glasses. The ingredients of Cider Cup are 1 quart sweet cider, 4 ounces sweet sherry, 4 ounces brandy, Apple slices, 1 pint chilled club soda.

Cider-Caramel Sauce recipe. Place cider in heavy large skillet. Scrape in seeds from vanilla bean; add bean. Boil cider mixture until reduced to 2 cups, about 15 minutes. Add sugar and butter. Cook until sauce thickens slightly and is reduced to 1 1/2 cups, stirring occasionally, about 6 minutes longer. Can be made 3 days ahead. Transfer to small bowl. Cover; chill. Remove vanilla bean before using.) Cider-Caramel Sauce has 432.0 calories. Cider-Caramel Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Cider-Caramel Sauce has 16.0 grams of fat. Cider-Caramel Sauce has 26.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cider-Caramel Sauce are 4 cups apple cider, 1 vanilla bean, split lengthwise, 1/2 cup (packed) dark brown sugar, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter.

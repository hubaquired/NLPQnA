Fettuccine with Red Clam Sauce recipe.  Heat oil in heavy medium skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until golden brown, about 1 minute. Add tomato paste and stir 1 minute. Add clam juice, reserved 1/2 cup juice from clams and parsley and simmer until slightly thickened, about 15 minutes. Add clams and red pepper flakes and heat through. Season with salt and pepper. Pour sauce over fettuccine. Toss thoroughly and serve. Fettuccine with Red Clam Sauce has 918.0 calories. Fettuccine with Red Clam Sauce has 68.0 grams of protein. Fettuccine with Red Clam Sauce has 25.0 grams of fat. Fettuccine with Red Clam Sauce has 2227.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fettuccine with Red Clam Sauce are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 5 garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/2 tablespoons tomato paste, 1 8-ounce bottle clam juice, 2 6 1/2-ounce cans chopped clams, drained, 1/2 cup juice reserved, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, Pinch of dried red pepper flakes, Salt and pepper, 8 ounces fettuccine, freshly cooked.

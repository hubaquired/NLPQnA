Cilantro Slaw recipe. Place coleslaw mix and cilantro in large bowl. Whisk canola oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper in medium bowl to blend. Add to cabbage mixture; toss to coat. Cilantro Slaw has 229.0 calories. Cilantro Slaw has 1.0 grams of protein. Cilantro Slaw has 19.0 grams of fat. Cilantro Slaw has 417.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cilantro Slaw are 12 ounces purchased shredded three-color coleslaw mix (about 7 cups), 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro, 3 tablespoons canola oil, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1/2 teaspoon coarse kosher salt, 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper.

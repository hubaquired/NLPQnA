Sugar Snap Pea, Radish, and Cucumber Salad recipe.  Cook peas in a saucepan of boiling salted water just until they turn a brighter shade of green, about 30 seconds. Drain in a colander and rinse under cold water to stop cooking. Cut halved cucumber and radishes crosswise into 1/4-inch-thick slices. Toss peas, cucumber, radishes, and sesame seeds with vinegars and season with salt and pepper. Sugar Snap Pea, Radish, and Cucumber Salad has 71.0 calories. Sugar Snap Pea, Radish, and Cucumber Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Sugar Snap Pea, Radish, and Cucumber Salad has 3.0 grams of fat. Sugar Snap Pea, Radish, and Cucumber Salad has 33.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sugar Snap Pea, Radish, and Cucumber Salad are 1/2 lb sugar snap peas, trimmed and, if large, halved diagonally, 1 English cucumber, halved lengthwise and seeded, 1 bunch radishes (1 lb), 1/4 cup sesame seeds, toasted, 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar, 1 teaspoon cider vinegar.Active time: 20 min Start to finish: 20 min.

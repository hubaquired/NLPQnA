Blue Hawaiian recipe. Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth. The ingredients of Blue Hawaiian are 3 oz (1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons) light rum, 2 oz (1/4 cup) blue Curaçao, 1/2 cup unsweetened pineapple juice, 1/4 cup well-stirred canned cream of coconut (not coconut milk) such as Coco López, 3 cups ice cubes, Garnish : a pineapple wedge. Fruit Juice Rum Alcoholic Coconut Pineapple Gourmet Drink.

Crispy Baby Yukon Gold Potatoes recipe.  Arrange racks in upper and lower thirds of oven; preheat to 450°F. Toss potatoes with oil, thyme, salt, pepper, and nutmeg in a large bowl until coated. Arrange in an even layer, cut side down, on two rimmed baking sheets. Roast potatoes, rotating sheets front to back and top to bottom halfway through, until crisp and golden brown on the bottom, about 25 minutes. Serve with Horseradish-Yogurt Sauce, if desired. The ingredients of Crispy Baby Yukon Gold Potatoes are 4 pounds baby Yukon Gold potatoes, halved, 3 tablespoons canola oil, 1 tablespoon chopped thyme leaves, 2 teaspoons kosher salt, 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg, Horseradish-Yogurt Sauce (for serving; optional).You can’t go wrong with roasted potatoes for a holiday meal. A mix of fresh thyme and nutmeg keeps this version interesting. Our tangy Horseradish-Yogurt Sauce is the perfect accompaniment.

Strawberries with Cassis recipe.  Combine first 4 ingredients in large bowl. Let stand at room temperature 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Refrigerate.) Whip cream and powdered sugar in large bowl until stiff peaks form. Spoon 2/3 cup berries and some juices into each of 20 balloon glasses. Top each with spoonful of whipped cream and serve. Strawberries with Cassis has 179.0 calories. Strawberries with Cassis has 1.0 grams of protein. Strawberries with Cassis has 6.0 grams of fat. Strawberries with Cassis has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberries with Cassis are 6 1-pint baskets strawberries, hulled and halved or quartered (depending on size of berries), 1 1/4 cups crème de cassis liqueur, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 3 cups chilled whipping cream, 3 tablespoons powdered sugar.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less, but requires additional unattended time.

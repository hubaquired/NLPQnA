Nasturtium Butter recipe.  Mix 3 tablespoons chopped nasturtium flowers red, yellow, and/or orange) and 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt into 1/2 cup 1 stick) room-temperature unsalted butter in a small bowl. Season with freshly ground black pepper. Place on parchment or waxed paper and roll up into a log. Chill until firm, 1 hour. Do ahead: Butter can be made 2 days ahead. Keep chilled.

Orange Pineapple Refresher recipe. In a pitcher stir together the orange juice, the pineapple juice, and the lemon juice. Fill tall glasses with ice cubes, drizzle a little grenadine into each glass, and pour in the orange juice mixture. Orange Pineapple Refresher has 112.0 calories. Orange Pineapple Refresher has 1.0 grams of protein. Orange Pineapple Refresher has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange Pineapple Refresher are 4 cups fresh orange juice, 1 1/2 cups unsweetened pineapple juice, 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, grenadine to taste.

Mustard Dill Sauce recipe. In a bowl combine well all ingredients and season with pepper. Sauce may be made 1 day ahead and kept chilled, covered. Let sauce come to room temperature and whisk before serving. Mustard Dill Sauce has 278.0 calories. Mustard Dill Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Mustard Dill Sauce has 27.0 grams of fat. Mustard Dill Sauce has 472.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mustard Dill Sauce are 1/2 cup coarse-grained mustard, 1/4 cup water, 1/2 cup heavy cream, 1/4 cup olive oil, 4 teaspoons sugar, 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill, or to taste.This recipe was created to accompany Broiled Salmon Fillet with Mustard Dill Sauce Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

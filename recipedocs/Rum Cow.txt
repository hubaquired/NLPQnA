Rum Cow recipe.  Combine rum, vanilla, bitters, sugar, and milk in a cocktail shaker. Grate a little nutmeg on top and add ice. Shake vigorously and pour into a 10-ounce highball glass. Rum Cow has 282.0 calories. Rum Cow has 8.0 grams of protein. Rum Cow has 8.0 grams of fat. Rum Cow has 105.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rum Cow are 1 1/2 ounces light rum, 2 drops vanilla, Dash Angostura bitters, 2 teaspoons sugar, 1 cup milk, Nutmeg, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

Tuxedo recipe.  Combine all ingredients, except peel, in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass; drop in peel.Garnish with cherry. The ingredients of Tuxedo are 1 to 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1 to 1 1/2 ounces dry vermouth, 2 dashes Pernod, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Twist lemon peel, Dash Maraschino, 2 dashes orange bitters, Maraschino cherry.

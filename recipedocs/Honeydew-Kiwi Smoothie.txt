Honeydew-Kiwi Smoothie recipe. Combine all ingredients in blender and process until smooth. Divide smoothie between 2 glasses and serve. The ingredients of Honeydew-Kiwi Smoothie are 2 cups 1-inch cubes peeled seeded honeydew melon, 2 cups crushed ice, 1 1/4 cups 1/2-inch cubes peeled kiwi, 10 large fresh mint leaves, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 tablespoon honey.Fresh mint and lime juice add a bright finish to this drink.

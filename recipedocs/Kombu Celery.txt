Kombu Celery recipe.  Toss celery, furikake, sesame oil, and soy sauce in a small bowl to coat. Chill uncovered 30 minutes to let flavors meld. Serve topped with sesame seeds. The ingredients of Kombu Celery are 5 celery stalks, strings removed, cut into 3x1/2-inch sticks, 1 tablespoon furikake, 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, Toasted sesame seeds (for serving).The crunchy, salty, sesame-drenched celery sticks at Bar Goto in New York are so good, you’ll forget they’re made from vegetables.

Corn and Herb-Bread Sausage Stuffing recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter 13x9x2 glass baking dish. Sauté first 3 ingredients in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat until sausage is brown and vegetables are tender, breaking up sausage with fork, about 15 minutes. Drain off fat. Transfer sausage mixture to large bowl. Add both stuffing mixes, poultry seasoning, sage and pepper and toss until well blended. Gradually mix in broth. Transfer stuffing to prepared dish. Cover with foil and bake until heated through, about 40 minutes. Uncover and bake until slightly crusty on top, about 5 minutes longer. Corn and Herb-Bread Sausage Stuffing has 284.0 calories. Corn and Herb-Bread Sausage Stuffing has 11.0 grams of protein. Corn and Herb-Bread Sausage Stuffing has 10.0 grams of fat. Corn and Herb-Bread Sausage Stuffing has 867.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Corn and Herb-Bread Sausage Stuffing are 1 pound bulk pork sausage, 2 cups chopped celery, 2 cups chopped onion, 5 cups corn bread stuffing mix (about one 12-ounce package), 5 cups herbed-bread stuffing mix, 1 teaspoon dried poultry seasoning, crumbled, 1 teaspoon dried rubbed sage, 1/2 teaspoon pepper, 3 cups canned low-salt chicken broth.If this stuffing is to be baked inside the holiday turkey, reduce the amount of chicken broth to 2 1/4 cups.

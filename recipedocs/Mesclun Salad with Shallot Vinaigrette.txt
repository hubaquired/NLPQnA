Mesclun Salad with Shallot Vinaigrette recipe.  Whisk together shallot, mustard, and vinegar. Add oil in a slow stream, whisking until emulsified, and season with salt and pepper. Just before serving, toss salad greens with just enough dressing to coat. Mesclun Salad with Shallot Vinaigrette has 94.0 calories. Mesclun Salad with Shallot Vinaigrette has 1.0 grams of protein. Mesclun Salad with Shallot Vinaigrette has 9.0 grams of fat. Mesclun Salad with Shallot Vinaigrette has 37.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mesclun Salad with Shallot Vinaigrette are 1/4 cup finely chopped shallot, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 1/3 cup olive oil (preferably French) or safflower oil, 1 pound mesclun (mixed baby salad greens).Feel free to create your own mesclun mix. We suggest baby red oakleaf, baby arugula, mâche, and bite-size pieces of Belgian endive, frisée, and radicchio.

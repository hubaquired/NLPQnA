The ingredients of Roasted Beet and Goat Cheese Salad are 16 ounces (about 4) medium golden and/or red beets, 1/4 cup grapeseed oil, 1/4 cup sherry vinegar, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1 teaspoon dark sesame oil, 2 cups arugula, 2 medium vine-ripened tomatoes, cut into thin wedges, 1 fennel bulb, trimmed and thinly shaved, 2 ounces goat cheese, crumbled.Hari Pulapaka, chef and owner of Cress Restaurant in DeLand, Florida, scored a spot at a local community garden to sprout his organic produce for vitamin-packed salads like this one. The homemade dressing he created stays fresh in the fridge for a week.

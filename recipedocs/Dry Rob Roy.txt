Dry Rob Roy recipe.  Combine all ingredients except the peel in a mixing glass and stir gently to chill as well as dilute the drink. Strain into a cocktail glass, twist the peel over the drink to release the oil and then drop it in. For a weaker drink, reduce the Scotch to 1 1/2 ounces. In some recipes the bitters are omitted or replaced with orange bitters. Dry Rob Roy has 299.0 calories. Dry Rob Roy has 2.0 grams of protein. Dry Rob Roy has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Dry Rob Roy are 3 ounces Scotch whisky, 1/2 ounce sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, 5 or 6 ice cubes, Twist orange or lemon peel.

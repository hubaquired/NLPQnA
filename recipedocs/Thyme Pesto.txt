Thyme Pesto recipe.  Finely chop first 5 ingredients in processor. With machine running, gradually add 1/2 cup olive oil. Continue processing until pesto is almost smooth. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Pesto can be prepared up to 1 week ahead. Cover tightly and refrigerate.) Thyme Pesto has 851.0 calories. Thyme Pesto has 17.0 grams of protein. Thyme Pesto has 85.0 grams of fat. Thyme Pesto has 427.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Thyme Pesto are 1 1/2 cups loosely packed fresh parsley, 1/2 cup loosely packed fresh thyme leaves or 1 tablespoon dried, crumbled, plus 1/2 cup fresh parsley, 1/2 cup (about 2 ounces) grated Parmesan, 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts or walnuts, 2 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup olive oil.Parsley and thyme replace the usual basil in this sauce.

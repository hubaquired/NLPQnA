Pasta with Alfredo-Pesto Sauce recipe.  Finely chop basil, pine nuts and garlic in processor. With machine running, gradually add olive oil through feed tube and process until smooth. Mix in 1/2 Parmesan. Transfer pesto to small jar. Can be prepared 4 days ahead. Pour enough olive oil over sauce to cover. Cover and refrigerate.) Bring cream to boil in heavy medium saucepan. Whisk in pesto. Season sauce and remaining 1/2 cup Parmesan in large bowl. Toss to coat pasta evenly and serve. Pasta with Alfredo-Pesto Sauce has 859.0 calories. Pasta with Alfredo-Pesto Sauce has 28.0 grams of protein. Pasta with Alfredo-Pesto Sauce has 44.0 grams of fat. Pasta with Alfredo-Pesto Sauce has 407.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pasta with Alfredo-Pesto Sauce are 1 cup fresh basil leaves, 1/3 cup pine nuts or walnuts, 2 garlic cloves, 1/3 cup olive oil, 1 cup grated Parmesan, 1 cup whipping cream, 1 pound capellini or spaghetti, freshly cooked.

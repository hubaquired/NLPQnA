Katchkie Farm Cool Cucumber Yogurt Soup recipe.  Combine all the ingredients except the radishes in a food processor. Puree until smooth, then strain through a fine sieve into a large bowl. Refrigerate the soup until it is well chilled, about 2 hours or up to a day before serving. Thin the soup with just a small amount of water if it is thicker than you like. Ladle the soup into bowls, top with the radishes, and serve. Katchkie Farm Cool Cucumber Yogurt Soup has 338.0 calories. Katchkie Farm Cool Cucumber Yogurt Soup has 26.0 grams of protein. Katchkie Farm Cool Cucumber Yogurt Soup has 18.0 grams of fat. Katchkie Farm Cool Cucumber Yogurt Soup has 1904.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Katchkie Farm Cool Cucumber Yogurt Soup are 2 1/4 cups plain Greek yogurt, 1 1/4 pounds Kirby cucumbers, trimmed, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1- inch pieces, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder, 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger, Thinly sliced radishes.This soup is about as easy as opening a can, and it is especially good for lunch on a hot day or poured out of a thermos at a picnic; it is so refreshing and satisfying. And if you have cucumbers in your garden, here is a way besides salad or pickles to use your bounty. This recipe can easily be doubled.

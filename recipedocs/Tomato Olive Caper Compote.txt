Tomato Olive Caper Compote recipe.  Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté for 5 minutes, until soft. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute more. Add the tomatoes, olives, capers, and salt and cook over medium-high heat for 10–15 minutes, until most of the liquid has evaporated. Serve over grilled chicken, fish, pasta or rice. The ingredients of Tomato Olive Caper Compote are 1 teaspoon oil, 1 small onion, diced, 1 clove garlic, minced, 4 medium tomatoes, roughly chopped, 1/4 cup black olives, quartered, 1 tablespoons capers, 1/2 teaspoon salt.

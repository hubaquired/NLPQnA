Strawberries with Marsala recipe.  Toss together strawberries, sugar, and Marsala until sugar is dissolved. Let macerate at room temperature, tossing occasionally, 30 minutes before serving. Strawberries with Marsala has 53.0 calories. Strawberries with Marsala has 1.0 grams of protein. Strawberries with Marsala has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberries with Marsala are 2 lb strawberries (2 qt), trimmed, halved if large, 1/4 cup sugar, 1/4 cup sweet Marsala wine.Fragole Al Marsala.

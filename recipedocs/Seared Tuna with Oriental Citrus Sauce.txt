Seared Tuna with Oriental Citrus Sauce recipe. In a large saucepan combine citrus juices, tamari or soy sauce, vinegar, oil, and gingerroot and simmer 15 minutes. Pour sauce through a fine sieve into a bowl. Sauce may be made 1 day ahead and chilled, covered. Reheat sauce before preceeding.) Preheat oven to 400°F.. In a large non-stick skillet, heat oil over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking. Coat 2 tuna steaks with sesame seeds and sear 1 minute on each side, transferring to a baking sheet. Coat and sear remaining 4 tuna steaks in batches in same manner. Cook tuna in middle of oven 3 minutes for medium-rare. Cut each tuna steak into 4 slices and arrange slices, cut side up, on 6 plates. Pour about 1/4 cup sauce around tuna on each plate. Seared Tuna with Oriental Citrus Sauce has 345.0 calories. Seared Tuna with Oriental Citrus Sauce has 46.0 grams of protein. Seared Tuna with Oriental Citrus Sauce has 10.0 grams of fat. Seared Tuna with Oriental Citrus Sauce has 2061.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Seared Tuna with Oriental Citrus Sauce are 2 1/2 cups fresh orange juice, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3/4 cup tamari or soy sauce, 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon Asian sesame oil, 1/3 cup chopped peeled fresh gingerroot, 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, six 6-ounce 1-inch-thick tuna steaks.

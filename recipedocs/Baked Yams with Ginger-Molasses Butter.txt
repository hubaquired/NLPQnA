Baked Yams with Ginger-Molasses Butter recipe.  Mix first 6 ingredients in small bowl to blend. Stir in crystallized ginger. Season with salt and generous amount of pepper. Can be made 3 days ahead. Cover; chill. Bring ginger-molasses butter to room temperature before using.) Preheat oven to 350°F. Pierce yams in several places with fork; bake on rimmed baking sheet until tender when pierced with fork, about 50 minutes. Cut lengthwise slit in each yam and press in ends to open top. Spoon 2 tablespoons ginger-molasses butter into each yam and serve. Baked Yams with Ginger-Molasses Butter has 392.0 calories. Baked Yams with Ginger-Molasses Butter has 4.0 grams of protein. Baked Yams with Ginger-Molasses Butter has 15.0 grams of fat. Baked Yams with Ginger-Molasses Butter has 137.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Yams with Ginger-Molasses Butter are 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature, 3 tablespoons (packed) golden brown sugar, 1 tablespoon mild-flavored (light) molasses, 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, Pinch of ground cloves, 3 tablespoons minced crystallized ginger, 6 8-ounce yams (red-skinned sweet potatoes), rinsed, patted dry.

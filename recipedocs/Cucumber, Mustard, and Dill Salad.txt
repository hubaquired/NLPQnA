Cucumber, Mustard, and Dill Salad recipe. Whisk together vinegar, mustard, salt, and sugar in a bowl, then add oil in a slow stream, whisking. Halve cucumber lengthwise and remove seeds with a small spoon, then cut halves crosswise into 1/8-inch-thick slices. Add cucumber and dill to vinaigrette, tossing to coat. Cucumber, Mustard, and Dill Salad has 56.0 calories. Cucumber, Mustard, and Dill Salad has 1.0 grams of protein. Cucumber, Mustard, and Dill Salad has 4.0 grams of fat. Cucumber, Mustard, and Dill Salad has 322.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber, Mustard, and Dill Salad are 2 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste, 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar, 1 tablespoon mild olive oil, 1 large seedless cucumber (usually plastic-wrapped; 1 lb), peeled, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill.

Herb Grilled Chicken Wings recipe. Combine garlic, oregano, rosemary, and oil in a large resealable plastic bag; season with salt and pepper. Add chicken wings, seal bag, and turn to coat. Chill at least 1 hour or overnight. Prepare grill for medium heat. Remove wings from marinade and grill, covered and turning occasionally, until golden brown and crisp, 15-20 minutes. DO AHEAD: Chicken can be marinated 1 day ahead. Keep chilled. Herb Grilled Chicken Wings has 569.0 calories. Herb Grilled Chicken Wings has 40.0 grams of protein. Herb Grilled Chicken Wings has 44.0 grams of fat. Herb Grilled Chicken Wings has 202.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Herb Grilled Chicken Wings are 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped, 1/4 cup chopped fresh oregano, 1/4 cup chopped fresh rosemary, 1/4 cup olive oil, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 2 pounds chicken wings.The seductively high ratio of skin to meat is what makes wings taste so good, and grilling takes full advantage of that. This herb marinade is bright and summery, and light enough to let the skin get nice and crisp.

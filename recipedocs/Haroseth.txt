Haroseth recipe. Pulse together figs, apricots, and dates in a food processor until finely chopped, then transfer to a bowl and stir in walnuts and wine. Sprinkle spices evenly over mixture and stir until combined well. Haroseth has 273.0 calories. Haroseth has 4.0 grams of protein. Haroseth has 13.0 grams of fat. Haroseth has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Haroseth are 2/3 cup dried Mission figs (6 oz), 2/3 cup dried apricots (6 oz), 1/3 cup pitted dates (4 oz), 1 1/3 cups walnuts (4 oz), finely chopped, toasted , and cooled, 1/4 cup sweet red wine such as Manischewitz Extra Heavy Malaga, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne, 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger.Dried Fruit and Nut Paste.

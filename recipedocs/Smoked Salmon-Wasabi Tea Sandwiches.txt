Smoked Salmon-Wasabi Tea Sandwiches recipe.  Mix wasabi powder and 2 teaspoons water in medium bowl to form paste. Add cream cheese; using electric mixer, beat until well combined. Place all bread slices on work surface. Spread each with wasabi cream cheese, dividing equally. Top 6 bread slices with smoked salmon, dividing equally. Sprinkle lemon peel, then cilantro over salmon. Top with remaining bread slices, cheese side down. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Wrap sandwiches individually in paper towels and refrigerate.) Trim crusts. Cut each sandwich into 4 triangles. Transfer to platter cut side up. Smoked Salmon-Wasabi Tea Sandwiches has 79.0 calories. Smoked Salmon-Wasabi Tea Sandwiches has 4.0 grams of protein. Smoked Salmon-Wasabi Tea Sandwiches has 4.0 grams of fat. Smoked Salmon-Wasabi Tea Sandwiches has 162.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Smoked Salmon-Wasabi Tea Sandwiches are 1 tablespoon wasabi powder, 2 teaspoons water, 8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature, 12 very thin slices whole wheat bread, 8 ounces thinly sliced smoked salmon, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro.Look for wasabi powder in the spice aisle or the Asian foods section of the market.

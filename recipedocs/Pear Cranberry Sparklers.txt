Pear Cranberry Sparklers recipe.  Combine nectar and juice up to 3 days in advance. Pour into champagne flutes or wineglasses; top with seltzer or Prosecco right before serving. The ingredients of Pear Cranberry Sparklers are 2 cups pear nectar, 1/2 cup unsweetened cranberry juice (or 3/4 cup cranberry juice cocktail for a sweeter drink), Seltzer or Prosecco, to top off.Is one made with cocktails in hand.

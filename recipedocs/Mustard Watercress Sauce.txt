Mustard Watercress Sauce recipe.  In a saucepan cook the shallot in the butter over moderately low heat, stirring, until it is softened, add the wine, and boil the mixture until the wine is almost evaporated. Remove the pan from the heat and whisk in the sour cream, the mustards, the watercress, the water, and salt and pepper to taste. Serve the sauce at room temperature with poached fish or steamed or grilled vegetables. Mustard Watercress Sauce has 189.0 calories. Mustard Watercress Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Mustard Watercress Sauce has 18.0 grams of fat. Mustard Watercress Sauce has 241.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mustard Watercress Sauce are 1/4 cup minced shallots, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1/4 cup dry white wine, 1 cup sour cream, 3 tablespoons coarse-grained mustard, 2 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard, 1 cup finely chopped watercress, 1/3 cup hot water.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

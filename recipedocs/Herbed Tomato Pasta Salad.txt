Herbed Tomato Pasta Salad recipe. Stir together tomatoes, sugar, and herbs in a large bowl until combined well. Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente, then drain. Add hot pasta to tomato mixture. Cook garlic in olive oil in a 1-quart saucepan over moderately high heat, stirring, 1 minute. Add garlic with oil to pasta and tomatoes and toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Herbed Tomato Pasta Salad has 846.0 calories. Herbed Tomato Pasta Salad has 18.0 grams of protein. Herbed Tomato Pasta Salad has 43.0 grams of fat. Herbed Tomato Pasta Salad has 33.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Herbed Tomato Pasta Salad are 2 1/2 lb cherry tomatoes, quartered, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 1/4 cups mixed chopped fresh herbs, 1 lb campanelle (bell-shaped pasta), 5 garlic cloves, chopped, 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, Accompaniment: grated ricotta salata.Active time: 20 min Start to finish: 30 min.

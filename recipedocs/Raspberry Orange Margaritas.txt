Raspberry Orange Margaritas recipe.  Puree raspberries in a blender. Force puree through a fine sieve into a small bowl and discard solids. In a cocktail shaker, combine 1/2 cup puree and remaining ingredients and shake well. Pour drink over ice into tall glass. Raspberry Orange Margaritas has 215.0 calories. Raspberry Orange Margaritas has 2.0 grams of protein. Raspberry Orange Margaritas has 1.0 grams of fat. Raspberry Orange Margaritas has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Raspberry Orange Margaritas are 2 cups (about 10 ounces) raspberries, 3/4 cup fresh orange juice, 3/4 cup white tequila, 2 tablespoons sugar.

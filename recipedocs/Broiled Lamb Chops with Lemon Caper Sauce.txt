Broiled Lamb Chops with Lemon Caper Sauce recipe. Pat the lamb chops dry, sprinkle them with salt and pepper to taste, and broil them on the rack of a broiler pan under a preheated broiler about 4 inches from the heat for 6 minutes. Turn the chops, broil them for 4 minutes more for medium-rare meat, and let them stand for 5 minutes. While the chops are standing, in a small saucepan melt the butter and stir in the zest, the capers, the lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste. Transfer the chops to 2 plates and pour the sauce over them. Broiled Lamb Chops with Lemon Caper Sauce has 477.0 calories. Broiled Lamb Chops with Lemon Caper Sauce has 17.0 grams of protein. Broiled Lamb Chops with Lemon Caper Sauce has 45.0 grams of fat. Broiled Lamb Chops with Lemon Caper Sauce has 98.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Broiled Lamb Chops with Lemon Caper Sauce are two 1 1/2-inch-thick loin lamb chops, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest, 1 teaspoon drained bottled capers, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

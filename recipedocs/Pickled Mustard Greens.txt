Pickled Mustard Greens recipe.  Rinse greens. Fill large bowl with cold water. Add greens and soak 10 minutes. Drain greens; rinse and drain again. Cut out and discard core from greens, then coarsely chop leaves will yield about 3 1/2 cups). Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté 30 seconds. Add chopped greens. Stir 3 minutes. Sprinkle sugar over, then 1/4 teaspoon salt; stir to blend. Mix in jalapeño chile; stir 1 minute. Transfer to bowl and cool. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 week ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Also known as preserved kohlrabi or mustard cabbage; sold at Asian markets. The ingredients of Pickled Mustard Greens are 2 10-ounce packages pickled mustard greens (such as Caravelle brand cai chua)*, 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 red jalapeño chile, seeded, chopped.

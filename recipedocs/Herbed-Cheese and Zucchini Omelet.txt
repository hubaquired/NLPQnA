Herbed-Cheese and Zucchini Omelet recipe.  Melt butter in heavy large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add zucchini and sauté until tender, about 3 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer zucchini to plate. Remove skillet from heat. Beat yolks and creamy herb cheese in small bowl to blend. Using clean dry beater, beat egg whites in medium bowl until soft peaks form. Fold yolk mixture into whites. Place same skillet over medium-high heat. When butter sizzles, pour egg mixture into skillet. Sprinkle zucchini evenly over. Cook until omelet begins to set, about 3 minutes. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over. Using large spatula, fold omelet over, forming semicircle. Cover skillet; cook until omelet puffs and is cooked through, about 3 minutes. Slide omelet onto large plate. The ingredients of Herbed-Cheese and Zucchini Omelet are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1 large zucchini, trimmed, cut into matchstick-size pieces, 4 eggs, separated, 1/4 cup creamy garlic and herb cheese (such as Rondelé), 3/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese.If you enjoy spicy food, pass some hot pepper sauce to sprinkle on top of this light, soufflé-like omelet.

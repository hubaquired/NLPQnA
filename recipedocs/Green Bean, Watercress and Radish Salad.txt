Green Bean, Watercress and Radish Salad recipe.  Cook beans in large pot of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Drain beans; rinse under cold water. Drain and pat dry. Whisk lemon juice, vinegar and mustard in medium bowl to blend. Whisk in oil and broth. Add chopped watercress. Season with salt and pepper. Toss beans, trimmed watercress and radishes in bowl with dressing. Green Bean, Watercress and Radish Salad has 139.0 calories. Green Bean, Watercress and Radish Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Green Bean, Watercress and Radish Salad has 11.0 grams of fat. Green Bean, Watercress and Radish Salad has 88.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Bean, Watercress and Radish Salad are 2 1/2 pounds green beans, trimmed, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar, 4 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1/2 cup olive oil, 1/2 cup canned chicken broth, 1 cup chopped watercress leaves, 1 bunch watercress, ends trimmed, 1 1/2 cups thinly sliced radishes.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

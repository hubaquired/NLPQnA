Kona Swizzle recipe. Gently muddle or mix with a swizzle stick) 1/4 cup spiced rum or try Chairman's Reserve Spiced Rum), 4 1/2 teaspoons orgeat syrup such as Brooklyn Orgeat Works or Monin), 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon simple syrup, and 2 mint sprigs in a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake well. Pour into a Pilsner or Collins glass. Fill glass halfway with crushed ice, pushing mint toward bottom of the glass. Stir with a swizzle stick. Top with more crushed ice, mounding over rim of the glass. Top with 2 dashes Peychaud's bitters and 1 dash Angostura bitters. Garnish with 2 mint sprigs. The ingredients of Kona Swizzle are 1/4 cup spiced rum, 4 1/2 teaspoons orgeat syrup, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon simple syrup, 4 mint sprigs, 2 dashes Peychaud's bitters, 1 dash Angostura bitters.Sure, you can mix it with cola, but we prefer our spiced rum in a proper cocktail like the Kona Swizzle, a floral riff on the classic Queen's Park Swizzle, Created by New York city bartender Brian Miller.

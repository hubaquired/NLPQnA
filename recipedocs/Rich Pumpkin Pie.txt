Rich Pumpkin Pie recipe.  Line a 9" pie tin with pastry and place foil on top. Fill with dry beans and bake in a 400°F. oven for 10 minutes. Remove the beans and foil. Combine the pumpkin with the sugar, eggs, cream, seasonings and cognac and blend well. Pour through a strainer into the pie shell. Sprinkle with chopped candied ginger and bake in a 375°F. oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the pumpkin is set. Serve slightly warm with cheese or whipped cream, or both. Rich Pumpkin Pie has 314.0 calories. Rich Pumpkin Pie has 6.0 grams of protein. Rich Pumpkin Pie has 16.0 grams of fat. Rich Pumpkin Pie has 235.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rich Pumpkin Pie are Pastry for 9" pie, 2 cups cooked puréed pumpkin, 1 cup brown sugar, 6 eggs, lightly beaten, 2 cups cream, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves, 1/2 teaspoon mace, 1/3 cup cognac, 4 tablespoons finely chopped candied ginger.

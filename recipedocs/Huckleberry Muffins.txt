Huckleberry Muffins recipe. Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the egg, oil, and milk. Drop in the huckleberries, mix, and pour the batter into the cups of a greased muffin tin. Top with a crumble of cinnamon, butter, and brown sugar to taste), and bake at 400ºF for about 20 minutes. Huckleberry Muffins has 257.0 calories. Huckleberry Muffins has 3.0 grams of protein. Huckleberry Muffins has 10.0 grams of fat. Huckleberry Muffins has 249.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Huckleberry Muffins are 1 1/2 cup flour, 3/4 cup sugar, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 egg, 1/3 cup vegetable oil, 1/3 cup milk, Huckleberries (at least two big fistfuls), Cinnamon, Butter, Brown sugar.Tart huckleberries add zing to these tender muffins, topped off with a crackly, sweet brown sugar–cinnamon crust.

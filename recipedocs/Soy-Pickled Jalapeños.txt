The ingredients of Soy-Pickled Jalapeños are 3 large fresh jalapeños (4 inches), sliced 1/8 inch thick, 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup water, 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest, 2 teaspoons packed dark brown sugar.Green chiles pickled in soy sauce and vinegar are a traditional Korean banchan, so food editor Lillian Chou, who created this menu, was surprised when she discovered a similar dish made with jalapeños and soy sauce at a taco bar in Mexico. But it's no wonder the combination has universal appeal. Here, Chou calls for brown sugar and lemon zest in place of vinegar, and the ingredients hit all the right spots: hot, salty, and a little sweet.

Melon and Prosciutto Risotto recipe. Bring 6 cups vegetable broth to a simmer. Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add 1/2 medium cantaloupe peeled, seeded, grated), 2 chopped shallots, and 1 chopped garlic clove; cook, stirring, until liquid is thickened, 8-10 minutes. Add 2 cups arborio rice and 1/4 cup dry white wine; cook for 2 minutes. Add broth by 1/2-cupfuls, stirring often and cooking until liquid is almost absorbed before adding more, until all broth has been added and rice is tender, 25-30 minutes. Stir in 1/4 cup mascarpone and 1 ounce. chopped prosciutto; top with thinly sliced basil. Melon and Prosciutto Risotto has 346.0 calories. Melon and Prosciutto Risotto has 7.0 grams of protein. Melon and Prosciutto Risotto has 10.0 grams of fat. Melon and Prosciutto Risotto has 207.0 grams of sodium.

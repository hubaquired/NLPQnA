Cantaloupe Conserve recipe. Bring sugar, water, and lemon juice to a boil, stirring occasionally until sugar is dissolved, then boil, uncovered, until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Stir in cantaloupe and raisins and simmer, uncovered, stirring frequently, until cantaloupe is translucent and syrup is thickened, 15 to 20 minutes. Transfer conserve to a metal bowl, then set bowl in a larger bowl of ice and cold water to cool, stirring occasionally. Cantaloupe Conserve has 130.0 calories. Cantaloupe Conserve has 1.0 grams of protein. Cantaloupe Conserve has 20.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cantaloupe Conserve are 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup water, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 large ripe cantaloupe, seeds and rind discarded and flesh cut into 1-inch pieces (4 cups), 1/3 cup golden raisins, Accompaniment: plain yogurt.Spooning this conserve over yogurt makes a light, sweet summer dessert.

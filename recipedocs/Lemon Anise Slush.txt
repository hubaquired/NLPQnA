Lemon Anise Slush recipe.  Blend juice, sugar, and anise seeds in a blender until seeds are finely ground, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Add vodka or water and enough ice to fill blender, then blend until smooth. Serve immediately. Lemon Anise Slush has 201.0 calories. Lemon Anise Slush has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon Anise Slush are 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 teaspoon anise seeds, 3/4 cup vodka or water, About 4 cups ice cubes, cracked if large.

Beef Spice Rub recipe.  Toast cumin in a dry small heavy skillet over medium heat, shaking skillet frequently, until fragrant and a shade darker, about 2 minutes. Cool, then finely grind with remaining ingredients in grinder. Beef Spice Rub has 77.0 calories. Beef Spice Rub has 3.0 grams of protein. Beef Spice Rub has 3.0 grams of fat. Beef Spice Rub has 2885.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Beef Spice Rub are 3 tablespoons cumin seeds, 2 tablespoons black peppercorns, 1 1/2 tablespoons paprika (not hot), 1 tablespoon kosher salt, 1 (2-inch) piece cinnamon stick, 2 teaspoons whole allspice, 1 teaspoon hot red-pepper flakes, Equipment: an electric coffee/spice grinder.

Tropical Banana Splits recipe. Combine first 4 ingredients in medium bowl. Let stand 5 minutes. Place 2 banana pieces in each of 4 dishes. Top with 1 scoop of each sorbet. Spoon over fruit and juices. Sprinkle with coconut, if desired. The ingredients of Tropical Banana Splits are 1 1/2 cups diced strawberries, 1 kiwi, peeled, diced, 1/2 mango, peeled, diced, 1/4 cup coconut-flavored rum or liqueur, 4 bananas, peeled, cut lengthwise in half, 4 1/4-cup scoops apricot sorbet, 4 1/4-cup scoops raspberry sorbet, 2 tablespoons toasted coconut (optional).A quick, colorful and low-cal dessert.

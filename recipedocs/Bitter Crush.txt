Bitter Crush recipe.  Fill an 8- to 10-ounce highball glass with crushed ice. Stir together Aperol, rum, lemon juice, and bitters in a small glass measuring cup. Add sugar to taste, stirring until sugar is dissolved, then pour over ice. Bitter Crush has 162.0 calories. Bitter Crush has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bitter Crush are Crushed ice, 2 ounces (1/4 cup) Aperol (bitter orange aperitif), 1 1/2 ounces (3 tablespoons) white rum, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 2 dashes orange bitters, 1/2 to 1 teaspoon sugar, Garnish: 1 (1 1/2-inch-long) lemon twist.Aperol, a popular Italian spirit, is often served on the rocks.

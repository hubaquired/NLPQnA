Argentine Beef, Pork, and Hominy Stew recipe.  Stir together meats and water in a 12-quart heavy pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, partially covered, stirring occasionally, 3 1/2 hours. Add vegetables and paprika, then simmer, partially covered, stirring occasionally, 30 minutes, or until vegetables are tender. Add hominy, beans, salt, and pepper and simmer, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes. Argentine Beef, Pork, and Hominy Stew has 865.0 calories.

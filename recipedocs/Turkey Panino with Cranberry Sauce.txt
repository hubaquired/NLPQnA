Turkey Panino with Cranberry Sauce recipe.  Build a sandwich with bread, turkey, cheddar, and sage. Butter outer slices of bread and cook in a panini press or skillet until bread is toasted and cheese melts. Serve with cranberry sauce. The ingredients of Turkey Panino with Cranberry Sauce are Country-style bread, Sliced turkey, Sliced sharp white cheddar or Gruyère, Fresh sage leaves, Cranberry sauce.Transform leftover turkey into the ultimate grilled cheese.

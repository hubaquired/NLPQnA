Blue Cheese Dip recipe. Set strainer over medium bowl; line with double layer of cheesecloth. Place yogurt in cloth-lined strainer. Cover with plastic wrap; chill overnight. Discard liquid drained from yogurt. Transfer yogurt to processor. Add cheese and garlic; blend until smooth. Transfer dip to bowl. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill.) Sprinkle with celery seeds. The ingredients of Blue Cheese Dip are 1 16-ounce container plain nonfat yogurt (scant 2 cups), 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese (about 1 1/4 ounces), 1 garlic clove, minced, 1/4 teaspoon celery seeds.Drained nonfat yogurt—rather than mayonnaise or sour cream—provides the creamy texture. Start making this dip a day ahead, and try it with apple or pear slices.  Condiment Spread Sauce Cheese Dairy Appetizer No-Cook Low Fat Yogurt Blue Cheese Summer Healthy Bon Appétit.

Quatre-Épices recipe.  Put all of the ingredients in a spice mill or blender and process until evenly ground. Store in a cool, dark, dry place. Quatre-Épices has 25.0 calories. Quatre-Épices has 1.0 grams of protein. Quatre-Épices has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Quatre-Épices are 2 tablespoons (1/8 cup) white peppercorns, 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg, 1/2 teaspoon (about 12) whole cloves, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger."Four-spices" usually include four of five spices and are commonly used to season forcemeats for sausages and terrines. I include all five. This is one combination of spices that I try to keep on hand in small quantities.

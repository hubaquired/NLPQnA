Garlicky Cheese Dip recipe.  Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth, then chill, covered. Garlicky Cheese Dip has 131.0 calories. Garlicky Cheese Dip has 12.0 grams of protein. Garlicky Cheese Dip has 7.0 grams of fat. Garlicky Cheese Dip has 572.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Garlicky Cheese Dip are 1 cup whole-milk cottage cheese, 2 tablespoons sour cream, 1 hard-boiled large egg, forced through a medium-mesh sieve, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives, 1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh dill, 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped garlic, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspooon black pepper, 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin.

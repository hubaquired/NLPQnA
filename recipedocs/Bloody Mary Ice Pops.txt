Bloody Mary Ice Pops recipe. Blend all ingredients with 1/2 teaspoon each of salt and pepper in a blender until smooth, then strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a large measuring cup, pressing on and then discarding solids. Pour into molds. Freeze 30 minutes. Insert sticks, then freeze until firm, about 24 hours. Bloody Mary Ice Pops has 48.0 calories. Bloody Mary Ice Pops has 1.0 grams of protein. Bloody Mary Ice Pops has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bloody Mary Ice Pops are 2 1/4 cups tomato juice (18 ounces), 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon bottled horseradish, 3 or 4 drops Worcestershire sauce, 1/3 cup vodka, Equipment: 8 (1/3-cup) ice pop molds and 8 wooden sticks.

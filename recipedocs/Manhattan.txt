Manhattan recipe.  In mixing glass or cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine whiskey, vermouth, and bitters. Stir well, about 20 seconds, then strain into cocktail glass. Garnish with cherry and serve. Manhattan has 168.0 calories. Manhattan has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Manhattan are 2 ounces rye or bourbon whiskey, 1 ounce sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura or orange bitters, Maraschino cherry for garnish.This drink was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.

Cosmopolitan Champagne Cocktail recipe.  Stir together Cointreau, juices, and sugar and chill, covered, 2 to 6 hours. Just before serving, divide among 10 Champagne flutes and top off with Champagne. Cosmopolitan Champagne Cocktail has 148.0 calories. Cosmopolitan Champagne Cocktail has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cosmopolitan Champagne Cocktail are 1 1/4 cups Cointreau or Grand Marnier, 1 1/4 cups cranberry juice cocktail, 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons superfine granulated sugar, 4 cups chilled Champagne or other sparkling wine.We thought we would update the popular Cosmopolitan by substituting Champagne for the usual lemon vodka. We garnished the drinks with skewers of sugar-coated fresh cranberries (thawed frozen ones will work just fine, too).

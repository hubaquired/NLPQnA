Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper recipe. In a small bowl whisk together the vinegar and salt and black pepper to taste, add the oil in a stream, whisking, and whisk the dressing until it is emulsified. In a large bowl combine the lettuce, the arugula, the carrot, and the bell pepper, drizzle the dressing over the salad, and toss the salad until it is combined well. Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper has 270.0 calories. Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper has 2.0 grams of protein. Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper has 27.0 grams of fat. Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper has 21.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper are 4 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 1/2 cup olive oil, 10 cups loosely packed bite-size pieces of leaf lettuce, rinsed and spun dry, 3 cups arugula, course stems discarded and the leaves rinsed and spun dry, 1/2 cup grated carrot, 1/2 cup julienne strips of yellow bell pepper.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

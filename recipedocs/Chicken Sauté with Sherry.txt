Chicken Sauté with Sherry recipe. Melt butter in skillet and sauté chicken pieces, turning to color on all sides-do not let them get too brown, the chicken should be just a delicate golden color. Reduce flame and add salt, pepper, and ginger. Add 1/2 cup sherry and cook gently for 15 to 20 minutes, turning chicken once or twice to bathe it evently in the juices. When tender remove to a hot platter. Add remaining sherry to pan, turn up heat, cook, and stir, scraping up the brown crust from the pan and blending it into the sauce. When blended and hot, pour sauce over chicken. Serve with sautéed or fried potatoes and a green salad. With this, drink a dry white wine. Chicken Sauté with Sherry has 1012.0 calories. Chicken Sauté with Sherry has 74.0 grams of protein. Chicken Sauté with Sherry has 77.0 grams of fat. Chicken Sauté with Sherry has 1074.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chicken Sauté with Sherry are 3 to 4 pound chicken, quartered, backbone removed, 6 tablespoons butter, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon white pepper, 1/2 teaspoon powdered ginger, 1 cup dry sherry.

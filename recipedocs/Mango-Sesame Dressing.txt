Mango-Sesame Dressing recipe.  Combine all ingredients in blender. Cover tightly; blend until dressing is smooth. Season to taste with salt and pepper. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 week ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Mango-Sesame Dressing are 3/4 cup vegetable oil, 3/4 cup Major Grey's mango chutney, 6 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar, 3 large garlic cloves, peeled, 6 3/4 teaspoons soy sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons Asian sesame oil, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1 1/2 teaspoons dried crushed red pepper.You'll use some of the dressing for the grilled chicken and the rest for the noodle salad .

Chile-Cilantro Butter recipe. Mix 1 finely chopped seeded Fresno chile, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro or marjoram, 1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon or lime juice, and 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt into 1/2 cup 1 stick) room-temperature unsalted butter. Season with freshly ground black pepper. Place on parchment or waxed paper and roll up into a log. Chill until firm, 1 hour. Butter can be made 2 days ahead. Keep chilled.

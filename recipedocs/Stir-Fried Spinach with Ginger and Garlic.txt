Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Garlic recipe. Wash spinach well, discarding coarse stems, and drain in a colander. In a wok or large heavy skillet heat oil over moderately high heat until very hot but not smoking and stir-fry gingerroot and garlic until fragrant, 10 to 15 seconds. Add spinach and stir-fry until wilted, about 1 minute. Season spinach with salt and pepper. Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Garlic has 77.0 calories. Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Garlic has 4.0 grams of protein. Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Garlic has 5.0 grams of fat. Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Garlic has 112.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stir-Fried Spinach with Ginger and Garlic are a 10-ounce bag fresh spinach or 2 small bunches (about 1 1/4 pounds total), 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, 1 teaspoon minced peeled fresh gingerroot, 1 garlic clove, minced.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

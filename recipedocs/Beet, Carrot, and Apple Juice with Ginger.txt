Beet, Carrot, and Apple Juice with Ginger recipe.  Pass first 5 ingredients through a juicer. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a large bowl. Stir in lemon juice. Pour into glasses. The ingredients of Beet, Carrot, and Apple Juice with Ginger are 2 pounds beets (about 6 medium), trimmed, peeled, cut into 1" pieces, 1 pound carrots (about 4 large), trimmed, peeled, cut into 1" pieces, 1 Gala or Empire apple (about 8 ounces), cored, cut into 1" pieces, 1 Granny smith apple (about 8 ounces), cored, cut into 1" pieces, 1 3" piece fresh ginger, peeled, chopped into 1" pieces, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice.For the best results, chill the produce first.

Seared Salmon with Balsamic Glaze recipe.  Stir together vinegar, water, lemon juice, and brown sugar. Pat salmon dry and season with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking. Increase heat to high and sear salmon, skin sides up, until well browned, about 4 minutes. Turn fish over and sear until just cooked through, 3 to 4 minutes more. Transfer salmon to plates and carefully add vinegar mixture to skillet liquid will bubble vigorously and steam). Simmer, stirring, until thickened and reduced to about 1/3 cup, about 2 minutes. Spoon glaze over salmon. Seared Salmon with Balsamic Glaze has 407.0 calories. Seared Salmon with Balsamic Glaze has 35.0 grams of protein. Seared Salmon with Balsamic Glaze has 25.0 grams of fat. Seared Salmon with Balsamic Glaze has 106.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Seared Salmon with Balsamic Glaze are 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 1/4 cup water, 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 4 teaspoons packed light brown sugar, 4 (6-ounce) center-cut salmon fillets, 2 teaspoons vegetable oil.A tangy glaze brings out the sweetness of this crisp-crusted salmon.

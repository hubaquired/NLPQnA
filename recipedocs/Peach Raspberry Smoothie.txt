Peach Raspberry Smoothie recipe. Freeze peaches in 1 layer in a large sealed plastic bag until frozen, about 4 hours. Blend peaches with remaining ingredients in a blender until smooth. Thin with more water if necessary. Peach Raspberry Smoothie has 223.0 calories. Peach Raspberry Smoothie has 3.0 grams of protein. Peach Raspberry Smoothie has 1.0 grams of fat. Peach Raspberry Smoothie has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peach Raspberry Smoothie are 3/4 lb ripe peaches, pitted and cut into 1/2-inch wedges, 1 cup water, 1 cup fresh raspberries (8 oz), 1/4 cup sugar, 1/8 teaspoon almond extract.

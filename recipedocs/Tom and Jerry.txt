Tom and Jerry recipe.  Beat the egg white until stiff; beat the yolk until thick and lemon-colored. Combine them and beat in the sugar. Have ready a warmed 8-ounce mug. Add the egg mixture, brandy and rum, then fill with hot milk. Stir and top with a little grated nutmeg. Tom and Jerry has 336.0 calories. Tom and Jerry has 9.0 grams of protein. Tom and Jerry has 7.0 grams of fat. Tom and Jerry has 107.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tom and Jerry are 1 egg, 1 teaspoon superfine sugar, 1 1/2 ounces brandy, 1 1/2 ounces dark rum, 1/3 cup hot milk, Nutmeg.

Spiced Chili Sauce recipe.  Combine all of the ingredients in a small bowl and serve within 2 to 3 hours. For the freshest taste, serve this sauce within 1 hour.) The ingredients of Spiced Chili Sauce are 1/4 cup finely diced scallions (white part only), 1 ripe plum tomato, seeded and cut into 1/8-inch dice, 1 teaspoon minced red or green jalapeño chili, seeds removed according to desired hotness, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice (about 4 limes), 1/4 cup rice vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar, Salt and pepper, to taste, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves.Chilies, lime juice and mint enhance the flavor of our Thai Summer Rolls .

Zucchini and Carrots with Green Onions and Dill recipe. Roll up grated zucchini in large kitchen towel; press to dry. Heat oil in large deep skillet over high heat. Add 1 cup green onions and sauté 30 seconds. Add zucchini, carrots, and 2 tablespoons dill. Sauté until vegetables are just tender, tossing often, about 8 minutes. Mix in remaining 1/2 cup green onions and 2 tablespoons dill. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Zucchini and Carrots with Green Onions and Dill has 106.0 calories. Zucchini and Carrots with Green Onions and Dill has 3.0 grams of protein. Zucchini and Carrots with Green Onions and Dill has 6.0 grams of fat. Zucchini and Carrots with Green Onions and Dill has 72.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Zucchini and Carrots with Green Onions and Dill are 1 1/2 to 1 3/4 pounds zucchini, trimmed, coarsely grated, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1 1/2 cups chopped green onions (about 6 large), divided, 1 1/2 pounds carrots (about 5 large), peeled, coarsely grated, 4 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, divided.

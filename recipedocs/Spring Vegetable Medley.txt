Spring Vegetable Medley recipe.  Bring large pot of water to boil. Add carrots and cook 1 minute. Add asparagus, fennel and peas; cook until all are crisp tender, about 2 minutes longer. Drain vegetables. Return to pot. Add oil and tarragon and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Spring Vegetable Medley has 97.0 calories. Spring Vegetable Medley has 4.0 grams of protein. Spring Vegetable Medley has 5.0 grams of fat. Spring Vegetable Medley has 52.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spring Vegetable Medley are 6 ounces trimmed baby carrots, peeled, 1 pound asparagus, trimmed, cut into 4-inch lengths, 1 large fennel bulb, thinly sliced, 8 ounces sugar snap peas, stringed, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon.

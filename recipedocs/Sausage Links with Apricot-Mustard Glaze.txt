Sausage Links with Apricot-Mustard Glaze recipe.  Whisk apricot preserves in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat until melted and smooth, about 1 minute. Add mustard and whisk until beginning to simmer, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat. Stir in chopped rosemary. Season glaze to taste with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Stir over medium heat until heated through.) Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add sausages and sauté until browned and cooked through, about 10 minutes. Transfer to saucepan with glaze. Stir over medium heat until sausages are glazed, about 1 minute. Transfer to platter and serve. Sausage Links with Apricot-Mustard Glaze has 267.0 calories. Sausage Links with Apricot-Mustard Glaze has 10.0 grams of protein. Sausage Links with Apricot-Mustard Glaze has 20.0 grams of fat. Sausage Links with Apricot-Mustard Glaze has 566.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sausage Links with Apricot-Mustard Glaze are 1/2 cup apricot preserves, 1/4 cup sweet-hot mustard, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 18 ounces breakfast sausage links (such as pork links or hot and sweet links; about 20 links).A sweet-and-hot glaze dresses up purchased breakfast links. If you prefer extra spice, use the sausages labeled "hot and sweet links.".

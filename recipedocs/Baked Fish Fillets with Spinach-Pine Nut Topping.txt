Baked Fish Fillets with Spinach-Pine Nut Topping recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Butter rimmed baking sheet. Arrange fish on prepared sheet; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Combine spinach souffl, nuts, onions, garlic and lemon peel in medium bowl; stir to blend well. Spoon topping over fish. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake fish until just opaque in center, about 15 minutes. Transfer fish to plates. Baked Fish Fillets with Spinach-Pine Nut Topping has 309.0 calories. Baked Fish Fillets with Spinach-Pine Nut Topping has 41.0 grams of protein. Baked Fish Fillets with Spinach-Pine Nut Topping has 14.0 grams of fat. Baked Fish Fillets with Spinach-Pine Nut Topping has 301.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Fish Fillets with Spinach-Pine Nut Topping are 6 6- to 7-ounce sea bass or orange roughy fillets, 1 12-ounce package frozen spinach soufflé, thawed, 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted, 3 green onions, finely chopped, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese.

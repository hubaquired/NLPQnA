Green Beans with Olives recipe.  In a saucepan of boiling salted water boil the green beans with the orégano for 5 minutes, or until they are crisp-tender. Drain the beans, transfer them to a bowl, and toss them with the olives, the butter, the lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste. Green Beans with Olives has 98.0 calories. Green Beans with Olives has 2.0 grams of protein. Green Beans with Olives has 7.0 grams of fat. Green Beans with Olives has 48.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Beans with Olives are 1/2 pound green beans, trimmed, 1/2 teaspoon dried orégano, 4 Kalamata or other brine-cured black olives, pitted and chopped, 1 tablespoon softened unsalted butter, lemon juice to taste.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Scrod with Tomatoes, Bacon, and Sherry recipe. In a skillet cook the bacon over moderate heat, turning it, until it is crisp, transfer it with tongs to paper towels to drain, and crumble it. Pour all but 1 tablespoon of the fat from the skillet and in the remaining fat cook the onion, stirring, until it begins to turn golden. Add the tomatoes, the Sherry, the soy sauce, and the sugar and simmer the sauce for 5 minutes, or until it is thickened. Add the scrod and simmer it, covered, for 4 minutes. Turn the scrod and simmer it, covered, for 4 minutes more, or until it just flakes. Transfer the scod with a slotted spatula to 2 plates and keep warm, covered. If desired, boil the sauce for 1 minute to thicken it slightly.) Spoon the sauce over the scrod and sprinkle the scrod with the bacon. Scrod with Tomatoes, Bacon, and Sherry has 286.0 calories. Scrod with Tomatoes, Bacon, and Sherry has 35.0 grams of protein. Scrod with Tomatoes, Bacon, and Sherry has 13.0 grams of fat. Scrod with Tomatoes, Bacon, and Sherry has 500.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Scrod with Tomatoes, Bacon, and Sherry are 2 slices of lean bacon, 1 small onion, chopped, 1/2 cup chopped drained canned tomatoes, 3 tablespoons medium-dry Sherry, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 1/8 teaspoon sugar, two 6-ounce scrod fillets.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

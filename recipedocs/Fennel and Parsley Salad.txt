Fennel and Parsley Salad recipe.  Place the fennel and parsley in a serving bowl and toss to combine. Place the orange juice, oil, mustard, salt, and pepper in a bowl and whisk to combine. Pour over the salad and serve with the simple leek and ricotta tarts . The ingredients of Fennel and Parsley Salad are 4 baby fennel, trimmed and thinly sliced, 2 cups flat-leaf parsley leaves, 1/4 cup (2 fluid ounces) orange juice, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon seeded mustard, Sea salt and cracked black pepper.

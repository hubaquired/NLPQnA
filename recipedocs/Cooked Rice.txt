Cooked Rice recipe.  In a 5-quart heavy kettle bring water with rice to a boil over moderately high heat and boil mixture, uncovered and undisturbed, until water has evaporated and steam holes appear on surface of rice, about 8 minutes. Reduce heat to low and cook rice, covered and undisturbed, 15 minutes. Fluff rice with a fork. Cooked Rice has 439.0 calories. Cooked Rice has 8.0 grams of protein. Cooked Rice has 1.0 grams of fat. Cooked Rice has 13.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cooked Rice are 5 cups cold water, 2 1/2 cups long-grain white rice (not converted).Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

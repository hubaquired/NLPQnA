Light Lemon Yogurt Sauce recipe. Combine all of the ingredients in a small bowl. Refrigerate, covered, until needed. Light Lemon Yogurt Sauce has 82.0 calories. Light Lemon Yogurt Sauce has 8.0 grams of protein. Light Lemon Yogurt Sauce has 1.0 grams of fat. Light Lemon Yogurt Sauce has 408.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Light Lemon Yogurt Sauce are 1 cup plain nonfat yogurt, 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint or parsley, if available, or 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste.Gently drain the liquid from the top of the yogurt before combining with other ingredients so that the flavors are not diluted.

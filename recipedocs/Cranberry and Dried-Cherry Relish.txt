Cranberry and Dried-Cherry Relish recipe. Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Place half of mixture in processor. Using on/off turns, process until coarsely chopped. Transfer to medium bowl. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Chill at least 1 day and up to 2 days, stirring occasionally. Cranberry and Dried-Cherry Relish has 195.0 calories. The ingredients of Cranberry and Dried-Cherry Relish are 1 12-ounce package fresh cranberries, 1 cup dried tart cherries (about 5 ounces), 1 cup (packed) golden brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom.So good — and no cooking required.

Provolone Toasts recipe. Preheat broiler. Cut provolone to fit bread slices and set aside. Toast bread without cheese) on a large baking sheet 4 inches from heat until golden, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Turn bread over and arrange provolone on top. Broil until cheese is melted, 1 to 2 minutes. Provolone Toasts has 277.0 calories. Provolone Toasts has 14.0 grams of protein. Provolone Toasts has 12.0 grams of fat. Provolone Toasts has 658.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Provolone Toasts are 1/2 lb sliced provolone, 1 loaf Italian bread, cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-thick slices.

Creamy Fettuccine with Bacon recipe.  Cook bacon in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat until crisp. Using slotted spoon, transfer bacon to paper towel to drain. Pour off all but 1 tablespoon fat from skillet. Add carrots, peas and parsley and sauté 1 minute. Mix in cream and Parmesan and simmer sauce until it thickens slightly, about 3 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Place fettuccine in large bowl. Add cream mixture and toss well. Sprinkle with cooked bacon and serve. Creamy Fettuccine with Bacon has 1802.0 calories. Creamy Fettuccine with Bacon has 53.0 grams of protein. Creamy Fettuccine with Bacon has 135.0 grams of fat. Creamy Fettuccine with Bacon has 1305.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Fettuccine with Bacon are 8 ounces bacon, chopped, 1 cup shredded carrots, 1 cup frozen peas, thawed, 1 cup chopped fresh parsley, 2 cups whipping cream, 2 cups grated Parmesan, 1 pound fettuccine, freshly cooked.

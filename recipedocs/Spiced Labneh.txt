Spiced Labneh recipe.  Whisk labneh, allspice, and 1 1/2 teaspoons Aleppo pepper in a medium bowl; season with salt and black pepper. Drizzle with oil and top with more Aleppo pepper. Spiced Labneh has 126.0 calories. Spiced Labneh has 5.0 grams of protein. Spiced Labneh has 11.0 grams of fat. Spiced Labneh has 63.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spiced Labneh are 1 cup labneh (Lebanese strained yogurt), 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice, 1 1/2 teaspoons Aleppo pepper, plus more for serving, Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, Olive oil.

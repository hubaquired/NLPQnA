Watermelon and Cucumber Gazpacho recipe. Puree 4 cups watermelon in blender until smooth. Transfer puree to large bowl. Add remaining 1 cup diced watermelon and next 10 ingredients; stir to combine. Cover gazpacho and refrigerate until cold, at least 1 hour and up to 4 hours. Divide gazpacho among bowls; top with dollop of crème fraîche. Watermelon and Cucumber Gazpacho has 192.0 calories. Watermelon and Cucumber Gazpacho has 4.0 grams of protein. Watermelon and Cucumber Gazpacho has 6.0 grams of fat. Watermelon and Cucumber Gazpacho has 168.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watermelon and Cucumber Gazpacho are 1 3-pound seedless watermelon, diced (about 5 cups), divided, 1 small cucumber, peeled, seeded, diced (about 1 cup), 1 medium-size red bell pepper, seeded, diced (about 1 cup), 1 medium-size yellow bell pepper, seeded, diced (about 1 cup), 1 small jalapeño chile, seeded, minced, 3 pale green inner celery stalks, diced (about 1/2 cup), 1/2 small red onion, diced (about 1 cup), 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh mint, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup crème fraîche or sour cream.This fresh, colorful soup makes a great summer starter.

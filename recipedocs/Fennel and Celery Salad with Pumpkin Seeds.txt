Fennel and Celery Salad with Pumpkin Seeds recipe. Combine celery, fennel, parsley, and pumpkin seeds in large bowl. Whisk oil and lemon juice in small bowl. Season with salt and pepper; toss with vegetables. Toss in most of cheese; top with remaining cheese. The ingredients of Fennel and Celery Salad with Pumpkin Seeds are 3 celery stalks, cut crosswise into paper-thin slices, 1 small fennel bulb, trimmed, halved vertically, sliced paper-thin, 1/2 cup fresh Italian parsley leaves, 1/4 cup toasted salted pumpkin seeds (pepitas), 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese shavings (about 1 1/2 ounces).Use a V-slicer for the vegetables and a peeler for the Parmesan.

Brandy Blazer No.2 recipe.  Rinse an Old-fashioned glass with hot water to warm it. Add the sugar, orange peel, lemon peel, and brandy, and mash with a spoon or muddler. Light brandy and blaze for a few seconds. The ingredients of Brandy Blazer No.2 are 1 lump sugar, 1 twist orange peel, 1 twist lemon peel, 2 to 3 ounces brandy.

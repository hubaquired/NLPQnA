Fried Chickpeas and Spiced Nuts with Olives and Radishes recipe.  Transfer chickpeas, nuts, olives, and radishes to separate small dishes and serve together. The ingredients of Fried Chickpeas and Spiced Nuts with Olives and Radishes are Spicy Fried Chickpeas, Spiced Herbed Nuts, 1 pound (about 2 cups) mixed olives, 1 pound (about 15 small) radishes, trimmed.

Poached Pineapple in Cinnamon Syrup with Yogurt Cheese recipe.  In a fine sieve let the yogurt drain, covered and chilled, for 40 minutes. While the yogurt is draining, in a small saucepan combine the sugar, the cinnamon, and 3/4 cup water and boil the mixture for 5 minutes. Stir in the pineapple, simmer the mixture, covered, for 8 minutes, and transfer the pineapple with a slotted spoon to a metal bowl. Boil the syrup until it is reduced to about 1/2 cup, add the rum, and boil the syrup for 1 minute. Pour the syrup over the pineapple and chill the mixture, set in a larger bowl of ice and cold water. Divide the yogurt cheese between 2 bowls and spoon the pineapple mixture over it. Poached Pineapple in Cinnamon Syrup with Yogurt Cheese has 229.0 calories. Poached Pineapple in Cinnamon Syrup with Yogurt Cheese has 58.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Poached Pineapple in Cinnamon Syrup with Yogurt Cheese are 1 cup plain yogurt, 1/4 cup sugar, 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 cup fresh pineapple chunks, 1 tablespoon dark rum.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Goat Cheese and Mushroom Canapés recipe. Mix mushrooms with juice and oil; season with salt and pepper; let sit until wilted, 20 minutes. Mix with cheese, chives and thyme. Serve on toast points. The ingredients of Goat Cheese and Mushroom Canapés are 8 ounces cremini stemmed and diced, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1/4 cup olive oil, 8 ounces fresh goat cheese, 3 tablespoons chopped chives, 2 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme, 36 toast points.

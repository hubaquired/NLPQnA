The New York Sour recipe.  Combine 2 ounces rye or bourbon whiskey, 1 ounce fresh lemon juice, and 1 ounce simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Fill with ice, cover, and shake until outside of shaker is frosty, about 30 seconds. Strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice. Gently pour 1/2 ounce fruity red wine such as Shiraz or Malbec) over the back of a spoon held just above the drink's surface so wine floats on top. The New York Sour has 286.0 calories.

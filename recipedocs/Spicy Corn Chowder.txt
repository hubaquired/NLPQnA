Spicy Corn Chowder recipe. Melt butter in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, red and green bell peppers, cumin and cayenne and sauté until vegetables are tender, about 8 minutes. Add flour and stir 1 minute. Gradually mix in broth, milk and reserved corn liquid. Bring mixture to boil, whisking until smooth. Mix in corn and stir until corn is just heated through. Ladle soup into bowls. Garnish with cilantro if desired and serve. Spicy Corn Chowder has 267.0 calories. Spicy Corn Chowder has 6.0 grams of protein. Spicy Corn Chowder has 15.0 grams of fat. Spicy Corn Chowder has 488.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Corn Chowder are 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, 1 small onion, chopped, 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper, 1/2 cup diced green bell pepper, 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1/4 cup all purpose flour, 1 cup canned chicken broth, 1 cup milk (do not use low-fat or nonfat), 1 16- to 17-ounce can whole-kernel corn, drained, liquid reserved, Minced fresh cilantro (optional).

Bulgur, Pine Nut, and Red Pepper Pilaf recipe. In a small heavy saucepan cook onion in oil over moderate heat, stirring, until softened. Stir in broth, bell pepper, and bulgur and bring liquid to a boil. Cook mixture, covered, over low heat 12 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed. Remove pan from heat and let stand, covered, 5 minutes. Stir in pine nuts, chives, and salt and pepper to taste. Bulgur, Pine Nut, and Red Pepper Pilaf has 365.0 calories. Bulgur, Pine Nut, and Red Pepper Pilaf has 12.0 grams of protein. Bulgur, Pine Nut, and Red Pepper Pilaf has 15.0 grams of fat. Bulgur, Pine Nut, and Red Pepper Pilaf has 204.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bulgur, Pine Nut, and Red Pepper Pilaf are 1 small onion, chopped fine, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons chicken broth, 3 tablespoons finely diced red bell pepper, 3/4 cup bulgur*, 2 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted lightly, 2 teaspoons minced fresh chives, *available at natural foods stores and many supermarkets..Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

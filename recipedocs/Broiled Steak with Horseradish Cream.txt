Broiled Steak with Horseradish Cream recipe.  Preheat broiler. Oil rack of a broiler pan. Pat steak dry and sprinkle all over with 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Broil steak on rack of broiler pan 3 inches from heat 4 to 5 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer to a cutting board and let stand, loosely covered with foil, 5 minutes. Stir together sour cream, horseradish, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a small bowl and serve with steak. Broiled Steak with Horseradish Cream has 485.0 calories. Broiled Steak with Horseradish Cream has 40.0 grams of protein. Broiled Steak with Horseradish Cream has 34.0 grams of fat. Broiled Steak with Horseradish Cream has 582.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Broiled Steak with Horseradish Cream are 1 (1-inch-thick) sirloin steak (1 1/2 to 2 lb), 3/4 teaspoon salt, 3/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/2 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons drained bottled horseradish.

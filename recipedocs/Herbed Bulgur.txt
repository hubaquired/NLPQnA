Herbed Bulgur recipe.  Combine bulgur and boiling-hot water in a large bowl and let stand 20 minutes. Fluff with a fork and stir in herbs, oil, and salt and pepper to taste. The ingredients of Herbed Bulgur are 3/4 cup bulgur, 1 cup boiling-hot water, 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon, 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh chives, 1 tablespoon olive oil.A versatile alternative to couscous, this is a summer starch that doesn't have to be served warm. If you have fresh or cooked leftover vegetables in the kitchen, toss them in to make a main-course salad. You can also play with the herbs to suit the dishes this will accompany.

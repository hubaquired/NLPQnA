Jerked Shrimp with Melon Salsa recipe.  Prepare barbecue medium heat). Toss shrimp, oil, 2 teaspoons lime juice, and jerk seasoning blend in medium bowl. Let marinate 10 minutes. Meanwhile, toss cantaloupe, honeydew, cilantro, red onion, and remaining 2 teaspoons lime juice in medium bowl. Season salsa to taste with salt and pepper. Thread shrimp onto metal skewers. Grill until opaque in center, occasionally brushing with marinade, about 5 minutes. Serve with salsa and lime wedges. Jerked Shrimp with Melon Salsa has 245.0 calories. Jerked Shrimp with Melon Salsa has 2.0 grams of protein. Jerked Shrimp with Melon Salsa has 21.0 grams of fat. Jerked Shrimp with Melon Salsa has 41.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Jerked Shrimp with Melon Salsa are 6 large uncooked shrimp, peeled, deveined, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 4 teaspoons fresh lime juice, 2 teaspoons jerk or Caribbean-style seasoning blend, 3/4 cup diced cantaloupe, 3/4 cup diced honeydew melon, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1/4 cup diced red onion, 4 lime wedges.A lightly dressed salad of baby greens would complement this entrée nicely; serve rum-drizzled grilled pound cake slices afterward.

Cauliflower with Ginger and Mustard Seeds recipe.  In a large skillet heat the oil over moderate heat until it is hot but not smoking and in it cook the mustard seeds, covered, stirring occasionally, until the popping subsides. Add the gingerroot and the turmeric and cook the mixture, stirring, for 30 seconds. Add the cauliflower, stirring to coat it with the oil, and 1/2 cup water and steam the mixture, covered, adding more water a few tablespoons at a time if it evaporates, for 6 to 10 minutes, or until the cauliflower is just tender. Season the cauliflower mixture with the lemon juice and salt and pepper and stir in the coriander. Cauliflower with Ginger and Mustard Seeds has 102.0 calories. Cauliflower with Ginger and Mustard Seeds has 3.0 grams of protein. Cauliflower with Ginger and Mustard Seeds has 8.0 grams of fat. Cauliflower with Ginger and Mustard Seeds has 43.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cauliflower with Ginger and Mustard Seeds are 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, 2 teaspoons black mustard seeds (available at East Indian markets and some specialty foods shops) or yellow mustard seeds, 2 teaspoons grated peeled fresh gingerroot, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric, 1 large head of cauliflower (about 1 3/4 to 2 pounds), cut into in small flowerets about 1 inch in diameter, 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, or to taste, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander if desired.

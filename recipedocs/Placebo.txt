Placebo recipe. In cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine first 4 ingredients. Cover, shake vigorously, then strain into 4 tall glasses. Add ginger ale and stir. Garnish with lemon wedges and serve. The ingredients of Placebo are 1/4 cup Monin brand Granny Smith apple syrup (see Tip, below), 1/4 cupSimple Syrup, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice (from about 4 limes), 6 tablespoons fresh lemon juice (from about 2 lemons), 1 1/2 cups ginger ale, 4 lemon wedges for garnish.This sweet-tart liquor-less cocktail is very popular at restaurant Frisson, in San Francisco, which mixologist Duggan McDonnell has helped turn into a drinking destination.

Arugula, Endive, and Radicchio Salad recipe. In a bowl whisk together oil and vinegar and season with salt and pepper. Discard coarse stems from arugula. Thinly slice radicchio and cut endives crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces. In a large bowl toss greens with vinaigrette until coated well. Arugula, Endive, and Radicchio Salad has 100.0 calories. Arugula, Endive, and Radicchio Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Arugula, Endive, and Radicchio Salad has 9.0 grams of fat. Arugula, Endive, and Radicchio Salad has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Arugula, Endive, and Radicchio Salad are 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons white-wine vinegar, 2 bunches arugula (about 1 pound), 1 small head radicchio, 2 large Belgian endives.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

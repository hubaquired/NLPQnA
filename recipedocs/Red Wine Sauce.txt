Red Wine Sauce recipe.  In large skillet over medium-high heat cook butter and onion until onion is golden, about 6 minutes. Add red wine, thyme, and honey to skillet and boil until mixture is reduced by 2/3, about 10 minutes. Add chicken broth to skillet and boil until reduced to generous 1 cup, about 1 minute. Whisk in butter, salt, and pepper. Red Wine Sauce has 139.0 calories. Red Wine Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Red Wine Sauce has 8.0 grams of fat. Red Wine Sauce has 416.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red Wine Sauce are 2 tablespoons butter, 1 1/2 cups finely chopped onion, 2 cups dry red wine, 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme, 1 teaspoon honey, 2 cups low-salt chicken broth, 2 tablespoons butter, Salt and pepper.Serve with mustard-seed-crusted pork medallions.

Oranges with Pomegranate Molasses and Honey recipe.  Arrange orange slices, overlapping slightly, on large rimmed platter. Whisk honey, pomegranate molasses, ground cinnamon, and sea salt in small bowl to blend; drizzle evenly over oranges. Sprinkle oranges evenly with chopped dates. DO AHEAD: Can be made 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. A thick pomegranate syrup; available at some supermarkets and at Middle Eastern markets and from adrianascaravan.com. The ingredients of Oranges with Pomegranate Molasses and Honey are 8 large navel oranges (preferably Cara Cara), all peel and white pith cut away, oranges sliced into thin rounds, 1/4 cup floral honey (such as tupelo or orange blossom), 3 tablespoons pomegranate molasses*, 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt, 8 large Medjool dates, pitted, chopped.Here's the perfect ending to the feast: a light, bright dessert of oranges, dates, and cinnamon.

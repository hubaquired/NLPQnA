Creamy Broccoli and Carrot Slaw recipe. Combine mayonnaise, fresh lemon juice, onion and mustard in medium bowl; whisk to blend. Add broccoli and carrots; toss to coat. Season slaw to taste with salt and pepper. Refrigerate to blend flavors, about 20 minutes. Creamy Broccoli and Carrot Slaw has 192.0 calories. Creamy Broccoli and Carrot Slaw has 2.0 grams of protein. Creamy Broccoli and Carrot Slaw has 17.0 grams of fat. Creamy Broccoli and Carrot Slaw has 207.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Broccoli and Carrot Slaw are 3 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 teaspoons grated onion, 1 1/4 teaspoons coarse-grained Dijon mustard, 1 1/4 cups shredded peeled broccoli stems, 3/4 cup shredded peeled carrots.To make this easy dish even easier, prepare just the dressing and use two cups of packaged broccoli, coleslaw, a mixture of shredded broccoli stems, carrots and red cabbage.

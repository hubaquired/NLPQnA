Avocado Salad with Bell Pepper and Tomatoes recipe. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, garlic, and cayenne. Season with salt. Scoop out flesh from avocado halves, reserving shells, and chop. Transfer to a bowl and add bell pepper, tomatoes, scallion, and chopped cilantro. Drizzle with dressing and season with salt. Gently stir to combine. Spoon mixture into reserved shells. Garnish with whole cilantro leaves and serve immediately. The ingredients of Avocado Salad with Bell Pepper and Tomatoes are 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil, Juice of 1/2 lime, 1 small garlic clove, minced, Pinch of cayenne pepper, Coarse salt, 1 firm, ripe avocado, halved and pitted, 1/2 yellow bell pepper, ribs and seeds removed, diced, 6 cherry tomatoes, halved, 1 scallion, trimmed and thinly sliced, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro leaves, plus whole leaves for garnish.Avocado shells make handy vessels for a bright salad made with the scooped-out flesh. Lime juice, garlic, and a pinch of cayenne flavor the dressing. The salad can also be served as a topping for quesadillas or as a fresh filling for tacos.

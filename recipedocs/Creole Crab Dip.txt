Creole Crab Dip recipe. With motor running, drop garlic into a food processor and chop. Stop motor and add roasted red peppers, vinegar, cayenne to taste, and 1/2 teaspoon salt to food processor and purée until smooth. Add mayonnaise and process until combined. Transfer roasted red pepper mixture to a heavy medium pot and heat over low heat. Gently stir in crabmeat and cook until just heated through, then stir in chives. Creole Crab Dip has 273.0 calories. Creole Crab Dip has 18.0 grams of protein. Creole Crab Dip has 21.0 grams of fat. Creole Crab Dip has 717.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creole Crab Dip are 2 garlic cloves, 1 cup sliced bottled roasted red peppers, 1 1/2 teaspoons red wine vinegar, 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon cayenne, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1 pound crabmeat, picked over, 1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped chives, Accompaniment: crackers or crudités.

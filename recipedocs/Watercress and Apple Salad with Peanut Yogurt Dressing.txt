Watercress and Apple Salad with Peanut Yogurt Dressing recipe.  Cut apples into 2-inch-long matchsticks, then toss with 1 teaspoon lime juice. Whisk together peanut butter, yogurt, water, salt, and remaining 2 teaspoons lime juice, then toss with apples and watercress. Watercress and Apple Salad with Peanut Yogurt Dressing has 55.0 calories. Watercress and Apple Salad with Peanut Yogurt Dressing has 2.0 grams of protein. Watercress and Apple Salad with Peanut Yogurt Dressing has 2.0 grams of fat. Watercress and Apple Salad with Peanut Yogurt Dressing has 91.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watercress and Apple Salad with Peanut Yogurt Dressing are 3/4 lb apples (2), cored, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter, 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 1 tablespoon water, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 8 cups watercress sprigs (2 to 3 bunches).Active time: 30 min Start to finish: 30 min.

Mixed Berries with Mascarpone-Limoncello Cream recipe. Combine whipping cream, mascarpone, 3 tablespoons sugar, and limoncello in large bowl. Beat until soft peaks form. Combine all berries, raspberry preserves, lemon peel, and remaining 1/2 cup sugar in another large bowl; toss gently. Divide berry mixture among 8 coupes. Top with mascarpone cream. The ingredients of Mixed Berries with Mascarpone-Limoncello Cream are 3/4 cup chilled whipping cream, 1/2 8-ounce container mascarpone cheese, 3 tablespoons plus 1/2 cup sugar, 3 tablespoons limoncello (lemon liqueur), 8 cups assorted fresh berries (such as raspberries, blueberries, blackberries, and hulled sliced strawberries; about 2 pounds), 3/4 cup raspberry preserves, 1 tablespoon grated lemon peel.Limoncello can be found at most supermarkets and at liquor stores.

Orange-Poppy Seed Scones recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly flour heavy large baking sheet. Mix 2 1/2 cups flour, sugar, poppy seeds, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt in large bowl. Add butter and rub in with fingertips until mixture resembles coarse meal. Mix in 2 teaspoons grated orange peel. Whisk egg and 2 tablespoons orange juice in medium bowl to blend. Add to flour mixture; stir just until blended. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface; knead gently until smooth. Divide dough in half. Pat each half into 3/4-inch-thick round. Cut each round into 6 wedges. Arrange wedges on prepared baking sheet. Bake until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Serve warm. Orange-Poppy Seed Scones has 238.0 calories. Orange-Poppy Seed Scones has 4.0 grams of protein. Orange-Poppy Seed Scones has 13.0 grams of fat. Orange-Poppy Seed Scones has 136.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange-Poppy Seed Scones are 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour, 1/3 cup sugar, 3 tablespoons poppy seeds, 1 1/2 teaspoons cream of tartar, 3/4 teaspoon baking soda, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup (1 1/2 sticks) chilled unsalted butter, cut into small pieces, 2 teaspoons grated orange peel, 1 large egg, 2 tablespoons orange juice.

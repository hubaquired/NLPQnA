Rajas con Crema recipe.  Roast and peel chilies . Wearing rubber gloves, cut chilies into 1/3-inch-thick strips. Halve onion lengthwise and cut each half lengthwise into 1/4-inch-thick slices. In a heavy skillet cook onion in oil over moderately low heat, stirring frequently, until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chilies and salt to taste and cook, stirring, 5 minutes. Add créme fraîche or cream and cook, stirring, 2 minutes. Rajas may be made 1 day ahead and chilled, covered. Reheat rajas before serving. Rajas con Crema has 28.0 calories. Rajas con Crema has 1.0 grams of protein. Rajas con Crema has 2.0 grams of fat. Rajas con Crema has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rajas con Crema are 2 pounds fresh poblano chilies (about 8), 1 medium white onion (about 8 ounces), 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1/3 cup crème fraîche or heavy cream.Poblano Strips with Onions and Cream.

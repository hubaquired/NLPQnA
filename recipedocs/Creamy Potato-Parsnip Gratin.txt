Creamy Potato-Parsnip Gratin recipe. Preheat oven to 450°F. Butter 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Arrange 1/3 of potatoes in even layer in dish. Top with bay leaves, then 1/2 of parsnips. Top with 1/3 of potatoes, remaining parsnips, then remaining potatoes, sprinkling each layer with salt and pepper. Pour cream and half and half over. Cover dish with foil. Bake until vegetables are tender, about 1 hour. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Uncover and let stand at room temperature.) Bake gratin uncovered at 450°F until sauce bubbles thickly and top is golden brown, about 20 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes; serve. Creamy Potato-Parsnip Gratin has 692.0 calories. Creamy Potato-Parsnip Gratin has 10.0 grams of protein. Creamy Potato-Parsnip Gratin has 55.0 grams of fat. Creamy Potato-Parsnip Gratin has 82.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Potato-Parsnip Gratin are 2 1/3 pounds unpeeled Yukon Gold potatoes, scrubbed, cut into 1/8-inch-thick rounds, 4 Turkish bay leaves, 1 2/3 pounds large parsnips, peeled, cut into 1/8-inch-thick rounds, 2 cups whipping cream, 2 cups half and half.

Zingy Red Sauce recipe.  Purée 2 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup chopped roasted red peppers from a jar about 2), 1/2 cup unsalted, roasted almonds, and 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar in a food processor until smooth. With motor running, add 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil and process until smooth; season with kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper, and more vinegar, if desired. DO AHEAD: Sauce can be made 2 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Zingy Red Sauce are 2 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup chopped roasted red peppers from a jar (about 2), 1/2 cup unsalted, roasted almonds, 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, Kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper.

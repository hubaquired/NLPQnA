Cabbage and Bacon Mashed Potatoes recipe.  In a large skillet cook bacon over moderate heat until crisp and with a slotted spoon transfer to paper towels to drain. Add cabbage to bacon fat in skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until golden, about 5 minutes. Add water and simmer until evaporated and cabbage is tender. Add bacon and season with salt and pepper. Stir mixture into potatoes. The ingredients of Cabbage and Bacon Mashed Potatoes are 3 bacon slices, chopped, 1 1/4 pounds cabbage (about 1/2 small head), cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1/2 cup water, Basic Mashed Potatoes.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Turkey Sausage Patties recipe.  Makes 4 servings. Stir together all ingredients except oil in a large bowl until combined well. Form 1/4 cups of sausage mixture into patties 3 inches in diameter; about 8 total) using moistened hands and arrange on a wax-paper-lined tray. Lightly brush a 12-inch nonstick skillet with oil and heat over moderate heat until hot but not smoking. Cook patties in batches of 4, turning over once, until browned and cooked through, about 6 minutes per batch. Transfer to a plate as cooked and keep warm, covered with foil. The ingredients of Turkey Sausage Patties are 3/4 pound ground turkey (not labeled "all breast meat"), 1 medium Bosc pear (6 ounces), peeled, cored, and coarsely grated, 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh sage, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice, 1 large egg, lightly beaten, Vegetable oil for brushing skillet.These patties get their moistness and delicious flavor from a combination of light and dark meat.

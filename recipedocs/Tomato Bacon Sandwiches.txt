Tomato Bacon Sandwiches recipe. In a large heavy skillet cook the bacon over moderate heat, stirring, until it is crisp, transfer it to paper towels to drain, and chop it fine. In a bowl stir together the bacon, the mayonnaise, and the mustard. Spread the bacon mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice, sandwich the tomatoes between the slices, and cut each sandwich into 4 triangles. Tomato Bacon Sandwiches has 96.0 calories. Tomato Bacon Sandwiches has 2.0 grams of protein. Tomato Bacon Sandwiches has 6.0 grams of fat. Tomato Bacon Sandwiches has 141.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato Bacon Sandwiches are 8 slices of bacon, chopped, 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard, 30 slices of homemade-type white bread, crusts removed, 15 thick large tomato slices.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Spiced Mango Lassi recipe.  Combine diced mango, yogurt, 1 cup ice cubes, milk, honey, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and ground cardamom in blender. Puree until smooth. Pour smoothie into 2 glasses. Garnish each glass with mango wedge and serve. The ingredients of Spiced Mango Lassi are 1 1/2 cups diced peeled pitted mango (about 1 1/2 large), 1 cup plain nonfat yogurt, 1 cup ice cubes, 1/4 cup low-fat (1%) milk, 1 1/2 tablespoons honey, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/8 teaspoon ground cardamom, 2 mango wedges or slices (for garnish).This rendition of India's popular yogurt shake makes a cooling counterpoint to any spicy dish.

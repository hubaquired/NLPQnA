Spinach Salad with Pecorino, Pine Nuts, and Currants recipe. Whisk first 5 ingredients in small bowl. Whisk in oil. Season with salt and pepper. Toss spinach with vinaigrette in large bowl. And cheese and toss. The ingredients of Spinach Salad with Pecorino, Pine Nuts, and Currants are 1 1/2 tablespoons dried currants, 1 1/2 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted, coarsely chopped, 1 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 1/2 teaspoons minced shallot, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 6 cups (packed) baby spinach leaves, Shaved Pecorino Romano cheese.Be sure to buy four extra cups of spinach if you plan to make the frittata.

Arugula-Chicory Salad with Pine Nuts and Goat-Cheese Toasts recipe. Preheat broiler. Whisk together vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, and salt and pepper to taste and add oil in a slow stream, whisking. Toss arugula, chicory, pine nuts, and tomatoes in a large bowl with just enough vinaigrette to coat, then divide among 4 plates. Spread goat cheese on baguette slices and broil on a baking sheet 6 inches from heat until cheese is softened and edges of bread are golden, about 2 minutes. Arrange toasts on salads and drizzle any remaining vinaigrette over cheese. Arugula-Chicory Salad with Pine Nuts and Goat-Cheese Toasts has 336.0 calories. The ingredients of Arugula-Chicory Salad with Pine Nuts and Goat-Cheese Toasts are 1 teaspoon red-wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 pound arugula, tough stems discarded, 1/2 head chicory (white and pale-green parts only), torn into pieces (2 cups), 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted, 3/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved, 6 ounces soft mild goat cheese, 8 (1/3-inch-thick) diagonal baguette slices.The contrast of textures and flavors—crunchy pine nuts, creamy goat cheese, acidic tomatoes, and spicy arugula—adds complexity to this simple salad. It makes a great side dish to steak or chicken or a light lunch for two.

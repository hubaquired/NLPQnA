Veal, Mushroom and Red Wine Sauce recipe. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add veal, garlic and rosemary; sauté until veal is no longer pink, breaking up large clumps with back of spoon, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms; sauté 5 minutes. Add tomatoes, wine, broth and tomato paste. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium; simmer until mixture thickens to sauce consistency, stirring occasionally, about 30 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Veal, Mushroom and Red Wine Sauce has 388.0 calories. Veal, Mushroom and Red Wine Sauce has 25.0 grams of protein.

Chili-Lime Sauce recipe. Whisk lime juice, 1/4 cup water, carrot, fish sauce, sugar and chili-garlic sauce in medium bowl to blend. *Available at Asian markets and in the Asian foods section of some supermarkets. Chili-Lime Sauce has 75.0 calories. Chili-Lime Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Chili-Lime Sauce has 1425.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chili-Lime Sauce are 5 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup water, 1/4 cup finely grated carrot, 2 tablespoons fish sauce (nam pla)*, 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 teaspoons chili-garlic sauce*.

Red Sangria recipe.  Combine citrus slices and other firm fruits in a large, nonreactive container. Add wine, liqueur, brandy, and lemon verbena. Cover and refrigerate overnight or up to 3 days). When ready to serve, put some soft fruits into each wine glass and fill two-thirds full with the Sangria. Top off with a splash of club soda. Red Sangria has 598.0 calories. Red Sangria has 2.0 grams of protein. Red Sangria has 1.0 grams of fat. Red Sangria has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red Sangria are 2 oranges, thinly sliced, 1 lemon, thinly sliced, 1 lime, thinly sliced, 4 cups fresh firm fruits, prepared and sliced as appropriate, 1 gallon full-bodied dry red wine, such as Cabernet Sauvignon, 1 1/2 to 2 cups orange liqueur, such as Cointreau or Triple Sec, 1 1/2 cups Presidente brandy, 2 bunches lemon verbena, washed and stemmed (about 2 cups), Fresh soft fruits, prepared and sliced as appropriate, Club soda, champagne, or sparkling wine.Editor's note: The recipe and introductory text below are from Fonda San Miguel: Thirty Years of Food and Art, by Tom Gilliland, Miguel Ravago, and Virginia B.

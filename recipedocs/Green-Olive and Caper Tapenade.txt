Green-Olive and Caper Tapenade recipe. In a food processor combine capers, olives, anchovies, and garlic. With motor running, add oil in a stream and puree until mixture forms a coarse paste. Tapenade keeps, covered and chilled, 1 week.) Green-Olive and Caper Tapenade has 841.0 calories. Green-Olive and Caper Tapenade has 4.0 grams of protein. Green-Olive and Caper Tapenade has 92.0 grams of fat. Green-Olive and Caper Tapenade has 1973.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green-Olive and Caper Tapenade are 5 tablespoons drained capers, 1/2 cup pimiento-stuffed green olives (a 3-ounce jar), 2 flat anchovy fillets, 4 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil.Serve this tapenade on bruschetta or as a sandwich spread.

Eggplant and Tomato Pizza recipe. Preheat oven to 450°F. Lightly oil 9x9x2-inch baking pan. Heat oil in heavy medium skillet over medium-high heat. Add eggplant and onion and sauté until vegetables soften and brown lightly, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Unroll pizza dough. Fit into prepared pan, pressing up at edges to create 1-inch-high rim. Spread eggplant mixture evenly over bottom of crust. Arrange tomatoes over. Sprinkle with cheese and marjoram. Bake until topping is heated through and edges of pizza are brown and crisp, about 15 minutes. Cut into squares. Eggplant and Tomato Pizza has 687.0 calories. Eggplant and Tomato Pizza has 24.0 grams of protein. Eggplant and Tomato Pizza has 25.0 grams of fat. Eggplant and Tomato Pizza has 1174.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Eggplant and Tomato Pizza are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 cups 1/2-inch cubes Japanese eggplant (about 8 ounces), 1 medium onion, thinly sliced, 1 10-ounce tube refrigerated pizza dough, 8 ounces plum tomatoes, thinly sliced, 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, 3/4 teaspoon dried marjoram.

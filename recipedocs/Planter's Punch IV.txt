Planter's Punch IV recipe. In a cocktail shaker half-filled with ice cubes, combine rum, lime juice, Triple Sec, grenadine, bitters and orange juice. Shake the drink for 30 seconds, and strain it into 4 tall glasses filled with ice cubes. Garnish drinks with the skewers and the lime slices. Planter's Punch IV has 184.0 calories. Planter's Punch IV has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Planter's Punch IV are 2/3 cup dark rum, 1/4 cup fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup Triple Sec, 1 tablespoon grenadine, 1/2 teaspoon Angostura bitters, 1/2 cup fresh orange juice, Garnish: 4 bamboo skewers, each threaded with a lime wedge, an orange wedge and a maraschino cherry; lime slices.

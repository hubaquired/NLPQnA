Damn the Weather II recipe. In a large pitcher stir together well the port and the juices. Divide the drink among 6 glasses filled with ice cubes. Top off the drinks with the soda and garnish them with the lemon slices. The ingredients of Damn the Weather II are 2 cups Ruby Port, 1 cup unsweetened pineapple juice, 1 cup fresh orange juice, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, Chilled club soda or seltzer water, 6 lemon slices for garnish.

Romaine with Oranges and Black Olives recipe.  In a bowl stir together the orange sections, the onion, the olives, the oil, the vinegar, the cumin, and salt and pepper to taste, add the romaine, and toss the salad well. Romaine with Oranges and Black Olives has 115.0 calories. Romaine with Oranges and Black Olives has 1.0 grams of protein. Romaine with Oranges and Black Olives has 11.0 grams of fat. Romaine with Oranges and Black Olives has 74.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Romaine with Oranges and Black Olives are 1 navel orange, rind and pith cut away with a serrated knife and the ;flesh sectioned, 1/4 red onion, sliced thin, 6 oil-cured black olives, pitted and cut into slivers, 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil (preferably extra-virgin), 1 teaspoon Sherry vinegar or cider vinegar, a pinch of ground cumin, 2 cups shredded romaine, washed well and spun dry.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

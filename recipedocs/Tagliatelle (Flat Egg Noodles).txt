Tagliatelle (Flat Egg Noodles) recipe.  Make pasta all'uovo . When the dough has rested, roll it out with a wooden rolling pin into a thin sheet less than 1/16 inch 1 millimeter) thick. If you use a pasta machine to roll the dough, finish at the smallest setting. If this is too thin for you to handle, stop at the next-to-last setting instead. Nobody will care. Spread the pasta sheet out on the board and roll it loosely like a jellyroll. With a large, sharp knife, cut the roll at less than 1/4-inch 5-millimeter) intervals. You can also use a pasta machine to cut the noodles. In that case, trim the sheet into regular lengths up to 24 inches 60 centimeters). Feed the sheets through the cutting attachment.

Chicken Clemenceau recipe. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Melt butter in a large frying pan over medium-high heat; add chicken and stir. Cook for 5 minutes. Add wine, garlic, and potatoes. Cover and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until potatoes are soft and wine has cooked out. Remove cover and add mushrooms. Stir to combine. Continue cooking until mushrooms are soft. Add peas, parsley, and paprika; stir to combine. Cook until well-blended, being careful not to mash the vegetables. Season to taste with white pepper. Chicken Clemenceau has 383.0 calories. Chicken Clemenceau has 33.0 grams of protein. Chicken Clemenceau has 12.0 grams of fat. Chicken Clemenceau has 1738.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chicken Clemenceau are 3 6-ounce boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes, Salt and pepper, Salt and pepper, 3 tablespoons butter, 1/2 cup white wine, 1 tablespoon chopped garlic, 3 medium white potatoes, cut into a 1/2-inch dice (about 2 cups), 1 cup sliced mushrooms, 1/2 cup cooked green peas, 1 teaspoon chopped parsley, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, White pepper.

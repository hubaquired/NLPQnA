Citrus and Pineapple Compote recipe.  In a bowl combine the grapefruit sections, the orange slices, and the pineapple chunks. In a small saucepan combine the reserved juices, the Sherry, the honey, and a pinch of salt and simmer the mixture, stirring, for 1 minute. Pour the mixture over the fruit and toss the compote gently. Citrus and Pineapple Compote has 132.0 calories. Citrus and Pineapple Compote has 1.0 grams of protein. Citrus and Pineapple Compote has 42.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Citrus and Pineapple Compote are 1 pink grapefruit, the rind and the pith cut away with a serrated knife, the grapefruit cut into sections, and the juice reserved, 1 navel orange, the rind and the pith cut away with a serrated knife, the orange sliced crosswise, and the juice reserved, 1/2 pineapple, peeled, cored, cut into 1-inch chunks, and the juice reserved, 3 tablespoons medium-dry sherry, 2 tablespoons honey.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

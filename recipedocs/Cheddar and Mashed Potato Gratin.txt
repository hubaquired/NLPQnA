Cheddar and Mashed Potato Gratin recipe. Preheat oven to 400°F. Cook bacon and shallots in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat until bacon is crisp, stirring frequently, about 10 minutes. Stir in mashed potatoes and herbs. Mix in 1/4 cup milk, adding more milk if mixture is dry. Season potato mixture with salt and pepper. Transfer to 2-quart gratin dish. Sprinkle cheese over and bake until cheese is beginning to brown, about 30 minutes. Cheddar and Mashed Potato Gratin has 345.0 calories. Cheddar and Mashed Potato Gratin has 11.0 grams of protein. Cheddar and Mashed Potato Gratin has 20.0 grams of fat. Cheddar and Mashed Potato Gratin has 743.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cheddar and Mashed Potato Gratin are 4 bacon slices, chopped, 1 cup sliced shallots, 6 cups leftover mashed potatoes, 1 tablespoon each chopped fresh parsley, thyme, and chives, 1/4 cup (or more) whole milk, 1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese.

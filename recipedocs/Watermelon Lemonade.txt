Watermelon Lemonade recipe. Working in 2 batches, blend watermelon and lemon juice in processor or blender until smooth. Transfer to pitcher. Bring 3 1/2 cups water and sugar to boil in medium saucepan over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Add hot syrup to pitcher with watermelon-lemon juice. Refrigerate until cold, about 2 hours. Fill tall glasses with ice. Pour Watermelon Lemonade over and garnish glasses with lemon wedges. Watermelon Lemonade has 636.0 calories. Watermelon Lemonade has 11.0 grams of protein. Watermelon Lemonade has 3.0 grams of fat. Watermelon Lemonade has 22.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watermelon Lemonade are 8 cups 1-inch pieces seedless watermelon (from about 4 1/2 pounds), 1 cup fresh lemon juice, 3 1/2 cups water, 1 cup sugar, Ice cubes, Lemon wedges.For an extra kick, add a dash of vodka to this refreshing drink.

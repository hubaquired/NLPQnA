Peanut Punch recipe.  Mix cornstarch and water in a small saucepan, add milk, peanut butter, and sugar to taste. Cook over moderate heat, stirring with a whisk until thoroughly smooth and thick. Cool and refrigerate before serving. Serve in tumblers or goblets with or without ice. Peanut Punch has 490.0 calories. Peanut Punch has 18.0 grams of protein. Peanut Punch has 33.0 grams of fat. Peanut Punch has 116.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peanut Punch are 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1/2 cup water, 2 cups milk, 6 tablespoons peanut butter, Sugar to taste.

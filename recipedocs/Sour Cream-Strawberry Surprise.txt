Sour Cream-Strawberry Surprise recipe.  Mix strawberries into sour cream. Add sugar to taste. Stir until light pink. Refrigerate about half an hour. The ingredients of Sour Cream-Strawberry Surprise are 2 pints strawberries, hulled and halved, 16 oz lowfat sour cream, Raw sugar.Actress Marissa Matrone shares a recipe her grandmother taught her.

Butter Pie Crust Dough recipe.  Blend flour, sugar, and salt in processor. Add butter and cut in, using on/off turns, until coarse meal forms. Add 3 tablespoons water. Using on/off turns, blend just until moist clumps form, adding more water by 1/2 tablespoonfuls if dough is dry. Gather dough into ball; flatten into disk. Wrap in plastic; refrigerate 1 hour. Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep chilled. Soften slightly at room temperature before rolling.) Butter Pie Crust Dough has 1435.0 calories. Butter Pie Crust Dough has 19.0 grams of protein. Butter Pie Crust Dough has 95.0 grams of fat. Butter Pie Crust Dough has 1227.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Butter Pie Crust Dough are 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour, 1/2 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup (1 stick) chilled unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch cubes, 3 tablespoons (or more) ice water.For a double-crust pie, double the ingredients, divide the dough in half, and form two disks.

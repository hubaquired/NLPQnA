Simple Homemade Beef Stock recipe. Heat oil in large pot over high heat. Sprinkle bones with salt and pepper. Add bones and onion to pot. Sauté until bones and onion are deep brown, turning often, about 20 minutes. Add 4 quarts water and bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer uncovered until stock is reduced to 3 to 4 cups, about 3 1/2 hours. Strain, discarding bones and onion. DO AHEAD: Can be made 3 days ahead. Refrigerate uncovered until cold, then cover and keep chilled. Spoon off and discard all fat before using. Simple Homemade Beef Stock has 27.0 calories. Simple Homemade Beef Stock has 2.0 grams of protein. Simple Homemade Beef Stock has 2.0 grams of fat. Simple Homemade Beef Stock has 59.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Simple Homemade Beef Stock are 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 4 pounds meaty beef bones (such as shank or neck bones), 1 large onion, sliced, 4 quarts water.An easy version of the classic.

Raita Refresher recipe. Combine ingredients; cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour but no longer than 3. Stir well before serving. The ingredients of Raita Refresher are 1 cup plain nonfat yogurt, 1/4 cup diced (1/4 inch) hothouse (seedless) cucumber, 1 tablespoon orange zest, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 1 scallion, sliced thinly, 1 teaspoon snipped chives, Black pepper, to taste.This recipe is an accompaniment for Summer Vegetable Curry.

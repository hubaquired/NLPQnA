Manhattan II recipe.  In a small cocktail pitcher combine the bourbon, the vermouth, the bitters, and 1 cup ice cubes, stir the mixture well, and strain it into an Old-fashioned glass. Garnish with the lemon twist. Manhattan II has 121.0 calories. Manhattan II has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Manhattan II are 1 1/2 ounces (1 jigger) bourbon or other whiskey, 1 tablespoon sweet vermouth, a dash of Angostura bitters, 1 lemon twist for garnish, Ice cubes.

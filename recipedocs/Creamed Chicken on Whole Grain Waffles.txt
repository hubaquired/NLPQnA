Creamed Chicken on Whole Grain Waffles recipe. Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in heavy medium skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and sauté until just cooked through, about 3 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer chicken to plate. Melt remaining 1 tablespoon butter in same skillet over medium-high heat. Add flour and whisk 1 minute. Gradually whisk in broth, cream and tarragon. Bring to boil, whisking constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer sauce 1 minute. Add Madeira and simmer until sauce thickens, about 2 minutes. Return chicken and any juices to sauce. Simmer until chicken is heated through, about 1 minute. Arrange 2 waffles on each plate; top with creamed chicken. Creamed Chicken on Whole Grain Waffles has 950.0 calories. Creamed Chicken on Whole Grain Waffles has 27.0 grams of protein. Creamed Chicken on Whole Grain Waffles has 70.0 grams of fat. Creamed Chicken on Whole Grain Waffles has 886.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamed Chicken on Whole Grain Waffles are 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1/2-inch cubes, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1 1/2 tablespoons all purpose flour, 1 cup canned low-salt chicken broth, 1/3 cup whipping cream, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon or 1 teaspoon dried, 1 tablespoon Madeira or dry Sherry, 4 frozen whole grain waffles, toasted.Serve the waffles with sautéed green beans and mushrooms, and end the meal with fresh pineapple and giant gingersnaps.

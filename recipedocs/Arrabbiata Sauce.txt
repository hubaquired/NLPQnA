Arrabbiata Sauce recipe.  Simmer tomatoes, chile, and oil in a 4-quart heavy saucepan, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened, about 1 1/2 hours. During last 10 minutes of cooking, add garlic and stir sauce frequently to prevent scorching. Remove from heat, then stir in basil and salt and pepper to taste.

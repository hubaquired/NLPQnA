The ingredients of Roasted-Pepper and Almond Mayonnaise are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup drained bottled roasted red peppers, chopped, 1/4 cup salted roasted almonds, 1 garlic clove, smashed, 2 teaspoons Sherry vinegar, or to taste, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne.This romesco-style sauce is good on grilled fish or vegetables as well as burgers.

Candied Kumquats recipe.  Bring water and sugar to boil in heavy small saucepan, stirring until sugar dissolves. Add kumquat slices. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until kumquats become translucent and tender, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes. Cool. Do ahead Candied kumquats can be made 3 days ahead. Cover and chill. Candied Kumquats has 234.0 calories. Candied Kumquats has 1.0 grams of protein. Candied Kumquats has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Candied Kumquats are 1 cup water, 1/2 cup sugar, 4 ounces kumquats (about 14), each cut crosswise into 4 slices, seeded.These are also great tossed into a salad of bitter greens, over a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or as a garnish for crème caramel or panna cotta.

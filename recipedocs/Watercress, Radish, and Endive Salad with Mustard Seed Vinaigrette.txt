Watercress, Radish, and Endive Salad with Mustard Seed Vinaigrette recipe. Stir mustard seeds in small dry skillet over medium heat until seeds begin to pop, about 3 minutes. Transfer to small bowl; cool. Add vinegar, lemon juice, and mustard to mustard seeds. Gradually whisk in oil. Season dressing with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Watercress, Radish, and Endive Salad with Mustard Seed Vinaigrette are 1 1/2 teaspoons mustard seeds, 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1/3 cup olive oil, 2 bunches watercress, thick stems trimmed (about 8 cups), 2 small heads of Belgian endive, cut lengthwise into 1/4-inch-wide strips, 2 bunches radishes, trimmed, sliced.

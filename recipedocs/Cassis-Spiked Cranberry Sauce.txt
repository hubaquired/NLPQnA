Cassis-Spiked Cranberry Sauce recipe.  Bring 1 cup water, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup crème de cassis to boil in heavy medium saucepan over high heat, stirring to dissolve sugar. Add fresh cranberries; return to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer until cranberries pop and sauce thickens, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in dried cranberries, ketchup, and 2 tablespoons crème de cassis. Chill until cold, at least 4 hours and up to 3 days. Cassis-Spiked Cranberry Sauce has 214.0 calories. Cassis-Spiked Cranberry Sauce has 31.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cassis-Spiked Cranberry Sauce are 1 cup water, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons crème decassis (black-currant-flavored liqueur), 1 12-ounce bag fresh cranberries, 1 cup dried cranberries, 1 tablespoon ketchup.Surprise — a bit of ketchup helps the sweet and tart flavors blend.

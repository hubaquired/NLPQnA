Parsley-Almond Salsa Verde recipe.  Mix garlic, parsley, chives, oil, and almonds in a small bowl; season with salt and pepper. DO AHEAD: Salsa verde can be made 2 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Parsley-Almond Salsa Verde are 2 garlic cloves, grated, 1 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons chopped almonds, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper.

Easy Garlic and Onion Bread Wedges recipe.  Preheat oven to 450°F. Brush pizza crust lightly with olive oil. Combine remaining ingredients in small bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spread mayonnaise mixture evenly over crust. Bake until crust is crisp and topping heated through, about 10 minutes. Easy Garlic and Onion Bread Wedges has 317.0 calories. Easy Garlic and Onion Bread Wedges has 8.0 grams of protein. Easy Garlic and Onion Bread Wedges has 18.0 grams of fat. Easy Garlic and Onion Bread Wedges has 529.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Easy Garlic and Onion Bread Wedges are 1 16-ounce baked cheese pizza crust (such as Boboli), Olive oil, 1/2 cup regular or reduced-calorie mayonnaise, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup chopped red onion, 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil or 1 tablespoon dried, crumbled, 4 large garlic cloves, minced.

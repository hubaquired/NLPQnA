Zucchini in Mint Vinaigrette recipe. In a small bowl whisk together the sugar, the salt, the lemon juice, and the oil. Transfer the vinaigrette to a skillet, bring it to a boil, and add the zucchini, stirring to coat it with the dressing. Simmer the zucchini for 3 minutes, or until it is crisp-tender, stir in the mint, and let the zucchini cool in the dressing. Serve the zucchini at room temperature. Zucchini in Mint Vinaigrette has 257.0 calories. Zucchini in Mint Vinaigrette has 28.0 grams of fat. Zucchini in Mint Vinaigrette has 313.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Zucchini in Mint Vinaigrette are 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 2 zucchini, each cut into 1/4-inch slices, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches recipe. Stir 1/4 cup hot pepper sauce, butter and dressing mix in heavy medium saucepan over low heat until sauce is smooth; add more hot sauce, if desired. Arrange roll bottoms on plates. Top each with lettuce leaf and 2 tablespoons sauce. Sprinkle chicken with salt; dust lightly with flour, then brush generously with remaining sauce. Dust lightly with flour again. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken; sauté until brown and cooked through, about 5 minutes per side. Complete sandwiches with chicken, blue cheese dressing and roll tops. The ingredients of Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches are 1/4 to 1/3 cup hot pepper sauce, 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) butter, 1 0.6-ounce envelope dry Italian salad dressing mix, 4 kaiser rolls, halved, 4 lettuce leaves, 4 boneless chicken breast halves with skin, All purpose flour, Additional vegetable oil, 2 tablespoons olive oil, Purchased blue cheese dressing.The best flavors of Buffalo wings are in these spicy sandwiches.

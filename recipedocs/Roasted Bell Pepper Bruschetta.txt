Roasted Bell Pepper Bruschetta recipe. Combine first 3 ingredients in medium bowl. Add 1 teaspoon oil. Grill or toast bread. Brush with remaining 2 teaspoons oil. Season generously with pepper. Top with bell pepper mixture. Cut each into 4 pieces and serve. The ingredients of Roasted Bell Pepper Bruschetta are 1/2 cup drained roasted red bell peppers (in jar), rinsed, drained, patted dry, thinly sliced, 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar or red wine vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram, crumbled, 3 teaspoons olive oil, 2 4x6-inch sourdough or French bread slices.Tidbits to serve with before-dinner drinks.

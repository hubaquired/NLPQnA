Sun-Dried Tomato Vinaigrette recipe. In a saucepan simmer the sun-dried tomatoes in 2 inches water for 3 minutes, or until they are tender, drain them, and mince them. In a bowl whisk together the tomatoes, the vinegars, and the garlic paste, add the oil in a stream, whisking, and whisk the vinaigrette until it is emulsified. Stir in the basil. The ingredients of Sun-Dried Tomato Vinaigrette are 2 sun-dried tomato halves, 1 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 1/2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 1/2 garlic clove, minced and mashed to a paste with 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/3 cup olive oil, 1 tablespoon minced fresh <a href="">basil</a> leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Rice with Dill, Walnuts, and Golden Raisins recipe.  In a saucepan with a tight-fitting lid bring water to a boil with salt. Stir in rice and cook, covered, over low heat 20 minutes, or until water is absorbed and rice is tender. While rice is cooking, lightly toast walnuts and chop coarse. Chop dill. Fluff rice with a fork and stir in walnuts, dill, raisins, zest, vinegar, oil, and salt and pepper to taste. Rice with Dill, Walnuts, and Golden Raisins has 363.0 calories. Rice with Dill, Walnuts, and Golden Raisins has 6.0 grams of protein. Rice with Dill, Walnuts, and Golden Raisins has 6.0 grams of fat. Rice with Dill, Walnuts, and Golden Raisins has 304.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rice with Dill, Walnuts, and Golden Raisins are 1 1/2 cups water, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup long-grain rice, 1/4 cup walnuts, 1/4 cup fresh dill leaves, 1/4 cup golden raisins, 1/2 teaspoon finely grated fresh lemon zest, 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar, 1/2 tablespoon olive oil.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

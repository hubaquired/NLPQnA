Mexican Chicken Sliders recipe.  In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients, except the oil, and mix to thoroughly combine. Divide the mixture into 8 equal portions and form into patties. You will use about 1/4 cup of the mixture per patty. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook for 3 minutes on each side, or until chicken is cooked through. Serve with your favorite burger accompaniments. Mexican Chicken Sliders has 248.0 calories. Mexican Chicken Sliders has 25.0 grams of protein. Mexican Chicken Sliders has 15.0 grams of fat. Mexican Chicken Sliders has 672.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mexican Chicken Sliders are 1 pound ground chicken breast, 1 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, 1 teaspoon cumin, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon onion powder, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 large egg, 1 tablespoon oil.

Strawberry Kir Royale recipe. Mix strawberries and sugar in medium bowl. Let stand 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Divide between 2 large wine-glasses. Pour 1/4 cup champagne and 1/2 tablespoon cassis into each glass. The ingredients of Strawberry Kir Royale are 2 cups halved strawberries, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 cup chilled champagne or sparkling wine, 1 tablespoon crème de cassis.

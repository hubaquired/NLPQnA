Sauté of Spring Peas with Tarragon recipe.  Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add peas and snow peas. Stir to coat. Cook until just tender, stirring occasionally, about 3 minutes. Stir in tarragon. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately. Sauté of Spring Peas with Tarragon has 122.0 calories. Sauté of Spring Peas with Tarragon has 6.0 grams of protein. Sauté of Spring Peas with Tarragon has 5.0 grams of fat. Sauté of Spring Peas with Tarragon has 284.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sauté of Spring Peas with Tarragon are 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 1/2 pounds fresh peas, shelled or 1 3/4 cups frozen, thawed, 8 ounces snow peas, trimmed, 3 tablespoons fresh whole tarragon leaves, Salt and freshly ground pepper.For a pretty decorative touch, snip a V at one end of each snow pea.

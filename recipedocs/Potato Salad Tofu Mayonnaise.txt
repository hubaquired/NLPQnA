Potato Salad Tofu Mayonnaise recipe. In a large saucepan combine the potatoes with enough water to cover them by 1 inch, bring the water to a boil, and boil the potatoes for 15 to 20 minutes, or until they are tender. While the potatoes are cooking, in a blender blend the tofu, the lemon juice, the tarragon, and 2 tablespoons water until the mixture is just combined, with the motor running add the oil in a stream and blend the "mayonnaise" until it is emulsified. Drain the potatoes, quarter them, and in a bowl toss them with the onion, the parsley, the mayonnaise, and salt and pepper to taste. Potato Salad Tofu Mayonnaise has 315.0 calories. Potato Salad Tofu Mayonnaise has 8.0 grams of protein. Potato Salad Tofu Mayonnaise has 15.0 grams of fat. Potato Salad Tofu Mayonnaise has 49.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Potato Salad Tofu Mayonnaise are 1 pound small red potatoes (about 7), scrubbed, 3 ounces soft tofu, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1/8 teaspoon dried tarragon, 2 tablespoons olive oil (preferably extra-virgin), 1/2 small red onion, sliced thin, 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Cabbage Salad with Mustard Vinaigrette recipe. Cut cabbage into thin shreds and chop parsley. In a small bowl whisk together mustard, vinegar, and salt until combined. Whisk in oil in a slow stream, whisking until emulsified. In a large bowl toss together cabbage, parsley, vinaigrette, and salt and pepper to taste until combined. Chill salad, covered, at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours. Cabbage Salad with Mustard Vinaigrette has 167.0 calories. Cabbage Salad with Mustard Vinaigrette has 4.0 grams of protein. Cabbage Salad with Mustard Vinaigrette has 12.0 grams of fat. Cabbage Salad with Mustard Vinaigrette has 375.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cabbage Salad with Mustard Vinaigrette are a 1 1/2-pound piece white or green cabbage, a 1/2-pound piece red cabbage, 1/21/3 cup packed fresh flat-leafed parsley leaves, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 2 1/2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.

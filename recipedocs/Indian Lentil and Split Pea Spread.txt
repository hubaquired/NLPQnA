Indian Lentil and Split Pea Spread recipe.  Cook lentils and split peas in large pot of boiling salted water until very tender, about 35 minutes. Drain. Heat 1/3 cup oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add turmeric and cumin and stir 1 minutes. Transfer mixture to processor. Add lentils and split peas, cilantro, lemon juice, chili powder and remaining 3 tablespoons oil. Process until smooth. Season generously with salt and pepper. Transfer to medium bowl. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Bring to room temperature before serving.) Serve with pita bread. Indian Lentil and Split Pea Spread has 262.0 calories. Indian Lentil and Split Pea Spread has 10.0 grams of protein. Indian Lentil and Split Pea Spread has 12.0 grams of fat. Indian Lentil and Split Pea Spread has 34.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Indian Lentil and Split Pea Spread are 1 cup dried lentils, picked over, 1 cup split peas, picked over, 1/3 cup plus 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1 cup chopped red onion, 2 tablespoons chopped garlic, 2 teaspoons turmeric, 2 teaspoons ground cumin, 1 cup (packed) fresh cilanto leaves, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon chili powder, Pita bread, cut into triangles.

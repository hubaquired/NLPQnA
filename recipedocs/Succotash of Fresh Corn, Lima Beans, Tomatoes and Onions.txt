Succotash of Fresh Corn, Lima Beans, Tomatoes and Onions recipe. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and sprinkle with coarse salt. Sauté until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic; stir until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes, corn, and lima beans. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until corn and lima beans are tender and tomatoes are soft, about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season to taste with salt and pepper. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill. Rewarm before continuing. Stir in basil and serve. The ingredients of Succotash of Fresh Corn, Lima Beans, Tomatoes and Onions are 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 1/2 cups chopped onion, Coarse kosher salt, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 3 cups chopped red tomatoes (about 1 1/2 pounds), 2 1/4 cups corn kernels cut from 4 ears of corn (preferably 2 ears of white corn and 2 ears of yellow corn), 2 cups fresh lima beans (from about 2 pounds pods) or 10 to 11 ounces frozen lima beans or baby butter beans, thawed, 3 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil.In this side dish, the veggies are cooked until just tender to retain their fresh-from-the-market flavor. For a delicious finishing touch, a handful of sliced basil is stirred in right before serving.

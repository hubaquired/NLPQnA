Roasted Bell Pepper Salad with Pine Nuts recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Place all bell peppers in large bowl. Pour 6 tablespoons oil over peppers; toss to coat. Place peppers, cut side down, on 2 large rimmed baking sheets. Sprinkle with salt. Roast until peppers are soft and slightly blackened, about 1 hour, reversing sheets after 30 minutes. Cool slightly; peel, if desired. Cut peppers into 1/4-inch-wide strips. Transfer to large bowl; add accumulated juices from baking sheets. Add 3 tablespoons vinegar and remaining 2 tablespoons oil; toss gently. Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Can be prepared 1 day ahead.) Drain peppers; discard juices. Transfer peppers to platter. Season with salt, pepper and more vinegar, if desired. Toss gently; sprinkle with pine nuts. Roasted Bell Pepper Salad with Pine Nuts has 96.0 calories. Roasted Bell Pepper Salad with Pine Nuts has 2.0 grams of protein. Roasted Bell Pepper Salad with Pine Nuts has 7.0 grams of fat. Roasted Bell Pepper Salad with Pine Nuts has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Bell Pepper Salad with Pine Nuts are 6 large red bell peppers, halved, seeded, 6 large yellow bell peppers, halved, seeded, 6 large orange bell peppers, halved, seeded, 8 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoons (or more) balsamic vinegar, 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted.

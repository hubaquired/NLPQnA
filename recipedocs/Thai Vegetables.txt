Thai Vegetables recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over high heat. Add ginger and garlic; sauté 20 seconds. Add bell pepper and mushrooms; sauté until pepper is crisp-tender, about 3 minutes. Add green onions and bok choy and sauté until just wilted, about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Thai Vegetables has 67.0 calories. Thai Vegetables has 2.0 grams of protein. Thai Vegetables has 4.0 grams of fat. Thai Vegetables has 66.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Thai Vegetables are 1 tablespoon oriental sesame oil, 2 teaspoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 1 red bell pepper, cut lengthwise into thin strips, 6 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stemmed, thinly sliced, 3 large green onions, thinly sliced on sharp diagonal, 3 cups thinly sliced bok choy (from 1 bunch; green leaf tops only).

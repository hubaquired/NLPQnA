Classic Tomato Toast with Mayonnaise and Chives recipe. Combine mayonnaise and 2 Tbsp. chives in a small bowl. Spread each toast with 1 heaping Tbsp. chive-mayonnaise. Divide tomato slices among toasts and sprinkle each with salt, pepper, and sugar, if using. Top with remaining 2 Tbsp. chives. Classic Tomato Toast with Mayonnaise and Chives has 194.0 calories. Classic Tomato Toast with Mayonnaise and Chives has 4.0 grams of protein. Classic Tomato Toast with Mayonnaise and Chives has 12.0 grams of fat. Classic Tomato Toast with Mayonnaise and Chives has 713.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Classic Tomato Toast with Mayonnaise and Chives are 6 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh chives, divided, 6 slices toasted white bread (such as Wonder Bread or Martin's potato bread), 1 pound very ripe multicolored tomatoes, sliced 1/4" thick, 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt, 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon sugar (optional).Let the flavors of perfect summer tomatoes shine on this simple-is-best toast.

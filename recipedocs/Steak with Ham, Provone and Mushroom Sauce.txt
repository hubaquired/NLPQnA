Steak with Ham, Provone and Mushroom Sauce recipe.  Heat 2 tablespoons oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms, shallot and garlic and sauté until mushrooms are brown and tender, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer mixture to small bowl. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil in same skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle steaks with salt and pepper. Add steaks to skillet and sauté just until brown, about 1 1/2 minutes per side. Remove skillet from heat. Top each steak with prosciutto slice, 1 tablespoon mushroom mixture, then cheese slice and parsley sprig. Cover; cook over medium-high heat until cheese melts, about 1 minute. Transfer steaks to plates; tent with foil. Return remaining mushroom mixture to skillet. Add Marsala and boil until reduced almost to glaze, about 6 minutes. Spoon sauce over steaks. Steak with Ham, Provone and Mushroom Sauce has 8414.0 calories. The ingredients of Steak with Ham, Provone and Mushroom Sauce are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 pound mushrooms, coarsely chopped, 1 large shallot, chopped, 1 large garlic clove, chopped, 4 1/2-inch-thick beef tenderloin steaks, 4 thin prosciutto or ham slices, trimmed to fit steaks, 4 thin slices provolone cheese, trimmed to fit steaks, 4 small Italian parsley sprigs, 1/2 cup dry Marsala.

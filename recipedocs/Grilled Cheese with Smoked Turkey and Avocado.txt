Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado recipe.  Thinly slice mozzarella. Pit, peel, and thinly slice avocado. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice and turn slices over. Spread mustard on bread slices and top 2 slices with mozzarella, avocado, and turkey. Season turkey with salt and pepper and top with remaining 2 bread slices, buttered sides up. Heat a heavy skillet over moderate heat until hot but not smoking and cook sandwiches until bread is crisp and cheese is melted, about 1 1/2 minutes on each side. Serve sandwiches with cucumber salad. Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado has 596.0 calories. Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado has 35.0 grams of protein. Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado has 35.0 grams of fat. Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado has 831.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado are 3 ounces whole-milk mozzarella, 1/2 firm-ripe California avocado, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened, 4 slices firm pumpernickel, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 6 ounces thinly sliced smoked turkey, Accompaniment:Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

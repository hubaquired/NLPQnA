Red Snapper with Leeks and Tomatoes recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Sprinkle fish on both sides with salt and pepper; place in 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add leeks and garlic and sauté until leeks are tender and beginning to brown, about 6 minutes. Add tomatoes and tarragon to skillet; stir until tomatoes soften, about 2 minutes. Mix in vinegar; season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon leek mixture over fish fillets. Bake until fish fillets are just opaque in center, about 18 minutes. Red Snapper with Leeks and Tomatoes has 256.0 calories. Red Snapper with Leeks and Tomatoes has 33.0 grams of protein. Red Snapper with Leeks and Tomatoes has 9.0 grams of fat. Red Snapper with Leeks and Tomatoes has 111.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red Snapper with Leeks and Tomatoes are 4 5- to 6-ounce red snapper fillets, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 3 medium leeks (white and pale green parts only), thinly sliced, 2 large garlic cloves, chopped, 3/4 pound plum tomatoes, chopped, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon or 1 teaspoon dried, 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar.Accompany the fish with mashed potatoes, rice pilaf or buttered noodles.

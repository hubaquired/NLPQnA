Lemon Pepper Dressing recipe. Whisk together zest, juice, mustard, pepper, sugar, and salt until sugar and salt are dissolved. Add oil in a slow stream, whisking until emulsified. Lemon Pepper Dressing has 184.0 calories. Lemon Pepper Dressing has 20.0 grams of fat. Lemon Pepper Dressing has 137.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon Pepper Dressing are 1/4 teaspoon finely grated fresh lemon zest, or to taste, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 teaspoons ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon sugar, Scant 1/2 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup olive oil.

Banana-Orange Smoothie recipe.  Combine 1 container yogurt, 1 banana, 1 orange, 8 cherries and 6 strawberries in blender. Blend on medium speed until smooth. Divide between 2 glasses. Repeat with remaining ingredients. The ingredients of Banana-Orange Smoothie are 2 8-ounce containers low-fat cherry yogurt, 2 bananas, peeled, cut into pieces, 2 oranges, peeled, white pith removed, cut into segments, 16 frozen dark cherries, 12 frozen strawberries.

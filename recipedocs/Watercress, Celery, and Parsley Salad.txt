Watercress, Celery, and Parsley Salad recipe. In a bowl whisk together vinegar, mustard, and salt and pepper to taste and whisk in oil until dressing is emulsified. Add watercress, celery, and parsley and toss to combine well. Watercress, Celery, and Parsley Salad has 136.0 calories. Watercress, Celery, and Parsley Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Watercress, Celery, and Parsley Salad has 14.0 grams of fat. Watercress, Celery, and Parsley Salad has 124.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watercress, Celery, and Parsley Salad are 2 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 bunch watercress, coarse stems discarded, washed well and spun dry (about 3 cups), 1 celery rib, sliced thin crosswise, 1/4 cup fresh flat-leafed parsley leaves, washed well and spun dry.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

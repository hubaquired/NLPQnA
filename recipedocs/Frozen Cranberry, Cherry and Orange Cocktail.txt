Frozen Cranberry, Cherry and Orange Cocktail recipe.  In a blender, blend cracked ice, cranberry liqueur, Cherry Heering, Grand Marnier and, if desired, sugar for 30 seconds. Pour into a chilled 12-ounce glass. Garnish with the twist of orange. Frozen Cranberry, Cherry and Orange Cocktail has 145.0 calories. Frozen Cranberry, Cherry and Orange Cocktail has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Frozen Cranberry, Cherry and Orange Cocktail are 1 cup cracked ice, 1 pony (1 ounce) cranberry liqueur, 1 pony (1 ounce) Cherry Heering, 1 pony (1 ounce) Grand Marnier, 1 tablespoon superfine granulated sugar, or to taste, Garnish: twist of orange.

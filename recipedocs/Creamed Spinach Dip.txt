Creamed Spinach Dip recipe.  In wide, deep, heavy-bottomed saucepan over moderate heat, bring cream to simmer. Cook, uncovered and without stirring, until thickened enough to coat back of wooden spoon, about 5 minutes. Stir in spinach and cook, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes. Transfer to bowl and cool slightly. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve warm. Creamed Spinach Dip has 171.0 calories. Creamed Spinach Dip has 4.0 grams of protein. Creamed Spinach Dip has 16.0 grams of fat. Creamed Spinach Dip has 89.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamed Spinach Dip are 3/4 cup heavy cream, 1 cup frozen spinach (2/3 of a 10-ounce package), thawed, chopped, and drained well or spun dry in salad spinner, 1/2 cup sour cream, at room temperature.The addition of sour cream makes this creamed spinach extra-tangy and echoes the traditional sour cream served with classic potato pancakes.

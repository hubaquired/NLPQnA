Tehina recipe. Place sesame paste in the bowl of a food processor with water, lemon juice, garlic cloves, and cumin. Pulse to combine, then process until the mixture has the consistency of thick cream. Transfer to a bowl and add salt to taste. Tehina will keep for at least a week, tightly covered in the refrigerator. Tehina has 118.0 calories. Tehina has 4.0 grams of protein. Tehina has 9.0 grams of fat. Tehina has 262.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tehina are 1/2 cup sesame paste, 1 cup water, Juice of 2 lemons, 4 garlic cloves, chopped, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, Salt, to taste.

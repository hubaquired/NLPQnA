Tuscan Kale with Sesame Oil recipe. Rinse kale; shake dry, leaving some water clinging. Heat olive and sesame oils in a large skillet over medium heat. Add kale; season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook, tossing occasionally, until just tender, 7–10 minutes. DO AHEAD: Kale can be made 5 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Tuscan Kale with Sesame Oil are 2 bunches Tuscan kale, ribs and stems removed, leaves torn, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper.Sesame oil will become bitter if it gets too hot, so cook over gentle heat.

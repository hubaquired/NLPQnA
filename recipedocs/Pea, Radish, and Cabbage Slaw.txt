Pea, Radish, and Cabbage Slaw recipe.  Combine snap peas, radish matchsticks, red cabbage, and peas in a medium bowl. Add remaining ingredients to a jar with a lid. Shake to mix well. Toss salad with dressing just before serving. Scatter radish rounds over the top. Pea, Radish, and Cabbage Slaw has 176.0 calories. Pea, Radish, and Cabbage Slaw has 3.0 grams of protein. Pea, Radish, and Cabbage Slaw has 14.0 grams of fat. Pea, Radish, and Cabbage Slaw has 65.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pea, Radish, and Cabbage Slaw are 4 ounces sugar snap peas, thinly sliced crosswise, 1 bunch radishes, half thinly sliced into matchsticks and half thinly sliced into rounds, 4 ounces red cabbage, finely shredded, 4 ounces fresh peas or frozen peas, thawed, 1 lemon, zested and juiced, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar, Sea salt, Freshly ground black pepper.Light, crunchy, and downright pretty, this fresh spring slaw will brighten any spread.

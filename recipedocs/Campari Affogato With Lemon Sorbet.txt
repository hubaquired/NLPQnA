Campari Affogato With Lemon Sorbet recipe.  Mix Campari and orange juice in a small bowl. Divide sorbet among 4 small serving bowls or coffee cups. Pour 2 Tbsp. Campari mixture into each bowl. Top with orange zest. Campari Affogato With Lemon Sorbet has 178.0 calories. Campari Affogato With Lemon Sorbet has 6.0 grams of sodium.

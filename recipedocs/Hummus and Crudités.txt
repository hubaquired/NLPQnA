Hummus and Crudités recipe.  Puree beans, garlic, 3 tablespoons olive oil, and lemon juice in processor. Add 3 tablespoons oil, sugar, and cumin. Puree until very smooth, about 1 1/2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl. Cover; chill at least 1 hour. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 week ahead. Keep chilled. Hummus and Crudités has 220.0 calories. Hummus and Crudités has 2.0 grams of protein. Hummus and Crudités has 21.0 grams of fat. Hummus and Crudités has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hummus and Crudités are 1 15-to 16-ounce can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained, 1 small garlic clove, pressed, 6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin.Pack carrot and celery sticks along with this lemony hummus.

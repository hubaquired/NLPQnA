Herb and Lemon Goat Cheese Spread recipe.  Place goat cheese in small bowl. Mix thyme, lemon peel and garlic in another small bowl; season with ground black pepper. Mix half of thyme mixture into goat cheese. Add olive oil to remaining half of thyme mixture. Form cheese into 2 1/2-inch round; flatten slightly. Place in center of plate. Spoon oil-thyme mixture over top of cheese. Surround cheese with baguette slices and serve. The ingredients of Herb and Lemon Goat Cheese Spread are 5 ounces (about 3/4 cup) soft fresh goat cheese, room temperature, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 1 garlic clove, minced, 5 teaspoons olive oil, 16 French-bread baguette slices, toasted.Delicious with toasted bread, as presented here, or with celery sticks, red bell pepper strips or Belgian endive leaves.

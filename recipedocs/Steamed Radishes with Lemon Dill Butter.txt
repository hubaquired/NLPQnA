Steamed Radishes with Lemon Dill Butter recipe.  In a steamer set over boiling water steam the radishes, covered, for 5 minutes, or until they are just tender. In a large skillet melt the butter over moderately low heat, add the radishes, the lemon juice, the dill, and salt and pepper to taste, and heat the mixture, stirring, until the radishes are heated through. Steamed Radishes with Lemon Dill Butter has 70.0 calories. Steamed Radishes with Lemon Dill Butter has 1.0 grams of protein. Steamed Radishes with Lemon Dill Butter has 6.0 grams of fat. Steamed Radishes with Lemon Dill Butter has 46.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed Radishes with Lemon Dill Butter are 1 pound radishes, trimmed and sliced thin (about 4 cups), 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, or to taste, 4 teaspoons finely chopped fresh dill.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Layer Cake recipe.  Pour in the order listed so that each ingredient floats on top of the one before it. Carefully place the cherry on top. Chill before serving. . The ingredients of Layer Cake are 1/2 ounce crème de cacao, 1/2 ounce apricot brandy, 1/2 ounce heavy cream, Maraschino cherry.

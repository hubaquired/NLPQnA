Shrimp with Snow Peas recipe. Combine egg white, 1 tablespoon cornstarch and wine in medium bowl. Add shrimp and stir to coat. Let stand 30 minutes. Combine catsup, soy sauce and sugar in small bowl. Set aside. Heat oil in wok or heavy large skillet over high heat. Add onion, garlic and ginger and stir-fry until onion and garlic begin to brown, about 3 minutes. Stir shrimp; add and stir-fry until shrimp are just opaque, about 2 minutes. Add dissolved cornstarch and stir until sauce thickens. Shrimp with Snow Peas has 263.0 calories. Shrimp with Snow Peas has 18.0 grams of protein. Shrimp with Snow Peas has 15.0 grams of fat. Shrimp with Snow Peas has 917.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp with Snow Peas are 1 egg white, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 tablespoon dry white wine, 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled, deveined, butterflied, 1 tablespoon catsup, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 2 teaspoons sugar, 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 1 onion, chopped, 3 large garlic cloves, minced, 2 teaspoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 24 snow peas, strings removed, 2 teaspoons cornstarch dissolved in 2 tablespoons water.

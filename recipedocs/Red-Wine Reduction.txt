Red-Wine Reduction recipe. Sauté onion, celery, carrots, and garlic in oil in a 6-quart heavy pot over moderately high heat, stirring, until golden, about 10 minutes. Add wines and simmer, stirring occasionally, until mixture is reduced to 1 quart, about 1 hour. Pour through a sieve into a saucepan and simmer until reduced to 2 cups. Reheat reduction and season with salt and pepper before using. Red-Wine Reduction has 410.0 calories. Red-Wine Reduction has 1.0 grams of protein. Red-Wine Reduction has 3.0 grams of fat. Red-Wine Reduction has 41.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red-Wine Reduction are 1 large onion, chopped, 2 small celery ribs, chopped, 2 carrots, chopped, 3 garlic cloves, chopped, 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 1/2 (750-ml) bottles dry red wine (4 1/2 cups), 1 (750-ml) bottle Ruby Port wine (3 cups).

Gingered Mint Pesto recipe.  In a food processor blend together all ingredients with salt and pepper to taste until smooth. Pesto keeps, surface covered with plastic wrap, chilled, 1 week. Gingered Mint Pesto has 667.0 calories. Gingered Mint Pesto has 7.0 grams of protein. Gingered Mint Pesto has 65.0 grams of fat. Gingered Mint Pesto has 182.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gingered Mint Pesto are 2 1/2 cups packed fresh mint leaves, washed well and spun dry, 1 1/2 tablespoons minced peeled fresh gingerroot, 1/3 cup salted roasted cashews, 1/2 cup olive oil, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 1/4 teaspoon sugar.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

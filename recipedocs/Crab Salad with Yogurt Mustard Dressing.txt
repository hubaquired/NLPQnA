Crab Salad with Yogurt Mustard Dressing recipe.  In a bowl whisk together yogurt, sour cream, paprika, mustard, lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste. Add crab meat, scallion, and celery and toss salad gently. Serve salad with toast points. The ingredients of Crab Salad with Yogurt Mustard Dressing are 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons sour cream, 1/8 teaspoon paprika, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, fresh lemon juice to taste, 1 cup lump crab meat, picked over, 1 scallion, chopped fine, 1 celery rib, chopped fine, toast points as an accompaniment.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Red and Yellow Pepper Relish recipe. Melt butter with oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion; sauté until golden, about 5 minutes. Add peppers; sauté until just tender, about 3 minutes. Add olives, mustard and garlic. Stir 1 minute. Remove from heat. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer relish to bowl. Cool. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Can be made 2 days ahead. Chill.) Bring to room temperature before serving. Red and Yellow Pepper Relish has 163.0 calories. Red and Yellow Pepper Relish has 2.0 grams of protein. Red and Yellow Pepper Relish has 14.0 grams of fat. Red and Yellow Pepper Relish has 120.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red and Yellow Pepper Relish are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 large onion, thinly sliced, 1 red bell pepper, coarsely chopped, 1 yellow bell pepper, coarsely chopped, 1/3 cup coarsely chopped pitted Kalamata olives, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 1 large garlic clove, chopped.Try this with any roasted meat or as a topper for crostini.

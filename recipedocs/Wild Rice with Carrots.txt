Wild Rice with Carrots recipe.  Bring all ingredients to a boil in a 2- to 3-quart saucepan, then simmer, covered, until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, 50 minutes to 1 hour. The ingredients of Wild Rice with Carrots are 2 cups water, 1 cup wild rice (6 ounces), 2 carrots, cut into 1/4-inch dice, 1 small onion, chopped, 1 teaspoon salt.

Roast Beef and Arugula Crostini with Olive-Red Pepper Relish recipe.  Mix red peppers, olives, thyme, and vinegar in small bowl for relish. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Reserve 2 tablespoons goat cheese for garnish. Spread remaining goat cheese on toasted baguette slices. Divide arugula among toasts. Fold roast beef slices to fit toasts; place atop arugula. Sprinkle beef slices lightly with salt and pepper. Top each with 1/3 teaspoon reserved goat cheese and small dollop of relish. Arrange crostini on platter and serve. The ingredients of Roast Beef and Arugula Crostini with Olive-Red Pepper Relish are 3 tablespoons chopped drained roasted red peppers from jar, 3 tablespoons slivered pitted black olives (such as Kalamata), 1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme, 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar, 5 ounces fresh goat cheese, 16 1/4-inch-thick diagonal baguette slices, toasted, 1 ounce baby arugula, 16 very thin slices rare roast beef.

Mignonette Sauce recipe. Combine all ingredients and chill. Serve with chilled oysters or clams on the half shell. Mignonette Sauce has 54.0 calories. Mignonette Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Mignonette Sauce has 751.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mignonette Sauce are 1 tablespoon coarsely ground white or black peppercorns (vary amount according to taste), 1/2 cup white or red wine vinegar, 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallots or sweet onions, Salt to taste.

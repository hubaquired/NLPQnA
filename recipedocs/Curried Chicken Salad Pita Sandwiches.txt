Curried Chicken Salad Pita Sandwiches recipe. Whisk mayonnaise, yogurt, and curry powder in medium bowl. Mix in chicken and green onions. Season chicken salad to taste with salt and pepper. Divide chicken salad and lettuce among each pita half. Place 2 pita halves on each of 4 plates and serve. Curried Chicken Salad Pita Sandwiches has 1080.0 calories. Curried Chicken Salad Pita Sandwiches has 70.0 grams of protein. Curried Chicken Salad Pita Sandwiches has 75.0 grams of fat. Curried Chicken Salad Pita Sandwiches has 632.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Curried Chicken Salad Pita Sandwiches are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 6 tablespoons plain nonfat yogurt, 2 1/4 teaspoons red curry powder, 3 cups finely diced chicken from 2 purchased roast chickens (1 pound total), 3/4 cup thinly sliced green onions, 2 cups shredded hearts of romaine, 4 pita breads, halved, pockets open.These healthy sandwiches get a kick from the curry powder. Serve with: Potato chips, red grapes, and a rice wine-marinated cucumber salad.

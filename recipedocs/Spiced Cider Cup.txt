Spiced Cider Cup recipe. Combine the ingredients in a saucepan and heat to the boiling point. Remove from the heat and allow the spices to steep for 3 or 4 hours. Strain, chill and serve with ice cubes in tumblers or goblets. If you like, the drink may be reheated and served in 8-ounce mugs. Spiced Cider Cup has 117.0 calories. Spiced Cider Cup has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spiced Cider Cup are 1 quart sweet cider, 1 two-inch cinnamon stick, 1/2 teaspoon whole cloves, 1/4 teaspoon whole allspice.

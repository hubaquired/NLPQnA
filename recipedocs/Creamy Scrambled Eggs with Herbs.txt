Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Herbs recipe.  Beat eggs in large bowl to blend. Beat in next 6 ingredients. Season with salt and pepper. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add egg mixture and stir until eggs are scrambled, about 4 minutes. Serve immediately. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Herbs has 266.0 calories. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Herbs has 12.0 grams of protein. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Herbs has 23.0 grams of fat. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Herbs has 217.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Herbs are 6 eggs, 4 ounces light cream cheese, diced, 1/4 cup chopped green onions, 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil or 2 teaspoons dried, crumbled, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, 2 tablespoons milk, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh oregano or 1/2 teaspoon dried, crumbled, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter.

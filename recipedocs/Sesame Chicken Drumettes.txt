Sesame Chicken Drumettes recipe.  Combine first 6 ingredients in heavy medium ovenproof skillet. Add chicken and turn to coat. Cover skillet and refrigerate chicken, turning occasionally, 6 hours or overnight. Preheat oven to 350 °F. Bake chicken in oven in skillet 20 minutes. Turn chicken over and continue baking until chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes longer. Using tongs, transfer chicken to plate. Boil liquid in skillet over medium-high heat until reduced to glaze, about two minutes. Pour glaze over chicken. Sprinkle chicken with sesame seeds. Sesame Chicken Drumettes has 282.0 calories. Sesame Chicken Drumettes has 19.0 grams of protein. Sesame Chicken Drumettes has 16.0 grams of fat. Sesame Chicken Drumettes has 747.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sesame Chicken Drumettes are 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons Sherry, 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoons honey, 1 1/2 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 3 garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/2 teaspoons oriental sesame oil, 14 chicken wing drumettes, 2 teaspoons sesame seeds, toasted.

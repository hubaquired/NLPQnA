Santa Sangria recipe. Put fruit and ice in wine glasses. Combine rosé and juice in a pitcher; pour into glasses. The ingredients of Santa Sangria are 2 large green apples, cored and cut into thin slices, 2 cups frozen raspberries, 6 cups rosé, 1 cup pomegranate juice.This jolly beverage gives "fruit cocktail" a fun new meaning.

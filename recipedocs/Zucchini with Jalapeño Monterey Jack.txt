Zucchini with Jalapeño Monterey Jack recipe. Spread the zucchini in a microwave-safe dish. In a small bowl toss the jalapeño Monterey Jack with the flour, sprinkle the mixture over the zucchini mixture on high power 100%), uncovered, for 3 to 4 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender and the cheese is melted. Season the zucchini mixture with salt and pepper. Zucchini with Jalapeño Monterey Jack has 127.0 calories. Zucchini with Jalapeño Monterey Jack has 8.0 grams of protein. Zucchini with Jalapeño Monterey Jack has 9.0 grams of fat. Zucchini with Jalapeño Monterey Jack has 177.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Zucchini with Jalapeño Monterey Jack are 1 medium zucchini, scrubbed and cut into 1/4-inch thick slices, 1/2 cup coarsely grated jalapeño Monterey Jack, 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

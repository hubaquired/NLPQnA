Watermelon-Lemonade Ice Pops recipe.  Combine all ingredients in processor. Puree until very smooth. Divide mixture among 8 ice pop molds each about 1/4 to 1/3 cup capacity). Cover and freeze until firm, at least 4 hours and up to 5 days. Watermelon-Lemonade Ice Pops has 36.0 calories. Watermelon-Lemonade Ice Pops has 39.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watermelon-Lemonade Ice Pops are 2 cups (packed) finely chopped seeded watermelon, 3/4 cup frozen lemonade concentrate (half of 12-ounce can), thawed, 3 tablespoons sugar, Pinch of salt.

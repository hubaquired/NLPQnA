Tom Douglas' Bing Cherry Salmon recipe. Sauté cherries and tarragon in olive oil for 3 to 5 minutes. Add vinegar, salt, and pepper to taste. Place salmon in oven for 3 minutes at 450°. Spoon sauce over salmon and serve warm. Tom Douglas' Bing Cherry Salmon has 628.0 calories. Tom Douglas' Bing Cherry Salmon has 21.0 grams of protein. Tom Douglas' Bing Cherry Salmon has 39.0 grams of fat. Tom Douglas' Bing Cherry Salmon has 1035.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tom Douglas' Bing Cherry Salmon are 2 cups bing cherries, halved, 2 sprigs fresh tarragon, leaved and chopped, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons red wine vingear, Salt and pepper to taste, 1 3-ounce fillet salmon.

Cucumbers and Dill in Horseradish Cream recipe.  Stir together sour cream, horseradish, dill, scallion, salt, and pepper in a bowl, then stir in cucumber until coated. Let stand for 30 minutes to allow flavors to develop. Cucumbers and Dill in Horseradish Cream has 96.0 calories. Cucumbers and Dill in Horseradish Cream has 2.0 grams of protein. Cucumbers and Dill in Horseradish Cream has 8.0 grams of fat. Cucumbers and Dill in Horseradish Cream has 359.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumbers and Dill in Horseradish Cream are 2/3 cup sour cream, 3 tablespoons bottled horseradish (not drained), 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh dill, 1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped white part of scallion, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1 (14-ounce) seedless cucumber (usually plastic-wrapped), sliced 1/8 inch thick.

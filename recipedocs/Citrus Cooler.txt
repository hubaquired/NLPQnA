Citrus Cooler recipe. Stir 1 cup water and sugar in small saucepan over medium-low heat until sugar dissolves. Bring to boil. transfer syrup to pitcher and chill until cold. Add orange juice, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and lemon juice to pitcher and stir to blend. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Keep chilled.) Fill 4 glasses with ice. Pour citrus cooler over and serve. Citrus Cooler has 106.0 calories. Citrus Cooler has 1.0 grams of protein. Citrus Cooler has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Citrus Cooler are 1 cup water, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 cup fresh orange juice, 1 cup fresh grapefruit juice, 1/4 cup fresh lime juice, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, Ice cubes.

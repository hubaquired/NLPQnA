Turkey Chili Verde recipe. Heat oil in heavy medium saucepan over high heat. Add turkey; sauté until just golden, about 2 minutes. Reduce heat to medium. Sprinkle cornmeal and cumin over turkey and stir 1 minute. Add green onions and stir just until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add 1 cup broth and chilis and simmer until chili thickens, about 5 minutes. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill. Rewarm over low heat, thinning with additional broth, if desired.) Mix in cilantro. Serve chili over rice. The ingredients of Turkey Chili Verde are 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 8 ounces turkey breast strips, cut into 1/2-inch-long pieces, 1 tablespoon cornmeal, 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 cup chopped green onions, 1 cup (or more) canned low-salt chicken broth, 1/4 cup canned diced green chilis, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, Cooked white rice.Serve this lively chili with warm tortillas and a colorful plate of sliced avocado, tomato and red onion drizzled with lemon juice.

Grits with Goat Cheese and Chives recipe.  Bring broth and water to boil in heavy medium saucepan. Gradually stir in grits. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook 4 minutes. Uncover; simmer until thickened to consistency of thin mashed potatoes, stirring often, about 3 minutes. Add sliced chives and cheese. Stir until cheese melts. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Top with chive pieces. Grits with Goat Cheese and Chives has 152.0 calories. Grits with Goat Cheese and Chives has 6.0 grams of protein. Grits with Goat Cheese and Chives has 5.0 grams of fat. Grits with Goat Cheese and Chives has 637.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grits with Goat Cheese and Chives are 2 14 1/2-ounce cans chicken broth, 1/2 cup water, 1 cup quick-cooking grits, 1/3 cup sliced fresh chives or green onions, 4 ounces soft fresh goat cheese (such as Bûcheron or Montrachet), Fresh chives or green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces.

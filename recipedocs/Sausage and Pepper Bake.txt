Sausage and Pepper Bake recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine first 6 ingredients in 13 x 9 x 2-inch glass baking dish. Pour oil over and stir to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Brown sausages in heavy large nonstick skillet over high heat, turning often, about 8 minutes. Arrange on pepper mixture. Bake until sausages are cooked through and peppers are tender, stirring occasionally, about 45 minutes. Sausage and Pepper Bake has 307.0 calories. Sausage and Pepper Bake has 26.0 grams of protein.

Mulled Cider with Winter Spices recipe. Mix first 10 ingredients in large saucepan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer mixture 30 minutes to blend flavors. Strain mulled cider into mugs. Serve cider, passing applejack brandy separately, if desired. Mulled Cider with Winter Spices has 123.0 calories. Mulled Cider with Winter Spices has 35.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mulled Cider with Winter Spices are 12 cups apple cider, 1/4 cup orange juice, Peel (orange part only) from 1 orange, cut into strips, 1 bay leaf, 1 tablespoon (packed) brown sugar, 2 cinnamon sticks, 2 whole cloves, 2 whole allspice, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, Pinch of salt, 1 1/2 cups applejack brandy (optional).

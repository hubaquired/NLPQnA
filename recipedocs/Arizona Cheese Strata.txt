Arizona Cheese Strata recipe. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly butter 9-inch-diameter glass pie plate. Whisk milk, egg, salt and pepper in medium bowl to blend. Mix in cheese, green onion and chili. Add bread and stir until moistened. Transfer mixture to prepared pie dish. Let stand until bread absorbs most of custard, pressing several times to submerge cubes, about 5 minutes. Bake strata uncovered until light brown around edges, top is crusty and knife inserted into center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Combine salsa and chopped fresh cilantro in small bowl. Cut strata into wedges. Serve with salsa. Arizona Cheese Strata has 536.0 calories. Arizona Cheese Strata has 31.0 grams of protein. Arizona Cheese Strata has 28.0 grams of fat. Arizona Cheese Strata has 1800.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Arizona Cheese Strata are 1 cup milk, 2 large eggs, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon pepper, 1 cup (packed) grated Monterey Jack cheese, 3 tablespoons sliced green onion, 2 teaspoons minced jalapeño chili, 2 cups cubed white sandwich bread (about 3 slices), 1/2 cup bottled chunky salsa, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro.

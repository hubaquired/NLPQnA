Korean Steak Tartare recipe.  Peel 1 pear quarter; cut into very small cubes about 1/8"). Place in a small bowl and toss with 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice. Cut remaining pear quarters into thin slices. Place in a medium bowl and toss with remaining 1 teaspoon lemon juice. Whisk scallions, soy sauce, 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, sesame oil, garlic, and pepper in a medium bowl. Add filet mignon and pear cubes and gently toss until just combined; season to taste with salt. Neatly spoon tartare over pear slices. Sprinkle with remaining 1 teaspoon sesame seeds and garnish with chives. Korean Steak Tartare has 65.0 calories. Korean Steak Tartare has 4.0 grams of protein. Korean Steak Tartare has 4.0 grams of fat. Korean Steak Tartare has 106.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Korean Steak Tartare are 1 Asian pear, cored, quartered, 1 1/4 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, divided, 2 tablespoons finely chopped scallions, 2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce, 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds, divided, 1 1/2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil, 1 garlic clove, finely grated, 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, 8 ounces filet mignon, cut into very small cubes (about 1/8"), Kosher salt, 2" pieces of chives.The combination of crisp Asian pear and toasted sesame oil gives this stalwart a modern twist.

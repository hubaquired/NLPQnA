Italian Spoon Biscuits with Tomato Sauce and Cheese recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Cut each biscuit into 8 pieces; place in prepared dish. Add sauce, green onions and bell pepper and stir to blend well. Bake until top is set and almost firm to touch, about 35 minutes. Sprinkle cheese over evenly. Bake until cheese melts, about 5 minutes longer. Let stand 10 minutes. Cut into squares and serve. Italian Spoon Biscuits with Tomato Sauce and Cheese has 331.0 calories. Italian Spoon Biscuits with Tomato Sauce and Cheese has 898.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Italian Spoon Biscuits with Tomato Sauce and Cheese are 3 10- to 11-ounce cans refrigerated buttermilk biscuits, 2 cups prepared tomato pasta sauce, 1 cup sliced green onions, 1 green bell pepper, chopped, 2 cups (packed) grated mozzarella cheese (about 8 ounces).This unusual dish — which combines biscuits, tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese — is a cross between a meatless lasagna and a pizza bread.

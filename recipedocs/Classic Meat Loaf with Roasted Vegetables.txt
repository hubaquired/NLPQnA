Classic Meat Loaf with Roasted Vegetables recipe.  Preheat oven to 375°F. Mash bread, eggs and 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce in large bowl until smooth paste forms. Mix in onion, 1/3 cup ketchup, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Add beef and mix thoroughly. Mound meat mixture in 13x9x2-inch baking pan, shaping into 8x4-inch loaf. Spread remaining 1 tablespoon ketchup over meat loaf. Pour broth and remaining 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce into pan around meat loaf. Arrange vegetables around meat loaf. Cover pan with foil. Bake 45 minutes. Uncover; bake until vegetables are tender and meat loaf is cooked through, about 35 minutes. Using spatula, transfer meat loaf to platter.

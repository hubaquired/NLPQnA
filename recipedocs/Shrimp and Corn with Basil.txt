Shrimp and Corn with Basil recipe.  Melt butter in a large nonstick skillet over moderately high heat, then sauté corn and shrimp, stirring, until shrimp are cooked through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in scallions and basil and season with salt and pepper. Shrimp and Corn with Basil has 256.0 calories. Shrimp and Corn with Basil has 19.0 grams of protein. Shrimp and Corn with Basil has 14.0 grams of fat. Shrimp and Corn with Basil has 659.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp and Corn with Basil are 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, Kernels from 2 ears corn, 1/2 pound shelled large shrimp, 4 scallions, chopped, 1/4 cup fresh basil, thinly sliced.This American variation of a Chinese stir-fry—combined with a loaf of crusty bread—is a meal in itself.

Shrimp Louis recipe. Whisk mayonnaise, chili sauce, mustard, lemon juice and lemon peel in small bowl to blend. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Arrange greens on 2 plates. Top greens with 1 avocado half, rounded side down. Fill avocado cavities with shrimp. Spoon dressing over; sprinkle with green onion. Shrimp Louis has 430.0 calories. Shrimp Louis has 10.0 grams of protein. Shrimp Louis has 38.0 grams of fat. Shrimp Louis has 764.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp Louis are 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons purchased chili sauce, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel, 4 cups mixed baby greens, 1 avocado, peeled, halved, pitted, 10 cooked peeled deveined extra-large shrimp, 1 green onion, finely chopped.A new version of the crab classic, this shrimp salad is great for lunch or dinner. Serve it with artisanal breads like walnut, olive or roasted garlic; then complete the meal with angel food cake, whipped cream and fresh raspberries.

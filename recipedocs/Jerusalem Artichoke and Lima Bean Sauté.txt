Jerusalem Artichoke and Lima Bean Sauté recipe. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add artichokes and cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add peppers and beans. Stir to coat with butter. Season with salt and pepper. Add water, cover and cook until limas are tender, stirring occasionally, about 6 minutes. Jerusalem Artichoke and Lima Bean Sauté has 155.0 calories. Jerusalem Artichoke and Lima Bean Sauté has 5.0 grams of protein. Jerusalem Artichoke and Lima Bean Sauté has 4.0 grams of fat. Jerusalem Artichoke and Lima Bean Sauté has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Jerusalem Artichoke and Lima Bean Sauté are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1 pound Jerusalem artichokes (sun chokes), scrubbed, quartered lengthwise, cut crosswise into 1/8-inch-thick slices, 2 red bell peppers, chopped, 1 10-ounce package frozen baby lima beans, thawed, 1/2 cup water.

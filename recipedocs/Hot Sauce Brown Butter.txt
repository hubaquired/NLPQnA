Hot Sauce Brown Butter recipe. Cook butter in a small skillet over medium heat until it foams then browns be careful not to let it burn), about 5 minutes. Quickly transfer butter to a small bowl, scraping in browned solids. Let cool. Chill brown butter 10 minutes. Stir to distribute solids. Continue to chill, stirring every 10 minutes, until butter starts to solidify. Stir in lemon zest, lemon juice, hot sauce, and salt. Transfer to a piece of plastic; roll into a log. Freeze 1 hour before using. Hot Sauce Brown Butter has 69.0 calories. Hot Sauce Brown Butter has 8.0 grams of fat. Hot Sauce Brown Butter has 112.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Sauce Brown Butter are 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 teaspoons hot sauce, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt.In addition to looking at the color of the liquid butter as it cooks, keep your eyes trained on the solids; the butter is ready when the bits are very dark brown.

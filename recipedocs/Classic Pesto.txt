Classic Pesto recipe.  Combine first 4 ingredients in blender. Blend until paste forms, stopping often to push down basil. Add both cheeses and salt; blend until smooth. Transfer to small bowl. The ingredients of Classic Pesto are 4 cups fresh basil leaves (from about 3 large bunches), 1/2 cup olive oil, 1/3 cup pine nuts, 2 garlic cloves, 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup freshly grated pecorino Sardo or Parmesan cheese, 1 teaspoon coarse kosher salt.Some say using a blender rather than a food processor results in a smoother puree. When combining pesto with pasta, Ligurians mix a small ladle of the cooking water into the pesto just before adding the noodles; this dilutes the concentrated sauce and helps it adhere to the pasta.

Roasted-Almond Ricotta Pesto with Olives recipe. With food processor running, drop in garlic and finely chop. Stop motor and add almonds, basil, lemon juice, parmesan, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper, then process until finely chopped. With motor running, add oil, blending until incorporated. Transfer to a large bowl. Stir in ricotta and olives and season with salt. Toss hot spaghetti with pesto. Thin with some of cooking water. Roasted-Almond Ricotta Pesto with Olives has 390.0 calories. Roasted-Almond Ricotta Pesto with Olives has 14.0 grams of protein. Roasted-Almond Ricotta Pesto with Olives has 35.0 grams of fat. Roasted-Almond Ricotta Pesto with Olives has 248.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted-Almond Ricotta Pesto with Olives are 1 small garlic clove, 2/3 cup roasted unsalted almonds, 4 cups basil leaves, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1/4 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 3/4 cup ricotta (preferably fresh), 1/2 cup chopped pitted Kalamata olives, Accompaniment: 1 pound spaghetti, cooked and drained, reserving 1 cup cooking water.

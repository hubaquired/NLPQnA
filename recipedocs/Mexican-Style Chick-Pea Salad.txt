The ingredients of Mexican-Style Chick-Pea Salad are 2 canned chipotle chiles in adobo*, 3 fresh jalapeño chiles, 1 medium onion, 2 garlic cloves, 3 medium vine-ripened tomatoes, 4 cups drained canned chick-peas (two 19-ounce cans), 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 cup fresh lime juice plus additional to taste, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves, ground black pepper, *available at some specialty foods shops..

Vanilla and Almond Frosting recipe. Using electric mixer, beat butter in large bowl until fluffy. Gradually beat in sugar, then cream and vanilla. Divide between 2 bowls. Mix 1 teaspoon vanilla into 1 bowl of base frosting. Mix almond extract into second bowl of base frosting. Mix in yellow food coloring, 1 drop at a time, until desired shade is reached. The ingredients of Vanilla and Almond Frosting are 3 cups (6 sticks) unsalted butter, room temperature, 10 1/2 cups (2 1/2 to 2 3/4 pounds) powdered sugar, 3 tablespoons whipping cream, 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon almond extract, 6 drops yellow food coloring, Unsprayed (nontoxic) mini roses.One buttercream base makes two frosting flavors.

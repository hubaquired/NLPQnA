Guacamole with Pear and Pomegranate Seeds recipe.  In a molcajete or food processor, grind onion, chiles, and salt into a rough paste. Gradually add the avocado, coarsely mashing it you'll want it to be chunky). Stir in the lime juice. Fold in the pear, grapes, and 1/2 cup of the pomegranate seeds. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds over the guacamole and serve. Guacamole with Pear and Pomegranate Seeds has 218.0 calories. Guacamole with Pear and Pomegranate Seeds has 3.0 grams of protein. Guacamole with Pear and Pomegranate Seeds has 17.0 grams of fat. Guacamole with Pear and Pomegranate Seeds has 249.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Guacamole with Pear and Pomegranate Seeds are 1/3 cup white onions, finely chopped, 3 to 4 serrano chiles, finely chopped, with seeds, 1 teaspoon coarse salt, 2 pounds ripe California avocadoes (about 4 large), 2 to 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3/4 cup pear, peeled and finely diced, 3/4 cup seedless grapes, halved, 3/4 cup pomegranate seeds, Special equipment: molcajete y tojolote (mexican mortar and pestle) or food processor.

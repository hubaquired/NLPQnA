Indian Pudding with Dried-Fruit Compote recipe. In a heavy saucepan stir together milk and cornmeal and bring to a simmer, stirring. Stir in molasses, ginger, cinnamon, and a pinch salt and simmer mixture, stirring, 30 minutes, or until very thick. Add butter, stirring until melted. Remove pan from heat and cool pudding 20 minutes, covered. In a saucepan simmer cider, brown sugar, water, and lemon juice, stirring occasionally, until sugar is dissolved. Add fruits and simmer 5 minutes more, or until fruits are softened and plumped up. Put a spoonful of pudding in each of 8 shallow bowls or soup plates using a 2-ounce 1/4 cup) ice-cream scoop and top with some compote. Indian Pudding with Dried-Fruit Compote has 282.0 calories. Indian Pudding with Dried-Fruit Compote has 5.0 grams of protein. Indian Pudding with Dried-Fruit Compote has 3.0 grams of fat. Indian Pudding with Dried-Fruit Compote has 58.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Indian Pudding with Dried-Fruit Compote are 2 1/2 cups low-fat (1%) milk, 1/3 cup cornmeal, 1/3 cup unsulfured molasses, 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, 1 cup apple cider, 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar, 1/4 cup water, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/2 cup dried apricots, 1/2 cup pitted prunes, 1/2 cup dried cranberries.

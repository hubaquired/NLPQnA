Raspberry, Strawberry, and Orange Granita recipe. Combine orange juice, 3/4 cup water, honey, sugar, and orange peel in blender. Add half of strawberries and half of raspberries; puree until almost smooth. Add remaining berries; puree until smooth. Pour mixture out onto 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Freeze until edges are icy and center is slushy, about 1 hour 30 minutes. Stir icy edges into slushy center. Freeze granita until solid, stirring every 45 minutes, about 3 1/2 hours longer. Using fork, scrape granita down length of pan, forming icy flakes. Cover and freeze at least 1 hour and up to 2 days. Spoon granita into glasses. The ingredients of Raspberry, Strawberry, and Orange Granita are 1 cup orange juice, 3/4 cup water, 1/2 cup honey, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon grated orange peel, 1 16-ounce package fresh strawberries, hulled, quartered (about 3 1/2 cups), 2 1/2-pint baskets fresh raspberries.

Spring Fever recipe.  In a highball glass filled with ice cubes, stir together the rum and the Rose's lime juice, fill the glass with the tonic water, and garnish the cocktail with the lime. The ingredients of Spring Fever are 2 ounces (2 ponies) white rum, 1 1/2 ounces (1 jigger) Rose's lime juice, Chilled tonic water, Lime slices for garnish.(Rum Lime Tonics).

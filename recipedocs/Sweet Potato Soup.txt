Sweet Potato Soup recipe. Place sweet potato, butter, flour, seasonings, brown sugar, Kitchen Bouquet and chicken broth in blender. Cover container and blend until contents are smooth, about 15 seconds. Pour into saucepan. Add milk. Cook, stirring constantly, until soup comes to a boil. Let simmer over low heat for 5 minutes, Serve immediately. Sweet Potato Soup has 155.0 calories. Sweet Potato Soup has 5.0 grams of protein. Sweet Potato Soup has 6.0 grams of fat. Sweet Potato Soup has 1056.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sweet Potato Soup are 1 1/2 cups diced cooked sweet potatoes, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon flour, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 1/4 teaspoon ginger, 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg, 1 tablespoon brown sugar, 1 teaspoon Kitchen Bouquet, 1 1/2 cups chicken broth, 1 cup milk.

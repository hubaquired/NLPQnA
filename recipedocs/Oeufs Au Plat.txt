Oeufs Au Plat recipe.  Rub a room-temperature 9-inch skillet with the butter. Place the skillet on the burner without turning it on. Crack the eggs into the skillet on opposite sides of the pan from each other. Turn the heat on to medium-low and cook the eggs until the whites have coagulated and turned opaque. Season the eggs with salt and serve with Green Onion Sausage and Shrimp Gravy , if you like. Oeufs Au Plat has 105.0 calories. Oeufs Au Plat has 6.0 grams of protein. Oeufs Au Plat has 9.0 grams of fat. Oeufs Au Plat has 281.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Oeufs Au Plat are 2 teaspoons softened butter, 2 eggs, 2 pinches salt.Editor's note: Serve these fried eggs with Chef John Besh's Green Onion Sausage and Shrimp Gravy .

Mixed Greens with Bacon and Herbs recipe.  Cook bacon in heavy large skillet until crisp. Transfer to paper towels to drain. Toss escarole, mixed greens, onions, basil, cilantro, and tarragon in large bowl. Add oil; toss. Add vinegar; toss. Season salad with salt and pepper. Divide among 6 plates. Sprinkle with bacon and serve. Mixed Greens with Bacon and Herbs has 300.0 calories. Mixed Greens with Bacon and Herbs has 6.0 grams of protein. Mixed Greens with Bacon and Herbs has 29.0 grams of fat. Mixed Greens with Bacon and Herbs has 267.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mixed Greens with Bacon and Herbs are 8 bacon slices, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 4 cups (packed) coarsely chopped escarole (from 1 head), 1 5-ounce bag mixed baby greens (about 8 cups), 4 green onions, chopped (white and pale green parts only), 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar.With bacon and lots of fresh herbs, this salad has a delicious balance of flavors.

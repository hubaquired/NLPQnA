Broiled Salmon Fillet with Mustard Dill Sauce recipe. Preheat broiler. Rinse salmon and pat dry. Arrange salmon, skin side down, in a foil-lined jelly-roll pan and rub thoroughly with soy sauce. Season salmon with salt and pepper and broil about 4 inches from heat 12 to 15 minutes, or until just cooked through. Transfer salmon to a platter and garnish with herbs. Serve salmon warm or at room temperature with mustard dill sauce. The ingredients of Broiled Salmon Fillet with Mustard Dill Sauce are a 2 1/2-to-3 pound salmon fillet, soy sauce for rubbing the salmon, fresh bay leaves and thyme sprigs for garnish if desired, Mustard Dill Sauce.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

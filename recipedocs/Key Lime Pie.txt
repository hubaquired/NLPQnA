The ingredients of Key Lime Pie are 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs from 9 (2 1/4-inch by 4 3/4-inch) crackers, 2 tablespoons sugar, 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk, 4 large egg yolks, 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons fresh or bottled Key lime juice (if using bottled, preferably Manhattan brand), 3/4 cup chilled heavy cream.Key limes are also known as Mexican or West Indian limes. If you can't find them in your area, substitute bottled Key lime juice. We've tried several different brands in our test kitchens, and prefer the taste of Manhattan.* This recipe is modified from the classic one found on many condensed milk and Key lime juice labels; we've added additional lime juice for more tartness.

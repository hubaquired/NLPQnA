Junior's Russian Dressing recipe. Whisk the ketchup, mayonnaise, chili sauce, and relish together in a deep medium bowl. Stir together the pickle, green pepper, and onion until mixed in a small bowl. Add to the ketchup-mayonnaise mixture and whisk until evenly distributed throughout. Store in the refrigerator, tightly covered, and use within 2 days. Serve cold. Junior's Russian Dressing has 337.0 calories. Junior's Russian Dressing has 2.0 grams of protein. Junior's Russian Dressing has 26.0 grams of fat. Junior's Russian Dressing has 1303.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Junior's Russian Dressing are 3/4 cup ketchup, 1/2 cup Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise (don't use light or nonfat), 1/3 cup bottled chili sauce, 1/4 cup drained pickle relish, Half of a large dill pickle, finely chopped, 1/3 cup finely chopped green bell pepper, 1/4 cup finely chopped onion.This dressing is served on the side with Junior's Reuben sandwiches. It's so good that you'll find many other foods it matches up perfectly with, like sliced roasted turkey or chicken salad, a cold poached salmon salad, cold roast beef, or a fresh tomato salad.

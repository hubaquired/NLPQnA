Melon Ball recipe.  In a long-stemmed glass filled with ice cubes, stir together Midori, vodka and orange juice and garnish the drink with a skewer. The ingredients of Melon Ball are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) Midori, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) vodka, 1/3 cup strained fresh orange juice.

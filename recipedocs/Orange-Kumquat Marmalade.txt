Orange-Kumquat Marmalade recipe.  Using a knife, remove peel from orange. Set orange aside. Cut white pith from peel. Place peel in a medium saucepan and add cold water to cover by 1". Bring to a boil; drain. Repeat 2 more times. Let cool slightly. Finely chop peel and reserved orange; place in a medium saucepan and add sugar, kumquats, both peppers, and 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until water is evaporated, 35–45 minutes. Let cool; mix in orange juice. DO AHEAD: Marmalade can be made 2 weeks ahead. Cover and chill. Orange-Kumquat Marmalade has 176.0 calories. Orange-Kumquat Marmalade has 1.0 grams of protein. Orange-Kumquat Marmalade has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange-Kumquat Marmalade are 1 large navel orange, 2/3 cup sugar, 1/2 cup kumquats, sliced, 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, 1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper, 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice.

Guacamole recipe.  Mash onion and chiles with 3/4 teaspoon salt in a molcajete or with a mortar and pestle, or mince and mash with a heavy knife. Transfer to a bowl. Pit and peel avocados and mash in bowl with chile mixture. Stir in cilantro and lime juice. Guacamole has 247.0 calories. Guacamole has 3.0 grams of protein. Guacamole has 22.0 grams of fat. Guacamole has 12.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Guacamole are 1/3 cup finely chopped white onion, 1 to 2 serrano chiles, thinly sliced (including seeds), 2 pounds ripe avocados (about 4), 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, 1 to 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice (to taste).

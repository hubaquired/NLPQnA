Fish House Punch recipe. Place the lemon juice and sugar in a punch bowl and stir until sugar is dissolved. Add a large block of ice, brandy, peach brandy, rum, club soda, tea if you wish) and stir well. Decorate with fruits in season. Serve in 4-ounce punch glasses. The ingredients of Fish House Punch are 3 cups lemon juice, 1 cup superfine sugar, Block of ice, 1 1/2 quarts brandy, 1 pint peach brandy, 1 pint rum, 1 quart club soda, 1 quart strong cold black tea (optional), Fruits in season.

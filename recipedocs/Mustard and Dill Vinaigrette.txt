Mustard and Dill Vinaigrette recipe. In a bowl whisk together the brown sugar, the mustard, the vinegar, and salt to taste, add the oil in a stream, whisking, and whisk the vinaigrette until it is emulsified. Stir in the dill. Mustard and Dill Vinaigrette has 168.0 calories. Mustard and Dill Vinaigrette has 18.0 grams of fat. Mustard and Dill Vinaigrette has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mustard and Dill Vinaigrette are 1 teaspoon firmly packed brown sugar, 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard, 2 tablespoons white-wine vinegar, 1/3 cup vegetable oil, 1 tablespoon minced fresh dill.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

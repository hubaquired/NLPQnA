Barbecue Turkey Sandwiches recipe. Combine first 3 ingredients in medium bowl. Let stand at room temperature until onion softens, tossing occasionally, about 2 hours; or cover and chill up to 1 day. The ingredients of Barbecue Turkey Sandwiches are 1 small red onion, thinly sliced, 1 cup finely shredded red cabbage, 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, 1 pound thickly sliced cooked turkey meat, cut into matchstick-size strips, 2/3 cup purchased barbecue sauce, 1/4 cup homemade turkey broth or low-salt chicken broth, 4 soft sandwich buns, halved, toasted.These are especially good when made with leftover grilled turkey.

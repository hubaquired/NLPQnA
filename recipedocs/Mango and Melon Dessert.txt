Mango and Melon Dessert recipe. In a food processor, purée mango, adding 1 tbsp water, if necessary, to achieve a puddinglike consistency. Add spices; blend thoroughly. Add sugar to taste. Cover and chill 3 to 4 hours. When ready to serve, cut watermelon in half. Using a melon baller, scoop out about 2 cups of balls. Pour purée into 4 shallow bowls and arrange melon balls on top. Garnish with mint sprigs. The ingredients of Mango and Melon Dessert are 2 1/2 cups diced fresh mango, 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger, Confectioners' sugar, 1 small watermelon (preferably seedless), Mint sprigs, for garnish.This refreshing treat, from Mridula Baljekar, author of Indian Cooking Without Fat (Metro Books), is a cool finish to spicy dishes.

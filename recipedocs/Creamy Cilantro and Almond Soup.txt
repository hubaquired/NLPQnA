Creamy Cilantro and Almond Soup recipe. Combine 2 cups broth, 2 cups cilantro, parsley, cream cheese, and almonds in blender; blend until smooth. Heat 4 1/2 cups broth in large saucepan. Whisk in herb-cheese mixture and marjoram. Simmer 25 minutes to blend flavors. Transfer 1 cup soup and remaining 2 cups cilantro to blender; puree until smooth. Add puree to soup in pan. Season with salt and pepper. Bring soup to simmer, thinning with more broth if desired. Creamy Cilantro and Almond Soup has 301.0 calories. Creamy Cilantro and Almond Soup has 14.0 grams of protein. Creamy Cilantro and Almond Soup has 24.0 grams of fat. Creamy Cilantro and Almond Soup has 297.0 grams of sodium.

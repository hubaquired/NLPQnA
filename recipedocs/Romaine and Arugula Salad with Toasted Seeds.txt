Romaine and Arugula Salad with Toasted Seeds recipe.  Combine all seeds in heavy small skillet. Add large pinch of salt. Cook over low heat until white sesame seeds are pale golden, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes. Using potato masher, press mixture in skillet until coriander seeds are coarsely crushed. Place lemon juice and peel in small bowl. Whisk in olive oil. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Seeds and dressing can be made 8 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature.) Combine romaine and arugula in large bowl. Add dressing and half of seeds; toss to coat. Sprinkle with remaining seeds and serve. Romaine and Arugula Salad with Toasted Seeds has 102.0 calories. The ingredients of Romaine and Arugula Salad with Toasted Seeds are 2 teaspoons sesame seeds (preferably black and white), 1 teaspoon whole coriander seeds, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon (packed) finely grated lemon peel, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1 large head of romaine lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces (about 16 cups), 4 cups baby arugula leaves.

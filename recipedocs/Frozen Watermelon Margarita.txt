Frozen Watermelon Margarita recipe. Remove rind from watermelon and cut enough fruit into 1-inch pieces to measure 5 cups. Freeze watermelon in a sealable plastic bag at least 3 hours and up to 1 week. In a 5- to 6-cup blender blend frozen watermelon with remaining ingredients until thick and smooth. Frozen Watermelon Margarita has 191.0 calories. Frozen Watermelon Margarita has 1.0 grams of protein. Frozen Watermelon Margarita has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Frozen Watermelon Margarita are a 3-pound piece watermelon, 1 cup white Tequila, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice (from about 4 limes), 1/4 cup sugar, or to taste.In these margaritas, frozen watermelon takes the place of ice.

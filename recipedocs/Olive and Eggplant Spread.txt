Olive and Eggplant Spread recipe.  Preheat oven to 375°F. Rub eggplant with 2 tablespoons oil and bake, cut sides down, in a small baking pan in middle of oven until flesh is browned and very tender, about 50 minutes. Scoop flesh from eggplant into a food processor, discarding skin, and purée with olives, capers, remaining 2 tablespoons oil, and salt and pepper to taste. Olive and Eggplant Spread has 157.0 calories. Olive and Eggplant Spread has 1.0 grams of protein. Olive and Eggplant Spread has 15.0 grams of fat. Olive and Eggplant Spread has 108.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Olive and Eggplant Spread are 1 (1-pound) eggplant, halved lengthwise and flesh scored in a crosshatch pattern, 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 cup Kalamata or other brine-cured black olives, rinsed, patted dry, and pitted, 1 tablespoon drained bottled capers, rinsed.

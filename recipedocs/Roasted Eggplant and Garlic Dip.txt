Roasted Eggplant and Garlic Dip recipe. Preheat oven to 425°F. Separate garlic cloves without peeling and tightly wrap together in foil. Prick eggplant with a fork. In a shallow baking pan roast garlic and eggplant in middle of oven until very tender, about 30 minutes for garlic and about 45 minutes for eggplant. Unwrap garlic and peel, transferring garlic to a food processor. Scrape flesh from eggplant into food processor, discarding skin. Purée mixture until smooth and, with motor running, add oil and vinegar until combined. Season dip with salt and pepper and serve with pita toasts. Roasted Eggplant and Garlic Dip has 176.0 calories. Roasted Eggplant and Garlic Dip has 2.0 grams of protein. Roasted Eggplant and Garlic Dip has 14.0 grams of fat. Roasted Eggplant and Garlic Dip has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Eggplant and Garlic Dip are 2 small heads garlic, 1 eggplant (1 pound), 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1 teaspoon red-wine vinegar, or to taste.

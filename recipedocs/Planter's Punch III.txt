Planter's Punch III recipe.  Pour lime juice into a cocktail shaker and add sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Add rum, ice and bitters and shake vigorously. Pour mixture, unstrained, into a 9-ounce goblet. Planter's Punch III has 321.0 calories. Planter's Punch III has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Planter's Punch III are 1 ounce lime juice, 2 tablespoons superfine sugar, 3 ounces dark rum, 4 ounces crushed ice, Dash Angostura bitters.This is the traditional planter's punch that follows the old rhyme: One of sour (lime juice), two of sweet (sugar), three of strong (rum), four of weak (ice or water).

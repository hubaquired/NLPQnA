Prunes in Wine and Armagnac recipe. In a stainless-steel saucepan combine the wine and the sugar, bring the mixture to a boil, stirring until the sugar is dissolved, and boil it for 1 minute. Add the prunes and the Armagnac and simmer the mixture, covered, for 15 minutes. Let the mixture cool, covered, for 15 minutes and spoon it into a 1 1/2-quart ceramic or glass container. Let the mixture cool completely, covered. The prunes keep, covered and chilled, for 3 months. Prunes in Wine and Armagnac has 372.0 calories. Prunes in Wine and Armagnac has 2.0 grams of protein. Prunes in Wine and Armagnac has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Prunes in Wine and Armagnac are 2 cups dry red wine, 3/4 cup sugar, two 12-ounce boxes pitted prunes, 1 cup Armagnac.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

Maple-Soy-Glazed Salmon recipe.  Whisk syrup, soy sauce, and adobo in pie dish to blend. Add salmon; turn to coat. Let marinate 30 minutes, turning occasionally. Drain marinade into small saucepan. Heat heavy nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until slightly charred outside and just opaque in center, about 3 minutes per side. Meanwhile, boil marinade until reduced to scant 1/4 cup sauce, about 4 minutes. Place 1 salmon fillet on each of 4 plates, drizzle with sauce, and serve. Maple-Soy-Glazed Salmon has 431.0 calories. Maple-Soy-Glazed Salmon has 36.0 grams of protein. Maple-Soy-Glazed Salmon has 23.0 grams of fat. Maple-Soy-Glazed Salmon has 993.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Maple-Soy-Glazed Salmon are 1/3 cup maple syrup, 1/4 cup soy sauce, 1 teaspoon adobo sauce from canned chipotles in adobo, 4 6-ounce skinless salmon fillets.Serve the salmon with steamed rice and edamame.

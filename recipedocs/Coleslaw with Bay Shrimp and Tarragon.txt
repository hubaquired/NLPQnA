Coleslaw with Bay Shrimp and Tarragon recipe. Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice and tarragon in large bowl; whisk to blend. Mix in cabbage, carrots and onion. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Mix in shrimp. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes and up to 4 hours. Coleslaw with Bay Shrimp and Tarragon has 338.0 calories. Coleslaw with Bay Shrimp and Tarragon has 18.0 grams of protein. Coleslaw with Bay Shrimp and Tarragon has 24.0 grams of fat. Coleslaw with Bay Shrimp and Tarragon has 856.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coleslaw with Bay Shrimp and Tarragon are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon, 6 cups thinly sliced green cabbage, 1 1/2 cups coarsely grated carrots, 1 cup thinly sliced red onion, 1 pound cooked bay shrimp.Thanks to the addition of some shrimp, coleslaw becomes a light main-course salad. Flaked cooked fish would also work nicely.

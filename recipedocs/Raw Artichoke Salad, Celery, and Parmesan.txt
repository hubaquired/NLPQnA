Raw Artichoke Salad, Celery, and Parmesan recipe.  Whisk oil and juice in a medium bowl. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Working with 1 artichoke at a time, break off outer leaves until only pale-green ones remain. Cut 1/2" off the top; trim dark green parts around stem and heart. Slice lengthwise very thinly and toss in dressing. Repeat with remaining artichokes. Mix in celery and remaining ingredients. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to a platter and top with cheese. The ingredients of Raw Artichoke Salad, Celery, and Parmesan are 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, Kosher salt, Freshly ground black pepper, 12 baby artichokes (about 1 pound), stems trimmed, 1 cup thinly sliced celery, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, 2 ounces shaved Parmesan.To prevent cut artichokes from browning, work with 1 artichoke at a time and lightly toss it in the lemon dressing as you go.

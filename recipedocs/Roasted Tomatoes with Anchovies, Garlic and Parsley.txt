Roasted Tomatoes with Anchovies, Garlic and Parsley recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Arrange tomatoes, rounded side down, in single layer in 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Sprinkle with anchovies, parsley, garlic and dried red pepper, then salt and pepper. Drizzle with oil. Bake tomatoes uncovered until tender, wrinkled and starting to brown at edges, about 40 minutes. The ingredients of Roasted Tomatoes with Anchovies, Garlic and Parsley are 1 1/2 pounds large plum tomatoes, each cut lengthwise into 6 wedges, 10 anchovy fillets, chopped, 1/4 cup chopped Italian parsley, 2 garlic cloves, chopped, 1/4 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper, 1/2 cup olive oil.Serve this rustic tomato mixture over pasta or as part of an antipasto platter.

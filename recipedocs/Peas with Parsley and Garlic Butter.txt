Peas with Parsley and Garlic Butter recipe.  Melt butter in heavy large saucepan over medium-low heat. Add garlic and sauté until very fragrant, about 3 minutes. Add peas and broth. Increase heat to medium-high and cook until peas are tender, about 4 minutes. Mix in parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Peas with Parsley and Garlic Butter has 199.0 calories. Peas with Parsley and Garlic Butter has 6.0 grams of protein. Peas with Parsley and Garlic Butter has 12.0 grams of fat. Peas with Parsley and Garlic Butter has 186.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peas with Parsley and Garlic Butter are 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, 5 large garlic cloves, thinly sliced, 1 pound frozen peas, thawed, 1/4 cup canned vegetable broth, 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley.Serve this side dish with rice or mashed potatoes to soak up the flavorful sauce.

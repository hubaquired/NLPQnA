Roasted Bosc Pears recipe.  Preheat oven to 375°F. Melt butter with oil in small ovenproof skillet over medium heat. Add pear halves, turning to coat. Arrange pears, cut side down, in skillet; sprinkle with water. Roast until tender, about 20 minutes. Cool. Peel and core roasted pears; cut into 3/4-inch cubes. Roasted Bosc Pears has 171.0 calories. Roasted Bosc Pears has 13.0 grams of fat. Roasted Bosc Pears has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Bosc Pears are 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 Bosc pear, halved, 1 tablespoon water.

White Chocolate Mousse recipe.  Whisk eggs, sugar and Frangelico in large metal bowl to blend. Set bowl over saucepan of simmering water do not allow bottom of bowl to touch water); whisk constantly until mixture thickens and thermometer inserted into mixture registers 140°F. for 3 minutes, about 7 minutes total. Remove bowl from over water. Add white chocolate; whisk until smooth. Cool completely, whisking occasionally. Beat cream in medium bowl to medium-firm peaks. Fold whipped cream into white chocolate mixture in 3 additions mixture will be soft). Chill until spreadable, about 3 hours. White Chocolate Mousse has 231.0 calories. White Chocolate Mousse has 3.0 grams of protein. White Chocolate Mousse has 13.0 grams of fat. White Chocolate Mousse has 43.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of White Chocolate Mousse are 2 large eggs, 1/3 cup sugar, 3 tablespoons Frangelico (hazelnut liqueur), 6 ounces good-quality white chocolate (such as Baker's or Lindt), chopped, 1 cup chilled whipping cream.This recipe originally accompanied Frozen White Chocolate and Hazelnut Dacquoise .

Papaya Smoothie recipe.  Combine all ingredients in blender. Purée until smooth. Pour into 2 chilled tall glasses and serve. Papaya Smoothie has 257.0 calories. Papaya Smoothie has 4.0 grams of protein. Papaya Smoothie has 1.0 grams of fat. Papaya Smoothie has 49.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Papaya Smoothie are 2 cups chopped peeled seeded papaya (about 1 medium), 1 cup chilled pineapple juice, 1/2 cup low-fat (1%) milk, 1/2 cup sliced banana, 4 ice cubes, 1 tablespoon honey, 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice.

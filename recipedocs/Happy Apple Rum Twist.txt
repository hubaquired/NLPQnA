Happy Apple Rum Twist recipe. Combine all ingredients except the peel in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass and drop in the peel. Happy Apple Rum Twist has 144.0 calories. Happy Apple Rum Twist has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Happy Apple Rum Twist are 1 1/2 ounces light or dark rum, 3 ounces apple juice or cider, 1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice, 1/2 cup crushed ice, Twist lime peel.

Potato, Leek and Fennel Soup recipe.  Melt butter in heavy large pot over medium-high heat. Add leeks and fennel and sauté until leeks are translucent, about 7 minutes. Add broth and potatoes and bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer soup until potatoes are very tender, about 25 minutes. Working in batches, purée soup in blender. Return to same pot. Rewarm soup if necessary. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle soup into bowls; garnish with reserved fennel fronds and serve. Potato, Leek and Fennel Soup has 159.0 calories. Potato, Leek and Fennel Soup has 7.0 grams of protein. Potato, Leek and Fennel Soup has 4.0 grams of fat. Potato, Leek and Fennel Soup has 73.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Potato, Leek and Fennel Soup are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 2 cups sliced leeks (white and pale green parts only), 2 cups sliced fennel bulb, fronds reserved for garnish, 4 14 1/2-ounce cans low-salt chicken broth, 2 pounds red-skinned potatoes, peeled, cut into 1/2-inch pieces (about 4 cups).This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

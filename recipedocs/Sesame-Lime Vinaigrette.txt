Sesame-Lime Vinaigrette recipe. Combine shallot and lime juice in a large jar or medium bowl; season with salt and pepper and let sit 10 minutes. Add sesame oil, vegetable oil, soy sauce, and honey to shallot mixture; cover and shake or whisk) to combine. Season sesame-lime vinaigrette with salt and pepper.

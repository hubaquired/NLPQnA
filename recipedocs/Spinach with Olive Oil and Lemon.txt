Spinach with Olive Oil and Lemon recipe. Add spinach to boiling water; cook 1 minute. Drain; wring out. Toss with oil and salt. Add lemon juice; toss again. The ingredients of Spinach with Olive Oil and Lemon are 12 ounces fresh baby spinach, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Juice of 1/4 lemon.This recipe originally accompanied Monte Cristo Chicken.

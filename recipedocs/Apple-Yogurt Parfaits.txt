Apple-Yogurt Parfaits recipe. Set out 4 large wine or parfait glasses. In each, layer 1/4 apple, 1/4 cup yogurt, 2 teaspoons ground flaxseeds, 1 teaspoon agave nectar and 1 tablespoon raisins. Repeat all layers twice in each glass. The ingredients of Apple-Yogurt Parfaits are 3 Honeycrisp apples, diced, 3 cups nonfat plain Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup ground flaxseeds, 1/4 cup agave nectar or honey, 3/4 cup golden raisins.The juicy Honeycrisp apple is the sweetest of the bunch. Combine it (instead of berries) with tart, creamy Greek yogurt for a seasonal, satisfying breakfast.  Breakfast Dessert Vegetarian Quick & Easy Yogurt Raisin Apple Fall Healthy Self.

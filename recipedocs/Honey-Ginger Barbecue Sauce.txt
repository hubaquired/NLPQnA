Honey-Ginger Barbecue Sauce recipe.  Stir together all sauce ingredients in a 2- to 2 1/2-quart heavy saucepan and briskly simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until thickened and reduced to about 1 1/4 cups, 25 to 30 minutes. Stir frequently toward end of cooking to prevent sticking.) Honey-Ginger Barbecue Sauce has 298.0 calories. Honey-Ginger Barbecue Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Honey-Ginger Barbecue Sauce has 1430.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honey-Ginger Barbecue Sauce are 1 1/2 cups apple cider vinegar, 1/2 cup mild honey, 1/2 cup ketchup, 1 1/2 tablespoons hot sauce, such as Tabasco, 4 large garlic cloves, minced, 2 tablespoons minced peeled ginger, 1 teaspoon salt.This sweet and tangy sauce is perfect for glazing grilled meats, seafood, and veggies.

Black Beans with Garlic, Cumin, and Cilantro recipe. Rinse black beans and drain. Chop garlic. In a nonstick skillet cook garlic and cumin in oil over moderate heat, stirring, until fragrant. Add black beans, juice or water, and salt and cook, stirring, until beans are heated through. Stir in cilantro. Black Beans with Garlic, Cumin, and Cilantro has 361.0 calories. Black Beans with Garlic, Cumin, and Cilantro has 16.0 grams of protein. Black Beans with Garlic, Cumin, and Cilantro has 15.0 grams of fat. Black Beans with Garlic, Cumin, and Cilantro has 1222.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Black Beans with Garlic, Cumin, and Cilantro are a 16- to 19-ounce can black beans, 2 garlic cloves, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/3 cup tomato juice or water, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro.

Lime-Pickled Red Onion recipe. Combine onion and lime juice in a small bowl. Season with salt and toss. Let sit, tossing occasionally, at least 20 minutes. Just before serving, drain onion and toss with cilantro and sumac. Onion can be pickled 3 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Lime-Pickled Red Onion are 1 medium red onion, halved through root end, thinly sliced, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, Kosher salt, 1/4 cup cilantro leaves with tender stems, coarsely chopped, 2 teaspoons sumac.Acidity helps preserve the alliums’ flavor; either vinegar or citrus juice would do the trick.  Bon Appétit Condiment Onion Pickles Lime Juice Cilantro.

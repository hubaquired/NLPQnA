Steamed New Potatoes with Mint and Parsley recipe.  In a steamer set over boiling water steam the potatoes, covered, for 10 to 12 minutes, or until they are just tender. While the potatoes are steaming, in a bowl stir together the mint, the parsley, the butter, the sour cream, and salt and pepper to taste. Add the potatoes to the herb mixture and toss them well. Steamed New Potatoes with Mint and Parsley has 183.0 calories. Steamed New Potatoes with Mint and Parsley has 3.0 grams of protein. Steamed New Potatoes with Mint and Parsley has 7.0 grams of fat. Steamed New Potatoes with Mint and Parsley has 35.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed New Potatoes with Mint and Parsley are 3/4 pound small red potatoes, quartered lengthwise, 2 teaspoons minced fresh mint leaves, 2 teaspoons minced fresh parsley leaves, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened, 1 tablespoon sour cream.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

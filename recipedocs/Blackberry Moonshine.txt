Blackberry Moonshine recipe. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker. Top with ice and shake vigorously. Strain into a shot glass. Blackberry Moonshine has 41.0 calories. The ingredients of Blackberry Moonshine are 3/4 ounce unaged white whiskey (e.g., moonshine), 1/4 ounce blackberry liqueur, 1/4 ounce blackberry puree (or muddle one blackberry with a splash of simple syrup), Juice from 1 lemon wedge.

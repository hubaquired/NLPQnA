Leap Year recipe.  Combine all ingredients, except the peel, in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass, twist peel over the drink to release the oil, and then drop in the peel. Leap Year has 137.0 calories. Leap Year has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Leap Year are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1/4 ounce sweet vermouth, 1/4 ounce Grand Marnier, Dash lemon juice, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Twist lemon peel.

Sesame Carrots recipe.  Peel and cut 4 medium carrots into long matchstick-size pieces. Heat 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil in a large skillet, preferably nonstick, over medium heat. Add carrots and cook, stirring occasionally, until just tender, 3-4 minutes. Season with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper. Sesame Carrots has 28.0 calories. Sesame Carrots has 2.0 grams of fat. Sesame Carrots has 26.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sesame Carrots are 4 medium carrots, 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil, kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper.

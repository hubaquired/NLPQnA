Green Bean and Cherry Tomato Salad with Herb Dressing recipe. Cook beans in large pot of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 3 minutes. Drain. Transfer to bowl of ice water and cool. Drain well. Place in large bowl. Mix next 5 ingredients in small bowl; stir into beans. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley and cherry tomatoes. Green Bean and Cherry Tomato Salad with Herb Dressing has 117.0 calories. Green Bean and Cherry Tomato Salad with Herb Dressing has 2.0 grams of protein. Green Bean and Cherry Tomato Salad with Herb Dressing has 9.0 grams of fat. Green Bean and Cherry Tomato Salad with Herb Dressing has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Bean and Cherry Tomato Salad with Herb Dressing are 1 1/2 pounds slender green beans, trimmed, cut into 2-inch pieces, 3/4 cup chopped seeded tomatoes, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1/4 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley, 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes.

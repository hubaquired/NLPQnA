Parsley Vinaigrette recipe. Pulse parsley, oil, lemon juice, vinegar, and garlic in a food processor until well blended. Season to taste with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Parsley Vinaigrette are 2 cups (packed) flat-leaf parsley (leaves and stems), 1/2 cup olive oil, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/2 tablespoon Champagne vinegar, 1 garlic clove, crushed, Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper.Mullen likes to spoon leftover vinaigrette over fish or grilled vegetables.

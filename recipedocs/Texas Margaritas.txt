Texas Margaritas recipe.  In a blender, combine all ingredients except salt. Blend on high until smooth and slushy. Serve in salt-rimmed glasses, if desired. Texas Margaritas has 139.0 calories. Texas Margaritas has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Texas Margaritas are 6 ounces (3/4 cup) frozen limeade concentrate, 6 ounces (3/4 cup) tequila, 4 ounces (1/2 cup) beer, 2 ounces (1/4 cup) orange juice, 2 cups ice cubes, Coarse salt (optional).Editor's Note: This recipe originally accompanied a story on Mother's Day.

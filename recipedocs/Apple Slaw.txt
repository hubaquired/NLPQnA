Apple Slaw recipe. Put the apple, cabbage, garlic, chile, spring onion, and cilantro in a medium bowl. Pour over the vinegar, season with salt and pepper, and toss to combine. Apple Slaw has 202.0 calories. Apple Slaw has 4.0 grams of protein. Apple Slaw has 1.0 grams of fat. Apple Slaw has 59.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apple Slaw are 2 green apples, coarsely grated, 1/3 head red cabbage, coarsely grated, 1 garlic clove, crushed, 1 red chile, finely chopped (keep the seeds if you like it hot), 8 scallions (spring onions), finely sliced, 1 handful cilantro, leaves picked, 1 1/4 cups (300 ml) white wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar.Serve this tangy vinegar-based slaw with roasted pork or Guy Turland's smoky Barbecue Brisket.

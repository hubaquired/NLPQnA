Spirited Hot Apple Cider recipe.  Using vegetable peeler, remove peel orange part only) in strips from orange. Melt butter in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add peel, sugar, 3 cinnamon sticks, allspice and cloves; stir 1 minute. Add cider; bring to simmer. Reduce heat to low; simmer 15 minutes. Mix in rum and applejack. Strain into mugs. Garnish with additional cinnamon sticks, if desired. Spirited Hot Apple Cider has 287.0 calories. Spirited Hot Apple Cider has 1.0 grams of protein. Spirited Hot Apple Cider has 6.0 grams of fat. Spirited Hot Apple Cider has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spirited Hot Apple Cider are 1 orange, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar, 3 cinnamon sticks, 10 whole allspice, 10 whole cloves, 6 cups apple cider, 1/4 cup dark rum, 1/2 cup applejack, Additional cinnamon sticks (optional).

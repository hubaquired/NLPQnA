Cal-a-Vie Turkey Cheeseburgers with Chipotle Mayonnaise recipe. Combine the ground turkey, shallot, scallions, garlic, egg, Worcestershire sauce, tarragon, salt and pepper in a bowl and mix well. Add wheat germ if needed to bind the mixture. Shape into 4 patties. Grill the patties until cooked through. Spread the Chipotle Mayonnaise on the <homemade sun-dried="" tomato="" buns=""></homemade>and add 1 turkey patty and 1 slice of cheese to each burger. Garnish with lettuce, tomato, onion and pickle. The ingredients of Cal-a-Vie Turkey Cheeseburgers with Chipotle Mayonnaise are 1 pound ground turkey, 1 tablespoon chopped shallot, 1 tablespoon chopped scallions, 1 teaspoon chopped garlic, 1 egg, or 2 egg whites, 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon pepper, wheat germ (optional), 2 tablespoons Chipotle Mayonnaise, 4 Homemade Sun-Dried Tomato Buns, 4 slices mozzarella cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion and pickle, for garnish.Editor's note: The recipe below is excerpted from Cal-a-Vie Living: Gourmet Spa Cuisine and is part of a healthy and delicious spa menu developed exclusively for Epicurious by Cal-a-Vie.

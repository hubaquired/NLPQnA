Catfish with Green Olives recipe.  Stir together olives, oil, zest, and 2 tablespoons parsley in a bowl. Arrange catfish, skinned sides down, in an oiled 12-inch nonstick skillet, tucking thinner ends of each fillet under. Season fish with salt and pepper, then mound olive mixture on top. Put a round of parchment or wax paper on top of olive mixture, then tightly cover skillet. Cook catfish over moderate heat until just cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes. Serve sprinkled with remaining tablespoon parsley. Catfish with Green Olives has 307.0 calories. Catfish with Green Olives has 26.0 grams of protein. Catfish with Green Olives has 21.0 grams of fat. Catfish with Green Olives has 635.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Catfish with Green Olives are 1 cup pimiento-stuffed green olives, drained, rinsed, and chopped, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 teaspoon finely grated fresh lemon zest, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 4 (6-ounce) catfish fillets, Accompaniment: lemon wedges.

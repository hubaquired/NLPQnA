Farfalle with Tomatoes and Feta Cheese recipe.  Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Combine the pasta, tomatoes, olives, onions, capers, cheese, and basil. Pour on the vinaigrette and toss lightly. Farfalle with Tomatoes and Feta Cheese has 364.0 calories. Farfalle with Tomatoes and Feta Cheese has 13.0 grams of protein. Farfalle with Tomatoes and Feta Cheese has 4.0 grams of fat. Farfalle with Tomatoes and Feta Cheese has 194.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Farfalle with Tomatoes and Feta Cheese are 1 1/2 pounds farfalle or bow tie pasta, 6 roma tomatoes, seeded and diced, 1/2 cup calamata olives, pitted, 1/3 cup diced red onion, 4 teaspoons drained capers, 3 ounces feta cheese, crumbled, 10 basil leaves, julienned, Balsamic Vinaigrette.Editor's note: You can substitute heirloom or cherry tomatoes if you like.

Milk Punch with Brandy and Rum recipe.  In a heavy saucepan, combine the milk, light cream, brandy, dark rum, and sugar. Heat the mixture over moderate heat, stirring, until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is hot. Pour the mixture into a heat-proof punch bowl, rinsed with boiling water beforehand, and garnish the punch with grated nutmeg. Milk Punch with Brandy and Rum has 160.0 calories. Milk Punch with Brandy and Rum has 2.0 grams of protein. Milk Punch with Brandy and Rum has 7.0 grams of fat. Milk Punch with Brandy and Rum has 34.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Milk Punch with Brandy and Rum are 4 cups milk, 2 cups light cream, 1 cup brandy, 3/4 cup dark rum, 1/4 cup fine granulated sugar, Garnish: grated nutmeg.

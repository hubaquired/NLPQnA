Frozen Margarita recipe. Rub the rim of an old-fashioned glass with a cut lime and dip it in salt to lightly coat it. Chill the glass. In a blender, blend the ingredients, including the ice cubes, for 30 seconds. Pour into the glass. Frozen Margarita has 204.0 calories. Frozen Margarita has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Frozen Margarita are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) tequila, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) fresh lime juice, 1/2 teaspoon superfine granulated sugar, 1 pony (1 ounce) Triple Sec or Cointreau, 5 ice cubes.

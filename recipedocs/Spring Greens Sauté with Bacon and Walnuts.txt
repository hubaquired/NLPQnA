Spring Greens Sauté with Bacon and Walnuts recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-low heat. Add bacon; sauté until almost crisp, about 6 minutes. Add walnuts; sauté 1 minute. Add greens and toss until wilted but still bright green, 5 to 7 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl. Sprinkle with cheese, if desired, and serve. The ingredients of Spring Greens Sauté with Bacon and Walnuts are 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 1 thick-cut bacon slice, chopped, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts, 1 bunch dandelion greens, Swiss chard, or mustard greens (about 12 ounces), tough bottom stems removed, leaves cut into 1-inch pieces (about 8 cups), 2 tablespoons finely grated Gruyère cheese (optional).The bacon and sautéed nuts play well against the greens. If you like your food a bit tart, add a splash of Sherry vinegar.

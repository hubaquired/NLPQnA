Deviled Ham recipe.  In a food processor grind the ham until it is minced, add the mustard, the butter, the honey, and the cloves, and blend the mixture until it is smooth. Transfer the mixture into a bowl and stir in the onion, the pickle, and pepper to taste. Serve the deviled ham with crackers or as a sandwich filling. Deviled Ham has 185.0 calories. Deviled Ham has 10.0 grams of protein. Deviled Ham has 13.0 grams of fat. Deviled Ham has 765.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Deviled Ham are 3/4 pound cooked ham, cut into pieces, 1/4 cup Dijon-style mustard, 1/2 stick (1/4 cup) unsalted butter, softened, 1 tablespoon honey, 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves, 3 tablespoons minced onion, 3 tablespoons minced sweet pickle.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

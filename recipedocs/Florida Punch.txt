Florida Punch recipe.  Combine sugar and water in a saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until the sugar is dissolved. Cool. Combine the sugar syrup with the fruit juices and pour over ice cubes in a punch bowl. Add the ginger ale and serve at once in 8-ounce goblets. Florida Punch has 188.0 calories. Florida Punch has 2.0 grams of protein. Florida Punch has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Florida Punch are 2 cups superfine sugar, 3 cups water, 2 46-ounce cans blended orange and grapefruit juice, 1 1/2 cups lime juice, 1 1/2 quarts ginger ale, 16 ice cubes.

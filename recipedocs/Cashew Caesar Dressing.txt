Cashew Caesar Dressing recipe.  Blend anchovies, cashews, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder in a blender on low speed until cashews are broken up. Increase speed to high and blend until nuts are very finely ground. With motor running, gradually add oil and 2 Tbsp. water; blend, adding more water if too thick, until smooth. Season with salt. Dressing can be made 5 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Cashew Caesar Dressing are 4 oil-packed anchovy fillets, 1/4 cup raw cashews, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/2 cup olive oil, Kosher salt.Try this drizzled over roasted potatoes or as a sub for mayo in chicken salad.

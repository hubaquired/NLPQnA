Pear-Potato Salad recipe.  Heat the olive oil in a large sauté pan over medium heat. Add the potatoes and sauté until golden, about 12 minutes. Add the pears, onion, garlic, almonds, and curry powder and sauté for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and season with salt and pepper. Toss with the lemon juice and spinach. The ingredients of Pear-Potato Salad are 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 pound Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters or 1/2-inch pieces, 2 Bosc pears, peeled, cored, and cut into 1-inch cubes, 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced, 1 garlic clove, minced, 1/4 cup blanched almonds, roughly chopped, 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, Juice of 1 lemon, 1 cup shredded baby spinach.Editor's note: Serve this salad with Marcus Samuelsson's Maple-Glazed Tuna .

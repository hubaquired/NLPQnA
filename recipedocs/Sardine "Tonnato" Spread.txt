Sardine "Tonnato" Spread recipe.  Mash together all ingredients in a bowl with a fork until combined well. Sardine "Tonnato" Spread has 212.0 calories. Sardine "Tonnato" Spread has 13.0 grams of protein. Sardine "Tonnato" Spread has 17.0 grams of fat. Sardine "Tonnato" Spread has 353.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sardine "Tonnato" Spread are 2 (3 3/4-oz) cans brisling sardines packed in olive oil, drained, 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 2 teaspoons drained bottled capers, chopped, 1 teaspoon finely grated fresh lemon zest, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/8 teaspoon salt.This combination is inspired by the creamy sauce for vitello tonnato, but uses sardines rather than tuna for a more lively fish flavor.

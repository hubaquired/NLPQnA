Peas and Pea Tendrils with Lemon Dressing recipe. Whisk oil, lemon juice, and sugar in small bowl to blend; set dressing aside. Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Add fresh or frozen peas and cook 3 minutes. Add pea tendrils; cook 1 minute. Drain well. Return vegetables to pot; add oil-lemon dressing and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Peas and Pea Tendrils with Lemon Dressing has 130.0 calories. Peas and Pea Tendrils with Lemon Dressing has 4.0 grams of protein. Peas and Pea Tendrils with Lemon Dressing has 9.0 grams of fat. Peas and Pea Tendrils with Lemon Dressing has 32.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peas and Pea Tendrils with Lemon Dressing are 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon sugar, 2 cups shelled fresh peas or one 10-ounce package frozen petite peas, unthawed, 8 ounces pea tendrils, cut into 4-inch lengths.

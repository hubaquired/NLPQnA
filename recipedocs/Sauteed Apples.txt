Sauteed Apples recipe. Cook butter in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until beginning to brown. Add apples and sauté until tender, about 9 minutes. Add all remaining ingredients; stir to blend. Cool. Can be made 2 days ahead. Cover and chill.) Sauteed Apples has 351.0 calories. Sauteed Apples has 2.0 grams of protein. Sauteed Apples has 5.0 grams of fat. Sauteed Apples has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sauteed Apples are 3 tablespoons butter, 4 large Granny Smith apples (about 2 1/4 pounds), peeled, cored, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 2 tablespoons golden brown sugar, 1 tablespoon honey, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon grated orange peel.Try these with roast pork, as well.

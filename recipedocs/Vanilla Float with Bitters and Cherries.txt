Vanilla Float with Bitters and Cherries recipe.  Place ice cream in a chilled tall glass. Top with seltzer, bitters, and cherry brandy or kirsch, if desired, and garnish with cherries. The ingredients of Vanilla Float with Bitters and Cherries are 3 scoops vanilla ice cream, Seltzer, 1 tablespoon Angostura bitters, 1 ounce cherry brandy or kirsch (optional), Luxardo maraschino cherries (for serving).

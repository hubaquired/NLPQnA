Rosemary Mashed Potatoes recipe. Cook potatoes in large pot of boiling salted water until tender, about 40 minutes. Drain. Return potatoes to pot; mash. Combine half and half and rosemary in small saucepan; bring to simmer. Remove from heat; stir into mashed potatoes. Add butter; stir to melt. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Cover; let stand at room temperature. Before serving, stir over low heat until warm.) Rosemary Mashed Potatoes has 290.0 calories. Rosemary Mashed Potatoes has 6.0 grams of protein. Rosemary Mashed Potatoes has 14.0 grams of fat. Rosemary Mashed Potatoes has 26.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rosemary Mashed Potatoes are 4 1/2 pounds russet potatoes (about 5 large), peeled, cut into 2-inch pieces, 1 1/2 cups half and half, 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh rosemary, 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature.

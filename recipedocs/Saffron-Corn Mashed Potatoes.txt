Saffron-Corn Mashed Potatoes recipe.  Prepare perfect mashed potatoes but do not add milk). Meanwhile, heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and sauté for 2 minutes. Add fresh corn kernels garlic. Cook until corn is soft, stirring often, about 4 minutes. Add milk and saffron. Bring just to boil; remove from heat, cover, and let steep 10 minutes. Stir into potatoes. Season with salt. Saffron-Corn Mashed Potatoes has 108.0 calories. Saffron-Corn Mashed Potatoes has 2.0 grams of protein. Saffron-Corn Mashed Potatoes has 5.0 grams of fat. Saffron-Corn Mashed Potatoes has 242.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Saffron-Corn Mashed Potatoes are Perfect Mashed Potatoes, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 3/4 cup chopped onion, 1 cup fresh corn kernels (cut from 1 ear of corn), 4 minced garlic cloves, 1/2 cup milk, 1/8 teaspoon saffron threads, Salt.

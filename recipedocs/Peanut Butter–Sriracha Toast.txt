Peanut Butter–Sriracha Toast recipe.  Spread peanut butter on a slice of toast. Top with sliced scallion, fresh cilantro leaves, and Sriracha. Finish with lime juice and flaky sea salt. The ingredients of Peanut Butter–Sriracha Toast are Peanut butter, Slice of toast, Sliced scallion, Fresh cilantro leaves, Sriracha, Fresh lime juice, Flaky sea salt (such as Maldon).The fresh herbs and scallion might seem strange, but they work.

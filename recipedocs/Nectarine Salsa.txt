Nectarine Salsa recipe.  Stir together all ingredients and let stand at room temperature, stirring occasionally, 1 hour, to allow flavors to develop. Nectarine Salsa has 68.0 calories. Nectarine Salsa has 2.0 grams of protein. Nectarine Salsa has 146.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Nectarine Salsa are 3 ripe large nectarines (1 to 1 1/4 lb total), pitted and chopped, 1/2 cup chopped white onion, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh serrano chile, including seeds, 1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh cilantro, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt.We like to serve this fresh salsa with grilled fish, chicken, or pork, or as a dip for tortilla chips.

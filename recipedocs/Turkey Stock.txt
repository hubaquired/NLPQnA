Turkey Stock recipe. Break up the carcass so that it its into a 6-quart or larger pot. Add water and onion, carrot, and celery stalk. Bring to boil, skimming off any foam. Add parsley sprigs, thyme, and peppercorns. Simmer 2 to 3 hours. Strain, then skim off any fat from surface of the broth. The ingredients of Turkey Stock are Carcass of turkey, 3 quarts water, 1 onion, coarsely chopped, 1 carrot, coarsely chopped, 1 celery stalk, coarsely chopped, 6 parsley sprigs, 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme, 1/4 teaspoon whole peppercorns.

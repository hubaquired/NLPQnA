Sticky Ginger Rice with Peas recipe.  Place rice in strainer. Rinse under cold water until water runs clear. Drain well. Transfer rice to heavy medium saucepan. Add 3 cups cold water. Stir in ginger. Bring water to boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook until rice is tender, about 12 minutes. Remove from heat. Sprinkle peas over rice do not stir into rice). Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Gently stir peas into rice. Season rice lightly with salt. Transfer to bowl and serve. Sticky Ginger Rice with Peas has 196.0 calories. Sticky Ginger Rice with Peas has 5.0 grams of protein. Sticky Ginger Rice with Peas has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sticky Ginger Rice with Peas are 1 1/2 cups uncooked medium-grain white rice, 3 cups cold water, 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger, 1 cup frozen petite peas.

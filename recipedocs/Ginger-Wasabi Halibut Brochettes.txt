Ginger-Wasabi Halibut Brochettes recipe.  Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Stir mustard and wasabi in medium bowl to blend. Mix in chives, Sherry and ginger. Add fish; stir gently to coat. Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes. Brush grill rack with oil. Thread fish on skewers alternately with bell pepper squares. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Grill until fish is just opaque in center and peppers are slightly charred, turning occasionally, about 8 minutes. Ginger-Wasabi Halibut Brochettes has 188.0 calories. Ginger-Wasabi Halibut Brochettes has 28.0 grams of protein. Ginger-Wasabi Halibut Brochettes has 6.0 grams of fat. Ginger-Wasabi Halibut Brochettes has 272.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger-Wasabi Halibut Brochettes are 1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 3/4 teaspoon wasabi powder (Japanese horseradish powder) or 1 1/2 tablespoons prepared white horseradish, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, 1 1/2 tablespoons dry Sherry, 1 tablespoon minced peeled fresh ginger, 10 ounces halibut steak, cut into 1-inch pieces, Olive oil, Bamboo skewers (soaked in water 15 minutes), Red bell pepper squares.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

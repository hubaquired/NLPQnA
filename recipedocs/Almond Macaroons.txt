Almond Macaroons recipe.  Preheat oven to 350Â°F. and lightly butter a baking sheet. In a food processor pulse 1 cup almonds with granulated sugar until ground fine. Add egg white, almond extract, and a pinch salt and pulse until combined. Roll mixture into 16 balls, about 1 inch in diameter, and arrange about 2 inches apart on baking sheet. Slightly flatten balls and dust lightly with confectioners' sugar. Gently press 1 almond into each cookie. Bake macaroons in middle of oven 10 minutes, or until pale golden. Transfer macaroons to a rack and cool completely. Macaroons keep 4 days in an airtight container at room temperature. The ingredients of Almond Macaroons are 1 cup whole almonds (preferably blanched), 2/3 cup granulated sugar, 1 large egg white, 1/4 teaspoon almond extract, confectioners' sugar for dusting, about 16 whole almonds.A nice accompaniment to Blackberry and Apricot Compote.

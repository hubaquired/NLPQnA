Almond Aioli recipe. Purée almonds, oil, salt, and 1/2 cup water in a high-powered blender on high speed until thick, light, and smooth, about 3 minutes. Aioli can be made 2 days ahead. Cover and chill. Thin with water if needed. Almond Aioli has 93.0 calories. Almond Aioli has 1.0 grams of protein. Almond Aioli has 10.0 grams of fat. Almond Aioli has 119.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Almond Aioli are 1/2 cup slivered almonds, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/2 tsp kosher salt.Blanched almonds go into a blender with water and oil and come out as a luscious aioli. Because the nuts aren't toasted, the sauce doesn't actually taste very nutty. It's more decadent than yogurt, more subtle than mayo.

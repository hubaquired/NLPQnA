Corpse Reviver recipe.  Shake with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. The ingredients of Corpse Reviver are 1 ounce gin, 1/2 ounce Cointreau, 1/2 ounce Lillet Blonde, 3/4 ounce fresh lemon juice, Dash of Absente.Who cares what is in this cocktail? The name sold me when I read it in Harry Craddock's The Savoy Cocktail Book (1930). This is one of many variations.  Gin Alcoholic Cocktail Party Cocktail Lemon Triple Sec Fortified Wine Drink.

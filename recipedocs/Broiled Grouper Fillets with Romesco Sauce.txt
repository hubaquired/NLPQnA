Broiled Grouper Fillets with Romesco Sauce recipe.  Finely grind almonds, bread, garlic, and red pepper flakes in a food processor. Add roasted peppers, vinegar, and salt, then purée, adding oil in a slow stream. Season with black pepper. Preheat broiler. Put fish on a lightly oiled shallow 1-inch-deep) baking pan, skin sides down. Brush fish with oil and season with salt and pepper. Broil 3 inches from heat, without turning, until just cooked through, about 7 minutes. Broiled Grouper Fillets with Romesco Sauce has 421.0 calories. The ingredients of Broiled Grouper Fillets with Romesco Sauce are 1/3 cup whole blanched almonds, toasted, 1 slice firm white sandwich bread, crust discarded and bread torn into pieces, 2 large garlic cloves, 1/2 teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped drained bottled roasted red peppers, 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 4 (6-ounce) pieces grouper fillet, 1 to 2 tablespoons olive oil.With boiled potatoes and grilled vegetables, you have an easy dinner.

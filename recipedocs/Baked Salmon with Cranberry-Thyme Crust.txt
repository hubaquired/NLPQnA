Baked Salmon with Cranberry-Thyme Crust recipe. Preheat oven to 375°F. Oil baking sheet. Sprinkle salmon with salt and pepper. Place skin side down on sheet. Combine panko, cranberries, onion, 2 tablespoons melted butter, thyme, and lemon peel in medium bowl; blend well. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon onto salmon, dividing equally. Press to adhere. Drizzle with remaining melted butter. Bake until topping is golden and salmon is just opaque in center, about 20 minutes. Baked Salmon with Cranberry-Thyme Crust has 287.0 calories. Baked Salmon with Cranberry-Thyme Crust has 13.0 grams of protein. Baked Salmon with Cranberry-Thyme Crust has 17.0 grams of fat. Baked Salmon with Cranberry-Thyme Crust has 180.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Salmon with Cranberry-Thyme Crust are 4 6- to 7-ounce salmon fillets, 3/4 cup panko (Japanese breadcrumbs) or fresh breadcrumbs, 1/4 cup dried cranberries, chopped, 1/4 cup chopped green onion, 3 tablespoons butter, melted, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel.Accompany this colorful dish with rice pilaf and baby peas with pearl onions.

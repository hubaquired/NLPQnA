Poppy Seed Crackers recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Stir together egg, water, and salt. Brush each tortilla on 1 side with egg wash, then, holding over a bowl, sprinkle with poppy seeds to cover completely. Cut out 7 or 8 stars from each coated tortilla you’ll have to press hard; use a pot holder to protect your hands), transferring to 2 baking sheets, seeded sides up. Bake, 1 sheet at a time, in middle of oven until crisp, about 15 minutes. Cool crackers on a rack. Poppy Seed Crackers has 27.0 calories. Poppy Seed Crackers has 1.0 grams of protein. Poppy Seed Crackers has 1.0 grams of fat. Poppy Seed Crackers has 50.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Poppy Seed Crackers are 1 large egg, 2 tablespoons cold water, 1 teaspoon salt, 8 (6- to 7-inch) flour tortillas, 1/2 cup poppy seeds (2.6-oz jar), Special equipment: a 2-inch star cookie cutter.We loved L.A. chef Mary Sue Milliken's crisp baked-tortilla crackers in her pool-party menu (August 2000).

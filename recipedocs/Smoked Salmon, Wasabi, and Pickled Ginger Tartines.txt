Smoked Salmon, Wasabi, and Pickled Ginger Tartines recipe. Cut cucumber into thin rounds using slicer. Stir together wasabi and cream cheese. Spread mixture on baguette, then make open-face sandwiches with cucumber, salmon, and ginger. The ingredients of Smoked Salmon, Wasabi, and Pickled Ginger Tartines are 1/2 seedless cucumber, 1 tablespoon wasabi paste (from a tube), 4 ounces cream cheese, softened, 1 (24-inch) baguette, quartered crosswise, then each quarter halved horizontally and lightly toasted, 1/2 pound thinly sliced smoked salmon, 1/4 cup drained bottled pickled ginger, an adjustable-blade slicer, lemon wedges.

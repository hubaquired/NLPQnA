Oysters with Finger Lime and Ginger Mignonette recipe. Combine lime juice, oil, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger, and sugar in a small bowl. Scrape out pearls from finger lime and stir into vinaigrette. Spoon over oysters just before serving. Oysters with Finger Lime and Ginger Mignonette has 109.0 calories. Oysters with Finger Lime and Ginger Mignonette has 10.0 grams of protein. Oysters with Finger Lime and Ginger Mignonette has 5.0 grams of fat. Oysters with Finger Lime and Ginger Mignonette has 155.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Oysters with Finger Lime and Ginger Mignonette are 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 tablespoon rice bran oil, 1 tablespoon unseasoned rice vinegar, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 1/2 teaspoon finely grated peeled ginger, Small pinch of sugar, 1 finger lime, halved lengthwise, or 1/2 lime, white pith and peel removed, finely chopped, 12 oysters, freshly shucked.In place of vinegar, finger limes give this mignonette its zing (pulp from regular limes works just as well).

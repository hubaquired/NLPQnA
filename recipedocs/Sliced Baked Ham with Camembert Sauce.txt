Sliced Baked Ham with Camembert Sauce recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F with rack in middle. Overlap ham slices on a baking sheet and cover with foil. Bake until just heated through, 8 to 10 minutes. Bring cream to a simmer, then whisk in cheese until smooth. If necessary, purée in a blender, then reheat.) Whisk in yolk and cook, whisking, 1 minute. Serve ham with sauce. The ingredients of Sliced Baked Ham with Camembert Sauce are 1 1/2 pounds thick-sliced good-quality baked ham such as Virginia, 1/2 cup heavy cream, 1 firm-ripe Camembert, chilled, rind discarded and cheese coarsely chopped, 1 large egg yolk, Broccolini or green beans.The French know how to take the proverbial ham-and-cheese combo and turn it into something special.

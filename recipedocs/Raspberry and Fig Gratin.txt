Raspberry and Fig Gratin recipe.  Preheat broiler. Arrange raspberries and figs in 10-inch-diameter broilerproof baking dish. Stir sour cream until smooth; spoon evenly over fruit. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Broil close to heat source until brown sugar melts and bubbles, about 4 minutes. Garnish with mint and serve warm. Raspberry and Fig Gratin has 235.0 calories. Raspberry and Fig Gratin has 2.0 grams of protein. Raspberry and Fig Gratin has 11.0 grams of fat. Raspberry and Fig Gratin has 33.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Raspberry and Fig Gratin are 2 baskets fresh raspberries, 6 fresh figs, quartered, 8 ounces sour cream, 1/2 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar, Mint sprigs.

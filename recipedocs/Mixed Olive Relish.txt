Mixed Olive Relish recipe.  Working with several brine-cured olives at a time and using flat side of a large heavy knife, press olives to crush them and remove pits. Coarsely chop all olives and in a bowl toss with onion, oil, lemon juice, and capers. Relish may be prepared up to this point 2 days ahead and chilled, covered. Bring relish to room temperature before serving. Stir in parsley. Mixed Olive Relish has 200.0 calories. Mixed Olive Relish has 12.0 grams of protein. Mixed Olive Relish has 15.0 grams of fat. Mixed Olive Relish has 767.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mixed Olive Relish are 2 cups mixed brine-cured black and green olives, 1/2 cup drained pimiento-stuffed green olives (about 3 ounces), 1/3 cup finely chopped red onion, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, or to taste, 1 tablespoon drained capers, chopped, 1/3 cup finely chopped fresh flat-leafed parsley leaves.

Watermelon-Honeydew Kebabs with Lime-Poppy Seed Dressing recipe.  Whisk first 4 ingredients in medium bowl. Gradually whisk in oil. Using large melon baller, scoop out 72 balls of honeydew. Using 1 1/4-inch round fluted cookie cutter, cut out 48 rounds from watermelon slices. Alternate 3 honeydew balls and 2 watermelon rounds on each skewer. Can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover dressing and kebabs separately; chill.) Serve kebabs with dressing. The ingredients of Watermelon-Honeydew Kebabs with Lime-Poppy Seed Dressing are 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons poppy seeds, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 2 3-pound honeydew melons, halved, seeded, 1 small seedless watermelon, cut into 3/4-inch-thick slices, 24 6-inch bamboo skewers.Most kids will like the dressing, but you might want to put it on the side just in case. Serve pink lemonade to the kids; the grown-ups might prefer iced tea or sangria.

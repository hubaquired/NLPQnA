Potato and Parmesan Gratin recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Butter 13 x 9 x 2-inch glass baking dish. Layer 1/3 of potatoes evenly in prepared dish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Drizzle with 1/3 of melted butter. Top with 1/3 of Parmesan cheese. Repeat layering twice more with remaining potatoes, butter and Parmesan cheese. Pour milk evenly over. Bake potatoes 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350°F. Continue to bake until potatoes are tender, top is golden brown and most of milk is absorbed, about 1 hour longer. Remove from oven; let stand 10 minutes and serve. Potato and Parmesan Gratin has 696.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Potato and Parmesan Gratin are 2 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled, cut into 1/8-inch-thick slices, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, melted, 2 cups (packed) freshly grated Parmesan cheese (about 10 ounces), 2 cups whole milk.(TORTINO DI PATATE ALLA PARMIGIANA).

Chorizo-Lemon Butter recipe. Put the softened butter in a bowl. In a small saucepan, cook the chorizo over moderate heat until the fat just starts to render, about 2 minutes. Scrape the chorizo and fat into the butter. Add the lemon zest, lemon juice and parsley and use a fork to mix together well. Season with salt, if desired, and serve. The chorizo-lemon butter can be refrigerated for up to 2 days. Bring to room temperature before serving. Chorizo-Lemon Butter has 204.0 calories. Chorizo-Lemon Butter has 4.0 grams of protein. Chorizo-Lemon Butter has 21.0 grams of fat. Chorizo-Lemon Butter has 244.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chorizo-Lemon Butter are 3 ounces Spanish chorizo, finely chopped, 1 stick of butter, softened, Zest and juice of 1 lemon, 1/2 cup flat-leaf parsley leaves, coarsely chopped, Salt.

Onion and Spinach Dip recipe.  Heat oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking, then saut√© onion and shallots, stirring, until lightly browned, about 2 minutes. Reduce heat to moderate and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 8 minutes more. Add spinach and cook, stirring, just until wilted, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Transfer spinach mixture to a bowl, then stir in cream cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper until combined well. Chill, covered, at least 1 hour. The ingredients of Onion and Spinach Dip are 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 large onion, chopped (1 3/4 cups), 1/2 lb shallots, chopped, 1/2 lb baby spinach, coarsely chopped, 3 ounce cream cheese, softened, 1 (16-ounce) container sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper.

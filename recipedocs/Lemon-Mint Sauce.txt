Lemon-Mint Sauce recipe. Whisk garlic, oil, parsley, mint, and lemon juice in a medium bowl; season with salt and pepper. Spoon half of sauce onto a platter. Place eggplants on top, season with salt, and spoon remaining sauce over. Let sit 30 minutes to let flavors meld. DO AHEAD: Lemon-Mint Sauce can be made 2 hours ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Lemon-Mint Sauce are 1 small garlic clove, finely chopped, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, Coal-Roasted Eggplants.This bright vinaigrette is good for a lot more than just grilled eggplant.

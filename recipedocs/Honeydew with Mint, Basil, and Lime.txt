Honeydew with Mint, Basil, and Lime recipe. In a bowl combine all ingredients and chill. The ingredients of Honeydew with Mint, Basil, and Lime are 3 cups 1-inch cubes honeydew melon (about 1/2 melon), 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 tablespoon fresh opal basil leaves or chopped fresh basil leaves, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves, sugar to taste if desired.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

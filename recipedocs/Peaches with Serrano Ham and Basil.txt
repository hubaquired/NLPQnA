Peaches with Serrano Ham and Basil recipe.  Toss together peaches, sugar, vinegar, and cumin and let stand 10 minutes. Cut ham slices in half lengthwise, then wrap each piece around a wedge of peach. Top with a basil leaf and secure with a wooden or metal pick. Peaches with Serrano Ham and Basil has 15.0 calories. Peaches with Serrano Ham and Basil has 1.0 grams of protein. Peaches with Serrano Ham and Basil has 54.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peaches with Serrano Ham and Basil are 3 peaches, each cut into 8 wedges, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon Sherry vinegar, 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/4 pound thinly sliced serrrano ham, 24 small basil leaves.

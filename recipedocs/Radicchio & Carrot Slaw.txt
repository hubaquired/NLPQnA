Radicchio & Carrot Slaw recipe.  In a large mixing bowl, toss all the ingredients together. Serve chilled. Radicchio & Carrot Slaw has 160.0 calories. Radicchio & Carrot Slaw has 1.0 grams of protein. Radicchio & Carrot Slaw has 15.0 grams of fat. Radicchio & Carrot Slaw has 374.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Radicchio & Carrot Slaw are 2 heads radicchio, cut in half, then sliced thinly, 2/3 cup mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 teaspoon dried fennel seed, 3/4 cup roughly grated or julienned carrots, 1/3 cup white wine vinegar, Salt and pepper.This wonderful slaw is a great twist on classic cole slaw. If you like your slaw crunchier, make it just before the party.

Apple and Raisin Slaw recipe. Combine cabbages, carrots, apple, raisins and sunflower seeds in very large bowl. Whisk yogurt, dill, vegetable oil and vinegar in medium bowl to blend. Add dressing to cabbage mixture and toss to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Apple and Raisin Slaw has 102.0 calories. Apple and Raisin Slaw has 4.0 grams of protein. Apple and Raisin Slaw has 4.0 grams of fat. Apple and Raisin Slaw has 48.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apple and Raisin Slaw are 5 cups coarsely chopped red cabbage (about 1/2 medium head), 5 cups coarsely chopped green cabbage (about 1/2 medium head), 1 cup coarsely grated carrots (about 2 medium), 1 large tart green apple (such as Granny Smith), cored, coarsely chopped, 1/2 cup raisins, 1/2 cup raw unsalted sunflower seeds, toasted, 1 1/2 cups plain nonfat yogurt, 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill or 3 tablespoons dried dillweed, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar.An interesting and slightly sweet salad that's great with sandwiches, burgers and grilled chicken.

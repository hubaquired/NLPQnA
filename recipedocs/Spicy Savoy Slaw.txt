Spicy Savoy Slaw recipe.  Whisk together vinegar, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until sugar and salt are dissolved, then whisk in chile, ginger, and sesame oil. Add remaining ingredients and toss well. Let stand, uncovered, at room temperature, tossing occasionally, until wilted, about 30 minutes. Spicy Savoy Slaw has 92.0 calories. Spicy Savoy Slaw has 4.0 grams of protein. Spicy Savoy Slaw has 2.0 grams of fat. Spicy Savoy Slaw has 341.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Savoy Slaw are 5 tablespoons seasoned rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 to 2 teaspoons minced fresh serrano chile (including seeds), 1 teaspoon finely grated peeled fresh ginger, 1 1/2 teaspoons Asian sesame oil, 1 1/2 pounds Savoy cabbage, thinly sliced (6 cups), 1/4 pound snow peas, trimmed and very thinly sliced lengthwise (1 cup), 1/4 red bell pepper, cut into thin matchsticks.

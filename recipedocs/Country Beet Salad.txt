Country Beet Salad recipe.  Whisk oil and vinegar in small bowl to blend. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Place beets in medium bowl; mix in 2 tablespoons dressing. Sauté bacon in heavy large skillet over medium heat until crisp. Using slotted spoon, transfer bacon to paper towels and drain. Whisk 1 tablespoon bacon drippings into dressing. Add bread to remaining drippings in skillet and sauté until crisp, about 6 minutes. Place croutons in large bowl. Add greens, eggs, pine nuts, beets and bacon. Toss with enough dressing to coat. The ingredients of Country Beet Salad are 1/2 cup olive oil, 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar, 1 15-ounce can baby beets, drained, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 4 slices bacon, chopped, 4 cups 1-inch cubes French bread, 1 4 1/2-ounce package mixed greens, 2 hard-boiled eggs, shelled, diced, 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted.

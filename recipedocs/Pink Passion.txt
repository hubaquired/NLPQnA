Pink Passion recipe.  Stir together juice, Grand Marnier, and vodka in a 2-cup measure, then pour into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well, then strain into 2 Champagne flutes. Add 1 teaspoon grenadine to each cocktail grenadine will sink to bottom of glass). Pink Passion has 152.0 calories. Pink Passion has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pink Passion are 1/2 cup passion-fruit juice, 1/4 cup Grand Marnier, 2 tablespoons vodka, 2 teaspoons grenadine, chilled, Special equipment: a cocktail shaker.

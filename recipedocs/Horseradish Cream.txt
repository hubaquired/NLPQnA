Horseradish Cream recipe.  Mix sour cream, horseradish, green onions, parsley, and lemon juice in small bowl. Season with coarse salt and pepper. Cover and chill at least 1 hour. The ingredients of Horseradish Cream are 1 1/2 cups sour cream (about 12 ounces), 1/2 cup prepared white horseradish (about one 5-ounce jar), drained, 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, Coarse kosher salt.

Bitter Orange Kindler recipe.  In a tall glass, combine triple sec and Campari with 3 cubes of ice. Fill the glass with chilled quinine water and stir to blend. Garnish with a strip of orange peel. Bitter Orange Kindler has 305.0 calories. Bitter Orange Kindler has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bitter Orange Kindler are 2 ounces triple sec, 2 ounces Campari, 3 cubes of ice, Chilled quinine water, Garnish: a strip of orange peel.

Tomato, Caper and Olive Vinaigrette recipe.  Whisk together lemon juice and mustard. Add oil in a slow stream, whisking until emulsified. Stir in tomatoes, olives, capers, and salt and pepper to taste. Tomato, Caper and Olive Vinaigrette has 397.0 calories. Tomato, Caper and Olive Vinaigrette has 1.0 grams of protein. Tomato, Caper and Olive Vinaigrette has 43.0 grams of fat. Tomato, Caper and Olive Vinaigrette has 279.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato, Caper and Olive Vinaigrette are 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered, 2/3 cup Kalamata or other brine-cured black olives, pitted and halved, 2 tablespoons drained capers, rinsed.

Mint Caipirinha Ice Pops recipe. Purée all ingredients in a blender until smooth. Skim off any foam, then pour into molds. Freeze 30 minutes. Insert sticks, then freeze until firm, about 24 hours. The ingredients of Mint Caipirinha Ice Pops are 1 cup mint leaves, 3/4 cup fresh lime juice, 1 cup water, 3/4 cup superfine granulated sugar, 1/3 cup cachaça, Equipment: 8 (1/3-cup) ice pop molds and 8 wooden sticks.Inspired by Brazil's national drink, these boozy popsicles are made with cachaça, lime juice, and mint.

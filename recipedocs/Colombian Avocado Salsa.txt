Colombian Avocado Salsa recipe. Purée chiles, vinegar, and 1 teaspoon salt in a blender. Transfer to a bowl and stir in remaining ingredients. Colombian Avocado Salsa has 150.0 calories. Colombian Avocado Salsa has 4.0 grams of protein. Colombian Avocado Salsa has 11.0 grams of fat. Colombian Avocado Salsa has 34.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Colombian Avocado Salsa are 1/3 cup chopped fresh ají dulce chiles (seeded if desired), 1 1/2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh habanero chile with seeds, 6 tablespoons distilled white vinegar, 2 hard-boiled large eggs, coarsely chopped, 2 (6- to 8-ounce) firm-ripe avocados, coarsely mashed (1 1/2 cups), 1 cup chopped tomato, 1 cup chopped white onion, 3/4 cup chopped cilantro.

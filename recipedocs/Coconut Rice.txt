Coconut Rice recipe.  Rinse rice in a large bowl with cool water until water runs clear. Drain rice. Combine rice, coconut cream, sugar, salt, and 2 cups water in a medium saucepan. Bring just to a boil, stirring to dissolve sugar, then cover and reduce heat to low. Alternatively, cook rice in an electric rice steamer.) Cook until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, 40-45 minutes. Fluff rice with a fork; cover and let sit for 20 minutes. Coconut Rice has 369.0 calories. Coconut Rice has 6.0 grams of protein. Coconut Rice has 14.0 grams of fat. Coconut Rice has 323.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coconut Rice are 2 cups jasmine rice, 1 cup coconut cream, 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar, 1 teaspoon kosher salt.If you can only find regular coconut milk, buy 2 cans and use the thick cream that's floating at the top. The coconut cream will caramelize during cooking, leaving sweet brown flecks in the rice.

The ingredients of Bow Thai Pasta with Shrimp are 3 large garlic cloves, crushed, 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger, 1 bunch fresh cilantro, stemmed (about 2 cups), 1/4 cup dry-roasted peanuts, 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper, 1/2 cup peanut oil, 8 ounces bow tie pasta, 12 ounces cooked peeled deveined medium shrimp, 4 green onions, chopped (including green tops), 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice.Bow tie-shaped pasta is mixed with Thai-flavored pesto sauce and tossed with cooked shrimp for a fast, colorful entrée.

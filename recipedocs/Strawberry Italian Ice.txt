Strawberry Italian Ice recipe.  Put a 9-or-10 inch metal cake pan in the freezer. In a blender blend the strawberries, the sugar, and the lemon juice until the mixture is smooth and the sugar is dissolved. Add the ice cubes, blend the mixture until it is smooth, and pour it into the cold pan. Freeze the mixture for 30 to 40 minutes, or until it is frozen around the edge but still soft in the center, stir the strawberry ice, mashing the frozen parts with a fork, and spoon it into 2 bowls. Strawberry Italian Ice has 144.0 calories. Strawberry Italian Ice has 1.0 grams of protein. Strawberry Italian Ice has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Italian Ice are 1 pint fresh strawberries, trimmed, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 2 cups ice cubes (about 11).Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

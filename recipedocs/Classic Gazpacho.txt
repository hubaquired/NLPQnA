Classic Gazpacho recipe. Cut 1 tomato, 1/2 cucumber and 1/2 onion into 1-inch pieces and transfer to processor. Add bell pepper and puree. Transfer to bowl. Add tomato juice, cilantro, vinegar, oil and hot pepper sauce. Seed remaining tomato. Dice remaining tomato and cucumber and onion halves and add to soup. Season with salt and pepper. Refrigerate. Can be prepared 2 days ahead.) Serve well chilled. Classic Gazpacho has 138.0 calories. Classic Gazpacho has 2.0 grams of protein. Classic Gazpacho has 10.0 grams of fat. Classic Gazpacho has 24.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Classic Gazpacho are 2 large tomatoes (about 1 pound), 1 large cucumber, peeled, halved lengthwise, seeded, 1 medium onion, 1 large roasted red bell pepper (available in jars), 3 cups tomato juice, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1/3 cup red wine vinegar, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco).

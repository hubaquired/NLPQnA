Quick Preserved Lemon Zest recipe. Remove the zest from the lemons with a vegetable peeler be careful not to take off too much of the white pith) and place in a small bowl. Toss with the salt, sugar, bay leaves, chili, and the juice of one of the lemons. Refrigerate for 2 hours. Remove the zest from the mixture, rinse, pat dry, and cover with extra-virgin olive oil to store in the refrigerator for up to a month. The ingredients of Quick Preserved Lemon Zest are 4 lemons, scrubbed, 1/4 cup kosher salt, 1/4 cup sugar, 4 bay leaves, coarsely chopped or crushed, 1 dried chili pod, split open and coarsely chopped, Extra-virgin olive oil to cover.Editor's note: The recipe below is from Govind Armstrong's Small Bites, Big Nights.

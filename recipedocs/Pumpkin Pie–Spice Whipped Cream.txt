Pumpkin Pie–Spice Whipped Cream recipe.  Using an electric mixer on medium-low speed or a whisk, beat cream, powdered sugar, ginger, nutmeg, salt, and 1/4 tsp. cinnamon in a large bowl until medium peaks form. Transfer whipped cream to a serving bowl and top with more cinnamon. Cream can be made 2 hours ahead; cover and chill. Pumpkin Pie–Spice Whipped Cream has 223.0 calories. Pumpkin Pie–Spice Whipped Cream has 1.0 grams of protein. Pumpkin Pie–Spice Whipped Cream has 22.0 grams of fat. Pumpkin Pie–Spice Whipped Cream has 53.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pumpkin Pie–Spice Whipped Cream are 2 cups heavy cream, 2 tablespoons powdered sugar, 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg, Pinch of kosher salt, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, plus more for serving.Can’t get enough of pumpkin pie spice?

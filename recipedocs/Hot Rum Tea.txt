Hot Rum Tea recipe. In a glass or china mug, combine the lemon juice, rum, and the lemon peel. Fill the glass with tea, stir the drink with the cinnamon stick, and serve it immediately. The ingredients of Hot Rum Tea are 1 teaspoon lemon juice, 2 ounces rum, Slice of lemon peel studded with 2 cloves, Hot tea, Cinnamon stick.

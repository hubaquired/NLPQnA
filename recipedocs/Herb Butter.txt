Herb Butter recipe.  In a small bowl cream together the butter, the chives, the parsley, the tarragon, the mustard, and salt and pepper to taste. Let the butter stand, covered, in a cool place for 1 hour to allow the flavors to develop. Serve the butter with seafood, chicken, or meat. Herb Butter has 206.0 calories. Herb Butter has 23.0 grams of fat. Herb Butter has 13.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Herb Butter are 1 1/2 sticks (3/4 cup) unsalted butter, softened, 2 tablespoons minced fresh chives, 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley leaves, 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon leaves, 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

Roasted Red Pepper Aïoli recipe.  Combine the first 4 ingredients in a food processor. Blend for approximately 30 seconds and, while blending, drizzle the grape-seed oil into the mixture in a slow stream, followed by the olive oil. Season with the salt and pepper and keep cold until serving. Roasted Red Pepper Aïoli has 263.0 calories. Roasted Red Pepper Aïoli has 1.0 grams of protein. Roasted Red Pepper Aïoli has 28.0 grams of fat. Roasted Red Pepper Aïoli has 49.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Red Pepper Aïoli are 2 large egg yolks, 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 2 teaspoons lemon juice, 6 ounces roasted and peeled red peppers, 1/2 cup grape-seed oil, 1/2 cup olive oil, Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Honeydew-Lime Pops recipe. Purée all ingredients and 1/4 cup water in a food processor until smooth. Set a fine-mesh strainer over a medium pitcher; strain, pressing on solids to extract purée. Divide among molds. Cover; insert ice-pop sticks. Freeze until firm. Dip bottoms of molds into hot water for 20-30 seconds to loosen pops. Remove pops from molds and serve. Honeydew-Lime Pops has 179.0 calories. Honeydew-Lime Pops has 1.0 grams of protein. Honeydew-Lime Pops has 32.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honeydew-Lime Pops are 4 cups 1" cubes peeled honeydew melon, 1/3 cup fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup mild honey, 1/4 cup sugar.

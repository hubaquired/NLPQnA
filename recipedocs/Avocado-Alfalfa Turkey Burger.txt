Avocado-Alfalfa Turkey Burger recipe.  In a bowl, combine ketchup and molasses; season with salt and pepper. In another bowl, crush avocado with juice; season with salt. Form turkey into four patties. Coat a grill pan or grill rack with cooking spray. Grill patties over medium-high heat until internal temperature reaches 165Â°F, 20 minutes. Top each with 1 cheese slice. Divide ketchup mixture, burgers, tomatoes, avocado mixture and alfalfa sprouts among buns. The ingredients of Avocado-Alfalfa Turkey Burger are 1/4 cup ketchup, 1 1/2 tablespoons pomegranate molasses (available at Whole Foods), 1 Hass avocado, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 pound ground turkey (97 percent lean), Vegetable-oil cooking spray, 4 slices Monterey Jack cheese, 2 small vine-ripened tomatoes, sliced, 4 ounces alfalfa sprouts, 4 whole-wheat hamburger buns, toasted.Tart, fruity pomegranate molasses brightens up the mellow turkey.

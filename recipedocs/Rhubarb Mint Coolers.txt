Rhubarb Mint Coolers recipe.  Cut rhubarb into 1/4-inch pieces and, in a saucepan, bring to a boil with water, sugar, and mint leaves. Simmer mixture, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes rhubarb will disintegrate) and cool 15 minutes. Pour mixture through a fine sieve into a pitcher, pressing hard on solids. Chill mixture, covered, until cold, about 3 hours, and up to 2 days. Serve coolers over ice in glasses, garnished with mint sprigs. Rhubarb Mint Coolers has 220.0 calories. Rhubarb Mint Coolers has 1.0 grams of protein. Rhubarb Mint Coolers has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rhubarb Mint Coolers are 1 pound trimmed rhubarb, 5 cups water, 1 cup sugar, 1/4 cup packed fresh mint leaves, Garnish: fresh mint sprigs.

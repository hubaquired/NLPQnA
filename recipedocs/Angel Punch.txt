Angel Punch recipe.  Combine all ingredients except soda, and refrigerate for an hour or two. Pour over ice in a punch bowl and add the soda. Serve in 4-ounce punch glasses. Angel Punch has 50.0 calories. Angel Punch has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Angel Punch are 1 cup sugar syrup, 1 pint lemon juice, 1 quart strong green tea, 2 quarts white grape juice, 1 block ice, 2 quarts chilled club soda.

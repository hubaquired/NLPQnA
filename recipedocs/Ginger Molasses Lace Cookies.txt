Ginger Molasses Lace Cookies recipe.  In a 1- to 1 1/2-quart heavy saucepan bring sugar, butter, molasses, and lemon juice to a boil over moderate heat, stirring, and boil 1 minute. Remove pan from heat and stir in flour, ginger, and a pinch salt until mixture is smooth. Cool dough to room temperature. Form and bake cookies as directed below. Preheat oven to 350Â°F and line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper. Roll level 1/2-teaspoons dough into balls and arrange 4 inches apart on baking sheets. Bake cookies in batches in upper and lower thirds of oven, switching position of sheets halfway through baking, 10 minutes, or until cookies are flat and golden. Transfer parchment with cookies to racks to cool. Cool baking sheets and line with fresh parchment between batches. Ginger Molasses Lace Cookies has 22.0 calories. The ingredients of Ginger Molasses Lace Cookies are 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar, 3/4 stick (6 tablespoons) unsalted butter, 2 tablespoons unsulfured molasses, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1/3 cup all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon ground ginger.

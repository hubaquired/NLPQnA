The Elvis recipe.  Combine grapefruit juice, gin, and liqueur in a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice and shake until outside of shaker is frosty, about 30 seconds. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice and top off with ale. Garnish with grapefruit twist. The Elvis has 168.0 calories. The Elvis has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of The Elvis are 3 ounces fresh pink grapefruit juice, 1 1/2 ounces London dry gin, 1/4 ounce St-Germain (elderflower liqueur), India Pale Ale (for serving), Grapefruit twist (for serving).Bitter notes from grapefruit and ale balance the floral sweetness of the gin and liqueur—one sip and it will all make sense.

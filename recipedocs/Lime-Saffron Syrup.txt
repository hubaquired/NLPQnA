Lime-Saffron Syrup recipe.  Combine the water, lime juice, sugar, lime zest, saffron threads, and cardamom pods in a medium saucepan over high heat. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 7 minutes, which is just long enough for the flavors to fuse. Remove from the heat and let cool. Lime-Saffron Syrup has 199.0 calories. Lime-Saffron Syrup has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lime-Saffron Syrup are 3/4 cup water, 1/4 cup lime juice, 1 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon grated lime zest, 5 saffron threads, 5 cardamom pods, crushed.

Spicy-Sour Dressing recipe. Heat grapeseed oil in heavy medium skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and sauté until golden, about 2 minutes. Add carrot and bell pepper; sauté 2 minutes. Add vinegar, lime juice, and sugar. Bring to boil, stirring until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat and cool. Transfer dressing to blender; add chili oil. Blend until smooth. Season to taste with salt. Transfer to bowl. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill.) *Available in the Asian foods section of some supermarkets and at Asian markets. Spicy-Sour Dressing has 226.0 calories. Spicy-Sour Dressing has 1.0 grams of protein. Spicy-Sour Dressing has 14.0 grams of fat. Spicy-Sour Dressing has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy-Sour Dressing are 1 tablespoon grapeseed oil or vegetable oil, 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced, 1/4 cup 1/8-inch cubes peeled carrot, 1/4 cup 1/8-inch cubes red bell pepper, 7 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar, 5 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup (packed) golden brown sugar, 1 tablespoon chili oil*.

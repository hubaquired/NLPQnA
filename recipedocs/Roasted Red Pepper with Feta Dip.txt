Roasted Red Pepper with Feta Dip recipe. Char peppers directly over gas flame or in broiler until blackened on all sides. Enclose in paper bag 10 minutes. Peel, seed, and quarter peppers. Puree peppers, oil, garlic, and cheese in processor. Season with salt and pepper. Chill at least 3 hours or overnight. Roasted Red Pepper with Feta Dip has 204.0 calories. Roasted Red Pepper with Feta Dip has 7.0 grams of protein. Roasted Red Pepper with Feta Dip has 17.0 grams of fat. Roasted Red Pepper with Feta Dip has 396.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Red Pepper with Feta Dip are 4 red bell peppers, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 garlic cloves, peeled, 2 cups crumbled feta cheese.Crudités and toasted pita triangles are good dippers for this Greek mezede.

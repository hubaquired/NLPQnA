Peppercorn, Mustard and Dill Vinegar recipe.  Place first 5 ingredients in 1-quart jar. Add bay leaves, lemon slices and dill sprigs. Place lid on jar; seal. Store in cool dry place at least 1 and up to 6 weeks. Peppercorn, Mustard and Dill Vinegar has 139.0 calories. Peppercorn, Mustard and Dill Vinegar has 3.0 grams of protein. Peppercorn, Mustard and Dill Vinegar has 4.0 grams of fat. Peppercorn, Mustard and Dill Vinegar has 12.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peppercorn, Mustard and Dill Vinegar are 3 cups white wine vinegar, 3 tablespoons yellow mustard seeds, 1 tablespoon whole allspice, 1 tablespoon white peppercorns, 1 teaspoon dill seeds, 3 bay leaves, 2 lemon slices, halved, 1 medium bunch fresh dill sprigs.Typical Danish ingredients are featured in this easy-to-make condiment; it would be wonderful incorporated into a vinaigrette for cold poached salmon.

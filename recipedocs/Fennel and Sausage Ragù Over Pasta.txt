Fennel and Sausage Ragù Over Pasta recipe.  Trim stems from fennel bulb and cut away any brown spots from outer layer. Chop and reserve 2 tablespoons fronds and chop bulb. Sauté fennel bulb and onion in oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderately high heat, stirring, until beginning to brown. Add sausage and cook, stirring and breaking up lumps with a fork, until no longer pink. Add wine and simmer until reduced by about half, then add marinara sauce and simmer, stirring frequently, until vegetables are tender and sauce is thickened, about 10 minutes. While sauce simmers, cook pasta in a pot of boiling salted water until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and toss with sauce. Sprinkle with reserved fennel fronds. The ingredients of Fennel and Sausage Ragù Over Pasta are 1 fennel bulb (sometimes called anise) with fronds, 1 medium onion, chopped, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 lb sweet Italian sausage, casings discarded, 1/2 cup dry white wine, 2 cups prepared marinara sauce, 1 lb rotini, fusilli, or other spiral pasta.This ragù would also be excellent served over instant polenta instead of pasta.

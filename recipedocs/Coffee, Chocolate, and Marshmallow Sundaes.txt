Coffee, Chocolate, and Marshmallow Sundaes recipe.  Bring whipping cream and 1/2 cup marshmallow creme to simmer in small saucepan. Remove from heat. Add chocolate and whisk until melted and smooth. Cool marshmallow sauce at least 10 minutes and up to 1 hour. Place 1 scoop of ice cream in each bowl. Pour marshmallow sauce over. Sprinkle sundaes with almonds, top with dollop of marshmallow creme, and serve. Coffee, Chocolate, and Marshmallow Sundaes has 906.0 calories. Coffee, Chocolate, and Marshmallow Sundaes has 11.0 grams of protein. Coffee, Chocolate, and Marshmallow Sundaes has 63.0 grams of fat. Coffee, Chocolate, and Marshmallow Sundaes has 100.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coffee, Chocolate, and Marshmallow Sundaes are 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1/2 cup marshmallow creme, plus more for topping, 6 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) or semisweet chocolate, chopped, 1 1/2 pints (about) coffee ice cream with almonds and chocolate, 2/3 cup sliced almonds, toasted.

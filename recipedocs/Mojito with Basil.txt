Mojito with Basil recipe.  In a tall glass with back of a spoon crush mint and basil with sugar and lime juice until sugar is dissolved and stir in rum. Add ice cubes and top off drink with club soda or seltzer water. Stir drink well and garnish with mint, basil, and lime. The ingredients of Mojito with Basil are 2 fresh mint sprigs, chopped, 2 fresh basil sprigs, chopped, 1 tablespoon sugar, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 1/2 ounces (3 tablespoons) light rum, Chilled club soda or seltzer water.(Rum, Lime, Mint and Basil Cocktail)Garnishes: fresh mint and basil sprigs and lime slices.

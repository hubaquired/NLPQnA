Caramelized Apples recipe.  Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat; sprinkle sugar over. Stir until sugar begins to melt, about 1 minute. Add apples. Sauté until apples are brown and tender and juices form, about 10 minutes. Add cream and simmer until sauce thickens slightly, about 2 minutes. Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. Rewarm apples over medium heat.) Caramelized Apples has 405.0 calories. Caramelized Apples has 1.0 grams of protein. Caramelized Apples has 24.0 grams of fat. Caramelized Apples has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Caramelized Apples are 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 pounds Golden Delicious apples, peeled, cored, cut into 1/2-inch cubes, 2/3 cup whipping cream.

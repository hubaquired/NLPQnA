Garlicky Broccoli Rabe recipe. Blanch broccoli rabe in an 8-quart pot of boiling salted water , uncovered, until tender, about 6 minutes. Immediately transfer with tongs to a large bowl of ice and cold water to stop cooking. Drain in a colander, pressing to extract excess water. Transfer to a cutting board and chop. Cook garlic in oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until just golden, then add broccoli rabe and cook, stirring frequently, until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Garlicky Broccoli Rabe has 147.0 calories. Garlicky Broccoli Rabe has 5.0 grams of protein. Garlicky Broccoli Rabe has 13.0 grams of fat.

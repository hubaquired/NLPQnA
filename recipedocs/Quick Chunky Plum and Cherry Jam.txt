Quick Chunky Plum and Cherry Jam recipe. Mix plums, cherries, sugar, and allspice in heavy large saucepan. Cover and cook over low heat until sugar dissolves and juices form, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes. Uncover and increase heat to medium. Cook until plums start to fall apart and preserves are thickened and reduced to 2 1/4 cups, stirring occasionally, about 25 minutes longer. If desired, break up any large plum pieces. Transfer to bowl and refrigerate until cold, about 2 hours. Quick Chunky Plum and Cherry Jam has 294.0 calories. Quick Chunky Plum and Cherry Jam has 2.0 grams of protein. Quick Chunky Plum and Cherry Jam has 1.0 grams of fat. Quick Chunky Plum and Cherry Jam has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Quick Chunky Plum and Cherry Jam are 1 1/2 pounds dark-skinned plums (about 6 large), quartered, pitted, 1 cup sweet cherries, pitted, 1 cup sugar, 3/4 teaspoon ground allspice.Serve this jam with scones and tea, spread over pancakes, alongside grilled pork, or swirled into yogurt.

Chill Chaser recipe.  In a bowl, beat the egg yolks until they are thick and light. Warm the honey and add it, in a stream, stirring constantly. Beat in the scalded milk and orange-flavored liqueur. Divide the mixture among six 10-ounce, heat-proof mugs, rinsed with boiling water, and garnish each drink with grated orange rind. Chill Chaser has 346.0 calories. Chill Chaser has 6.0 grams of protein. Chill Chaser has 6.0 grams of fat. Chill Chaser has 77.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chill Chaser are 4 egg yolks, 1/4 cup honey, 4 cups scalded milk, 1 1/4 cups orange-flavored liqueur, Garnish: grated orange rind.

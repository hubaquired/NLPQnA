Shrimp with Fennel, Dill, and Feta recipe.  Heat 2 tablespoons oil from sun-dried tomatoes in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add fennel; sauté 3 minutes. Add sun-dried tomatoes and garlic and stir 1 minute. Sprinkle shrimp with salt and pepper. Add shrimp to skillet and cook until just opaque in center, about 1 minute per side. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with dill, then feta. Shrimp with Fennel, Dill, and Feta has 229.0 calories. Shrimp with Fennel, Dill, and Feta has 26.0 grams of protein. Shrimp with Fennel, Dill, and Feta has 9.0 grams of fat. Shrimp with Fennel, Dill, and Feta has 1162.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp with Fennel, Dill, and Feta are 1/2 cup sliced oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, 2 tablespoons oil reserved, 2 medium fennel bulbs, trimmed, halved, thinly sliced crosswise (about 3 1/2 cups), 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/3 pounds uncooked large shrimp, peeled, deveined, tails left intact, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, 2/3 cup crumbled feta cheese.

Gouda, Bacon and Leek Pancake recipe. Preheat oven to 425°F. Using heavy 10-inch nonstick ovenproof skillet, fry bacon over medium-high heat until brown and crisp. Transfer to paper towels to drain. Crumble bacon. Pour off all but 2 tablespoons bacon drippings from skillet. Add leeks to skillet and sauté over medium heat until tender and beginning to brown, about 8 minutes. Combine eggs, milk, flour, mustard and sugar in processor and blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Scatter bacon over leeks in skillet. Pour egg mixture over. Sprinkle Gouda over. Transfer to oven and bake until pancake puffs and cheese melts, about 15 minutes. Loosen pancake from skillet. Cut into wedges and serve. Gouda, Bacon and Leek Pancake has 501.0 calories. Gouda, Bacon and Leek Pancake has 23.0 grams of protein. Gouda, Bacon and Leek Pancake has 36.0 grams of fat. Gouda, Bacon and Leek Pancake has 736.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gouda, Bacon and Leek Pancake are 8 bacon slices, 2 large leeks (white and light green parts only), coarsely chopped, 3 eggs, 3/4 cup milk, 1/2 cup all purpose flour, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1 cup firmly packed shredded Gouda (about 4 ounces).

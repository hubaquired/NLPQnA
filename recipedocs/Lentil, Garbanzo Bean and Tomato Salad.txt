Lentil, Garbanzo Bean and Tomato Salad recipe.  Combine 2 1/2 cups water, lentils, diced carrots and bay leaf in heavy medium saucepan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until lentils are just tender, about 25 minutes. Drain; transfer to large bowl. Cool. Mix all remaining ingredients into lentils. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 4 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Lentil, Garbanzo Bean and Tomato Salad has 484.0 calories. Lentil, Garbanzo Bean and Tomato Salad has 21.0 grams of protein. Lentil, Garbanzo Bean and Tomato Salad has 19.0 grams of fat. Lentil, Garbanzo Bean and Tomato Salad has 278.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lentil, Garbanzo Bean and Tomato Salad are 2 1/2 cups water, 1 cup lentils, 2 large carrots, peeled, diced, 1 bay leaf, 1 15- to 16-ounce can garbanzo beans (chick-peas), rinsed, drained, 1/2 basket cherry tomatoes, halved, 1 cup chopped fresh parsley, 5 green onions, chopped, 4 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice.

Mexican Street Corn recipe. Spread grilled corn with a thin layer of crema, then roll in cheese to coat. Sprinkle with cayenne. Squeeze lime juice from wedges to taste. Mexican Street Corn has 78.0 calories. Mexican Street Corn has 3.0 grams of protein. Mexican Street Corn has 7.0 grams of fat. Mexican Street Corn has 180.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mexican Street Corn are 3/4 cup Mexican crema or mayonnaise, 1/2 lb queso cotija, shredded, or feta, crumbled, Cayenne to taste, Accompaniment: lime wedges.

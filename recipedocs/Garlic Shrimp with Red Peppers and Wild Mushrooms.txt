Garlic Shrimp with Red Peppers and Wild Mushrooms recipe.  Char peppers over gas flame or in broiler until blackened on all sides. Transfer to paper bag and let stand 10 minutes to steam. Peel, seed and quarter peppers. Arrange peppers on platter. Heat 6 tablespoons oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and sauté until tender, about 8 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Arrange on platter with bell peppers. Can be prepared 6 hours ahead. Cover and let stand at room temperature.) Heat remaining oil in same skillet over medium-high hat. Add garlic and chile and sauté 2 minutes. Add shrimp and sauteé until cooked through, about 3 minutes. Mix in parsley. Spoon shrimp over mushrooms and peppers.

Cider Vinaigrette recipe. Boil cider in a small heavy saucepan until reduced to about 1/3 cup, 12 to 15 minutes. Whisk together reduced cider, shallots, vinegar, and mustard with 1/4 teaspoon each of salt and pepper. Add oil in a slow stream, whisking until combined. Cider Vinaigrette has 523.0 calories. Cider Vinaigrette has 1.0 grams of protein. Cider Vinaigrette has 48.0 grams of fat. Cider Vinaigrette has 85.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cider Vinaigrette are 1 cup unfiltered apple cider, 3 tablespoons finely chopped shallots, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, 2 teaspoons grainy mustard, 1/3 cup olive oil.

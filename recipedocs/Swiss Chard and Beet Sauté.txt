Swiss Chard and Beet Sauté recipe. Cook bacon in heavy large skillet over medium heat until crisp. Using slotted spoon, transfer to paper towels. Add onion to drippings and sauté until tender, about 5 minutes. Add chard and cook until tender, stirring frequently, about 6 minutes. Add vinegar and stir 1 minute. Remove from heat. Mix in bacon and beets. Season generously with salt and pepper. Swiss Chard and Beet Sauté has 167.0 calories. Swiss Chard and Beet Sauté has 7.0 grams of protein. Swiss Chard and Beet Sauté has 9.0 grams of fat. Swiss Chard and Beet Sauté has 622.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Swiss Chard and Beet Sauté are 3 slices bacon, coarsely chopped, 1 onion, chopped, 1 large bunch red Swiss chard, stalks trimmed, leaves sliced, 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1 16-ounce can or jar julienned beets, drained.

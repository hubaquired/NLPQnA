Egg Sandwich with Green Bean Slaw recipe.  Whisk 1/2 cup crumbled feta, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, and 2 tablespoons olive oil; season with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper. Toss with 1/4 thinly sliced red onion, 2 thinly sliced radishes, 1/2 pound green beans shaved with a peeler), and 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives. Build 4 sandwiches with 8 slices bread, 4 sliced hard-boiled eggs, and slaw. Egg Sandwich with Green Bean Slaw has 374.0 calories. Egg Sandwich with Green Bean Slaw has 16.0 grams of protein. Egg Sandwich with Green Bean Slaw has 19.0 grams of fat. Egg Sandwich with Green Bean Slaw has 590.0 grams of sodium.

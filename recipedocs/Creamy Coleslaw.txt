Creamy Coleslaw recipe. Whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined well, then toss with cabbage and carrots. Let stand, uncovered, at room temperature, tossing occasionally, until wilted, about 30 minutes. Serve at room temperature or chilled. Creamy Coleslaw has 154.0 calories. Creamy Coleslaw has 2.0 grams of protein. Creamy Coleslaw has 13.0 grams of fat. Creamy Coleslaw has 268.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Coleslaw are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, 3/4 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 2 lb green cabbage, quartered, cored, and thinly sliced (8 cups), 3 medium carrots, shredded.Active time: 15 min Start to finish: 45 min.

Bourbon Balls recipe. Mix all together and shape in small balls the size of a large cherry, and roll in confectioner's sugar. Makes about 40 balls, depending upon the size. The ingredients of Bourbon Balls are 4 cups ground vanilla wafers, 1 cup ground pecans, 1 cup confectioner's sugar, 3 tablespoons white karo, 1/2 cup good bourbon whiskey.Editor's note: This recipe appears as part of our editors' Christmas Cookie Swap, 10 beloved holiday recipes from the editors of Epicurious and Gourmet Live.

Mustard Wax Beans recipe.  Cook the beans in boiling salted water until just tender but still crisp. Drain. Mix remaining ingredients in a large pot and cook until thick, stirring constantly. Add the drained beans, bring the mixture to a full boil and pour into hot, sterilized preserving jars. Cover and seal. Mustard Wax Beans has 324.0 calories. Mustard Wax Beans has 6.0 grams of protein. Mustard Wax Beans has 2.0 grams of fat. Mustard Wax Beans has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mustard Wax Beans are 5 1/2 pounds wax beans, trimmed and cut into 1" pieces, 3 cups sugar, 2 tablespoons celery seed, 1 tablespoon turmeric, 3/4 cup flour, 3/8 cup dry mustard, 4 cups cider vinegar.

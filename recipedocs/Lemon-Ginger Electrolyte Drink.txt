Lemon-Ginger Electrolyte Drink recipe. Finely grate ginger and, using a flexible spatula, press solids into a fine-mesh sieve set over a small bowl; discard pulp. You should have about 1 tsp. ginger juice. Combine ginger juice, lemon juice, lime juice, agave, and salt in a large measuring cup or bowl. Stir in mineral water. Pour over 2 glasses filled with ice. Lemon-ginger mixture can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill. Stir vigorously before adding mineral water. Lemon-Ginger Electrolyte Drink has 99.0 calories. Lemon-Ginger Electrolyte Drink has 3.0 grams of protein. Lemon-Ginger Electrolyte Drink has 1.0 grams of fat. Lemon-Ginger Electrolyte Drink has 493.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon-Ginger Electrolyte Drink are 1 (4") piece ginger, peeled, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 2 teaspoons agave nectar or honey, 1/8 teaspoon fine sea salt, 2 3/4 cups mineral or coconut water.Refresh after a grueling workout or exhausting day with this electrolyte-packed water, with a natural boost of energy, vitamins, and minerals.

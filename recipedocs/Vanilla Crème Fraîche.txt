Vanilla Crème Fraîche recipe. Spoon crème fraîche into small bowl; scrape in seeds from vanilla bean and stir to blend. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate. *Available at some supermarkets and specialty foods stores. Vanilla Crème Fraîche has 111.0 calories. Vanilla Crème Fraîche has 1.0 grams of protein. Vanilla Crème Fraîche has 11.0 grams of fat. Vanilla Crème Fraîche has 27.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Vanilla Crème Fraîche are 1 8-ounce container crème fraîche*, 1/2 vanilla bean, split lengthwise.

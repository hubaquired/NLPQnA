Insalata Mista recipe. Using vegetable peeler and working over large bowl, cut carrot into long thin ribbons. Add lettuce, radicchio and fennel. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over salad and toss to coat. Season salad to taste with salt and pepper. Insalata Mista has 129.0 calories. Insalata Mista has 1.0 grams of protein. Insalata Mista has 11.0 grams of fat. Insalata Mista has 40.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Insalata Mista are 1 large carrot, peeled, 1 small head Bibb lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces, 1 head radicchio, torn into bite-size pieces, 1 large fennel bulb, trimmed, cut crosswise into 1/8-inch-thick slices, 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice or red wine vinegar.(MIXED SALAD).

Peach, Prosciutto & Ricotta Crostini recipe.  Grill bread slices. Halve, pit, and thinly slice peach. Spoon about 1 tablespoon ricotta onto each toast and sprinkle with ground pepper. Tear prosciutto into feathery pieces and drape a few slices over ricotta on each. Drizzle each with honey and top with 2 peach slices. The ingredients of Peach, Prosciutto & Ricotta Crostini are 12 slices ciabatta bread, 1 ripe peach, 1 tablespoon fresh ricotta (preferably sheep's milk), Freshly ground black pepper, 4 thin slices of prosciutto, Honey.This is inspired by the Italian combination of fruit and ham (think melon and prosciutto) by way of Georgia. .

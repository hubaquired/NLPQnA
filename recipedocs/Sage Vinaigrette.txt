Sage Vinaigrette recipe.  Chop the raw garlic in the food processor. Add the Dijon, roasted garlic, herbs, and vinegar. Process well. Add olive oil slowly. Season with salt and pepper. Season with salt and pepper and a splash or two of vinegar. Sage Vinaigrette has 168.0 calories. Sage Vinaigrette has 18.0 grams of fat. Sage Vinaigrette has 137.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sage Vinaigrette are 1 clove garlic, minced, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons roasted garlic, 1 tablespoon minced sage, 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 1 cup pure olive oil, Salt and pepper.

Guacamole with Aleppo Pepper recipe. Combine first 10 ingredients in a bowl and mash with a fork. Taste and adjust seasoning. Fold in the cilantro, sprinkle with additional Aleppo, and serve. Guacamole with Aleppo Pepper has 180.0 calories. Guacamole with Aleppo Pepper has 2.0 grams of protein. Guacamole with Aleppo Pepper has 16.0 grams of fat. Guacamole with Aleppo Pepper has 247.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Guacamole with Aleppo Pepper are 3 avocados, roughly chopped, 2 to 3 tablespoons lime juice, 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil, 3 cloves garlic, poached in hot water for 2 minutes and minced, 1 to 2 jalapeño peppers (depending on heat), stemmed, seeded, and minced, 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion, rinsed in cool water and drained, 1/4 teaspoon cumin, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1/8 teaspoon Aleppo pepper, plus more for garnish, 1/4 cup chopped cilantro.

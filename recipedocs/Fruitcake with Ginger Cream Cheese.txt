Fruitcake with Ginger Cream Cheese recipe. In a bowl stir together the cream cheese, softened, and the gingerroot and chill the mixture for 30 minutes. Spread the ginger cream cheese on the fruitcake. Makes about 1/2 cup ginger cream cheese. Fruitcake with Ginger Cream Cheese has 109.0 calories. Fruitcake with Ginger Cream Cheese has 2.0 grams of protein. Fruitcake with Ginger Cream Cheese has 8.0 grams of fat. Fruitcake with Ginger Cream Cheese has 89.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fruitcake with Ginger Cream Cheese are a 3-ounce package cream cheese, 1 tablespoon minced peeled fresh gingerroot, thin slices of fruitcake.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

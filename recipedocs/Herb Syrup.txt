Herb Syrup recipe.  Bring 1 cup to boil in a small saucepan over low heat and add the sugar and herb. Boil until the sugar is completely dissolved, about 2 minutes. Strain through a colander into a bowl. Let the syrup cool and combine with the fruit. Serve immediately or store for us to 1 month in the refrigerator. Herb Syrup has 198.0 calories. Herb Syrup has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Herb Syrup are 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary or mint. Sauce Quick & Easy Mint Rosemary.

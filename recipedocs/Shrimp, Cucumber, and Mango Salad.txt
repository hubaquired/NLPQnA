Shrimp, Cucumber, and Mango Salad recipe.  Mix vinegar and sugar in small bowl until sugar dissolves. Whisk in mustard and mayonnaise. Cover and chill. Can be made 1 day ahead. Keep chilled.) Combine cucumbers, mango, shrimp, and dill in large bowl. Pour dressing over; toss to coat. Season with salt and hot pepper sauce. Arrange 2 lettuce leaves on each of 6 plates. Top with shrimp salad. Shrimp, Cucumber, and Mango Salad has 262.0 calories. Shrimp, Cucumber, and Mango Salad has 16.0 grams of protein. Shrimp, Cucumber, and Mango Salad has 13.0 grams of fat. Shrimp, Cucumber, and Mango Salad has 696.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp, Cucumber, and Mango Salad are 3 tablespoons distilled white vinegar, 3 tablespoons sugar, 6 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 6 tablespoons mayonnaise, 4 large pickling cucumbers, peeled, cut into 1/2-inch cubes (about 2 cups), 1 large mango, peeled, pitted, cut into 1/2-inch cubes (about 1 1/2 cups), 1 pound cooked medium shrimp, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, Hot pepper sauce, 12 Bibb lettuce leaves.What to drink: Sparkling wine would pair well with these sweet-spicy flavors.

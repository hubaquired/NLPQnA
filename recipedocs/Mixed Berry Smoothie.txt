Mixed Berry Smoothie recipe.  Combine all ingredients in blender. Pureé until very smooth. Pour into glasses. Mixed Berry Smoothie has 120.0 calories. Mixed Berry Smoothie has 2.0 grams of protein. Mixed Berry Smoothie has 1.0 grams of fat. Mixed Berry Smoothie has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mixed Berry Smoothie are 2 cups mixed fresh berries (such as strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, and blackberries), 1 cup diced peeled pitted mango (about 1 large), 1 cup ice cubes, 1/2 cup chilled nonfat soy milk, 3 tablespoons sugar.If desired, the smoothie can be strained to remove the berry seeds, but the seeds contain lots of beneficial dietary fiber.

Tequila Cosmopolitan recipe.  Run a lime wedge around the rim of a Martini glass. Dip the rim in sugar. Combine tequila with cranberry juice concentrate, water, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker. Fill with ice. Shake vigorously until icy cold, then strain into glass. Tequila Cosmopolitan has 203.0 calories. Tequila Cosmopolitan has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tequila Cosmopolitan are Lime wedge, Sugar, 1/4 cup blanco or reposado tequila, 2 1/2 tablespoons thawed frozen cranberry juice concentrate, 2 tablespoons water, 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice.

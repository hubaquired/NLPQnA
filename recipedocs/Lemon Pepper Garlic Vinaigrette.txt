Lemon Pepper Garlic Vinaigrette recipe.  Mince garlic, then mash to a paste with 3/4 teaspoon salt. Whisk together garlic paste, pepper, lemon zest and juice, vinegar, and mustard, then whisk in oil until emulsified. Whisk in herb, if using. Lemon Pepper Garlic Vinaigrette has 151.0 calories. Lemon Pepper Garlic Vinaigrette has 16.0 grams of fat. Lemon Pepper Garlic Vinaigrette has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon Pepper Garlic Vinaigrette are 2 to 3 large garlic cloves, 1 1/2 to 2 tablespoons coarsely ground black pepper, 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest, 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 cup chopped fresh tarragon or basil (optional).This is part of the recipe Chicken Wings Five Ways.

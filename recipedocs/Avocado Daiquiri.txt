Avocado Daiquiri recipe.  Combine ingredients in blender and blend on high until just smooth, about 20 seconds. Divide mixture between two cocktail glasses and serve. Avocado Daiquiri has 381.0 calories. Avocado Daiquiri has 2.0 grams of protein. Avocado Daiquiri has 16.0 grams of fat. Avocado Daiquiri has 34.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Avocado Daiquiri are 2 ounces light rum, 2 ounces gold rum, 1/2 ounce fresh lemon juice, 1/2 ounce fresh lime juice, 2 ounces simple syrup, 1/4 medium-ripe avocado, peeled and pitted, 1/2 ounce half-and-half, 1 1/2 cups ice.This delicious tweak on a classic is from Hip Sips by Lucy Brennan (Chronicle Books, Spring 2007) and was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.

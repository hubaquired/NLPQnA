Mango, Mint, and Pineapple Smoothie recipe. Place all the ingredients and 3 ice cubes in a blender and blend on high speed for about 60 seconds or until smooth. Pour into a chilled glass and enjoy. The ingredients of Mango, Mint, and Pineapple Smoothie are 1/2 cup chopped mango, 1/2 cup chopped pineapple, 1/2 cup nonfat high-protein plain Greek yogurt or kefir, 4 mint leaves, torn into pieces.This smoothie transports you to the tropics with the mango and pineapple. I usually make it in my Chicago kitchen on a rainy day when I need some bright flavors.  HarperCollins Smoothie Breakfast Mango Pineapple Mint Yogurt Quick and Healthy.

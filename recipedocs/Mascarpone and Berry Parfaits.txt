Mascarpone and Berry Parfaits recipe.  Blend 1 cup chopped strawberries, 2/3 cup raspberries and sugar in processor to smooth puree. Drop 1 whole strawberry into each of 4 Champagne flutes or wineglasses. Top with 2 rounded tablespoons mascarpone cheese and 2 tablespoons fruit puree. Repeat layering 2 more times. Top each parfait with some of remaining cheese, then 3 raspberries. Chill at least 1 hour or cover and chill overnight. Mascarpone and Berry Parfaits has 440.0 calories. Mascarpone and Berry Parfaits has 7.0 grams of protein. Mascarpone and Berry Parfaits has 39.0 grams of fat. Mascarpone and Berry Parfaits has 415.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mascarpone and Berry Parfaits are 1 cup chopped strawberries plus 4 whole strawberries, hulled, 2/3 cup plus 12 raspberries, 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 8-ounce containers mascarpone cheese.An easy substitute for the mascarpone, a soft Italian cream cheese, is 12 ounces cream cheese blended with six tablespoons whipping cream and 1/4 cup sour cream.

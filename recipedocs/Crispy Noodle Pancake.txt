Crispy Noodle Pancake recipe. Cook noodles in large pot of boiling water 1 1/2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Drain. Rinse with cold water. Drain well. Toss with green onions, 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, and pepper in bowl. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil and sesame oil in a heavy 9-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add noodles and flatten slightly. Cook without stirring until light brown, about 6 minutes. Turn and cook second side until light brown, 6 minutes. Drain on paper towels. Cut into wedges. Crispy Noodle Pancake has 540.0 calories. Crispy Noodle Pancake has 13.0 grams of protein. Crispy Noodle Pancake has 27.0 grams of fat. Crispy Noodle Pancake has 23.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Crispy Noodle Pancake are 6 ounces fresh chow mein noodles, 4 green onions, chopped, 1 teaspoon plus 1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 1/2 tablespoons oriental sesame oil.

Spanish Manhattan recipe. Combine the whiskey, sherry, dash of bitters and ice cubes in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with the cherry. The ingredients of Spanish Manhattan are 2 1/2 ounces blended whiskey, 3/4 ounces dry sherry, Dash Angostura bitters, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Maraschino cherry.

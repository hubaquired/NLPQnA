Iced Tea Sangria with Fresh Fruit recipe. Bring water to boil in medium saucepan. Remove from heat. Add tea bags; steep 3 minutes. Remove tea bags; pour tea into pitcher. Add sugar to taste and stir until sugar dissolves. Cool. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Mix fruit in bowl. Divide among four 16-ounce glasses. Pour 1/4 cup wine, then 1 cup tea into each glass. Fill glasses with ice cubes and serve. The ingredients of Iced Tea Sangria with Fresh Fruit are 4 cups water, 2 tea bags, 4 to 5 tablespoons sugar, 1 peach or apple, peeled, pitted or seeded, chopped, 4 large strawberries, hulled, halved, 1 orange, all peel and white pith removed, cut into 3/4-inch pieces, 1 cup dry red wine, Ice cubes.This is a refreshing drink for the cocktail hour.

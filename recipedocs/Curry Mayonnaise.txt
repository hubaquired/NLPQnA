Curry Mayonnaise recipe.  In a bowl stir together the mayonnaise, the curry powder, the lime juice, and the cayenne. Chill the mayonnaise, covered, for 1 hour and garnish it with the paprika. Serve the mayonnaise with chicken or vegetables. Curry Mayonnaise has 202.0 calories. Curry Mayonnaise has 22.0 grams of fat. Curry Mayonnaise has 167.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Curry Mayonnaise are 1 cup bottled mayonnaise, 2 teaspoons curry powder, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne, paprika for garnish.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

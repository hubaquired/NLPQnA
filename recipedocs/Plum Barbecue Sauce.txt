Plum Barbecue Sauce recipe.  Cook onion in oil in a 3- to 4-quart heavy saucepan over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until golden, about 5 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients and simmer, uncovered, stirring more frequently toward end of cooking, until plums fall apart and sauce is thickened, about 1 hour. Cool to room temperature. Serve with pork or chicken. Plum Barbecue Sauce has 199.0 calories. Plum Barbecue Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Plum Barbecue Sauce has 5.0 grams of fat. Plum Barbecue Sauce has 245.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Plum Barbecue Sauce are 1 small onion, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 1 pound black plums (4), pitted and coarsely chopped, 1 1/4 cups water, 1/4 cup plum jam, 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon salt.

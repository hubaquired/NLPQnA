Butternut Squash with Tangerine and Sage Glaze recipe.  Preheat oven to 375°F. Cut neck off each squash. Peel necks and cut crosswise into generous 1/3-inch-thick slices. Overlap slices in 2 large baking dishes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Spoon 6 tablespoons tangerine juice over squash. Spoon cream over squash; dot with butter. Bake squash until beginning to brown, about 30 minutes. Mix tangerine peel into remaining 10 tablespoons juice; drizzle over squash. Place 1 sage leaf atop each squash round. Bake until squash are very tender and tops are browned in spots, about 30 minutes longer do not turn squash over at any point). Can be prepared 4 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. Before serving, heat in 375°F oven until warm, about 15 minutes.) Butternut Squash with Tangerine and Sage Glaze has 146.0 calories. Butternut Squash with Tangerine and Sage Glaze has 2.0 grams of protein. Butternut Squash with Tangerine and Sage Glaze has 4.0 grams of fat. Butternut Squash with Tangerine and Sage Glaze has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Butternut Squash with Tangerine and Sage Glaze are 8 pounds butternut squash with long necks, 1 cup fresh tangerine juice, divided, 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, 2 tablespoons grated tangerine peel, Fresh sage leaves (about 30).Squash with long, thin necks will yield uniform rounds when sliced and ensure even cooking.

Coconut Date Nut Balls recipe. In a food processor blend the graham cracker crumbs, the dates, 1/2 cup of the coconut, and the honey until the mixture is just combined. Add the walnuts and blend the mixture until it forms a ball. Have ready in a shallow dish the remaining 3/4 cup coconut, form the date mixture into walnut-size balls, and roll the balls in the coconut, pressing the coconut slightly to make it adhere. Coconut Date Nut Balls has 253.0 calories. Coconut Date Nut Balls has 2.0 grams of protein. Coconut Date Nut Balls has 9.0 grams of fat. Coconut Date Nut Balls has 139.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coconut Date Nut Balls are 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 pound (about 1 1/2 cups) pitted dried dates, 1 1/4 cups sweetened flaked coconut, toasted and cooled, 1/4 cup honey, 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped fine.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

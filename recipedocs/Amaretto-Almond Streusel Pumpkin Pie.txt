Amaretto-Almond Streusel Pumpkin Pie recipe.  Replace 2 tablespoons cream with amaretto liqueur in Perfect Pumpkin Pie. Mix cookie crumbs with almonds. Sprinkle over filling just before baking. Bake and cool pie as directed. Can be made 8 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. The ingredients of Amaretto-Almond Streusel Pumpkin Pie are 2 tablespoons amaretto liqueur, Perfect Pumpkin Pie, 20 Amaretti di Saronno cookies (about 4 1/4 ounces), crumbled into pieces that are 1/2 inch or smaller, 1/4 cup sliced almonds.

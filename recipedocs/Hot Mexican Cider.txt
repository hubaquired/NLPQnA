Hot Mexican Cider recipe. In a saucepan combine the cider, the lemon juice, and the salt, bring the mixture to a simmer, and skim it. Add the tequila and the liqueur and heat the mixture over moderate heat, stirring until it is hot. Divide the mixture among 8 heated mugs and garnish each drink with 1 of the lemon slices. Hot Mexican Cider has 219.0 calories. Hot Mexican Cider has 153.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Mexican Cider are 5 cups apple cider, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 2/3 cups tequila, 1/3 cup orange-flavored liqueur, 8 lemon slices for garnish.

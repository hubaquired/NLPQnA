Crispy Salt-and-Vinegar Potatoes recipe.  Combine potatoes, 1 cup vinegar, and 1 tablespoon kosher salt in a medium saucepan; add water to cover by 1". Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until potatoes are tender, 20–25 minutes; drain and pat dry. Heat butter in a large skillet over mediumhigh heat. Add potatoes; season with kosher salt and pepper. Cook, tossing occasionally, until golden brown and crisp, 8–10 minutes. Drizzle with remaining 2 tablespoons vinegar. Serve topped with chives and sea salt. The ingredients of Crispy Salt-and-Vinegar Potatoes are 2 pounds baby Yukon Gold potatoes, halved, quartered if large, 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar, 1 tablespoon kosher salt, plus more, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, Freshly ground black pepper, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, Flaky sea salt (such as Maldon).Cooking the potatoes in vinegar seasons them from within, and a final drizzle boosts the flavor.

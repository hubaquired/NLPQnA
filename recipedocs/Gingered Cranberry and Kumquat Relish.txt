Gingered Cranberry and Kumquat Relish recipe.  Using on/off turns, coarsely chop cranberries in processor. Transfer cranberries to medium bowl. Mince half of kumquats, removing any seeds. Thinly slice remaining kumquats, removing any seeds. Mix all kumquats, sugar, and ginger into chopped cranberries as mixture sits, sugar will dissolve). Cover relish and refrigerate at least 4 hours. Cranberry relish can be prepared 1 week ahead. Cover tightly and keep refrigerated.) The ingredients of Gingered Cranberry and Kumquat Relish are 1 12-ounce bag fresh cranberries, 16 kumquats, stemmed, rinsed, patted dry, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup minced crystallized ginger.Kumquats lend their bright citrus snap to a no-cooking-required relish.

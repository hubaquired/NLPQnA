Curried Yogurt Dip with Crisp Steamed Broccoli recipe.  Line strainer with double-thick layer of cheesecloth, extending over sides; set over deep bowl. Add yogurt to strainer. Chill overnight liquid will drain from yogurt and yogurt will thicken). Transfer yogurt to small bowl; discard liquid. Stir oil and garlic in small nonstick skillet over medium heat 30 seconds. Stir in curry powder. Whisk curry mixture into yogurt. Season with salt. Chill at least 1 hour and up to 1 day. Steam broccoli until crisp-tender, about 2 minutes. Rinse under cold water. Chill until cold. Serve with dip. The ingredients of Curried Yogurt Dip with Crisp Steamed Broccoli are 3 cups plain nonfat yogurt, 3 cups plain nonfat yogurt, 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder, 3 cups broccoli florets.Start making this dip one day before you plan to serve it, since the yogurt has to drain and thicken overnight. A piece of cheesecloth is needed for the first step of the recipe.

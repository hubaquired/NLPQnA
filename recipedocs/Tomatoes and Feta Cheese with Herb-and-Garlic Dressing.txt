Tomatoes and Feta Cheese with Herb-and-Garlic Dressing recipe.  Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl; gradually whisk in oil. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Arrange tomatoes on platter. Sprinkle with olives and cheese. Drizzle with enough dressing to coat. Serve, passing remaining dressing separately.

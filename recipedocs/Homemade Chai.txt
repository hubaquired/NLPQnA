Homemade Chai recipe.  Combine first 5 ingredients in medium saucepan. Using mallet or back of large spoon, lightly crush or bruise spices. Add 6 cups water; bring to boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, partially cover pan, and simmer gently 10 minutes. Remove from heat. Add tea bags and steep 5 minutes. Discard tea bags. Add milk and sugar. Bring tea just to simmer over high heat, whisking until sugar dissolves. Strain chai into teapot and serve hot. Homemade Chai has 105.0 calories. Homemade Chai has 3.0 grams of protein. The ingredients of Homemade Chai are 2-inch piece fresh ginger, cut into thin rounds, 2 cinnamon sticks, 2 teaspoons black peppercorns, 10 whole cloves, 6 cardamom pods, 6 cups cold water, 6 bags of black tea (preferably Darjeeling), 2 cups whole milk, 1/2 cup (packed) golden brown sugar.A popular coffee-bar alternative to latte, Indian-inspired chai combines black tea, spices, and milk.

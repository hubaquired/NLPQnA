Spiced Lemonade recipe.  Put sugar syrup, cloves and cinnamon in a small saucepan and simmer together for 5 minutes. Add lemon juice and let stand for one hour. Strain and combine with the water in a pitcher. Stir, and pour into tumblers over crushed ice. Spiced Lemonade has 92.0 calories. Spiced Lemonade has 23.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spiced Lemonade are 1 cup sugar syrup, 12 whole cloves, 1 stick cinnamon, 1 1/2 cups lemon juice, 1 quart water, Crushed ice.

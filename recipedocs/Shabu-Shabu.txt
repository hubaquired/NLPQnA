Shabu-Shabu recipe.  Arrange steak on large platter, then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate. Arrange cabbage, mushrooms, leeks, tofu, and spinach on another large platter, then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate. In large pot, bring 8 cups water to boil on stove, then transfer to tabletop stove. Place meat and vegetables in pot and swish around until meat is cooked through, about 15 seconds.

Spiced Blush Wine Compote recipe. Combine all ingredients in heavy medium saucepan over low heat. Stir until sugar dissolves. Increase heat to medium. Simmer until fruits are tender and syrup is slightly reduced, about 25 minutes. Cool compote. Cover and refrigerate until cold, at least 3 hours. Can be prepared up to 3 days ahead.) The ingredients of Spiced Blush Wine Compote are 1 8-ounce package dried mixed fruit, 1 orange, cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices, ends discarded, 2 cups white Zinfandel, 1/4 cup sugar, 5 large slices crystallized ginger, 2 cinnamon sticks, broken.

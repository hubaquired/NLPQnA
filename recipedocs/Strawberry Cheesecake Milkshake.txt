Strawberry Cheesecake Milkshake recipe.  In two batches if necessary, blend together ice cream, cream cheese, milk, vanilla, lemon zest, and strawberries in a blender until smooth, 1 to 2 minutes. The ingredients of Strawberry Cheesecake Milkshake are 3 cups vanilla ice cream, 6 ounces cream cheese, 1 cup milk, 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest, 1 pound frozen strawberries (not in syrup).What could possibly be better than a strawberry milkshake? A strawberry cheesecake milkshake! The cream cheese, which has a tang of its own that's enhanced by the lemon zest, helps make this milkshake as thick as, well, concrete (the very apt Missouri term for a milkshake).
If the idea of frozen strawberries hits you as totally counter to your locavore sensibilities, know that they are another secret to a milkshake thick enough to eat with a spoon, and if you grow your own or buy them at the farmers' market, we urge you to freeze them for this shake.

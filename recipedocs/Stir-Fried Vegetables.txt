Stir-Fried Vegetables recipe.  Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add vegetables and sauté until crisp-tender, about 8 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Stir-Fried Vegetables has 57.0 calories. Stir-Fried Vegetables has 1.0 grams of protein. Stir-Fried Vegetables has 4.0 grams of fat. Stir-Fried Vegetables has 25.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stir-Fried Vegetables are 1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 4 ounces green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces (about 1 1/4 cups), 4 small carrots, peeled, cut into matchstick-size strips, 1 red bell pepper, cut into matchstick-size strips.

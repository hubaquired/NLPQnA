PBJ Crepes recipe. Make crepes per recipe directions and place on a plate. Spread one heaping tablespoon of peanut butter on one crepe and then spread with 1 heaping tablespoon of preserves on top. Repeat with remaining crepes. Roll crepe into a log and slice to serve. The ingredients of PBJ Crepes are 1 recipe crepes, Peanut butter, room temperature, Fruit preserves. Kid-Friendly Lunch Jam or Jelly Peanut Butter Weelicious.

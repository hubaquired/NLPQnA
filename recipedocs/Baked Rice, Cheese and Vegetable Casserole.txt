Baked Rice, Cheese and Vegetable Casserole recipe. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion and bell pepper and sauté until tender, about 8 minutes. Add corn and tomato and sauté 3 minutes. Add rice, 1 cup cheese, cream and thyme and stir until cheese melts and mixture is heated through. Transfer mixture to 8-cup soufflé dish. Preheat broiler. Sprinkle remaining 1 cup cheese over rice mixture. Broil until cheese melts, about 2 minutes. Baked Rice, Cheese and Vegetable Casserole has 349.0 calories. Baked Rice, Cheese and Vegetable Casserole has 12.0 grams of protein. Baked Rice, Cheese and Vegetable Casserole has 19.0 grams of fat. Baked Rice, Cheese and Vegetable Casserole has 33.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Rice, Cheese and Vegetable Casserole are 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, 1 onion, chopped, 1 green bell pepper, chopped, 1 1/2 cups frozen whole kernel corn, thawed, 1 large tomato, seeded, chopped, 3 cups cooked rice (about 1 cup raw), 2 cups grated Swiss cheese (about 6 ounces), 3 tablespoons whipping cream, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme or 1 teaspoon dried.A rich side dish for pork or lamb chops.

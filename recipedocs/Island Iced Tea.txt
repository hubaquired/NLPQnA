Island Iced Tea recipe.  In a cocktail shaker half-filled with ice cubes, combine tequila, rum, gin, vodka, Triple Sec and lemon juice. Shake the mixture for 30 seconds, and strain it into 2 tall glasses filled with ice cubes. Divide the cola between the glasses and garnish drinks with lemon slices. Island Iced Tea has 197.0 calories. Island Iced Tea has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Island Iced Tea are 1 pony (1 ounce) light tequila, 1 pony (1 ounce) light rum, 1 pony (1 ounce) gin, 1 pony (1 ounce) vodka, 1 ounce (1 pony) Triple Sec, 1 pony (1 ounce) fresh lemon juice, 1/3 cup cola, Garnish: 2 lemon slices.

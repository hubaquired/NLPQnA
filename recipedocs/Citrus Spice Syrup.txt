Citrus Spice Syrup has 1.0 grams of protein. Citrus Spice Syrup has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Citrus Spice Syrup are 2 cups water, 2 cups sugar, peel from 2 oranges, peel from 1 lemon, 6 whole cloves, 2 cinnamon sticks.

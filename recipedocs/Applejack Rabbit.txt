Applejack Rabbit recipe.  Half fill a cocktail shaker with cracked ice and pour over it the apple brandy, the lemon juice, and maple syrup. Shake the mixture vigorously and strain it into a chilled cocktail glass. Applejack Rabbit has 96.0 calories. Applejack Rabbit has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Applejack Rabbit are Cracked ice, 2 ounces apple brandy, Juice of 1/2 lemon, 1 tablespoon maple syrup. Alcoholic Cocktail Party Lemon Apple Brandy Maple Syrup Gourmet Drink.

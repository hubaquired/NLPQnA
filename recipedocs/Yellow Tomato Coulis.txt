Yellow Tomato Coulis recipe. In a blender puree tomatoes, white-wine vinegar, salt, and olive oil until smooth. Pour tomato mixture through a sieve into a bowl, pressing on solids. Yellow tomato coulis keeps, covered and chilled, 2 days. Serve yellow tomato coulis garnished with halved tomatoes. Yellow Tomato Coulis has 95.0 calories. Yellow Tomato Coulis has 4.0 grams of fat. Yellow Tomato Coulis has 437.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Yellow Tomato Coulis are 1 pound yellow pear or cherry tomatoes (about 2 2/3 cups), 2 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Garnish: halved yellow pear or cherry tomatoes.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

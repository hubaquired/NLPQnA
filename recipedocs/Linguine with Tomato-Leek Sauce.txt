Linguine with Tomato-Leek Sauce recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add leeks and bell pepper. Sauté until just beginning to soften, about 5 minutes. Add cauliflower, tomatoes and tarragon and cook until cauliflower is tender, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Pour sauce over pasta. Serve, passing Parmesan separately. Linguine with Tomato-Leek Sauce has 792.0 calories. Linguine with Tomato-Leek Sauce has 21.0 grams of protein. Linguine with Tomato-Leek Sauce has 30.0 grams of fat. Linguine with Tomato-Leek Sauce has 58.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Linguine with Tomato-Leek Sauce are 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 large leeks (white and pale green parts only), chopped, 1 red bell pepper, diced, 1/2 head cauliflower, cut into small florets, 4 large tomatoes, seeded, chopped, 1/4 teaspoon dried tarragon, crumbled, 8 ounces linguine, freshly cooked, Grated Parmesan.The mild flavor of leeks adds a nice touch to the pasta's tomato-based sauce.

Carnation Lemon Bars recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine flour and powdered sugar in a medium bowl. Cut in butter with pastry blender or 2 knives until mixture is crumbly. Press lightly onto bottom and halfway up sides of ungreased 13 x 9-inch baking pan. Bake for 20 minutes. Beat sweetened condensed milk and eggs in a large mixing bowl until fluffy. Beat in lemon juice, flour, baking powder, salt, and food coloring until blended. Fold in grated lemon peel; pour over crust. Bake until filling is set and crust is golden brown, about 20 to 25 minutes. Cool in pan on wire rack. Refrigerate for 2 hours. Cut into bars; sprinkle with powdered sugar. The ingredients of Carnation Lemon Bars are 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 1 cup butter or margarine, softened, 1 (14-ounce) can Nestlé Carnation Sweetened Condensed Milk, 4 large eggs, 2/3 cup lemon juice, 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 4 drops yellow food coloring (optional), 1 tablespoon grated lemon peel, Sifted powdered sugar (optional).These bars are as refreshing as an icy glass of lemonade, and perfect for entertaining, potlucks, or picnics.

Whole Corn on the Grill recipe. Prepare a grill for medium heat. Grill corn, rotating occasionally, until husks are blackened some will flake and fall off) and kernels are tender with some browned and charred spots, 25–35 minutes. Let corn cool slightly, then shuck. Serve with Charred Spring Onion and Sesame-Chile Butter. Whole Corn on the Grill has 124.0 calories. Whole Corn on the Grill has 4.0 grams of protein. Whole Corn on the Grill has 1.0 grams of fat. Whole Corn on the Grill has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Whole Corn on the Grill are 4 ears of corn, in husk, Charred Spring Onion and Sesame-Chile Butter.How to choose corn: Look for tight, squeaky, green husks and rows of kernels that feel taut and full when you run your hands over the cobs.

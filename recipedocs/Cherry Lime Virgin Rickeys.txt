Cherry Lime Virgin Rickeys recipe.  Pour 2 tablespoons lime syrup into each of 10 tall 8-ounce) glasses. Carefully pour 1 tablespoon grenadine over back of a long spoon inserted halfway into each glass to prevent colors from blending). Gently top off drinks with seltzer, pouring it over back of spoon.

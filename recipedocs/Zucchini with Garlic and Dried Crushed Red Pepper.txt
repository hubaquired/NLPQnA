Zucchini with Garlic and Dried Crushed Red Pepper recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add zucchini; sauté until slices are light golden and tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and red pepper; stir 1 minute. Season to taste with sea salt. Transfer zucchini to serving platter, sprinkle with parsley, and serve. The ingredients of Zucchini with Garlic and Dried Crushed Red Pepper are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 2 pounds zucchini, halved crosswise, each half cut lengthwise into 4 flat slices, 4 garlic cloves, minced, 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper, Sea salt (preferably fleur de sel), 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley.

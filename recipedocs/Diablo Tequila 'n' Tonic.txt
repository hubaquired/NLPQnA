Diablo Tequila 'n' Tonic recipe.  Using peeler, remove peel from lime green part only) in strips. Combine peel, tequila and jalapeños in glass jar with tight-fitting lid. Chill overnight. Fill 8 tall glasses with ice cubes. Pour 3 tablespoons tequila and 3/4 cup tonic water into each. Diablo Tequila 'n' Tonic has 194.0 calories. Diablo Tequila 'n' Tonic has 23.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Diablo Tequila 'n' Tonic are 1 lime, 2 cups white tequila, 2 to 3 jalapeños, halved lengthwise seeded, 6 cups (1 1/2 quarts) tonic water.

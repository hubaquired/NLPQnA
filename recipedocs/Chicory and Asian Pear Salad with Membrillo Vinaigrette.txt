Chicory and Asian Pear Salad with Membrillo Vinaigrette recipe. Heat vinegar in a small saucepan over medium. Add membrillo, whisking to dissolve, then whisk in mustard. Season with salt and pepper; let cool. Toss pears and chicories in a large bowl. Whisk oil into membrillo mixture; season with salt. Drizzle over salad and toss to coat; season with salt and pepper. Serve topped with walnuts. The ingredients of Chicory and Asian Pear Salad with Membrillo Vinaigrette are 1/3 cup Sherry or red wine vinegar, 2 tablespoons membrillo (quince paste) or orange marmalade, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 2 medium Asian pears, cut into bite-size pieces, 8 cups mixed chicories (such as radicchio, escarole, and/or frisee), torn into 2" pieces, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/2 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped.Membrillo lends subtle sweetness to the dressing; look for it at any well-stocked cheese counter.

Milk Chocolate Pudding recipe. Whisk together sugar, cornstarch, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt in a 2-quart heavy saucepan, then gradually whisk in milk and cream. Bring to a boil over moderately high heat, whisking constantly, then boil, whisking, 2 minutes. Mixture will be thick.) Remove from heat. Whisk in chocolate and vanilla until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and chill pudding, its surface covered with wax paper to prevent a skin from forming), until cold, at least 2 hours. The ingredients of Milk Chocolate Pudding are 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 1/2 cups whole milk, 1/2 cup heavy cream, 4 ounces fine-quality milk chocolate, chopped, 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract, lightly sweetened whipped cream.

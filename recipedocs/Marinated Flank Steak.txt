Marinated Flank Steak recipe. Whisk first 5 ingredients to blend in 13x9x2-inch glass dish, season with pepper. Add steak and turn to coat. Marinate steak up to 2 hours at room temperature or cover and refrigerate up to 1 day, turning steak occasionally. Preheat broiler. Remove steak from marinade. Broil steak to desired doneness, about 4 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer steak to platter. Thinly slice steak across grain on diagonal and serve. Marinated Flank Steak has 271.0 calories. The ingredients of Marinated Flank Steak are 4 large shallots, chopped, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons dry red wine, 1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme, 1 1 1/2-pound flank steak, trimmed.

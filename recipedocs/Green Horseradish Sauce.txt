The ingredients of Green Horseradish Sauce are 1 10-ounce package frozen chopped spinach, thawed, 1/4 cup chopped arugula leaves, 1/4 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley leaves, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 6 tablespoons drained prepared white horseradish, 2 1/2 tablespoons (or more) dry white wine, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice.

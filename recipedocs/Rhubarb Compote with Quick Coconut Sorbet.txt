Rhubarb Compote with Quick Coconut Sorbet recipe. Combine first 6 ingredients in heavy pot. Bring to boil, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until mixture thickens slightly, about 10 minutes. Transfer to medium bowl. Refrigerate compote until cold. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill.) Spoon into bowls. Top with sorbet and serve. The ingredients of Rhubarb Compote with Quick Coconut Sorbet are 2 pounds fresh rhubarb, trimmed, cut into 1-inch pieces (about 7 cups), 1/2 cup currant jelly, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup minced, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel, Quick Coconut Sorbet or 1 pint purchased coconut sorbet.

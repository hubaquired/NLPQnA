Yogurt Poppy Seed Dressing recipe. Mince garlic, then mash to a paste with a pinch of salt using a heavy knife. Add 3/4 teaspoon garlic paste or to taste) to a bowl with remaining ingredients and whisk until smooth. Let dressing stand about 1 hour to allow flavors to develop. Yogurt Poppy Seed Dressing has 74.0 calories. Yogurt Poppy Seed Dressing has 4.0 grams of protein. Yogurt Poppy Seed Dressing has 4.0 grams of fat. Yogurt Poppy Seed Dressing has 576.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Yogurt Poppy Seed Dressing are 1 garlic clove, 3/4 cup plain yogurt (whole-milk or low-fat), 2 tablespoons water, 1 teaspoon poppy seeds, 3/4 teaspoon mild honey, 1/2 teaspoon salt.

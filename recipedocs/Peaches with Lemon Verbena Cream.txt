Peaches with Lemon Verbena Cream recipe.  Bring cream to a simmer in a small saucepan. Remove from heat; add 1/4 cup lemon verbena. Cover. Let steep 15 minutes. Strain cream into a medium bowl. Cover and chill at least 1 hour. Preheat oven to 450°. Place peaches in a baking dish. Brush with butter and sprinkle with salt and 4 tablespoons sugar. Roast until peaches start to caramelize, 20-25 minutes. Let cool in dish. Beat infused cream and remaining 2 tablespoons sugar to soft peaks. Serve peaches with cream and torn lemon verbena. The ingredients of Peaches with Lemon Verbena Cream are 1 cup heavy cream, 1/4 cup (packed) fresh lemon verbena leaves plus more, torn, for serving, 4 large ripe peaches, halved, pitted, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature, Pinch of kosher salt, 6 tablespoons sugar, divided.Some leaves deserve to infiltrate the dessert menu, like lemon verbena, infused here in a whipped cream that can be spooned over just about anything.

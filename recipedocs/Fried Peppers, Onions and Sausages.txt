Fried Peppers, Onions and Sausages recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add peppers, onions, garlic and vinegar and sauté 10 minutes. Cover and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Meanwhile, cook sausages in another heavy large skillet over medium-high heat until brown and cooked through, turning occasionally, about 15 minutes. Transfer to platter. Surround with peppers.

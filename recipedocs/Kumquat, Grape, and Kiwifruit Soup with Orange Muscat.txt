Kumquat, Grape, and Kiwifruit Soup with Orange Muscat recipe.  In a saucepan simmer water with sugar and kumquats 10 minutes and stir in Muscat and lemon juice. Simmer mixture 3 minutes. Chill soup until cold, at least 2 hours, and up to 3 days. Stir grapes and kiwifruits into soup and ladle into 6 soup bowls. The ingredients of Kumquat, Grape, and Kiwifruit Soup with Orange Muscat are 1 1/2 cups water, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup kumquats, sliced thin and seeded, 1/2 cup Orange Muscat such as Essensia, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/2 pound black grapes (about 1 1/2 cups), halved, seeded, and sliced very thin, 3 kiwifruits, peeled, halved lengthwise, and sliced thin.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

Apricot Pear Cordials recipe. Divide rock candy among containers. Add fruit and cover with vodka. Seal containers tightly and let stand for 6 weeks, preferably in a cool, dark place. Serve as an after-dinner drink or drizzle over ice cream. Apricot Pear Cordials has 441.0 calories. Apricot Pear Cordials has 2.0 grams of protein. Apricot Pear Cordials has 11.0 grams of fat. Apricot Pear Cordials has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apricot Pear Cordials are 1 1/2 pounds rock candy (see tip, below), 1 1/2 pounds dried pears and/or apricots, 2 liters vodka.Look for 1-quart cordial bottles, carafes, or mason jars with necks wide enough for adding the fruit (and removing it, if desired, after the liqueur has matured).

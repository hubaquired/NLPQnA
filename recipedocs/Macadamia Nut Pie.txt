Macadamia Nut Pie recipe.  Preheat oven to 325°F. Whisk sugar, corn syrup, eggs, butter and vanilla in large bowl to blend. Fill pie crust with nuts. Pour sugar mixture over nuts in pie crust. Bake pie until center puffs slightly, about 55 minutes. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Let stand 2 hours at room temperature before serving.) Serve with vanilla ice cream, if desired. Macadamia Nut Pie has 546.0 calories. Macadamia Nut Pie has 6.0 grams of protein. Macadamia Nut Pie has 39.0 grams of fat. Macadamia Nut Pie has 182.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Macadamia Nut Pie are 3/4 cup (packed) golden brown sugar, 1/2 cup dark corn syrup, 3 large eggs, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 1 frozen 9-inch deep-dish pie crust, 2 cups roasted unsalted macadamia nuts, Vanilla ice cream (optional).

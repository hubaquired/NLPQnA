Red Cabbage and Carrot Slaw recipe.  In a large bowl toss together the carrot and the cabbage. In a small bowl whisk together the vinegar, the sugar, the oil, and the salt. Just before serving add the dressing to the vegetables and toss the slaw well. Red Cabbage and Carrot Slaw has 62.0 calories. Red Cabbage and Carrot Slaw has 1.0 grams of protein. Red Cabbage and Carrot Slaw has 1.0 grams of fat. Red Cabbage and Carrot Slaw has 256.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red Cabbage and Carrot Slaw are 4 cups finely shredded carrot, 4 cups finely shredded red cabbage, 1/2 cup rice vinegar (available at Asian markets and some supermarkets), 1 tablespoon sugar, or to taste, 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

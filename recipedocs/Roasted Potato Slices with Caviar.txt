Roasted Potato Slices with Caviar recipe. Preheat oven to 400°F. Brush both sides of potato slices with olive oil. Place on baking sheet and season lightly with salt and pepper. Bake until golden brown on bottom sides, about 15 minutes. Turn slices over and continue baking until second sides are golden brown, about 15 minutes. Transfer to plate. Top slices with small dollops of sour cream. Sprinkle with chives. Spoon small amount of caviar atop sour cream and chives. Serve immediately. The ingredients of Roasted Potato Slices with Caviar are 3 small baking potatoes (about 5 ounces each), cut crosswise into 1/4-to 1/3-inch-thick slices, Olive oil, Sour cream, Chopped fresh chives, Caviar.

Stir-Fried Sesame Asparagus recipe.  Stir soy sauce and sugar in small bowl until sugar dissolves. Heat oil in heavy large skillet. Add garlic and stir 15 seconds. Add asparagus and stir-fry until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Add soy mixture and toss until asparagus is coated, about 1 minute longer. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl; sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve. Stir-Fried Sesame Asparagus has 138.0 calories. Stir-Fried Sesame Asparagus has 6.0 grams of protein. Stir-Fried Sesame Asparagus has 9.0 grams of fat. Stir-Fried Sesame Asparagus has 445.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stir-Fried Sesame Asparagus are 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 teaspoons sugar, 2 tablespoons oriental sesame oil, 2 large garlic cloves, chopped, 2 pounds asparagus, ends trimmed, cut on diagonal into 2-inch-long pieces, 4 teaspoons sesame seeds.

Guinness Mustard recipe. Whisk all ingredients in small bowl to blend. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. DO AHEAD: Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated. Guinness Mustard has 62.0 calories. Guinness Mustard has 3.0 grams of protein. Guinness Mustard has 3.0 grams of fat. Guinness Mustard has 854.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Guinness Mustard are 1/2 cup coarse-grained Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons regular Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons Guinness stout or other stout or porter, 1 tablespoon minced shallot, 1 teaspoon golden brown sugar.

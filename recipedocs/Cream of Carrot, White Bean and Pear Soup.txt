Cream of Carrot, White Bean and Pear Soup recipe.  Melt butter in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat. Add leeks and sauté until tender, about 10 minutes. Add 2 cups water, carrots, beans, pear and rosemary; bring soup to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until carrots are tender, about 15 minutes. Purée soup in 2 batches in blender until smooth. Return to saucepan; mix in half and half. Thin with additional water by 1/4 cupfuls, if desired. Season soup with salt and pepper. Rewarm if necessary. Ladle soup into bowls and serve. Cream of Carrot, White Bean and Pear Soup has 646.0 calories. Cream of Carrot, White Bean and Pear Soup has 27.0 grams of protein. Cream of Carrot, White Bean and Pear Soup has 24.0 grams of fat. Cream of Carrot, White Bean and Pear Soup has 348.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cream of Carrot, White Bean and Pear Soup are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1 cup thinly sliced leeks (white and pale green parts only), 2 cups (or more) water, 1 1/2 cups thinly sliced peeled carrots, 1 15- to 16-ounce can Great Northern beans, drained, 1 cup coarsely chopped peeled pear, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary or 1 1/2 teaspoons dried, 1/4 cup half and half.The creamy soup would be great with large sesame-coated breadsticks and a spinach salad with marinated mushrooms.

Avocado Salsa recipe. Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl. Season with salt. Can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Add avocado to salsa just before serving. Avocado Salsa has 126.0 calories. Avocado Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein. Avocado Salsa has 12.0 grams of fat. Avocado Salsa has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Avocado Salsa are 2 large tomatoes, seeded, diced, 1/2 red onion, chopped, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 to 2 large jalapeño chilies, seeded but not deveined, minced, 1 large avocado, diced.

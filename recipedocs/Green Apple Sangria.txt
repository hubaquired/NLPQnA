Green Apple Sangria recipe.  Stir apples, orange zest and juice, cinnamon, brandy, lemon juice, sugar, and Cointreau in a large pitcher to combine. Let sit at room temperature for 1 hour. DO AHEAD: Sangria base can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill. Add wine to sangria base and stir to combine. Pour sangria over ice, top off with club soda, and garnish with orange slices. Green Apple Sangria has 208.0 calories. Green Apple Sangria has 1.0 grams of protein. Green Apple Sangria has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Apple Sangria are 2 green apples (such as Granny Smith), cored, cut into 3/4" pieces, Zest of 1 orange, removed in long strips with a vegetable peeler, 3/4 cup fresh orange juice, 2 cinnamon sticks, 3/4 cup brandy, 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/3 cup sugar, 1/4 cup Cointreau or other orange liqueur, 1 750-milliliter bottle Spanish red wine, 1 cup (about) club soda, Orange slices.This lightly spiced sangria is packed with green apple and citrus flavor.

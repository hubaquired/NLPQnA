Strawberry Red-Currant Parfait recipe. Gently heat jelly, bay leaves, and vinegar in a 1- to 2-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring, until melted. Remove from heat and let stand while preparing strawberries. Trim strawberries and halve lengthwise, then transfer to a bowl. Reheat jelly mixture over low heat and pour over strawberries. Let macerate, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes. Serve berries and juices with ice cream, layered if desired. Strawberry Red-Currant Parfait has 250.0 calories. Strawberry Red-Currant Parfait has 3.0 grams of protein. Strawberry Red-Currant Parfait has 8.0 grams of fat. Strawberry Red-Currant Parfait has 63.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Red-Currant Parfait are 1/3 cup red-currant jelly, 2 Turkish bay leaves or 1 California, 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar, 1 pound strawberries (1 quart), 1 pint vanilla ice cream or plain thick yogurt such as Greek.This sophisticated blend of strawberries, red-currant jelly, balsamic vinegar, and bay leaves may just replace chocolate sauce as your favorite ice cream topping.

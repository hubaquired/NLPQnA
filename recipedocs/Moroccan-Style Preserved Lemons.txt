Moroccan-Style Preserved Lemons recipe.  Blanch lemons in boiling water 5 minutes, then drain. When cool enough to handle, cut each lemon into 8 wedges, discarding seeds. Toss lemons with kosher salt in a bowl, then pack lemons, along with their salt, tightly into jar. Add enough lemon juice to cover lemons. Seal jar and let lemons stand at room temperature, shaking gently once a day, for 5 days. Add oil to jar and refrigerate. Moroccan-Style Preserved Lemons has 8.0 calories.

Tequila Mojito recipe.  In a highball glass, mix lime juice, sugar, and mint. Mash mint leaves with the back of a spoon until the sugar dissolves. Fill the glass with ice. Add tequila and club soda; stir to blend. Tequila Mojito has 205.0 calories. Tequila Mojito has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tequila Mojito are 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 4 teaspoons sugar, 12 large fresh mint leaves, 1/4 cup blanco or reposado tequila, 1/4 cup club soda.

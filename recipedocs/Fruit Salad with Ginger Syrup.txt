Fruit Salad with Ginger Syrup recipe.  Bring water, sugar, and ginger to a boil in a 2-quart saucepan, then stir until sugar is dissolved. Simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, then remove from heat and let steep 15 minutes. Pour ginger syrup through a sieve into a bowl, discarding ginger. Chill, covered, at least 2 hours. Toss fruit and mint with 1/4 cup syrup, or to taste. Fruit Salad with Ginger Syrup has 542.0 calories. Fruit Salad with Ginger Syrup has 4.0 grams of protein. Fruit Salad with Ginger Syrup has 1.0 grams of fat. Fruit Salad with Ginger Syrup has 24.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fruit Salad with Ginger Syrup are 3 cups water, 2 cups sugar, 2 cups thinly sliced fresh ginger (1/2 lb; from a 10-inch piece), left unpeeled, 4 cups (1-inch pieces) summer fruit (such as mixed berries, melons, peaches, and/or nectarines), 3 tablespoons small mint leaves.This recipe yields more ginger syrup than you'll need for the fruit salad. Add a spoonful to a glass of club soda, sparkling water, or iced tea for a refreshing summer drink.

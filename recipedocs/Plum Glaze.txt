Plum Glaze recipe. The ingredients of Plum Glaze are 1 1/2 cups fresh orange juice, 1/4 cup Chinese plum sauce, 1/4 cup honey, 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoons orange marmalade, 2 tablespoons finely minced ginger, 1 tablespoon finely minced garlic.A glaze lacquers and flavors the bird.

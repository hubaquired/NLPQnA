Black Bean, Jícama, and Grilled Corn Salad recipe. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Brush corn with 1 tablespoon olive oil. Grill corn until tender and brown in spots, turning occasionally, about 10 minutes. Cool slightly. Cut off corn kernels; place in large bowl. Add black beans, jicama, carrots, green onions, cilantro, and basil. Whisk lime juice, orange juice, lime peel, cumin, and remaining 4 tablespoons oil in small bowl. Mix dressing into bean salad. Season generously with salt and pepper. Can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover; chill. Let stand at room temperature 1 hour before serving.) Black Bean, Jícama, and Grilled Corn Salad has 227.0 calories. Black Bean, Jícama, and Grilled Corn Salad has 8.0 grams of protein. Black Bean, Jícama, and Grilled Corn Salad has 9.0 grams of fat. Black Bean, Jícama, and Grilled Corn Salad has 157.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Black Bean, Jícama, and Grilled Corn Salad are 2 large ears of corn, husked, 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided, 2 15-ounce cans black beans, rinsed, drained, 1 cup 1/3-inch dice peeled jicama, 1/2 cup 1/3-inch dice peeled carrots, 1/3 cup thinly sliced green onions, 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1/4 cup (packed) chopped fresh basil, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 2 tablespoons orange juice, 2 1/2 teaspoons grated lime peel, 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin.

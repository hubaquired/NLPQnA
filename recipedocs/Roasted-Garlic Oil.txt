Roasted-Garlic Oil recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Place garlic cloves and 1 tablespoon olive oil in small ovenproof skillet; stir to coat. Roast in oven until garlic is just golden, shaking pan occasionally, about 15 minutes. Add extra-virgin olive oil; cool in skillet. Transfer oil and garlic to jar. Seal tightly; store in refrigerator up to 2 weeks keep garlic in oil for flavor and looks, but use only oil in recipes). Roasted-Garlic Oil has 258.0 calories. Roasted-Garlic Oil has 29.0 grams of fat. Roasted-Garlic Oil has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted-Garlic Oil are 6 large garlic cloves, peeled, halved, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 cup extra-virgin olive oil. Condiment Spread Sauce Garlic Roast Bon Appétit.

Blueberry Banana Shake recipe.  In a blender, blend the banana, the blueberries, the honey, and the yogurt until the mixture is smooth and chill the mixture for 1 hour if desired. Pour the mixture into chilled glasses. Frozen blueberries result in slightly thicker shakes.) The ingredients of Blueberry Banana Shake are 1 banana cut into pieces, 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries, 1/4 cup honey, 2 cups plain yogurt.

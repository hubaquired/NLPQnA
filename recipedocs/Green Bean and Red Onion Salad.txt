Green Bean and Red Onion Salad recipe.  On a steamer rack set over boiling water steam green beans, covered, until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Halve onion lengthwise and slice thin crosswise. In a bowl whisk together vinegar, oil, and salt and pepper to taste until emulsified and add onion, tossing to combine. Add warm beans and toss to combine well. Green Bean and Red Onion Salad has 174.0 calories. Green Bean and Red Onion Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Green Bean and Red Onion Salad has 14.0 grams of fat. The ingredients of Green Bean and Red Onion Salad are 1/2 pound green beans, trimmed and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces, 1 small red onion, 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

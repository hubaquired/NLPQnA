Strawberry Coconut Pineapple Shake recipe.  In a blender, blend the strawberries, the cream of coconut, the frozen yogurt, and the pineapple juice until the mixture is smooth and pour the mixture into chilled glasses. Strawberry Coconut Pineapple Shake has 196.0 calories. Strawberry Coconut Pineapple Shake has 2.0 grams of protein. Strawberry Coconut Pineapple Shake has 6.0 grams of fat. Strawberry Coconut Pineapple Shake has 47.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Coconut Pineapple Shake are 8 ounces strawberries, hulled and sliced (about 1 cup), 1/2 cup well-stirred canned cream of coconut, 1 pint vanilla frozen yogurt, 1 cup chilled unsweetened pineapple juice.

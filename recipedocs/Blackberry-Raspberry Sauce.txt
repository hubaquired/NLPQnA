Blackberry-Raspberry Sauce recipe. Mash 1/2 cup blackberries with fork in small bowl. Puree remaining berries in processor. Pour puree into strainer set over bowl, pressing on solids to extract as much liquid as possible. Discard solids in strainer. Whisk preserves and mashed berries into puree. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Blackberry-Raspberry Sauce has 172.0 calories. Blackberry-Raspberry Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Blackberry-Raspberry Sauce has 1.0 grams of fat. Blackberry-Raspberry Sauce has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blackberry-Raspberry Sauce are 2 cups frozen blackberries, thawed, 1/4 cup black raspberry preserves.

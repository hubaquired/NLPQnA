Buckwheat Bacon Pancakes recipe.  Preheat oven to 200Â°F. Stir together flours, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add egg, buttermilk, and 2 tablespoons oil and stir just until blended. Stir in bacon. Heat a griddle or large heavy skillet over moderate heat until hot, then lightly brush with remaining 1/2 tablespoon oil.

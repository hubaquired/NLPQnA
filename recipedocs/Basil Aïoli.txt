Basil Aïoli recipe.  Mix all ingredients in medium bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour to allow flavors to develop. Can be prepared 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Basil Aïoli has 303.0 calories. Basil Aïoli has 33.0 grams of fat. Basil Aïoli has 250.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Basil Aïoli are 3/4 cup mayonnaise, 1/3 cup finely chopped fresh basil, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic, 1 1/2 teaspoons grated lemon peel.Also a good sauce for grilled or sautéed fish. The basil could easily — and deliciously — be replaced by cilantro, parsley or chives.  Condiment Spread Sauce No-Cook Quick & Easy Mayonnaise Lemon Basil Spring Bon Appétit California.

Blood Orange Syrup recipe. Stir all ingredients in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat until sugar dissolves. Increase heat and boil until syrup is reduced to 1 1/2 cups, about 20 minutes. Refrigerate until cold. Cover and keep refrigerated up to 2 days. Blood Orange Syrup has 196.0 calories. Blood Orange Syrup has 1.0 grams of protein. Blood Orange Syrup has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blood Orange Syrup are 3 cups fresh blood orange juice or regular orange juice, 9 tablespoons sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoons grated blood orange peel or regular orange peel.Blood oranges have red flesh and sweet-tart juice. They're available at farmers' markets and, increasingly, at supermarkets. This syrup would also be good over waffles.

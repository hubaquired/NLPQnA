Homemade Cheese Spread With Garlic and Herbs recipe.  Combine cream cheese, butter, garlic, and salt in the bowl of a food processor. Pulse until smooth. Add parsley and chives and pulse a few times until well combined. Pack mixture into a small bowl, ramekin, or glass jar. Cover and chill at least 30 minutes. Serve with crackers or toasts. Cheese can be made 5 days ahead. Cover and chill. Homemade Cheese Spread With Garlic and Herbs has 165.0 calories. Homemade Cheese Spread With Garlic and Herbs has 2.0 grams of protein. Homemade Cheese Spread With Garlic and Herbs has 17.0 grams of fat. Homemade Cheese Spread With Garlic and Herbs has 219.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Homemade Cheese Spread With Garlic and Herbs are 8 ounces cream cheese, softened, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened, 1 small garlic clove, quartered, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4 cup chopped parsley leaves, 1 tablespoon chopped chives, Crackers or toasts (for serving).Here's a DIY version of the flavored, spreadable Gournay cheese originally from France.

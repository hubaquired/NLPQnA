Aji Amarillo-Pineapple Salsa recipe. Combine all ingredients in medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Let stand 2 hours, tossing occasionally. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) *Available at Latin American markets. Aji Amarillo-Pineapple Salsa has 60.0 calories. Aji Amarillo-Pineapple Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein. Aji Amarillo-Pineapple Salsa has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Aji Amarillo-Pineapple Salsa are 3 cups 1/4-inch cubes peeled cored pineapple (about half of 1 large), 1 cup chopped yellow bell pepper, 1/2 cup chopped green onions, 1/3 cup finely chopped red onion, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 2 tablespoons chopped seeded red jalapeño chiles, 4 teaspoons hot pepper Aji Amarillo paste* (from 7.5-ounce jar).Aji amarillo is a Peruvian chile. This salsa uses a jarred paste made from these flavorful and fiery bright yellow peppers.

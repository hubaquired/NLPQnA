Creamy Caramel Sauce recipe.  In a heavy saucepan stir together sugar, water, corn syrup, and a pinch salt and boil over moderate heat, stirring, until sugar is dissolved. Boil mixture, without stirring, until a golden caramel. Remove pan from heat and stir in cream. Cool sauce. Sauce may be made 2 days ahead and chilled, covered. Bring sauce to room temperature before using. Creamy Caramel Sauce has 625.0 calories. Creamy Caramel Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Creamy Caramel Sauce has 22.0 grams of fat. Creamy Caramel Sauce has 60.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Caramel Sauce are 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup water, 1/4 cup light corn syrup, 6 tablespoons heavy cream.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

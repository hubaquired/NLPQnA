Marjoram-Honey Vinaigrette recipe. Combine the first 6 ingredients in a mixing bowl. Let stand for 30 minutes. The ingredients of Marjoram-Honey Vinaigrette are 1/3 cup champagne vinegar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 shallot, finely chopped, 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped, 2/3 cup grape-seed oil, Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

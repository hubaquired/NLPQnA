Asian Pear and Frisée Salad recipe.  Boil vinegar with sugar and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a small heavy saucepan, stirring, until reduced by half, about 3 minutes. Transfer to a cup. Wash leeks and pat dry. Cook in oil with 1/4 teaspoon salt in cleaned saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes. Arrange frisée and pear on plates. Drizzle with hot leeks in oil, then vinegar reduction. Grind pepper over salads. Asian Pear and Frisée Salad has 228.0 calories. Asian Pear and Frisée Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Asian Pear and Frisée Salad has 14.0 grams of fat. Asian Pear and Frisée Salad has 43.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Asian Pear and Frisée Salad are 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar, 1 teaspoon packed brown sugar, 2 medium leeks (white and pale green parts only), halved lengthwise and thinly sliced, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/2 pound frisée, torn (8 cups), 1 large Asian pear (8 to 10 ounces), thinly sliced.Juicy Asian pear and a balsamic reduction play against the bitter edge of frisée—further mellowed by leeks hot from the pan.

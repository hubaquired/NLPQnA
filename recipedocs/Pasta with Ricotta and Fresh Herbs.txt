Pasta with Ricotta and Fresh Herbs recipe. Blend ricotta cheese, milk and Parmesan in processor until smooth. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add onion; sauté until beginning to brown, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and sauté 2 minutes. Add ricotta mixture, basil, chives and parsley to skillet; stir until heated through, about 5 minutes. Mix in rotelle. Season with salt and pepper. Pasta with Ricotta and Fresh Herbs has 292.0 calories. Pasta with Ricotta and Fresh Herbs has 19.0 grams of protein. Pasta with Ricotta and Fresh Herbs has 20.0 grams of fat. Pasta with Ricotta and Fresh Herbs has 309.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pasta with Ricotta and Fresh Herbs are 1 15-ounce container low-fat ricotta cheese, 2/3 cup nonfat milk, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 2 teaspoons olive oil, 1 cup chopped onion, 2 garlic cloves, chopped, 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil, 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives or green onions, 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, 12 ounces.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

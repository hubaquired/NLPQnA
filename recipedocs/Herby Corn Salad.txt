Herby Corn Salad recipe. Whisk 1/4 cup olive oil and 2 tablespoons Sherry vinegar in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Add 1 thinly sliced small shallot and 1/2 cup torn fresh herbs such as dill, mint, and/or chives). Fold in 3 cups raw or cooled blanched fresh corn kernels; season with salt and pepper.

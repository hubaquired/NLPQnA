Quince Sambal recipe. Peel and core the quince, and grate coarsely or slice into the finest slivers. Pile in a bowl, sprinkle with salt and set aside for 1-2 hours. Rinse under cold running water, drain well, and dry thoroughly with a clean tea towel. Mix with the remaining ingredients, cover and chill until required. The ingredients of Quince Sambal are 1 ripe quince, salt, 1 small onion, grated, 2 ml (1/2 teaspoon) crushed garlic, 1 small red or green chilli, sliced, seeded and finely chopped, 30 ml (2 tablespoons) sugar, 30 ml (2 tablespoons) lemon juice.This recipe originally accompanied Lobster Curry.

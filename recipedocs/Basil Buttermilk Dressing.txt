Basil Buttermilk Dressing recipe.  Mince garlic and mash to a paste with salt using a large heavy knife. Whisk together buttermilk, mayonnaise, basil, and garlic paste in a small bowl. Chill, covered, 1 hour to allow flavors to develop. Basil Buttermilk Dressing has 301.0 calories. Basil Buttermilk Dressing has 3.0 grams of protein. Basil Buttermilk Dressing has 30.0 grams of fat. Basil Buttermilk Dressing has 635.0 grams of sodium.

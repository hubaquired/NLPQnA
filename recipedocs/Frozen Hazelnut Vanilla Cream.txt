Frozen Hazelnut Vanilla Cream recipe. In a blender, grind the fine hazelnuts, add Frangelico, cognac, ice cream and 1 cup cracked ice. Blend the mixture, scraping down the sides occasionally, until it is smooth but still frozen. Divide the mixture between 2 stemmed glasses. The ingredients of Frozen Hazelnut Vanilla Cream are 1/4 cup hazelnuts, toasted lightly, rubbed in a dish towel to remove some of the skins, and cooled, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) Frangelico, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) cognac, 1 cup vanilla ice cream, softened slightly.

Herbed Shrimp Dip recipe. Bring medium saucepan of lightly salted water to boil. Add shrimp and cook just until bright pink and opaque in center, about 3 minutes. Drain; rinse under cold water. Peel and devein shrimp; transfer to processor. Add green onions, shallot, and tarragon; using on/off turns, process until shrimp are finely chopped. Transfer shrimp mixture to medium bowl. Mix in mayonnaise and next 3 ingredients. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and chill at least 2 hours. Can be made 1 day ahead. Keep chilled.) Herbed Shrimp Dip has 304.0 calories. Herbed Shrimp Dip has 9.0 grams of protein. Herbed Shrimp Dip has 29.0 grams of fat. Herbed Shrimp Dip has 506.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Herbed Shrimp Dip are 1 pound uncooked large shrimp, unpeeled, 2 green onions, coarsely chopped, 1 shallot, chopped, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon, 1 cup mayonnaise, 1 cup sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce.This crowd-pleasing dip is a natural with potato chips and good with crudités, too.

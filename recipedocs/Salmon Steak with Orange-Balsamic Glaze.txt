Salmon Steak with Orange-Balsamic Glaze recipe.  Place first 7 ingredients in a large sealable plastic bag. Shake well. Add salmon and refrigerate 30 minutes to 1 hour. Remove steaks from marinade and pat dry with a paper towel. Coat grill with cooking spray and heat on high. Grill steaks, turning once halfway through, until no longer translucent in the center, about 4 minutes per side. The ingredients of Salmon Steak with Orange-Balsamic Glaze are 1/4 cup orange juice concentrate, 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, 4 salmon steaks (about 6 ounces each), Vegetable cooking spray.

Zucchini Noodles with Anchovy Butter recipe. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add anchovies and cook, breaking them up with a wooden spoon, until dissolved, about 5 minutes. Combine zucchini ribbons, Parmesan, red pepper flakes, and anchovy butter in a large serving bowl. Serve immediately. Zucchini Noodles with Anchovy Butter has 137.0 calories. Zucchini Noodles with Anchovy Butter has 6.0 grams of protein. Zucchini Noodles with Anchovy Butter has 11.0 grams of fat. Zucchini Noodles with Anchovy Butter has 288.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Zucchini Noodles with Anchovy Butter are 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, 6 oil-packed anchovy fillets, 2-3 large zucchini, thinly sliced into noodle-like ribbons with a vegetable peeler or mandoline (about 6 cups), 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes.

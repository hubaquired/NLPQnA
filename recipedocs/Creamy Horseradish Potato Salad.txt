Creamy Horseradish Potato Salad recipe. Whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, horseradish, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until smooth. Add chives and potatoes and stir to coat. Creamy Horseradish Potato Salad has 341.0 calories. Creamy Horseradish Potato Salad has 5.0 grams of protein. Creamy Horseradish Potato Salad has 18.0 grams of fat. Creamy Horseradish Potato Salad has 453.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Horseradish Potato Salad are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/3 cup sour cream, 3 tablespoons bottled white horseradish (not drained), 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 1/3 cup chopped fresh chives, 3 pounds cooked small (2-inch) boiling potatoes, cooled to room temperature and quartered.

American Beauty recipe.  Combine the brandy, vermouth, crème de menthe, orange juice, and grenadine in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass. Float the port on top. American Beauty has 158.0 calories. American Beauty has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of American Beauty are 1 ounce brandy, 1/2 ounce dry vermouth, 1/4 teaspoon white crème de menthe, 1 ounce orange juice, 1 teaspoon grenadine, 1 ounce tawny port, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

Cointreau Spritz recipe. Pour 4 ounces dry Prosecco or other sparkling white wine into an ice-filled large wine or rocks glass. Add 2 ounces strained fresh orange juice and 1/2 ounce Cointreau and top off with club soda. Gently stir together; garnish with half of an orange wheel. Cointreau Spritz has 237.0 calories.

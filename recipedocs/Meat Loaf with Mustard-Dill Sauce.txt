Meat Loaf with Mustard-Dill Sauce recipe.  Preheat oven to 450°F. Stir 4 tablespoons sour cream, 1 tablespoon dill and 1 teaspoon mustard in small bowl for sauce. Combine beef, pork, breadcrumbs, onion and allspice in large bowl. Add remaining 2 tablespoons sour cream, 1 1/2 tablespoons dill and 3 teaspoons mustard and blend well. Shape mixture into 4 oval loaves, each about 3/4 inch thick. Place loaves on baking sheet. Bake until cooked through, about 20 minutes. Serve meat loaves with sauce.

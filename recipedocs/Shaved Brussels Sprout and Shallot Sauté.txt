Shaved Brussels Sprout and Shallot Sauté recipe. Working in small batches, place brussels sprouts in feed tube of processor fitted with thin slicing disk; slice. Melt butter with olive oil in large pot over medium heat. Add shallots; sauté until almost translucent, about 3 minutes. Add garlic; stir 1 minute. Add brussels sprouts; increase heat to medium-high and sauté until tender, about 8 minutes. Stir in 3 tablespoons pine nuts and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl. Sprinkle with remaining 1 tablespoon pine nuts and serve. Shaved Brussels Sprout and Shallot Sauté has 174.0 calories. Shaved Brussels Sprout and Shallot Sauté has 5.0 grams of protein. Shaved Brussels Sprout and Shallot Sauté has 11.0 grams of fat. Shaved Brussels Sprout and Shallot Sauté has 31.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shaved Brussels Sprout and Shallot Sauté are 1 3/4 pounds brussels sprouts, outer leaves removed, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 12 medium shallots, thinly sliced (about 2 cups), 6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced, 4 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted, divided, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice.

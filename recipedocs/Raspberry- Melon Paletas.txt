Raspberry- Melon Paletas recipe.  Remove skin and seeds from half of a 3-pound cantaloupe. Purée flesh with 1/2 pint fresh raspberries, 1 cup simple syrup, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, and 2 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar until smooth. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a large measuring cup, divide among ice-pop molds, insert sticks, and freeze until solid, at least 6 hours. Raspberry- Melon Paletas has 145.0 calories. Raspberry- Melon Paletas has 1.0 grams of protein. Raspberry- Melon Paletas has 37.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Raspberry- Melon Paletas are 1/2 3-pound cantaloupe, 1/2 pint fresh raspberries, 1 cup simple syrup, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar.

Roasted Red Pepper Soup with Orange Cream recipe.  Heat oil in heavy medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Add shallots and sauté 5 minutes. Add red peppers with their liquid. Stir in sugar; sauté 2 minutes. Add 2 cups broth and simmer 5 minutes. Cool soup slightly. Working in batches, puree soup in blender. Return soup to pan. Bring to simmer; stir in orange juice. Thin soup with additional broth, if desired. Season with salt and pepper. Whisk whipping cream and orange peel in small bowl until slightly thickened. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle soup into bowls. Drizzle orange cream over. Sprinkle with basil and serve. Roasted Red Pepper Soup with Orange Cream has 114.0 calories. Roasted Red Pepper Soup with Orange Cream has 4.0 grams of protein. Roasted Red Pepper Soup with Orange Cream has 7.0 grams of fat. Roasted Red Pepper Soup with Orange Cream has 46.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Red Pepper Soup with Orange Cream are 1 tablespoon olive oil, 2/3 cup sliced shallots (about 4), 1 15-ounce jar roasted red peppers packed in water, 1 teaspoon sugar, 2 cups (or more) low-salt chicken broth, 1/2 cup orange juice, 2 tablespoons whipping cream, 3/4 teaspoon grated orange peel, Thinly sliced fresh basil leaves.A fantastic way to use jarred peppers.

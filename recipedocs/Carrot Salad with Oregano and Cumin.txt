Carrot Salad with Oregano and Cumin recipe.  In a bowl whisk together oregano, cumin, zest, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste and whisk in oil in a stream until dressing is emulsified. Add carrots and toss to combine well. Carrot Salad with Oregano and Cumin has 86.0 calories. Carrot Salad with Oregano and Cumin has 1.0 grams of protein. Carrot Salad with Oregano and Cumin has 7.0 grams of fat. Carrot Salad with Oregano and Cumin has 40.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Carrot Salad with Oregano and Cumin are 2 tablespoons fresh oregano leaves, chopped fine, a rounded 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, or to taste, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1 pound carrots (about 8 medium), peeled and shredded fine.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

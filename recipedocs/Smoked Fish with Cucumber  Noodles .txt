Smoked Fish with Cucumber  Noodles  recipe.  Whisk together yogurt, horseradish, mustard, and lemon juice. Season with 1/4 teaspoon salt. Cut thin ribbons of cucumber with slicer, then very thinly slice onion. Toss with half of yogurt sauce. Discard skin from fish and break fish into large flakes.

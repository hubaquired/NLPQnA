Cucumber-Yogurt Salad with Mint recipe. Place cucumbers in colander. Sprinkle with salt. Let stand 15 minutes. Rinse cucumbers. Drain on paper towels. Combine mint, garlic, and oregano in large bowl. Stir in yogurt and cream. Add cucumber and stir to coat. Season with pepper. Refrigerate until chilled. Can be prepared 5 hours ahead.) Cucumber-Yogurt Salad with Mint has 62.0 calories. Cucumber-Yogurt Salad with Mint has 2.0 grams of protein. Cucumber-Yogurt Salad with Mint has 3.0 grams of fat. Cucumber-Yogurt Salad with Mint has 603.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber-Yogurt Salad with Mint are 3 medium cucumbers, peeled, seeded, diced, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 1 tablespoon fresh mint, minced, 1 garlic clove, minced, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1 8-ounce container plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons whipping cream, Pepper.

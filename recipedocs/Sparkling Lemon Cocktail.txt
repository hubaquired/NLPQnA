Sparkling Lemon Cocktail recipe. Muddle lemons, sugar cubes, and bitters in a large measuring glass until lemon slices are softened and sugar is crushed. Divide mixture among 8 wine glasses; fill glasses with Prosecco, pouring very slowly. Sparkling Lemon Cocktail has 177.0 calories. Sparkling Lemon Cocktail has 10.0 grams of sodium.

Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter 8x8x2-inch glass baking dish. Cook sausage in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat until brown, breaking into small pieces with back of spoon, about 5 minutes. Mix in flour, then milk. Cook until mixture thickens and comes to boil, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes. Arrange potatoes in prepared dish. Top with 1/3 of green onions, 1 cup cheese, 1/3 of green onions, sausage mixture and remaining 1/4 cup cheese. Bake casserole until potatoes are tender, about 45 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining green onions and serve. Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole has 421.0 calories. Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole has 21.0 grams of protein. Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole has 29.0 grams of fat. Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole has 743.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole are 1 pound bulk breakfast sausage, 2 tablespoons all purpose flour, 1 1/2 cups milk (do not use low-fat or nonfat), 1 1-pound package frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, 4 green onions, finely chopped, 1 1/4 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese.

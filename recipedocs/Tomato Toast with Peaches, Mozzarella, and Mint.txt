Tomato Toast with Peaches, Mozzarella, and Mint recipe. Spread each toast evenly with butter and layer mozzarella, tomatoes, and peaches on top. Top with mint, drizzle with oil, and season with sea salt. The ingredients of Tomato Toast with Peaches, Mozzarella, and Mint are 6 thick slices brioche or pain de mie, toasted, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature, 5 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced 1/4" thick, 2 very ripe heirloom tomatoes, sliced 1/4" thick, 2 very ripe peaches, sliced 1/4" thick, Fresh mint leaves, Extra-virgin olive oil, Flaky sea salt.In love with Caprese salad? Then you'll definitely want to try this new take on the classic, which gets a dose of sweetness from ripe peaches.

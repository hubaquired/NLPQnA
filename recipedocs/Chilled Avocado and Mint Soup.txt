Chilled Avocado and Mint Soup recipe.  Combine first 7 ingredients in processor. Add 4 tablespoons mint; blend until smooth. Transfer to large bowl. Gradually whisk in 3 cups broth. Season soup to taste with salt and pepper. Cover; chill until cold, about 2 hours. Can be made 6 hours ahead. Keep chilled. Thin with more broth by 1/4 cupfuls, if desired. Rewhisk before serving.) Ladle soup into bowls. Garnish with diced tomatoes and remaining 2 tablespoons mint. Chilled Avocado and Mint Soup has 184.0 calories. Chilled Avocado and Mint Soup has 7.0 grams of protein. Chilled Avocado and Mint Soup has 13.0 grams of fat. Chilled Avocado and Mint Soup has 234.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chilled Avocado and Mint Soup are 4 cups diced peeled avocados (about 4 medium), 3 1/4 cups chilled buttermilk, 5 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup chopped green onions, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 teaspoon minced seeded serrano chile, 1 teaspoon chili powder, 6 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves, divided, 3 cups (or more) low-salt chicken broth, 1 cup diced seeded tomatoes.Fresh mint makes this starter all the more cooling. Use Haas avocados for the creamiest texture. What to drink: Throughout the meal, go with the classics — beer and Margaritas.

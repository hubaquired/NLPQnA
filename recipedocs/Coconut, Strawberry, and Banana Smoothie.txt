Coconut, Strawberry, and Banana Smoothie recipe. Place first 4 ingredients in blender. Cover; blend until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. Divide smoothie among 4 glasses; garnish with strawberries. The ingredients of Coconut, Strawberry, and Banana Smoothie are 3/4 cup whole milk, 3/4 cup canned sweetened cream of coconut (such as Coco López), 1 15- to 16-ounce bag frozen unsweetened strawberries, 1 medium banana, peeled, diced, Fresh strawberries.Anne Marie Gaspard of Gros Islet, St. Lucia, writes: "I've always enjoyed cooking — for my family and for myself. My children are grown, so when they visit on Sundays, I spoil them with good home-cooked meals. During the week I like to make dishes that are different but still easy.".

Grapefruit Gimlet recipe.  Place first 4 ingredients in cocktail shaker; fill with ice. Cover and shake quickly and vigorously for 8 seconds. Strain into 2 Martini glasses. Garnish with lime slices. Grapefruit Gimlet has 192.0 calories. Grapefruit Gimlet has 13.0 grams of sodium. Ruby Red grapefruit vodka from Napa Valley stands in for gin, and fresh lime juice adds just the right amount of tartness.

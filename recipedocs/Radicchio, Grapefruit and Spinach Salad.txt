Radicchio, Grapefruit and Spinach Salad recipe. Combine vinegar and fennel seeds in medium bowl. Gradually whisk in oil. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Cut all peel and white pith from grapefruits. Cut grapefruits between membranes to release segments. Stir segments into dressing. Let stand at least 15 minutes and up to 1 hour. Toss radicchio, spinach and olives in bowl. Add grapefruit segments and dressing to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Radicchio, Grapefruit and Spinach Salad has 220.0 calories. Radicchio, Grapefruit and Spinach Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Radicchio, Grapefruit and Spinach Salad has 19.0 grams of fat. Radicchio, Grapefruit and Spinach Salad has 115.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Radicchio, Grapefruit and Spinach Salad are 5 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon fennel seeds, crushed, 1/2 cup olive oil, 2 white grapefruits, 1 10-ounce head radicchio, torn into bite-size pieces, 8 ounces baby spinach leaves, 1/2 cup Kalamata olives or other brine-cured black olives, pitted.Light, refreshing and beautiful.

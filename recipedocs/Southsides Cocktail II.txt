Southsides Cocktail II recipe.  In a small saucepan bring water and sugar to a boil, stirring, and boil until sugar is completely dissolved. Cool syrup. Syrup may be made 2 weeks ahead and chilled, covered. In a blender, blend lemon juice, 2/3 cup syrup, and mint until completely smooth and add additional syrup to taste. Lemon mixture may be made 4 hours ahead and chilled in a sealable container. Fill 4 glasses with ice and add liquor and lemon mixture. Top off Southsides with seltzer or club soda. Southsides Cocktail II has 356.0 calories. Southsides Cocktail II has 36.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Southsides Cocktail II are 1 1/3 cups fresh lemon juice, 2/3 cup simple syrup, 1/4 cup packed fresh mint leaves, 6 ounces gin, vodka, or rum, or to taste seltzer or club soda (optional), 1 cup water, 1 cup sugar.Lemon Gin Cocktail.

Spinach and Celery Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette recipe. Combine celery and spinach in large bowl. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over; sprinkle generously with salt and pepper and toss to coat. Divide among 6 plates. Top each with 2 Asiago-Pepper Frico. Spinach and Celery Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette has 91.0 calories. Spinach and Celery Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette has 1.0 grams of protein. Spinach and Celery Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette has 9.0 grams of fat. Spinach and Celery Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette has 35.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spinach and Celery Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette are 4 large celery stalks, very thinly sliced on diagonal, 6 ounces baby spinach leaves (about 10 cups loosely packed), 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoons fresh Meyer lemon juice or regular lemon juice, Asiago-Pepper Frico.Asiago frico — easy-to-make crispy cheese wafers — top this salad.

Bing Cherry and Raspberry Sauce recipe. Combine cherries, 1 cup raspberries, sugar and cinnamon in saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to boil, stirring until sugar dissolves. Reduce heat; simmer until cherries soften, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir brandy and 1 cup raspberries into sauce. Discard cinnamon sticks. Serve warm or at room temperature. Bing Cherry and Raspberry Sauce has 110.0 calories. Bing Cherry and Raspberry Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Bing Cherry and Raspberry Sauce has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bing Cherry and Raspberry Sauce are 2 cups coarsely chopped pitted Bing cherries, 2 cups fresh raspberries or frozen unsweetened, thawed, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 cinnamon sticks, broken in half, 4 teaspoons brandy.Try this cinnamon-scented sauce over vanilla ice cream, pound cake or both.

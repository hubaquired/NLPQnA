Sweet Shallot Vinaigrette recipe. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, cook the shallots and sugar, stirring constantly, until the shallots start to soften and brown, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat and combine the caramelized shallots, vinegar, and oil in a blender and mix until well blended. Season with salt and pepper. Chill for at least an hour before using. Use immediately or keep for up to 2 weeks. Sweet Shallot Vinaigrette has 128.0 calories. Sweet Shallot Vinaigrette has 14.0 grams of fat. Sweet Shallot Vinaigrette has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sweet Shallot Vinaigrette are 1/2 cup minced shallots, 1 tablespoon brown sugar, 1/2 cup white wine vinegar, 1 cup canola oil, Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper.This is a light and very easy vinaigrette.

Spinach-Garlic Yogurt recipe.  Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add half of spinach and cook, tossing often, until just wilted; transfer to a colander and let cool. Repeat with 1 tablespoon oil and remaining spinach. Squeeze spinach well to remove excess water, then coarsely chop. Mix chopped spinach, garlic, yogurt, cilantro, and remaining 1 tablespoon oil in a medium bowl; season with salt. Cover and chill at least 1 hour before serving. DO AHEAD: Spinach-garlic yogurt can be made 1 day ahead. Keep chilled garlic flavor will intensify as it sits). The ingredients of Spinach-Garlic Yogurt are 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided, 2 11-ounce packages baby spinach, 1 garlic clove, finely grated, 1 1/2 cups plain 2% fat Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro, Kosher salt.This light, tangy yogurt accompaniment wakes up any dish.

Lime Sugar recipe. Remove zest from limes in strips with a vegetable peeler and cut away any white pith from zest pith imparts a bitter flavor). Chop zest about 1/2 cup), then grind in a food processor with sugar until mixture is pale green with bits of zest still visible. Lime Sugar has 310.0 calories. Lime Sugar has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lime Sugar are 6 limes, 2 1/4 cups sugar.This sugar, used in the cookies and lime ice, is great to have on hand for sprinkling on cut fruit or to add a boost to iced tea.

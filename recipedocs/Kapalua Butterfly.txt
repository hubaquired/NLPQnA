Kapalua Butterfly recipe. Combine first 6 ingredients in blender. Chill in freezer 30 minutes.Add ice to pitcher; blend until ice is finely crushed. Pour into brandy glasses. The ingredients of Kapalua Butterfly are 2/3 cup pineapple juice, 1/2 cup dark rum, 6 tablespoons canned cream of coconut (such as Coco López)*, 1/4 cup sweet and sour mix, 2 tablespoons grenadine, 2 teaspoons frozen orange juice concentrate, unthawed, 20 (about) ice cubes, Available in the liquor department of most supermarkets..

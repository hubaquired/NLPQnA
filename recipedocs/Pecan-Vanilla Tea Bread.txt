Pecan-Vanilla Tea Bread recipe.  Preheat oven to 325°F. Butter and flour 9– to 10–cup nonstick Bundt pan. Whisk flour, pudding mix, and nutmeg in medium bowl. Using electric mixer, beat sugar and eggs in large bowl 5 minutes. Blend in dry ingredients, then butter batter will be thick). Fold in buttermilk, then nuts and raisins. Transfer to prepared pan. Bake bread until tester inserted near center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool in pan 5 minutes. Turn out onto rack; cool. Pecan-Vanilla Tea Bread has 303.0 calories. Pecan-Vanilla Tea Bread has 4.0 grams of protein. Pecan-Vanilla Tea Bread has 14.0 grams of fat. Pecan-Vanilla Tea Bread has 278.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pecan-Vanilla Tea Bread are 2 cups self–rising flour, 1/4 cup instant vanilla pudding mix, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1 1/2 cups sugar, 3 large eggs, 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted, 1/4 cup buttermilk, 1 1/4 cups pecan pieces, lightly toasted, 1/3 cup raisins.

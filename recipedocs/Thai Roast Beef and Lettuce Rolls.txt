Thai Roast Beef and Lettuce Rolls recipe. Whisk together lime juice, fish sauce, sugar, garlic, and chile in a medium bowl until sugar is dissolved. Toss roast beef with half of sauce to coat. Season with salt. Arrange leaves, sprigs, and carrots on a platter and serve with beef. To assemble, fill each lettuce leaf with a slice of beef, a mint sprig, a cilantro sprig, and some carrots. Serve with remaining sauce. *Available at Asian markets and some supermarkets. Thai Roast Beef and Lettuce Rolls has 135.0 calories. Thai Roast Beef and Lettuce Rolls has 11.0 grams of protein. Thai Roast Beef and Lettuce Rolls has 6.0 grams of fat. Thai Roast Beef and Lettuce Rolls has 740.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Thai Roast Beef and Lettuce Rolls are 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup Asian fish sauce*, 1/4 cup granulated or light brown sugar, 2 large garlic cloves, minced, 2 teaspoons minced small fresh green chile such as Thai or serrano, including seeds, or 1 teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes, 16 very thin slices rare roast beef (3/4 to 1 lb), 16 large lettuce leaves, from 1 head Boston or 2 heads Bibb, 16 fresh mint sprigs, 16 fresh cilantro sprigs, 2 carrots, cut into 1/8-inch-thick matchsticks.Active time: 15 min Start to finish: 15 min.

Sweet Chutney recipe. Blend all ingredients in processor. Transfer to bowl. Can be prepared 2 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Sweet Chutney has 266.0 calories. Sweet Chutney has 22.0 grams of fat. Sweet Chutney has 182.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sweet Chutney are 1 cup mayonnaise or reduced calorie mayonnaise, 2/3 cup Major Grey's Chutney, 1 teaspoon curry powder, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper.This recipe was created to accompany Crudités and Grilled Sausages with Sweet and Hot Chutneys.

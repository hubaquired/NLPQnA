Swiss Cheese with Walnuts and Mustard recipe.  In a bowl whisk together the mustard, the sour cream, the tarragon, the scallion, and pepper to taste, add the Swiss cheese and the walnuts, and combine the mixture well. Serve the mixture with the crackers. Swiss Cheese with Walnuts and Mustard has 267.0 calories. Swiss Cheese with Walnuts and Mustard has 17.0 grams of protein. Swiss Cheese with Walnuts and Mustard has 21.0 grams of fat. Swiss Cheese with Walnuts and Mustard has 94.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Swiss Cheese with Walnuts and Mustard are 3 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard, 3 tablespoons sour cream, 1/4 teaspoon dried tarragon, crumbled, 1/2 cup thinly sliced scallion, 2 1/2 cups coarsely grated Swiss cheese (about 1/2 pound), 1/2 cup chopped walnuts, toasted lightly and cooled, crackers as an accompaniment.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

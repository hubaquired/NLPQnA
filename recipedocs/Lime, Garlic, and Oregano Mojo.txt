Lime, Garlic, and Oregano Mojo recipe. With a wire whisk, blend the olive oil, lime juice, garlic, cumin, oregano, and salt in a mixing bowl. Keep refrigerated for up to 2 days. The ingredients of Lime, Garlic, and Oregano Mojo are 3/4 cup olive oil, 1 1/2 cups freshly squeezed lime juice, 3 tablespoons minced garlic, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/2 cup chopped fresh oregano leaves, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt.This is the "he-man" of mojos, and the classic combination of lime, garlic, and oregano is a must for grilled red meats.

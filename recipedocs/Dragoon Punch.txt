Dragoon Punch recipe. The ingredients of Dragoon Punch are 3 pints porter, 3 pints ale, 1/2 pint brandy, 1/2 pint sherry, 1/2 cup sugar syrup, 3 large lemons, thinly sliced, Block of ice, 2 bottles chilled champagne.This is said to be the cavalryman's answer to Artillery Punch.

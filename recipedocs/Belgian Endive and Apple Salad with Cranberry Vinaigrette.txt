Belgian Endive and Apple Salad with Cranberry Vinaigrette recipe. Whisk oil, cranberry juice concentrate and vinegar in small bowl to blend. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Combine endive and apples in medium bowl. Pour dressing over; toss to coat. Sprinkle cranberries, green onions and walnuts over and serve. The ingredients of Belgian Endive and Apple Salad with Cranberry Vinaigrette are 2 tablespoons olive oil (preferably extra-virgin), 2 tablespoons frozen cranberry juice concentrate, thawed, 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar, 3 heads Belgian endive, thinly sliced crosswise, 2 Red Delicious or Fuji apples, unpeeled, cored, chopped, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cranberries, 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions, 1/4 cup walnuts, toasted, coarsely chopped.A crunchy and colorful salad with a lively dressing. Pomegranate juice and pomegranate seeds can be substituted for the cranberry juice concentrate and cranberries.

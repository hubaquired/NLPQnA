White Chocolate Frosting recipe. Stir 18 ounces white chocolate in top of double boiler set over barely simmering water until melted and smooth do not let top pan touch water). Remove from over water; cool to barely lukewarm. Using electric mixer, beat 2 1/2 packages cream cheese and 1 1/2 cups butter in large bowl until fluffy. Gradually add melted white chocolate, beating to blend well. Add 1/2 cup liqueur in 4 additions, beating to blend after each. Reserve remaining ingredients for second batch of frosting. The ingredients of White Chocolate Frosting are 36 ounces good-quality white chocolate (such as Baker's or Lindt,), chopped, 5 8-ounce packages cream cheese, room temperature, 3 cups (6 sticks)unsalted butter, room temperature, 1 cup Cointreau or other orange liqueur.This recipe was created to accompany Chocolate Strawberry Orance Wedding Cake .

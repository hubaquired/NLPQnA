Rice Pudding with Cranberry Walnut Sauce recipe.  In a heavy saucepan, simmer milk, rice, sugar, salt and 1/2 cup half-and-half, covered, until rice is tender and most liquid is absorbed, about 30 minutes. While rice is cooking, in a small saucepan simmer jelly, water, cranberries or raisins, and lemon juice, covered, stirring occasionally, until jelly melts and sauce thickens slightly. Keep sauce warm. In a measuring cup, beat together with a fork egg, vanilla and remaining 1/4 cup half-and-half. Remove rice from heat and stir in egg mixture. Cook rice pudding over low heat, stirring constantly, until thick and creamy, about 5 minutes. Stir walnuts into sauce and serve rice pudding with sauce. The ingredients of Rice Pudding with Cranberry Walnut Sauce are 1 1/3 cups milk, 1/2 cup converted rice, 3 tablespoons sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup half-and-half, 1/4 cup red currant jelly, 1/4 cup water, 2 tablespoons dried cranberries or raisins, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 large egg, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, 1 tablespoon chopped toasted walnuts.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

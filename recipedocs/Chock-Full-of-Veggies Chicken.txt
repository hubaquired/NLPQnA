Chock-Full-of-Veggies Chicken recipe.  Stir-fry chicken in oil about 5 minutes. Add veggies. Set teriyaki sauce aside and stir-fry 5 minutes more. While chicken cooks, prepare rice. Stir sauce into chicken and veggies. Serve hot with rice. The ingredients of Chock-Full-of-Veggies Chicken are 1 lb chicken strips, 2 tsp canola oil, 1 bag (21 oz) frozen veggies (get one that comes with teriyaki sauce), 2 cups Minute brand brown rice.It's the ultimate shortcut stir-fry.

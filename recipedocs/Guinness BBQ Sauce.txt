Guinness BBQ Sauce recipe.  Boil the Guinness in a saucepan with the star anise if using) over high heat until reduced by half. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer for 5 minutes, or until thick. Cool and remove the star anise. Store in a tightly closed container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week. Guinness BBQ Sauce has 168.0 calories. Guinness BBQ Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Guinness BBQ Sauce has 1.0 grams of fat. Guinness BBQ Sauce has 1493.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Guinness BBQ Sauce are 2 cups Guinness ale, 1 star anise (optional), 1 cup ketchup, 1/4 cup dark brown mustard, 1/3 cup honey, 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce, 2 teaspoons coarse salt, 2 teaspoons ground black pepper.Best with beef, pork, lamb, game meats.

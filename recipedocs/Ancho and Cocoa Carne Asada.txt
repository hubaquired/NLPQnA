Ancho and Cocoa Carne Asada recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Stir together ancho powder, cocoa, and cinnamon. Pat steak dry and season with 3/4 teaspoon salt, then coat with spice mixture. Heat oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until it shimmers, then cook steak, turning once, 8 to 9 minutes for medium-rare. While steak cooks, wrap tortillas in foil and warm in oven. Let steak rest 5 minutes on a cutting board, then thinly slice across the grain at an angle. Serve steak with warm tortillas and coleslaw mix. Ancho and Cocoa Carne Asada has 193.0 calories. Ancho and Cocoa Carne Asada has 9.0 grams of protein.

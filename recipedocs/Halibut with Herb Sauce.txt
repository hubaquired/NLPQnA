Halibut with Herb Sauce recipe. Puree first 5 ingredients in processor. Season sauce to taste with salt and pepper. Preheat broiler. Brush fish with 2 tablespoons oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Broil until just opaque in center, about 5 minutes per side. Transfer fish to plates. Spoon sauce over and serve. Halibut with Herb Sauce has 318.0 calories. Halibut with Herb Sauce has 32.0 grams of protein. Halibut with Herb Sauce has 20.0 grams of fat. Halibut with Herb Sauce has 117.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Halibut with Herb Sauce are 6 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, 6 6-ounce halibut fillets, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.The halibut is delicious with sautéed green beans and steamed new potatoes.

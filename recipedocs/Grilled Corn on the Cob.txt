Grilled Corn on the Cob recipe.  Prepare grill. Peel back corn husks carefully, without breaking off, and discard silks. Fold husks back into place and tie ends together with strips of outer husk or kitchen string. In large bowls cover corn with cold water and soak 10 minutes. Drain corn and grill on a rack set 5 to 6 inches over glowing coals, turning occasionally, 15 minutes. Grilled Corn on the Cob has 126.0 calories. Grilled Corn on the Cob has 5.0 grams of protein. Grilled Corn on the Cob has 2.0 grams of fat. Grilled Corn on the Cob has 22.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Corn on the Cob are 8 ears corn in husks, outer layer of husks discarded or torn into strips for tying corn.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Rob Roy I recipe. Combine all ingredients except the peel in a mixing glass and stir gently to chill as well as dilute the drink. Strain into a cocktail glass. Twist the peel over the drink to release the oil and then drop it in. For a weaker drink, reduce the Scotch to 1 1/2 ounces and the vermouth to 1/2 ounce. In some recipes the bitters are omitted or replaced with orange bitters. Rob Roy I has 311.0 calories. Rob Roy I has 2.0 grams of protein. Rob Roy I has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rob Roy I are 3 ounces Scotch whisky, 1 ounce sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, 5 or 6 ice cubes, Twist orange or lemon peel.

Smoked Trout Crostini with Grilled Fennel and Red Onion recipe. On separate baking sheets, arrange bread and vegetables; brush both sides with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat) or preheat broiler. Grill or broil bread until crisp, about 2 minutes per side if grilled, and 1 to 2 minutes per side if broiled. Grill or broil vegetables until golden and just tender, about 4 minutes per side if grilled, and 3 to 4 minutes per side if broiled. DO AHEAD: Can be made 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. Arrange bread, vegetables, and trout on large platter. Garnish with fennel fronds. Serve, allowing guests to assemble crostini. Available at some supermarkets and at specialty foods stores and delicatessens. The ingredients of Smoked Trout Crostini with Grilled Fennel and Red Onion are 20 1/2-inch-thick diagonal slices crusty baguette, 20 1/2-inch wedges fresh fennel bulb (from about 2 large bulbs), fronds reserved for garnish, 20 1/2-inch wedges red onion (from 2 large onions), Olive oil, 3 4 1/2-ounce packages skinless smoked trout fillets,* broken into chunks.

Linguine with Turkey Sausage and Pesto recipe.  Heat olive oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add sausage, bell pepper, mushrooms and green onions. Sauté mixture until vegetables soften, about 7 minutes. Add pasta, pesto sauce and 1/2 cup Parmesan. Toss until mixture is combined. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Divide pasta mixture among plates. Pass additional Parmesan separately. Linguine with Turkey Sausage and Pesto has 1096.0 calories. Linguine with Turkey Sausage and Pesto has 41.0 grams of protein. Linguine with Turkey Sausage and Pesto has 70.0 grams of fat. Linguine with Turkey Sausage and Pesto has 1440.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Linguine with Turkey Sausage and Pesto are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 3/4 pound fully cooked turkey sausage (such as kielbasa), cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1 red bell pepper, diced, 6 large mushrooms (about 6 ounces), sliced, 4 green onions, thinly sliced, 3/4 pound linguine, freshly cooked, 1/2 cup purchased pesto sauce, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, Additional grated Parmesan cheese.

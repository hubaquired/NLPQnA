Dim Sum Dipper recipe. In a small bowl, combine the sambal, vinegar, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Mix and use or store. Serve as a dipping sauce for Ming Tsai's pork pot stickers and cranberry-crab rangoon . Dim Sum Dipper has 67.0 calories. Dim Sum Dipper has 5.0 grams of protein. Dim Sum Dipper has 3.0 grams of fat. Dim Sum Dipper has 3670.0 grams of sodium.

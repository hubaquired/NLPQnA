Stir-Fried Cabbage recipe. Heat vegetable oil in heavy large pot over medium-high heat. Add ginger and stir 30 seconds. Add half of cabbage and toss until wilted, about 4 minutes. Add remaining cabbage, green onions, and sesame oil. Toss until all cabbage is crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Stir-Fried Cabbage has 105.0 calories. Stir-Fried Cabbage has 2.0 grams of protein. Stir-Fried Cabbage has 7.0 grams of fat. Stir-Fried Cabbage has 30.0 grams of sodium.

Cucumber and Watermelon Salad with Hoisin-Lime Dressing recipe. Combine cucumbers and watermelon in medium bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 15 minutes and up to 4 hours. Drain; discard liquid. Whisk lime juice and hoisin sauce in small bowl to blend. Pour dressing over cucumber-watermelon mixture and toss gently. Season salad to taste with pepper. Sprinkle salad with cilantro, mint, and then peanuts. Serve immediately. Cucumber and Watermelon Salad with Hoisin-Lime Dressing has 98.0 calories. Cucumber and Watermelon Salad with Hoisin-Lime Dressing has 3.0 grams of protein. Cucumber and Watermelon Salad with Hoisin-Lime Dressing has 4.0 grams of fat. Cucumber and Watermelon Salad with Hoisin-Lime Dressing has 134.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber and Watermelon Salad with Hoisin-Lime Dressing are 1 1/2 large English hothouse cucumbers, cut into 1/2-inch pieces (about 3 cups), 3 cups 1/2-inch cubes seeded watermelon, 3 1/2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, 1/3 cup coarsely chopped lightly salted dry-roasted peanuts.A Vietnamese-style salad that makes a refreshing accompaniment to any kind of barbecued meat.

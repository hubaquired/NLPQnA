Sesame Sauce recipe. Pureé all ingredient in processor until smooth. Sesame Sauce has 118.0 calories. Sesame Sauce has 4.0 grams of protein. Sesame Sauce has 9.0 grams of fat. Sesame Sauce has 169.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sesame Sauce are 1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds, 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons instant dashi, 1 1/2 tablespoons mirin, 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce.Serve this super-easy sauce alongside Shabu-Shabu.

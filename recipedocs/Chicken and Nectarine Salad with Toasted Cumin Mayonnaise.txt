Chicken and Nectarine Salad with Toasted Cumin Mayonnaise recipe. Toast cumin in heavy small skillet over medium heat until slightly darkened, about 1 minute. Transfer to small bowl. Add mayonnaise and lemon juice; whisk dressing until smooth. Toss chicken, nectarines, celery, and onion in large bowl. Stir in dressing. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon salad into lettuce leaves and sprinkle with almonds. Divide among 4 plates and serve. Chicken and Nectarine Salad with Toasted Cumin Mayonnaise has 593.0 calories. Chicken and Nectarine Salad with Toasted Cumin Mayonnaise has 47.0 grams of protein. Chicken and Nectarine Salad with Toasted Cumin Mayonnaise has 40.0 grams of fat. Chicken and Nectarine Salad with Toasted Cumin Mayonnaise has 318.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chicken and Nectarine Salad with Toasted Cumin Mayonnaise are 2 teaspoons cumin seeds, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 5 cups shredded or diced cooked chicken, 1 1/2 cups diced firm but ripe nectarines, 3/4 cup chopped celery (about 2 stalks), 2/3 cup chopped red onion Romaine or butter lettuce leaves, 1/2 cup toasted sliced almonds.Serve with: Fresh sugar snap peas, grape tomato salad, and dinner rolls.

Chili-Braised Boneless Short Ribs recipe.  Combine first 4 ingredients in 9x13-inch baking dish. Add beef and turn to coat with marinade. Cover and refrigerate 5 hours or overnight. Preheat oven to 350°F. Turn beef ribs. Cover with foil and bake until meat is very tender when pierced with fork, turning occasionally, about 2 hours. Sprinkle with green onions and serve. The ingredients of Chili-Braised Boneless Short Ribs are 2 10-ounce cans enchilada sauce, 1 large onion, finely chopped, 1/2 cup hoisin sauce, 4 large garlic cloves, minced, 3 pounds boneless beef short ribs, 3 green onions, sliced.

Parmesan-Pepper Biscuits recipe.  Pulse cheese with butter in a food processor until combined. Add flour, yolk, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper, then pulse until a dough forms. Roll into a 6-inch log 1 1/2 inches thick). Wrap in parchment paper and chill until firm, at least 1 hour. Preheat oven to 350Â°F with rack in middle. Cut log into 1/4-inch-thick slices and arrange 1 inch apart on a baking sheet. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Bake until golden, about 15 minutes. Cool to warm on a rack. The ingredients of Parmesan-Pepper Biscuits are 1 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano or Pecorino Romano (2 ounces), 1/2 stick unsalted butter, softened, 2/3 cup all-purpose flour, 1 large egg yolk, 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper, divided.

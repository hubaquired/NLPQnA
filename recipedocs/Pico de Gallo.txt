Pico de Gallo recipe.  Place 1 cup cold water and 2 ice cubes in a medium bowl. Add onion; stir, then discard ice; drain well. Transfer to a medium bowl. Add tomatoes, cilantro, and jalapeño, if desired. Season with salt. The ingredients of Pico de Gallo are 1/2 medium white onion, diced, 2 cups diced tomatoes, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tablespoon minced jalapeño (optional), Kosher salt.This classic Mexican topping can be made with or without jalapeños. Keep in mind that adding the seeds ups the heat.

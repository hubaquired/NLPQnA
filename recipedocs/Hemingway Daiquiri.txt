Hemingway Daiquiri recipe.  Shake the ingredients in an iced shaker until good and frosty, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass. The ingredients of Hemingway Daiquiri are 1 1/2 ounces rum (lighter rum is better), 1/4 ounce Maraschino liqueur, 3/4 ounce simple syrup, 1/2 ounce grapefruit juice, 3/4 ounce lime juice.Said to be created for Ernest Hemingway by Constantino Ribalaigua Vert at El Floridita in Havana.  Liqueur Rum Alcoholic Grapefruit Lime Drink.

Chicken with Lemon and Spices recipe.  Mix chicken, lemon juice and turmeric in medium bowl. Marinate 30 minutes. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, ginger and cumin seeds and sauté until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Add chicken with marinade; sauté until most of marinade evaporates, about 3 minutes. Add tomatoes with juices, chili powder, salt and paprika. Cover; simmer 7 minutes. Uncover; simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce thickens, about 8 minutes longer. Remove from heat. Mix in sour cream. Season with salt and pepper. Chicken with Lemon and Spices has 151.0 calories. Chicken with Lemon and Spices has 6.0 grams of protein. Chicken with Lemon and Spices has 11.0 grams of fat. Chicken with Lemon and Spices has 485.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chicken with Lemon and Spices are 4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 2-inch pieces, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 large onion, chopped, 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger, 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 14 1/2-ounce can diced tomatoes in juice, 1 teaspoon chili powder, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon paprika, 2 tablespoons sour cream.Serve with basmati or regular white rice.

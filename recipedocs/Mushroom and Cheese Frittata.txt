Mushroom and Cheese Frittata recipe.  Preheat broiler. Melt butter in heavy 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and sauté until golden brown, about 8 minutes. Beat eggs and green onions to blend in bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Pour egg mixture over mushrooms and stir briefly. Let eggs begin to set around edges. Lift edges and tilt pan, letting uncooked portion flow under cooked eggs. Cook until eggs are almost set but still slightly moist, about 30 seconds. Arrange cheese slices atop frittata. Broil until cheese melts and bubbles. Slide frittata onto plate and serve. Mushroom and Cheese Frittata has 376.0 calories. Mushroom and Cheese Frittata has 23.0 grams of protein. Mushroom and Cheese Frittata has 29.0 grams of fat. Mushroom and Cheese Frittata has 170.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mushroom and Cheese Frittata are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 6 ounces mushrooms, sliced, 4 large eggs, 2 green onions, sliced, 2 8x4-inch slices Swiss cheese (about 2 ounces).

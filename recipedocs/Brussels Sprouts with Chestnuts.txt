Brussels Sprouts with Chestnuts recipe. Bring butter, salt, pepper, and 1 cup water to a boil over high heat in a deep 12-inch heavy skillet, then add Brussels sprouts and simmer, partially covered, stirring occasionally, until crisp-tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove lid and boil over moderately high heat until water is evaporated and sprouts are lightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes. Add cream and remaining 1/4 cup water and bring to a boil, stirring. Add chestnuts, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until heated through, about 2 minutes. Brussels Sprouts with Chestnuts has 196.0 calories. Brussels Sprouts with Chestnuts has 5.0 grams of protein. Brussels Sprouts with Chestnuts has 14.0 grams of fat. Brussels Sprouts with Chestnuts has 260.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Brussels Sprouts with Chestnuts are 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 1 1/4 cups water, 2 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved lengthwise (8 cups), 1 cup heavy cream, 2/3 cup bottled roasted whole chestnuts (4 oz), coarsely crumbled.A touch of cream transforms this dish into a sumptuous side that exposes the luxurious character of this often homely vegetable.

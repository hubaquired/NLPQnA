Teriyaki Salmon recipe.  Bring 1 1/2" water to a boil in a medium skillet. Season generously with salt; reduce heat to medium-low. Add salmon, skin side down, cover, and simmer gently until opaque throughout, 5–10 minutes. Transfer to a medium bowl as finished; remove skin and pour teriyaki sauce over. DO AHEAD: Salmon can be made 3 days ahead; cover and chill. Teriyaki Salmon has 119.0 calories. Teriyaki Salmon has 12.0 grams of protein. Teriyaki Salmon has 8.0 grams of fat. Teriyaki Salmon has 62.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Teriyaki Salmon are Kosher salt, 1 pound salmon fillet, cut into 8 pieces, 1/4 cup Canal House Teriyaki Sauce.The salmon absorbs more teriyaki flavor as it sits, making it even better (and breakfast that much quicker) if done in advance.

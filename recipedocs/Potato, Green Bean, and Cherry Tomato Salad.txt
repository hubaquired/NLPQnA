Potato, Green Bean, and Cherry Tomato Salad recipe.  Cook beans in pot of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Drain. Transfer to medium bowl. Refresh under cold running water. Drain well. Cook potatoes in pot of boiling salted water until just tender, about 8 minutes. Drain. Transfer to large bowl. Add 2 tablespoons orange juice and 2 tablespoons vinegar; toss to coat. Cool to room temperature, occasionally stirring gently. Add green beans, tomatoes, and parsley to potatoes. Whisk remaining 6 tablespoons orange juice, 1 tablespoon vinegar, oil, and capers in small bowl to blend. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Add dressing to salad and toss to combine. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature.) Potato, Green Bean, and Cherry Tomato Salad has 274.0 calories. Potato, Green Bean, and Cherry Tomato Salad has 6.0 grams of protein. Potato, Green Bean, and Cherry Tomato Salad has 13.0 grams of fat. Potato, Green Bean, and Cherry Tomato Salad has 220.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Potato, Green Bean, and Cherry Tomato Salad are 1 1/4 pounds slender green beans, trimmed, 2 pounds small red-skinned new potatoes, quartered, 8 tablespoons fresh orange juice, 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar, 1 1-pint basket cherry or teardrop tomatoes, halved, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Italian parsley, 1/3 cup olive oil, 6 tablespoons drained capers.Orange juice adds zip to the dressing.

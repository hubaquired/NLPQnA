Singapore Slings recipe. Stir together gin, Benedictine, kirsch, grenadine, and sugar until sugar is dissolved. Serve over ice in tall glasses with a splash of club soda. Singapore Slings has 206.0 calories. Singapore Slings has 25.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Singapore Slings are 8 ounces (1 cup) distilled gin, 8 ounces (1 cup) Benedictine liqueur, 3 ounces (1/3 cup) kirsch or other cherry-flavored brandy, 1 1/2 ounces (3 tablespoons) grenadine, 1/2 cup superfine granulated sugar, 1 liter club soda, chilled, Garnish: maraschino cherries.Heavily spiked, our take on the Singapore Sling highlights the aromatic quality of Benedictine, a Cognac-based liqueur that smells of honey, citrus, and herbs.

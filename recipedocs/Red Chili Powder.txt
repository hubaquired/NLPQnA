Red Chili Powder recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Using small sharp knife, cut stems off chilies and discard. Cut chilies open along 1 long side. Discard seeds. Place chilies on baking sheet. Roast until chilies are firm and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Cool. Break chilies into small pieces. Working in batches, finely grind chilies in spice grinder, blender or coffee grinder. Can be prepared 2 weeks ahead. Store airtight at room temperature.) *Sold at Latin American markets, specialty foods stores and some supermarkets. The ingredients of Red Chili Powder are 6 dried ancho or pasilla chilies*, 6 dried guajillo or pasilla chilies*. Blender Quick & Easy Hot Pepper Bon Appétit.

Swordfish with Balsamic Brown Butter Sauce recipe.  Simmer butter in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat until deep golden brown, swirling pan occasionally, about 6 minutes. Remove from heat. Whisk in vinegar, honey and mustard. Season sauce with salt and pepper. Brush fish with oil; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Heat heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add 3 swordfish steaks. Sauté just until opaque in center, about 4 minutes per side. Transfer to plates; tent loosely with foil to keep warm. Repeat with remaining fish. Whisk sauce over low heat to rewarm if necessary. Spoon sauce over fish. Sprinkle with capers and, if desired, tomatoes. Swordfish with Balsamic Brown Butter Sauce has 440.0 calories. Swordfish with Balsamic Brown Butter Sauce has 237.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Swordfish with Balsamic Brown Butter Sauce are 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 6 6-ounce swordfish steaks (each about 3/4 inch thick), Olive oil, 2 tablespoons drained capers, Chopped tomatoes (optional).Easy, elegant fare from chef John Jones at Renato's in Palm Beach.

Banana and Blueberry Overnight Oats recipe.  Place the banana, yogurt, almond milk and protein powder into a blender and blend until smooth. Pour the mixture into a bowl and stir in the oats, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight. When ready to eat, top with the nuts, blueberries and raspberries. Banana and Blueberry Overnight Oats has 213.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Banana and Blueberry Overnight Oats are 1 banana, roughly chopped, 1/3 cup full-fat yogurt, 1 cup almond milk, 1 scoop (30g) strawberry protein powder, 1 1/4 cups rolled oats, A handful of pistachios or other nuts, blueberries and raspberries to serve.This is a quick and easy breakfast to have ready to go, after jump-starting your day with a morning workout.

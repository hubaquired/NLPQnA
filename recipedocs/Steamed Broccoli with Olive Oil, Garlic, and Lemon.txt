Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil, Garlic, and Lemon recipe. Discard tough lower third of broccoli stem. Peel remaining stem and cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-thick slices. Cut broccoli into 2-inch florets. In a steamer set over boiling water steam broccoli, covered, until crisp-tender, 4 to 5 minutes. While broccoli is steaming, finely chop garlic and in a small skillet combine with oil, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste. Heat garlic mixture over moderate heat until garlic is fragrant. In a bowl toss broccoli with garlic mixture. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil, Garlic, and Lemon has 170.0 calories. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil, Garlic, and Lemon has 7.0 grams of protein. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil, Garlic, and Lemon has 11.0 grams of fat. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil, Garlic, and Lemon has 76.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil, Garlic, and Lemon are 1 small bunch broccoli (about 3/4 pound), 1 garlic clove, 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice.

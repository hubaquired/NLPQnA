Brown Butter Sauce with Lemon Beurre Meunière recipe. Cut butter into pieces and in a small heavy skillet melt over moderately low heat. Simmer butter until golden brown with a nutty aroma, about 5 minutes. Remove skillet from heat and immediately add lemon juice, salt, parsley, and pepper to taste, swirling skillet to incorporate butter will foam). Serve sauce immediately. Brown Butter Sauce with Lemon Beurre Meunière has 82.0 calories. Brown Butter Sauce with Lemon Beurre Meunière has 9.0 grams of fat. Brown Butter Sauce with Lemon Beurre Meunière has 69.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Brown Butter Sauce with Lemon Beurre Meunière are 1/2 stick (1/4 cup) unsalted butter, 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh flat-leafed parsley leaves.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

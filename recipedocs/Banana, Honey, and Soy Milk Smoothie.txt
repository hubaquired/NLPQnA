Banana, Honey, and Soy Milk Smoothie recipe.  Combine all ingredients in blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses and serve immediately. The ingredients of Banana, Honey, and Soy Milk Smoothie are 1 1/2 bananas, peeled, cut into 1/2-inch rounds, frozen, 1 cup light (1%) soy milk, 1 cup ice cubes, 1 tablespoon honey, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.

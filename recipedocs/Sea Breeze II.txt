Sea Breeze II recipe. In a long-stemmed glass filled with ice cubes, stir together the vodka, the grapefruit juice and the cranberry juice and garnish the drink with the grapefruit wedge. Sea Breeze II has 156.0 calories. Sea Breeze II has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sea Breeze II are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) vodka, 1/4 cup chilled grapefruit juice, 1/4 cup chilled cranberry juice, Garnish: 1 small grapefruit wedge.

Orange Banana Shake recipe.  In a blender, blend banana, orange juice, lime juice, yogurt and sugar until the shake is smooth. Pour the shake into a chilled cocktail glass, and garnish it with the orange and lime slices. Orange Banana Shake has 164.0 calories. Orange Banana Shake has 4.0 grams of protein. Orange Banana Shake has 2.0 grams of fat. Orange Banana Shake has 30.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange Banana Shake are 1/2 banana, cut into pieces, 1/2 cup fresh orange juice, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 1 teaspoon sugar, Garnish: 1 lime slice and 1 orange slice.

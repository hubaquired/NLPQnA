Cauliflower-Leek Purée recipe.  Place cauliflower in large microwave-safe bowl. Add leeks and 1 cup broth. Cover bowl with plastic wrap. Microwave on high until vegetables are very soft, about 14 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer vegetables to processor. Blend until smooth. Mix in cooking broth by tablespoonfuls to thin, if desired. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill. Rewarm, covered, in microwave before serving.) Cauliflower-Leek Purée has 23.0 calories. Cauliflower-Leek Purée has 2.0 grams of protein. Cauliflower-Leek Purée has 149.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cauliflower-Leek Purée are 6 cups 1-inch pieces cauliflower, 1 cup chopped leeks (white and pale green parts only), 1 cup canned chicken broth.This recipe originally accompanied Sugar Snap Peas and Baby Carrots with Cauliflower-Leek Purée .

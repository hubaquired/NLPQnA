Elemental Miso Soup recipe. Combine wakame and 2 Tbsp. water in a small bowl. Let sit, stirring occasionally, until softened, 25–30 minutes. Bring dashi to a simmer in a small saucepan. Add tofu and wakame and return to a very gentle simmer. Remove from heat. Submerge a fine-mesh sieve into liquid, add miso to sieve, and stir until miso dissolves. Divide among bowls and serve topped with scallion. Elemental Miso Soup has 86.0 calories. Elemental Miso Soup has 9.0 grams of protein. Elemental Miso Soup has 4.0 grams of fat. Elemental Miso Soup has 918.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Elemental Miso Soup are 1 tablespoon dried wakame seaweed, 3 cups Dashi, 1/2 cup silken tofu, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1/4 cup miso, 1 scallion, very thinly sliced.Just like the miso soup you get in restaurants, and we mean that in a good way.

The ingredients of Moroccan Beet Leaf or Swiss Chard Salad (Salade de Blettes) are 1/3 cup peanut, grapeseed, or vegetable oil, 2 bunches of Swiss chard or beet leaves with stems, coarsely chopped (about 1 pound), 4 cloves garlic, minced, Salt to taste, 1 teaspoon sweet paprika, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 teaspoon harissa*, or to taste, 1/4 cup white vinegar or lemon juice, Freshly ground pepper to taste.MOROCCAN COOKS USUALLY MAKE this tasty salad with Swiss chard, but I have seen it also with beet leaves. Eaten all year round, it is prepared by Moroccans on Rosh Hashanah for their Sephardic Seder, when they say a series of blessings over squash, leeks, dates, pomegranates, black-eyed peas, apples, the head of a fish or a lamb, and Swiss chard and beet greens.

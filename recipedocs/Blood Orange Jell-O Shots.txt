Blood Orange Jell-O Shots recipe. Soak gelatin in 1/2 cup cold water in a small bowl 10 minutes. Meanwhile, stir vodka, lemon juice, simple syrup, Campari, bitters, and 1 1/4 cups blood orange juice in a large measuring glass. Heat gelatin mixture and remaining 1/2 cup blood orange juice in a small saucepan over low, stirring to dissolve gelatin; add to vodka mixture. Strain into 2-oz. plastic cups; chill until set, at least 6 hours and up to 5 days. Blood Orange Jell-O Shots has 85.0 calories. Blood Orange Jell-O Shots has 1.0 grams of protein. Blood Orange Jell-O Shots has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blood Orange Jell-O Shots are 2 envelopes unflavored powdered gelatin (about 5 teaspoons), 1 cup vodka, 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 cup simple syrup, 1/4 cup Campari, 2 teaspoons Angostura bitters, 1 3/4 cups fresh blood orange juice, divided.Potent—but so much classier than your dive bar shots.

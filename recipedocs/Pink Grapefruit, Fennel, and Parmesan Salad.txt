Pink Grapefruit, Fennel, and Parmesan Salad recipe. Whisk vinegar and honey in large bowl to blend. Whisk in olive oil; season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Add endive, butter lettuce, and fennel to bowl; toss to coat. Top salad with grapefruit sections, then Parmesan cheese, and serve. Pink Grapefruit, Fennel, and Parmesan Salad has 135.0 calories. Pink Grapefruit, Fennel, and Parmesan Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Pink Grapefruit, Fennel, and Parmesan Salad has 9.0 grams of fat. Pink Grapefruit, Fennel, and Parmesan Salad has 12.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pink Grapefruit, Fennel, and Parmesan Salad are 1 1/2 tablespoons Champagne vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon honey, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 heads of Belgian endive, halved lengthwise, cut lengthwise into thin strips, 1 head of butter lettuce, leaves torn, 1 cup thinly sliced fresh fennel bulb, 3 small pink grapefruits, sectioned, 1 ounce Parmesan cheese, shaved with vegetable peeler.Inspired by the classic Sicilian winter salad of blood oranges and fennel.

Grilled Pineapple Salsa recipe. Prepare a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Grill the pineapple slices for 2 minutes per side, or until tender and grill-marked. Transfer the slices to a cutting board and chop them. Put the chopped pineapple in a medium bowl and stir in the cilantro, serrano chile, and lime juice. Season the salsa to taste with sugar, salt, and pepper. Serve warm. Grilled Pineapple Salsa has 47.0 calories. Grilled Pineapple Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein.

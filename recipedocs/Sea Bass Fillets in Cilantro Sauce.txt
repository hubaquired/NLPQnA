Sea Bass Fillets in Cilantro Sauce recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Arrange fish in 13 x 9 x 2-inch glass baking dish; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Puree cilantro, cream, ginger and chili in blender. Add yogurt and just blend in. Season sauce with salt and pepper; spoon over fish. Bake fish until just opaque in center, about 25 minutes. Sea Bass Fillets in Cilantro Sauce has 326.0 calories. Sea Bass Fillets in Cilantro Sauce has 33.0 grams of protein. Sea Bass Fillets in Cilantro Sauce has 20.0 grams of fat. Sea Bass Fillets in Cilantro Sauce has 140.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sea Bass Fillets in Cilantro Sauce are 6 6-ounce sea bass fillets, 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro, 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1 tablespoon chopped peeled fresh ginger, 1 tablespoon chopped seeded jalapeño chili, 1/2 cup plain yogurt.Serve the baked fish with rice and a salad of tomatoes, cucumber and green onions.

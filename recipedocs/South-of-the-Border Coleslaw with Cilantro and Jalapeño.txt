South-of-the-Border Coleslaw with Cilantro and Jalapeño recipe. Combine cabbage, bell pepper, cilantro, and jalapeño in large bowl; toss to blend. Whisk oil, lime juice, and cumin in small bowl to blend. Pour dressing over cabbage mixture. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Toss before serving.) The ingredients of South-of-the-Border Coleslaw with Cilantro and Jalapeño are 4 cups thinly sliced green cabbage (from about 1/2 large head), 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 small jalapeño chili, seeded, minced, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin.Serve this zesty salad alongside grilled chili-rubbed chicken or steak, or grilled fish tacos.

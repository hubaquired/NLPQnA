Cucumber-Lime Agua recipe.  Additional ice cubes for serving) Puree cucumbers, ice, grapes, water, and lime juice along with 10 mint leaves in blender. Strain. DO AHEAD: Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill. Fill 6 glasses with more ice; pour agua over. Garnish with additional mint sprigs. Cucumber-Lime Agua has 31.0 calories. Cucumber-Lime Agua has 1.0 grams of protein. Cucumber-Lime Agua has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber-Lime Agua are 1 pound Persian or English hothouse cucumbers, peeled, cut into 1-inch pieces, 2 cups ice cubes, 1 cup green seedless grapes, 1 cup cold water, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 10 large mint leaves plus sprigs for garnish.For a sweeter take on this cool spa drink, add 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar or agave nectar, available at Whole Foods, other natural foods stores, and at wildorganics.net.

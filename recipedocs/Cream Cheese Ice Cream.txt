Cream Cheese Ice Cream recipe.  Blend cream cheese, milk, lemon juice, sugar, and salt in a blender until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, then stir in cream. Freeze cream cheese mixture in ice cream maker. Transfer to an airtight container and put in freezer to harden, at least 2 hours. Let ice cream soften 5 minutes before serving. Cream Cheese Ice Cream has 214.0 calories. Cream Cheese Ice Cream has 3.0 grams of protein. Cream Cheese Ice Cream has 13.0 grams of fat. Cream Cheese Ice Cream has 156.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cream Cheese Ice Cream are 8 oz cream cheese, softened, 1 cup milk, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 3/4 cup sugar, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup heavy cream, Special equipment: an ice cream maker.Active time: 5 min Start to finish: 2 1/2 hr (includes freezing).

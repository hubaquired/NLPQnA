Strawberry Mosel Cup recipe. In a punch bowl, combine Mosel, cognac and Grand Marnier. Add a block of ice or ice cubes. Stir until very cold, then stir in seltzer. Garnish the punch withstrawberries. Strawberry Mosel Cup has 486.0 calories. Strawberry Mosel Cup has 1.0 grams of protein. Strawberry Mosel Cup has 45.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Mosel Cup are 2 bottles chilled Mosel (a medium dry German white wine), 2/3 cup cognac, 1/2 cup Grand Marnier, 2 cups chilled seltzer or club soda, 1 pint strawberries, hulled (and quartered if large).

Creamed Broccoli with Parmesan recipe.  Peel broccoli stems, then coarsely chop stems and florets. Cook broccoli in boiling salted water 1 1/2 teaspoons salt for 4 quarts water) until just tender, 4 to 6 minutes. Drain in a colander and run under cold water to stop cooking. Simmer cream, garlic, nutmeg, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon pepper in a medium saucepan, uncovered, until slightly thickened and reduced to about 2/3 cup, about 5 minutes. Add broccoli and simmer, mashing with a potato masher, until coarsely mashed and heated through, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in parmesan and lemon juice. The ingredients of Creamed Broccoli with Parmesan are 1 bunch broccoli (1 1/4 pounds), 1 cup heavy cream, 2 garlic cloves, smashed and peeled, 1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg, 3 tablespoons grated parmesan, 1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice.Broccoli seems like something new in this rich, satisfyingly chunky side dish, dolled up with parmesan and lemon juice.

Quick Beef with Broccoli recipe.  Toss together cornstarch, salt, pepper, and beef in a bowl until meat is coated. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in wok over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking, then stir-fry beef until just cooked through, about 1 minute. Transfer with a slotted spoon to another bowl and keep warm, loosely covered with foil. Add remaining tablespoon oil to wok along with broccoli and garlic and stir-fry over moderately high heat until broccoli is just tender and garlic is pale golden, about 2 minutes. Add soy sauce and water and bring to a boil. Return meat to skillet and cook, stirring, until sauce is thickened, about 2 minutes. The ingredients of Quick Beef with Broccoli are 1 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 3/4 pound sirloin beef tips, sliced against the grain 1/8 inch thick, 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, 10 ounces frozen broccoli florets, thawed, 3 garlic cloves, chopped, 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 1/3 cup water, Special equipment: a well-seasoned 14-inch flat-bottomed wok, Accompaniment: rice, Garnish: fresh cilantro sprigs.Who needs takeout?

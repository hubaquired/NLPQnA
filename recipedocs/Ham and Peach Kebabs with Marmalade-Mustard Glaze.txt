Ham and Peach Kebabs with Marmalade-Mustard Glaze recipe.  Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Whisk marmalade, oil, mustard and lemon juice in small bowl to blend. Alternate ham pieces and peach chunks on 4 metal skewers. Brush with marmalade glaze. Sprinkle with pepper.

Herb and Onion Focaccia recipe.  Preheat oven to 425Â°F. Unroll the pizza dough as directed on the package onto a parchment-lined cookie sheet. Sprinkle half of the herbs on the dough and fold the dough in half lengthwise. Brush the top with olive oil and evenly distribute the remaining herbs, onion, and cheese. Salt and pepper to taste. Bake for 15 minutes until the foccacia is golden brown and crispy. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and serve sliced. You can use a pizza stone or pizza pan if you have one. Make sure the pan is heated in the oven thirty minutes before you put the dough on it. The ingredients of Herb and Onion Focaccia are Pillsbury Pizza Dough: 1 Package (10 ounces), Rosemary: 2 tablespoons, finely chopped, Thyme: 2 tablespoons, chopped, Olive oil: 2 tablespoons, red onion: 1, julienned, Parmesan cheese: 2 tablespoons, grated, Salt and pepper to taste, Extra-virgin olive oil: Enough for drizzling.Editor's note: The recipe below is excerpted from Katie Brown Entertains.

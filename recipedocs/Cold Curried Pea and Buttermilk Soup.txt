The ingredients of Cold Curried Pea and Buttermilk Soup are 4 large shallots, chopped, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 2 teaspoons curry powder (preferably Madras), 3 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth, 2 cups water, 2 (10-ounce) packages frozen peas, 1 (3/4-pound) bunch spinach, stems discarded (about 8 cups packed leaves), 1 cup well-shaken buttermilk.The buttermilk in this soup gives it a light, tangy richness that harmonizes with the sweetness of the peas and the gentle bite of the curry. Though it looks lovely in a bowl, serve this soup in drinking glasses, to be sipped rather than spooned, for a nice change of pace.

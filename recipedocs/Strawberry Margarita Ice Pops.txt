Strawberry Margarita Ice Pops recipe.  Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth, then force through a fine sieve into a large glass measuring cup. Pour into molds and add sticks. Freeze at least 24 hours. Strawberry Margarita Ice Pops has 104.0 calories. Strawberry Margarita Ice Pops has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Margarita Ice Pops are 1 1/4 lb strawberries, hulled and halved, 1/2 cup white tequila, 1/2 cup superfine granulated sugar, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, Special equipment: 8 (1/3-cup) ice pop molds and 8 wooden sticks.To make these ice pops nonalcoholic, substitute freshly squeezed orange juice for the tequila.

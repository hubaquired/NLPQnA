Manhattan I recipe.  Combine the whiskey, vermouth, dash of bitters, and ice cubes in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with the cherry. Manhattan I has 205.0 calories. Manhattan I has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Manhattan I are 2 1/2 ounces blended whiskey, 3/4 ounces sweet vermouth, Dash Angostura bitters, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Maraschino cherry.

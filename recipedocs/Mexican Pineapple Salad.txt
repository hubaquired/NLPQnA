Mexican Pineapple Salad recipe. Toss together all ingredients with 3/4 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Mexican Pineapple Salad has 343.0 calories. Mexican Pineapple Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Mexican Pineapple Salad has 15.0 grams of fat. Mexican Pineapple Salad has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mexican Pineapple Salad are 1 (3-pound) pineapple, peeled and diced, 1/2 pound jicama, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch pieces, 1 (7-to 8-ounce) avocado, cut into cubes, 1 small red onion, thinly sliced (1/2 cup), 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar.Juicy pineapple meets creamy avocado, crisp jicama, and red onion in a refreshing salad that goes especially well with the cilantro-chipotle tilapia .

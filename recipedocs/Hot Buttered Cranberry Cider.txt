Hot Buttered Cranberry Cider recipe.  In a stainless steel or enameled saucepan combine the cranberry juice, the cider, and the cinnamon stick, bring the liquid to a boil, and simmer the mixture for 10 minutes. Remove the pan from the heat, stir in the rum, the honey, and the butter, and stir the mixture until the butter is melted. Ladle the cider into heated mugs. Hot Buttered Cranberry Cider has 284.0 calories. Hot Buttered Cranberry Cider has 9.0 grams of fat. Hot Buttered Cranberry Cider has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Buttered Cranberry Cider are 4 cups cranberry juice cocktail, 4 cups apple cider, One 3-inch cinnamon stick, 1 cup dark rum, 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste, 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, cut into bits.

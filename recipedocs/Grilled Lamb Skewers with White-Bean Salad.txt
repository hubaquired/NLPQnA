Grilled Lamb Skewers with White-Bean Salad recipe. Prepare grill for cooking over medium-hot charcoal moderate heat for gas). While grill is heating, mince garlic, then mash to a paste with salt and pepper using a large heavy knife. Reserve half of garlic paste in a large salad bowl. Whisk together remaining garlic paste and 2 tablespoons olive oil in another large bowl, then add lamb and toss to coat. Divide lamb among skewers, leaving a little space between pieces for even cooking). Whisk vinegar into reserved garlic paste, then add remaining 1/4 cup oil in a slow stream, whisking until emulsified. Add beans, celery, olives, pine nuts, and mint, then toss to coat. Grill lamb, turning as grill marks appear on each side, about 6 minutes total for medium-rare. Serve with bean salad. Grilled Lamb Skewers with White-Bean Salad has 1304.0 calories. Grilled Lamb Skewers with White-Bean Salad has 71.0 grams of protein. Grilled Lamb Skewers with White-Bean Salad has 76.0 grams of fat. Grilled Lamb Skewers with White-Bean Salad has 1391.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Lamb Skewers with White-Bean Salad are 4 large garlic cloves, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 2 lb boneless lamb shoulder, cut into 1-inch cubes, 2 tablespoons Sherry vinegar or red-wine vinegar, 2 (15- to 19-oz) cans cannellini beans, rinsed and drained (about 3 cups), 1 celery rib, thinly sliced, 1/4 cup brine-cured black olives, such as Kalamata, pitted and quartered, 1/2 cup pine nuts (2 1/4 oz), toasted, 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint, 6 to 8 metal skewers.Tender chunks of lamb served kebab-style are paired with a traditional Mediterranean-inspired salad in this simple grilled dish.

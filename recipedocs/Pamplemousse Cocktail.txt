Pamplemousse Cocktail recipe.  Bring first 4 ingredients to boil in small saucepan, stirring to dissolve sugar. Cool. Do ahead Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Combine pomegranate mixture, vodka, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and mint leaves in large pitcher. Fill pitcher with ice. Stir vigorously. Strain drink into Martini glasses. Garnish each glass with mint sprig and serve. Pamplemousse Cocktail has 197.0 calories. Pamplemousse Cocktail has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pamplemousse Cocktail are 7 tablespoons water, 5 tablespoons pomegranate juice, 1/4 cup sugar, 4 teaspoons honey, 1 1/2 cups vodka, 3/4 cup fresh grapefruit juice, 1/4 cup fresh lime juice, 18 fresh mint leaves plus 6 sprigs for garnish, Ice cubes.Pamplemousse is the French word for grapefruit, which is one of the main ingredients in this vodka drink.

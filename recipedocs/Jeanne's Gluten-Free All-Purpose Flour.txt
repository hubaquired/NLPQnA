Jeanne's Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour recipe.  In a large bowl, whisk together the brown and white rice flours, sweet rice flour, tapioca flour, and xanthan gum thoroughly. Transfer the mix to an airtight container. Store in a cool, dark place for up to 6 weeks or in the refrigerator for up to 4 months. Jeanne's Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour has 297.0 calories. Jeanne's Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour has 4.0 grams of protein. Jeanne's Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour has 1.0 grams of fat. Jeanne's Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour has 16.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Jeanne's Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour are 1 1/4 cups/170 grams brown rice flour, 1 1/4 cups/205 grams white rice flour, 1 cup/165 grams sweet rice flour, 1 cup/120 grams tapioca flour, scant 2 tsp xanthan gum.Editor's note: Use this gluten-free flour mixture to make Jeanne Sauvages' Spritz Cookies or Sufganiyot (Jelly Doughnuts).

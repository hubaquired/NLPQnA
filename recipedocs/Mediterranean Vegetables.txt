Mediterranean Vegetables recipe. Preheat oven to 400°F. Spray large baking sheet with nonstick spray. Toss onion and next 5 ingredients together on prepared baking sheet; spread in single layer. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake vegetables until tender and brown around edges, stirring occasionally, about 35 minutes. The ingredients of Mediterranean Vegetables are Nonstick vegetable oil spray, 1 large red onion, cut into 1/4-inch wedges, 2 medium zucchini, sliced diagonally, 2 yellow crookneck squash, sliced diagonally, 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme, 1 teaspoon olive oil.

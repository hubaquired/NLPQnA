Ginger Lime Cordial recipe. In a small pot, bring 7 ounces water, sugar, and ginger to a boil. Stir to dissolve sugar. Remove from heat, add zest, and steep for 30 minutes. Strain, add lime juice, and let cool. Ginger Lime Cordial has 125.0 calories. Ginger Lime Cordial has 1.0 grams of protein. Ginger Lime Cordial has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger Lime Cordial are 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar, 3/4 cup roughly chopped unpeeled fresh ginger (about 3 ounces), 1 tablespoon packed lime zest (from about 2 limes), 1/4 cup lime juice (from 2 to 3 limes).

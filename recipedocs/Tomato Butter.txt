Tomato Butter recipe. Broil cherry tomatoes until the skins blister and juices are released; let cool. Blitz with salt in a food processor until coarsely puréed, then stir into softened butter. Dollop onto grilled fish, cooked rice, or crostini. Tomato Butter has 3.0 calories. Tomato Butter has 40.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato Butter are Cherry tomatoes, Salt, Softened butter.Use this sweet condiment as a topping for grilled fish, cooked rice, or crostini.

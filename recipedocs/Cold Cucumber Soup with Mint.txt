Cold Cucumber Soup with Mint recipe. In a bowl with a fork mash together yolk and vinegar to form a smooth paste and stir in sour cream until smooth. In a blender purée cucumber and mint with buttermilk and salt to taste until smooth. Add purée to sour cream mixture in a stream, whisking. Divide soup between 2 chilled bowls. Cold Cucumber Soup with Mint has 203.0 calories. Cold Cucumber Soup with Mint has 6.0 grams of protein. Cold Cucumber Soup with Mint has 14.0 grams of fat. Cold Cucumber Soup with Mint has 156.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cold Cucumber Soup with Mint are 1 hard-boiled large egg yolk, 1 tablespoon rice vinegar, 1/2 cup chilled sour cream, 1 chilled seedless cucumber (about 1 pound), peeled, halved lengthwise, cored, and cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, washed and spun dry, 1/2 cup chilled well-shaken buttermilk.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Gochujang-Date Sauce recipe. Place 5 pitted Medjool dates in a small bowl; cover with boiling water and let sit until softened, about 10 minutes. Drain; transfer dates to a food processor. Add 1 cup gochujang Korean hot pepper paste) and 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil. Purée until smooth. Gochujang-Date Sauce has 140.0 calories. Gochujang-Date Sauce has 4.0 grams of protein.

Mango and Red Pepper Barbeque Sauce recipe. In a skillet heat oil over moderate heat until hot but not smoking and cook onion and bell pepper with salt until softened and edges begin to brown. Stir in vinegar, molasses, Worcestershire sauce, and allspice and bring to a boil. Simmer mixture 1 minute to blend flavors and cool slightly. Spoon mixture into a blender. Add mango and jalapeños and blend until smooth. Mango and Red Pepper Barbeque Sauce has 275.0 calories. Mango and Red Pepper Barbeque Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Mango and Red Pepper Barbeque Sauce has 10.0 grams of fat. Mango and Red Pepper Barbeque Sauce has 901.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mango and Red Pepper Barbeque Sauce are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 medium onion, chopped, 1 medium red bell pepper, chopped, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/3 cup red-wine vinegar, 3 tablespoons molasses, 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce, 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice, 1 medium mango (about 1 pound), cut into chunks, 2 to 3 fresh jalapeño chilies, or to taste, seeded (wear rubber gloves).Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Molasses-and-Spice Pumpkin Pie recipe.  Mix first 6 ingredients in large bowl to blend. Add pumpkin, eggs, cream, milk, and molasses; whisk filling to blend. Preheat oven to 350°F. Pour filling into crust. Bake pie until set in center and slightly puffed around edges, about 1 hour 5 minutes. Cool pie on rack. Molasses-and-Spice Pumpkin Pie has 247.0 calories.

Fennel, Cucumber, and Tarragon Salsa recipe. In a bowl stir together the fennel, the cucumber, the tarragon, the zest, the yogurt, the orange juice, the sugar, and salt to taste. Chill the salsa, covered, for at least 30 minutes and up to 1 hour. Serve the salsa with grilled seafood. Fennel, Cucumber, and Tarragon Salsa has 25.0 calories. Fennel, Cucumber, and Tarragon Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein. Fennel, Cucumber, and Tarragon Salsa has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fennel, Cucumber, and Tarragon Salsa are 1 fennel bulb (sometimes called anise), chopped (about 2 cups), 1 unwaxed cucumber, chopped (about 2 cups), 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon leaves, 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly grated orange zest, 1/3 cup plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice, 1 teaspoon sugar.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

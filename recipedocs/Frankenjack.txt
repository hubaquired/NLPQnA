Frankenjack recipe.  Combine all ingredients, except the cherry, in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass and drop in cherry. The ingredients of Frankenjack are 1 ounce gin, 1 ounce dry vermouth, 1/2 ounce apricot brandy, 1/2 ounce Cointreau, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Maraschino cherry.

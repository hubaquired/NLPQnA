Cream Cheese Frosting recipe. Beat cream cheese, butter and vanilla in large bowl until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in sugar. Cover and refrigerate until firm enough to spread, about 15 minutes. The ingredients of Cream Cheese Frosting are 3 8-ounce packages cream cheese, room temperature, 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature, 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar.

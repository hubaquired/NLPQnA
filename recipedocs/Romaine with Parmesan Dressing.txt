Romaine with Parmesan Dressing recipe.  Drizzle lemon juice and oil over romaine in a medium bowl and toss to combine. Add cheese and toss again. Season with salt and pepper. Romaine with Parmesan Dressing has 249.0 calories. Romaine with Parmesan Dressing has 11.0 grams of protein. Romaine with Parmesan Dressing has 21.0 grams of fat. Romaine with Parmesan Dressing has 399.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Romaine with Parmesan Dressing are 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 2 1/2 cups 1-inch pieces hearts of romaine (from 1 head), 2 tablespoons finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.

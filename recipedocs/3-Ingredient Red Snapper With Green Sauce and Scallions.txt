3-Ingredient Red Snapper With Green Sauce and Scallions recipe.  Heat 1 Tbsp. oil in a medium skillet over medium-high. Add scallions, season with salt, and cook, turning occasionally, until scallions are charred in spots and slightly softened, about 1 minute. Transfer to a plate. Wipe out skillet. Heat 2 Tbsp. oil in same skillet over medium-high. Season fish with salt and cook, skin side down, until very crisp, about 4 minutes. Flip fish, add green juice and scallions, and cook until fish is just cooked through, about 2 minutes more. Taste and season with salt, if needed. Divide fish and scallions among bowls; top with green sauce. Drizzle with oil and serve. The ingredients of 3-Ingredient Red Snapper With Green Sauce and Scallions are 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided, plus more, 2 bunches scallions, Kosher salt, 4 (6-ounce) skin-on red snapper or any mild white fish fillets, 3/4 cup cold-pressed, vegetable-only green juice.In Epi's 3-Ingredient Recipes series, we show you how to make great food with just three ingredients (plus staples like oil, salt, and pepper).  3-Ingredient Recipes Snapper Fish Green Onion Scallion Quick and Healthy Quick & Easy Dinner.

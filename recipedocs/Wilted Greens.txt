Wilted Greens recipe. Melt butter in heavy large pot over medium-high heat. Add all greens and stock. Cover and cook until greens wilt, stirring occasionally, about 3 minutes. Uncover; cook until juices thicken slightly, about 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Wilted Greens has 135.0 calories. Wilted Greens has 6.0 grams of protein. Wilted Greens has 10.0 grams of fat. Wilted Greens has 429.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Wilted Greens are 3 tablespoons butter, 1 large bunch Swiss chard, stems cut from leaves and discarded, leaves torn, 1 large bunch mustard greens, stems trimmed, leaves torn, 1 10-ounce bag spinach leaves, 1/3 cup chicken stock or canned low-salt chicken broth.

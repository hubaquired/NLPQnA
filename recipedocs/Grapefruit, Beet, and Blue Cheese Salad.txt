Grapefruit, Beet, and Blue Cheese Salad recipe.  Divide watercress between 2 salad plates and arrange grapefruit sections and cheese decoratively on top. In a small bowl toss together beets, 2 teaspoons oil, and vinegar and divide between salads. Drizzle salads with remaining oil and season with salt and pepper. Grapefruit, Beet, and Blue Cheese Salad has 179.0 calories. Grapefruit, Beet, and Blue Cheese Salad has 5.0 grams of protein. Grapefruit, Beet, and Blue Cheese Salad has 13.0 grams of fat. Grapefruit, Beet, and Blue Cheese Salad has 228.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grapefruit, Beet, and Blue Cheese Salad are 1/2 bunch watercress, coarse stems discarded, 1 grapefruit, peel and pith cut away with a serrated knife and sections cut free from membranes, 1 ounce chilled fine-quality blue cheese, cut into small thin slices, 2 peeled cooked beets, grated coarse (about 1 cup), 4 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar, coarse salt to taste, coarsely ground pepper to taste.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

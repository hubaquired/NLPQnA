Corn Chowder recipe.  Combine first 5 ingredients in heavy medium saucepan. Simmer over medium heat until soup is slightly thickened, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper and serve. The ingredients of Corn Chowder are 1 17-ounce can creamed corn, 2 cups milk (do not use lowfat or nonfat), 1 1/2 cups frozen diced hash brown potatoes (about 6 ounces), 1 green onion, chopped, 3 pieces bacon, cooked, crumbled, Salt and pepper.

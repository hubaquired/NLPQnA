Dill Sauce recipe.  Mix mayonnaise with sour cream. Add green onion, parsley, dill and salt and pepper. Dill Sauce has 231.0 calories. Dill Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Dill Sauce has 25.0 grams of fat. Dill Sauce has 292.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Dill Sauce are 2/3 cup mayonnaise, 2/3 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons green onion, finely chopped, 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon dill, finely chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried), salt and pepper to taste, N/A pepper.This sauce accompanies Fish Mixed Grill .

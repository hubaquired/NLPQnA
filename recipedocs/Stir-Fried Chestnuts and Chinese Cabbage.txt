Stir-Fried Chestnuts and Chinese Cabbage recipe. Heat peanut oil in a wok or 12-inch heavy skillet over high heat until just beginning to smoke then stir-fry garlic and ginger with salt until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add cabbage and cook, tossing, until just beginning to wilt, about 2 minutes, then stir in chestnuts and sugar. Reduce heat to moderate and cook covered, 3 minutes, then stir in soy sauce and sesame oil. Serve immediately. Stir-Fried Chestnuts and Chinese Cabbage has 266.0 calories. Stir-Fried Chestnuts and Chinese Cabbage has 3.0 grams of protein. Stir-Fried Chestnuts and Chinese Cabbage has 9.0 grams of fat. Stir-Fried Chestnuts and Chinese Cabbage has 295.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stir-Fried Chestnuts and Chinese Cabbage are 3 tablespoons peanut oil, 2 teaspoons minced garlic, 2 teaspoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 pound Napa cabbage, quartered lengthwise, cored, and sliced diagonally into 1/4-inch-wide strips, 2 cups bottled peeled cooked whole chestnuts (10 ounces) halved, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 teaspoon Asian sesame oil.An Asian treatment of chestnuts that will be right at home with all your side dishes.

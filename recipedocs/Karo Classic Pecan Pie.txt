Karo Classic Pecan Pie recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. In a mixing bowl, mix corn syrup, eggs, sugar, butter, and vanilla using a spoon. Stir in pecans. Pour filling into pie crust. Bake on center rack of oven until center reaches 200°F and springs back when tapped lightly,  about 55 to 70 minutes.

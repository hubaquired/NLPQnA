Apple Fritters recipe.  Stir together flour, granulated sugar, and salt, then add beer and whisk to combine. Heat oil in a 5-quart heavy pot until thermometer registers 375Â°F. Dip apple slices in batter, shaking off excess, and fry, about 8 at a time, until golden, about 1 1/2 minutes on each side. Transfer fritters with a slotted spoon to paper towels to drain. Serve warm, dusted with confectioners sugar. Apple Fritters has 66.0 calories. Apple Fritters has 4.0 grams of fat. Apple Fritters has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apple Fritters are 1 cup all-purpose flour, 3 tablespoons granulated sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 cup cold beer (not dark), 10 cups vegetable oil, 2 apples (1 lb), peeled, halved lengthwise, cored, and sliced 1/4 inch thick, Confectioners sugar for dusting.

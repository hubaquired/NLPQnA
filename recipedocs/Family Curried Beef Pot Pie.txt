Family Curried Beef Pot Pie recipe.  Heat the oil in a large, heavy pot over low heat. Add the onion and cook, stirring, 8 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger, curry powder and flour; cook, stirring constantly, 2 minutes. Add the carrots, potatoes, broth and cinnamon sticks; simmer, partially covered, 20 minutes. Add the chutney, raisins, peas, tomato, parsley, beef, salt and pepper; cook 5 minutes more. Place the mixture in a 1 1/2-quart, round, ovenproof casserole dish. On a floured surface, roll the pastry into a circle 2 inches larger than the dish. Brush the dish rim with some of the egg ; cover with pastry, crimping the edges around the rim. Cut 3 slits in the top and brush with remaining egg. Bake in a 350Â°F oven until golden, 40 minutes. The ingredients of Family Curried Beef Pot Pie are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 large onion, cut into 1/4-inch dice, 1 tablespoon minced garlic, 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger, 2 tablespoons curry powder, 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour, 1 cup diced (1 inch) carrots, 1 cup diced (1/2 inch) peeled potatoes, 3 cups beef broth, 2 cinnamon sticks, 3 inches long, 2 tablespoons chopped mango chutney, 1/2 cup golden raisins, 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed, 1 tomato, cut into 1/2-inch dice, 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley, 2 cups diced (1/2 inch) leftover roast beef, Salt and pepper, to taste, 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed.Cooking the curry powder with the vegetables mellows its flavor.

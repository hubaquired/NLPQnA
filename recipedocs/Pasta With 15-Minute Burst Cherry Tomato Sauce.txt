Pasta With 15-Minute Burst Cherry Tomato Sauce recipe.  Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente; drain and transfer to a large bowl. Meanwhile, heat oil in a 12" skillet or wide heavy saucepan over medium-high. Add garlic, then tomatoes, pepper, sugar, and 1 tsp. salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes burst and release their juices to form a sauce, 6â€“8 minutes. Toss pasta with tomato sauce and basil. Top with Parmesan. The ingredients of Pasta With 15-Minute Burst Cherry Tomato Sauce are 1 pound pasta, Kosher salt, 1/2 cup olive oil, 2 large garlic cloves, finely chopped, 3 pints cherry tomatoes, 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, Pinch of sugar, 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh basil, Freshly grated Parmesan (for serving).Juicy sweet cherry tomatoes burst open in warm olive oil, creating a luxuriously silky sauce that comes together in minutes.

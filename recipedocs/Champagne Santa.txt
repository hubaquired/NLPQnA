Champagne Santa recipe. In a chilled cocktail shaker, combine the Madeira, brandy, and a dash of bitters. Shake the mixture vigorously and pour it into a champagne flute. Fill the glass with chilled champagne and stir lightly to blend. The ingredients of Champagne Santa are 3 ounces Sercial Madeira, 1/2 ounce raspberry brandy, Dash of orange bitters, Chilled Champagne.

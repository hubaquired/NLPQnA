Rich Sweet Pastry Dough recipe. In a bowl with a pastry blender or in a food processor blend or pulse together flour, sugar, and salt. Add butter and blend or pulse until mixture resembles coarse meal. Add yolks and, if blending by hand, stir until mixture forms a dough. If using a food processor pulse until mixture forms large clumps and with hands form mixture into a dough. On a work surface smear dough in 3 or 4 forward motions with heel of hand to slightly develop gluten in flour and make dough easier to work with. Form dough into a ball and flatten to form a disk. Chill dough, wrapped in plastic wrap, at least 1 hour and up to 1 day. Rich Sweet Pastry Dough has 226.0 calories. Rich Sweet Pastry Dough has 4.0 grams of protein. Rich Sweet Pastry Dough has 14.0 grams of fat. Rich Sweet Pastry Dough has 103.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rich Sweet Pastry Dough are 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/3 cup sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 1/2 sticks (3/4 cup) cold unsalted butter, cut into bits, 6 large egg yolks.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

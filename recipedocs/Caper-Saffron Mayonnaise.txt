Caper-Saffron Mayonnaise recipe. Bring water and saffron to simmer in small saucepan. Remove from heat; let stand 10 minutes. Place mayonnaise in small bowl; whisk in saffron mixture. Add remaining ingredients; stir until blended. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill at least 2 hours. Can be made 3 days ahead. Keep chilled.) Caper-Saffron Mayonnaise has 155.0 calories. Caper-Saffron Mayonnaise has 17.0 grams of fat. Caper-Saffron Mayonnaise has 226.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Caper-Saffron Mayonnaise are 1 tablespoon water, 1/2 teaspoon packed saffron threads, 3/4 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup drained capers plus 1 teaspoon caper liquid, 2 tablespoons mixed chopped fresh herbs (such as thyme, oregano and parsley) or 2 teaspoons dried, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic.Offer this colorful condiment with assorted grilled vegetables or grilled tuna.

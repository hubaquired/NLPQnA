Oven-Fried Herbed Potatoes recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine potatoes, oil, garlic, herbs and pepper in large bowl and toss to coat. Spread potatoes in single layer in baking pan. Bake until brown and cooked through, turning once, about 1 hour. Season potatoes to taste with salt and serve immediately. Oven-Fried Herbed Potatoes has 733.0 calories. Oven-Fried Herbed Potatoes has 10.0 grams of protein. Oven-Fried Herbed Potatoes has 41.0 grams of fat. Oven-Fried Herbed Potatoes has 1190.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Oven-Fried Herbed Potatoes are 2 pounds russet potatoes, cut into 2-inch cubes, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 4 cloves garlic, minced, 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley, 1 teaspoon dried basil, crumbled, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crumbled, Pepper, Salt.This recipe doubles easily.

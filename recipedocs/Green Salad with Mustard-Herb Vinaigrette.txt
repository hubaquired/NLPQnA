Green Salad with Mustard-Herb Vinaigrette recipe. Whisk together vinegar, mustard, and broth in a large bowl, then add oil in a stream, whisking until emulsified. Season with salt and pepper and whisk in mixed herbs. Add lettuce and toss with vinaigrette. The ingredients of Green Salad with Mustard-Herb Vinaigrette are 1 teaspoon white-wine vinegar, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon fat-free chicken broth, 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons finely chopped mixed fresh herbs such as chives, tarragon, and parsley, 1 head Boston lettuce (1/2 lb), leaves separated and torn into bite-size pieces.

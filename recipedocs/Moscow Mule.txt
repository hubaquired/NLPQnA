Moscow Mule recipe.  Put the ice cubes in a highball glass, add the vodka, fill with ginger beer and garnish with lime slice. The ingredients of Moscow Mule are 2 or 3 ice cubes, 3 ounces vodka, Ginger beer, 1 teaspoon lime juice, Slice of lime. Vodka Alcoholic Ginger Cocktail Party Cocktail Lime House & Garden Drink.

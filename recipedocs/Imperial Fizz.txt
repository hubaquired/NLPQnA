Imperial Fizz recipe.  Combine all ingredients except the champagne in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a highball glass and fill with champagne. The ingredients of Imperial Fizz are 1 1/2 ounces rye or bourbon, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon superfine sugar, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Chilled champagne.

Vanilla Ice Cream with Sesame Candies and Halvah recipe.  Start by placing sesame candies in a large resealable plastic bag. Crush the candies with a rolling pin until broken into small pieces. Divide ice cream among 6 bowls. Top with crushed candy and crumbled halvah. Drizzle with honey. The ingredients of Vanilla Ice Cream with Sesame Candies and Halvah are 3 1/2 ounces sesame candies*, 1 1/2 pints vanilla ice cream, Crumbled halvah*, Honey, *Sesame candies (such as Joyva's Sesame Crunch) and halvah (a Middle Eastern sweet) are available at some supermarkets and a Middle Eastern markets..

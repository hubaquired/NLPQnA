Grilled Rib-Eye Steaks with Cucumber Relish recipe. Prepare grill. In a bowl combine all ingredients with salt to taste. Season steaks with salt and generously with pepper. Grill steaks on an oiled rack set 5 to 6 inches over glowing coals 4 to 5 minutes on each side for medium-rare meat. Alternatively, steaks may be grilled in a hot well-seasoned ridged grill pan over moderately high heat in the same manner.) Serve steaks with relish. Grilled Rib-Eye Steaks with Cucumber Relish has 36.0 grams of fat. Grilled Rib-Eye Steaks with Cucumber Relish has 104.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Rib-Eye Steaks with Cucumber Relish are 1 1/2 cups diced seeded cucumber, 3 tablespoons finely chopped red onion, 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped jalapeño chili, or to taste (wear rubber gloves), 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, two 1-inch-thick rib-eye steaks at room temperature, freshly ground black pepper.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Almond Crust recipe. Blend flour, almonds, sugar and salt in processor until nuts are finely ground. Add butter and cut in using on/off turns until mixture resembles coarse meal. Mix in almond extract and enough water to form moist clumps. Knead dough briefly on work surface to combine; flatten into disk. Wrap in plastic; refrigerate until firm before rolling, at least 2 hours and up to 1 day. Almond Crust has 1919.0 calories. Almond Crust has 29.0 grams of protein. Almond Crust has 123.0 grams of fat. Almond Crust has 610.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Almond Crust are 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour, 1/2 cup slivered blanched almonds (about 2 ounces), 1/4 cup sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup (1 stick) chilled unsalted butter, cut into pieces, 3/4 teaspoon almond extract, 2 tablespoons (about) ice water.This recipe was created to be used for Fresh Plum Frangipane Tart , Strawberry-Lemon Tartlets and Mixed Nut Tartlets .

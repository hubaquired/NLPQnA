Simple Spinach Dip recipe. Squeeze as much liquid out of the spinach as possible with a clean dishtowel or paper towels. Melt butter in a medium pan over medium-high heat. Sauté shallot until soft, 3–5 minutes. Stir in paprika and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add spinach, cream cheese, cream, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring, until warmed through and slightly reduced, 8–10 minutes; add more cream if needed to reach desired consistency. Dip can be made 1 day ahead. Store in an airtight container and chill. Warm before serving. Simple Spinach Dip has 302.0 calories. Simple Spinach Dip has 7.0 grams of protein. Simple Spinach Dip has 27.0 grams of fat. Simple Spinach Dip has 553.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Simple Spinach Dip are 1 (10-ounce) package frozen spinach, thawed, drained, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 medium shallot, finely chopped, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, 8 ounces cream cheese, 3/4 cup (or more) heavy cream, 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper.Sometimes simple is better.

Room 4 Dessert Sour recipe. Fill highball glass with ice cubes. In cocktail shaker filled with ice, add whiskey, lime juice, sugar, and egg white. Shake vigorously for about 10 seconds, then strain into highball. Slowly pour Chambord over back of a spoon into glass and serve. Room 4 Dessert Sour has 257.0 calories. Room 4 Dessert Sour has 4.0 grams of protein. Room 4 Dessert Sour has 58.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Room 4 Dessert Sour are 2 ounces good-quality whiskey, 1 ounce fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon superfine sugar, 1/2 egg white, 1 tablespoon, plus 1 teaspoon Chambord.This recipe is adapted from Will Goldfarb, the innovative chef behind Room 4 Dessert in New York City.

Smoked Salmon Bruschetta with Tomato-Avocado Salsa recipe. Combine all ingredients in bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Preheat broiler. Brush 1 side of bread with oil. Sprinkle with pepper. Broil both sides until golden, about 2 minutes per side. Arrange bread, oil side up, on work surface. Top with salmon. Cut each toast in half. Arrange on platter. Top each with some salsa. Serve, passing remaining salsa separately. Smoked Salmon Bruschetta with Tomato-Avocado Salsa has 239.0 calories. Smoked Salmon Bruschetta with Tomato-Avocado Salsa has 10.0 grams of protein. Smoked Salmon Bruschetta with Tomato-Avocado Salsa has 13.0 grams of fat. Smoked Salmon Bruschetta with Tomato-Avocado Salsa has 361.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Smoked Salmon Bruschetta with Tomato-Avocado Salsa are 3 large plum tomatoes (about 8 ounces), seeded, chopped, 1/2 yellow bell pepper, seeded, chopped, 3 tablespoons chopped red onion, 1 jalapeño chili, seeded, chopped, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon olive oil, 1/2 avocado, peeled, cut into small cubes, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, 4 1/2-in-thick bread slices from crusty round loaf, Olive oil, 1/4 pound thinly sliced smoked salmon.

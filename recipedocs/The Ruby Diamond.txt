The Ruby Diamond recipe.  Combine gin, mezcal, Cappelletti, lemon juice, and orange juice in a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice and shake until outside of shaker is frosty, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled champagne coupe or Nick and Nora glass. The ingredients of The Ruby Diamond are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1 1/2 ounces mezcal, 3/4 ounce Cappelletti, 3/4 ounce fresh lemon juice, 1/4 ounce fresh orange juice.This smoky blush cocktail features Cappelletti, a Campari-like aperitif that shares its ruby red color but is slightly less sweet and not quite as bitter.

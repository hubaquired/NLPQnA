Cucumber Salad with Pineapple and Jalapeño recipe.  Bring first 4 ingredients to boil in heavy small saucepan, stirring until sugar dissolves. Simmer until reduced to 2/3 cup, about 4 minutes. Transfer syrup to large bowl and refrigerate until cold. Add pineapple to syrup. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Add cucumber and next 3 ingredients to pineapple mixture; stir to coat. Line plates with lettuce leaves. Spoon salad atop lettuce. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve. Cucumber Salad with Pineapple and Jalapeño has 138.0 calories. Cucumber Salad with Pineapple and Jalapeño has 1.0 grams of protein. Cucumber Salad with Pineapple and Jalapeño has 1.0 grams of fat. Cucumber Salad with Pineapple and Jalapeño has 198.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber Salad with Pineapple and Jalapeño are 3/4 cup sugar, 2/3 cup white vinegar, 2 tablespoons water, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 cup 1/3-inch pieces peeled cored fresh pineapple, 1 English hothouse cucumber, cut into 1/3-inch pieces, 1 carrot, peeled, cut into matchstick-size strips, 1/3 cup thinly sliced red onion, 1 tablespoon minced seeded jalapeño chili, 1 head green leaf lettuce, leaves separated, 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires addtional sitting time.

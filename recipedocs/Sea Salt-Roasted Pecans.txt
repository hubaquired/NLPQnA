Sea Salt-Roasted Pecans recipe. Preheat oven to 325°F. Toss pecans and melted butter in medium bowl to coat. Add sea salt and toss. Spread pecans in single layer on rimmed baking sheet. Bake until fragrant and slightly darkened in color, about 15 minutes. Cool pecans on baking sheet. Sea Salt-Roasted Pecans has 279.0 calories. Sea Salt-Roasted Pecans has 3.0 grams of protein. Sea Salt-Roasted Pecans has 30.0 grams of fat. Sea Salt-Roasted Pecans has 206.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sea Salt-Roasted Pecans are 2 cups pecan halves, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, 1 1/4 teaspoons fine sea salt.

Blender Chermoula Sauce recipe.  Toast coriander and cumin seeds in a dry small skillet, tossing occasionally, until very fragrant, about 2 minutes. Let cool, then lightly crush with a heavy skillet. Purée toasted seeds, garlic, oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, paprika, salt, and red pepper flakes in a blender until spices are ground and mixture is very smooth. Add cilantro, parsley, and mint; process until well combined but slightly textured. Sauce can be made 3 days ahead; chill in an airtight container. Blender Chermoula Sauce has 763.0 calories. Blender Chermoula Sauce has 3.0 grams of protein. Blender Chermoula Sauce has 82.0 grams of fat. Blender Chermoula Sauce has 755.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blender Chermoula Sauce are 3/4 teaspoon coriander seeds, 3/4 teaspoon cumin seeds, 2 garlic cloves, 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon smoked paprika, 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4–1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, 1 cup (packed) cilantro leaves with tender stems, 1 cup (packed) parsley leaves with tender stems, 1/2 cup (packed) mint leaves.Made with fresh herbs, garlic, lemon, and warm spices, this Moroccan sauce makes a great marinade or finishing sauce for meat and seafood.

Rosé in June Fizz recipe. Combine all ingredients except the soda in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a 10-ounce highball glass. Top with several dashes of soda. The ingredients of Rosé in June Fizz are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1 1/2 ounces framboise, 1/2 cup orange juice, 4 tablespoons lime juice, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Club soda.

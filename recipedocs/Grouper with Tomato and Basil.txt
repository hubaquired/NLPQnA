Grouper with Tomato and Basil recipe.  Working off heat, put 1 tablespoon oil in a 10-inch nonstick skillet and add fish, turning to coat with oil on both sides. Arrange fish skinned sides down and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Toss together tomato, garlic if using), basil, remaining tablespoon oil, and salt and pepper to taste in a small bowl, then mound on top of fish. Cover skillet with a tight-fitting lid and cook fish over moderately high heat until just cooked through, about 8 minutes. Grouper with Tomato and Basil has 336.0 calories. Grouper with Tomato and Basil has 44.0 grams of protein. Grouper with Tomato and Basil has 16.0 grams of fat. Grouper with Tomato and Basil has 413.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grouper with Tomato and Basil are 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 (1-lb) piece grouper or red snapper fillet (3/4 inch thick), skinned and halved crosswise, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon black pepper, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped tomato, 1 small garlic clove, minced (optional), 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil, Accompaniment: fresh corn spoon bread.

Pineapple-Avocado Salsa recipe. Stir all ingredients together and season with salt. Pineapple-Avocado Salsa has 121.0 calories. Pineapple-Avocado Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein. The ingredients of Pineapple-Avocado Salsa are 1 cup 1/4-inch-dice fresh pineapple, 2 plum tomatoes, seeded and cut into 1/4-inch dice, 1 firm-ripe California avocado, cut into 1/4-inch dice, 1/2 cup chopped sweet onion, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh jalapeño chile, including seeds, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons fresh orange juice, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.

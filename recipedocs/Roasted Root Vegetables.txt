Roasted Root Vegetables recipe.  Preheat oven to 425°F. Combine all vegetables in large bowl. Add oil; toss to coat thoroughly. Season with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Cover and let stand at room temperature.) Arrange vegetables on 2 heavy large baking sheets. Roast vegetables until tender and beginning to turn golden brown, stirring occasionally, about 50 minutes. Transfer to large bowl and serve. Roasted Root Vegetables has 252.0 calories. Roasted Root Vegetables has 3.0 grams of protein. Roasted Root Vegetables has 11.0 grams of fat. Roasted Root Vegetables has 44.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Root Vegetables are 4 small turnips, peeled, halved, sliced, 4 medium parsnips, peeled, sliced, 2 large rutabagas, peeled, quartered, sliced, 2 yams (red-skinned sweet potatoes), peeled, sliced, 1 large acorn squash, peeled quartered, seeded cut into 1/3-inch-thick slices, 1/2 cup olive oil.

Balsamic Truffles recipe. Follow recipe for bittersweet chocolate truffle base , stirring aged balsamic vinegar into lukewarm cream. Mix in melted chocolate. Chill truffle base and form truffles. Drop each freshly coated truffle or just–formed, uncoated truffle) into bowl of hazelnuts; turn to coat. The ingredients of Balsamic Truffles are bittersweet chocolate truffle base, 1 tablespoon plus 1/4 teaspoon aged balsamic vinegar, Chopped toasted salted hazelnuts.

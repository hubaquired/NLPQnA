Spring Peas with Lettuce and Mint recipe. Bring broth to boil in medium saucepan. Add peas and simmer until heated through, about 4 minutes. Stir in lettuce and mint. Season to taste with salt. The ingredients of Spring Peas with Lettuce and Mint are 1/2 cup canned low-salt chicken broth, 1 10-ounce package frozen peas, 1 cup thinly sliced romaine lettuce, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint.This recipe was created to accompany Seared Scallops with Tarragon Cream.

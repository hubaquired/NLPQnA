Roasted Veg With Nutritional Yeast recipe. Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss vegetables with oil on a rimmed baking sheet to coat; season with salt and pepper. Roast until deep golden brown and tender, 20–25 minutes. Let cool slightly, then toss with nutritional yeast. Veggies can be made 5 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Roasted Veg With Nutritional Yeast are 2 small heads of broccoli, cut into florets with some stalk attached, or 1 medium acorn squash, seeds removed, cut into 1/2-inch slices, 2 tablespoons virgin coconut oil, warmed to liquefy if needed, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast.Try this in a frittata, folded into a grain salad, or in a hash with bacon.

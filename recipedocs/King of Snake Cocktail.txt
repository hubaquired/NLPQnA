King of Snake Cocktail recipe. Muddle ginger and sugar in cocktail shaker until paste forms. Add all remaining ingredients except crushed ice; cover and shake 15 seconds. Fill 2 old-fashioned glasses with crushed ice. Strain cocktail into glasses and serve. King of Snake Cocktail has 173.0 calories. King of Snake Cocktail has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of King of Snake Cocktail are 1 1/3-inch slice peeled fresh ginger, coarsely chopped, 2 tablespoons (packed) golden brown sugar, 2 cups ice cubes, 1/4 cup vodka, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons chile-pepper-flavored vodka (such as Absolut Peppar or Stolichnaya Pepper), 2 tablespoons Cointreau or other orange liqueur, Crushed ice.Chopped ginger and chile-infused vodka add a spicy bite to this flavor-packed cocktail.

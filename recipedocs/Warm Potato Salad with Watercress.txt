Warm Potato Salad with Watercress recipe. Peel potatoes and cut into 3/4-inch pieces, then cook in a 3- to 4-quart saucepan of boiling salted water , uncovered, until just tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Drain in a colander. While potatoes are cooking, whisk together vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and sugar in a large heatproof bowl until combined, then stir in shallots and let stand until ready to use. Toss potatoes with vinegar mixture, then add oil and watercress, tossing gently to combine. Warm Potato Salad with Watercress has 352.0 calories. Warm Potato Salad with Watercress has 6.0 grams of protein. Warm Potato Salad with Watercress has 18.0 grams of fat. Warm Potato Salad with Watercress has 348.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Warm Potato Salad with Watercress are 3 lb yellow-fleshed potatoes such as Yukon Gold, 1/4 cup cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon grainy mustard, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 cup thinly sliced shallots (2 medium), 1/2 cup olive oil, 2 bunches watercress (4 cups loosely packed), coarse stems discarded.Adding watercress to potato salad gives this old standby new life. We particularly like the contrast of the crisp, peppery green against the warm, vinegar- and mustard-spiced potatoes.

Arugula with Lemon and Olive Oil recipe. Toss together arugula, lemon juice, and salt in a bowl. Drizzle with oil and toss to combine well. Arugula with Lemon and Olive Oil has 43.0 calories. Arugula with Lemon and Olive Oil has 5.0 grams of fat. Arugula with Lemon and Olive Oil has 52.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Arugula with Lemon and Olive Oil are 3 ounces baby arugula (6 cups), 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons olive oil.

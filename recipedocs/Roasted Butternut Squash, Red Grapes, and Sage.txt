Roasted Butternut Squash, Red Grapes, and Sage recipe.  Preheat oven to 425°F. Combine butternut squash, grapes, onion, and sage in large bowl. Drizzle with oil and melted butter. Season generously with salt and pepper. Toss to coat. Spread out onto large rimmed baking sheet. Roast until squash and onion begin to brown, stirring occasionally, about 50 minutes. Transfer to platter, sprinkle with toasted pine nuts, and serve. Roasted Butternut Squash, Red Grapes, and Sage has 335.0 calories.

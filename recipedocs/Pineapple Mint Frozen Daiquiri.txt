Pineapple Mint Frozen Daiquiri recipe. In a blender blend all ingredients until smooth but still thick. Divide mixture between 2 large stemmed glasses and garnish with mint and/or pineapple. Pineapple Mint Frozen Daiquiri has 214.0 calories. Pineapple Mint Frozen Daiquiri has 1.0 grams of protein. Pineapple Mint Frozen Daiquiri has 12.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pineapple Mint Frozen Daiquiri are 1/3 cup light or amber rum, or to taste, 1 1/2 cups 1-inch pineapple chunks, 2 tablespoons superfine sugar, or to taste, 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice, or to taste, 3 fresh mint leaves, 2 cups ice cubes, Garnish: mint sprigs and/or pineapple chunks skewered with wooden picks.

Creamed Corn with Basil recipe.  Working in a large bowl, cut kernels from cobs with a sharp knife and set aside. Scrape back of knife several times against cobs to extract remaining corn pulp, scraping into a large glass measure. You should have at least 3/4 cup of corn pulp. If not, add 2 tablespoons crème fraîche.) Transfer pulp to a blender and purée with 2 tablespoons crème fraîche until smooth. Cook shallot in butter in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until softened, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in kernels, then add corn purée, water, salt, and pepper and simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until kernels are just tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in basil to taste. Creamed Corn with Basil has 160.0 calories. Creamed Corn with Basil has 5.0 grams of protein. Creamed Corn with Basil has 7.0 grams of fat. Creamed Corn with Basil has 412.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamed Corn with Basil are 8 large ears corn, husked, 2 to 4 tablespoons crème fraîche or heavy cream, 1 large shallot, finely chopped (1/4 cup), 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 cup water, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 2 to 3 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil.

Peas with Garlic and Thyme recipe. Cook garlic in butter in a heavy saucepan over moderately low heat, stirring, until garlic turns pale golden, about 2 minutes. Add peas, water, and thyme and simmer briskly, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until liquid is evaporated and peas are tender, 8 to 10 minutes. Stir in salt and season with pepper. Peas with Garlic and Thyme has 173.0 calories. Peas with Garlic and Thyme has 9.0 grams of protein. Peas with Garlic and Thyme has 5.0 grams of fat. Peas with Garlic and Thyme has 204.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peas with Garlic and Thyme are 2 garlic cloves, smashed with flat side of a knife, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 2 cups shelled fresh peas (2 to 2 1/2 pounds in pods), 2/3 cup water, 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, 1/2 teaspoon salt.

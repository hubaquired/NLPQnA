Steamed Beets with Tarragon recipe.  Peel beets and halve lengthwise. Slice beets crosswise 1/8 inch thick and steam on a rack set over boiling water, covered, until tender, 10 to 12 minutes. Toss with remaining ingredients and salt and pepper to taste until butter is melted. Steamed Beets with Tarragon has 80.0 calories. Steamed Beets with Tarragon has 2.0 grams of protein. Steamed Beets with Tarragon has 3.0 grams of fat. Steamed Beets with Tarragon has 90.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed Beets with Tarragon are 1 bunch beets, 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallots, 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh tarragon, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, cut into bits, 1 teaspoon cider vinegar, 1/4 teaspoon sugar.These beets would be a great way to liven up simple roasted meats such as pork and chicken.

Pineapple Bowl recipe.  In a punch bowl set in a hollowed-out block of ice, combine Southern Comfort and champagne. Stir in scoops of pineapple sherbet gently. Garnish the punch with pineapple slices and strawberries. Pineapple Bowl has 562.0 calories. Pineapple Bowl has 1.0 grams of protein. Pineapple Bowl has 1.0 grams of fat. Pineapple Bowl has 46.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pineapple Bowl are 1 1/4 cups Southern Comfort, 3 bottles chilled dry champagne, 1 pint pineapple sherbet, or to taste, Garnish: pineapple slices and strawberries (hulled and, if large, quartered).

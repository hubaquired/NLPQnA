Bantam Corn Relish recipe. Scrape the corn kernels from the cobs. Combine the corn kernels with remaining ingredients in a large pot and bring to a boil. Turn heat to low and simmer the mixture for 1/2 hour. Pour into hot, sterilized preserving jars, cover and seal. Bantam Corn Relish has 275.0 calories. Bantam Corn Relish has 5.0 grams of protein. Bantam Corn Relish has 2.0 grams of fat. Bantam Corn Relish has 800.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bantam Corn Relish are 10 large (or 15 small) ears tender young corn, 1 green pepper, seeded and minced, 1/2 red pepper, seeded and minced, 2 large yellow onions, peeled and minced, 2 teaspoons salt, 3/4 teaspoon celery seed, 1 1/4 teaspoons dry mustard, 1 1/3 cups cider vinegar, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric.

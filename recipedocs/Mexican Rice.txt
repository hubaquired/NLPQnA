Mexican Rice recipe.  Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and serrano; sauté until onion is translucent, about 9 minutes. Mix in rice; stir 2 minutes. Mix in broth and salt; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook until rice is just tender and broth is absorbed, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat. Let stand, covered, 5 minutes. Transfer rice to bowl; cool to barely lukewarm, stirring occasionally, about 1 1/2 hours. Add 1/2 cup Cilantro Dressing to rice; toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature.) Mexican Rice has 216.0 calories. Mexican Rice has 5.0 grams of protein. Mexican Rice has 3.0 grams of fat. Mexican Rice has 249.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mexican Rice are 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 large white onion, chopped, 4 garlic cloves, minced, 1 tablespoon minced seeded serrano chiles, 2 cups long-grain white rice, 3 1/4 cups low-salt chicken broth, 3/4 teaspoon salt, Cilantro Dressing.

Strawberries with Molasses Sour Cream Sauce recipe.  In a small bowl whisk together sour cream, sugar, vanilla, and 1 tablespoon molasses until smooth. Divide strawberries between 2 bowls and top with sour cream sauce. Drizzle remaining molasses over servings. Strawberries with Molasses Sour Cream Sauce has 231.0 calories. The ingredients of Strawberries with Molasses Sour Cream Sauce are 1/2 cup sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract, 2 tablespoons unsulfured molasses, 1 pint strawberries, hulled and, if large, halved.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

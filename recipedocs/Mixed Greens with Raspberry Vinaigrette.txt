Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette recipe. Blend vinegar and shallots in blender or processor. With machine running, gradually add oil. Season with salt and pepper. Combine romaine, radicchio and endive in large bowl. Toss with enough vinaigrette to coat. Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette has 276.0 calories. Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette has 2.0 grams of protein. Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette has 27.0 grams of fat. Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette are 1/2 cup raspberry vinegar, 2/3 cup chopped fresh shallots, 3/4 cup olive oil, 1 small head romaine lettuce, thinly sliced (about 6 cups), 1 large head radicchio, thinly sliced, 1 large head Belgian endive, thinly sliced.This recipe originally accompanied Goat Cheese Soufflé and Mixed Greens with Raspberry Vinaigrette .

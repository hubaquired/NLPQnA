Caper-Parsley Sauce recipe. Blend all ingredients in processor until coarse puree forms. Season sauce with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill.) The ingredients of Caper-Parsley Sauce are 2/3 cup olive oil, 6 tablespoons drained capers, 6 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, 6 anchovy fillets, chopped, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 large garlic cloves, halved.This recipe is an accompaniment for Fish Cakes with Caper-Parsley Sauce This versatile sauce, which can be made one day ahead, also complements roast beef, hard-boiled eggs or potato salad.

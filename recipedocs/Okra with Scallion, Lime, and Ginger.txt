Okra with Scallion, Lime, and Ginger recipe. Steam okra in a steamer over boiling water, covered, until just tender, about 7 minutes. Transfer to a serving dish. Cook scallions in oil with 1/2 teaspoon salt in a small skillet over medium heat, stirring, just until softened, 1 to 3 minutes. Pour over okra and toss with ginger. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over okra. Okra with Scallion, Lime, and Ginger has 144.0 calories. Okra with Scallion, Lime, and Ginger has 3.0 grams of protein. Okra with Scallion, Lime, and Ginger has 11.0 grams of fat. Okra with Scallion, Lime, and Ginger has 13.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Okra with Scallion, Lime, and Ginger are 1 pound small to medium okra, trimmed, leaving tops and tips, 1 cup chopped scallions (about 1 bunch), 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 (2- by 1-inch) piece peeled ginger, cut into very thin matchsticks, Lime wedges.Steaming unlocks the succulence of okra. Here, the pods are tossed with slivered ginger and a Vietnamese-inspired scallion oil in an all-purpose dish that's great with grilled steak or chicken, or with nothing more than a fragrant bowlful of jasmine rice.

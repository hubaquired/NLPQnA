Red-Onion Meat Loaf recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions and sauté until just tender, about 6 minutes. Add garlic; sauté 1 minute. Transfer onion mixture to large bowl. Add 1/2 cup ketchup and all remaining ingredients; mix just until blended. Shape mixture into 9x4-inch loaf on large baking sheet; spread remaining 1/2 cup ketchup over top and sides. Bake meat loaf until thermometer inserted into center registers 160°F, about 1 hour. Let stand 10 minutes. Transfer to platter and serve. Red-Onion Meat Loaf has 306.0 calories. Red-Onion Meat Loaf has 18.0 grams of protein. Red-Onion Meat Loaf has 16.0 grams of fat. Red-Onion Meat Loaf has 865.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red-Onion Meat Loaf are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 1/4 pounds red onions, chopped, 4 garlic cloves, chopped, 1 cup ketchup, 1/2 pound ground pork, 1/2 pound ground veal, 1/2 pound lean ground beef, 1 1/2 cups fresh breadcrumbs made from white bread (about 3 slices), 1 5.5-ounce can vegetable juice, 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper.

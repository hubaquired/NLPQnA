Cranberry-Tangerine Christmas Cooler recipe. Combine cranberry juice and tangerine juice in large pitcher. Add lime juice and stir to blend. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Cut thin slice off tops and bottoms of tangerines. Cut each horizontally into three 1/4- to 1/3-inch-thick rounds. Divide cooler among 6 tall glasses. Garnish with tangerine rounds and serve.

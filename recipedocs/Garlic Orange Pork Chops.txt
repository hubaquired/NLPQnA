Garlic Orange Pork Chops recipe.  Pat pork dry and season with salt and pepper. In a skillet heat oil over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking and brown chops until golden. Add garlic, orange juice, wine, and lemon juice and simmer, covered partially, until pork is tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Garlic Orange Pork Chops has 871.0 calories. Garlic Orange Pork Chops has 93.0 grams of protein. Garlic Orange Pork Chops has 48.0 grams of fat. Garlic Orange Pork Chops has 248.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Garlic Orange Pork Chops are four 1/2-inch-thick pork chops, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 1/2 cup fresh orange juice, 1/4 cup dry white wine, 1 to 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

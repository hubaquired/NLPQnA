Morels in Cream on Brioche (Morilles à la Crème) recipe.  Heat cream in a small saucepan until hot. Heat butter in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderately high heat until foam subsides, then sauté morels, stirring frequently, until golden, 6 to 8 minutes. Sprinkle in flour and cook, stirring, 1 minute. Stir in hot cream and reduce heat to low. Gently simmer, covered, stirring once, until morels are tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Season with salt and white pepper. Arrange 5 toasts on each of 4 plates and spoon morels and sauce on top. Serve immediately. Morels in Cream on Brioche (Morilles à la Crème) has 366.0 calories. Morels in Cream on Brioche (Morilles à la Crème) has 9.0 grams of protein. Morels in Cream on Brioche (Morilles à la Crème) has 23.0 grams of fat. Morels in Cream on Brioche (Morilles à la Crème) has 226.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Morels in Cream on Brioche (Morilles à la Crème) are 1 cup heavy cream, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 pound fresh morels, trimmed, washed well, and patted dry, 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour, Freshly ground white pepper, 5 thin slices brioche or challah bread, crusts discarded, each slice cut into 4 triangles and toasted.

Blackberries with Mint Tea Syrup and Yogurt recipe.  Pour boiling water over tea and steep 1 minute. Add sugar and stir to dissolve. Cool. Divide blackberries among 4 bowls. Pour mint tea syrup over berries. Spoon dollop of yogurt atop berries and serve. The ingredients of Blackberries with Mint Tea Syrup and Yogurt are 1/4 cup boiling water, 1 mint tea bag, 1 tablespoon sugar, 4 cups blackberries, Plain whole-milk Greek-style yogurt.Depending on the flavors and types of tea your kids like, feel free to experiment.

Milkie Way Malt recipe. Place two 8-ounce glasses in freezerto chill. Combine first 4 ingredients and 1 tablespoon caramel sauce in blender; process on high speed until thick and smooth, about 30 seconds. Squirt chocolate syrup and caramel sauce into both glasses; split malt mixture between glasses. Top with malted milk balls. The ingredients of Milkie Way Malt are 2 1/2 cups vanilla bean ice cream, 1/3 cup whole milk, 1 tablespoon malted milk powder, 1 tablespoon milk chocolate chips, 1 tablespoon caramel sauce plus additional for drizzling, Chocolate syrup, Malted milk balls, coarsely crushed.Vanilla ice cream with malted milk, chocolate, caramel sauce, and crushed malt balls make for a candy bar–inspired treat.

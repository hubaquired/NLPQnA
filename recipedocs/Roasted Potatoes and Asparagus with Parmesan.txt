Roasted Potatoes and Asparagus with Parmesan recipe.  Preheat oven to 450°F with rack in upper third. Toss together asparagus, potatoes, oil, and 1/2 teaspoon each of salt and pepper in a large shallow baking pan, spreading evenly. Roast, stirring once, 20 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese and roast until cheese is melted and golden in spots, about 3 minutes more. Roasted Potatoes and Asparagus with Parmesan has 236.0 calories. Roasted Potatoes and Asparagus with Parmesan has 8.0 grams of protein. Roasted Potatoes and Asparagus with Parmesan has 13.0 grams of fat. Roasted Potatoes and Asparagus with Parmesan has 139.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Potatoes and Asparagus with Parmesan are 1 pound asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces, 1 pound Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into 1-inch pieces, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1/3 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.We always look forward to fresh asparagus at the market. Roasting it with buttery Yukon Golds—and a handful of Parmigiano-Reggiano, of course—deepens the flavor.

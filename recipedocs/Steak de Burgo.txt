Steak de Burgo recipe.  Melt butter in heavy small saucepan over medium heat. Remove from heat. Add garlic. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Let garlic butter stand 2 hours at room temperature. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Sprinkle steaks with salt and pepper. Grill steaks to desired doneness, about 4 minutes per side for medium-rare. Place pan of garlic butter at edge of grill to rewarm. Transfer steaks to plates. Spoon garlic butter over. Sprinkle with herbs and serve. Steak de Burgo has 359.0 calories.

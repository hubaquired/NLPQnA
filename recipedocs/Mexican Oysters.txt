Mexican Oysters recipe.  Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Let stand 30 minutes. Preheat broiler. Arrange oysters on broiler-proof pan. Spoon salsa over oysters, dividing evenly. Broil until heated through, about 5 minutes. Serve with bread to soak up juices. The ingredients of Mexican Oysters are 3 plum tomatoes, seeded, diced, 3 green onions, chopped, 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 teaspoons ground cumin, 1 jalapeño chili, seeded, chopped, 12 fresh oysters, shucked, left on half shell, Crusty French bread.Also try the salsa with some corn bread or scrambled eggs for a spicy treat.

Spiced Fig Preserves recipe. Finely chop lemon in processor. Add figs. Using on/off turns, process until figs are coarsely pureed. Transfer mixture to heavy large saucepan. Add 2 1/4 cups sugar, cinnamon stick, 2 1/4 teaspoons minced ginger and 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves. Simmer until mixture thickens to jam consistency and candy thermometer registers 200°F, stirring often, about 20 minutes. Discard cinnamon stick. Divide hot preserves among hot clean jars. Cover tightly and refrigerate up to 2 months. Spiced Fig Preserves has 377.0 calories. Spiced Fig Preserves has 1.0 grams of protein. Spiced Fig Preserves has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spiced Fig Preserves are 1/2 lemon (unpeeled), thickly sliced, seeded, 1 1/2 pounds fresh ripe figs, halved (about 4 cups), 2 1/4 cups sugar, 1 3-inch cinnamon stick, 2 1/4 teaspoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves.

Jasmine Whipped Cream recipe. Combine chilled cream and tea in bowl. Cover; chill overnight or up to 2 days. Strain cream-tea mixture through fine strainer into mixing bowl; discard solids. The ingredients of Jasmine Whipped Cream are 1 cup chilled heavy whipping cream, 1 tablespoon good-quality loose-leaf jasmine tea or 3 jasmine tea bags, 1 tablespoon sugar.

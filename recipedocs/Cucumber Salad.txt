Cucumber Salad recipe. Finely grind shrimp if using) in a blender until very fluffy, about 1 minute. Stir together lime juice, sugar, and fish sauce in a large bowl until sugar is dissolved, then add cucumber and shallot, tossing to coat. Serve salad sprinkled with dried shrimp. Cucumber Salad has 40.0 calories. Cucumber Salad has 1.0 grams of protein. Cucumber Salad has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber Salad are 2 tablespoons fine-quality dried shrimp such as BDMP or Sea Emperor brands (optional), 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons nam pla (Asian fish sauce; preferably Thai), 2 seedless cucumbers (usually plastic-wrapped; 1 1/2 lb total), halved lengthwise and sliced crosswise 1/8 inch thick, 2 small shallots, thinly sliced (2 tablespoons).Yam Taeng Kwa.

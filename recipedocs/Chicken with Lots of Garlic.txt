Chicken with Lots of Garlic recipe.  Pat chicken dry and sprinkle with pepper and 3/4 teaspoon salt. Dredge chicken in flour, shaking off excess. Heat oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking. Brown chicken in 2 batches, turning over once, about 5 minutes total per batch. Transfer to a plate. Add garlic to skillet and cook, stirring, until just golden, about 2 minutes. Add wine and boil 1 minute. Add broth, then cover skillet and simmer until garlic is tender, about 15 minutes. Return chicken, skin side up, to skillet and simmer, covered, until just cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes. Transfer chicken to a platter. Mash 8 garlic cloves into sauce and season with remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt. Sprinkle with parsley. Spoon sauce and garlic over chicken. Chicken with Lots of Garlic has 922.0 calories. Chicken with Lots of Garlic has 71.0 grams of protein. Chicken with Lots of Garlic has 62.0 grams of fat. Chicken with Lots of Garlic has 948.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chicken with Lots of Garlic are 1 (3- to 3 1/2-lb) chicken, cut into 8 serving pieces, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 20 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup dry white wine, 3/4 cup reduced-sodium chicken broth, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley.

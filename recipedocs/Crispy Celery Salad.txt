Crispy Celery Salad recipe. Place all of the celery in a bowl. Toss with the oil and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Just before serving, toss with the parsley and mint. Serve immediately. It's delicious served on a bed of thinly sliced beets. The ingredients of Crispy Celery Salad are 5 or 6 outer celery ribs, trimmed and thinly sliced on the diagonal, Small inner celery ribs, including the leaves, chopped, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon red-wine vinegar, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste, 1/4 cup small whole fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped mint leaves.Flat-leaf parsley is also known as Italian parsley. It's not only a different shape than curly-leaf parsley but also has a stronger flavor.  Salad No-Cook Picnic Vegetarian Quick & Easy Vinegar Mint Celery Summer Parsley Parade.

Grilled Lamb Skewers with Spiced Mint Marinade recipe.  Combine all ingredients except lamb in medium bowl for marinade. Thread lamb onto metal skewers. Place skewers in 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Pour marinade over. Cover with plastic wrap; chill overnight. Transfer lamb skewers to baking sheet. Pour marinade into small saucepan. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Sprinkle lamb with salt and pepper. Grill to desired doneness, turning, about 8 minutes for medium-rare. Transfer to platter. Boil marinade 5 minutes. Strain, then spoon over lamb and serve. Grilled Lamb Skewers with Spiced Mint Marinade has 481.0 calories. Grilled Lamb Skewers with Spiced Mint Marinade has 33.0 grams of protein.

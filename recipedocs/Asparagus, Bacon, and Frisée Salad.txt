Asparagus, Bacon, and Frisée Salad recipe. In a skillet cook bacon over moderate heat until crisp. Drain bacon on paper towels and crumble. Trim asparagus and in a saucepan of boiling salted water cook until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. In a colander drain asparagus and refresh under cold water. Cut asparagus into 1/2-inch-thick pieces, leaving about 2 inches of tops intact. In a large bowl whisk together vinegar, mustard, and salt and pepper to taste and add oil in a stream, whisking until emulsified. Tear frisée or chicory into bite-size pieces and add to dressing with asparagus and bacon. Toss salad to combine. Asparagus, Bacon, and Frisée Salad has 453.0 calories. Asparagus, Bacon, and Frisée Salad has 10.0 grams of protein. Asparagus, Bacon, and Frisée Salad has 43.0 grams of fat. Asparagus, Bacon, and Frisée Salad has 401.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Asparagus, Bacon, and Frisée Salad are 5 slices bacon (about 1/4 pound), 1/2 pound asparagus, 1 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1/2 head frisée (French curly endive) or 1/4 head chicory (curly endive).

Penne with Tuna, Basil, and Lemon recipe. Bring a 4-quart pasta pot three fourths full with salted water to a boil for penne. Mince garlic. Finely grate enough lemon zest to measure 1 tablespoon and squeeze 1 teaspoon juice. Cut basil into thin strips. In a large bowl toss together garlic, zest, juice, basil, and tuna with oil from can. Boil penne until al dente and drain in a colander. Add penne to bowl and toss with salt and pepper to taste. Penne with Tuna, Basil, and Lemon has 601.0 calories. Penne with Tuna, Basil, and Lemon has 40.0 grams of protein. Penne with Tuna, Basil, and Lemon has 9.0 grams of fat. Penne with Tuna, Basil, and Lemon has 362.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Penne with Tuna, Basil, and Lemon are 1/2 pound penne, 1 garlic clove, 1 lemon, 1/4 cup packed fresh basil leaves, a 6-ounce can tuna in olive oil (not drained).This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

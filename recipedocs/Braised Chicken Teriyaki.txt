Braised Chicken Teriyaki recipe. Stir first 6 ingredients in heavy large pot over low heat until sugar dissolves. Add chicken. Cover pot and simmer until chicken is just cooked through, turning occasionally, about 50 minutes. Transfer chicken to platter; cover with foil to keep warm. Increase heat and boil sauce until reduced to 1 1/2 cups, about 15 minutes. Spoon sauce over. Braised Chicken Teriyaki has 1253.0 calories. Braised Chicken Teriyaki has 102.0 grams of protein. The ingredients of Braised Chicken Teriyaki are 1 cup soy sauce, 3/4 cup sake or dry white wine, 3/4 cup canned low-salt chicken broth, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 4 large garlic cloves, minced, 1 4 1/2-pound fryer chicken.Serve over freshly steamed rice and garnish with chopped green onions.

Smash recipe. Put sugar, mint and a little water in a small tumbler or Old-fashioned glass and muddle to crush the mint and dissolve the sugar. Add ice and pour in the liquor. Finish with a little club soda, if you like, and garnish with a sprig of mint. Smash has 168.0 calories. Smash has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Smash are 1 teaspoon superfine sugar, 2 sprigs fresh mint, 1/2 cup crushed ice, 1 1/2 to 3 ounces liquor (brandy, gin, rum or any whiskey), Club soda, Fresh mint for garnish.

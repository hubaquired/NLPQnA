Maple Old-Fashioned recipe.  Mix whiskey, maple syrup, bitters, and 1 tsp. water in an old-fashioned glass until syrup is dissolved. Add a single large ice cube, then garnish with orange peel. Maple Old-Fashioned has 161.0 calories. Maple Old-Fashioned has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Maple Old-Fashioned are 2 ounces rye whiskey or bourbon, 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup, Dash of Angostura bitters, Orange peel (for garnish).This twist on the classic whiskey cocktail is the perfect companion to the fall season.

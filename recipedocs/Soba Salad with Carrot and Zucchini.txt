Soba Salad with Carrot and Zucchini recipe.  Whisk together lime juice, soy sauce, ginger, sugar, and oil in a large bowl. Add remaining ingredients and toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Serve at room temperature. Soba Salad with Carrot and Zucchini has 381.0 calories. Soba Salad with Carrot and Zucchini has 12.0 grams of protein. Soba Salad with Carrot and Zucchini has 15.0 grams of fat. Soba Salad with Carrot and Zucchini has 1812.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Soba Salad with Carrot and Zucchini are 3 to 4 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 6 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons finely minced peeled fresh ginger, 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar, 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 1/2 lb soba noodles, freshly cooked to tender, rinsed under cold water, and drained, 3 carrots, cut into 1/8-inch matchsticks, 2 medium zucchini, cut into 1/8-inch matchsticks, 1 bunch scallions, thinly sliced crosswise.Active time: 30 min Start to finish: 30 min.

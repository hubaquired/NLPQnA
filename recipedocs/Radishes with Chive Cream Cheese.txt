Radishes with Chive Cream Cheese recipe.  Trim root ends of radishes so radishes will stand upright and cut 1/4 inch from stem ends. With a small melon-ball cutter scoop out center of each radish, leaving a 1/3-inch-thick shell. In a small bowl whisk together cream cheese, lemon juice, chives, and salt and pepper to taste. With a small knife fill radishes with cream cheese mixture. Radishes with Chive Cream Cheese has 43.0 calories. Radishes with Chive Cream Cheese has 1.0 grams of protein. Radishes with Chive Cream Cheese has 3.0 grams of fat. Radishes with Chive Cream Cheese has 55.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Radishes with Chive Cream Cheese are 8 medium radishes, 2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh chives.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

The Last Word recipe. Pour gin, Chartreuse, lime juice, and maraschino liqueur into a cocktail shaker. Add ice; cover and shake vigorously 10 times. Strain into 2 chilled coupe or Martini glasses. Garnish each with a lime twist. The Last Word has 201.0 calories. The Last Word has 4.0 grams of sodium.

Curry-Lime Yogurt recipe. In medium bowl, whisk together all ingredients. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Taste and add more lime juice if desired. Curry-Lime Yogurt has 156.0 calories. Curry-Lime Yogurt has 9.0 grams of protein. Curry-Lime Yogurt has 8.0 grams of fat. Curry-Lime Yogurt has 114.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Curry-Lime Yogurt are 2 cups plain yogurt, 2 teaspoons curry powder, 1/4 teaspoon fresh lime juice, plus more to taste.Lime juice and curry powder enliven this simple yogurt dipping sauce. It's so quick and easy that you can make it while the pancakes are cooking. For best results, use full-fat yogurt. In a pinch, low-fat will do, but do not use nonfat.

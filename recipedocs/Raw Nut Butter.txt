Raw Nut Butter recipe. Put the nuts in a food processor and process for 20–30 minutes, stopping and scraping down the side as needed every 5 minutes. This takes more time than you’d expect, so be patient! It’s ready when the oils have released from the nuts and the consistency is like smooth butter. Stir in the raw cacao to make a delicious chocolate spread. Transfer to a glass jar, seal, and store in the fridge. The ingredients of Raw Nut Butter are 2–3 cups nuts of your choice, such as peanuts, hazelnuts, almonds, pistachios or cashews, 1 teaspoon raw cacao (optional).Don’t like dairy butter? Give this a try. You can use almost any nut, so get creative.  HarperCollins Nut Raw Chocolate Hazelnut Almond Pistachio Cashew Condiment Spread Peanut Butter.

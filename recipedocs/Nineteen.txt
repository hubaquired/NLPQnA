Nineteen recipe. Combine all ingredients, except the cherry, in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass; add cherry. The ingredients of Nineteen are 3/4 ounce gin, 3/4 ounce kirsch, 3/4 ounce dry vermouth, Dash Angostura bitters, 1/4 teaspoon sugar syrup, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Maraschino cherry.

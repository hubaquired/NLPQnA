Marinated Cauliflower, Olive, and Arugula Salad recipe. Whisk vinegar and mustard in small bowl; gradually whisk in oil. Season dressing generously with salt and pepper. Combine cauliflower, olives, and celery in large bowl. Add 3/4 cup dressing; toss to coat. Cover salad and remaining dressing separately; chill overnight. Mix green onions and parsley into salad. Arrange arugula on platter; drizzle with remaining dressing. Top arugula with cauliflower mixture. Sprinkle with pepper. Marinated Cauliflower, Olive, and Arugula Salad has 216.0 calories. Marinated Cauliflower, Olive, and Arugula Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Marinated Cauliflower, Olive, and Arugula Salad has 21.0 grams of fat. Marinated Cauliflower, Olive, and Arugula Salad has 267.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Marinated Cauliflower, Olive, and Arugula Salad are 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 6 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 1 cup canola oil, 1 large head of cauliflower, cut into 1-inch florets (about 6 cups), 2 cups Kalamata olives, pitted, halved, 1 1/2 cups chopped celery, 1 bunch green onions, chopped, 1/4 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley, 16 cups arugula (about 10 ounces).Some of the ingredients need to marinate overnight in the Dijon-red wine vinaigrette, so plan accordingly.

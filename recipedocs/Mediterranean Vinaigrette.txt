Mediterranean Vinaigrette recipe.  Whisk together lemon juice, garlic clove, rosemary, red pepper flakes, and salt. Add olive oil in a slow stream, whisking. Mediterranean Vinaigrette has 166.0 calories. Mediterranean Vinaigrette has 18.0 grams of fat. Mediterranean Vinaigrette has 478.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mediterranean Vinaigrette are 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped, 1 teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes, 1 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/3 cup olive oil.

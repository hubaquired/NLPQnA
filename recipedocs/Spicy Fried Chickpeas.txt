Spicy Fried Chickpeas recipe. In large heavy pot, heat 3 inches oil until deep-fat thermometer registers 375°F. Carefully add chickpeas and fry until crisp and dark golden, 5 to 7 minutes. Using fine-mesh spider or slotted spoon, transfer to paper towel-lined plate. Sprinkle immediately with paprika and salt and serve hot. Chickpeas can be made up to 4 hours ahead and stored at room temperature. Rewarm in microwave or broiler before serving.) Spicy Fried Chickpeas has 290.0 calories. The ingredients of Spicy Fried Chickpeas are Vegetable oil (for deep-frying), 2 (15-ounce) cans chickpeas, drained, rinsed, drained again on paper towels, and patted dry, 3/4 teaspoon smoked hot paprika, such as Pimentón de la Vera, 3/4 teaspoon flaky sea salt, such as Maldon.These irresistible nibbles are at their best served within minutes of deep-frying. If that seems like a little much for Thanksgiving dinner, you can fry them up to 4 hours ahead and warm them up in the broiler or microwave as your guests arrive.

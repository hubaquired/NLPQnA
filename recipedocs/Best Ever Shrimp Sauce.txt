Best Ever Shrimp Sauce recipe.  Force garlic through a press into a bowl and stir together with remaining ingredients. Chill sauce at least 4 hours and up to 3 days. Serve sauce with shrimp and/or crudités. The ingredients of Best Ever Shrimp Sauce are 1 garlic clove, 1 cup mayonnaise, 1/2 cup chili sauce (such as Heinz), 1 tablespoon drained bottled horseradish, or to taste, 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard, or to taste, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne, or to taste, 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice, or to taste, Accompaniment: chilled boiled shrimp and/or crudités.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less; but requires additional sitting time.

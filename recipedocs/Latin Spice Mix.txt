Latin Spice Mix recipe. Combine cumin, peppercorns and coriander in heavy medium skillet. Stir over medium heat until fragrant and toasted, about 8 minutes. Cool slightly. Finely grind toasted spices in blender. Transfer to small bowl. Mix in sugar and salt. Latin Spice Mix has 129.0 calories. Latin Spice Mix has 4.0 grams of protein. Latin Spice Mix has 4.0 grams of fat. Latin Spice Mix has 1455.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Latin Spice Mix are 1/4 cup cumin seeds, 3 tablespoons whole black peppercorns, 1 tablespoon coriander seeds, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt.This is used in four of the dishes in the menu; the recipe makes enough for them all.

Pineapple and Mint Chutney recipe. In a bowl combine the mint with the pineapple, the onion, the sugar, and a pinch of salt and stir the mixture until it is combined well. Let the chutney stand, covered, for 1 hour. The chutney may be made 1 week in advance and kept covered and chilled. Serve the chutney with grilled meat and fish. Pineapple and Mint Chutney has 215.0 calories. Pineapple and Mint Chutney has 3.0 grams of protein. Pineapple and Mint Chutney has 1.0 grams of fat. Pineapple and Mint Chutney has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pineapple and Mint Chutney are 1 1/2 cups loosely packed fresh mint leaves, rinsed, spun dry, and chopped fine, a 4 1/2-pound pineapple, peeled, cored, and cut into 1/3-inch dice, 1/3 cup minced red onion, 1/4 cup sugar, or to taste.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

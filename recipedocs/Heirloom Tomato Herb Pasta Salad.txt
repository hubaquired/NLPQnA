Heirloom Tomato Herb Pasta Salad recipe. Whisk together oil, crème fraîche, vinegar, sugar if using), 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper in a large bowl. Coarsely chop tomatoes and toss with shallot and dressing. Marinate until ready to use, at least 10 minutes. While tomatoes stand, cook fusilli in a pasta pot of well-salted boiling water until al dente. Drain in a colander and immediately add to tomato mixture, tossing to combine. Cool to warm or room temperature do not chill), tossing occasionally, then stir in herbs. Heirloom Tomato Herb Pasta Salad has 453.0 calories. The ingredients of Heirloom Tomato Herb Pasta Salad are 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/3 cup crème fraîche, 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar, 1 to 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar (optional), 2 pounds mixed heirloom tomatoes, 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallot (from 1 medium), 1 pound fusilli or other short spiral pasta, 3/4 cup chopped mixed herbs such as basil, parsley, thyme, oregano, and tarragon.Spontaneity is the key to this pasta salad; it's not something you can make ahead and chill. The main ingredients can be harvested from a backyard plot or balcony pot, or simply be a reward for your produce-shopping prowess at the farmers market.

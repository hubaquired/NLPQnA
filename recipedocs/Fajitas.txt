Fajitas recipe.  Arrange the steak, chicken, tomatoes, onions and limes decoratively on the platter. Sprinkle with parsley. Place tortillas in a napkin-lined basket. Put the other toppings in bowls. To serve yourself, fill a tortilla with steak or chicken; add toppings to taste. Don't overstuff.) Roll up and enjoy. The ingredients of Fajitas are Skirt Steak Fajita strips, Chicken Fajita strips, 2 to 4 cups diced tomatoes, 2 or 3 large onions, thinly slivered, 4 limes, quartered, 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley or cilantro, for garnish, 16 flour tortillas (7 1/2-inch diameter), warmed, Tomato Papaya Salsa, 2 to 4 cups guacamole, store-bought or homemade, 2 to 3 cups sour cream, 2 to 3 cups grated Monterey Jack.Start with a large platter.

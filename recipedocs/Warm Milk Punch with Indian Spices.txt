Warm Milk Punch with Indian Spices recipe.  Combine first 6 ingredients in medium saucepan and bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Add tea bags or leaves and remove saucepan from heat. Cover pan and let mixture steep 30 minutes. Strain liquid into another saucepan. Add milk and sugar to tea. Bring to simmer, stirring until sugar dissolves. Divide punch among 4 glasses. Garnish with orange peel strips and additional cinnamon sticks, if desired.

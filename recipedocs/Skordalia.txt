Skordalia recipe. Mix ingredients together in a small bowl. Serve with Barba Yianni's Grilled Lamb. Skordalia has 44.0 calories. Skordalia has 2.0 grams of protein. Skordalia has 2.0 grams of fat. Skordalia has 362.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Skordalia are 1 cup plain yogurt, 4 garlic cloves, pressed, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint or 1 tablespoon dried, salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.Skordalia is often made with mashed potatoes or bread soaked in water, but we feel the yogurt gives it more flavor. Skordalia is also good with grilled fish or chicken breasts.  Condiment Spread Sauce Dairy Garlic Herb No-Cook Quick & Easy Yogurt Lemon Mint Healthy.

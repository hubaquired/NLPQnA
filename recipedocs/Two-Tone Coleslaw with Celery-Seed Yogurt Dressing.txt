Two-Tone Coleslaw with Celery-Seed Yogurt Dressing recipe.  Whisk yogurt, mayonnaise, vinegar, onion, sugar, and celery seeds in large bowl to blend. Add green and red cabbage, bell pepper, carrots, and 1 tablespoon dill. Toss to blend. Season salad to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Keep refrigerated. Toss to blend before using.) Sprinkle salad with remaining 1 tablespoon dill and serve. The ingredients of Two-Tone Coleslaw with Celery-Seed Yogurt Dressing are 1/2 cup plain low-fat yogurt, 1/3 cup light mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar, 2 tablespoons grated onion, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 teaspoon celery seeds, 3 cups thinly sliced green cabbage, 3 cups thinly sliced red cabbage, 1/2 large green bell pepper, thinly sliced into strips, 1/2 cup coarsely grated peeled carrots, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, divided.This pretty salad would be great alongside grilled sausages, chicken, or burgers.

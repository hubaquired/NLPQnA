Cucumber-Dill Sauce recipe.  Toss cucumber with 1/2 teaspoon salt in medium bowl. Let stand 30 minutes. Transfer to colander and rinse well. Pat cucumber dry with paper towels. Mix cucumber, sour cream, milk and dill in small bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours. Can be made 1 day ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Cucumber-Dill Sauce has 195.0 calories. Cucumber-Dill Sauce has 3.0 grams of protein. Cucumber-Dill Sauce has 18.0 grams of fat. Cucumber-Dill Sauce has 633.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber-Dill Sauce are 1 medium cucumber, peeled, seeded, cut into 1/4-inch pieces, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons whole milk, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill.

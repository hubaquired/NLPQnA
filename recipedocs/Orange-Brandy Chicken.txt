Orange-Brandy Chicken recipe.  Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with rosemary, salt and pepper. Add chicken to skillet and sauté until brown, about 3 minutes per side. Add orange juice concentrate, chicken broth and brandy and simmer until chicken is just cooked through, 5 minutes. Transfer chicken to plate; tent with foil to keep warm. Boil pan juices until thickened to sauce consistency, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes. Spoon sauce over chicken. Garnish with orange slices, if desired, and serve. Orange-Brandy Chicken has 400.0 calories. Orange-Brandy Chicken has 37.0 grams of protein. Orange-Brandy Chicken has 22.0 grams of fat. Orange-Brandy Chicken has 193.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange-Brandy Chicken are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 4 boneless chicken breast halves, 1 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary or 1/2 teaspoon dried, 1/3 cup frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed, 1/3 cup canned chicken broth, 2 tablespoons brandy, Orange slices (optional).

Hot-Smoked Salmon with Caper Cream Cheese recipe.  Mix cream cheese, yogurt, lemon juice, 1/4 onion, and 2 tablespoons capers in a medium bowl; season with salt and pepper. Serve caper cream cheese with salmon, bread, dill sprigs, lemon wedges, and more capers and onion. DO AHEAD: Cream cheese can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Hot-Smoked Salmon with Caper Cream Cheese are 12 ounces cream cheese, room temperature, 1/3 cup plain whole Greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1/4 small red onion, finely chopped, plus more for serving, 2 tablespoons drained capers plus more for serving, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 4 ounces hot-smoked salmon or trout, Toasted spelt bread, dill sprigs, and lemon wedges (for serving).Hot-smoked salmon, unlike cured, is fully cooked.

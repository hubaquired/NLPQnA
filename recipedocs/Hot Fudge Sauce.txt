Hot Fudge Sauce recipe.  Bring cream and corn syrup to simmer in heavy medium saucepan. Remove from heat. Add all chocolate and stir until melted and smooth. Can be made 2 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Rewarm Hot Fudge Sauce over medium-low heat just until heated through before serving.) Hot Fudge Sauce has 685.0 calories. Hot Fudge Sauce has 9.0 grams of protein. Hot Fudge Sauce has 44.0 grams of fat. Hot Fudge Sauce has 52.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Fudge Sauce are 1 cup whipping cream, 1/2 cup light corn syrup, 6 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped, 6 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) chocolate, chopped.

Cucumber, Jícama, and Pickled Ginger Salad recipe. Whisk together vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved, then add oil in a slow stream, whisking until emulsified. Toss together cucumber, jicama, ginger, and dressing in a bowl until combined well, then let stand, covered and chilled, 15 minutes. Cucumber, Jícama, and Pickled Ginger Salad has 107.0 calories. Cucumber, Jícama, and Pickled Ginger Salad has 1.0 grams of protein. Cucumber, Jícama, and Pickled Ginger Salad has 7.0 grams of fat. Cucumber, Jícama, and Pickled Ginger Salad has 151.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber, Jícama, and Pickled Ginger Salad are 2 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 seedless cucumber (1 1/4 lb; usually plastic-wrapped), cored and cut into 1/8-inch-thick matchsticks, 1/2 lb jicama, peeled and cut into 1/8-inch-thick matchsticks (2 cups), 2 tablespoons drained bottled pickled ginger, finely chopped.

Sweet-Potato and Parsnip Purée recipe. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and parsnips and boil gently until tender, about 12 minutes. Drain well and transfer to a food processor. Add butter and purée until smooth. Add milk, brown sugar, and salt and blend well. Season with pepper. Sweet-Potato and Parsnip Purée has 295.0 calories. Sweet-Potato and Parsnip Purée has 4.0 grams of protein. Sweet-Potato and Parsnip Purée has 10.0 grams of fat. Sweet-Potato and Parsnip Purée has 367.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sweet-Potato and Parsnip Purée are 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 4 parsnips, peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1/4 cup whole milk, 3 tablespoons packed light brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt.The gentle sweetness of this simple side dish goes well with the slightly spicy flank steak .

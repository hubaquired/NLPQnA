Spicy-Sweet Kumquats recipe.  Stir together sugar and chili powder. Whisk egg whites with water until dissolved. Working with 1 kumquat at a time, dip 1 side in whites, letting excess drip off, then dip in sugar mixture. Transfer to a rack to dry, coated side up. The ingredients of Spicy-Sweet Kumquats are 6 tablespoons granulated sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder, 4 teaspoons powdered egg whites (see Cooks' notes), 1/4 cup warm water, 60 kumquats (preferably thin-skinned).Pop one of these in your mouth for a burst of bittersweet flavor.

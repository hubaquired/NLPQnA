Herbed Chickpeas recipe.  Place chickpeas in a large skillet or Dutch oven and add garlic and oil; season with salt and pepper. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until chickpeas are crisped and some have split open these will be the most delicious ones), 10–15 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in herbs. The ingredients of Herbed Chickpeas are 2 (15.5-ounce cans) chickpeas, rinsed, patted dry, 4 garlic cloves, crushed, 1/3 cup olive oil, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 2 1/2 cups chopped mixed tender herbs (such as parsley, cilantro, chives, and/or basil).Crispy, salty, creamy, and full of protein (really!), these chickpeas should be a staple in your kitchen.

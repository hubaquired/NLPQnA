Tangy Avocado-Orange Salad recipe.  Using small sharp knife, cut off peel and white pith from oranges. Working over large bowl, cut between membranes to release orange segments. Add lettuce, avocado, and vinegar to bowl; toss gently. Season with salt and pepper; divide among 4 plates. Tangy Avocado-Orange Salad has 111.0 calories. Tangy Avocado-Orange Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Tangy Avocado-Orange Salad has 5.0 grams of fat. Tangy Avocado-Orange Salad has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tangy Avocado-Orange Salad are 2 oranges, 1/2 small head of red leaf lettuce, coarsely torn, 1 small avocado, halved, pitted, peeled, diced, 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar.For a richer version, add a little olive oil.

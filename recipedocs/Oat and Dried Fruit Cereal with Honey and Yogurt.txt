Oat and Dried Fruit Cereal with Honey and Yogurt recipe. Combine oats, 1/2 cup yogurt, fried fruits, nuts, honey and lemon juice in medium bowl; stir to blend. Refrigerate at least 1 hour and up to 6 hours. Stir remaining 1/2 cup yogurt into mixture. Serve, passing additional yogurt and honey, if desired.

Minted Simple Syrup recipe.  In heavy medium saucepan over medium heat, stir together water and sugar until sugar dissolves. Increase heat slightly, then simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Take pan off heat, add mint leaves, and steep 15 minutes. Strain, then refrigerate syrup until cold, about 3 hours. Can be prepared 1 week ahead. Cover and keep refrigerated.) Minted Simple Syrup has 197.0 calories. Minted Simple Syrup has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Minted Simple Syrup are 1 cup water, 1 cup sugar, 1 bunch mint.

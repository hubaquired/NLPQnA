Coffee Cream recipe. Blend ricotta, sugar and coffee granules in processor. Add cream and brandy and process until smooth and slightly thickened. Divide mixture into six 8-ounce custard cups. Cover with plastic and refrigerate until well chilled. Coffee Cream has 396.0 calories. Coffee Cream has 18.0 grams of protein. Coffee Cream has 26.0 grams of fat. Coffee Cream has 135.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coffee Cream are 2 pounds whole milk ricotta cheese, 3/4 cup powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons instant coffee granules, 1 cup chilled whipping cream, 3 tablespoons brandy.This is a fabulous pudding-like dessert that's very easy to make.

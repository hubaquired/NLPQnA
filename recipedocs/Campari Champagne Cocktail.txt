Campari Champagne Cocktail recipe. Stir honey and Campari in 2-cup measuring cup until honey dissolves. Stir in orange juice. Cover and chill until cold, at least 30 minutes and up to 4 hours. Pour 3 tablespoons Campari mixture into each of 6 chilled Champagne flutes. Fill glasses with Champagne. Garnish with strawberries, if desired. Campari Champagne Cocktail has 185.0 calories. Campari Champagne Cocktail has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Campari Champagne Cocktail are 4 1/2 tablespoons honey, 4 1/2 tablespoons Campari, 3/4 cup fresh orange juice, 1 750-ml bottle champagne, chilled, 6 small strawberries (optional).The sweetness of honey and the bite of Campari combine in this refreshing drink; its flavor is reminiscent of grapefruit.

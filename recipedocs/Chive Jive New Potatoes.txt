Chive Jive New Potatoes recipe.  Combine broth, potatoes and bay leaves in heavy large saucepan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover partially and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer potatoes to large bowl. Add wine to broth and boil until reduced to 1/3 cup, about 12 minutes. Discard bay leaves. Return potatoes to pot. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Stir potatoes over medium heat until heated through, about 4 minutes. Stir in chives. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl and serve. The ingredients of Chive Jive New Potatoes are 3 cups canned low-salt chicken broth, 1 1/2 pounds small red-skinned potatoes, rinsed, 2 bay leaves, 1/4 cup dry white wine, 1/2 cup chopped fresh chives or green onions.The potatoes are cooked in chicken broth and then tossed with chives to give the dish a boost of flavor with almost no additional fat.

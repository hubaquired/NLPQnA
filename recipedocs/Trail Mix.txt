Trail Mix recipe.  In a large bowl toss together well the M&M;'s, the peanuts, the raisins, and the apricots. Divide the trail mix among 6 cups and garnish the cups with the chocolate twig candied. Trail Mix has 508.0 calories. Trail Mix has 11.0 grams of protein. Trail Mix has 24.0 grams of fat. Trail Mix has 156.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Trail Mix are 1 1/4 cups M&M;'s, 1 1/4 cups salted peanuts, 1 1/4 cups raisins, 1/2 cup finely chopped dried apricots, chocolate twig candies (available at specialty foods shops) for garnish.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

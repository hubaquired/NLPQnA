Sea Bass with Artichokes, Zucchini and Tomatoes recipe.  Preheat oven to 475°F. Drain marinade from artichokes into 9-inch-diameter glass pie dish. Add fish; turn to coat with marinade. Sprinkle fish with salt and pepper. Scatter artichokes, tomatoes, zucchini, basil and garlic around fish. Pour clam juice over. Bake until fish is just opaque in center, about 20 minutes. Sea Bass with Artichokes, Zucchini and Tomatoes has 257.0 calories. Sea Bass with Artichokes, Zucchini and Tomatoes has 40.0 grams of protein. Sea Bass with Artichokes, Zucchini and Tomatoes has 4.0 grams of fat. Sea Bass with Artichokes, Zucchini and Tomatoes has 765.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sea Bass with Artichokes, Zucchini and Tomatoes are 1 6-ounce jar marinated artichoke hearts, 2 5- to 6-ounce sea bass fillets, 8 ounces plum tomatoes, chopped, 1 zucchini, cut into rounds, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 3/4 cup bottled clam juice.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

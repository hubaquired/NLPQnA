Cherry Tomato Relish recipe.  Whisk vinegar and oregano in medium bowl to blend. Gradually whisk in oil. Mix in green chilies and green onions. Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover; chill.) Add tomatoes; toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Cherry Tomato Relish are 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 4 teaspoons chopped fresh oregano, 3/4 cup olive oil, 1/8 cup drained canned diced mild green chilies, 4 green onions, finely chopped, 4 cups halved cherry tomatoes.Halved cherry tomatoes and diced green chilies make a chunky relish for the tri-tip.

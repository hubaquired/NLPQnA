Molasses, Rum, and Ginger Milk Punch recipe.  In a punch bowl whisk together the molasses and the ginger, whisk in the milk, the half-and-half, the rum, the brandy, and chill the punch, covered, for at least four hours, or until it is very cold. Serve the punch in punch glasses and sprinkle it with the nutmeg. Molasses, Rum, and Ginger Milk Punch has 274.0 calories. Molasses, Rum, and Ginger Milk Punch has 5.0 grams of protein. Molasses, Rum, and Ginger Milk Punch has 9.0 grams of fat. Molasses, Rum, and Ginger Milk Punch has 72.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Molasses, Rum, and Ginger Milk Punch are 3/4 cup unsulfured molasses, 2 teaspoons ground ginger, or to taste, 4 cups chilled milk, 2 cups chilled half-and-half, 1 cup light rum, 1/2 cup brandy, Garnish: freshly grated nutmeg for sprinkling the punch.

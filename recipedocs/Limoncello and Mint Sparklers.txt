Limoncello and Mint Sparklers recipe. Combine mint and limoncello in a bowl, then gently bruise mint with a pestle or wooden spoon to release flavor). Chill, covered, 1 hour. Pour limoncello through a fine-mesh sieve into a pitcher, pressing firmly on mint and then discarding it. Just before serving, stir in lemon juice, sparkling water, and enough ice to fill pitcher. Limoncello and Mint Sparklers has 154.0 calories. Limoncello and Mint Sparklers has 1.0 grams of protein. Limoncello and Mint Sparklers has 24.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Limoncello and Mint Sparklers are 1 cup loosely packed fresh mint leaves, 1 cup chilled limoncello, 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 3 cups chilled sparkling water, Garnish: fresh mint sprigs; lemon slices.

Toreador recipe. In a cocktail shaker, combine the tequila, crème de cacao, cream and ice cubes, and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with the cocoa. The ingredients of Toreador are 2 ounces tequila, 1 ounce dark crème de cacao, 1 ounce heavy cream, 1/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

Sazerac Cocktails recipe.  Swirl 1/2 teaspoon Herbsaint or Pernod in each of 4 Martini glasses to coat inside of glasses. Fill cocktail shaker with ice; add bourbon, bitters, simple syrup, and lemon twist. Shake well; strain into glasses. The ingredients of Sazerac Cocktails are 2 teaspoons Herbsaint or Pernod, 1 cup bourbon, 1/2 teaspoon Peychaud's bitters or angostura bitters, 4 teaspoons simple syrup, lemon twist.

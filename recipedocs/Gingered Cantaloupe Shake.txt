Gingered Cantaloupe Shake recipe.  In a blender, blend the cantaloupe pieces, the ginger, and the frozen yogurt until the mixture is smooth. Gingered Cantaloupe Shake has 4.0 grams of fat. Gingered Cantaloupe Shake has 90.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gingered Cantaloupe Shake are A 1 3/4-pound cantaloupe, seeded and cut into 1/2-inch pieces (about 2 cups), plus cantaloupe slices for garnish, 1 tablespoon chopped crystallized ginger, 1 pint vanilla frozen yogurt or ice cream.

Spicy Chicken with Yogurt and Dill recipe.  Place chicken in medium bowl; sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. Stir in plain yogurt. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours. Heat vegetable oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shallots, chilies, ginger and cumin; sauté until shallots are tender, about 2 minutes. Add chicken with yogurt and sauté until chicken begins to brown, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 3 minutes. Uncover skillet. Stir in dill. Increase heat to medium-high and simmer until sauce thickens slightly, about 1 minute. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Spicy Chicken with Yogurt and Dill has 432.0 calories. Spicy Chicken with Yogurt and Dill has 54.0 grams of protein. Spicy Chicken with Yogurt and Dill has 18.0 grams of fat. Spicy Chicken with Yogurt and Dill has 134.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Chicken with Yogurt and Dill are 2 pounds skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cut crosswise into 1-inch-wide strips, 3/4 cup plain yogurt, 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, 2 large shallots, chopped, 2 tablespoons minced seeded red jalapeño chilies, 2 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 2 teaspoons ground cumin, 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill.Serve this entrée with steamed white rice and warm pita rounds.

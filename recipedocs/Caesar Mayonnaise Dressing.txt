Caesar Mayonnaise Dressing recipe. In a bowl whisk together the garlic paste, the anchovy paste, the lemon juice, the mustard, and the Worcestershire sauce, add the mayonnaise, the Parmesan, and pepper to taste, and whisk the dressing until it is combined well. Toss the dressing with salad greens or serve it as a dip with crudités. Caesar Mayonnaise Dressing has 172.0 calories. Caesar Mayonnaise Dressing has 2.0 grams of protein. Caesar Mayonnaise Dressing has 18.0 grams of fat. Caesar Mayonnaise Dressing has 210.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Caesar Mayonnaise Dressing are 2 small garlic cloves, minced and mashed to a paste with 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon anchovy paste, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard, 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 1 cup bottled mayonnaise, 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

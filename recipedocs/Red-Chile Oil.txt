Red-Chile Oil recipe. Heat oil with red-pepper flakes in a 1- to 1 1/2-quart heavy saucepan over moderate heat, swirling pan occasionally, until red-pepper flakes begin to sizzle, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Pour into a heatproof bowl and cool to room temperature. Stir before using. Red-Chile Oil has 247.0 calories. Red-Chile Oil has 27.0 grams of fat. Red-Chile Oil has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red-Chile Oil are 1/4 cup peanut oil, 1 tablespoon dried hot red-pepper flakes.We like to make up a batch of this oil and keep it on hand for seasoning Asian dishes just before serving.

Mushrooms with Garlic and Madeira recipe. Heat olive oil in heavy large skillet over high heat. Add mushrooms and sauté until brown but not juicy, about 6 minutes. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add Madeira and cook until evaporated, about 1 minute. Mix in whipping cream and sliced basil; stir until sauce coats mushrooms, about 1 minute. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Mushrooms with Garlic and Madeira has 352.0 calories. Mushrooms with Garlic and Madeira has 6.0 grams of protein. Mushrooms with Garlic and Madeira has 35.0 grams of fat. Mushrooms with Garlic and Madeira has 27.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mushrooms with Garlic and Madeira are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1 pound whole small mushrooms or medium-size, halved, 2 tablespoons chopped garlic, 1/4 cup Madeira, 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1/4 cup thinly sliced fresh basil.Try these inside an omelet, with steak or on toast as a first course or snack.

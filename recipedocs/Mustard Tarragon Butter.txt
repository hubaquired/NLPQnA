Mustard Tarragon Butter recipe. Blend all ingredients with salt and pepper to taste in a food processor until smooth. Chill butter, covered, until just firm but still malleable, about 30 minutes. Transfer butter to a sheet of wax paper and roll into a 6-inch log. Chill, wrapped in wax paper and then foil, until firm, about 1 hour. Mustard Tarragon Butter has 210.0 calories. Mustard Tarragon Butter has 1.0 grams of protein. Mustard Tarragon Butter has 23.0 grams of fat. Mustard Tarragon Butter has 73.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mustard Tarragon Butter are 1 1/2 sticks (3/4 cup) unsalted butter, softened, 2 1/2 tablespoons whole-grain or coarse-grain mustard, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon.This butter is delicious on grilled fish or roast chicken, and will keep, chilled, up to 1 week, or frozen up to 1 month.

Sauteed Shrimp with Mustard-Chive Sauce recipe.  Whisk cream, clam juice and mustard in small bowl to blend. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and stir 1 minute. Add shrimp and chives and sauté 2 minutes. Add cream mixture. Simmer until shrimp are just opaque in center and sauce thickens slightly, about 2 minutes. Spoon rice onto plates. The ingredients of Sauteed Shrimp with Mustard-Chive Sauce are 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1/3 cup bottled clam juice, 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 3 tablespoons butter, 3 large garlic cloves, chopped, 1 1/4 pounds uncooked large shrimp, 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh chives or green onion tops, Cooked white rice.A sophisticated party dish.

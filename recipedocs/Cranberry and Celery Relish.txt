Cranberry and Celery Relish recipe. Simmer cranberries, sugar, water, and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a heavy medium saucepan, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until most of cranberries have burst, 15 to 20 minutes. Transfer to a bowl and cool completely. Just before serving, stir in celery and almonds. Cranberry and Celery Relish has 222.0 calories. Cranberry and Celery Relish has 2.0 grams of protein. Cranberry and Celery Relish has 3.0 grams of fat. Cranberry and Celery Relish has 28.0 grams of sodium.

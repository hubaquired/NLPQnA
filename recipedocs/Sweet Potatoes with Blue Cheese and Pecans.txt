Sweet Potatoes with Blue Cheese and Pecans recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Roast sweet potatoes on a rimmed baking sheet until tender, about 1 hour. Let cool slightly. Remove skins and transfer sweet potatoes to a large heavy saucepan over low heat. Add cream cheese and butter. Mash until well blended and creamy. Season with salt and pepper. Stir over low heat until hot. Spoon into a warm serving dish and garnish with cheese and pecans. The ingredients of Sweet Potatoes with Blue Cheese and Pecans are 3 pounds sweet potatoes (about 6), 3/4 cup (1 1/2 8-ounce packages) cream cheese, room temperature, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, cut into 1" cubes, room temperature, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 1/4 cup mild blue cheese, 1/4 cup store-bought candied pecans.The Reid family crumbles blue cheese on top to add a salty bite.

Chinese Peanut Sauce recipe. Mix peanut butter, soy sauce and garlic in medium bowl. Whisk in 1/2 cup hot water. Add remaining ingredients; whisk to blend. Season with salt and pepper. Let stand at room temperature 1 hour or cover and refrigerate up to 1 day. Chinese Peanut Sauce has 244.0 calories. Chinese Peanut Sauce has 9.0 grams of protein. Chinese Peanut Sauce has 19.0 grams of fat. Chinese Peanut Sauce has 513.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chinese Peanut Sauce are 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter (do not use old-fashioned or freshly ground), 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 4 teaspoons minced garlic, 1/2 cup hot water, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar, 2 teaspoons sugar, 3/4 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper.

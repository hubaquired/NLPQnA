Moonraker recipe. Pour the tequila and pineapple juice into a highball glass almost filled with ice cubes. Stir well. Drop the curaçao into the center of the drink. The ingredients of Moonraker are 1 1/2 ounces tequila, 4 ounces pineapple juice, 1/2 ounce blue curaçao.

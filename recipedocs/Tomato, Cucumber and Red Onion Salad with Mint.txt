Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion Salad with Mint recipe.  SERVINGS Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise; scrape out seeds. Cut halves diagonally into 1/2-inch-wide pieces. Place in large bowl. Add vinegar, sugar and salt. Let stand at room temperature 1 hour; toss occasionally. Add tomatoes, red onion, mint and oil to cucumbers and toss to blend. Season salad with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion Salad with Mint are 2 large English hothouse cucumbers, 1/3 cup red wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 teaspoon salt, 3 large tomatoes, seeded, coarsely chopped, 2/3 cup coarsely chopped red onion, 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint, 3 tablespoons olive oil.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Sliced Strip Steak with Arugula and Parsley recipe.  Prepare grill for medium-high heat. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper. Grill, turning several times, until lightly charred, 7–9 minutes for medium-rare. Transfer to a cutting board; let rest at least 15 minutes before thinly slicing. Toss arugula, onion, chile, caper berries, Parmesan, and parsley in a medium bowl. Drizzle with lemon juice and oil and toss to coat; season with salt and pepper. Drizzle steak with oil and season with salt and pepper; serve with salad. Steak can be grilled 1 day ahead. Let cool; cover and chill. Bring to room temperature before slicing.

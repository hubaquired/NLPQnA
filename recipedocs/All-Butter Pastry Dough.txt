All-Butter Pastry Dough recipe.  Whisk together flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl or pulse in a food processor). Blend in butter with your fingertips or a pastry blender or pulse) just until most of mixture resembles coarse meal with some roughly pea-size butter lumps. Drizzle 1/3 cup ice water over mixture and gently stir with a fork or pulse) until incorporated. If it doesn't hold together, add more ice water 1 tablespoon at a time, stirring or pulsing) until just incorporated, then test again. Do not overwork dough, or pastry will be tough. Turn out dough onto a lightly floured surface and divide into 8 portions. With heel of your hand, smear each portion once or twice in a forward motion to help distribute fat. Gather dough together, with a pastry scraper if you have one, and press into a ball. Divide in half and form into 2 disks. Wrap each disk in plastic wrap and chill until firm, at least 1 hour.

Boiled Coffee recipe. Bring all ingredients to a full boil in a large nonreactive saucepan, partially covered, then simmer, tightly covered, 5 to 7 minutes. Let stand, covered, off heat until grounds settle, about 2 minutes. Carefully strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a pot. The ingredients of Boiled Coffee are 10 tablespoons medium-ground full-flavored coffee, 10 cups cold water (preferably filtered), 5 empty eggshells, crushed.Boiling this strong coffee with eggshells clarifies it by helping the grounds to settle.

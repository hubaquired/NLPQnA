Port Cobbler recipe. Fill two-thirds of a tumbler with crushed ice, add orange juice and curaçao, and stir. Pour in the port and decorate with orange slice and pineapple stick. The ingredients of Port Cobbler are Crushed ice, 1 teaspoon orange juice, 1 teaspoon curaçao, 4 ounces port (approx.), Orange slice, Pineapple stick.

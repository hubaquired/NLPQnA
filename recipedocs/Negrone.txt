Negrone recipe.  In a cocktail shaker, combine the gin, the Campari, the vermouth, the orange juice, and 1/2 cup ice cubes, shake the cocktail until it is chilled, and strain it into a glass. Negrone has 317.0 calories. Negrone has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Negrone are 2 ounces (2 ponies) gin, 2 ounces (2 ponies) Campari, 1 ounce (1 pony) sweet vermouth, 2 tablespoons strained fresh orange juice.

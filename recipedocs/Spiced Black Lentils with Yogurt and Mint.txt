Spiced Black Lentils with Yogurt and Mint recipe.  Stir coriander, cumin, mustard seeds, and 2 tablespoons oil in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat until sizzling, about 1 minute. Add onion and garlic; cook, stirring often, until just softened, about 5 minutes. Add lentils and 3 cups broth; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, partially covered, adding broth as needed to keep lentils covered, until lentils are soft, 30–40 minutes. Stir in vinegar; season with salt and pepper. Transfer lentils to a large bowl and top with yogurt. Season with salt and pepper, drizzle with oil, and top with mint. DO AHEAD: Lentils can be cooked 2 days ahead. Let cool; cover and chill. The ingredients of Spiced Black Lentils with Yogurt and Mint are 1/2 teaspoon coriander seeds, crushed, 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds, crushed, 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds, 2 tablespoons olive oil plus more, 1 small onion, finely chopped, 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped, 1 cup black beluga lentils, 3 cups (or more) low-sodium vegetable broth or chicken broth, 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 0 freshly ground pepper, 1 cup plain Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, thinly sliced.Aptly named beluga lentils look like little beads of caviar. They cook quickly, and they hold their shape. Use them in this recipe, or substitute.

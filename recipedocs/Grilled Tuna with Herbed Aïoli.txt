Grilled Tuna with Herbed Aïoli recipe.  Whisk together first 6 ingredients in shallow dish for marinade. Place mayonnaise in separate small bowl. Whisk in 1 1/2 tablespoons marinade. Set aioli aside. Sprinkle fish with salt and pepper. Place fish in marinade in dish, turning to coat evenly. Marinate 1 hour at room temperature, turning fish occasionally. Oil grill rack. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Grill fish to desired doneness, about 3 minutes per side for medium. Top fish with aioli and serve. Grilled Tuna with Herbed Aïoli has 475.0 calories. Grilled Tuna with Herbed Aïoli has 49.0 grams of protein. Grilled Tuna with Herbed Aïoli has 29.0 grams of fat. Grilled Tuna with Herbed Aïoli has 202.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Tuna with Herbed Aïoli are 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme, 2 teaspoons dried tarragon, 2 large garlic cloves, finely chopped, 1/3 cup mayonnaise, 4 7-ounce tuna steaks (each about 1 inch thick).Serve with: Steamed new potatoes and mixed green salad with a citrus vinaigrette. Dessert: A lemon tart from the bakery.  Fish Herb Low Carb Tuna Summer Grill Barbecue Bon Appétit.

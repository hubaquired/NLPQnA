Scrambled Eggs with Tomato, Goat Cheese, and Mint recipe.  Whisk eggs in large bowl until well blended. Add tomato, goat cheese, green onions, chopped mint, salt and pepper; whisk to blend. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add shallots; sauté 3 minutes. Add remaining 2 tablespoons butter to skillet and let melt. Add egg mixture; cook 2 minutes without stirring. Using spatula or large spoon, gently stir and turn over portions of the egg mixture until cooked through but soft, about 4 minutes. Divide scrambled eggs among 4 plates. Garnish with mint sprigs, if desired, and serve. Scrambled Eggs with Tomato, Goat Cheese, and Mint has 321.0 calories. The ingredients of Scrambled Eggs with Tomato, Goat Cheese, and Mint are 10 large eggs, 1 large tomato, seeded, chopped, 1/4 cup coarsely crumbled soft fresh goat cheese (such as Montrachet), 1/4 cup chopped green onions, 1/4 cup chopped fresh peppermint or other fresh mint, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, 3 tablespoons butter, 2 shallots, chopped, Fresh mint sprigs (optional).

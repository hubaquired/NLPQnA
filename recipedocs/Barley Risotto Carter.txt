Barley Risotto Carter recipe. In a large heavy saucepan cook onion in butter over moderate heat, stirring, until softened. Add 2 cups broth, water, and barley and simmer, covered, until most of liquid is absorbed and barley is al dente, about 30 minutes. Gradually add remaining cup broth, about 1/4 cup at a time, stirring constantly and letting each addition be absorbed before adding next, until absorbed. Stir in remaining ingredients and remove pan from heat. Season "risotto" with salt and pepper. Barley Risotto Carter has 398.0 calories. Barley Risotto Carter has 14.0 grams of protein. Barley Risotto Carter has 18.0 grams of fat. Barley Risotto Carter has 305.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Barley Risotto Carter are 1/2 cup finely chopped onion, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 3 cups chicken broth, 1 cup water, 1 cup pearl barley, 1 cup frozen peas, thawed, 1/2 cup pecans, toasted and chopped, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley leaves, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint leaves, 2 teaspoons freshly grated lemon zest.This recipe came to be when I was preparing risotto and realized I was out of Arborio rice; I used barley instead. The results were so delicious that I now prefer this version.  Herb Side High Fiber Lemon Mint Pecan Barley Gourmet Chicago Illinois.

Lime Syrup recipe.  Stir together sugar, water, and a pinch of salt in a small saucepan and simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until syrup has thickened slightly, 5 to 8 minutes. Transfer to a glass measure and chill, uncovered, until cool, then stir in lime juice and food coloring. Lime Syrup has 326.0 calories. Lime Syrup has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lime Syrup are 2 1/2 cups sugar, 1 cup water, 6 tablespoons fresh lime juice, strained through a fine-mesh sieve, 4 drops green food coloring (if making cherry lime rickeys).This sweet-tart syrup will be enough for both the rickeys and the fruit cup.

Mixed-Berry Chocolate-Toffee Bites recipe.  Line large baking sheet with foil. Stir chocolate in small bowl set over saucepan of simmering water until melted, smooth, and warm. Remove bowl from over water. Mix orange peel into chocolate, if desired. Spoon by teaspoonfuls onto foil, spacing about 1 1/2 inches apart do not spread). Top with berries and toffee, making sure toppings touch melted chocolate. Chill until chocolate sets, about 15 minutes. Remove from foil. The ingredients of Mixed-Berry Chocolate-Toffee Bites are 6 ounces bittersweet or semisweet chocolate or high-quality milk chocolate, chopped, 1 1/2 teaspoons grated orange peel (optional), 2 cups mixed fresh berries (such as blackberries, raspberries, and blueberries), 2 1.4-ounce English toffee candy bars (such as Skor), cut into 1/4-inch pieces.These confections combine melted chocolate, berries, and bits of toffee. Serve them with your after-dinner coffee or give them as a gift. (Keep refrigerated; let stand 30 minutes before serving.).

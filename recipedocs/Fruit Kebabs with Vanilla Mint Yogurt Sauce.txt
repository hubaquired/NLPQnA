Fruit Kebabs with Vanilla Mint Yogurt Sauce recipe. In a bowl whisk together the yogurt, the sugar, the vanilla, and the mint. The sauce may be made 1 day in advance and kept overed and chilled.) Tread the fruits, alternating the pineapple, the kiwis, the strawberries, and the honeydew, onto the skewers. The fruit kebabs may be make 1 day in advance and kept overed and shilled.) Serve the fruit kebabs with the sauce. The ingredients of Fruit Kebabs with Vanilla Mint Yogurt Sauce are 1 cup plain yogurt, 2 teaspoons sugar, 1/8 teaspoon vanilla, 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh mint leaves, 1 pineapple, peeled, cored, and cubed, 3 kiwi fruits, peeled and slices, 2 pints strawberries, hulled, 1 honeydew melon, cubed, twenty-four 6-inch wooden skewers.

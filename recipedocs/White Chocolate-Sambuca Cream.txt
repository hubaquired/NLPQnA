White Chocolate-Sambuca Cream recipe.  Combine 1/3 cup whipping cream and chocolate in medium metal bowl. Set over saucepan of barely simmering water do not let bottom of bowl touch water). Stir until mixture is smooth. Remove from over water. Cool 15 minutes. Beat remaining 1 2/3 cups whipping cream, sambuca and vanilla in large bowl until soft peaks form. Fold 1/4 of whipped cream into white chocolate mixture to lighten. Fold in remaining whipped cream in 2 additions. Chill topping up to 8 hours. White Chocolate-Sambuca Cream has 179.0 calories. White Chocolate-Sambuca Cream has 1.0 grams of protein. White Chocolate-Sambuca Cream has 14.0 grams of fat. White Chocolate-Sambuca Cream has 23.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of White Chocolate-Sambuca Cream are 2 cups chilled whipping cream, 4 ounces good-quality white chocolate, chopped, 2 tablespoons sambuca, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.

Bacon-Wrapped Trout recipe.  Prepare grill for medium heat. Season trout inside and out with Four Seasons Blend. Stuff with herbs and wrap with bacon. Grill trout, turning, until bacon is crisp and a paring knife inserts easily into flesh and is warm when removed, 15-20 minutes. do ahead: Fish can be prepped 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill. Bacon-Wrapped Trout has 1085.0 calories. Bacon-Wrapped Trout has 100.0 grams of protein. Bacon-Wrapped Trout has 72.0 grams of fat. Bacon-Wrapped Trout has 974.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bacon-Wrapped Trout are 6 14-16-ounce rainbow trout, butterflied, deboned, 1 tablespoon Four Seasons Blend, 1 bunch fresh flat-leaf parsley, 1 bunch fresh thyme, 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves, thinly sliced, 1 1/2 pound thinly sliced bacon.The bacon wrap is a genius way to secure flavorful fresh herbs inside the fish.

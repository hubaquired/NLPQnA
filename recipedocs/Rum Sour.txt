Rum Sour recipe. Combine the rum, lime juice, sugar syrup, and crushed ice in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a Delmonico sometimes called a Whiskey Sour) glass and decorate with the orange slice and cherry. The ingredients of Rum Sour are 3 ounces dark rum, 3 tablespoons lime juice, Sugar syrup to taste, 1/2 cup crushed ice, Slice of orange, Maraschino cherry.

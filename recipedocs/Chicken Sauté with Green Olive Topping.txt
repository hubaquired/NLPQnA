Chicken Sauté with Green Olive Topping recipe.  Using on/off turns, coarsely chop garlic in food processor. Add olives, parsley, and lemon peel; blend until finely chopped. Mix in lemon juice. Sprinkle chicken on both sides with cumin, cayenne pepper, and salt. Heat olive oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and sauté until browned and just cooked through, about 3 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to 4 plates. Add olive topping to drippings in skillet; cook over medium-high heat until warm, about 1 minute. Spoon topping over chicken and serve. Chicken Sauté with Green Olive Topping has 301.0 calories. Chicken Sauté with Green Olive Topping has 33.0 grams of protein. Chicken Sauté with Green Olive Topping has 17.0 grams of fat. Chicken Sauté with Green Olive Topping has 348.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chicken Sauté with Green Olive Topping are 2 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup drained pimiento-stuffed green olives (about 2 1/2 ounces), 1/2 cup (packed) whole fresh parsley sprigs, 1 teaspoon minced lemon peel, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves (about 5 ounces each), flattened to 1/2-inch thickness, 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cumin, 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 3 tablespoons olive oil.Serve with: Tabbouleh (bulgur salad), and green beans sautéed with almonds.

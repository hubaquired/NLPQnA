Tomato-Rubbed Bruschetta recipe. Prepare grill. Brush bread slices on both sides with oil and grill on a rack set 5 to 6 inches over glowing coals until golden, about 1 minute on each side. While bread is grilling, halve garlic and tomato crosswise. Rub grilled bread with garlic and tomato to moisten it you may have tomato left over) and sprinkle with paprika and salt. Tomato-Rubbed Bruschetta has 155.0 calories. Tomato-Rubbed Bruschetta has 2.0 grams of protein. Tomato-Rubbed Bruschetta has 11.0 grams of fat. Tomato-Rubbed Bruschetta has 126.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato-Rubbed Bruschetta are 4 (1/2-inch-thick diagonal) slices Italian bread or baguette, 1 1/2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 garlic clove, 1 small vine-ripened tomato, 1/4 teaspoon paprika, coarse salt to taste.This bruschetta tastes best with tomatoes fresh from the garden.

Cabbage and Apple Slaw recipe. In a bowl stir together the yogurt, the sour cream, and the honey, add the cabbage, the apple, the onion, the parsley, and salt and pepper to taste, and stir the salad until it is combined well. Cabbage and Apple Slaw has 191.0 calories. Cabbage and Apple Slaw has 4.0 grams of protein. Cabbage and Apple Slaw has 8.0 grams of fat. Cabbage and Apple Slaw has 66.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cabbage and Apple Slaw are 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 1/4 cup sour cream, 2 teaspoons honey, 2 cups coarsely shredded red cabbage, 1 Granny Smith apple, peeled and chopped fine, 3 tablespoons minced onion, 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

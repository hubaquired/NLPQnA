Pink Gin recipe.  Pour the bitters into an 8-ounce wine-glass and swill them around it. Add the gin and dilute with water or, if preferred, add the ice cubes. Pink Gin has 153.0 calories. Pink Gin has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pink Gin are 2 dashes Angostura bitters, 2 ounces gin, 2 ounces water, or 2 to 3 ice cubes.

Stilton Dressing recipe.  Coarsely mash Stilton with a fork in a bowl. Stir in sour cream, water, vinegar, and pepper until combined well, then stir in chives. Season with salt if desired. Stilton Dressing has 383.0 calories. Stilton Dressing has 20.0 grams of protein. Stilton Dressing has 32.0 grams of fat. Stilton Dressing has 1071.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stilton Dressing are 2/3 cup crumbled Stilton (from a 6- to 7-oz wedge, rind discarded), 1/4 cup sour cream, 1/4 cup water, 2 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh chives.

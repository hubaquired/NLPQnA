Sake-Steamed Sea Bass with Ginger and Green Onions recipe.  Cook rice according to package directions. Meanwhile, combine sake and next 3 ingredients in large skillet deep enough to hold steamer rack. Bring liquid to boil. Reduce heat; simmer 5 minutes. Arrange fish on rack; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place rack in skillet. Top fish with onions; drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil. Cover skillet; steam fish until opaque in center, about 5 minutes. Remove steamer rack. Mix cilantro into juices in skillet. Spoon rice onto plates. Top with fish, juices from skillet, and sesame seeds. The ingredients of Sake-Steamed Sea Bass with Ginger and Green Onions are 1 cup uncooked medium-grain rice, 3/4 cup sake, 3/4 cup bottled clam juice, 1 tablespoon minced peeled ginger, 1 garlic clove, flattened, 4 5-ounce sea bass fillets, 2 large green onions, chopped, 4 teaspoons soy sauce, 1 teaspoon oriental sesame oil, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, 2 teaspoons sesame seeds, toasted.

Clams au Gratin recipe. Sauté the mushrooms and shallot or onion in 6 tablespoons butter for 3 minutes. Add the parsley, tomato and salt and pepper to taste. Spoon over clams. Sprinkle with crumbs, dot with butter and bake at 400°F. for 5-8 minutes, or until clams are just heated through. Clams au Gratin has 105.0 calories. Clams au Gratin has 9.0 grams of protein. Clams au Gratin has 3.0 grams of fat. Clams au Gratin has 382.0 grams of sodium.

Paris's Crimson Champagne Cocktail recipe. In a medium saucepan, boil the cranberry juice cocktail, the ginger, the cinnamon stick, and the star anise for half an hour or until reduced to 1 cup. Let cool. Fill a Champagne flute half-full with Champagne and add the brandy. Add 1 ounce Brandy-Cranberry Syrup and mix. Garnish with fresh cranberry. The ingredients of Paris's Crimson Champagne Cocktail are 1 quart cranberry juice cocktail, 4 slices fresh ginger, 1 cinnamon stick, 1 piece star anise, 1/4 cup brandy, Champagne, 1 fresh cranberry, for garnish.

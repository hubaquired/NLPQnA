Boulevardier recipe. Combine 2 ounces bourbon, 1 ounce Campari, and 1 ounce sweet vermouth preferably Antica Formula) in a large mixing glass filled with ice. Stir until glass is very cold, about 1 minute. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass or coupe or a rocks glass filled with fresh ice. Garnish with a lemon twist. Boulevardier has 231.0 calories.

Fine and Dandy recipe.  Combine all ingredients, except the cherry, in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass; add cherry. The ingredients of Fine and Dandy are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1/2 ounce Cointreau, 1/2 ounce lemon juice, Dash Angostura bitters, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Maraschino cherry.

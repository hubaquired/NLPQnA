Grainy-Mustard Mashed Potatoes with Sausage recipe.  Cook potato chunks in large pot of boiling salted water until tender, about 30 minutes. Return to pot. Meanwhile, cook sausage in heavy large skillet over medium heat until brown and cooked through, about 8 minutes. Add milk and mustard; bring to simmer. Mash potatoes; gradually mix in milk mixture. Add butter and stir to melt. Season with salt and pepper. Grainy-Mustard Mashed Potatoes with Sausage has 327.0 calories. Grainy-Mustard Mashed Potatoes with Sausage has 11.0 grams of protein. Grainy-Mustard Mashed Potatoes with Sausage has 13.0 grams of fat. Grainy-Mustard Mashed Potatoes with Sausage has 264.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grainy-Mustard Mashed Potatoes with Sausage are 4 pounds russet potatoes, peeled, cut into large chunks, 8 ounces sweet Italian sausage, casings removed, crumbled, 1 1/3 cups whole milk, 3 1/2 tablespoons whole grain Dijon mustard, 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature.Offer these potatoes alongside pork roast or pan-fried chops.

Pork Noodle Soup with Cinnamon and Anise recipe. Gently simmer all ingredients except noodles in a 6-quart heavy pot, covered, skimming as needed, until pork is very tender, 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Transfer pork to a bowl. Discard bones, spices, and garlic. Coarsely shred meat. Skim fat from broth, then return meat and bring to a simmer. Rinse noodles, then stir into broth and simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until noodles are translucent and tender, about 6 minutes. The ingredients of Pork Noodle Soup with Cinnamon and Anise are 2 1/2 pounds country-style pork ribs, 6 cups water, 2/3 cup soy sauce, 2/3 cup Chinese Shaoxing wine or medium-dry Sherry, 1/4 cup packed dark brown sugar, 1 head garlic, halved crosswise, 3 (3-inch) cinnamon sticks, 1 whole star anise, 5 1/2 ounces bean thread (cellophane) noodles, Garnish: chopped cilantro; sliced scallions.

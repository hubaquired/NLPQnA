Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Spinach recipe.  Whisk together eggs and tarragon in a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Melt butter in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over moderate heat, then cook spinach, stirring occasionally, until just wilted. Add egg mixture and cream cheese and cook, stirring slowly, until eggs are just set, about 3 minutes. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Spinach has 237.0 calories. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Spinach has 16.0 grams of protein. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Spinach has 18.0 grams of fat. Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Spinach has 261.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Scrambled Eggs with Spinach are 8 large eggs, 1 teaspoon chopped fresh tarragon or 1/4 teaspoon dried tarragon, crumbled, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, 3/4 lb baby spinach, coarsely chopped, 2 oz cold cream cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes.Active time: 15 min Start to finish: 15 min.

Thai Green Papaya Salad recipe.  Whisk first 5 ingredients in medium bowl. Set dressing aside. Cook beans in medium saucepan of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Rinse under cold water. Cut into 2-inch pieces. Using julienne peeler, peel enough papaya to measure 6 cups. Place in large bowl. Add tomatoes, cilantro, green onions, chile, and green beans. Pour dressing over; toss. Sprinkle peanuts over and serve. The ingredients of Thai Green Papaya Salad are 5 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons (packed) palm sugar or golden brown sugar, 2 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons fish sauce, 2 tablespoons dried shrimp, chopped, 4 garlic cloves, minced, 3 Chinese long beans, halved crosswise or 15 green beans, 1 1 1/2- to 13/4-pound green papaya, peeled, halved, seeded, 10 large cherry tomatoes, halved, 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 2 green onions, very thinly sliced, 1 fresh red Thai chile with seeds, thinly sliced, 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped salted peanuts, Special equipment: Julienne peeler or box grater.For a light lunch, top the salad with grilled shrimp or tofu.

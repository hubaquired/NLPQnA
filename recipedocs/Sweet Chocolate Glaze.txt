Sweet Chocolate Glaze recipe.  Cook all ingredients in a small heavy saucepan over low heat, whisking until smooth. Cool 5 minutes and then pour warm glaze over cake. The ingredients of Sweet Chocolate Glaze are 3 ounces semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, chopped, 3 tablespoons orange juice or water, 3 tablespoons unsalted margarine or (if not keeping glaze pareve) butter, 3 tablespoons sugar.

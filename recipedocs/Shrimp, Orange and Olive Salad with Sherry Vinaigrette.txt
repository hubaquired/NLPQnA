Shrimp, Orange and Olive Salad with Sherry Vinaigrette recipe.  Whisk first 5 ingredients in large bowl for dressing. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Cut peel and white pith from oranges. Quarter oranges lengthwise, than slice crosswise. Add oranges, shrimp and olives to dressing. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Add lettuces and green onions to dressing mixture and toss well. Shrimp, Orange and Olive Salad with Sherry Vinaigrette has 286.0 calories. Shrimp, Orange and Olive Salad with Sherry Vinaigrette has 19.0 grams of protein. Shrimp, Orange and Olive Salad with Sherry Vinaigrette has 18.0 grams of fat. Shrimp, Orange and Olive Salad with Sherry Vinaigrette has 1042.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp, Orange and Olive Salad with Sherry Vinaigrette are 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil, 3 tablespoons Sherry wine vinegar or red wine vinegar, 1 garlic clove, pressed, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 teaspoon grated orange peel, 2 oranges, 1 pound medium-size cooked shrimp, peeled, deveined, 1 cup sliced green olives, 8 cups mixed baby lettuces, 2 green onions, minced.

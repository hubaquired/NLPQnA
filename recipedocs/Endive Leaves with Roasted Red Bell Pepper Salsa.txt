Endive Leaves with Roasted Red Bell Pepper Salsa recipe. Char peppers over gas flame or in broiler until blackened on all sides. Enclose in paper bag; let stand 10 minutes. Peel, seed and chop peppers. Mix fennel, olives, oil, vinegar, rosemary, garlic and peppers in medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill.) Arrange endive leaves on platter. Spoon 1 tablespoon salsa onto each endive leaf and serve. The ingredients of Endive Leaves with Roasted Red Bell Pepper Salsa are 2 large red bell peppers, 1/2 cup minced fresh fennel bulb, 2 tablespoons chopped brine-cured black olives (such as Kalamata), 1 tablespoon olive oil (preferably extra-virgin), 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar, 3/4 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary or 1/2 teaspoon dried, 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 24 Belgian endive leaves (from about 3 large heads), ends trimmed.In this colorful salsa, fennel, olives and rosemary combine with roasted bell peppers.

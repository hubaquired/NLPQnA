Broccolini with Italian Herb Oil recipe. Cook Broccolini in a 5-to 6-quarts pot of boiling salted water 2 teaspoons salt for 4 quarts water) until just tender, about 6 minutes, then drain. Meanwhile, blend herbs, garlic, anchovy paste, 1/8 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper in a blender until finely chopped. With motor running, add oil in a slow stream, blending until mixture is green and herbs are very finely chopped. Serve Broccolini tossed with herb oil. The ingredients of Broccolini with Italian Herb Oil are 1 1/2 pounds Broccolini, tough ends trimmed, 1/3 cup coarsely chopped flat-leaf parsley, 1 to 2 tablespoon coarsely chopped oregano, 1 small garlic clove, smashed, 1 teaspoon anchovy paste, 1/3 cup olive oil, Accompaniment: lemon wedges.A generous drizzle of parsley-oregano oil brings a wave of herbal freshness to slender stalks of simply cooked Broccolini.

Baby Lima Bean Soup with Mustard Greens, Peas, and Onions recipe.  Purée 1 cup lima beans with water in a blender until smooth, about 1 minute. Cook onion and garlic in butter in a 3-quart heavy saucepan over moderately low heat, stirring, until softened, about 6 minutes. Add purée, remaining lima beans, salt, and pepper and simmer, covered, stirring occasionally, 3 minutes. Stir in peas and mustard greens and simmer, covered, 3 minutes. Stir in chives and season with salt and pepper. Baby Lima Bean Soup with Mustard Greens, Peas, and Onions has 255.0 calories. Baby Lima Bean Soup with Mustard Greens, Peas, and Onions has 11.0 grams of protein. Baby Lima Bean Soup with Mustard Greens, Peas, and Onions has 10.0 grams of fat. Baby Lima Bean Soup with Mustard Greens, Peas, and Onions has 579.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baby Lima Bean Soup with Mustard Greens, Peas, and Onions are 1 (1-pound) bag frozen baby lima beans, thawed (3 cups), 2 1/2 cups water, 1 medium onion, finely chopped, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1 cup frozen baby peas, 2 cups coarsely chopped trimmed mustard greens, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives.

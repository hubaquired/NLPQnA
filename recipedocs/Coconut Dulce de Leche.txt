Coconut Dulce de Leche recipe. Whisk coconut milk, sugar, and coarse salt in heavy large skillet over medium heat until sugar dissolves. Increase heat to medium-high and boil until mixture is reduced to 2 1/2 cups, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes. Transfer sauce to small jars. Cool completely, then cover sauce and chill. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 month ahead. Keep chilled. Coconut Dulce de Leche has 239.0 calories. Coconut Dulce de Leche has 2.0 grams of protein. Coconut Dulce de Leche has 17.0 grams of fat. Coconut Dulce de Leche has 112.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coconut Dulce de Leche are 2 14-ounce cans unsweetened coconut milk, 1 1/2 cups (packed) golden brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon coarse kosher salt.This clever caramel sauce is made from coconut milk instead of the usual condensed milk.

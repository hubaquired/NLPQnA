Party Barbecue Sauce recipe.  Combine all the ingredients in a heavy saucepan. Bring to a slow boil; cook until thickened 25-30 minutes), stirring constantly to prevent the sauce from scorching. Strain into a bowl to remove the onion and garlic. Cool, then refrigerate for 2 days. The ingredients of Party Barbecue Sauce are 2 cups ketchup, 1/2 cup minced red onion, 1 quart water, 1 cup cider vinegar, 2 tablespoons paprika, 1 tablespoon honey, 1/4 cup each dark-brown sugar, chili powder, fresh lemon juice and Worcestershire sauce, 3 cloves of garlic, very finely minced.This recipe is an accompaniment for Annabel's Baby Back Ribs.

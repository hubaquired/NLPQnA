Sambal-Orange Vinaigrette recipe.  In a small bowl, whisk the sambal with the orange juice and rice vinegar. Gradually whisk in the oil and season with salt. The sambal-vinaigrette can be refrigerated for up to 2 days. Whisk before serving to re-emulsify. Sambal-Orange Vinaigrette has 504.0 calories. Sambal-Orange Vinaigrette has 55.0 grams of fat. Sambal-Orange Vinaigrette has 482.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sambal-Orange Vinaigrette are 1 tablespoon sambal oelek chili paste, 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice, 2 tablespoons rice vinegar, 1/4 cup grapeseed or other neutral oil, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt.

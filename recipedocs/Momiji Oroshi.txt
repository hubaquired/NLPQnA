Momiji Oroshi recipe. Pureé daikon in blender with 2 tablespoons water. Strain the mixture and drain 5 minutes. Transfer daikon to small bowl. Whisk in cayenne and paprika. Momiji Oroshi has 12.0 calories. Momiji Oroshi has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Momiji Oroshi are 3-ounce piece daikon, cut a into small pieces, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1 teaspoon paprika.This sauce adds a nice kick to Shabu-Shabu. Use it as you would wasabi in sushi restaurants.  Bon Appétit.

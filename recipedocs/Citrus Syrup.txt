Citrus Syrup recipe.  Combine the juices and sugar in a small saucepan and boil for 2 to 3 minutes over low heat. Remove from the heat and let cool. Pour the cooled syrup over the fruit or store for up to 1 month in the refrigerator. Citrus Syrup has 70.0 calories. Citrus Syrup has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Citrus Syrup are 1/4 cup orange juice, 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice, 2 tablespoons sugar. Sauce Vegetarian Quick & Easy Lime Juice Orange Juice.

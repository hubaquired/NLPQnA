Shoe String Potatoes (Pommes Pailles) recipe.  Heat 2 inches oil to 375°F in a wide 5- to 7-quart heavy pot at least 4 inches deep) over medium heat. Meanwhile, peel potatoes and cut lengthwise with slicer to make 1/8-inch-thick julienne strips. Fry potatoes in 5 or 6 small batches, stirring, until golden brown, about 2 minutes per batch.

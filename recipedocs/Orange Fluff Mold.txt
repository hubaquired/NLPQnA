Orange Fluff Mold recipe.  Mix gelatin and Jell-O with cottage cheese. Fold in Cool Whip; add oranges and pineapple. Pour into mold and refrigerate. The ingredients of Orange Fluff Mold are 1 large Cool Whip, 1 pound cottage cheese, 1 6-ounce package orange Jell-O, 1 package Knox gelatine, 1 cup mandarin oranges, drained, 1 large cup crushed pineapple, drained. Dairy Fruit Dessert No-Cook Orange Pineapple Chill Cottage Cheese.

Limonada recipe. In a pitcher, stir the lime juice with the sugar and 1 1/2 cups water until the sugar has dissolved. Cover and refrigerate until chilled. Fresh is best, but I think Limonada still tastes good after a couple of days in the refrigerator. Limonada has 224.0 calories. Limonada has 1.0 grams of protein. Limonada has 10.0 grams of sodium.

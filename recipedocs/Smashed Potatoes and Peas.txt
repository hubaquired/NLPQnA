Smashed Potatoes and Peas recipe.  Generously cover potatoes with cold water in a small saucepan. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt. Simmer, covered, until just beginning to get tender, about 8 minutes. Add peas and simmer, partially covered, until potatoes are tender, about 5 minutes. Drain, then return to pan. Add butter, milk, and 1/8 teaspoon each of salt and pepper. Let stand off heat 1 minute, then add scallion. Coarsely mash. Smashed Potatoes and Peas has 309.0 calories. Smashed Potatoes and Peas has 8.0 grams of protein. Smashed Potatoes and Peas has 14.0 grams of fat. Smashed Potatoes and Peas has 89.0 grams of sodium.

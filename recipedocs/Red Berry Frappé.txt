Red Berry Frappé recipe.  Place all the ingredients except garnish in a blender and blend until smooth. Serve in tall glasses with straws; garnish with a berry on the rim of each glass. The ingredients of Red Berry Frappé are 8 ounces strawberries, hulled and sliced, 4 ounces fresh raspberries, 2 scoops vanilla ice cream, 2 cups milk, 4 whole strawberries, for garnish.

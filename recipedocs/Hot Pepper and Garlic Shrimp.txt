Hot Pepper and Garlic Shrimp recipe. Pat shrimp dry. Cook garlic, red pepper flakes, and sea salt in oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderately low heat, stirring occasionally, until garlic is pale golden, 4 to 5 minutes. Increase heat to moderately high, then add shrimp and sauté, turning occasionally, until shrimp are just cooked through, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice, then transfer to a serving bowl. Serve warm or at room temperature. Hot Pepper and Garlic Shrimp has 112.0 calories. Hot Pepper and Garlic Shrimp has 11.0 grams of protein. Hot Pepper and Garlic Shrimp has 7.0 grams of fat. Hot Pepper and Garlic Shrimp has 441.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Pepper and Garlic Shrimp are 2 pounds large shrimp in shell (21 to 25 per pound), peeled, leaving tail and first segment of shell intact, and deveined, 10 large garlic cloves, thinly sliced, 1/4 teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes, 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, Accompaniment: lemon wedges.

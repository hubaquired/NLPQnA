Green Beans Braised with Tomatoes and Basil recipe.  Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until onion softens slightly, about 5 minutes. Add green beans, tomatoes, basil leaves, and 1/2 cup water. Cook until beans are crisp-tender, stirring and tossing occasionally, about 10 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl and serve. Green Beans Braised with Tomatoes and Basil has 110.0 calories. Green Beans Braised with Tomatoes and Basil has 3.0 grams of protein. Green Beans Braised with Tomatoes and Basil has 7.0 grams of fat. Green Beans Braised with Tomatoes and Basil has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Beans Braised with Tomatoes and Basil are 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 cup finely chopped white onion, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/2 pounds green beans, trimmed, 2 large plum tomatoes, finely chopped (about 1 cup), 1 cup (packed) fresh basil leaves, 1/2 cup water.Fagiolini in Umido.

Grilled Zucchini Bread with Mascarpone and Berries recipe. Light a grill or preheat a grill pan and oil the grates. Lightly brush both sides of the zucchini bread slices with oil, sprinkle with sugar, and grill over moderately high heat, turning once, until dark brown grill marks form on both sides, about 2 minutes per side. Transfer the zucchini bread to plates and top with a dollop or two of mascarpone. Spoon the berries on top and serve. Grilled Zucchini Bread with Mascarpone and Berries has 172.0 calories. The ingredients of Grilled Zucchini Bread with Mascarpone and Berries are Vegetable oil, for grilling, One 9-inch storebought or homemade zucchini bread, cut into 8 slices, Sugar, for sprinkling, 1 cup mascarpone cheese, 1/2 pint raspberries, 1/2 pint blueberries.Zucchini bread is one of those things that is almost always good but rarely spectacular and a slice can serve as a breakfast, snack or dessert. Sprinkling it with sugar and grilling it so it becomes nicely caramelized moves it firmly in the dessert category (and, along with adding a spoonful of mascarpone, makes it much closer to spectacular).

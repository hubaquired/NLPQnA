Mixed Green Salad with Mango, Jícama and Cashews recipe. Combine 1/2 cup mango with next 6 ingredients in blender. Puree until smooth. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Transfer to small bowl. Can be made 3 hours ahead. Cover; let stand at room temperature.) Combine lettuces, jicama and remaining 1 cup mango in large bowl. Toss with enough dressing to coat. Sprinkle with cashews. Serve, passing any remaining dressing separately. Mixed Green Salad with Mango, Jícama and Cashews has 192.0 calories. Mixed Green Salad with Mango, Jícama and Cashews has 3.0 grams of protein. Mixed Green Salad with Mango, Jícama and Cashews has 15.0 grams of fat. Mixed Green Salad with Mango, Jícama and Cashews has 88.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mixed Green Salad with Mango, Jícama and Cashews are 1 large ripe mango, peeled, pitted, cut into ¤-inch pieces (about 1 1/2 cups), 1/4 cup olive oil, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 3 tablespoons rice vinegar or 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1 10-ounce bag mixed baby lettuces, 1 cup diced peeled jicama, 1/2 cup salted roasted cashews.The dressing gets its beautiful color and smooth texture from pureed mango.

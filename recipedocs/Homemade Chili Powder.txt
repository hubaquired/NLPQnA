Homemade Chili Powder recipe.  Combine all of the ingredients in a small mixing bowl and stir until thoroughly blended. Store the chili powder in an airtight container. The ingredients of Homemade Chili Powder are 1 cup ancho powder, 1 teaspoon dried Mexican oregano, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin.When you combine powdered chiles with cumin and other ingredients to make a spice mix for chili con carne, you are turning chile powder into chili powder.

Pomegranate Mimosas recipe.  Combine juices and liqueur in a large pitcher. Slowly add sparkling wine and stir. Pomegranate Mimosas has 182.0 calories. Pomegranate Mimosas has 12.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pomegranate Mimosas are 2 cups chilled pomegranate juice, 1 cup chilled fresh orange juice, 1/2 cup orange-flavored liqueur (preferably Cointreau), 1 (750-ml) bottle well-chilled Cava or demi-sec Champagne.This holiday twist on the traditional brunch cocktail sparkles ruby red with pomegranate juice, its tart intensity offset by a hint of sweetness from fresh orange juice and Cointreau.

Glazed Smoked Duck with Candied Kumquats recipe.  Combine first 4 ingredients in saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to boil; add kumquats. Simmer until kumquats are tender and liquid is syrupy, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in Grand Marnier. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 week ahead. Cool, cover, and chill. Rewarm over low heat before continuing.) Strain glaze into small bowl. Discard cinnamon and bay leaf; reserve candied kumquats and glaze separately. Divide arugula among plates. Top each with 5 duck slices; brush with glaze. Place 1 kumquat quarter on each duck slice. Glazed Smoked Duck with Candied Kumquats has 261.0 calories. The ingredients of Glazed Smoked Duck with Candied Kumquats are 1 cup dry white wine, 1 cup apricot jam, 1 bay leaf, 2 cinnamon sticks, broken in half, 1 1/3 cups kumquats, stemmed, quartered, seeded, 2 teaspoons Grand Marnier, 3 cups arugula, 2 6-ounce smoked duck breasts, each cut into 15 thin slices.

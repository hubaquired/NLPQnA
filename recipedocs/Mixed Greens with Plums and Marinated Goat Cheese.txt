Mixed Greens with Plums and Marinated Goat Cheese recipe.  Whisk oil and 2 tablespoons basil in shallow bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Add cheese; spoon basil oil over. Cover; let stand at room temperature 2 hours. Remove cheese from basil oil. Reserve oil. Toss greens and 1/4 cup basil in large bowl. Add reserved basil oil and vinegar; toss. Season with salt and pepper. Divide among 6 plates. Top each salad with 1 cheese round and plum wedges. Mixed Greens with Plums and Marinated Goat Cheese has 259.0 calories. Mixed Greens with Plums and Marinated Goat Cheese has 7.0 grams of protein. Mixed Greens with Plums and Marinated Goat Cheese has 25.0 grams of fat. Mixed Greens with Plums and Marinated Goat Cheese has 163.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mixed Greens with Plums and Marinated Goat Cheese are 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons plus 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil, 1 7-ounce log soft fresh goat cheese, cut into 6 rounds, 8 ounces mixed baby greens, 2 1/2 tablespoons rice vinegar, 3 large ripe plums, pitted, each cut into 16 wedges.Marinate the cheese for a couple of hours, then assemble the salad just before serving.

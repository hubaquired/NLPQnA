The ingredients of Chopped Honey-Mustard Slaw are 1 2-pound head of green cabbage, quartered, cored, thinly sliced lengthwise, then chopped crosswise (about 16 cups), 1 tablespoon coarse kosher salt, 3/4 cup mayonnaise, 2 1/2 tablespoons honey, 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 1 1/2 teaspoons celery seeds, 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel, 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce.

Farmhouse Butternut Squash Soup recipe.  Cook bacon in a 4-to 6-quart heavy pot over medium heat until crisp. Transfer bacon to paper towels to drain. Add garlic and caraway seeds to fat in pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until garlic is pale golden, about 1 minute. Add squash, carrots, apple, thyme, bay leaves, broth, water, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper and boil, uncovered, until vegetables are tender, 15 to 20 minutes. The ingredients of Farmhouse Butternut Squash Soup are 4 bacon slices, 4 large garlic cloves, chopped, 1 teaspoon caraway seeds, 2 pounds butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and chopped, 1/2 pounds carrots, chopped, 1 Granny Smith apple, peeled, cored, and chopped, 3 thyme sprigs, 2 Turkish bay leaves or 1 California, 3 1/2 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth, 2 cups water, 1 to 1 1/2 teaspoons cider vinegar.Green apple and a dash of cider vinegar provide just the right amount of tart balance in this slightly sweet, down-home soup topped with homemade bacon bits.

Big Green Salad recipe. Tear romaine into bite-size pieces and put in a salad bowl. Halve cucumber lengthwise, then thinly slice diagonally and add to romaine along with celery. Whisk together remaining ingredients with 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a small bowl until emulsified. Add to salad and toss to coat. Big Green Salad has 106.0 calories. Big Green Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Big Green Salad has 7.0 grams of fat. Big Green Salad has 37.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Big Green Salad are 2 medium heads romaine, 1/2 seedless cucumber, 2 celery ribs, thinly sliced, 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon mild honey, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard.Something green and crisp is in order, and this simple salad is just the thing. A tangy from-scratch honey-mustard dressing will make salad eaters out of even the youngest at the table.

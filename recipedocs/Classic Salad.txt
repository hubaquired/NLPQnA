Classic Salad recipe.  Using a wooden spoon, mash garlic, anchovy, and a pinch each of salt and pepper in a large bowl. Stir in vinegar, then 1/4 cup oil. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and more oil, if desired. Add lettuce leaves and chives just before serving and toss to coat. Classic Salad has 24.0 calories. Classic Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Classic Salad has 1.0 grams of fat. Classic Salad has 56.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Classic Salad are 1 small garlic clove, chopped, 1 anchovy fillet packed in oil, drained, chopped, Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, 1 1/2 tablespoons white wine vinegar, 1/4 cup (or more) extra-virgin olive oil, 1 9-ounce head of freshest-possible lettuce, leaves separated, torn if large (about 6 cups), 1/2 cup minced chives.We use fresh lemon juice for this vinaigrette as often as we do vinegar.

Chilled Avocado Soup recipe. Scrape avocados from skin into processor. Add buttermilk and yogurt; purée until smooth. Mix in lime juice, jalepeño and chili powder. With machine running, blend in 1/2 cup broth. Season with salt and pepper. Chill soup until cold. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and keep chilled.) Thin soup with more broth, if desired. Spoon into bowls. Sprinkle with onion. Chilled Avocado Soup has 219.0 calories. Chilled Avocado Soup has 6.0 grams of protein. Chilled Avocado Soup has 16.0 grams of fat. Chilled Avocado Soup has 129.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chilled Avocado Soup are 2 ripe medium avocados, halved, pitted, 3/4 cup buttermilk, 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 1/2 tablespoons chopped seeded jalepeño chili, 1/2 teaspoon chili powder, 1/2 cup (about) canned low-salt chicken broth, Chopped red onion.Chili powder and chopped jalapeño add a touch of heat to this smooth appetizer. Try sprinkling it with chopped red onion for some crunch and color.  Soup Stew Vegetable No-Cook Yogurt Lime Avocado Summer Chill Jalapeño Bon Appétit.

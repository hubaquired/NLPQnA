Shanghai recipe.  Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass. The ingredients of Shanghai are 1 1/2 ounces dark rum, 1/2 ounce anisette, 3/4 ounce lemon juice, 2 dashes grenadine, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

Classic Daiquiri recipe. In cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine rum, lime juice, and simple syrup. Shake vigorously, then strain into cocktail glass. Classic Daiquiri has 177.0 calories. Classic Daiquiri has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Classic Daiquiri are 2 ounces light rum, 1 ounce plus one teaspoon fresh lime juice, 1/2 ounce simple syrup.Nothing like the ubiquitous frozen drinks that bear the same name, this classic cocktail is light, refreshing, and the perfect easy cocktail to while away the summer hours.

Candied Walnuts recipe.  Preheat oven to 325°F. Spray baking sheet with nonstick spray. Combine walnuts and all remaining ingredients in medium bowl; toss to coat. Spread nut mixture on prepared baking sheet some nuts may clump together). Bake until nuts are deep golden and sugar mixture is bubbling, stirring occasionally to break up clumps, about 15 minutes. Cool completely on baking sheet. Can be made 3 days ahead. Store in airtight container.) Candied Walnuts has 211.0 calories. Candied Walnuts has 4.0 grams of protein. Candied Walnuts has 17.0 grams of fat. Candied Walnuts has 204.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Candied Walnuts are Nonstick vegetable oil spray, 1 cup walnuts (about 3 1/2 ounces), 2 tablespoons light corn syrup, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, Generous pinch of cayenne pepper.

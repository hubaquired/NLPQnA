Radish and Mushroom Salad with Parsley recipe.  In a bowl whisk together oil, sour cream, lemon juice, parsley, and salt and pepper to taste until smooth. Add radishes and mushrooms and toss gently to coat with dressing. Nestle 1 lettuce leaf inside another, rib sides down, and arrange on plate to form a cup. Repeat with remaining lettuce and divide salad between lettuce cups. Radish and Mushroom Salad with Parsley has 244.0 calories. Radish and Mushroom Salad with Parsley has 7.0 grams of protein. Radish and Mushroom Salad with Parsley has 21.0 grams of fat. Radish and Mushroom Salad with Parsley has 50.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Radish and Mushroom Salad with Parsley are 2 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 2 tablespoons sour cream, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley leaves, 10 radishes, sliced thin, 6 medium white mushrooms, sliced thin, 4 Boston lettuce leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

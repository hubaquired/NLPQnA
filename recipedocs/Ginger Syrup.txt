Ginger Syrup recipe.  Thoroughly blend 1 cup water and ginger in blender. Bring remaining 2 cups water to boil. Add ginger-water mixture, lime juice, and lime peel. Remove from heat; cover and let steep 1 hour. Add sugar; stir to dissolve. Strain syrup into bowl. Chill until cold. Ginger Syrup has 237.0 calories. Ginger Syrup has 1.0 grams of protein. Ginger Syrup has 34.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger Syrup are 3 cups water, divided, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped peeled fresh ginger, 1/3 cup fresh lime juice, 1 tablespoon finely grated lime peel, 3/4 cup golden brown sugar.

Swordfish Kebabs recipe. Whisk first 6 ingredients in medium bowl to blend. Add fish and refrigerate at least 1 and up to 4 hours. Preheat barbecue medium heat) or preheat broiler. Drain fish, reserving marinade. Thread fish on skewers alternately with onions, green and bell peppers and mushrooms. Arrange skewers on grill. Grill until swordfish is just cooked through, brushing skewers frequently with marinade, about 5 minutes. Swordfish Kebabs has 515.0 calories. Swordfish Kebabs has 34.0 grams of protein. Swordfish Kebabs has 29.0 grams of fat. Swordfish Kebabs has 361.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Swordfish Kebabs are 1/2 cup dry white wine, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup lemon juice, 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, 1 pound swordfish, cut into 1-inch cubes, 12 bamboo skewers, 6 small boiling onions, peeled, 1 green bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces, 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces, 12 mushrooms.

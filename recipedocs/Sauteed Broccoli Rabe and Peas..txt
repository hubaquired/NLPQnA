Sauteed Broccoli Rabe and Peas. recipe. Trim stem ends from broccoli rabe and discard. Cut remainder crosswise into 1 1/2-inch pieces. Cook broccoli rabe and peas in a 4- to 6-quart pot of boiling salted water until broccoli rabe is wilted and stems are crisp-tender, 2 to 3 minutes. Drain well in a colander. Heat oil with garlic in a 12-inch heavy skillet over moderately high heat, turning garlic frequently, until garlic is golden, 1 to 2 minutes, then discard garlic. Add broccoli rabe, peas, salt, and pepper to skillet and sauté, stirring, until well coated with garlic oil, about 2 minutes. Sauteed Broccoli Rabe and Peas. has 202.0 calories. The ingredients of Sauteed Broccoli Rabe and Peas. are 1 (3/4- to 1-lb) bunch broccoli rabe, 10 oz frozen peas (about 2 cups), 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 3 large garlic cloves, peeled and lightly smashed with side of a large knife, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper.In this vibrant green dish, the bitterness of broccoli rabe comes in second place to the sweetness of the peas and the comforting familiarity of olive oil and garlic.

Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce recipe.  Char peppers over flame or in broiler until blackened on all sides. Enclose in paper bag. Let stand 10 minutes. Peel, seed and coarsely chop peppers. Transfer to processor. Add pine nuts, Parmesan, olive oil and garlic and process until peppers are finely chopped. Transfer mixture to large bowl. Stir in whipping cream. Season with salt and pepper. Sauce can be prepared 6 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce has 834.0 calories. Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce has 19.0 grams of protein. Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce has 77.0 grams of fat. Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce has 421.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce are 3 large red bell peppers, 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 tablespoon chopped garlic, 1 cup whipping cream.This recipe originally accompanied Scallops and Spinach Fettucine with Red Bell Pepper Cream Sauce.

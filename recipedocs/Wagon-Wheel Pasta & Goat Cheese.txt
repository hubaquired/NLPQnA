Wagon-Wheel Pasta & Goat Cheese recipe. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, then add the pasta. After 5 minutes, add the lima beans. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until browned, about 7 minutes. When the pasta is ready, drain it with the beans reserving 1/2 cup of the liquid), then return the pasta and beans to the pot along with the onion, crème fraîche, thyme if using), salt, pepper, goat cheese, and the reserved water. Toss together and serve. Wagon-Wheel Pasta & Goat Cheese has 422.0 calories. Wagon-Wheel Pasta & Goat Cheese has 18.0 grams of protein. Wagon-Wheel Pasta & Goat Cheese has 12.0 grams of fat. Wagon-Wheel Pasta & Goat Cheese has 382.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Wagon-Wheel Pasta & Goat Cheese are 1/2 pound wagon-wheel (or any short) pasta, 1 10-ounce box frozen lima beans, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 large onion, sliced thinly, 2 tablespoons crème fraîche (or sour cream), 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh thyme (optional), 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, 4 ounces creamy aged goat cheese, cut into small chunks.

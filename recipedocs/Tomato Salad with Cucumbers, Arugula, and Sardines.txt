Tomato Salad with Cucumbers, Arugula, and Sardines recipe. Combine cucumber, tomatoes, onion, parsley, arugula, mint, and all but 2 pieces sardines in large bowl; toss to combine. Place reserved sardines in medium bowl; mash with fork until smooth. Whisk in oil and lemon juice. Season dressing with salt and pepper; mix into salad. Transfer to serving bowl; sprinkle with lemon peel. Tomato Salad with Cucumbers, Arugula, and Sardines has 258.0 calories. Tomato Salad with Cucumbers, Arugula, and Sardines has 8.0 grams of protein. Tomato Salad with Cucumbers, Arugula, and Sardines has 21.0 grams of fat. Tomato Salad with Cucumbers, Arugula, and Sardines has 89.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato Salad with Cucumbers, Arugula, and Sardines are 1 cucumber, quartered lengthwise, thinly sliced crosswise, 1 1/4 pounds tomatoes, seeded, diced, 1/2 medium onion, cut into thin rings, 1/2 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped arugula, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, 8 oil-packed sardines, drained, diced, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel.Serve with crusty bread to sop up the juices.

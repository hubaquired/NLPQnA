Bacon-Wrapped Corn on the Cob recipe. Prepare a grill for low direct heat. Peel back the cornhusks but do not remove them. Pull out and discard all the silk. Working from top to bottom, wrap a strip of bacon in a spiral around each ear of corn. Season with pepper. Pull the husk back over the corn. Wrap a piece of foil tightly around the top 1 1/2 inches of the ear of corn. Or use a piece of kitchen twine soaked in water to tie the tops of the cornhusks closed.) Grill the corn about 10 minutes or until the husks are brown all over, turning occasionally. Let corn cool for 5 minutes before serving. Bacon-Wrapped Corn on the Cob has 184.0 calories. Bacon-Wrapped Corn on the Cob has 6.0 grams of protein. Bacon-Wrapped Corn on the Cob has 12.0 grams of fat. Bacon-Wrapped Corn on the Cob has 203.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bacon-Wrapped Corn on the Cob are 4 ears corn, unhusked, 4 slices bacon, Freshly ground black pepper.We have found that bacon gives the corn a nice smoky flavor, but it doesn't get brown and crispy.

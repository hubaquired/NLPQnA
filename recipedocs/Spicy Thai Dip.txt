Spicy Thai Dip recipe. Mix chiles, garlic, dried shrimp, and sugar with the water in a blender and gradually add the fish sauce and lime juice. Spicy Thai Dip has 20.0 calories. Spicy Thai Dip has 1.0 grams of protein. Spicy Thai Dip has 496.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Thai Dip are 4 hot red chiles (about 2 inches long), chopped, 4 garlic cloves, chopped, 2 tablespoons dried shrimp, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon water, 2 tablespoons Thai fish sauce, 3 to 4 tablespoons lime juice.Nam Prik.

Veal Scallops with Parsley, Lemon and Garlic recipe. Combine minced parsley, grated lemon peel and garlic in small bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over high heat. Season veal with salt and pepper. Add veal to skillet; sauté until golden brown and just cooked through, about 1 minute per side. Transfer veal to large plate. Add vermouth to skillet; boil 1 minute, scraping up browned bits. Add half of parsley mixture and any accumulated juices from plate and boil 1 minute longer . Spoon sauce over veal. Sprinkle remaining parsley mixture over and serve. Veal Scallops with Parsley, Lemon and Garlic has 193.0 calories. Veal Scallops with Parsley, Lemon and Garlic has 14.0 grams of protein. Veal Scallops with Parsley, Lemon and Garlic has 9.0 grams of fat. Veal Scallops with Parsley, Lemon and Garlic has 451.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Veal Scallops with Parsley, Lemon and Garlic are 3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 1 large garlic clove, minced, 1 1/2 tablespoons butter, 1/2 pound veal scallops (about 2 large), 1/3 cup dry vermouth.Serve this sophisticated entrée on a bed of spinach linguine that has been tossed with sautéed strips of red and yellow bell peppers.

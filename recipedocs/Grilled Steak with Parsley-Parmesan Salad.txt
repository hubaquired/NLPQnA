Grilled Steak with Parsley-Parmesan Salad recipe. Rub steak with 2 tablespoons oil; season generously with salt and pepper. Let sit at room temperature 30 minutes. Prepare a grill for medium-high heat; oil grill grate. Grill steak to desired doneness, 5–7 minutes per side for medium-rare. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing against the grain. Meanwhile, toss parsley, Parmesan, lemon juice, and remaining 1 tablespoon oil in a medium bowl; season with salt, pepper, and more lemon juice, if desired. Serve steak topped with parsley-Parmesan salad. Grilled Steak with Parsley-Parmesan Salad has 335.0 calories. Grilled Steak with Parsley-Parmesan Salad has 41.0 grams of protein. Grilled Steak with Parsley-Parmesan Salad has 18.0 grams of fat. Grilled Steak with Parsley-Parmesan Salad has 372.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Steak with Parsley-Parmesan Salad are 1 1/2 pounds flatiron steak, 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided, plus more, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 2 cups fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, 2 ounces Parmesan, shaved, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice.The rich and beefy flatiron is also known as a top blade steak, but you can use any cut.

Sangria II recipe.  In a bowl, combine wine, cognac, Triple Sec and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Stir in the orange and lemon slices and chill for 1 hour.Transfer to a punch bowl, stir in seltzer, and add a block of ice or ice cubes. Sangria II has 430.0 calories. Sangria II has 1.0 grams of protein. Sangria II has 29.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sangria II are 2 bottles chilled dry red wine, 1 cup cognac, 1/2 cup Triple Sec or Cointreau, 1/3 cup superfine granulated sugar, or to taste, 1 orange, sliced thin, 1 lemon, sliced thin, 2 cups chilled seltzer or club soda.

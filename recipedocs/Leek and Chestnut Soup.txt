Leek and Chestnut Soup recipe.  Clean and peel the chestnuts. Melt the butter in a pot and saut√© the chestnuts, leeks, and potatoes. Pour in the beer, let it evaporate, add broth to cover by 1 inch, and continue cooking. Pass the mixture through a food mill, reheat, and whisk in the cream and egg yolks. Heat, stirring until thickened, but do not let the mixture boil. Serve with bread. The ingredients of Leek and Chestnut Soup are 1/2 lb. fresh chestnuts, 3 tbsp. butter, 1 lb. leeks, sliced, 1 lb. potatoes, peeled and sliced, 1 cup beer, Beef broth, 1 1/4 cup heavy cream, 2 large egg yolks, Bread slices.Minestra di porri e castagne.

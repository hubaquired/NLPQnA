Garlic Bruschetta recipe.  On a baking sheet toast the bread slices in batches under a preheated broiler about 3 inches from the heat for about 1 minute on each side, or until they are golden, transferring them as they are toasted to a work surface. Rub one side of each toast slice with the cut side of a garlic clove, brush it with some of the oil, and season it with salt. Garlic Bruschetta has 201.0 calories. Garlic Bruschetta has 4.0 grams of protein. Garlic Bruschetta has 11.0 grams of fat. Garlic Bruschetta has 210.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Garlic Bruschetta are eight 3/4-inch-thick slices of crusty peasant bread (preferably from a large round loaf), 3 garlic cloves, halved crosswise, 6 tablespoons olive oil (preferably extra-virgin).

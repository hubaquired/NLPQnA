Feta and Mint Rice recipe. Heat oil in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add rice and stir 1 minute. Add broth. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook until broth is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Fluff rice with fork. Add feta and mint and mix in with fork. Season with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Feta and Mint Rice are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 small onion, chopped, 1 cup long-grain rice, 1 14 1/2-ounce can chicken broth, 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese, 3 tablespoons minced fresh mint, Salt and freshly ground pepper.An accompaniment to Greek-Style Shrimp.

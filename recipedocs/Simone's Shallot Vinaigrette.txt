Simone's Shallot Vinaigrette recipe.  Combine 1 finely chopped shallot, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, and 1 tablespoon unseasoned rice vinegar in a jar; season with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper. Let sit 20 minutes. Add 1/3 cup olive oil and cover. Shake to combine. DO AHEAD: can be made 1 week ahead and kept refrigerated. Bring to room temperature and shake before using. The ingredients of Simone's Shallot Vinaigrette are 1 finely chopped shallot, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon unseasoned rice vinegar, kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper, 1/3 cup olive oil.

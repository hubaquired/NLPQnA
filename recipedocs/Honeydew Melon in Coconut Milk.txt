Honeydew Melon in Coconut Milk recipe.  Stir together coconut milk, sugar, and lime juice in a small metal bowl until sugar is dissolved, then quick-chill in a metal bowl set in a larger bowl of ice and cold water, stirring occasionally, about 6 minutes. Halve melon and discard seeds. Scoop melon into balls with cutter. Divide melon among 8 serving dishes, then pour coconut milk over top. Serve immediately. Honeydew Melon in Coconut Milk has 215.0 calories. Honeydew Melon in Coconut Milk has 3.0 grams of protein. Honeydew Melon in Coconut Milk has 11.0 grams of fat. Honeydew Melon in Coconut Milk has 57.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honeydew Melon in Coconut Milk are 1 (13- to 14-ounce) can well-stirred unsweetened coconut milk, 3 tablespoons sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lime juice, or to taste, 1 large honeydew melon (5 pounds), chilled, Garnish: julienned lime zest, Special equipment: a melon-ball cutter.

Paprika Rice recipe. In a kettle bring the water to a boil, sprinkle in the rice and salt to taste, stirring until the water returns to a boil, and boil the rice for 10 minutes. Drain the rice in a large colander and rinse it. Set the colander over a large saucepan of boiling water and steam the rice, covered with a kitchen towel and the lid, for 15 minutes, or until it is fluffy and dry. The rice may be made 2 days in advance and kept chilled in an airtight container. Reheat the rice in the colander set over a saucepan of boiling water. Transfer the rice to a large bowl, sprinkle it with the paprika, and toss the mixture to coat the rice thoroughly. Paprika Rice has 213.0 calories. Paprika Rice has 4.0 grams of protein. Paprika Rice has 27.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Paprika Rice are 5 quarts water, 2 1/2 cups long-grain rice, 2 teaspoons paprika.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Sauteed Fennel, Capers and Arugula recipe.  Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add fennel, leek, bell pepper, capers and olives. Sauté until fennel is crisp-tender, about 3 minutes. Add arugula and sauté 1 minute. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Sauteed Fennel, Capers and Arugula has 48.0 calories. Sauteed Fennel, Capers and Arugula has 2.0 grams of protein. Sauteed Fennel, Capers and Arugula has 1.0 grams of fat. Sauteed Fennel, Capers and Arugula has 203.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sauteed Fennel, Capers and Arugula are 1 teaspoon olive oil, 1 1-pound fennel bulb, thinly sliced (about 3 cups), 1 cup thinly sliced leek (white and pale green parts only), 1/2 cup finely chopped orange bell pepper, 1/4 cup drained capers, 8 brine-cured black olives (such as Kalamata), pitted, thinly sliced, 1 1/4 cups finely chopped fresh arugula leaves.A lovely side dish with big flavors‑and only a single teaspoon of oil.

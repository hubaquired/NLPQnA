Four Spirits Eggnog recipe.  Beat the egg yolks until they are thick and pale. Add Cognac, dark rum, peach brandy, and applejack. Stir in confectioners' sugar and blend well. Add milk and heavy cream and refrigerate the eggnog for at least 5 hours. Before serving, fold in egg whites. Four Spirits Eggnog has 448.0 calories. Four Spirits Eggnog has 5.0 grams of protein. Four Spirits Eggnog has 9.0 grams of fat. Four Spirits Eggnog has 66.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Four Spirits Eggnog are 18 egg yolks, 1 fifth Cognac, 1 pint dark rum, 3/4 pint peach brandy, 1/2 pint applejack, 1 1/2 pounds confectioners' sugar, 3 quarts milk, 2 cups heavy cream, 18 egg whites, lightly beaten.

Nutty Brown Rice recipe. Bring water to a boil in a heavy medium saucepan. Add rice and 1/4 teaspoon salt, then reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, until rice is tender, about 40 minutes. Drain in a sieve. Heat butter in a large heavy skillet over medium heat until foam subsides. Add nuts and cook, stirring, until butter and nuts are golden brown, about 4 minutes. Add rice and nutmeg to skillet and toss to coat. Nutty Brown Rice has 589.0 calories. Nutty Brown Rice has 13.0 grams of protein. Nutty Brown Rice has 33.0 grams of fat. Nutty Brown Rice has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Nutty Brown Rice are 4 cups water, 1 1/2 cups short-grain brown rice, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 1/4 cups mixed nuts, chopped, 1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg.Even brown-rice skeptics will enjoy this textural dish full of butter-browned nuts.

Champagne Cocktail recipe. Soak the sugar cubes separately, one in Benedictine, one in crème de cacao, and place them in the bottom of a champagne flute. Fill with chilled champagne, preferably brut. Champagne Cocktail has 290.0 calories. Champagne Cocktail has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Champagne Cocktail are 2 small sugar cubes, 1 ounce Bénédictine, 1 ounce crème de cacao, 4 ounces champagne.

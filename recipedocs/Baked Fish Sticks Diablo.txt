Baked Fish Sticks Diablo recipe.  Preheat oven to 475°F. Whisk first 4 ingredients in pie dish. Mix breadcrumbs with cayenne in another shallow dish. Heat 2 baking sheets in oven until hot, about 5 minutes. Brush with oil. Dip fish, 1 strip at a time, in butter mixture, then in crumbs. Turn to coat. Place 1 inch apart on prepared sheets. Bake until crumbs are golden and fish is opaque in center, about 6 minutes per side. Garnish with lemon wedges; serve. Baked Fish Sticks Diablo has 427.0 calories. Baked Fish Sticks Diablo has 38.0 grams of protein. Baked Fish Sticks Diablo has 22.0 grams of fat. Baked Fish Sticks Diablo has 330.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Fish Sticks Diablo are 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) butter, melted, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill, 1 cup plain dried breadcrumbs, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1 1/2 pounds catfish or other white fish fillets, cut into 3- by 3/4-inch strips, Lemon wedges.Serve with: Tartar sauce, potatoes au gratin, and a spinach salad with cider vinaigrette. Dessert: Chocolate pudding.  Fish Mustard Bake Winter Dill Bon Appétit.

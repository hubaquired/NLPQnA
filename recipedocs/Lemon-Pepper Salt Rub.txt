Lemon-Pepper Salt Rub recipe.  Finely crush whole black peppercorns in a spice mill. Transfer to a small bowl; stir in lemon zest and kosher salt. Rub seasoning between your fingertips to break up any clumps of zest. DO AHEAD: Can be made 3 days ahead. Store airtight at room temperature. Lemon-Pepper Salt Rub has 22.0 calories.

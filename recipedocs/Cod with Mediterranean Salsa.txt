Cod with Mediterranean Salsa recipe.  In a bowl stir together olives, tomato, shallot, basil julienne,capers, zest, lemon juice, and 1 tablespoon oil to make salsa and season with freshly ground black pepper and salt. Season cod with salt. In a non-stick skillet heat remaining tablespoon oil over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking and cook cod about 4 minutes on each side, or until golden and cooked through. Spoon salsa over cod and garnish with basil sprigs. Cod with Mediterranean Salsa has 311.0 calories. Cod with Mediterranean Salsa has 32.0 grams of protein. Cod with Mediterranean Salsa has 17.0 grams of fat. Cod with Mediterranean Salsa has 345.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cod with Mediterranean Salsa are 1/3 cup Kalamata or other brine-cured black olives, pitted and diced, 1 plum tomato, seeded and chopped, 1 shallot, minced, 1 tablespoon julienne fresh basil leaves, 1 tablespoon drained capers, 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated orange zest, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons olive oil, freshly ground black pepper, two 6-ounce pieces cod fillet, Garnish: fresh basil sprigs.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Bulgur with Onion, Tomato, and Feta recipe. Cook onion in oil in a 2-quart saucepan over moderate heat until softened. Stir in bulgur and chile flakes until coated. Add tomato, sugar, and water and bring to a boil. Simmer, covered, until bulgur is tender and creamy like risotto), about 20 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand, covered, 3 minutes. Stir in 1/2 cup feta and season with salt. Top with remaining cup feta. Bulgur with Onion, Tomato, and Feta has 215.0 calories. Bulgur with Onion, Tomato, and Feta has 7.0 grams of protein.

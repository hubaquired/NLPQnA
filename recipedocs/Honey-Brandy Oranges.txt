Honey-Brandy Oranges recipe. Remove peel and white pith from oranges. Cut oranges between membranes and release segments into bowl. Stir remaining ingredients in small saucepan over medium-low heat just until syrup is bubbling at edges, about 6 minutes. Pour mixture over orange segments. Refrigerate until cold, about 1 hour. Can be made 1 day ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Honey-Brandy Oranges has 174.0 calories. Honey-Brandy Oranges has 2.0 grams of protein. Honey-Brandy Oranges has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honey-Brandy Oranges are 5 oranges, 1/4 cup brandy, 1/4 cup honey, 1 teaspoon grated orange peel.This recipe is an accompaniment for Coffee and Turrón Ice Cream Torte with Honey-Brandy Oranges .

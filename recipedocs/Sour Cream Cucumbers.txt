Sour Cream Cucumbers recipe.  Dissolve the salt and sugar in the vinegar, add the sour cream and stir smooth. You may like more or less vinegar, salt or sugar, but don't make the dressing too sweet. Add the chives, dill and celery seed. Slice the unpared cucumbers paper-thin and combine with the dressing. Chill for 1 hour or more. Sour cream cucumbers improve in taste as they stand. The flavor of the cucumbers seeps into the dressing. Sour Cream Cucumbers has 150.0 calories. Sour Cream Cucumbers has 2.0 grams of protein. Sour Cream Cucumbers has 12.0 grams of fat. Sour Cream Cucumbers has 323.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sour Cream Cucumbers are 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 scant tablespoon sugar, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, 1 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons chopped chives or a grating of onion, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, head and fronds, 1 teaspoon celery seed, 2 firm fresh cucumbers.

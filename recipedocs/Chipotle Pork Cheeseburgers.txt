Chipotle Pork Cheeseburgers recipe.  Prepare a gas grill for direct-heat cooking over medium heat; see Grilling Procedure . Gently mix pork, chipotle, garlic, and a scant teaspoon salt until just combined. Form into 4 1/2-inch-thick) patties. Oil grill rack, then grill patties, covered, 4 minutes. Flip patties and top with a slice of cheese. Grill, covered, until just cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes more. Spread buns with mayonnaise and assemble burgers with tomatillo, avocado, and cilantro.

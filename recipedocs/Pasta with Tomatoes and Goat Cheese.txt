Pasta with Tomatoes and Goat Cheese recipe. Cook pasta in a 6-quart pot of boiling salted water until just tender, then drain. While pasta is cooking, toss tomatoes with salt to taste in a bowl and let juices exude. Toss hot pasta with goat cheese in a large bowl until cheese is melted and coats pasta. Add tomatoes with juices, olives, basil, and salt and pepper to taste and toss to combine. Pasta with Tomatoes and Goat Cheese has 605.0 calories. Pasta with Tomatoes and Goat Cheese has 25.0 grams of protein. Pasta with Tomatoes and Goat Cheese has 13.0 grams of fat. Pasta with Tomatoes and Goat Cheese has 349.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pasta with Tomatoes and Goat Cheese are 1 lb gemelli or penne, 2 3/4 lb cherry tomatoes, halved, 5 to 6 oz soft mild goat cheese, crumbled, 2/3 cup coarsely chopped Kalamata or other brine-cured black olives, 3/4 cup torn fresh basil leaves.The goat cheese, when tossed with the hot pasta, melts and becomes a creamy sauce.

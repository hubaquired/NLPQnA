Roasted Asparagus with Lemon recipe. Preheat oven to 450°F. Mix lemon juice, oil and lemon peel in 15 x 10 x 2-inch glass baking dish. Add asparagus; turn to coat. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Roast asparagus until crisp-tender, turning occasionally, about 20 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature. Roasted Asparagus with Lemon has 36.0 calories. Roasted Asparagus with Lemon has 2.0 grams of protein. Roasted Asparagus with Lemon has 2.0 grams of fat. Roasted Asparagus with Lemon has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Asparagus with Lemon are 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon peel, 36 asparagus spears, trimmed.

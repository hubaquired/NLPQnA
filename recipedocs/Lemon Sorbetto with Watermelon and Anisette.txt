Lemon Sorbetto with Watermelon and Anisette recipe.  Stir together anisette and sugar in a large bowl. Using melon-ball cutter, scoop watermelon balls into bowl. Add mint and toss. Serve sorbetto with watermelon mixture. Lemon Sorbetto with Watermelon and Anisette has 236.0 calories. Lemon Sorbetto with Watermelon and Anisette has 2.0 grams of protein. Lemon Sorbetto with Watermelon and Anisette has 1.0 grams of fat. Lemon Sorbetto with Watermelon and Anisette has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon Sorbetto with Watermelon and Anisette are 3 tablespoons anisette (anise liqueur), 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 (2-pound) wedge chilled watermelon, 1 tablespoon chopped mint, 1 pint lemon sorbetto (sorbet) or Italian ice, Equipment: a 1-inch melon-ball cutter, Garnish: mint sprigs.

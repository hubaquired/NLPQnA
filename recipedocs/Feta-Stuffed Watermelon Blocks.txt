Feta-Stuffed Watermelon Blocks recipe.  Using a small melon baller, scoop out the top of each watermelon block. Divide feta among melon hollows. Top each block with basil. The ingredients of Feta-Stuffed Watermelon Blocks are 3 1/4 pounds watermelon, rind removed, cut into 1 1/2-inch blocks, 3 ounces crumbled feta, 2 large fresh basil leaves, very thinly sliced.They look fancy, but these bite-size cubes are simple. A melon baller keeps things neat, but a regular spoon works, too.

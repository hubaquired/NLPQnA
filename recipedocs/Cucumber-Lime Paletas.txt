Cucumber-Lime Paletas recipe.  Puree 1 peeled large cucumber 10–12 ounces) with 1 cup simple syrup, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, and 1 teaspoon finely grated peeled ginger until smooth. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a large measuring cup, divide among ice-pop molds, insert sticks, and freeze until solid, at least 6 hours. Cucumber-Lime Paletas has 116.0 calories. Cucumber-Lime Paletas has 24.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber-Lime Paletas are 1 peeled large cucumber (10–12 ounces), 1 cup simple syrup, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon finely grated peeled ginger.

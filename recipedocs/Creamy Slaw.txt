Creamy Slaw recipe. Whisk together mayonnaise and sour cream in a large bowl until smooth, then whisk in vinegar, salt, and pepper. Add remaining ingredients and toss well. Let stand, uncovered, at room temperature, tossing occasionally, until wilted, about 30 minutes. Creamy Slaw has 207.0 calories. Creamy Slaw has 3.0 grams of protein. Creamy Slaw has 17.0 grams of fat. Creamy Slaw has 448.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Slaw are 1/3 cup mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons sour cream, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon black pepper, 1 1/2 lb green cabbage, cored and thinly sliced (6 cups), 1 carrot, shredded, 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper, 1/4 cup finely chopped onion.

Best Cranberry Relish recipe. Combine ingredients in a saucepan. Cook over medium heat until the berries pop open about 10 minutes). Skim the foam off the surface with a metal spoon and discard. Cool to room temperature. Refrigerate, covered, for up to 3 months. The ingredients of Best Cranberry Relish are 16 ounces fresh cranberries, 2 cups granulated sugar, 1/2 cup cranberry juice, 1/2 cup fresh orange juice, 1 tablespoon grated orange zest.Fresh orange juice always makes the best cranberry relish. Zest whole oranges before squeezing them for juice.  Fruit Juice Side Stew Thanksgiving Cranberry Fall Simmer Parade.

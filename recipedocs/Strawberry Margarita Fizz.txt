Strawberry Margarita Fizz recipe. Rub rims of 2 chilled stemmed glasses with water and dip in sugar to coat them. In a blender, blend ice cream, chopped strawberries, Tequila, and lime juice until smooth but still thick and pour into glasses. Add club soda and garnish with whole strawberries and lime slices. Strawberry Margarita Fizz has 623.0 calories. Strawberry Margarita Fizz has 3.0 grams of protein. Strawberry Margarita Fizz has 8.0 grams of fat. Strawberry Margarita Fizz has 69.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Margarita Fizz are Sugar for coating rims of glasses, 1 1/2 cups softened strawberry ice cream, 1/2 cup chopped strawberries plus whole strawberries for garnish, 1 ounce (2 tablespoons) tequila, or to taste, 1/2 teaspoon fresh lime juice, 1/3 cup chilled club soda, Garnish: lime slices.

Coconut-Blueberry Smoothie recipe. Purée blueberries, coconut milk, mint, lime juice, honey, and 1 cup ice in a blender until smooth. Add more lime juice and honey, if desired. The ingredients of Coconut-Blueberry Smoothie are 1 1/2 cups fresh (or frozen, thawed) blueberries, 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk, 1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves, 1 teaspoon (or more) fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon (or more) honey.Raspberries or blackberries can be swapped in for the blueberries.

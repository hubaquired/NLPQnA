Basic Tea Punch recipe.  Combine all ingredients and sweeten to taste. Put ice in a punch bowl and pour the mixture over it. Serve in 4-ounce punch glasses. The ingredients of Basic Tea Punch are 2 cups strong freshly brewed tea, 6 cups any fruit juice, 2 cups chilled ginger ale or club soda, Sugar syrup to taste Block of ice.

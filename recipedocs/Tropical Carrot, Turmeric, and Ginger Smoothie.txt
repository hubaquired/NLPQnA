Tropical Carrot, Turmeric, and Ginger Smoothie recipe.  Using smoothie or ice crush setting, purée orange, carrot, mango, coconut water, hemp seeds, ginger, turmeric, cayenne, salt, and 1/2 cup ice in a blender until smooth. Tropical Carrot, Turmeric, and Ginger Smoothie has 104.0 calories. Tropical Carrot, Turmeric, and Ginger Smoothie has 3.0 grams of protein. Tropical Carrot, Turmeric, and Ginger Smoothie has 3.0 grams of fat. Tropical Carrot, Turmeric, and Ginger Smoothie has 170.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tropical Carrot, Turmeric, and Ginger Smoothie are 1 blood or navel orange, peel and white pith removed, 1 large carrot, scrubbed, coarsely chopped, 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks, 2/3 cup coconut water, 1 tablespoon shelled raw hemp seeds, 3/4 teaspoon finely grated peeled ginger, 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated peeled turmeric, Pinch of cayenne pepper, Pinch of kosher salt.A pinch of salt makes everything taste better, including savory smoothies.

Beet and Arugula Salad recipe. Peel beets and cut into 1/2-inch wedges. In a steamer set over boiling water steam beets until tender, about 10 minutes, and transfer to a bowl. Discard course stems from arugula. Wash arugula well and dry. In a bowl whisk together vinegar and salt and pepper to taste and whisk in oil until emulsified. Pour half of vinaigrette over beets and toss well. To vinaigrette remaining in bowl add arugula and toss well. Arrange arugula and beets on 2 plates. Beet and Arugula Salad has 309.0 calories. Beet and Arugula Salad has 4.0 grams of protein. Beet and Arugula Salad has 28.0 grams of fat. Beet and Arugula Salad has 110.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Beet and Arugula Salad are 1/2 pound beets without leaves (about 3 medium), 1 small bunch arugula, 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar, 1/4 cup olive oil.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Tequila Tropical Punch recipe.  In a highball glass, mix all ingredients. Fill the glass with ice cubes; stir to blend. Garnish with a fresh pineapple spear. Tequila Tropical Punch has 176.0 calories. Tequila Tropical Punch has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tequila Tropical Punch are 1/4 cup pineapple juice, 1/4 cup blanco or reposado tequila, 1 1/2 tablespoons thawed frozen passion fruit juice concentrate, 1/2 teaspoon fresh lime juice.

Kale with Pickled Shallots recipe.  Bring vinegar, oil, sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon salt to a simmer in a small saucepan. Stir in shallots and remove from heat. Bring water to a simmer in a large pot, then add kale, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper and cook, uncovered, stirring, until kale is wilted and crisp-tender and liquid has evaporated, 5 to 8 minutes. Toss with shallot mixture, then season with salt and pepper. Kale with Pickled Shallots has 224.0 calories. Kale with Pickled Shallots has 10.0 grams of protein. Kale with Pickled Shallots has 12.0 grams of fat. Kale with Pickled Shallots has 93.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Kale with Pickled Shallots are 1/4 cup red-wine vinegar, 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, Pinch of sugar, 1/4 lb thinly sliced shallots (1 cup), 3/4 cup water, 2 pounds kale (2 bunches), stems and center ribs discarded.Gently pickling the shallots mellows them and also adds hints of sweetness and acidity. Tossing them with some earthy, quick-cooked kale makes for a pleasing contrast in flavors.

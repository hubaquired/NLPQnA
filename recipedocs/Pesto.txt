Pesto recipe. With food processor running, drop in garlic and finely chop. Stop motor and add nuts, cheese, salt, pepper, and basil, then process until finely chopped. With motor running, add oil, blending until incorporated. Pesto has 751.0 calories. Pesto has 13.0 grams of protein. Pesto has 77.0 grams of fat. Pesto has 817.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pesto are 3 large garlic cloves, 1/2 cup pine nuts, 2 ounces Parmigiano-Reggiano, coarsely grated (2/3 cup), 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 3 cups loosely packed fresh basil, 2/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil.

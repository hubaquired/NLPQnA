Imperial Hotel Fizz recipe. Combine all ingredients except the soda in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a highball glass and fill with soda. Imperial Hotel Fizz has 181.0 calories. Imperial Hotel Fizz has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Imperial Hotel Fizz are 1 1/2 ounces whiskey, 3/4 ounce light rum, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, 1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice, 1 teaspoon superfine sugar, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Club soda.

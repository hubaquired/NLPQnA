Orecchiette with Corn, Basil, and Pine Nuts recipe.  Combine corn and 4 Tbsp. butter in a large bowl. Cook orecchiette in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup pasta cooking liquid, and add to corn mixture. Stir vigorously and add pasta cooking liquid and butter as needed to fully coat. Stir in basil, pepper, and remaining 1 tsp. salt. Transfer pasta to a serving bowl or platter and top with pine nuts. Serve immediately. The ingredients of Orecchiette with Corn, Basil, and Pine Nuts are 3 cups fresh corn (from about 4 ears), 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) butter, softened, cut into pieces, plus more to taste, 1 pound orecchiette, 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste, 2/3 cup torn fresh basil leaves, 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted.

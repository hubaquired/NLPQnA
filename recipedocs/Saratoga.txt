Saratoga recipe. Put lemon juice, sugar and bitters in a tall glass with ice cubes, stir to mix and fill with ginger ale. The ingredients of Saratoga are 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon superfine sugar, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, 2 or 3 ice cubes, Ginger ale.

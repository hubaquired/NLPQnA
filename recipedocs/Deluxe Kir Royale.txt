Deluxe Kir Royale recipe. Puree berries in processor. Press puree through sieve into small bowl; discard seeds. Spoon 1 tablespoon berry puree and 1 tablespoon crème de cassis into each of 4 champagne flutes. Fill each flute with champagne and serve. Deluxe Kir Royale has 222.0 calories. Deluxe Kir Royale has 1.0 grams of protein. Deluxe Kir Royale has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Deluxe Kir Royale are 1 1/4 cups frozen unsweetened blackberries (about 5 ounces), thawed, 4 tablespoons crème de cassis (preferably imported), 1 750-ml bottle chilled champagne or other sparkling wine.

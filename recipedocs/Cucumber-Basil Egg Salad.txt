Cucumber-Basil Egg Salad recipe.  Gently combine the eggs, cucumbers, shallots, green onions, and basil in a medium bowl. Stir in the mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Store in the refrigerator for up to three days. Cucumber-Basil Egg Salad has 215.0 calories. Cucumber-Basil Egg Salad has 6.0 grams of protein. Cucumber-Basil Egg Salad has 20.0 grams of fat. Cucumber-Basil Egg Salad has 250.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber-Basil Egg Salad are 6 hard-cooked eggs, diced (2 cups), 3/4 cup seeded, diced cucumbers (about 1/2 cucumber), 1/4 cup minced shallots, 1/2 cup sliced green onions (green part only), 3 tablespoons lightly packed chopped fresh basil, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper.The glories of summer are captured in this pale green egg salad redolent of fresh basil, green onions, and crunchy cucumbers. Serve it surrounded by greens or tucked into pita loaves or slices of crusty bread for a satisfying lunch.

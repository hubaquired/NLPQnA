Cranberry Sauce with Crystallized Ginger recipe.  Combine first 6 ingredients in heavy medium saucepan. Bring to boil, stirring until sugar dissolves. Boil until cranberries pop, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes. Cool. Mix in crystallized ginger. Can be prepared 3 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Cranberry Sauce with Crystallized Ginger has 141.0 calories. Cranberry Sauce with Crystallized Ginger has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry Sauce with Crystallized Ginger are 2 12-ounce bags cranberries, 1 1/2 cups sugar, 1 cup orange juice, 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon grated orange peel, 1 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/2 cup (about 2 ounces) minced crystallized ginger.

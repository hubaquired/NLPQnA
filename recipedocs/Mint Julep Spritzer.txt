Mint Julep Spritzer recipe.  Mix fresh lemon juice, sugar, and fresh mint leaves in small bowl until sugar dissolves; press on mint with back of spoon to crush lightly. Let stand 30 minutes. Strain mint and lemon mixture into pitcher. Mix in bourbon, then club soda and ice. Pour into 8 tall glasses. Garnish each drink with mint sprig. Mint Julep Spritzer has 145.0 calories. Mint Julep Spritzer has 1.0 grams of protein. Mint Julep Spritzer has 26.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mint Julep Spritzer are 3/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2/3 cup sugar, 1 cup (packed) fresh mint leaves, 1 cup bourbon, 2 10-ounce bottles of chilled club soda, 6 cups crushed ice, 8 fresh mint sprigs.For anyone born in the Bluegrass State, the mint julep has a special significance: It's traditionally served on Kentucky Derby Day, the first Saturday in May. But it makes a great cocktail for any warm-weather afternoon. We've added a splash of club soda to make the drink a little lighter and more refreshing.

Orange Cream Cheese Frosting recipe.  Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and butter in large bowl until well blended. Beat in sugar, then orange peel and vanilla. Beat in sour cream. Cover and refrigerate until frosting is firm enough to spread, about 30 minutes. Orange Cream Cheese Frosting has 289.0 calories. Orange Cream Cheese Frosting has 2.0 grams of protein. Orange Cream Cheese Frosting has 22.0 grams of fat. Orange Cream Cheese Frosting has 142.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange Cream Cheese Frosting are 2 8-ounce packages chilled cream cheese, 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature, 2 cups powdered sugar, 2 teaspoons grated orange peel, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/3 cup chilled sour cream.

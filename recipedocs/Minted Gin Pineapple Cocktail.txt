Minted Gin Pineapple Cocktail recipe. In a blender, blend cracked ice, pineapple, gin, white crème de menthe and lemon juice for 30 seconds. Pour into a chilled 12-ounce glass. Garnish with a spring of mint. Minted Gin Pineapple Cocktail has 311.0 calories. Minted Gin Pineapple Cocktail has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Minted Gin Pineapple Cocktail are 1 cup cracked ice, 6 tablespoons unsweetened pineapple juice, 2 ponies (2 ounces) gin, 1 pony (1 ounce) white crème de menthe, 1 pony (1 ounce) lemon juice, Garnish: sprig of mint.

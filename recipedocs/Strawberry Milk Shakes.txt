Strawberry Milk Shakes recipe. Place four 12-ounce glasses in freezer for 1 hour. Place 1 cup Strawberry Syrup, 1 cup milk, and 1 pint ice cream in blender; purée until smooth. Add second pint of ice cream and purée until almost smooth. Divide mixture among frozen glasses. Drizzle each with some Strawberry Syrup, garnish with strawberry, if desired, and serve with spoon and straw. The ingredients of Strawberry Milk Shakes are 1 cup Strawberry Syrup plus additional for drizzling, 1 cup whole milk, 2 pints strawberry ice cream, slightly softened, 4 strawberries with stems (optional; for garnish).Rich, creamy, and very strawberry.

Spicy Chicken Salad with Bell Pepper and Cilantro recipe.  Whisk first 5 ingredients in large bowl to blend. Mix in chicken, bell pepper, onion, cilantro and chili. Season chicken salad with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 1 hour ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Add lettuce to chicken salad; toss to combine. Mound salad in large bowl. Garnish with tomato wedges and serve. Spicy Chicken Salad with Bell Pepper and Cilantro has 472.0 calories. Spicy Chicken Salad with Bell Pepper and Cilantro has 30.0 grams of protein. Spicy Chicken Salad with Bell Pepper and Cilantro has 35.0 grams of fat. Spicy Chicken Salad with Bell Pepper and Cilantro has 136.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Chicken Salad with Bell Pepper and Cilantro are 1/2 cup olive oil, 1/3 cup fresh lime juice, 2 teaspoons chili powder, 2 teaspoons chopped garlic, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 4 cups diced cooked chicken (about 1 pound), 1 red bell pepper, chopped, 3/4 cup chopped onion, 3/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tablespoon chopped seeded jalapeño chili, 4 cups chopped romaine lettuce (about 1/2 small head), 2 large tomatoes, cut into wedges.Use leftover cooked chicken, or buy a cooked chicken at the market or a take-out place. Serve the salad with warm tortillas, or try the chicken mixture (without the lettuce) as a sandwich filling.

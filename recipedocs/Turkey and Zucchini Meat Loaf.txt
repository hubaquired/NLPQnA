Turkey and Zucchini Meat Loaf recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine first 9 ingredients in large bowl and mix well. Shape mixture on rimmed baking sheet into 8 x 4 x 2-inch loaf. Bake meat loaf 45 minutes. Remove from oven. Stir preserves and mustard in small bowl to blend. Spread glaze over top of meat loaf. Return to oven; bake until thermometer inserted into center registers 165°F, about 20 minutes longer. Transfer meat loaf to platter. Cut crosswise into slices and serve. The ingredients of Turkey and Zucchini Meat Loaf are 1 1/4 pounds ground turkey, 1 cup coarsely grated zucchini, 3/4 cup finely chopped onion, 1/2 cup dry seasoned breadcrumbs, 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, 1 large egg, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/4 cup peach preserves, 4 teaspoons Dijon mustard.The peach and mustard glaze gives this dish a sweet-tart flavor.  turkey Vegetable Bake Zucchini Fall Bon Appétit.

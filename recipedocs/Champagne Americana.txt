Champagne Americana recipe.  Mix bourbon, sugar and bitters in glass measuring cup. Let stand until sugar begins to dissolve, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes. Fill eight 8-ounce wineglasses with ice cubes. Add 1 lemon peel strip to each glass. Divide bourbon mixture among wineglasses. Fill glasses with Champagne and serve. Champagne Americana has 136.0 calories. Champagne Americana has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Champagne Americana are 1/2 cup bourbon, 3 1/2 tablespoons sugar, 1 teaspoon Angostura bitters, Ice cubes, 8 2-inch-long strips lemon peel (yellow part only), 1 750-ml bottle chilled dry Champagne or sparkling wine.This Franco-American combination of bourbon and bubbles gives you another reason to be thankful. It doubles easily to accommodate the number of guests around your holiday table.

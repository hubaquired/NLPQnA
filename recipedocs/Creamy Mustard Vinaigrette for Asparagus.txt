Creamy Mustard Vinaigrette for Asparagus recipe. In a blender or food processor blend the cream, the vinegar, the mustard, the shallot, the sugar, and the lemon juice until the mixture is combined well, with the motor running add the oil in a stream, and blend the vinaigrette until it is emulsified. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve the vinaigrette over asparagus. Creamy Mustard Vinaigrette for Asparagus has 152.0 calories. Creamy Mustard Vinaigrette for Asparagus has 17.0 grams of fat. Creamy Mustard Vinaigrette for Asparagus has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Mustard Vinaigrette for Asparagus are 3 tablespoons heavy cream, 1/4 cup white-wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon Dijon-style mustard or to taste, 1 tablespoon minced shallot, a pinch of sugar, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, or to taste, 3/4 cup olive oil.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

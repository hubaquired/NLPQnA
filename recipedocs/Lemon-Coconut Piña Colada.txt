Lemon-Coconut Piña Colada recipe.  Place first 6 ingredients in large blender. Add ice. Cover and blend until smooth. Pour into two 14-ounce glasses. Garnish with grated lemon peel and nutmeg. Dip the rim of each glass in water, then press into a bowl of coarsely crushed toasted shredded coconut. To crush the coconut easily, rub it several times in the palm of your hand. The ingredients of Lemon-Coconut Piña Colada are 3/4 cup pineapple juice, 1/2 cup canned sweetened cream of coconut (such as Coco López), 6 tablespoons white rum, 6 tablespoons citrus-flavored rum, 1/4 cup chilled whipping cream, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 4 cups crushed ice, Grated lemon peel, Ground nutmeg.The Piña Colada was born in Puerto Rico in the 1950s.

Grilled Shrimp with Ponzu Sauce recipe.  Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Whisk soy sauce, mirin, lemon juice, oil, ginger and lemon peel in shallow bowl to blend. Add shrimp and stir to coat; let marinate 10 minutes. Drain marinade into small saucepan and boil 1 minute. Grill shrimp until just opaque in center, turning occasionally, about 3 minutes. Divide bok choy between 2 plates and drizzle with some of warm marinade. Top with shrimp. Serve, passing remaining marinade as sauce, if desired Grilled Shrimp with Ponzu Sauce has 282.0 calories. Grilled Shrimp with Ponzu Sauce has 22.0 grams of protein. Grilled Shrimp with Ponzu Sauce has 15.0 grams of fat. Grilled Shrimp with Ponzu Sauce has 2129.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grilled Shrimp with Ponzu Sauce are 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 3 tablespoonsmirin (sweet Japanese rice wine), or 3 tablespoons dry Sherry mixed with 1 tablespoon sugar, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon chopped peeled fresh ginger, 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel, 10 uncooked extra-large shrimp (about 10 ounces), peeled, deveined, 2 cups thinly sliced bok choy or Napa cabbage.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

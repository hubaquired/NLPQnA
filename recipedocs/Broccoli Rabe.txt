Broccoli Rabe recipe. Cook broccoli rabe in boiling salted water until just tender, about 4 minutes. Drain and season with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Broccoli Rabe are 1/2 bunch broccoli rabe (5 ounces), hollow stems trimmed.

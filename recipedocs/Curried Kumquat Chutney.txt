Curried Kumquat Chutney recipe. Cook first 5 ingredients and 1 cup kumquats in heavy small saucepan over medium heat until reduced to scant 1 cup, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes. Transfer to small bowl. Chill until cold, then mix in remaining 1/3 cup kumquats and cilantro. Season chutney to taste with salt and pepper. Chill 1 hour. Curried Kumquat Chutney has 170.0 calories. Curried Kumquat Chutney has 1.0 grams of protein. Curried Kumquat Chutney has 1.0 grams of fat. Curried Kumquat Chutney has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Curried Kumquat Chutney are 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup white wine vinegar, 2 tablespoons chopped crystallized ginger, 2 tablespoons dried cranberries, 1 teaspoon curry powder, 1 1/3 cups thinly sliced seeded kumquats (6 to 7 ounces), divided, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro.This sweet-tart chutney is great alongside lamb or pork chops, atop goat cheese and crackers, or on biscuits with sliced ham.

Pollyanna recipe. Muddle orange and pineapple slices in the bottom of a cocktail shaker. Add the ice and remaining ingredients. Shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass. Pollyanna has 311.0 calories. Pollyanna has 1.0 grams of protein. Pollyanna has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pollyanna are 3 slices orange, 3 slices pineapple, 3 or 4 ice cubes, 2 ounces gin, 1/2 ounce sweet vermouth, 1/2 ounce grenadine.

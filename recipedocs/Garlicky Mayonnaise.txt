Garlicky Mayonnaise recipe.  Whisk 1 finely grated garlic clove, 1/2 finely chopped anchovy fillet, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/2 cup plain whole-milk yogurt, and 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice in a medium bowl; season with kosher salt and coarsely ground black pepper and more lemon juice, if desired. DO AHEAD: Garlicky Mayonnaise can be made 2 days ahead. Cover and chill. Garlicky Mayonnaise has 443.0 calories. Garlicky Mayonnaise has 3.0 grams of protein.

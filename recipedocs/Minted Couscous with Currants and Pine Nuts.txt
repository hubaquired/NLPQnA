Minted Couscous with Currants and Pine Nuts recipe. Bring broth and butter to boil in medium saucepan. Remove from heat; stir in couscous. Cover, let stand 5 minutes. Fluff couscous with fork. Transfer to bowl. Add currants, pine nuts, green onions, mint and dill; stir to blend. Season with salt and pepper. Minted Couscous with Currants and Pine Nuts has 423.0 calories. Minted Couscous with Currants and Pine Nuts has 12.0 grams of protein. Minted Couscous with Currants and Pine Nuts has 16.0 grams of fat. Minted Couscous with Currants and Pine Nuts has 42.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Minted Couscous with Currants and Pine Nuts are 2 14 1/2-ounce cans low-salt chicken broth, 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) butter, 3 cups couscous, 1/2 cup dried currants, 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted, 4 green onions, thinly sliced, 1/4 cup minced fresh mint, 2 tablespoons minced fresh dill.

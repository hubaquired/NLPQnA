Peach and Berry Compote recipe.  Blanch and peel peaches. Cut into 1/4-inch-thick wedges. Simmer peaches, wine, and sugar in a 3-quart saucepan, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes, then remove from heat. Immediately transfer mixture to a bowl and add berries, tossing to combine. Cool compote completely. Serve compote chilled or at room temperature. Peach and Berry Compote has 153.0 calories. Peach and Berry Compote has 2.0 grams of protein. Peach and Berry Compote has 1.0 grams of fat. Peach and Berry Compote has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peach and Berry Compote are 3 large peaches (1 1/2 lb), 1 cup sweet dessert wine such as Muscat de Beaumes-de-Venise, 3 tablespoons sugar, or to taste, 2 cups blueberries (3/4 lb), 2 cups raspberries (1/2 lb).

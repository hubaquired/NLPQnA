Blood Orange Champagne Cocktail recipe.  Spoon 1 1/2 teaspoons crème de cassis into each of 8 Champagne flutes or other glasses. Add 3 tablespoons juice to each glass. Fill each glass with Champagne, then stir gently. Garnish cocktails with slices of blood orange, if desired. Blood Orange Champagne Cocktail has 119.0 calories. Blood Orange Champagne Cocktail has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blood Orange Champagne Cocktail are 1/4 cup crème de cassis (black-currant liqueur), 1 1/2 cups strained fresh blood orange juice (from about 6 oranges), 1 750-ml bottle chilled brut Champagne or dry sparkling wine, 8 blood orange slices (optional).

Royal Rickey recipe.  In a tall glass filled with ice, combine gin, vermouth and lime juice. Fill glass with seltzer. Add raspberry syrup and stir. Garnish with slice of lime. Royal Rickey has 180.0 calories. Royal Rickey has 25.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Royal Rickey are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) gin, 1 tablespoon dry vermouth, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1/2 to 1/3 cup chilled seltzer, 1 tablespoon raspberry syrup, Garnish: slice of lime.

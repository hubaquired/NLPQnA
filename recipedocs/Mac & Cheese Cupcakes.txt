Mac & Cheese Cupcakes recipe. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Cook the macaroni until tender. Drain, toss with a little bit of the oil, and set aside. In a medium nonstick saucepan, whisk together the milk and cornstarch until well blended. Stirring often, bring to a boil over medium heat. Stir in the mustard, pepper, and salt. Fold the chives into the macaroni mixture. Spoon the mixture into the pans and top with bread crumbs to taste. Bake until golden on top, 15 to 25 minutes. Mac & Cheese Cupcakes has 60.0 calories.

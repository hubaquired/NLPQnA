Lazy Texas Brisket recipe.  Preheat oven to 325°F. Rub garlic into both sides of brisket. Combine all remaining ingredients except barbecue sauce in small bowl and mix well. Rub into brisket. Set brisket fat side up on large piece of foil and wrap tightly. Transfer to shallow roasting pan. Bake until tender, about 3 hours. Serve hot or cold with sauce. Lazy Texas Brisket has 526.0 calories. Lazy Texas Brisket has 34.0 grams of protein. Lazy Texas Brisket has 42.0 grams of fat. Lazy Texas Brisket has 323.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lazy Texas Brisket are 1 large garlic clove, minced, 1 4- to 5-pound beef brisket, 1 tablespoon chili powder, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon dried sage, crumbled, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon ground oregano, 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper, 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper, Broncbuster's Barbecue Sauce.

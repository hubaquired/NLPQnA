Persimmon Salsa recipe. Mix persimmons, onion, lime juice, basil, serrano chile, mint, and ginger in small bowl. Season salsa to taste with salt and pepper. Salsa can be made 4 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Bring to room temperature before serving.) Persimmon Salsa has 89.0 calories. Persimmon Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein. Persimmon Salsa has 2.0 grams of sodium.

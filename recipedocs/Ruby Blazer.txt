Ruby Blazer recipe. In a saucepan, heat the cranberry juice just to the boiling point. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the kirsch. Divide the mixture among six 10-ounce heat-proof mugs, rinsed with boiling water beforehand. Garnish each drink with an orange slice, if desired. Ruby Blazer has 129.0 calories. Ruby Blazer has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ruby Blazer are 2/3 cups cranberry juice, 1 1/3 cups kirsch, Garnish: an orange slice.

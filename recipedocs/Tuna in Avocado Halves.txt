Tuna in Avocado Halves recipe. Drain oil from tuna can into a small bowl and whisk in vinegar and salt and pepper to taste. In a bowl combine tuna and half of dressing, keeping tuna in large chunks. Halve and pit avocado and season with salt and pepper. Fill avocado halves with tuna and drizzle with remaining dressing. Tuna in Avocado Halves has 343.0 calories. Tuna in Avocado Halves has 27.0 grams of protein. Tuna in Avocado Halves has 22.0 grams of fat. Tuna in Avocado Halves has 365.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tuna in Avocado Halves are a 6-ounce can tuna in olive oil (not drained), 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 California avocado.

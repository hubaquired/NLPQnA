Turkey Cheddar Sandwiches with Honey Mustard recipe.  Preheat broiler. In a small bowl stir together honey and mustard. Slice tomato lengthwise. Arrange bread on a baking sheet and toast under broiler until golden on one side. Turn bread over and spread honey mustard on untoasted sides. Sprinkle Cheddar over honey mustard and broil until cheese is melted. Arrange tomato, turkey, and radicchio between melted Cheddar toasts, seasoning with salt and pepper. Turkey Cheddar Sandwiches with Honey Mustard has 21.0 grams of fat. Turkey Cheddar Sandwiches with Honey Mustard has 593.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Turkey Cheddar Sandwiches with Honey Mustard are 1 tablespoon honey, 1 tablespoon coarse-grained mustard, 1 plum tomato, 4 slices 7-grain or whole-grain bread, 3 ounces coarsely grated sharp Cheddar (about 3/4 cup), 1/4 pound sliced turkey breast, 4 small radicchio leaves.

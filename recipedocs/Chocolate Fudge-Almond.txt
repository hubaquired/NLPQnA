Chocolate Fudge-Almond recipe. In a food processor, combine all the ingredients and process until smooth. The mixture will have the consistency of a ganache. Use immediately, or store in a covered container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. Chocolate Fudge-Almond has 256.0 calories. Chocolate Fudge-Almond has 8.0 grams of protein. Chocolate Fudge-Almond has 18.0 grams of fat. Chocolate Fudge-Almond has 60.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chocolate Fudge-Almond are 1 cup raw almond butter, at room temperature, 1/2 cup maple syrup, 1/2 cup Green & Black's Organic cocoa powder, Seeds from 1/4 vanilla bean, 1 1/2 teaspoons nama shoyu.Editor's note: The recipe and introductory text below are excerpted from Charlie Trotter and Roxanne Klein's book, Raw.

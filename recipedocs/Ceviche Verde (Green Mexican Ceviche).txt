Ceviche Verde (Green Mexican Ceviche) recipe. Place the mahi mahi in a stainless-steel or nonreactive bowl. Season with salt and let stand 10 minutes, then add the lime juice and toss gently. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 to 1 1/2 hours. Add the tomatillos, olives, red onion, jalapeño, avocado, cilantro, and olive oil and toss gently to combine. Season to taste with salt and serve immediately in individual glass bowls or martini glasses, accompanied by tortilla chips or crackers. Ceviche Verde (Green Mexican Ceviche) has 417.0 calories. Ceviche Verde (Green Mexican Ceviche) has 34.0 grams of protein. Ceviche Verde (Green Mexican Ceviche) has 27.0 grams of fat. Ceviche Verde (Green Mexican Ceviche) has 286.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ceviche Verde (Green Mexican Ceviche) are 3 pounds mahi mahi, cut into 1/2-inch cubes, Kosher salt, 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice, 1 pound tomatillos, husked, rinsed, and cut into small dice, 24 green Manzanilla olives, pitted and minced, 1 red onion, minced, 2 jalapeños, minced, 2 firm Hass avocados, pitted, peeled, and cut into small dice, 1 bunch fresh cilantro leaves and stems, minced, 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, Tortilla chips or saltine crackers as desired.This recipe is part of the Epicurious Online Cooking School, in partnership with the Culinary Institute of America.

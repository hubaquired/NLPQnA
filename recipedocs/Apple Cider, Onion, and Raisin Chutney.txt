Apple Cider, Onion, and Raisin Chutney recipe. In a large saucepan combine the cider, the vinegar, the onions, the raisins, the brown sugar, the cloves, and salt and pepper to taste and boil the mixture, stirring occasionally, for 30 to 35 minutes, or until the liquid is reduced to a syrupy consistency. The chutney keeps, covered and chilled, for 1 week. Serve the chutney with roasted meats or poultry. Apple Cider, Onion, and Raisin Chutney has 233.0 calories. Apple Cider, Onion, and Raisin Chutney has 2.0 grams of protein. Apple Cider, Onion, and Raisin Chutney has 1.0 grams of fat. Apple Cider, Onion, and Raisin Chutney has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apple Cider, Onion, and Raisin Chutney are 6 cups apple cider, 1/2 cup cider vinegar, two 10-ounce cartons of fresh pearl onions, blanched in boiling water for 3 minutes, drained, and peeled, 3/4 cup raisins, 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar, a pinch of ground cloves.

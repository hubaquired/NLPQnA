The ingredients of Whiskey Flip are 3 ounces whiskey, 1 whole egg, 1 teaspoon superfine sugar, 2 teaspoons heavy cream (optional), 1/2 cup crushed ice, Nutmeg.Flips were originally hot drinks made with egg and a variety of liquors and wines. They were warmed up by means of a loggerhead, an iron tool with a long handle and bulbous end that could be heated and plunged, like a poker, into the drink. Today, however, flips are generally served cold. Historically, they have been most often served in the morning or at bedtime, because of their soothing character.

Honey Mustard Dressing recipe. In a bowl whisk together the mustard, the gingerroot, the vinegar, the soy sauce, and the garlic paste, add the oil in a stream, whisking, and whisk the dressing until it is emulsified. Stir in the chives and salt and pepper to taste. Toss the dressing with salad greens. Honey Mustard Dressing has 168.0 calories. Honey Mustard Dressing has 19.0 grams of fat. Honey Mustard Dressing has 118.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honey Mustard Dressing are 2 1/2 tablespoons honey mustard (available at specialty foods shops and some supermarkets), 1 teaspoon grated peeled fresh gingerroot, 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 1 small garlic clove, minced and mashed to a paste with 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 1 tablespoon minced fresh chives.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

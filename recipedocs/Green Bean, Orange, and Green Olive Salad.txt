Green Bean, Orange, and Green Olive Salad recipe.  Whisk first 3 ingredients in small bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Cook beans in large saucepan of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 3 minutes. Drain, rinse, and pat dry. DO AHEAD Dressing and beans can be prepared 2 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature. Cut oranges in half, then crosswise into 1/3-inch-thick slices; place in large bowl. Mix in olives, parsley, beans, and dressing. Transfer to shallow dish. The ingredients of Green Bean, Orange, and Green Olive Salad are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon honey, 12 ounces haricots verts or small slender green beans, trimmed, 2 oranges, all peel and white pith cut away, 1/2 cup small green olives (such as picholine), 1/2 cup fresh Italian parsley leaves.An unusual combination of flavors — sweet, savory, and briny.

Jeyplak recipe.  Combine gin, vermouth, Pernod, and ice in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a cherry. The ingredients of Jeyplak are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1/2 ounce sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Pernod, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Cherry. Gin Alcoholic Cocktail Party Cocktail Pernod Fortified Wine House & Garden Drink.

Green Bean Succotash recipe. Blanch green beans until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Transfer to bowl of ice water to cool; drain. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add green beans and sauté 1 minute. Add lima beans, corn, and bell pepper. Sauté until vegetables are crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Mix in butter and Old Bay; season to taste with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Green Bean Succotash are 8 ounces green beans, trimmed, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 cup frozen lima beans, thawed, 1 cup fresh corn kernels (cut from 2 medium ears), 3/4 cup diced red bell pepper, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning.This shows how well green beans play with other vegetables.

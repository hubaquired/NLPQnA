Bayou Pepper Relish recipe.  Grind all the vegetables together in a meat grinder, using the coarsest blade. Cover with boiling water and leave for 5 minutes. Combine remaining ingredients in a large pot and bring to a boil. Drain vegetables, add to pot and boil 5 minutes. Pour into hot, sterilized preserving jars, cover and seal. Bayou Pepper Relish has 324.0 calories. Bayou Pepper Relish has 4.0 grams of protein. Bayou Pepper Relish has 1.0 grams of fat. Bayou Pepper Relish has 1412.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bayou Pepper Relish are 12 medium green peppers, seeded, 12 medium red peppers, seeded, 12 small yellow onions, peeled, 2 1/4 cups cider vinegar, 2 2/3 cups sugar, 2 tablespoons salt.

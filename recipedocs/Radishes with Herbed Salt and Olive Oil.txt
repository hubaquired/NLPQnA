Radishes with Herbed Salt and Olive Oil recipe.  Combine garlic if using), chives, parsley, tarragon, oregano, lemon zest, and peppercorns in a small mixing bowl. Stir in salt and transfer herbed salt to a serving bowl. Pour oil into another small bowl. Serve radishes with oil and herbed salt for dipping. The ingredients of Radishes with Herbed Salt and Olive Oil are 1 small garlic clove, very finely minced or pushed through a garlic press (optional), 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon leaves, 2 teaspoons dried oregano, 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest, 1/2 teaspoon coarsely ground peppercorns, 1 1/2 teaspoons coarse sea salt, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 2 pounds radishes (about 2 dozen), trimmed and halved.Start off a holiday meal in the freshest way possible: With crunchy radishes dipped in olive oil and then into flavorful herbed salt.

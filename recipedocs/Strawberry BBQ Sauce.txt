Strawberry BBQ Sauce recipe.  In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine half of the strawberries, the chili sauce, vinegar, and garlic. Bring the mixture to a boil and simmer for 20 minutes to thicken. Add the remaining strawberries and cook just until heated through. Strawberry BBQ Sauce has 35.0 calories. Strawberry BBQ Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Strawberry BBQ Sauce has 184.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry BBQ Sauce are 4 cups strawberries, chopped, 1/2 cup chili sauce, 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar, 3 cloves garlic, minced.Kenna Jo created this recipe "to use a surplus of strawberries." She said it "brings a fresh and sweet take on traditional barbeque sauce." It's fantastic over pork or chicken, and it also makes a great sauce to serve with corn bread. Kenna Jo calls that "Strawberry BBQ Shortcake.".  Sauce Berry Fruit Fourth of July Father's Day Backyard BBQ Strawberry Summer Party Simmer Boil.

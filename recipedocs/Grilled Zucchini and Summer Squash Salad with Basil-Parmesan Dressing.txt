Grilled Zucchini and Summer Squash Salad with Basil-Parmesan Dressing recipe.  Prepare barbecue medium heat). Place zucchini and crookneck squash on large baking sheet; brush all over with 3 tablespoons oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Grill vegetables until tender and brown, turning occasionally, about 10 minutes. Transfer to plate and cool. Cut vegetables diagonally into 1-inch-wide pieces. Place in large bowl. Add basil, Parmesan cheese, balsamic vinegar and remaining 2 tablespoons oil and toss to blend. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. The ingredients of Grilled Zucchini and Summer Squash Salad with Basil-Parmesan Dressing are 4 medium-large zucchini, trimmed, halved lengthwise, 4 medium-large yellow crookneck squash, trimmed, halved lengthwise, 5 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil, 1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese (about 1 ounce), 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar.

Green Beans with Shiitake Mushrooms recipe.  Melt 3 tablespoons butter in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add shiitake mushrooms and sauté until tender, about 5 minutes. Transfer mushrooms to medium bowl. Melt remaining 3 tablespoons butter in same skillet. Add shallots and garlic and sauté until tender, about 2 minutes. Add green beans and toss to coat with butter. Pour broth over green bean mixture. Cover and simmer until liquid evaporates and green beans are crisp-tender, about 10 minutes. Stir in shiitake mushrooms. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer to platter and serve. Green Beans with Shiitake Mushrooms has 136.0 calories. Green Beans with Shiitake Mushrooms has 4.0 grams of protein. Green Beans with Shiitake Mushrooms has 9.0 grams of fat. Green Beans with Shiitake Mushrooms has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Beans with Shiitake Mushrooms are 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) butter, 8 ounces fresh shiitake mushrooms, stemmed, caps sliced, 2 shallots, chopped, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 2 pounds slender green beans, trimmed, 2/3 cup canned low-salt chicken broth.Green beans go upscale in this fresh take on a favorite Thanksgiving dish.

Arugula and Smoked Mozzarella Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette recipe. Mix radicchio, arugula, and tomatoes in large bowl. Place lemon juice in small bowl. Gradually whisk in olive oil. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Add to salad; toss. Divide among 6 plates. Sprinkle salads with nuts. Serve with cheese.

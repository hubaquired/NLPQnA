Cheddar and Green Pepper Spread recipe.  In a bowl toss together the Cheddars, the bell pepper, and the scallion, add the mayonnaise, the mustard, and black pepper to taste, and combine the mixture well. The spread keeps, covered and chilled, for 1 week. Serve the spread with crackers. Cheddar and Green Pepper Spread has 222.0 calories. Cheddar and Green Pepper Spread has 7.0 grams of protein. Cheddar and Green Pepper Spread has 21.0 grams of fat. Cheddar and Green Pepper Spread has 281.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cheddar and Green Pepper Spread are 1/4 pound extra-sharp yellow Cheddar, grated coarse, 1/4 pound extra-sharp white Cheddar, grated coarse, 1 green bell pepper, cut into very thin 1-inch-long strips, 1/2 cup thinly sliced scallion, 1/2 cup bottled mayonnaise, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon-style mustard, Saltine crackers as an accompaniment.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Pear, Blue Cheese, and Fig Napoleons recipe. Cut each pear lengthwise into 10 1/8- to 1/4-inch-thick) slices 5 from one side, then 5 more from the opposite side), avoiding core and discarding rounded outermost slices. Layer 4 pear slices with cheese and fig on each of 4 plates, beginning with largest pear slices, and ending with smallest. Sprinkle any remaining cheese and fig around napoleons. Pear, Blue Cheese, and Fig Napoleons has 174.0 calories. Pear, Blue Cheese, and Fig Napoleons has 6.0 grams of protein. Pear, Blue Cheese, and Fig Napoleons has 7.0 grams of fat. Pear, Blue Cheese, and Fig Napoleons has 286.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pear, Blue Cheese, and Fig Napoleons are 2 firm-ripe pears, 3 to 4 ounces firm blue cheese (about 2/3 cup), crumbled, 3 to 4 soft dried figs, stemmed and finely chopped (about 1/3 cup).

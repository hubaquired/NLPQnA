Blue Cheese Toasts recipe. Preheat broiler. Mix cheese and butter in small bowl. Spread cheese mixture over bread rounds, dividing equally. Arrange rounds on baking sheet. Sprinkle each lightly with sugar. Broil until topping is just brown and cheese softens, about 1 minute. Transfer toasts to platter. Blue Cheese Toasts has 881.0 calories. Blue Cheese Toasts has 34.0 grams of protein. Blue Cheese Toasts has 10.0 grams of fat. Blue Cheese Toasts has 1914.0 grams of sodium.

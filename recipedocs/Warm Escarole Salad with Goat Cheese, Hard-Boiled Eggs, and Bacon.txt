Warm Escarole Salad with Goat Cheese, Hard-Boiled Eggs, and Bacon recipe.  Divide escarole among 6 plates. Cook bacon in medium nonstick skillet over medium heat until crisp. Transfer to paper towels to drain; reserve skillet with bacon drippings. Finely chop bacon; set aside. Whisk olive oil and vinegar in small bowl to blend. Heat bacon drippings in skillet over medium heat. Add shallots; sauté until slightly softened, about 3 minutes. Add olive oil mixture and whisk just until heated through, about 1 minute. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Drizzle vinaigrette over escarole. Sprinkle with eggs, goat cheese, and bacon. The ingredients of Warm Escarole Salad with Goat Cheese, Hard-Boiled Eggs, and Bacon are 1 head of escarole, torn into large bite-size pieces (about 8 cups), 2 bacon slices, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar, 1/2 cup finely chopped shallots, 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped, 1 5.5-ounce log soft fresh goat cheese, coarsely crumbled.A lovely combination of contrasting tastes: bitter greens, salty-smoky bacon, tangy goat cheese, and sweet shallots.

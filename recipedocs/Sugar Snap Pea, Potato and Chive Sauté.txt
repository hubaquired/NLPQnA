Sugar Snap Pea, Potato and Chive Sauté recipe. Cook potatoes in large pot of boiling water until just tender. Drain. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add sugar snap peas and sauté 2 minutes. Add potatoes and sauté until heated through. Mix in chives. Season with salt and pepper. Sugar Snap Pea, Potato and Chive Sauté has 178.0 calories. Sugar Snap Pea, Potato and Chive Sauté has 4.0 grams of protein. Sugar Snap Pea, Potato and Chive Sauté has 7.0 grams of fat. Sugar Snap Pea, Potato and Chive Sauté has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sugar Snap Pea, Potato and Chive Sauté are 1 pound red-skinned new potatoes, quartered, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 10 ounces sugar snap peas, trimmed, 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives or green onions.

Honeymoon recipe. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass. The ingredients of Honeymoon are 1 1/2 ounces applejack, 2/3 ounces Bénédictine, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 3 dashes curaçao, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

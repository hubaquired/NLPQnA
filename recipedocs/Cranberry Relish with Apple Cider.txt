Cranberry Relish with Apple Cider recipe. Boil apple cider in heavy small saucepan until reduced to generous 1/2 cup, about 15 minutes. Add sugar; stir over medium heat until sugar dissolves. Transfer to bowl and chill until cold, about 1 hour. Using on/off turns, coarsely chop cranberries, apple, and lemon peel in processor. Add cider syrup; using on/off turns, chop finely. Transfer to bowl. Cover and chill overnight. DO AHEAD: Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep chilled. Bring to room temperature before serving. Stir in chopped parsley and serve. Cranberry Relish with Apple Cider has 218.0 calories. Cranberry Relish with Apple Cider has 1.0 grams of protein. Cranberry Relish with Apple Cider has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry Relish with Apple Cider are 2 cups apple cider, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 12-ounce bag fresh cranberries, 1 medium Granny Smith apple, peeled, cored, diced, 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated lemon peel, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley.Boiling down the cider gives great apple flavor; parsley adds color and freshness.

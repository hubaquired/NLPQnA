Blue Cheese and Caramelized Shallot Dip recipe.  Heat oil in heavy medium saucepan over medium-low heat. Add shallots. Cover and cook until shallots are deep golden brown, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes. Cool. Whisk together mayonnaise and sour cream in medium bowl to blend. Add blue cheese. Using rubber spatula, mash mixture until smooth. Stir in caramelized shallots. Season dip to taste with salt and pepper. Cover dip and refrigerate until flavors blend, about 2 hours. Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Serve dip chilled or at room temperature. Blue Cheese and Caramelized Shallot Dip has 534.0 calories. Blue Cheese and Caramelized Shallot Dip has 8.0 grams of protein. Blue Cheese and Caramelized Shallot Dip has 54.0 grams of fat. Blue Cheese and Caramelized Shallot Dip has 598.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blue Cheese and Caramelized Shallot Dip are 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 1 1/4 cups thinly sliced shallots (about 4 ounces), 3/4 cup mayonnaise, 3/4 cup sour cream, 4 ounces blue cheese, room temperature.The ultimate onion dip?

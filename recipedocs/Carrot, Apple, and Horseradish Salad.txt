Carrot, Apple, and Horseradish Salad recipe. In a bowl stir together the carrots, the apples, peeled and grated coarse, the sour cream, the horseradish to taste, the parsley, the lemon juice, the sugar, and salt and pepper to taste and chill the salad, covered, for 1 hour, or until it is cold. Carrot, Apple, and Horseradish Salad has 152.0 calories. Carrot, Apple, and Horseradish Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Carrot, Apple, and Horseradish Salad has 6.0 grams of fat. Carrot, Apple, and Horseradish Salad has 101.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Carrot, Apple, and Horseradish Salad are 2 1/2 cups coarsely grated carrot, 2 large Granny Smith apples, 1/2 cup sour cream, 2 to 3 tablespoons finely grated peeled fresh horseradish or drained bottled horseradish, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley leaves, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon sugar.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

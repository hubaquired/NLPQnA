Pine Nut and Basil Rice recipe. In a 1 1/2-quart heavy saucepan cook pine nuts in oil over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until golden, about 2 minutes. With a slotted spoon transfer pine nuts to a bowl. Add rice to oil in pan and cook over moderately low heat, stirring, 1 minute. Add water and bring to a boil. Cover rice and cook over very low heat until water is absorbed and rice is tender, about 18 minutes. Remove pan from heat and let rice stand, undisturbed, 5 minutes. Finely chop basil. Fluff rice with a fork and stir in basil, pine nuts, and salt and pepper to taste. Pine Nut and Basil Rice has 272.0 calories. Pine Nut and Basil Rice has 5.0 grams of protein. Pine Nut and Basil Rice has 16.0 grams of fat. Pine Nut and Basil Rice has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pine Nut and Basil Rice are 1/4 cup pine nuts, 2 teaspoons olive oil, 1/3 cup long-grain white rice, 2/3 cup water, 1/4 cup packed fresh basil leaves.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

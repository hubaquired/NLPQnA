Za'atar Aioli recipe. With machine running, drop garlic through feed tube of processor and chop finely. Add mayonnaise and lemon juice. With machine running, drizzle in oil in thin stream, blending until smooth. Transfer to small bowl. Whisk in za'atar and season with salt and pepper. Let stand at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to develop. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill. Bring to room temperature before using. Za'atar Aioli has 200.0 calories. Za'atar Aioli has 22.0 grams of fat. Za'atar Aioli has 132.0 grams of sodium.

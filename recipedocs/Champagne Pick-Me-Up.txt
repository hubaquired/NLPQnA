Champagne Pick-Me-Up recipe. Pour the brandy into a large saucer champagne glass or big wineglass, add the curaçao and Fernet-Branca and drop in an ice cube. Stir gently. Pour in the champagne. The ingredients of Champagne Pick-Me-Up are 1 1/2 ounces brandy, 3 dashes curaçao, 3 dashes Fernet-Branca, 1 ice cube, 4 ounces chilled champagne.

Sauteed Skirt Steak recipe. Preheat oven to 450°F. Brush steak with Worcestershire and marinate 15 minutes. Lightly pat steaks dry and season well with salt. Heat 1/2 tablespoon of oil in each of 2 12-inch) nonstick or heavy skillets over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking, then sauté steaks in batches, without crowding, 3 minutes on each side for medium-rare. Transfer steaks to a platter and let stand, loosely covered, 5 minutes. Sauteed Skirt Steak has 350.0 calories. Sauteed Skirt Steak has 34.0 grams of protein. Sauteed Skirt Steak has 23.0 grams of fat. Sauteed Skirt Steak has 142.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sauteed Skirt Steak are 3 lb skirt steak, cut into 8 pieces, 1 1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce, 1 tablespoon oil. Beef Sauté Quick & Easy Steak Gourmet.

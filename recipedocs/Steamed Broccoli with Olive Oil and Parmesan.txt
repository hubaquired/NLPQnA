Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil and Parmesan recipe. Cut broccoli into 1 1/2- to 2-inch-wide florets. Peel stem and cut lengthwise into 1/3-inch-wide sticks. Steam broccoli in a steamer rack set over boiling water, covered, until tender, 5 to 6 minutes. Transfer to a bowl and toss with oil, cheese, and salt and pepper to taste. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil and Parmesan has 189.0 calories. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil and Parmesan has 9.0 grams of protein. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil and Parmesan has 14.0 grams of fat. Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil and Parmesan has 203.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed Broccoli with Olive Oil and Parmesan are 1 1/2 pound broccoli (1 large bunch), tough stems discarded, 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 1/2 ounce finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano (3/4 cup).

Tomatillo-Avocado Dip recipe. Cook tomatillos in large pot of boiling water until soft but still whole, about 3 minutes. Drain. Place tomatillos in large bowl and refrigerate until cold, about 1 hour. Place tomatillos in blender. Add remaining ingredients. Blend until smooth. Transfer dip to bowl. Cover with plastic and refrigerate until cold. Can be prepared 6 hours ahead.) Tomatillo-Avocado Dip has 122.0 calories. Tomatillo-Avocado Dip has 2.0 grams of protein. Tomatillo-Avocado Dip has 9.0 grams of fat. Tomatillo-Avocado Dip has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomatillo-Avocado Dip are 1 pound tomatillos, husked, 1 avocado, pitted, peeled, 1 cup loosely packed cilantro leaves, 1/3 cup sour cream, 5 garlic cloves, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 1/2 medium jalape&;ntilde;o chilies, stemmed, seeded, chopped.This easy-to-make dip is also great as a topping for burritos and enchiladas.

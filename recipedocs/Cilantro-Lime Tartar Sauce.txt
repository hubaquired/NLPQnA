Cilantro-Lime Tartar Sauce recipe.  Stir together all sauce ingredients. Cilantro-Lime Tartar Sauce has 136.0 calories. Cilantro-Lime Tartar Sauce has 15.0 grams of fat. Cilantro-Lime Tartar Sauce has 170.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cilantro-Lime Tartar Sauce are 1 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup chopped dill pickles, 1/4 cup chopped red onion, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 2 tablespoons drained capers, chopped, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, or to taste, 2 teaspoons minced fresh jalapeño chile (including seeds).

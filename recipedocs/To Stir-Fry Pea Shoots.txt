To Stir-Fry Pea Shoots recipe.  Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large heavy skillet over high heat until surface of oil ripples. Add smashed garlic cloves and dried hot red pepper flakes, then stir-fry until garlic is pale golden. Toss in shoots and stir-fry until wilted and tender, 4 to 5 minutes. To Stir-Fry Pea Shoots has 92.0 calories. To Stir-Fry Pea Shoots has 3.0 grams of protein. To Stir-Fry Pea Shoots has 7.0 grams of fat. To Stir-Fry Pea Shoots has 90.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of To Stir-Fry Pea Shoots are 1 pound mature pea shoots, rinsed and drained, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 3 garlic cloves, smashed with the flat side of knife, 1/4 teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes.

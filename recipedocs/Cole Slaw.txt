Cole Slaw recipe. In a large bowl, whisk together dressing ingredients. Shred apple coarse and add to dressing with cabbage and carrots. Toss cole slaw well. Cole Slaw has 143.0 calories. Cole Slaw has 1.0 grams of protein. Cole Slaw has 11.0 grams of fat. Cole Slaw has 254.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cole Slaw are 1 Granny Smith apple, 1/2 medium green cabbage, halved lengthwise and sliced very thin (about 4 cups), 1/2 medium red cabbage, halved lengthwise and sliced very thin (about 4 cups), 2 carrots, shredded fine, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon honey, 2 teaspoons cider vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

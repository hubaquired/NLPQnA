Smoked Turkey, Apple and Cheese Quesadillas recipe.  out tortillas and spread each with 1 teaspoon honey mustard. Place 1 tortilla, mustard side up, in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Top one half with 1/8 cup Monterey Jack cheese, 1 ounce turkey breast, 1/4 of the apple slices and 1/4 cup cheddar. Fold tortilla in half over ingredients and gently press. Cook 3 minutes. Flip and cook until both sides are golden and cheese is oozing slightly, about 2 minutes more. Remove quesadilla from skillet and slice into thirds. Repeat with remaining tortillas. The ingredients of Smoked Turkey, Apple and Cheese Quesadillas are 4 whole-wheat flour tortillas (8 inches across), 4 teaspoons prepared honey mustard, 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack, 4 ounces smoked turkey breast deli meat, 1 Mcintosh apple, thinly sliced, 1 cup shredded reduced-fat cheddar.Mcintoshes soften quickly when cooked.

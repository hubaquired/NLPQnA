Smoked-Salmon and Cucumber Rolls recipe. Quarter cucumber lengthwise, cutting off and discarding rounded ends. Stir together cream cheese, chives, horseradish, salt, and pepper to taste until combined well. Pat cucumber dry. Arrange enough salmon to completely wrap 1 cucumber quarter in 1 layer on a sheet of plastic wrap, then spread with a thin layer of cream cheese mixture. Wrap coated salmon around cucumber quarter to cover completely, pressing gently to help it adhere. Repeat with remaining cucumber quarters. You may have leftover salmon.) Cut rolls crosswise into 3/4-inch pieces. Smoked-Salmon and Cucumber Rolls has 18.0 calories. Smoked-Salmon and Cucumber Rolls has 2.0 grams of protein. Smoked-Salmon and Cucumber Rolls has 1.0 grams of fat. Smoked-Salmon and Cucumber Rolls has 75.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Smoked-Salmon and Cucumber Rolls are 1 (12-inch) seedless cucumber (usually plastic-wrapped), 4 ounces cream cheese, softened (1/2 cup), 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh chives, 2 tablespoons drained bottled horseradish, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 pound thinly sliced smoked salmon.

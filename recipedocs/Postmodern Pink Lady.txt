Postmodern Pink Lady recipe.  In a cocktail shaker, combine gin, Chambord, cranberry juice and egg white, shake the drink vigorously, and strain it into a chilled cocktail glass. Note: If there is a problem with eggs in your region, do not prepare this recipe. Postmodern Pink Lady has 224.0 calories. Postmodern Pink Lady has 2.0 grams of protein. Postmodern Pink Lady has 30.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Postmodern Pink Lady are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) chilled gin, 2 teaspoons Chambord (black-raspberry-flavored liqueur), 1/2 cup chilled cranberry juice, 1 tablespoon lightly beaten egg white.

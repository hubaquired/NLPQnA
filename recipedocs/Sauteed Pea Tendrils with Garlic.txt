Sauteed Pea Tendrils with Garlic recipe. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over high heat. Add garlic and sauté until beginning to brown, about 2 minutes. Remove garlic. Add tendrils; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Add chicken broth and sauté until greens are slightly wilted and heated through, about 2 minutes. Serve immediately. The ingredients of Sauteed Pea Tendrils with Garlic are 1 tablespoon canola oil, 2 small garlic cloves, smashed, 12 ounces fresh pea tendrils, 1/2 cup low-salt chicken broth.If you have trouble finding pea tendrils, pea sprouts (young pea tendrils) work very well with this dish; you'll find them at Asian markets and farmers' markets.

Basic Butter Cookie Dough recipe.  In large bowl of a standing electric mixer beat together butter, sugar, and salt until light and fluffy. Beat in yolks, 1 at a time, and vanilla and beat until smooth. Beat in flour gradually, beating dough until just combined well. Basic Butter Cookie Dough has 330.0 calories. Basic Butter Cookie Dough has 4.0 grams of protein. Basic Butter Cookie Dough has 19.0 grams of fat. Basic Butter Cookie Dough has 92.0 grams of sodium.

Chocolate Chunk Share Cookie recipe.  Preheat oven to 160°C 325°F). Place the butter, both the sugars, egg, vanilla, flour, baking soda and cocoa in a food processor and process until a dough forms. Press the dough out to a 10-inch round on a baking tray lined with non-stick baking paper, scatter with the chocolate and lightly press the pieces into the dough. Bake for 15–20 minutes or until firm to the touch. Allow to cool slightly on the tray. Serve warm with ice cream and caramel sauce. Chocolate Chunk Share Cookie has 414.0 calories. Chocolate Chunk Share Cookie has 5.0 grams of protein. Chocolate Chunk Share Cookie has 17.0 grams of fat. Chocolate Chunk Share Cookie has 58.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chocolate Chunk Share Cookie are 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened, 3/4 cup (135g) brown sugar, 1/4 cup (55g) superfine sugar, 1 egg, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 1 cup (150g) plain (all-purpose) flour, 1/8 teaspoon baking soda, 1/4 cup (25g) cocoa powder, 1 1/2 ounces milk chocolate, chopped, 1 1/2 ounces white chocolate, chopped, Vanilla ice-cream and store-bought caramel sauce or dulce de leche, to serve.

Five-Spice Shortbread recipe.  Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. In a small bowl, combine the ginger, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, star anise, and sugar. Cut the chilled log into twenty 1/2-inch rounds. Dip one cut surface of each round into the spice mixture and arrange the rounds 2 inches apart on all sides on a parchment-lined or nonstick cookie sheet or sheets. Bake until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove the cookies with a spatula and cool on a wire rack. Five-Spice Shortbread has 13.0 calories. Five-Spice Shortbread has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Five-Spice Shortbread are 2 teaspoons ground ginger, 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, 2 teaspoons ground cardamom, 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves, 1/2 teaspoon ground star anise, 1/4 cup turbinado or other granulated sugar, 1 chilled log of butter shortbread cookie dough.Editor's note: This recipe is from Ming Tsai's Simply Ming.

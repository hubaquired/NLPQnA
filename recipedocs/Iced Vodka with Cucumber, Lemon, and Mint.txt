Iced Vodka with Cucumber, Lemon, and Mint recipe. Mix sugar, mint, and lemon slices in pitcher, mashing slightly with back of spoon. Mix in cucumber. Let stand 30 minutes. Add lemon juice, vodka, and 1/2 cup water; stir to dissolve sugar. Chill at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours. Mix in ice and serve. Iced Vodka with Cucumber, Lemon, and Mint has 185.0 calories. Iced Vodka with Cucumber, Lemon, and Mint has 1.0 grams of protein. Iced Vodka with Cucumber, Lemon, and Mint has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Iced Vodka with Cucumber, Lemon, and Mint are 1/3 cups sugar, 1 cup (packed) fresh mint leaves, 2 lemons, sliced into rounds, 1/2 small cucumber, sliced, 2 cups fresh lemon juice, 2 cups vodka, 1/2 cup water, 3 cups ice cubes.This one can also be a spritzer.

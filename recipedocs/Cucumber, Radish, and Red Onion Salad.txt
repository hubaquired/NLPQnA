Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad recipe.  Peel, halve, and seed cucumber. Diagonally cut cucumber into thin slices and cut radishes into julienne strips. In a bowl toss together all ingredients and season with salt and pepper. Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad has 45.0 calories. Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad has 1.0 grams of protein. Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad has 2.0 grams of fat. Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber, Radish, and Red Onion Salad are 3/4 cucumber, 2 radishes, 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion, 1 teaspoon olive oil, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice.This recipe was created to accompany Grilled Cheese with Smoked Turkey and Avocado.

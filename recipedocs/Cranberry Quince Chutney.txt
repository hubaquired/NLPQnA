Cranberry Quince Chutney recipe.  In a large saucepan combine all ingredients except onion and simmer, stirring occasionally, 30 minutes. Add onion and simmer 20 minutes, or until chutney is thickened. Chutney may be made 1 week ahead and chilled, covered. Serve chutney chilled or at room temperature. Cranberry Quince Chutney has 196.0 calories. Cranberry Quince Chutney has 1.0 grams of protein. Cranberry Quince Chutney has 309.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry Quince Chutney are a 12-ounce bag fresh or unthawed frozen cranberries, picked over, 1 cup quince preserves* or apple jelly (about 12 ounces), 1 red bell pepper, chopped (about 1 cup), 1/4 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar, 3/4 teaspoon ground coriander seeds, 1 teaspoon mustard seeds, 3/4 teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, 1/2 cup raisins, 1/4 cup cider vinegar, a 4-inch strip fresh lemon zest, removed with a vegetable peeler, 1 medium onion, sliced (about 1 cup), *available at specialty foods shops and some supermarkets.

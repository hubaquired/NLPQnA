Blue Cheese, Port, and Walnut Spread recipe.  In a food processor blend together the cheese, the butter, and the Port until the mixture is smooth and in a bowl combine well the cheese mixture and the walnuts. Transfer the spread to crocks or ramekins. The spread keeps, covered and chilled, for 3 weeks. Serve the spread with crackers. Blue Cheese, Port, and Walnut Spread has 276.0 calories. Blue Cheese, Port, and Walnut Spread has 10.0 grams of protein. Blue Cheese, Port, and Walnut Spread has 25.0 grams of fat. Blue Cheese, Port, and Walnut Spread has 522.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blue Cheese, Port, and Walnut Spread are 1 pound blue cheese (such as Stilton or Gorgonzola), crumbled, 1 stick (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, softened, 1/3 to 1/2 cup Tawny Port, 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

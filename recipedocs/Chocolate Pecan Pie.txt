Chocolate Pecan Pie recipe. Butter an 8" pie tin with the softened butter and press in the chopped nuts. Combine the syrup, eggs, salt and vanilla and beat well. Melt the chocolate bits over hot water and add them slowly to the syrup mixture, stirring constantly. Pour into the pie shell and sprinkle with coarsely broken pecans. The ingredients of Chocolate Pecan Pie are 1 tablespoon softened butter, 3/4 cup finely chopped pecans, 1/2 cup dark corn syrup, 2 eggs, slightly beaten, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate bits, 1/2 cup coarsely broken pecans.

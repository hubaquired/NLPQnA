Peppered Goat Cheese and Walnut Croutons recipe.  In a food processor grind fine the walnuts with the pepper and the salt and transfer the mixture to a shallow bowl. Working in small batches, roll the cheese cubes in the walnut mixture, coating them completely, and transfer them to a tray. The "croutons" keep, covered and chilled, for 3 days. Serve the "croutons" as a topping for tossed green or tomato salad. Peppered Goat Cheese and Walnut Croutons has 175.0 calories. Peppered Goat Cheese and Walnut Croutons has 11.0 grams of protein. Peppered Goat Cheese and Walnut Croutons has 14.0 grams of fat. Peppered Goat Cheese and Walnut Croutons has 315.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peppered Goat Cheese and Walnut Croutons are 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted lightly and cooled, 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 pound firm mild goat cheese, chilled well and cut into 1/4-inch cubes.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

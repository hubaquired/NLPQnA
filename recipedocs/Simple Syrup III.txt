Simple Syrup III recipe. In heavy medium saucepan over medium heat, stir together water and sugar until sugar dissolves. Increase heat slightly, then simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Refrigerate syrup until cold, about 3 hours. Can be prepared 1 week ahead. Cover and keep refrigerated.) Simple Syrup III has 129.0 calories. Simple Syrup III has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Simple Syrup III are 1 cup water, 1 cup sugar.Be sure to prepare the syrup at least a few hours before making drinks so that it has plenty of time to chill.

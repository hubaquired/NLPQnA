Roasted Chestnuts recipe.  Make a large X in each chestnut with chestnut knife or a sharp paring knife, cutting through shell. Toss chestnuts with oil in a bowl. Heat dry skillet over moderately low heat until hot, then roast chestnuts in skillet on stovetop, covered, stirring every few minutes, for 15 minutes total. Add water and continue to roast, covered, stirring occasionally, until water is evaporated and chestnuts are tender, about 5 minutes more. Serve hot. Roasted Chestnuts has 200.0 calories. Roasted Chestnuts has 2.0 grams of protein. Roasted Chestnuts has 2.0 grams of fat. Roasted Chestnuts has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Chestnuts are 1 1/2 to 2 pounds whole chestnuts in shell, 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, 1/4 cup water, Special equipment: a chestnut knife (optional); a large heavy skillet (preferably cast-iron) with a lid.

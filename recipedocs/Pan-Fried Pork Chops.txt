Pan-Fried Pork Chops recipe. Mix flour, cornstarch and garlic salt in medium bowl. Sprinkle pork chops with salt and pepper. Dredge pork chops in flour mixture do not shake off excess). Pour enough vegetable oil into heavy large skillet to reach depth of 1 inch. Heat oil to 350°F. Fry pork chops in oil until golden brown and cooked through, about 5 minutes per side Pan-Fried Pork Chops has 933.0 calories. Pan-Fried Pork Chops has 32.0 grams of protein. Pan-Fried Pork Chops has 80.0 grams of fat. Pan-Fried Pork Chops has 162.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pan-Fried Pork Chops are 1/2 cup all purpose flour, 1/4 cup cornstarch, 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt, 4 7-ounce center-cut pork loin rib chops (each about 3/4 inch thick), Vegetable oil (for frying).

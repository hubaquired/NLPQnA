Pepper Jack, Avocado and Black Beans in a Tortilla recipe. Place shredded cheese, beans and avocado onto 1 tortilla. Place 2nd tortilla on top. Close the sandwich. Spread evenly 1/2 teaspoon of lightly salted butter onto each side of the tortilla. Grill over moderate heat for approximately 3-4 minutes per side. Garnish with sour cream and salsa.

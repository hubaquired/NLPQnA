Savoy Cabbage and Arugula Salad recipe.  With a knife cut cabbage into very thin slices and coarsely chop arugula. In a large bowl toss together all ingredients with salt and pepper to taste until greens are coated. Savoy Cabbage and Arugula Salad has 126.0 calories. Savoy Cabbage and Arugula Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Savoy Cabbage and Arugula Salad has 9.0 grams of fat. Savoy Cabbage and Arugula Salad has 46.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Savoy Cabbage and Arugula Salad are half a 2-pound head Savoy cabbage, 3 cups packed arugula leaves, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1 1/2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 3/4 teaspoon sugar.

Chicken Vegetable Soup with Lime and Cilantro recipe.  Place chicken, broth, potatoes, carrots and coriander in large pot and bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until chicken and vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally, about 40 minutes. Add hominy, green onions, cilantro and lime juice and simmer 5 minutes to blend flavors. Season soup to taste with salt and pepper. Chicken Vegetable Soup with Lime and Cilantro has 559.0 calories.

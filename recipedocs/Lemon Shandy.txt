Lemon Shandy recipe.  In a small saucepan bring sugar and 1 cup water to a boil, stirring until sugar is dissolved, and stir in zest. Cool sugar syrup to room temperature. Transfer syrup to a small pitcher and stir in remaining 2 cups water, lemon juice, and mint. Chill lemonade until cold. Makes about 4 1/2 cups lemonade.) Pour 1/4 cup lemonade, or to taste, into each of 4 chilled beer glasses and top off with beer. The ingredients of Lemon Shandy are 1 cup sugar, 3 cups water, four 3-inch strips lemon zest, removed with a vegetable peeler, 1 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 fresh mint sprigs, chilled beer, such as pale ale.Shandy  — a drink made of beer mixed with lemonade, ginger ale or ginger beer  — first appeared in Britain in the mid-nineteenth century.

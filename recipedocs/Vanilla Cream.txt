Vanilla Cream recipe.  In a small food processor blend cream ingredients and a pinch salt until very smooth, about 1 minute. Chill cream, covered, at least 3 hours and up to 24. Vanilla Cream has 124.0 calories. Vanilla Cream has 8.0 grams of protein. Vanilla Cream has 5.0 grams of fat. Vanilla Cream has 241.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Vanilla Cream are 2/3 cup no-salt low-fat cottage cheese, 3 tablespoons sour cream, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, 1 to 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice.This recipe was created to accompany Honeydew in Cardamom Lime Syrup with Vanilla Cream.

Mashed Yams and Apples recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Boil yams in pot of boiling salted water until tender, about 20 minutes. Add apple; cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain well. Place yams and apples on large baking sheet. Bake until slightly dry, about 10 minutes. Blend yams and apple in processor until smooth. Add butter and spices; blend until butter melts. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Chill.) Transfer mixture to medium saucepan. Stir yams over medium-low heat until hot. Mashed Yams and Apples has 280.0 calories. Mashed Yams and Apples has 4.0 grams of protein. Mashed Yams and Apples has 6.0 grams of fat. Mashed Yams and Apples has 126.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mashed Yams and Apples are 2 pounds yams (red-skinned sweet potatoes), peeled, cut into 1-inch pieces, 1 8-ounce Granny Smith apple, peeled, cored, cut into 1-inch pieces, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice.This recipe was created to accompany Herb-coated Lamb with Port-Red Wine Sauce .

Quickest Madeira-Mushroom Gravy recipe. Heat olive oil in large saucepan; add onion. Sauté until golden. Add mushrooms; sauté until soft. Add Madeira, rosemary, gravy, chicken broth, and whipping cream. Simmer 5 minutes, whisking constantly. Season with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Quickest Madeira-Mushroom Gravy are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 large chopped onion, 2 cups sliced and stemmed shiitake mushrooms, 1/3 cup Madeira, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary, 2 cups purchased high-quality gravy, 1 cup low-salt chicken broth, 1/2 cup whipping cream.This uses store-bought gravy and can be done a day ahead.

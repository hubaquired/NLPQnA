Bacon, Egg, and Tomato Club Sandwiches recipe.  Stir together mayonnaise, chives, and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Spread 4 slices of toast with some of mayonnaise and top with half of egg, tomato, bacon, and herbs. Spread both sides of 4 more slices of toast with mayonnaise and place on top. Cover with remaining egg, tomato, bacon, herbs, and toast spread with mayonnaise). Secure sandwiches with picks and cut as desired. Bacon, Egg, and Tomato Club Sandwiches has 472.0 calories. The ingredients of Bacon, Egg, and Tomato Club Sandwiches are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons finely chopped chives, 12 slices sandwich bread, toasted, 4 hard-boiled eggs, thinly sliced and seasoned with salt, 3 to 4 medium tomatoes, thinly sliced, 1 pound sliced bacon, halved and cooked until crisp, 2 cups loosely packed mixed herb leaves such as flat-leaf parsley, cilantro, and basil.In recent years, panini-type grilled sandwiches have taken center stage, but there is nothing like a freshly made club sandwich. The triple-decker here amplifies the classic flavors of a BLT with sliced hard-boiled eggs, chive mayo, and a jumble of herbs.

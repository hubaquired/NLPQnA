Olive and Artichoke Tapenade recipe.  Finely chop first 5 ingredients in processor. Add 1 tablespoon parsley and oil; puree until coarse paste forms. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to small bowl. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon parsley. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill.) The ingredients of Olive and Artichoke Tapenade are 1 13 3/4-ounce can artichoke hearts, well drained, 1/4 cup walnuts, toasted, 8 large brine-cured green olives, pitted, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil.Serve this tangy and delicious hors d'oeuvre spread on toasted baguette slices.

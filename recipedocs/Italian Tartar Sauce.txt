Italian Tartar Sauce recipe.  In medium bowl, whisk together all ingredients. DO AHEAD: Sauce can be made ahead and refrigerated up to 3 days.) Italian Tartar Sauce has 76.0 calories. The ingredients of Italian Tartar Sauce are 3/4 cup mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon plus 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon capers, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon cornichons (gherkins), finely chopped, 1 anchovy fillet, minced, or 1/2 teaspoon anchovy paste, 1 clove garlic, minced, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper.This recipe originally accompanied Fritto Misto .

Maple-Glazed Yams with Pecan Topping recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Butter 13 x 9 x 2-inch glass baking dish. Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Add yams. Cook until water returns to simmer, about 4 minutes. Drain. Rinse under cold water. Arrange yams in prepared dish, overlapping slightly. Season with salt. Pour syrup over yams. Dot with 3 tablespoons butter. Cover and bake until yams are almost tender, about 25 minutes. Mix flour and brown sugar in medium bowl. Add remaining 5 tablespoons butter. Rub in with fingers until mixture resembles coarse meal. Mix in pecans. Sprinkle pecan mixture atop yams. Bake yams until tender, about 20 minutes. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Cool. Cover and let stand at room temperature. Rewarm, uncovered, in 375°F oven 20 minutes.) Maple-Glazed Yams with Pecan Topping has 337.0 calories. Maple-Glazed Yams with Pecan Topping has 3.0 grams of protein. Maple-Glazed Yams with Pecan Topping has 11.0 grams of fat. Maple-Glazed Yams with Pecan Topping has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Maple-Glazed Yams with Pecan Topping are 4 pounds yams (red-skinned sweet potatoes), peeled, cut into 1/4-inch-thick rounds, 1/2 cup pure maple syrup, 8 tablespoons (1 stick) chilled butter, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1/3 cup all purpose flour, 1/3 cup (packed) golden brown sugar, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped pecans.

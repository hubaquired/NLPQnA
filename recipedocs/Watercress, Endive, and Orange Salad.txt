Watercress, Endive, and Orange Salad recipe.  Using small sharp knife, cut peel and white pith from oranges. Cut oranges into 1 1/2-inch chunks. Set aside. Whisk vinegar and sugar in large bowl to blend. Gradually whisk in olive oil. Season vinaigrette to taste with salt and pepper. Add watercress, endive, and oranges; toss to coat. Divide salad among 6 plates. Sprinkle with walnuts and serve. Watercress, Endive, and Orange Salad has 155.0 calories. Watercress, Endive, and Orange Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Watercress, Endive, and Orange Salad has 13.0 grams of fat. Watercress, Endive, and Orange Salad has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watercress, Endive, and Orange Salad are 2 oranges, 1 1/2 tablespoons Sherry wine vinegar, Pinch of sugar, 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 bunch watercress, thick stems trimmed, 3 large heads of Belgian endive, trimmed, thinly sliced crosswise, 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped.

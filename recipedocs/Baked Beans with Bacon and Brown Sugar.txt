Baked Beans with Bacon and Brown Sugar recipe.  Put oven rack in upper third of oven and preheat oven to 400°F. Stir together tomatoes, brown sugar, mustard, and salt in a bowl. Gently stir in beans and transfer to a 2-quart shallow baking dish. Arrange onion slices in 1 layer over beans, then cover onion with bacon. Bake, uncovered, until bacon is browned and beans have absorbed most of liquid, about 1 1/4 hours. Baked Beans with Bacon and Brown Sugar has 339.0 calories. Baked Beans with Bacon and Brown Sugar has 17.0 grams of protein. Baked Beans with Bacon and Brown Sugar has 8.0 grams of fat. Baked Beans with Bacon and Brown Sugar has 499.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Beans with Bacon and Brown Sugar are 1 (14- to 15-ounce) can crushed tomatoes in thick purée, 1/4 cup packed dark brown sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 (19-ounce) cans small white beans, rinsed and drained (4 cups), 1 medium onion, cut crosswise into 1/4-inch-thick slices, 6 bacon slices (1/4 pound).

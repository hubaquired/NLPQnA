Lebanese Tomato "Salsa" recipe.  Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add tomatoes and garlic; season with salt, and cook, stirring often, until tomatoes release their juices, about 4 minutes. Add cayenne and mint, if using, and cook, stirring often, until sauce is slightly thickened, about 5 minutes; season with salt. Let cool. The ingredients of Lebanese Tomato "Salsa" are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 8 medium tomatoes (about 2 pounds), seeded, cut into 1/2" pieces, 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped, Kosher salt, 1/4-1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1 tablespoon dried mint or 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint (optional).Briefly cooking the tomatoes is key–the finished sauce should still be very fresh-tasting.

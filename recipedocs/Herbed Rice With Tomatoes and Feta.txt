Herbed Rice With Tomatoes and Feta recipe.  Cook rice according to package directions. Transfer to a large bowl and let cool slightly, about 10 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, feta, and Herbed Olive Oil and let sit at least 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld before serving. The ingredients of Herbed Rice With Tomatoes and Feta are 1 1/2 cups long-grain white rice, 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved, 8 ounces feta, crumbled, 3/4 cup Herbed Olive Oil.This vibrant green side dish is great served at room temperature, so you can make it a couple hours before your guests arrive for a dinner party.

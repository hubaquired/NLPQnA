Snow-Cap Margarita recipe.  In a blender blend all ingredients with 1 cup ice cubes until completely smooth. Serve Margarita garnished with lime slice in a stemmed glass. Snow-Cap Margarita has 426.0 calories. Snow-Cap Margarita has 2.0 grams of protein. Snow-Cap Margarita has 12.0 grams of fat. Snow-Cap Margarita has 29.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Snow-Cap Margarita are 2 ounces (1/4 cup) tequila, 1/4 cup well-stirred canned cream of coconut such as Coco Lopez, zest of 1 lime, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, or to taste, Garnish: 1 lime slice.

Oatmeal Date Drop Scones recipe.  In a bowl whisk together the milk, the egg, the brown sugar, and the vanilla until the mixture is combined well. In another bowl stir together the flour, the oats, the baking powder, the baking soda, and the salt and blend in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse meal. Sir in the dates and the milk mixture until the mixture just forms a sticky dough. Drop the dough by 1/3-cup measures onto an ungreased baking sheet and bake the scones in the middle of a preheated 400Â°F. for 15 to 18 minutes, or until they are golden. Alternatively the scones may be dropped onto a hot greased griddle and cooked over moderate heat, turning them, for 15 to 18 minutes, or until they are golden on both sides and cooked through.) The ingredients of Oatmeal Date Drop Scones are 3/4 cup milk, 1 large egg, 3 tablespoons light brown sugar, 1 tablespoon vanilla extract, 2 1/4 cups cake flour (not self-rising), 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats, 1 tablespoon double-acting baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 3/4 stick (6 tablespoons) cold unsalted butter, cut into bits, 1/2 cup chopped pitted dates.

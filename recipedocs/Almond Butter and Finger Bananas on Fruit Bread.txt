Almond Butter and Finger Bananas on Fruit Bread recipe. Spread a thick layer of the almond butter over each bread slice. Top with the banana slices and serve open-faced. The ingredients of Almond Butter and Finger Bananas on Fruit Bread are 3 tablespoons roasted almond butter, 2 slices banana or cranberry bread, 1 1/2 finger bananas (or 1/2 regular banana), peeled and sliced lengthwise.

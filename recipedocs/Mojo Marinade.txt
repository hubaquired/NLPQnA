Mojo Marinade recipe. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl and whisk until salt is dissolved. Reserve 2/3 cup marinade for sauce. Put pork or seafood in a glass, stainless-steel, or ceramic dish. Toss with remaining marinade. Cover; chill for 3-8 hours. Remove pork or seafood from marinade, pat dry, and grill. Spoon reserved sauce over meat or fish just before serving. Mojo Marinade has 96.0 calories. Mojo Marinade has 2.0 grams of protein. Mojo Marinade has 1.0 grams of fat. Mojo Marinade has 722.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mojo Marinade are 1 1/2 cups fresh orange juice, 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, 1/2 cup fresh oregano, coarsely chopped, 1/3 cup corn or vegetable oil, 10 garlic cloves, minced, 3 jalapeños, sliced into rounds, 1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt.Enough to marinate 2 pounds of pork (tenderloin, shoulder, or bone-in chops) or fish (firm, white-fleshed fish such as sea bass; shrimp or scallops).

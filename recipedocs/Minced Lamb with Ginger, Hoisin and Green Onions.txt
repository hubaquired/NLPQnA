Minced Lamb with Ginger, Hoisin and Green Onions recipe.  Combine orange juice and cornstarch in small bowl. Sauté lamb in heavy large skillet over high heat until cooked through, breaking up with back of spoon, about 5 minutes. Pour lamb with its juices into colander; drain. Heat oil in same skillet over high heat. Add ginger, garlic and orange peel; stir-fry 30 seconds. Add green onions 30 seconds. Add hoisin sauce and lamb to skillet; stir until blended. Add orange juice mixture; stir until thickened, about 1 minute. Spoon into lettuce leaves. Minced Lamb with Ginger, Hoisin and Green Onions has 417.0 calories. The ingredients of Minced Lamb with Ginger, Hoisin and Green Onions are 2 tablespoons orange juice, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 pound ground lamb, 1 tablespoon oriental sesame oil, 2 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic, 1 tablespoon minced orange peel, 1 bunch green onions, chopped, 1/4 cup hoisin sauce, Butter lettuce leaves.Serve this quick stir-fry with rice.

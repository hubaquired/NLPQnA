Island Rum Punch recipe. Stir together all ingredients except nutmeg in a pitcher. Serve over ice, sprinkled with a bit of nutmeg. Island Rum Punch has 176.0 calories. Island Rum Punch has 1.0 grams of protein. Island Rum Punch has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Island Rum Punch are (12-oz) can guava nectar, 1 1/2 cups fresh orange juice, 1 1/2 cups unsweetened pineapple juice, 1 1/2 cups rum, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice, 8 drops Angostura bitters, Freshly grated nutmeg to taste, Garnish: lime slices.

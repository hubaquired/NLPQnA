Salsa Verde recipe.  Combine all ingredients in processor. Using on/off turns, process until coarse paste forms. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill.) Salsa Verde has 356.0 calories. Salsa Verde has 3.0 grams of protein. Salsa Verde has 37.0 grams of fat. Salsa Verde has 160.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Salsa Verde are 3 cups loosely packed fresh Italian parsley leaves (from about 1 very large bunch), 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 3 green onions, sliced, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 3 garlic cloves, peeled, 2 drained anchovy fillets (optional), 1 tablespoon drained capers, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 1 1/2 teaspoons grated lemon peel.

Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish recipe. Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish Use this versatile relish alongside roast chicken or pork, or as a pizza topping. Preheat oven to 400°F. Brush bell peppers with 2 tablespoons oil; arrange on baking sheet. Roast until peppers are tender and begin to brown, turning occasionally, about 35 minutes. Cool peppers 10 minutes. Peel, seed and slice peppers. Heat remaining 4 tablespoons oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add tomatoes and garlic. Sauté until tomatoes soften, about 5 minutes. Add peppers to skillet; sauté about 3 minutes. Add cilantro and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Serve at room temperature. Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish has 178.0 calories. Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish has 2.0 grams of protein. Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish has 14.0 grams of fat. Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bell Pepper and Tomato Relish are 3 large red bell peppers, 3 large green bell peppers, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 3/4 pound plum tomatoes, seeded, chopped, 6 large garlic cloves, finely chopped, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar.

Chilled Watermelon Soup recipe. Combine 1 cup of the watermelon with mint and sugar in a bowl. Set aside. Blend remaining 8 cups watermelon, lemon juice, wine, and ginger in a blender until smooth. Let sit 1/2 hour. Strain soup; divide among 8 bowls. Top each with 1/8 cup reserved watermelon and 1 teaspoon feta. The ingredients of Chilled Watermelon Soup are 6 pounds yellow or red seedless watermelon, diced (9 cups), 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, 1 tablespoon sugar, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 cup lightly sweet white wine (such as Riesling) or 3/4 cup water mixed with 1/4 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon chopped ginger, 8 teaspoons crumbled feta, 1/4 cup sparkling wine (or sparkling water).Watermelon is a lycopene star — 1 cup has nearly 7 milligrams of the cancer fighter.

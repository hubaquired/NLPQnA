Peanut Butter and Grape Smoothie recipe.  Place ingredients in blender in the order listed and blend until smooth. The ingredients of Peanut Butter and Grape Smoothie are 1 tablespoon peanut butter, 1 1/2 cups frozen seedless grapes, 1/2 banana, sliced, 1/2 cup low-fat Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup almond milk, or regular low-fat milk.The classic combination of peanut butter and grape jelly inspired this kid-friendly smoothie.

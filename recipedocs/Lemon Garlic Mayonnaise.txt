Lemon Garlic Mayonnaise recipe. Remove crusts, then soak the bread in the milk for 1 minute; squeeze bread well and place in the bowl of a food processor with garlic and lemon zest. Pulse the machine on and off about 5 times to combine ingredients. Add the mayonnaise and yogurt; process until smooth. With the motor running, drizzle in olive oil through the feed tube and process until smooth. Remove the mixture to a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Serve with the Lemon Grilled Shrimp Cocktail. The ingredients of Lemon Garlic Mayonnaise are 2 slices white bread, torn into large pieces, 1/2 cup milk, 2 cloves of garlic, minced, Zest from 2 lemons, 1 cup mayonnaise, 1/2 cup plain nonfat yogurt, 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, Salt and pepper, to taste.Yogurt gives this dressing tang and lightens it a bit for the weight-conscious.

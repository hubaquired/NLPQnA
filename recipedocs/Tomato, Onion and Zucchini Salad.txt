Tomato, Onion and Zucchini Salad recipe.  Place alternating slices of tomatoes, zucchini and onion on serving platter. Sprinkle with herbs. Whisk together oil and vinegar and pour over salad. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 and up to 4 hours. Tomato, Onion and Zucchini Salad has 242.0 calories. Tomato, Onion and Zucchini Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Tomato, Onion and Zucchini Salad has 21.0 grams of fat. Tomato, Onion and Zucchini Salad has 599.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato, Onion and Zucchini Salad are 2 large tomatoes,sliced, 2 medium zucchini, sliced, 1 large onion, thinly sliced, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil or 1 teaspoon dried, crumbled, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano or 1 teaspoon dried, crumbled, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar or 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, Salt and pepper.

Lemon-Mint Julep recipe. Using back of spoon, mash mint leaves, sugar, and lemon juice in small bowl. Let stand 15 minutes. Strain mixture into tall glass, pressing on mint with back of spoon. Mix in bourbon. Fill with ice. Garnish with fresh mint sprig and serve. Lemon-Mint Julep has 248.0 calories. Lemon-Mint Julep has 1.0 grams of protein. Lemon-Mint Julep has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon-Mint Julep are 1/4 cup mint leaves, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/3 cup bourbon, resh mint sprig for garnish.

The ingredients of Italian Vegetable Salad with Creamy Garlic Dressing are 2 medium fennel bulbs, 1/4 head cauliflower, broken into large florets, 1/2 pound asparagus, trimmed, 3 large egg yolks, 1 garlic clove, grated (use a Microplane), 2 flat anchovy fillets in oil, chopped, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1/2 cup vegetable oil.Consider this a reintroduction to some old standbys, because cauliflower, asparagus, and fennel taste utterly new with a mellow garlicky dressing. Food editor Kay Chun, who developed the recipe, prefers to use vegetable oil for its light, neutral flavor, which allows the garlic and the deep savor of anchovies to shine. Tossing the salad right before serving ensures that it retains its crunch.

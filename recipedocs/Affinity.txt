Affinity recipe.  Combine all ingredients except the peel and cherry in a mixing glass and stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with the peel and cherry. This drink is sometimes made with orange bitters instead of Angostura bitters, and the peel and cherry are omitted. Affinity has 100.0 calories. Affinity has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Affinity are 3/4 ounce Scotch whisky, 3/4 ounce dry vermouth, 3/4 ounce sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura bitters (or orange bitters), 3 or 4 ice cubes, Twist lemon peel, Maraschino cherry.

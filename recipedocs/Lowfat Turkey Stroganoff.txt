Lowfat Turkey Stroganoff recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add turkey and onion and cook until turkey is brown, stirring occasionally, about 12 minutes. Mix in mushrooms, wine and nutmeg. Season turkey with salt and pepper. Reduce heat. Simmer 15 minutes. Puree cottage cheese, yogurt and lemon juice in blender or processor until smooth. Add to turkey and stir until heated through do not boil). Place fettuccine on platter. Pour turkey over. Sprinkle with paprika. Lowfat Turkey Stroganoff has 530.0 calories. Lowfat Turkey Stroganoff has 38.0 grams of protein. Lowfat Turkey Stroganoff has 18.0 grams of fat. Lowfat Turkey Stroganoff has 848.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lowfat Turkey Stroganoff are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 pound ground turkey, 1 medium onion, chopped, 2 cups sliced mushrooms, 1/3 cup dry white wine, Dash of nutmeg, Salt and pepper, 1 cup lowfat cottage cheese, 1/2 cup plain lowfat yogurt, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 8 ounces fettuccine, freshly cooked, Paprika.A mixture of cottage cheese, lowfat yogurt and lemon juice replaces the sour cream in the traditional version of this dish.

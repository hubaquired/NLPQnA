Goat Cheese, Salami, and Tomato on Mini-Bagels recipe.  Spread the bagel halves with the goat cheese, top each half with 2 salami triangles, 3 slices of tomato, and a basil sprig, and sprinkle the toppings lightly with pepper. Goat Cheese, Salami, and Tomato on Mini-Bagels has 209.0 calories. Goat Cheese, Salami, and Tomato on Mini-Bagels has 11.0 grams of protein. Goat Cheese, Salami, and Tomato on Mini-Bagels has 9.0 grams of fat. Goat Cheese, Salami, and Tomato on Mini-Bagels has 456.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Goat Cheese, Salami, and Tomato on Mini-Bagels are 10 mini-bagels, halved horizontally and toasted lightly, 6 ounces soft mild goat cheese, 10 slices of hard salami, each quartered into triangles, 60 slices of cherry tomato (about 20 tomatoes), 20 small basil sprigs.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

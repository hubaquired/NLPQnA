Basil and Garlic Mayonnaise recipe. Combine basil, garlic, and red wine vinegar in a processor. Blend until basil is finely chopped. Add mayonnaise and blend until smooth. Basil and Garlic Mayonnaise has 391.0 calories. Basil and Garlic Mayonnaise has 2.0 grams of protein. Basil and Garlic Mayonnaise has 39.0 grams of fat. Basil and Garlic Mayonnaise has 848.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Basil and Garlic Mayonnaise are 1/2 cup (packed) basil leaves, torn, 1/2 garlic clove, peeled, 1 1/2 teaspoons red wine vinegar, 1/2 cup light mayonnaise.

Honey and Soy Glazed Salmon recipe. In a small bowl whisk together honey, soy sauce, lime juice, mustard, and water. In a small non-stick skillet heat oil over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking and cook salmon 2 to 3 minutes on each side, or until golden and just cooked through. Transfer salmon to 2 plates. Add honey glaze to skillet and simmer, stirring, 1 minute. Pour glaze over salmon. Honey and Soy Glazed Salmon has 473.0 calories. Honey and Soy Glazed Salmon has 36.0 grams of protein. Honey and Soy Glazed Salmon has 28.0 grams of fat. Honey and Soy Glazed Salmon has 1036.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honey and Soy Glazed Salmon are 2 tablespoons honey, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1 tablespoon water, 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, two 6-ounce pieces salmon fillet.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

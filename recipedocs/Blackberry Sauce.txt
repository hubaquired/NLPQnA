Blackberry Sauce recipe. Puree all ingredients in processor. Strain into medium bowl, pressing on solids to extract as much liquid as possible. Cover and refrigerate until cold. Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Blackberry Sauce has 195.0 calories. Blackberry Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Blackberry Sauce has 1.0 grams of fat. Blackberry Sauce has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blackberry Sauce are 1 16-ounce bag frozen unsweetened blackberries, thawed, 3/4 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 to 2 tablespoons brandy (optional).This sauce accompanies Ginger, Fig and Cranberry Semifreddo . It's also great drizzled over vanilla ice cream.

Golden Fish Sticks recipe. Position rack in top third of oven and preheat to 500° F. Mix mayonnaise, relish and 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice in small bowl. Season sauce to taste with salt and pepper. Grind cornflakes in processor until coarse crumbs form. Transfer to bowl; mix in lemon peel. Mix butter and 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice in small bowl. Season fish with salt and pepper. Brush with lemon butter, then dip into cornflake mixture, coating completely. Arrange fish on baking sheet. Sprinkle with any remaining cornflake mixture. Bake fish until cooked through, about 10 minutes. Serve with sauce. Golden Fish Sticks has 533.0 calories. Golden Fish Sticks has 37.0 grams of protein. Golden Fish Sticks has 29.0 grams of fat. Golden Fish Sticks has 542.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Golden Fish Sticks are 2 1/2 tablespoons regular or low-fat mayonnaise, 1 1/2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish, 3 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 2 1/2 cups cornflakes, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, melted, 3/4 pound white fish fillets (such as orange roughy), cut crosswise into  3/4-inch-wide strips.We recommend french fries and a watercress and pickled beet salad as accompaniments; try chocolate cupcakes for dessert.

Sour Cherry Syrup recipe. In a medium saucepan set over medium heat, combine the cherries, sugar, and lemon juice and bring to a boil. Simmer for 30 minutes. Strain the syrup through a fine-mesh strainer and discard the fruit solids. Store the syrup in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 7 days. Sour Cherry Syrup has 327.0 calories. Sour Cherry Syrup has 1.0 grams of protein. Sour Cherry Syrup has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sour Cherry Syrup are 2 quarts fresh sour cherries, pitted, 2 cups sugar, Juice of 1/2 lemon.Since the fruit is tart and acidic, the taste for sour cherries right off the tree is an acquired one. When tamed with sweeteners, like sugar or honey, this fruit becomes a natural for liqueurs, preserves, and syrups.  Fruit Cherry Spring Summer Chill Simmer Boil Drink.

Tropical Smoothie recipe. Blend together papaya, mango, lime juice, water, and ginger in a blender until smooth. Add sorbet and ice and blend until smooth. Tropical Smoothie has 172.0 calories. Tropical Smoothie has 1.0 grams of protein. Tropical Smoothie has 1.0 grams of fat. Tropical Smoothie has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tropical Smoothie are 1 (1-lb) ripe papaya, peeled, seeded, and chopped (2 cups), 1 ripe mango, peeled, pitted, and chopped (2 cups), 5 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1/3 cup water, 2 teaspoons chopped peeled fresh ginger, 1 cup coconut sorbet, 1 cup ice cubes.

Panfried Tofu on Sesame Watercress with Soy-Orange Dressing recipe. Pat tofu dry. Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over moderately high heat until hot but not smoking, then saut√© tofu until golden brown, about 3 minutes on each side.

Basic Polenta recipe.  Combine 8 cups water and salt in heavy larger saucepan. Bring to a boil. Gradually add cornmeal, whisking until smooth. reduce heat to low. Cover and cook until polenta is thick and creamy, stirring frequently, about 30 minutes. Basic Polenta has 194.0 calories. Basic Polenta has 4.0 grams of protein. Basic Polenta has 1.0 grams of fat. Basic Polenta has 791.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Basic Polenta are 8 cups water, 2 teaspoons salt, 2 cups polenta (coarse cornmeal).This soft polenta is good topped with parmesan and fresh thyme and rosemary.

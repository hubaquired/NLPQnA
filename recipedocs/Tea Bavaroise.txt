Tea Bavaroise recipe. In a large bowl, beat the egg yolks with a pinch of salt until they are frothy. Beat in the sugar, a tablespoon at a time, and continue to beat the egg yolks until they are thick and light. Beat in the dark rum, the tea, and the scalded milk. Transfer the mixture to a heat-proof punch bowl, rinsed first with boiling water. Tea Bavaroise has 137.0 calories. Tea Bavaroise has 1.0 grams of protein. Tea Bavaroise has 1.0 grams of fat. Tea Bavaroise has 35.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tea Bavaroise are 8 egg yolks, Pinch of salt, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 cups dark rum, warmed, 1 cup of hot strong tea, 1 cup of scalded milk.

Creamed Spinach with Chère recipe. Preheat oven to 250°F. Bring 2 inches of water to boil in large pot. Add spinach and cook just until wilted, constantly pressing spinach down into water, about 2 minutes. Transfer 1/2 cup cooking liquid to medium skillet and reserve. Drain spinach into colander, firmly pressing out excess water. Transfer spinach to oven-proof platter. Place in oven to keep warm. Add cream, goat cheese, shallot and lemon peel to cooking liquid in skillet. Stir over medium-high heat until sauce is thick and reduced to 2/3 cup, about 6 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Pour sauce evenly over spinach. Serve immediately. Creamed Spinach with Chère has 145.0 calories. Creamed Spinach with Chère has 10.0 grams of protein. Creamed Spinach with Chère has 8.0 grams of fat. Creamed Spinach with Chère has 241.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamed Spinach with Chère are 3 10-ounce packages ready-to-use spinach leaves, 1/2 cup whipping cream, 2 ounces soft fresh goat cheese (such as Montrachet), 1 large shallot, minced, 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel.

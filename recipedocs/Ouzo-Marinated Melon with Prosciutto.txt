Ouzo-Marinated Melon with Prosciutto recipe. Fill melon cavity with ouzo and sprinkle with aniseed. Cover and chill 1 day. Pour juices from melon into small bowl and reserve. Cut melon into 4 wedges. Transfer to plates and drizzle with reserved juices. Drape prosciutto over melon. Season with pepper. Garnish with mint sprigs and serve. The ingredients of Ouzo-Marinated Melon with Prosciutto are 1/2 cantaloupe, 2 tablespoons ouzo (unsweetened anise liqueur), 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped aniseed, 8 thin sliced prosciutto or Parma ham, Fresh mint sprigs.Ouzo lends a subtle flavor to the melon.

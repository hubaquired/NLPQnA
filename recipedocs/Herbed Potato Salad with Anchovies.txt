Herbed Potato Salad with Anchovies recipe. Place potatoes in large pot. Add enough cold water to cover. Boil until just tender, about 30 minutes. Drain. Cool until just warm. Peel potatoes and cut into slices. Set aside. Combine remaining ingredients in large bowl. Add potato slices and toss well. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Refrigerate until well chilled. Herbed Potato Salad with Anchovies has 171.0 calories. Herbed Potato Salad with Anchovies has 3.0 grams of protein. Herbed Potato Salad with Anchovies has 9.0 grams of fat. Herbed Potato Salad with Anchovies has 112.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Herbed Potato Salad with Anchovies are 1 1/2 pounds red potatoes, 4 green onions, sliced, 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1/2-ounce can flat anchovy fillets, drained, chopped, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup red wine vinegar.

Carrot-Ginger Elixir recipe. Bring sugar, ginger, and 1/3 cup water to a boil in a small saucepan, stirring until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat; let steep for 10 minutes. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a medium pitcher you should have 1/2 cup); discard ginger. Cover and chill syrup. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 week ahead. Keep chilled. Fill four 6-ounce glasses with ice cubes. Add carrot juice and lime juice to ginger syrup in pitcher; stir well. Divide elixir among prepared glasses. Carrot-Ginger Elixir has 98.0 calories. Carrot-Ginger Elixir has 1.0 grams of protein. Carrot-Ginger Elixir has 40.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Carrot-Ginger Elixir are 1/3 cup raw sugar, 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped peeled ginger, 1 cup fresh carrot juice, 1/2 cup fresh lime juice.We've been making chef Scott Howard's ginger syrup in bulk and keeping it in the fridge for quick tonics and late- evening cocktails. If you have a juicer at home, by all means use it, but good store-bought 100 percent carrot juice will work just as well here.

Jellied Cranberry and Port Sauce recipe.  In a large saucepan combine the cranberries, the sugar, the Port, the zest, and the lemon juice, simmer the mixture, stirring occasionally, for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the berries have burst and the mixture has thickened, and let it cool. Spoon the mixture into a lightly oiled 1-quart decorative mold and chill it, covered, for at least 3 hours or overnight. Run a thin knife around the edge of the mold, dip the mold into warm water for 10 seconds, and invert it onto a serving plate. Jellied Cranberry and Port Sauce has 186.0 calories. Jellied Cranberry and Port Sauce has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Jellied Cranberry and Port Sauce are a 12-ounce bag of cranberries, picked over, 1 1/2 cups sugar, or to taste, 1 cup Tawny Port, 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, or to taste.

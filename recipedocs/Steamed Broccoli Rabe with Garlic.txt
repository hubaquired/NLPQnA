Steamed Broccoli Rabe with Garlic recipe. In a 12-inch skillet bring water and soy sauce with garlic to a boil. Add broccoli rabe and cook, covered, over moderately high heat 2 minutes. Remove cover and cook until water is evaporated and broccoli rabe is tender. Season broccoli rabe with salt. The ingredients of Steamed Broccoli Rabe with Garlic are 1/2 cup water, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 large garlic clove, sliced thin, 1 bunch broccoli rabe, hollow or coarse stems discarded.

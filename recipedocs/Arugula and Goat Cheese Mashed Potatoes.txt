Arugula and Goat Cheese Mashed Potatoes recipe. Cook potatoes in large pot of boiling salted water until tender, about 30 minutes. Drain. Return potatoes to pot. Stir over low heat until excess moisture evaporates. Add butter; mash potatoes until smooth. Bring milk to simmer in saucepan. Remove from heat. Add goat cheese; whisk until melted. Add milk mixture to potatoes; whisk until smooth. Stir in arugula. Season with salt and pepper. Arugula and Goat Cheese Mashed Potatoes has 341.0 calories. Arugula and Goat Cheese Mashed Potatoes has 11.0 grams of protein. Arugula and Goat Cheese Mashed Potatoes has 15.0 grams of fat. Arugula and Goat Cheese Mashed Potatoes has 144.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Arugula and Goat Cheese Mashed Potatoes are 3 pounds (about 5 large) russet potatoes, peeled, quartered, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, 1 1/4 cups whole milk, 5 ounces soft fresh goat cheese (such as Montrachet), crumbled, 1 cup (packed) chopped arugula leaves (about 2 large bunches).Terrific with roast chicken, lamb or steak.

Salmon with Arugula, Tomato and Caper Sauce recipe.  Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Preheat broiler. Brush both sides of salmon with oil; season with salt and pepper. Broil without turning until just cooked through, about 4 minutes. Transfer salmon to plates. Spoon tomato mixture over.

Braised Onion Ribbons with Celery recipe. In a large heavy skillet cook the onions in the oil, covered, over moderately low heat, stirring occasionally, for 45 minutes. Season the onions with salt and pepper and cook them, uncovered, over moderately high heat, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes, or until they are tender and beginning to turn golden. Add the celery and cook the mixture, stirring, for 5 minutes, or until the celery is crisp-tender. Serves 6. Braised Onion Ribbons with Celery has 193.0 calories. Braised Onion Ribbons with Celery has 3.0 grams of protein. Braised Onion Ribbons with Celery has 9.0 grams of fat. Braised Onion Ribbons with Celery has 48.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Braised Onion Ribbons with Celery are 3 1/2 pounds onions, halved lengthwise and cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-thick slices, 1/4 cup olive oil, 7 celery ribs, cut diagonally into 1/2-inch-thick slices.

Dance with a Dream recipe. In a shaker half-filled with ice cubes, combine the ingredients. Shake well. Strain into a cocktail glass. The ingredients of Dance with a Dream are 2 ounces brandy, 1/2 ounce Cointreau or Triple Sec, 1 teaspoon anisette.

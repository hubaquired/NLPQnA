Smoked Salmon on Cream Cheese Toasts with Chives recipe. Spread one side of toasts with cream cheese and top with salmon, wrapping salmon ends under toasts. Sprinkle salmon with chives and red onion and halve toasts diagonally. Divide toasts and lemon wedges between 2 plates and serve with endive salad. The ingredients of Smoked Salmon on Cream Cheese Toasts with Chives are 3 slices firm white sandwich bread, toasted, 3 tablespoons cream cheese, softened, 6 thin slices smoked salmon (about 6 ounces total), 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives, 1 teaspoon finely chopped red onion, 4 lemon wedges, Accompaniment if desired: Slivered Endive Salad.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

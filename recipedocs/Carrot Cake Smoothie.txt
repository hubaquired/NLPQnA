Carrot Cake Smoothie recipe.  Place ingredients in blender in the order listed and blend until smooth. Carrot Cake Smoothie has 403.0 calories. Carrot Cake Smoothie has 20.0 grams of protein. Carrot Cake Smoothie has 20.0 grams of fat. Carrot Cake Smoothie has 233.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Carrot Cake Smoothie are 2 tablespoons unsweetened shredded coconut (or flakes), 2 tablespoons roughly chopped walnuts, 3/4 cup grated carrot (1 large carrot), 1/2 cup frozen peeled orange segments, 1/2 banana, sliced and frozen, 1/2 cup low-fat Greek yogurt, 1 teaspoon honey, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/2 cup coconut water.This smoothie has all the familiar flavors of carrot cake: sweet coconut, buttery walnuts, fragrant cinnamon, and warm vanilla.

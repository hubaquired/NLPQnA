Mango and Passion Fruit Smoothie recipe. Combine all ingredients in blender and process until smooth. Divide smoothie between 2 glasses and serve. The ingredients of Mango and Passion Fruit Smoothie are 2 cups 1/2-inch cubes peeled mango, 2 cups crushed ice, 5 tablespoons strained passion fruit juice (from about 8 passion fruits; seeds discarded), 1/4 cup coconut water (such as Vita Coco), 1 teaspoon honey.Not to be confused with coconut milk, coconut water is the tart liquid found in the center of young coconuts.

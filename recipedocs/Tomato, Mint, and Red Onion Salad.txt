Tomato, Mint, and Red Onion Salad recipe.  Cut the tomatoes into 1/4-inch-thick slices and arrange half the slices in one layer in a shallow serving dish. In a small bowl combine the sugar and the salt and sprinkle the layer of tomatoes with half the mixture. Arrange the remaining tomato slices on top and sprinkle them with the remaining sugar mixture. Drizzle the tomatoes with the oil and the vinegar and let the salad stand at room temperature for 30 minutes. Sprinkle the salad with the mint and arrange the onion, drained and separated into rings, on top. Tomato, Mint, and Red Onion Salad has 59.0 calories. Tomato, Mint, and Red Onion Salad has 1.0 grams of protein. Tomato, Mint, and Red Onion Salad has 4.0 grams of fat. Tomato, Mint, and Red Onion Salad has 152.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato, Mint, and Red Onion Salad are 1 pound tomatoes (about 3), 1/4 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 teaspoon red-wine vinegar, 8 fresh mint leaves, sliced thin, 1 small red onion, sliced thin and soaked in ice water for 30 minutes.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

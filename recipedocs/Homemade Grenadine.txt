Homemade Grenadine recipe. Bring juice to simmer over medium heat and cook until reduced by half, about 7 minutes. Reduce heat and add sugar, stirring constantly until dissolved, about 2 minutes. Let cool, cover, and refrigerate syrup until cold, about 30 minutes. Can be made 1 week ahead. Keep chilled.) Homemade Grenadine has 210.0 calories. Homemade Grenadine has 8.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Homemade Grenadine are 6 ounces unsweetened pomegranate juice, such as Elite Naturel (www.organicjuiceusa.com), 3 ounces superfine sugar.Audrey Saunders, owner Pegu Club, in Manhattan, uses this grenadine in several cocktails, including the Jack Rose, which was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.

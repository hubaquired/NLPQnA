Cherry-Chocolate Chunk Ice Cream recipe.  Puree cherries in processor until almost smooth but some small pieces remain. Transfer to bowl. Add kirsch and let stand at room temperature 1 hour. Place ice cream in large bowl. Stir in cherry mixture and chocolate. Cover and freeze until firm, about 4 hours. Cherry-Chocolate Chunk Ice Cream has 233.0 calories. Cherry-Chocolate Chunk Ice Cream has 3.0 grams of protein. Cherry-Chocolate Chunk Ice Cream has 12.0 grams of fat. Cherry-Chocolate Chunk Ice Cream has 54.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cherry-Chocolate Chunk Ice Cream are 2 cups pitted fresh sweet cherries, 2 tablespoons kirsch (clear cherry brandy), 1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened, 4 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) or semisweet chocolate,coarsely chopped.A classic flavor combination-chocolate and cherries-turns everyday vanilla ice cream into a special dessert.

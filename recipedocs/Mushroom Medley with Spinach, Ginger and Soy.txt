Mushroom Medley with Spinach, Ginger and Soy recipe.  Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper, ginger and garlic and sauté 1 minute. Add all mushrooms and sauté until brown and tender, adding 2 to 3 tablespoons water if mixture is dry, about 10 minutes. Add spinach and green onions and stir until spinach wilts, about 2 minutes. Stir in soy sauce. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl. Serve, passing lemon wedges to squeeze over. Mushroom Medley with Spinach, Ginger and Soy has 103.0 calories. Mushroom Medley with Spinach, Ginger and Soy has 7.0 grams of protein. Mushroom Medley with Spinach, Ginger and Soy has 3.0 grams of fat. Mushroom Medley with Spinach, Ginger and Soy has 295.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mushroom Medley with Spinach, Ginger and Soy are 2 teaspoons oriental sesame oil, 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips, 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger, 1 1/2 tablespoons minced garlic, 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stems trimmed, caps sliced, 8 ounces button mushrooms, sliced, 4 ounces small oyster mushrooms, 1 10-ounce package ready-to-use spinach leaves, stems trimmed, 6 green onions, cut into thin strips, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, Lemon wedges.This colorful and delicious dish is perfect with fish, meat or poultry.

Tequila Cooler recipe.  In a tall glass three-fourths full of ice cubes, stir together tequila and Cuarenta y Tres and top off with seltzer or club soda. The ingredients of Tequila Cooler are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) tequila, 1 to 1 1/2 ounces Cuarenta y Tres (Spanish herbal fruit liqueur), Seltzer or club soda.

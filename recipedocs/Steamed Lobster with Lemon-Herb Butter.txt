Steamed Lobster with Lemon-Herb Butter recipe.  Cook lobsters in 2 large pots of boiling salted water until shells are pink and lobsters are just cooked through, about 11 minutes. Meanwhile, melt butter in small saucepan. Add lemon juice and herbs. Season with salt and pepper. Serve lobsters with lemon wedges and warm herb butter.

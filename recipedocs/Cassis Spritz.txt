Cassis Spritz recipe.  Pour 4 ounces dry Prosecco or other sparkling white wine into an ice-filled large wine or rocks glass. Add 1 ounce crème de cassis blackcurrant liqueur) and top off with club soda. Gently stir together; garnish with blackberries. The ingredients of Cassis Spritz are 4 ounces dry Prosecco or other sparkling white wine, 1 ounce crème de cassis (blackcurrant liqueur), Club soda, Blackberries.

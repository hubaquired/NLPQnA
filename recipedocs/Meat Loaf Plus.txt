Meat Loaf Plus recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Mix thoroughly. Transfer mixture to greased 8 1/2x4 1/2-inch loaf pan. Bake until meat loaf shrinks from sides of pan and top is brown, about 1 hour. Turn out onto platter and serve. Meat Loaf Plus has 501.0 calories. Meat Loaf Plus has 27.0 grams of protein. Meat Loaf Plus has 33.0 grams of fat. Meat Loaf Plus has 1228.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Meat Loaf Plus are 1 pound plus 2 ounces ground beef, 1 cup herbed seasoned stuffing, 2 eggs, beaten to blend, 1/2 cup dry red wine, 10 pimiento-stuffed olives, chopped, 3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley, 1 envelope onion soup mix, 2 garlic cloves, minced, Salt and pepper.

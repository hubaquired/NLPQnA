Wild Heart Cocktail recipe.  Combine Aperol, vermouth, lemon juice, and bitters in a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice and shake until outside of shaker is frosty, about 30 seconds. Strain into an ice-filled Old-Fashioned glass. Top with lemon soda and garnish with orange wheel. Wild Heart Cocktail has 111.0 calories. Wild Heart Cocktail has 1.0 grams of protein. Wild Heart Cocktail has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Wild Heart Cocktail are 1 1/4 ounces Aperol, 1 1/4 ounces vermouth (such as Dolin Blanc), 3/4 ounce fresh lemon juice, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, Bitter lemon soda (such as San Pellegrino Limonata or Fever-Tree Bitter Lemon), 1/2 orange wheel.The delicate orange flavor of Aperol melds well with bitter lemon soda in this crisp cocktail.

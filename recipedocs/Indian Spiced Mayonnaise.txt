Indian Spiced Mayonnaise recipe.  Combine 1 cup mint leaves, 1 cup cilantro leaves, minced jalapeño chili and chopped onion in work bowl of processor. Process until very finely chopped. Mix in cider vinegar. Add mayonnaise and process just until combined. Season mayonnaise to taste with salt and pepper. Mayonnaise can be prepared 3 days ahead. Cover tightly and refrigerate.) The ingredients of Indian Spiced Mayonnaise are 1 cup packed fresh mint leaves, 1 cup packed fresh cilantro leaves, 1 jalapeño chili, seeded, minced, 3 tablespoons chopped onion, 2 teaspoons cider vinegar, 1/2 cup mayonnaise.This zesty condiment is a wonderful addition to sandwiches.

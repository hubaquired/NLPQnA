Tropical Storm recipe.  Mix 2 cups fresh pineapple juice, 2 cups orange juice, 1 cup white rum, 3/4 cup high-proof dark rum, and 3 tablespoons Campari in a pitcher. Cover; chill for 4-12 hours. Divide among ice-filled glasses. Garnish with orange wedges and maraschino cherries. The ingredients of Tropical Storm are 2 cups fresh pineapple juice, 2 cups orange juice, 1 cup white rum, 3/4 cup high-proof dark rum, 3 tablespoons Campari, Orange wedges, Maraschino cherries.The key to this drink is fresh pineapple juice.

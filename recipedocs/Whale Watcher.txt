Whale Watcher recipe.  Combine sugar and water in small bowl and stir until sugar dissolves. Cool. Transfer to blender. Add all remaining ingredients and blend until smooth. Divide among 4 glasses. Whale Watcher has 86.0 calories. Whale Watcher has 3.0 grams of fat. Whale Watcher has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Whale Watcher are 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon hot water, 2 cups ice cubes, 1/2 cup grapefruit juice, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons amber rum, 2 tablespoons crème de banana, 2 tablespoons Myer's Original Rum Cream, 1 tablespoon grenadine.

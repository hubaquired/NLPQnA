Orange Vodka Cocktail recipe.  In a blender, blend cracked ice, vodka, curaçao, lemon juice and, if desired, sugar for 30 seconds. Pour into a chilled 12-ounce glass. Garnish with the slice of orange. Orange Vodka Cocktail has 209.0 calories. Orange Vodka Cocktail has 1.0 grams of protein. Orange Vodka Cocktail has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orange Vodka Cocktail are 1 cup cracked ice, 6 tablespoons orange juice, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) vodka, 1 pony (1 ounce) orange curaçao, 1 pony (1 ounce) lemon juice, 1 tablespoon superfine granulated sugar, or to taste, Garnish: slice of orange.

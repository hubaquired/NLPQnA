Maryland Eggnog recipe. Beat the eggs and the sugar until the mixture is a light foam. Stir in the heavy cream, milk, brandy, dark rum, and the ground nutmeg. Pour the ingredients from one vessel to another to mix them. Serve the eggnog when the surface is well frothed. Maryland Eggnog has 461.0 calories. Maryland Eggnog has 4.0 grams of protein. Maryland Eggnog has 32.0 grams of fat. Maryland Eggnog has 60.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Maryland Eggnog are 12 eggs, 1/2 cup fine granulated sugar, 4 quarts heavy cream, 1 quart milk, 1 1/2 quarts brandy, 1 pint dark rum, 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg.

Olives Baked in Red Wine recipe.  Preheat oven to 325°F. Combine olives, wine, fennel seeds, sliced garlic and olive oil in small baking dish. Bake uncovered until olives are heated through, about 20 minutes. Cool slightly. Serve olives warm. Olives Baked in Red Wine has 143.0 calories. Olives Baked in Red Wine has 1.0 grams of protein. Olives Baked in Red Wine has 11.0 grams of fat. Olives Baked in Red Wine has 443.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Olives Baked in Red Wine are 1 cup unpitted Kalamata olives or other brine-cured black olives, 1/2 cup dry red wine, 1/4 teaspoon fennel seeds, coarsely crushed, 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced, 2 teaspoons olive oil.Black olives absorb the flavor of red wine and fennel in this warm appetizer.

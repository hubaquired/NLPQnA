Campari Citrus Cooler recipe.  In a tall glass filled with ice, combine orange juice, grapefruit juice, vodka and Campari. Fill glass wth seltzer. Garnish with orange slice. Campari Citrus Cooler has 177.0 calories. Campari Citrus Cooler has 1.0 grams of protein. Campari Citrus Cooler has 23.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Campari Citrus Cooler are 1/4 cup orange juice, 1/4 cup grapefruit juice, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) vodka, 1 tablespoon Campari, or to taste, 1/3 to 1/2 cup chilled seltzer, Garnish: slice of orange.

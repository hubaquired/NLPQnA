Grog II recipe. In a glass or mug, combine the lemon juice, sugar lump, a clove studded lemon slice, strip of orange peel, and golden rum. Add boiling water to fill the mug and stir the drink with a cinnamon stick. The ingredients of Grog II are Juice of 1/2 lemon, Lump of sugar, Slice of lemon, studded with 3 cloves, Strip of orange peel, 1 jigger golden rum, Boiling water, Cinnamon stick. Rum Alcoholic Lemon Winter Cinnamon Clove Gourmet Drink.

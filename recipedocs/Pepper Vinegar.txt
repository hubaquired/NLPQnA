Pepper Vinegar recipe.  Combine 2 cups distilled white vinegar, 2 dried chiles de árbol, and 2 Scotch bonnet chiles or any of your favorite dried or fresh chiles), quartered lengthwise, in a 1-pint jar. Seal and shake. Let sit at room temperature for 1 week before using. Pepper Vinegar has 109.0 calories. Pepper Vinegar has 3.0 grams of protein. Pepper Vinegar has 1.0 grams of fat. Pepper Vinegar has 20.0 grams of sodium.

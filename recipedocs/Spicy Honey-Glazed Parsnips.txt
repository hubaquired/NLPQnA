Spicy Honey-Glazed Parsnips recipe.  Preheat oven to 450°F. Toss parsnips and oil on a rimmed baking sheet; season with salt and pepper. Roast parsnips, tossing occasionally, until tender and deep golden brown in spots, 35–40 minutes. Meanwhile, heat chiles de árbol, butter, vinegar, and honey in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until butter is melted. Drizzle chile-honey butter over parsnips and toss to coat. The ingredients of Spicy Honey-Glazed Parsnips are 2 pounds parsnips, peeled, cut into 3" lengths, halved, or quartered if large, 1/4 cup olive oil, Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper, 2 chiles de árbol, crushed, or 3/4 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon honey.Some parsnips can have a woody core, which you'll want to cut away before cooking.

Green Dragon I recipe.  Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake well. Strain into a cocktail glass. The ingredients of Green Dragon I are 1 1/2 ounce dry gin, 1 ounce green crème de menthe, 1/2 ounce kümmel, 1/2 ounce lemon juice, 4 dashes peach bitters, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

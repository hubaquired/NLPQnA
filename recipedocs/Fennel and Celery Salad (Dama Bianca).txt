Fennel and Celery Salad (Dama Bianca) recipe.  Halve fennel lengthwise, then thinly slice crosswise about 1/4 inch thick. Toss with celery and arrange on a platter with mozzarella. Whisk together zest, juice, oil, sea salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper and drizzle over salad. Fennel and Celery Salad (Dama Bianca) has 246.0 calories. Fennel and Celery Salad (Dama Bianca) has 9.0 grams of protein. Fennel and Celery Salad (Dama Bianca) has 22.0 grams of fat. Fennel and Celery Salad (Dama Bianca) has 365.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fennel and Celery Salad (Dama Bianca) are 2 medium fennel bulbs, stalks discarded, 6 pale inner (white) celery stalks, leaves discarded and stalks thinly sliced, 1 (1/2-pound) ball buffalo mozzarella (optional), roughly torn, 1/2 tablespoon grated lemon zest (preferably from an unwaxed organic lemon), 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 6 tablespoons good-quality fruity extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt.The inner stalks of celery and fennel, plus soft chunks of mozzarella, add up to a clean, cool salad that's quite striking (the name, which refers to a "woman in white," alludes to its pale hues).

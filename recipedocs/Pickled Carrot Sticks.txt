Pickled Carrot Sticks recipe.  Blanch carrots in a 4-quart nonreactive saucepan of boiling salted water 1 minute, then drain in a colander and rinse under cold water to stop cooking. Transfer carrots to a heatproof bowl. Bring remaining ingredients to a boil in saucepan, then reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes. Pour pickling liquid over carrots and cool, uncovered. Chill carrots, covered, at least 1 day for flavors to develop. Pickled Carrot Sticks has 47.0 calories. Pickled Carrot Sticks has 1.0 grams of protein. Pickled Carrot Sticks has 541.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pickled Carrot Sticks are 1 pound carrots, cut into 3 1/2- by 1/3-inch sticks, 1 1/4 cups water, 1 cup cider vinegar, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 garlic cloves, lightly crushed, 1 1/2 tablespoons dill seeds, 1 1/2 tablespoons salt.Zanne Stewart, Gourmet's executive food editor, originally developed these carrot sticks to take on a picnic, but they were such a hit they've become a staple in her refrigerator. Best of all, they don't need to be sealed in sterilized jars, so they're a snap to make.

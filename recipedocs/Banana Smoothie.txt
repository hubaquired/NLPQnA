Banana Smoothie recipe.  In a blender blend at high speed the bananas, the pineapple juice, the orange juice, the yogurt, and the honey until the mixture is smooth and frothy and divide the mixture among chilled glasses. Banana Smoothie has 138.0 calories. Banana Smoothie has 3.0 grams of protein. Banana Smoothie has 2.0 grams of fat. Banana Smoothie has 21.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Banana Smoothie are 2 bananas, 1 cup unsweetened pineapple juice, 1 cup fresh orange juice, 2/3 cup plain yogurt, honey to taste.

Casserole of Turkey with Rice recipe.  Sauté the onions in butter until just tender. Add the mushrooms, and sauté for 2 minutes or so. Combine in a casserole with the turkey, ham, stuffing, parsley, thyme, salt, and pepper. Add an additional tablespoon of fat and the curry powder to the skillet used for sautéing the onions and lightly sauté the rice. Add to the casserole and pour in the hot broth. Place in a preheated 375°F. oven to cook until rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed. Add more heated broth if necessary to finish cooking the rice. Casserole of Turkey with Rice has 502.0 calories. Casserole of Turkey with Rice has 30.0 grams of protein. Casserole of Turkey with Rice has 13.0 grams of fat. Casserole of Turkey with Rice has 1797.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Casserole of Turkey with Rice are 2 medium onions, chopped, Butter, 1/2 pound sliced mushrooms, 2 cups diced cold turkey, 1/2 cup diced ham, 1 cup crumbled leftover stuffing, 2 tablespoons chopped parsley, Pinch of thyme, Salt, freshly ground black pepper to taste, 1 tablespoon curry powder, 1 cup rice, 2 cups hot turkey or chicken broth.

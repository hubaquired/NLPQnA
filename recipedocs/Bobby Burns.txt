Bobby Burns recipe. Combine all ingredients with ice in a cocktail shaker. Stir and strain into a chilled martini glass. Twist lemon peel to release oil and then use as a garnish. Bobby Burns has 89.0 calories. Bobby Burns has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bobby Burns are 2 oz Monkey Shoulder Scotch, 3/4 oz Carpano Antica Formula sweet vermouth, 1/4 oz Bénédictine, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, Lemon peel.This clubby Scotch drink was created at the old Waldorf Astoria in New York City.

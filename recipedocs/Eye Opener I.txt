Eye Opener I recipe.  Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass. The ingredients of Eye Opener I are 1 1/2 ounces light rum, 1 teaspoon curaçao, 1 teaspoon crème de cacao, 1 teaspoon Pernod, 1/2 teaspoon superfine sugar, 1 egg yolk, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

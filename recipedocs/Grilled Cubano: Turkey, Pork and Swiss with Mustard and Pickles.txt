Grilled Cubano: Turkey, Pork and Swiss with Mustard and Pickles recipe. Cut roll. Spread mustard on top of roll. Place two slices of cheese onto each side of roll. Stack cheese, pork, turkey, and pickle onto bottom of roll. Close sandwich. Melt a tablespoon of butter in pan. Press down on sandwich and grill over moderate heat for approximately 3-4 minutes on both sides, pressing down firmly from time to time.

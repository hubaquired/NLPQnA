Spicy French Fries recipe.  In a large bowl stir together salt, cayenne, paprika, and coriander. In a 4-quart heavy kettle heat oil over moderate heat until a deep-fat thermometer registers 325°F. While oil is heating, cut potatoes lengthwise into 1/4-inch-thick sticks. Fry potatoes in oil 1 1/2 minutes potatoes will not be golden) and with a slotted spoon transfer to brown paper or paper towels to drain. Heat oil to 350°F. Return potatoes to oil and fry until golden and crisp, about 5 minutes. Transfer fries with a slotted spoon to clean brown paper or paper towels to drain and toss fries in spice mixture. Spicy French Fries has 364.0 calories. Spicy French Fries has 4.0 grams of protein. Spicy French Fries has 26.0 grams of fat. Spicy French Fries has 724.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy French Fries are 3/4 teaspoon coarse salt, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne, 1/8 teaspoon paprika, 1/8 teaspoon ground coriander seeds, 4 cups vegetable oil, 2 medium russet (baking) potatoes (about 12 ounces total).This recipe was created to accompany Garlic Dijon Skirt Steak.

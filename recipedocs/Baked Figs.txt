Baked Figs recipe. Preheat the oven to 170°C / 325°F / gas mark 3. Halve each fig lengthways through the stalk. Place the figs in a baking dish and sprinkle evenly with the sugar and port. Bake for about 40 minutes, or until the figs are soft with a good thick syrup. Baked Figs has 561.0 calories. Baked Figs has 1.0 grams of protein. Baked Figs has 32.0 grams of sodium.

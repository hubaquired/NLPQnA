Gremolata recipe. Using vegetable peeler, remove peel in long strips from lemon. Mince lemon peel. Transfer to small bowl. Mix in parsley and garlic. Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Gremolata has 18.0 calories. Gremolata has 1.0 grams of protein. Gremolata has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gremolata are 1 lemon, 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley, 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped.This would also be delicious sprinkled over roast meat, poultry and seafood.

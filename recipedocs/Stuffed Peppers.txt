Stuffed Peppers recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix first 9 ingredients in large bowl until well blended. Fill pepper halves with sausage mixture, dividing equally and mounding slightly. Arrange in 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill.) Bake peppers uncovered until tops are browned and thermometer inserted into filling registers 165°F, about 1 hour. Transfer peppers to platter. Garnish with rosemary sprigs and serve. Stuffed Peppers has 180.0 calories. Stuffed Peppers has 16.0 grams of protein. Stuffed Peppers has 8.0 grams of fat. Stuffed Peppers has 743.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stuffed Peppers are 1 1/2 pounds sweet Italian sausages, casings removed, 1 1/2 cups coarsely grated zucchini (about 1 large), 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion, 1/3 cup minced fresh parsley, 1/4 cup fine dry breadcrumbs, 1 large egg, 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary, 4 medium-size red bell peppers (each about 4 to 6 ounces), halved lengthwise, seeded, Fresh rosemary sprigs.Poivrons Farcis.

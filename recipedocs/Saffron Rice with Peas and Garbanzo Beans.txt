Saffron Rice with Peas and Garbanzo Beans recipe.  Cook fresh peas in medium saucepan of boiling salted water until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain and reserve. If using thawed peas, set aside. Place rice in sieve. Rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear. Drain. Melt butter in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and sauté until tender, about 8 minutes. Add rice; stir 1 minute. Add broth, salt and saffron; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir peas and garbanzo beans into rice. Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Transfer to bowl and serve. Saffron Rice with Peas and Garbanzo Beans has 385.0 calories. Saffron Rice with Peas and Garbanzo Beans has 11.0 grams of protein. Saffron Rice with Peas and Garbanzo Beans has 9.0 grams of fat. Saffron Rice with Peas and Garbanzo Beans has 436.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Saffron Rice with Peas and Garbanzo Beans are 2 cups shelled fresh peas or frozen, thawed, 2 cups basmati rice, 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) butter, 1 large onion, finely chopped, 3 cups canned low-salt chicken broth, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads, 1 15- to 16-ounce can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), rinsed, drained.

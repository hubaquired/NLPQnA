Chicken Salad with Cilantro and Ginger recipe. Combine soy sauce, oriental sesame oil, vinegar and ginger in small bowl. Cut chicken crosswise into 1/4-inch-wide strips. Place chicken in medium bowl. Mix in 1/4 cup of soy mixture. Let chicken marinate 30 minutes. Heat vegetable oil and chili oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken to skillet and cook until springy to touch, stirring constantly, about 2 minutes. Transfer chicken to plate and cool. Combine lettuce, cilantro, onion and tomato in large bowl and arrange on platter. Top mixture with chicken. Sprinkle salad with sesame seeds and almonds. Spoon some soy mixture over chicken salad. Serve, passing remaining soy mixture separately. Chicken Salad with Cilantro and Ginger has 470.0 calories. Chicken Salad with Cilantro and Ginger has 34.0 grams of protein. The ingredients of Chicken Salad with Cilantro and Ginger are 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup oriental sesame oil*, 2 tablespoons rice vinegar, 2 teaspoons grated peeled fresh ginger, 1 pound skinless boneless chicken breast halves, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1/2 teaspoon chili oil*, 1 head romaine lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces, 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced, 1 large ripe tomato, sliced, 1/4 cup sesame seeds, toasted, 1/4 cup slivered almonds, toasted, *Available at specialty foods stores, oriental markets and in the oriental section of some supermarkets..

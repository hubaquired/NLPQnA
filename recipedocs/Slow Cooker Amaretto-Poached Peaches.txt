Slow Cooker Amaretto-Poached Peaches recipe.  Toss peaches, lemon zest, amaretto, honey, salt, red pepper flakes, and 1 cup hot water in slow cooker. Cover and cook on high until peaches are tender when pierced with a fork, about 1 hour. Keep warm until ready to serve. Serve peaches and sauce over ice cream and top with basil. The ingredients of Slow Cooker Amaretto-Poached Peaches are 4 peaches (about 2 pounds), pitted, thinly sliced, 4 strips lemon zest (from 1 lemon), 3/4 cup amaretto, 1/4 cup honey, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt, 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, Vanilla ice cream (for serving), 1/2 cup basil leaves, torn if large, A (4-quart) slow cooker.The slow cooker doesn't have to be for all-day projects. This simple dessert is easy to assemble right before a dinner party, and the tender, boozy peaches will be ready to serve by the time you're ready for dessert.

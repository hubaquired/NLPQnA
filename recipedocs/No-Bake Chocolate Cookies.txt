No-Bake Chocolate Cookies recipe. Line a cookie sheet with waxed paper and set aside. In a large, heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the sugar, milk, cocoa, and butter and stir to combine. Cook over high heat and bring to a boil. Allow to boil for 1 1/2 minutes, remove from heat, then add the oats, coconut, vanilla, and marshmallows and stir well to combine. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheet and chill. No-Bake Chocolate Cookies has 63.0 calories. No-Bake Chocolate Cookies has 1.0 grams of protein. No-Bake Chocolate Cookies has 2.0 grams of fat. No-Bake Chocolate Cookies has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of No-Bake Chocolate Cookies are 2 cups sugar, 1/2 cup whole milk, 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder, 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, 3 cups rolled oats, 1 cup flaked coconut, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1 cup mini marshmallows.

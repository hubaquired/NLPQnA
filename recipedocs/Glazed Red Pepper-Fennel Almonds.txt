Glazed Red Pepper-Fennel Almonds recipe.  Preheat oven to 325°F. Line heavy baking sheet with foil; spray with nonstick spray. Combine sugar, fennel seeds, crushed red pepper, and salt in medium bowl. Mix in almonds and 1 tablespoon water. Spread mixture on prepared baking sheet in single layer. Bake until sugar melts and almonds are deep golden brown and glazed, stirring often, about 22 minutes. Separate almonds with fork; cool completely on sheet. Can be prepared 1 week ahead. Store in plastic bag.) Transfer almonds to bowl and serve. Glazed Red Pepper-Fennel Almonds has 170.0 calories. Glazed Red Pepper-Fennel Almonds has 5.0 grams of protein. Glazed Red Pepper-Fennel Almonds has 13.0 grams of fat. Glazed Red Pepper-Fennel Almonds has 175.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Glazed Red Pepper-Fennel Almonds are Nonstick vegetable oil spray, 3 tablespoons sugar, 2 teaspoons fennel seeds, 1 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 cup whole almonds, 1 tablespoon water.These can be prepared one week ahead.

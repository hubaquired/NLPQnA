Mushroom, Herb, and Goat Cheese Tart recipe. Preheat oven to 400°. Lay out pastry squares on 2 baking sheets lined with parchment paper. Bake until golden, about 14 minutes. Remove; let settle and cool. Melt butter in a large skillet and sauté mushrooms until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Divide mixture among pastry squares, dot with goat cheese, and sprinkle with Parmesan. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes. Cut tarts into 3" squares. Serve warm or at room temperature. The ingredients of Mushroom, Herb, and Goat Cheese Tart are 1 box frozen puff pastry (two 9" squares), 1/2 stick (4 tablespoons) butter, 7 cups thinly sliced fresh mushrooms (button, porcini, or a mix of any favorites), Salt, Freshly ground pepper, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme, 1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, 5 ounces goat cheese, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan.Thanks to frozen puff pastry, making this savory hors d'oeuvre is a snap.

Gin, Tea, and Lemon Fizz recipe. In small saucepan, bring 1 cup water to boil. Add tea leaves. Remove from heat. Cover and let steep 15 minutes. Chill overnight. Strain tea mixture into pitcher; discard tea leaves. Add lemonade concentrate and gin, then sparkling water to pitcher; stir to blend. Serve over ice. Gin, Tea, and Lemon Fizz has 104.0 calories. Gin, Tea, and Lemon Fizz has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gin, Tea, and Lemon Fizz are 1 cup water, 4 teaspoons black tea leaves or 4 tea bags (such as Darjeeling), 1 cup frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed, 1 cup gin, 2 cups sparkling water, Ice cubes.The tea needs to steep overnight, so begin preparing this ahead.

Court-Bouillon recipe.  Place all the ingredients except the salt in a pot and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for to minutes. Strain and season to taste with salt. Court-Bouillon has 48.0 calories. Court-Bouillon has 1.0 grams of protein. Court-Bouillon has 48.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Court-Bouillon are 4 cups water, 1/2 cup red wine vinegar, 1 thyme sprig, 2 flat-leaf parsley sprigs, 1 bay leaf, 3 garlic cloves, 1/2 carrot, peeled, 1/2 onion, 1 tablespoon peppercorns, Fine sea salt.This is a basic court-bouillon that can be used to poach any kind of shellfish and most firm meaty fish.

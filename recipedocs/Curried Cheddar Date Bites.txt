Curried Cheddar Date Bites recipe.  Chop walnuts and in a small skillet cook in oil over moderate heat, stirring, until lightly toasted. Cool nuts slightly. Preheat broiler. Coarsely grate apple and finely chop dates. In a bowl stir together all ingredients except pitas until combined well. Horizontally split pitas and arrange, cut sides down, on a large baking sheet. Broil pitas about 4 inches from heat until golden and crisp, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Remove baking sheet from oven and turn pitas over. Divide Cheddar mixture among pitas, spreading evenly. Broil pitas until cheese mixture begins to bubble, about 2 minutes. Cut each pita into 6 wedges. Curried Cheddar Date Bites has 62.0 calories. Curried Cheddar Date Bites has 2.0 grams of protein. Curried Cheddar Date Bites has 4.0 grams of fat. Curried Cheddar Date Bites has 63.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Curried Cheddar Date Bites are 1/3 cup walnuts, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 Granny Smith apple, 1/3 cup pitted dates, 2 cups grated extra-sharp Cheddar (about 8 ounces), 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leafed parsley leaves, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon apricot preserves, 3/4 teaspoon curry powder, three 5-inch pita loaves.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

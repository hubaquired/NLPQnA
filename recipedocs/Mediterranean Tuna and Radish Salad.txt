Mediterranean Tuna and Radish Salad recipe.  Whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, and salt in a small bowl. Combine the radishes, parsley, scallions, olives, and tuna in a medium-size bowl. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat. Divide the lettuce leaves among 4 plates. Top with the tuna salad. Serve immediately. Mediterranean Tuna and Radish Salad has 229.0 calories. Mediterranean Tuna and Radish Salad has 20.0 grams of protein. Mediterranean Tuna and Radish Salad has 12.0 grams of fat. Mediterranean Tuna and Radish Salad has 468.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mediterranean Tuna and Radish Salad are 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste, 1 pound radishes, thinly sliced, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves, 3 scallions, white and green parts, chopped, 6 kalamata or other large black olives, pitted and coarsely chopped, 2 6-ounce cans tuna (preferably imported and packed in olive oil), drained, 1 medium head romaine lettuce, washed, dried, and torn into bite-size pieces.Imported or domestic tuna packed in olive oil is a staple in my pantry. I make quick tomato sauces with it, use it on sandwiches, and especially in salads. This is a great main-dish salad, with tender tuna and crunchy, spicy radishes.

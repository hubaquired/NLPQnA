Peach Chutney recipe. Combine onions, currants, vinegar, and ginger in medium bowl. Cut peaches into 1/3-inch cubes. Add to onion mixture and toss to coat. Cover; chill at least 1 hour and up to 6 hours, tossing occasionally. Peach Chutney has 112.0 calories. Peach Chutney has 2.0 grams of protein. Peach Chutney has 1.0 grams of fat. Peach Chutney has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peach Chutney are 4 green onions, chopped, 1/4 cup dried currants, 1 tablespoon Sherry wine vinegar, 2 teaspoons grated peeled fresh ginger, 1 3/4 pounds peaches (about 4 medium), peeled, halved, pitted.Try this fresh, no-cook chutney with grilled chicken, sautéed shrimp, or lamb burgers.

White Barbecue Sauce recipe.  Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, lemon juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and 2 tsp. water in a medium bowl just to combine. Mix sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, and hickory smoke powder, if using, in a small bowl, then whisk into mayonnaise mixture. Season sauce generously with salt and pepper. Barbecue sauce can be made 1 week ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of White Barbecue Sauce are 1 cup mayonnaise (preferably Duke’s), 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon hot sauce (preferably Texas Pete), 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 4 teaspoons sugar, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon hickory smoke powder (optional), Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper.There are many different styles of barbecue and more than one kind of barbecue sauce. This one originated in Alabama, and if you know what’s good for you, you’ll dip your fried chicken in it.

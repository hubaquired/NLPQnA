Kemp's Pesto recipe.  Mince and mash garlic to a paste with sea salt. Pulse seeds in a food processor until finely ground. Add garlic paste, basil, and parsley to processor and pulse until finely chopped. Add cheese and oil and pulse to combine. Kemp's Pesto has 193.0 calories. Kemp's Pesto has 6.0 grams of protein. Kemp's Pesto has 18.0 grams of fat. Kemp's Pesto has 288.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Kemp's Pesto are 2 to 3 large garlic cloves, 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt, 1/3 cup raw green (hulled) pumpkin seeds (pepitas) or sunflower seeds, 2 cups packed basil leaves, 1 cup packed flat-leaf parsley sprigs, 1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil.Learn how our executive food editor rediscovered the joys of fresh pesto and made it her own.

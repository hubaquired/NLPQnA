Honey Lemon Buttermilk Dressing recipe.  In a bowl whisk together zest, lemon juice, and honey until combined well and whisk in buttermilk, yogurt, oil, vanilla, and a pinch of salt. Honey Lemon Buttermilk Dressing has 201.0 calories. Honey Lemon Buttermilk Dressing has 4.0 grams of protein. Honey Lemon Buttermilk Dressing has 11.0 grams of fat. Honey Lemon Buttermilk Dressing has 175.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honey Lemon Buttermilk Dressing are 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon honey, 1/2 cup buttermilk, 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 1/4 teaspoon vanilla.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

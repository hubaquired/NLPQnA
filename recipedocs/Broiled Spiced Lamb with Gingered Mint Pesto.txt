Broiled Spiced Lamb with Gingered Mint Pesto recipe.  Set rack about 6 inches from heat and preheat broiler. In a small bowl stir together cardamom, salt, and pepper to taste. Let lamb stand at room temperature 30 minutes. Pat lamb dry with paper towels and rub both sides with cardamom mixture. Broil lamb on rack of a broiler pan 14 minutes on each side for medium-rare. Transfer lamb to a cutting board and let stand 10 minutes. In a bowl whisk together pesto, vinegar, yogurt, and 2 tablespoons hot water, adding additional hot water if necessary to reach desired consistency. Carve lamb against grain into 1/2-inch-thick slices and serve with pesto. Broiled Spiced Lamb with Gingered Mint Pesto has 644.0 calories. Broiled Spiced Lamb with Gingered Mint Pesto has 56.0 grams of protein. Broiled Spiced Lamb with Gingered Mint Pesto has 44.0 grams of fat. Broiled Spiced Lamb with Gingered Mint Pesto has 955.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Broiled Spiced Lamb with Gingered Mint Pesto are 1 tablespoon ground cardamom, 2 teaspoons salt, two 2-pound boneless top rounds of lamb, 3/4 cupgingered mint pesto, 4 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 6 tablespoons plain yogurt, 2 to 3 tablespoons hot water.

Cranberry, Tangerine, and Crystallized-Ginger Relish recipe. Using on/off turns, coarsely chop cranberries in processor. Transfer to medium bowl. Using on/off turns, coarsely chop tangerine in processor. Combine with cranberries. Mix in remaining ingredients. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Can be made 1 week ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Serve cold or at room temperature. Cranberry, Tangerine, and Crystallized-Ginger Relish has 313.0 calories. Cranberry, Tangerine, and Crystallized-Ginger Relish has 1.0 grams of protein. Cranberry, Tangerine, and Crystallized-Ginger Relish has 31.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry, Tangerine, and Crystallized-Ginger Relish are 1 12-ounce bag cranberries, 1 6-ounce tangerine (unpeeled), halved, seeded, cut into 1-inch pieces, 3/4 cup sugar, 1/2 cup coarsely chopped crystallized ginger (about 2 1/2 ounces), 1/4 cup orange marmalade.

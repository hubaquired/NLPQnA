Honeydew Cucumber Smoothie recipe. Freeze honeydew in 1 layer in a large sealed plastic bag until frozen, about 4 hours. Blend honeydew with cucumber, yogurt, mint, and sugar to taste) in a blender until smooth. Thin with water if necessary. Honeydew Cucumber Smoothie has 147.0 calories. Honeydew Cucumber Smoothie has 6.0 grams of protein. Honeydew Cucumber Smoothie has 4.0 grams of fat. Honeydew Cucumber Smoothie has 81.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Honeydew Cucumber Smoothie are 1 1/2 lb piece ripe honeydew, seeded, rind discarded, and flesh cut into 1/2-inch pieces (3 cups), 1 (8-oz) cucumber, peeled and chopped (1 cup), 1 cup lemon yogurt, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, 1 to 2 teaspoons sugar.

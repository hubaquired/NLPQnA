Peanut and Scallion Relish recipe.  Combine scallion, peanuts, cilantro, and brown sugar in a small bowl; season with salt and pepper. Use immediately. Don't prepare this relish ahead of time—the cilantro will make the peanuts soggy.) Peanut and Scallion Relish has 165.0 calories. Peanut and Scallion Relish has 7.0 grams of protein. Peanut and Scallion Relish has 14.0 grams of fat. Peanut and Scallion Relish has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peanut and Scallion Relish are 1 large scallion, green parts only, thinly sliced, 3/4 cup coarsely chopped unsalted, dry-roasted peanuts, 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tablespoon light brown sugar, Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper.A favorite, try this versatile crunchy peanut mixture with braised-chicken-thigh lettuce wraps.

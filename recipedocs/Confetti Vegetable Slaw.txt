Confetti Vegetable Slaw recipe. In a bowl whisk together vinegar and salt and pepper to taste and whisk in oil in a stream until emulsified. Add vegetables and toss until combined well. Slaw may be made 6 hours ahead and chilled, covered. Confetti Vegetable Slaw has 97.0 calories. Confetti Vegetable Slaw has 1.0 grams of protein. Confetti Vegetable Slaw has 9.0 grams of fat. Confetti Vegetable Slaw has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Confetti Vegetable Slaw are 4 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 1/3 cup olive oil, 1 large red bell pepper, cut into 2-inch-long strips, 1 large yellow bell pepper, cut into 2-inch-long strips, 1 medium carrot, shredded fine, 2 celery ribs, cut into 2-inch-long thin strips.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

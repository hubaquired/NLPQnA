Pickled Red Onions with Cilantro recipe.  Bring vinegar, sugar, cumin, and salt to a boil in a 2-quart nonreactive heavy saucepan, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Add onions and simmer, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes, then transfer to a bowl and cool. Stir in cilantro. Pickled Red Onions with Cilantro has 95.0 calories. Pickled Red Onions with Cilantro has 1.0 grams of protein. Pickled Red Onions with Cilantro has 395.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pickled Red Onions with Cilantro are 1 cup cider vinegar, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, toasted, 1 teaspoon salt, 3 medium red onions, cut into 1-inch pieces, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro.This recipe is an accompaniment for Grilled Steaks with Red Chile Sauce.

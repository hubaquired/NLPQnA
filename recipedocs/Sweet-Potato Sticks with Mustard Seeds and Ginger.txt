Sweet-Potato Sticks with Mustard Seeds and Ginger recipe. Steam potatoes in steamer basket set over boiling water in a 12-inch-wide pot, covered, until tender, 5 to 6 minutes. Transfer carefully to a platter with a wide spatula to avoid breaking sticks. While potatoes steam, cook garlic and ginger in oil in a 10-inch heavy skillet over moderately low heat, stirring, until oil begins to sizzle, about 1 minute, then add mustard seeds, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring, until garlic is pale golden, 1 to 2 minutes. Spoon hot oil mixture over potatoes and sprinkle with cilantro. Sweet-Potato Sticks with Mustard Seeds and Ginger has 281.0 calories. The ingredients of Sweet-Potato Sticks with Mustard Seeds and Ginger are 3 1/2 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut crosswise into 1/4-inch-thick sticks, 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon finely chopped peeled fresh ginger, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon yellow mustard seeds, 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro, Special equipment: a large (11-inch) collapsible steamer basket.Fresh ginger and yellow mustard seeds make these sweet potatoes much more worldly than their candied incarnations. The cilantro takes them in an unexpected but very welcome direction.

Tomato, Corn, and Avocado Salsa recipe. Dice ripe tomatoes and combine with raw corn kernels, diced avocado, chopped cilantro, and fresh lime juice. Add some minced serrano chile for a little heat. Season with salt and more lime juice. The ingredients of Tomato, Corn, and Avocado Salsa are Ripe tomatoes, Raw corn kernels, Diced avocado, Chopped cilantro, Fresh lime juice, Minced serrano chile, Salt.This refreshing summer salsa gets some heat from serrano chile.

Spiced Pecans recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Melt butter in medium skillet over medium heat. Add chili powder, salt and pepper and stir to blend. Remove from heat. Add pecans and stir to coat. Transfer to rimmed baking sheet. Bake until pecans are golden, about 8 minutes. Cool. Coarsely chop pecans. Can be made 2 days ahead. Store airtight at room temperature.) Spiced Pecans has 197.0 calories. Spiced Pecans has 2.0 grams of protein. Spiced Pecans has 21.0 grams of fat. Spiced Pecans has 147.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spiced Pecans are 1 1/2 tablespoons butter, 1/2 teaspoon chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, 1 1/2 cups pecan halves and pieces.

Lime Mojo recipe.  Stir cumin in heavy skillet over medium-low heat until fragrant, about 4 minutes. Heat oil in heavy large saucepan until thermometer registers 175°Fdo not boil). Remove from heat. Mix in cumin, jalapeño, garlic, salt and pepper, then fresh lime juice, chopped cilantro and vinegar. Cool completely. Lime Mojo has 303.0 calories. Lime Mojo has 1.0 grams of protein. Lime Mojo has 33.0 grams of fat. Lime Mojo has 237.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lime Mojo are 2 tablespoons ground cumin, 1 1/2 cups olive oil, 1/4 cup minced seeded jalapeño chilies, 12 garlic cloves, minced, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, 3/4 cup fresh lime juice, 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 3 tablespoons Sherry wine vinegar.

Caviar on Pumpernickel with Sour Cream recipe.  Force eggs through a sieve into a small bowl and gently toss with onion. Trim 36 pumpernickel slices to 1 1/4-inch squares, then top each with 1/2 teaspoon sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon egg, and 1/2 teaspoon caviar. Caviar on Pumpernickel with Sour Cream has 22.0 calories. Caviar on Pumpernickel with Sour Cream has 1.0 grams of protein. Caviar on Pumpernickel with Sour Cream has 2.0 grams of fat. Caviar on Pumpernickel with Sour Cream has 68.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Caviar on Pumpernickel with Sour Cream are 2 large eggs, hard boiled, 1/4 cup minced white onion, 1 cocktail pumpernickel loaf, 1/2 cup sour cream, 150 g caviar (5 ounces; preferably osetra), Garnish: fresh chervil or chopped fresh chives.Tartines de Caviar à la Crème Aigre.

Buttermilk-Blue Cheese Dip recipe. Place onion in a small heatproof bowl. Bring vinegar, sugar, salt, and 1/4 cup water to a boil. Pour over onion; stir to coat. Cover; let pickle in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Whisk buttermilk, sour cream, and chives in a medium bowl until smooth. Stir in blue cheese. Drain pickled onion and stir into dip. Season with salt and pepper. DO AHEAD: Can be made 3 days ahead. Cover dip and refrigerate. Buttermilk-Blue Cheese Dip has 209.0 calories. Buttermilk-Blue Cheese Dip has 9.0 grams of protein. Buttermilk-Blue Cheese Dip has 16.0 grams of fat. Buttermilk-Blue Cheese Dip has 520.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Buttermilk-Blue Cheese Dip are 5 tablespoons finely chopped red onion, 1/4 cup white wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar, Pinch of kosher salt plus more, 1/4 cup buttermilk, 1/4 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese, Freshly ground black pepper.Pickled red onion adds crunch and punch. Serve with crudités.

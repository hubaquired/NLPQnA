Grand Marnier Smoothie recipe.  In a blender, blend ice cream, orange juice, Grand Marnier and zest until smooth but still thick. Pour mixture into 2 chilled stemmed glasses and garnish with orange slices. Grand Marnier Smoothie has 185.0 calories. Grand Marnier Smoothie has 2.0 grams of protein. Grand Marnier Smoothie has 4.0 grams of fat. Grand Marnier Smoothie has 28.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Grand Marnier Smoothie are 1/2 cup softened vanilla ice cream, 1 1/2 cups fresh orange juice, 1 ounce (2 tablespoons) Grand Marnier, or to taste, Garnish: 1/2 teaspoon freshly grated orange zest plus orange slices.

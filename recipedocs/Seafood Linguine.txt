Seafood Linguine recipe.  Heat olive oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until onion is golden brown, about 6 minutes. Stir in flour and coriander and cook 1 minute. Add wine and cream and bring to boil, stirring constantly. Add shrimp and scallops and simmer until shrimp are cooked through, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes. Mix in parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Pour over linguine and serve. Seafood Linguine has 601.0 calories. Seafood Linguine has 27.0 grams of protein. Seafood Linguine has 22.0 grams of fat. Seafood Linguine has 562.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Seafood Linguine are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 cup finely chopped onion, 5 garlic cloves, minced, 1 tablespoon all purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander, 3 tablespoons dry white wine, 1 cup whipping cream, 8 ounces uncooked shrimp, peeled, deveined, 8 ounces bay scallops, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, 12 ounces linguine, freshly cooked.

Cucumber, Sweet Onion, and Mint with Curried Mayonnaise on Peasant Bread recipe.  In a small bowl combine well the mayonnaise and the curry powder and spread the curried mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread. Layer half the bread slices with the cucumber, the onion, and the mint, sprinkle the mixture with salt and pepper to taste, and top the sandwiches with the remaining bread slices, pressing them firmly. The ingredients of Cucumber, Sweet Onion, and Mint with Curried Mayonnaise on Peasant Bread are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1 teaspoon curry powder, or to taste, 8 slices of peasant or country-style bread, 1 cucumber, peeled if desired and sliced thin diagonally, 1 sweet onion, such as Vidalia, sliced thin, 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Spice-Crusted Ham with Maple Mustard Sauce recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Remove any thick skin from ham and all but about 3/4-inch layer of fat. Mince and mash garlic with salt to a paste. Coarsely grind coriander and peppercorns in coffee/spice grinder and stir together with flour, butter, and garlic paste in a bowl. Spread spice paste all over ham, then put ham, cut side down, in a greased large roasting pan. Bake in middle of oven until crust is golden and an instant-read thermometer inserted into center do not touch bone) registers 150°F, about 1 1/4 hours. Transfer ham to a platter and let stand 15 minutes before slicing. Spice-Crusted Ham with Maple Mustard Sauce has 1068.0 calories. The ingredients of Spice-Crusted Ham with Maple Mustard Sauce are 1 (8- to 10-pound) fully cooked shank-end ham, 4 garlic cloves, 1 1/2 teaspoons fine sea salt, 1 tablespoon whole coriander seeds, 1 tablespoon mixed peppercorns (containing black, pink, white, and green peppercorns but not allspice), 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened, Accompaniment:Maple Mustard Sauce, Garnish: 2 large bunches fresh flat-leaf parsley, Special equipment: an electric coffee/spice grinder.

Shrimp with Olive Oil, Garlic and Lime recipe. Preheat oven to 450°F. Combine first 5 ingredients in 13 x 9 x 2-inch glass baking dish; whisk to blend. Mix in shrimp; let stand 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Bake shrimp until just opaque in center, about 15 minutes. Spoon shrimp and juices into bowls. Serve with lime wedges. Shrimp with Olive Oil, Garlic and Lime has 501.0 calories. Shrimp with Olive Oil, Garlic and Lime has 24.0 grams of protein. Shrimp with Olive Oil, Garlic and Lime has 42.0 grams of fat. Shrimp with Olive Oil, Garlic and Lime has 1212.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp with Olive Oil, Garlic and Lime are 3/4 cup olive oil, 12 large garlic cloves, chopped, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon coarse black pepper, 1 1/2 pounds uncooked large shrimp, unpeeled, Lime wedges.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

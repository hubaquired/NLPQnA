Oasis Carrot Salad recipe. Cook carrots in a medium saucepan of boiling water until tender, about 8 minutes. Drain, then rinse under cold water until cool. Drain well. Meanwhile, pulse onion and garlic with oil, vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a food processor until very finely chopped. Toss with carrots and chill at least 3 hours. Just before serving, toss salad with cilantro, parsley, and salt to taste. Oasis Carrot Salad has 111.0 calories. Oasis Carrot Salad has 1.0 grams of protein. Oasis Carrot Salad has 7.0 grams of fat. Oasis Carrot Salad has 81.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Oasis Carrot Salad are 1 pound carrots, sliced crosswise 1/4 inch thick, 2 tablespoons chopped onion, rinsed and drained, 1 garlic clove, chopped, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar, 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro, 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley.Lightly garlicky and herbed, Moroccan-inspired carrots are versatile enough to accompany almost any meal.

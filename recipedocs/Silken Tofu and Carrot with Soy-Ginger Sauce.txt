Silken Tofu and Carrot with Soy-Ginger Sauce recipe.  In a small bowl stir together soy sauce, vinegar, gingerroot, and sugar until sugar is dissolved. Peel and finely grate carrot. Carefully drain and halve tofu. Put tofu on 2 plates and spoon sauce over it. Top tofu with carrot and scallion. Silken Tofu and Carrot with Soy-Ginger Sauce has 94.0 calories. Silken Tofu and Carrot with Soy-Ginger Sauce has 9.0 grams of protein. Silken Tofu and Carrot with Soy-Ginger Sauce has 4.0 grams of fat. Silken Tofu and Carrot with Soy-Ginger Sauce has 688.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Silken Tofu and Carrot with Soy-Ginger Sauce are 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar, 3/4 teaspoon finely grated peeled fresh gingerroot, 3/4 teaspoon sugar, 1 small carrot, 1/2 pound silken or soft tofu, 1 tablespoon thinly sliced scallion greens.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

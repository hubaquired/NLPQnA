Heirloom Tomato Salad with Mozzarella and Basil recipe. Whisk together vinegar, mustard, salt, sugar, and pepper in a large bowl. Add oil in a slow stream, whisking constantly until dressing is emulsified. Add tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil and toss well. Season with salt and pepper. Heirloom Tomato Salad with Mozzarella and Basil has 345.0 calories. Heirloom Tomato Salad with Mozzarella and Basil has 15.0 grams of protein. Heirloom Tomato Salad with Mozzarella and Basil has 27.0 grams of fat. Heirloom Tomato Salad with Mozzarella and Basil has 668.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Heirloom Tomato Salad with Mozzarella and Basil are 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 4 lb mixed heirloom tomatoes, quartered if small or cut into 1/4-inch-wide wedges if larger, 1 lb cherry tomatoes, 1 lb very small mozzarella balls (1/4 inch; sometimes called perlini) or 1 lb lightly salted mozzarella, cut into 1/4-inch pieces, 1 1/2 cups loosely packed small basil leaves or torn large leaves.Allow this salad to sit a few minutes after tossing; the mozzarella milk and tomato juices emerge and add flavor to the dressing.

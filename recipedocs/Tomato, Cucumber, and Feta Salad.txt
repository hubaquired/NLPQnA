Tomato, Cucumber, and Feta Salad recipe. In a bowl whisk together the vinegar, the sugar, the oil, and salt and pepper to taste, add the tomatoes, the cucumber, the Feta, and the basil, and toss the salad well. Tomato, Cucumber, and Feta Salad has 196.0 calories. Tomato, Cucumber, and Feta Salad has 7.0 grams of protein. Tomato, Cucumber, and Feta Salad has 15.0 grams of fat. Tomato, Cucumber, and Feta Salad has 352.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tomato, Cucumber, and Feta Salad are 1 teaspoon red-wine vinegar, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 1/2 cups quartered cherry tomatoes, 1 small cucumber, peeled, halved lengthwise, seeded, and cut crosswise into 1/4-inch slices (about 1 1/2 cups), 1/2 cup crumbled Feta, 1/4 cup shredded fresh basil leaves.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

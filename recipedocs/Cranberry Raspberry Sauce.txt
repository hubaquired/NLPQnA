Cranberry Raspberry Sauce recipe. In a heavy saucepan combine the cranberries, the raspberries with the syrup, the brown sugar, and the cranberry juice cocktail, simmer the mixture, covered, stirring occasionally, for 10 to 15 minutes, or until the cranberries have burst and the sauce is thickened, and let the sauce cool. Cranberry Raspberry Sauce has 283.0 calories. Cranberry Raspberry Sauce has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry Raspberry Sauce are a 12-ounce bag of cranberries, picked over, a 10-ounce package frozen raspberries in light syrup, thawed, 1/2 cup firmly packed light brown sugar, 3/4 cup cranberry juice cocktail.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

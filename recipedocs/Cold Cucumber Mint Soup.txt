Cold Cucumber Mint Soup recipe. In a blender puree chopped cucumbers, yogurt, sour cream, mustard, and salt and pepper to taste and transfer to a bowl. Chill soup at least 6 hours or overnight. Stir in finely diced cucumber and chopped mint and garnish soup with cucumber slices and mint sprigs. Cold Cucumber Mint Soup has 153.0 calories. Cold Cucumber Mint Soup has 5.0 grams of protein. Cold Cucumber Mint Soup has 10.0 grams of fat. Cold Cucumber Mint Soup has 53.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cold Cucumber Mint Soup are 3 cucumbers, peeled, seeded, and chopped, plus 1 cup peeled, seeded, and finely diced cucumber, 1 cup plain yogurt, 2/3 cup sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon English-style dry mustard, or to taste, 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves, cucumber slices and mint sprigs for garnish.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

Fresh Tomato Sauce recipe.  Sauté the garlic in the olive oil until soft. Add the tomatoes, salt, and pepper and cook until the tomatoes begin to soften. Add all but a couple of tablespoons of the basil. Put the mixture in a blender and blend until smooth. Fresh Tomato Sauce has 139.0 calories. Fresh Tomato Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Fresh Tomato Sauce has 14.0 grams of fat. Fresh Tomato Sauce has 296.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fresh Tomato Sauce are 3 garlic cloves, crushed, 1/2 cup olive oil, 12 ripe Roma tomatoes, chopped, Salt and black pepper to taste, 1/2 cup chopped basil.

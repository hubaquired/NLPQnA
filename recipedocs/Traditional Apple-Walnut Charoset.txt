Traditional Apple-Walnut Charoset recipe. In large bowl, stir together all ingredients. Store, covered, at room temperature until ready to serve. Traditional Apple-Walnut Charoset has 174.0 calories. Traditional Apple-Walnut Charoset has 2.0 grams of protein. Traditional Apple-Walnut Charoset has 7.0 grams of fat. Traditional Apple-Walnut Charoset has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Traditional Apple-Walnut Charoset are 3 medium Gala or Fuji apples, peeled, cored, and finely diced, 1 1/2 cups walnut halves, lightly toasted, cooled, and coarsely chopped, 1/2 cup sweet red wine such as Manischewitz Extra Heavy Malaga, 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar.This is a standard Ashkenazi (Eastern European) recipe, with a little brown sugar added for depth.

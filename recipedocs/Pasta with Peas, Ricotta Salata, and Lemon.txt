Pasta with Peas, Ricotta Salata, and Lemon recipe. Fill a 4-quart kettle three fourths full with salted water and bring to a boil for pasta and peas. Tear basil into bite-size pieces and break ricotta salata into about 1/2-inch-thick pieces. Into a large bowl finely grate zest from lemon and squeeze enough juice to measure 1 tablespoon. Add basil, cheese, and oil. Cook pasta in boiling water until almost al dente. Stir in peas and continue to cook until pasta is al dente and peas are tender. Drain pasta and peas well. Add pasta and peas to bowl and toss to combine. Season pasta with salt and pepper and sprinkle with tarragon. Pasta with Peas, Ricotta Salata, and Lemon has 641.0 calories. The ingredients of Pasta with Peas, Ricotta Salata, and Lemon are 6 ounces dried pasta such as farfalle, 1 cup frozen peas, 1/4 cup packed fresh basil leaves, 3 ounces ricotta salata cheese*, 1 lemon, 1 1/2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh tarragon leaves, *available at specialty foods shops and many supermarkets.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

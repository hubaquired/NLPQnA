Smoked Turkey and Cheese Spread recipe.  Combine cream cheese and mayonnaise in processor; blend until smooth. Add turkey and process, using on/off turns, until coarse paste forms. Transfer mixture to medium bowl. Mix in 1/2 cup pecans and 4 tablespoons parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon into small serving bowl. Sprinkle top with remaining 2 tablespoons pecans and 2 tablespoons parsley. Can be prepared 3 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Let stand at room temperature 30 minutes before serving.) The ingredients of Smoked Turkey and Cheese Spread are 1 8-ounce package cream cheese, room temperature, 3 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1 cup coarsely chopped fully cooked smoked turkey (about 6 ounces), 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons chopped pecans, 6 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley.Delicious on crackers or as a filling for small tea sandwiches.

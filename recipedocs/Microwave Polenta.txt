Microwave Polenta recipe.  Whisk together broth and cornmeal in large bowl. Cook in microwave on High until thickened, about 15 minutes, whisking every 5 minutes to combine. Stir in bacon, Romano, butter and pepper and serve. Microwave Polenta has 531.0 calories. Microwave Polenta has 20.0 grams of protein. Microwave Polenta has 27.0 grams of fat. Microwave Polenta has 480.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Microwave Polenta are 2 13 3/4-ounce cans low-salt chicken broth, 3/4 cup yellow cornmeal, 5 strips bacon, cooked, crumbled (optional), 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons grated Romano, 3 tablespoons butter, 1/2 teaspoon pepper.Offer this Italian cornmeal side dish in place of potatoes or rice. It's best served immediately out of the oven.

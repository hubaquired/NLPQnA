Gratin of Greens recipe. Wash and dry the spinach, chop rather fine and cook in a little olive oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat until just wilted. Drain. Chop the chard and cook in the same manner. Dice the zucchini, and cook in 6 tablespoons oil until just tender. Combine the vegetables with salt, the rice and garlic. Transfer to a well-oiled heavy baking dish. Add 3-4 tablespoons olive oil, and heat in a 300Â°F. oven for 20 minutes. Add the eggs, well beaten, sprinkle with crumbs and return to the oven until the eggs just set. Eat warm or cold. Gratin of Greens has 250.0 calories. Gratin of Greens has 15.0 grams of protein. Gratin of Greens has 13.0 grams of fat. Gratin of Greens has 1735.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gratin of Greens are 2 pounds spinach, Olive oil, 2 pounds Swiss chard, 2 pounds zucchini, Salt to taste, 1 cup cooked rice, 3 cloves garlic, chopped, 6 eggs, Fine bread crumbs.

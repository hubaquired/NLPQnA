Red Ginger Juice recipe.  Juice the lime using a manual citrus juicer. Juice the remaining ingredients in a juice, and then combine with the lime juice. Store in an airtight glass container in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. The ingredients of Red Ginger Juice are 1 lime, 1 beet, 1 Granny Smith apple, sliced, 1 pear, sliced, 4 stalks, celery, 1 fennel bulb, chopped, 1 (1/4-inch) piece ginger root, 1 (1/4-inch) piece turmeric root.This juice is a real energy boost, and it's great when you need a pick-me-up or when you need to fuel a workout. The fruit provides energy, while the beet increases endurance, and the celery and fennel push out toxins.  Juicer Breakfast Brunch Vegetarian Apple Pear Celery Fennel Beet Healthy Vegan Drink.

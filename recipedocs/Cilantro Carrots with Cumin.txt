Cilantro Carrots with Cumin recipe.  Combine carrots and 6 tablespoons water in large saucepan. Season with salt. Cover and boil until carrots are crisp-tender, about 7 minutes. Drain off any excess water. Transfer carrots to large shallow bowl. Mix in lemon juice, oil, cumin and garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Cool. Add cilantro. Can be made 2 hours ahead. Let stand at cool room temperature.) Cilantro Carrots with Cumin has 99.0 calories. Cilantro Carrots with Cumin has 1.0 grams of protein. Cilantro Carrots with Cumin has 6.0 grams of fat. Cilantro Carrots with Cumin has 82.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cilantro Carrots with Cumin are 2 pounds carrots, each cut into 2-inch-long pieces, then quartered lengthwise, 6 tablespoons water, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons ground cumin, 2 garlic cloves, pressed, 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro.

Horseradish-Dill Schmear recipe. Mix cream cheese, dill, and horseradish in a small bowl; season with salt. The ingredients of Horseradish-Dill Schmear are 8 ounces whipped cream cheese (about 1 cup), 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh dill, 1/4 cup prepared horseradish, Kosher salt.Throw this spread together a day in advance—the flavors will meld and intensify as it sits.

Tarragon Mayonnaise recipe. Combine all ingredients in bowl and mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill.) The ingredients of Tarragon Mayonnaise are Makes 1 1/2 cups, 1 cup mayonnaise (regular or low-fat), 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 4 green onions, chopped, 1 tablespoon drained small capers, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon or 1 teaspoon dried, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 2 teaspoons prepared white horseradish. Fish Herb Sauté Spring Bon Appétit.

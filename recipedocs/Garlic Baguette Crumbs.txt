Garlic Baguette Crumbs recipe. Mince garlic and mash to a paste with a pinch of salt, then transfer to a bowl and stir in oil, salt, and pepper. Add baguette crumbs and stir until combined well. Garlic Baguette Crumbs has 95.0 calories. Garlic Baguette Crumbs has 1.0 grams of protein. Garlic Baguette Crumbs has 7.0 grams of fat. Garlic Baguette Crumbs has 103.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Garlic Baguette Crumbs are 1 garlic clove, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1 cup toasted baguette crumbs.

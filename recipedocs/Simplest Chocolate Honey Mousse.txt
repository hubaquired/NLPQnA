Simplest Chocolate Honey Mousse recipe.  Stir 3/4 cup cream, chocolate and honey in heavy medium saucepan over low heat until chocolate melts and mixture is smooth. Cool, stirring occasionally. In large bowl, beat 1 1/4 cups cream until soft peaks form. Fold cream into chocolate mixture in 2 additions. Divide mousse among eight 3/4-cup ramekins. Refrigerate until set, about 2 hours. Whip remaining 1/2 cup cream to firm peaks. Spoon a dollop of cream in center of each mousse. Simplest Chocolate Honey Mousse has 354.0 calories. Simplest Chocolate Honey Mousse has 3.0 grams of protein. Simplest Chocolate Honey Mousse has 24.0 grams of fat. Simplest Chocolate Honey Mousse has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Simplest Chocolate Honey Mousse are 2 1/2 cups chilled whipping cream, 12 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) or semisweet chocolate, chopped, 5 tablespoons honey.Sweetening the mousse with honey adds a Provençal touch to a classic French dessert.

Tomato-Onion Topping recipe. Mix all ingredients in small bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill.) The ingredients of Tomato-Onion Topping are 1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes, 1/2 cup chopped onion, 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tablespoon minced seeded jalapeño chili.

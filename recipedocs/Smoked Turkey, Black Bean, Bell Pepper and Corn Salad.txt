Smoked Turkey, Black Bean, Bell Pepper and Corn Salad recipe. Combine turkey, beans, corn, tomatoes, bell peppers and onion in a bowl. Whisk all dressing ingredients in another bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Add dressing to turkey mixture and toss to combine. Divide arugula among 4 plates and top with turkey salad. The ingredients of Smoked Turkey, Black Bean, Bell Pepper and Corn Salad are 3 cups (about 3/4 pound) diced, cooked smoked turkey breast, 1 can (15.5 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained, 1 1/2 cups fresh corn kernels, 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved, 1 cup diced bell peppers (any colors), 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion, 3 cups arugula, 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint (plus leaves for garnish), 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic, 1/2 cup tomato juice, 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon sherry (or balsamic) vinegar, 1 tablespoon olive oil.This no-cook quickie dinner is less than 18 percent fat.

Steamed Clams with Pasta recipe. Cook linguine in pot of boiling salted water until tender but still firm to bite; drain. Meanwhile, cook clams in large pot with parsley, garlic, olive oil, and red pepper. Let cook until clams open, about ten minutes. Divide pasta among bowls. Top with seafood mixture and juices, discarding any clams that do not open; serve. Steamed Clams with Pasta has 715.0 calories. Steamed Clams with Pasta has 77.0 grams of protein. Steamed Clams with Pasta has 10.0 grams of fat. Steamed Clams with Pasta has 2734.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed Clams with Pasta are 1 pound linguine, 6 pounds littleneck or other small clams, scrubbed, 1/2 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley, 3 garlic cloves, chopped, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper.Improv: Try mussels instead of clams; substitute basil for the parsley.

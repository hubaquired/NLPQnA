Sweet Tea recipe.  Boil 3 cups of the water, then add the tea bags; continue boiling for 1 minute. Cover and set aside to steep for about 15 minutes. Remove and discard the tea bags. Add sugar, stirring to dissolve. Pour the tea mixture into a 1-gallon container, then add the remaining 7 cups cold water. Stir to combine. Sweet Tea has 97.0 calories. Sweet Tea has 16.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sweet Tea are 10 cups cold water, 2 family-size or 8 regular black tea bags, 3/4 cup sugar.

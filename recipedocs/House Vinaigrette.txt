House Vinaigrette recipe. In a bowl whisk together vinegars, mustard, sea salt, and white pepper until mustard is incorporated. In a very slow stream whisk in olive oil and then corn oil and whisk until emulsified. Vinaigrette keeps, covered and chilled, 1 week. House Vinaigrette has 183.0 calories. House Vinaigrette has 20.0 grams of fat. House Vinaigrette has 147.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of House Vinaigrette are 3 tablespoons red-wine vinegar, 3 tablespoons Sherry vinegar*, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1 teaspoon fine sea salt, 2 pinches freshly ground white pepper, 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon corn oil, *available at specialty foods shops and some supermarkets.

Marjoram Vinaigrette recipe. Thoroughly combine ingredients. Taste and adjust seasonings. Marjoram Vinaigrette has 61.0 calories. Marjoram Vinaigrette has 2.0 grams of protein. Marjoram Vinaigrette has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Marjoram Vinaigrette are 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar, 4 tablespoons lime, lemon or orange-flavored olive oil, 2 shallots, minced, 1 tablespoon fresh marjoram leaves, coarsely chopped, 1 to 2 serrano chiles, minced, Sugar to taste (optional).Editor's note: The recipe is from Susan Curtis and Nicole Curtis Ammerman's book Santa Fe School of Cooking: Flavors of the Southwest.

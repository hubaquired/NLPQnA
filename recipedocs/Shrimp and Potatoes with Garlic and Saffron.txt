Shrimp and Potatoes with Garlic and Saffron recipe. Cook potatoes in large saucepan of boiling water until just tender. Drain. Combine wine and saffron in small bowl. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-low-heat. Add garlic and sauté until just beginning to color, about 3 minutes. Add shrimp and hot pepper flakes; increase heat to medium-high and stir to coat shrimp with oil. Add tomato paste and wine mixture. Bring to boil, stirring constantly. Cook until shrimp are just tender, about 5 minutes. Transfer shrimp to bowl, using slotted spoon. Add potatoes to skillet and boil until liquid thickens to sauce consistency, about 6 minutes. Return shrimp and any juices in bowl to skillet. Mix in peas and marjoram and heat through. Season with salt and pepper and serve. Shrimp and Potatoes with Garlic and Saffron has 437.0 calories. Shrimp and Potatoes with Garlic and Saffron has 36.0 grams of protein. Shrimp and Potatoes with Garlic and Saffron has 15.0 grams of fat. Shrimp and Potatoes with Garlic and Saffron has 1497.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Shrimp and Potatoes with Garlic and Saffron are 1 1/2 pounds baby red potatoes, quartered, 2 cups dry white wine, 3/8 teaspoon saffron threads, 1/3 cup olive oil, 12 large garlic cloves, minced, 3 pounds uncooked jumbo shrimp (about 10 to 12 per pound), peeled, deveined, 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper flakes, 3 tablespoons tomato paste, 1 10-ounce package frozen baby peas, thawed, 2 teaspoons dried marjoram, crumbled.

Carrot and Bell Pepper Slaw recipe.  In a bowl whisk together the yogurt, the mayonnaise, the mustard, and the lemon juice, add the carrot, the bell pepper, and the scallion, and toss the slaw until it is combined well. Season with slaw with salt and pepper. Carrot and Bell Pepper Slaw has 155.0 calories. Carrot and Bell Pepper Slaw has 2.0 grams of protein. Carrot and Bell Pepper Slaw has 12.0 grams of fat. Carrot and Bell Pepper Slaw has 160.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Carrot and Bell Pepper Slaw are 2 tablespoons plain yogurt, 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, 1 cup coarsely grated carrot, 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch julienne strips, 1 scallion, chopped fine.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

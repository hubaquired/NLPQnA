Grilled Stone Fruit Sangria recipe.  Prepare a grill or grill pan for medium heat; oil grates. Grill peach, plum, and nectarine halves, flipping once, until lightly charred and tender, 8–10 minutes. Transfer to a cutting board and let cool, then cut into 1" chunks. Muddle grilled fruit and 6 halved cherries in a large pitcher with a wooden spoon. Top with wine, orange juice, brandy, and simple syrup; stir to incorporate. Chill at least 1 hour. Fill glasses with ice. Add sangria and muddled fruit and top each with a cherry. The ingredients of Grilled Stone Fruit Sangria are Vegetable oil (for grill), 2 peaches, halved, pitted, 2 plums, halved, pitted, 1 nectarine, halved, pitted, 12 cherries, 6 halved, pitted, 6 left whole for serving, 1 (750ml) bottle red wine, 1/2 cup orange juice, 1/4 cup brandy, 1/4 cup simple syrup.Sangria doesn’t have to be a sugary mess. Grilled stone fruit adds a natural caramelized sweetness to this refreshing drink.

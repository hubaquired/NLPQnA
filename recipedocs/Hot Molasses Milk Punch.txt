Hot Molasses Milk Punch recipe. In a saucepan combine the sugar, 1 tablespoon water, the molasses, and the ginger, and bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring. Add the milk in a stream, whisking, and heat the mixture over moderate heat, stirring until it is hot, but do not let it boil. Stir in the rum, divide the mixture between 2 heated mugs, and sprinkle each drink with some of the nutmeg. Hot Molasses Milk Punch has 339.0 calories. Hot Molasses Milk Punch has 8.0 grams of protein. Hot Molasses Milk Punch has 8.0 grams of fat. Hot Molasses Milk Punch has 109.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Molasses Milk Punch are 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon unsulfured dark molasses, 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger, 2 cups milk, 1/2 cup dark rum, freshly grated nutmeg for garnsih.

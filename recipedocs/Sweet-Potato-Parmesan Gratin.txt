Sweet-Potato-Parmesan Gratin recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter 13x9-inch oval gratin dish. Whisk broth, flour, rosemary, salt, and pepper to blend in heavy large saucepan. Bring to boil, whisking frequently. Add potatoes and bring to boil. Transfer half of potato-broth mix to prepared dish. Sprinkle with 3/4 cup cheese. Top with remaining potato-broth mix. Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes. Uncover; sprinkle with remaining 3/4 cup cheese. Continue baking uncovered until potatoes are tender and top browns, about 45 minutes. Cool slightly. The ingredients of Sweet-Potato-Parmesan Gratin are 1 1/2 cups canned low-salt chicken broth, 4 teaspoons all purpose flour, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper, 3 pounds red-skinned sweet potatoes (yams), peeled, thinly sliced, 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese (about 4 1/2 ounces).Marshmallows need not apply: This gratin gets a savory Parmesan cheese topping.

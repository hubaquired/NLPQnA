Spiced Peach Lassi recipe. In a blender, puree peaches, buttermilk, ice cubes, sugar, cardamom, cumin, salt and liqueur, if desired, until smooth and frothy. Add sugar to taste. Divide among 4 glasses and top with pistachios. Serve immediately. The ingredients of Spiced Peach Lassi are 1 1/2 lb ripe peaches (about 5 medium) quartered and pitted (4 cups), 2 cups lowfat buttermilk, chilled, 1 cup ice cubes, 1/4 cup sugar, plus more to taste, 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom, 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup peach liqueur (optional), 2 tablespoons shelled unsalted pistachios, coarsely chopped.Buttermilk, packed with calcium and probiotics, lends a subtle tanginess to this spice-spiked lassi. Finish with a sprinkle of chopped pistachios for a hit of crunch.  Alcoholic Peach Pistachio Summer Buttermilk Cardamom Cumin Self Drink.

Cherries in Almond Syrup Over Greek Yogurt recipe. Bring cherries, almond syrup, sugar, lemon juice, and 1/4 cup water to a simmer in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, stirring until sugar is dissolved. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until liquid is slightly syrupy but cherries still hold their shape, about 5 minutes. Transfer mixture to a bowl and let cool completely. Chill until cold. DO AHEAD: Can be made 1 week ahead. Cover and keep chilled. Divide yogurt among bowls. Spoon cherries with juices over. Garnish with chopped almonds. The ingredients of Cherries in Almond Syrup Over Greek Yogurt are 4 cups pitted fresh red or black cherries (about 1 1/2 pounds), 1/4 cup almond syrup (such as Monin or Torani), 1/4 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 7 cups plain Greek yogurt, stirred to loosen, 1 cup whole raw almonds, coarsely chopped.If you can't find almond syrup, floral, almond-flavored orgeat syrup is a fine substitute.

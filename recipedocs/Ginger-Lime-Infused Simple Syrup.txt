Ginger-Lime-Infused Simple Syrup recipe.  Combine the sugar, water, ginger, and lime zest in a small saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally until the sugar dissolves and the mixture is clear. Turn off the heat and allow the ginger and lime to steep in the syrup for 3 hours. Strain the syrup into an airtight glass jar. Store for up to 1 month in the refrigerator Ginger-Lime-Infused Simple Syrup has 211.0 calories. Ginger-Lime-Infused Simple Syrup has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger-Lime-Infused Simple Syrup are 1 cup sugar, 1 cup water, 3-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into 6 chunks, zest of 2 limes.

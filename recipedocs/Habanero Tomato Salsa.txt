Habanero Tomato Salsa recipe.  Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Season to taste with salt. DO AHEAD: Can be made 2 hours ahead. Cover and let stand at room temperature. Habanero Tomato Salsa has 53.0 calories. Habanero Tomato Salsa has 2.0 grams of protein. Habanero Tomato Salsa has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Habanero Tomato Salsa are 3 large tomatoes, cut into 1/4-inch cubes, 2 cups finely chopped white onion, 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro, 6 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 6 habanero chiles or 8 serrano chiles, seeded, finely chopped, 3 tablespoons fresh orange juice.Wear gloves when working with super-hot chiles like habaneros.

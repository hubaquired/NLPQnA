Kielbasa Sausage with Spiced Sauerkraut recipe. Combine onion, sauerkraut, wine, bay leaves, caraway seeds and peppercorns in heavy large saucepan. Cover and simmer mixture 30 minutes. Add sausage and simmer 20 minutes. Discard bay leaves and serve. Kielbasa Sausage with Spiced Sauerkraut has 395.0 calories. Kielbasa Sausage with Spiced Sauerkraut has 19.0 grams of protein. Kielbasa Sausage with Spiced Sauerkraut has 28.0 grams of fat. Kielbasa Sausage with Spiced Sauerkraut has 1592.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Kielbasa Sausage with Spiced Sauerkraut are 1 large onion, diced, 1 16-ounce can sauerkraut, 1 cup dry white wine, 2 bay leaves, 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds, 6 peppercorns, 1 pound Kielbasa sausage, sliced.For a cozy supper, accompany this quickly prepared dish with pumpernickel bread and hot mustard.

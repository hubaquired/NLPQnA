Ginger Cranberry Sauce recipe. Combine all of the ingredients in a heavy saucepan. Cook over medium heat until the berries pop open, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat. Skim foam off the surface with a metal spoon and discard. Cool to room temperature. Refrigerate, covered, for up to 3 months. The ingredients of Ginger Cranberry Sauce are 16 ounces fresh cranberries, picked over and rinsed, 2 cups granulated sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup fresh orange juice, 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger, Finely grated zest from 1 orange.Cranberries add sparkle to the meal.

Curried Pumpkin Seeds recipe. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line large rimmed baking sheet with parchment; spray with nonstick spray. Whisk egg white, lime juice, and oil in large bowl. Add next 4 ingredients; whisk. Add pumpkin seeds; toss. Transfer to baking sheet, spreading evenly. Bake until toasted and fragrant, stirring often, about 24 minutes. Cool on sheet. Do ahead: Can be made 5 days ahead. Store toasted seeds airtight at room temperature. Curried Pumpkin Seeds has 263.0 calories. Curried Pumpkin Seeds has 13.0 grams of protein. Curried Pumpkin Seeds has 22.0 grams of fat. Curried Pumpkin Seeds has 368.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Curried Pumpkin Seeds are Nonstick vegetable oil spray, 1 large egg white, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 teaspoon olive oil, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 tablespoon curry powder, 1 1/2 teaspoons coarse kosher salt, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 12 ounces hulled pumpkin seeds (about 2 1/2 cups).

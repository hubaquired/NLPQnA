No-Bake Chocolate and Cream Cheese Pie recipe. Combine bittersweet chocolate, cream and unsweetened chocolate in heavy medium saucepan. Stir over medium-low heat until melted and smooth. Cool to just lukewarm. Beat next 3 ingredients in large bowl until well blended. Beat in chocolate mixture. Spoon filling into crust; smooth top. Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours and up to 2 days. Serve chilled. No-Bake Chocolate and Cream Cheese Pie has 520.0 calories. No-Bake Chocolate and Cream Cheese Pie has 6.0 grams of protein. No-Bake Chocolate and Cream Cheese Pie has 35.0 grams of fat. No-Bake Chocolate and Cream Cheese Pie has 320.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of No-Bake Chocolate and Cream Cheese Pie are 6 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) or semisweet chocolate, chopped, 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1 ounce unsweetened chocolate, chopped, 2 8-ounce packages cream cheese, room temperature, 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon vanilla extract, 1 6-ounce purchased chocolate crumb crust.Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate shavings for a lovely presentation.

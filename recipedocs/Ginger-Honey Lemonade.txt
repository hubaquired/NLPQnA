Ginger-Honey Lemonade recipe. Bring 1 cup water, honey, sugar, and ginger to boil in heavy medium saucepan over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Boil 5 minutes. Cool. Strain syrup into pitcher. Mix in lemon juice and remaining 2 cups water. Fill pitcher with ice. Let stand 5 minutes. Fill 4 to 6 tall glasses with ice; add lemonade. Ginger-Honey Lemonade has 247.0 calories. Ginger-Honey Lemonade has 1.0 grams of protein. Ginger-Honey Lemonade has 11.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger-Honey Lemonade are 3 cups water, 1/2 cup honey, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup minced peeled fresh ginger, 1 1/4 cups fresh lemon juice, Ice cubes.A lovely drink for a picnic.

Blue Cheese Dip with Onion and Celery recipe. Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Using electric mixer, beat until blended. Transfer to serving bowl, cover and chill dip at least 1 hour and up to 3 days. Blue Cheese Dip with Onion and Celery has 404.0 calories. Blue Cheese Dip with Onion and Celery has 5.0 grams of protein. Blue Cheese Dip with Onion and Celery has 41.0 grams of fat. Blue Cheese Dip with Onion and Celery has 514.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Blue Cheese Dip with Onion and Celery are 1 8-ounce package cream cheese, room temperature, 1 cup crumbled Roquefort or other blue cheese (about 3 1/2 ounces), 1 cup mayonnaise, 1 cup sour cream, 1/2 cup finely chopped onion, 1/2 cup finely chopped celery, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 teaspoon celery salt, 1 teaspoon ground black pepper.

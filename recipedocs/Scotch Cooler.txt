Scotch Cooler recipe.  Put the Scotch, crème de menthe, and ice cubes in a highball glass. Fill with soda. The ingredients of Scotch Cooler are 3 ounces Scotch whiskey, 3 dashes white crème de menthe, 3 or 4 ice cubes, Chilled club soda.

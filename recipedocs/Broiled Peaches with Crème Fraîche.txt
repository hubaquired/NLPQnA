Broiled Peaches with Crème Fraîche recipe. Preheat broiler. Line a small shallow baking pan with foil and arrange peaches cut sides up. Sprinkle peaches evenly with sugar and let stand 10 minutes. Broil 4 to 5 inches from heat until sugar is golden brown and peaches are tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Serve warm. Broiled Peaches with Crème Fraîche has 52.0 calories. The ingredients of Broiled Peaches with Crème Fraîche are 4 firm-ripe peaches, halved and pitted, 3 tablespoons sugar, Accompaniment: crème fraîche or sour cream.

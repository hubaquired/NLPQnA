Quick-Pickled Strawberries recipe. Place strawberries in a 1-qt. heatproof jar. Bring vinegar, sugar, salt, and 2/3 cup water to a boil in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring to dissolve sugar and salt. Pour over strawberries. Let cool; cover and chill. Strawberries can be pickled 5 days ahead. Keep chilled. Quick-Pickled Strawberries has 169.0 calories. Quick-Pickled Strawberries has 1.0 grams of protein. Quick-Pickled Strawberries has 2886.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Quick-Pickled Strawberries are 1 pound ripe but firm strawberries, hulled, halved if large, 1 1/2 cups white balsamic vinegar, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons kosher salt.After you finish pickling the strawberries, hang on to the brine, which can be used in place of mild vinegars.

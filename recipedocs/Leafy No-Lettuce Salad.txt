Leafy No-Lettuce Salad recipe. Mix lemon juice and shallot in a small bowl and season with salt and pepper. Let macerate for 30 minutes. Whisk in 1/4 cup oil and season to taste with salt and pepper. Whisk in more oil if dressing is too tart. Arrange nectarine slices on a serving platter. Scatter almonds and blue cheese over. Combine all greens and herbs in a large bowl. Add dressing to coat amount needed will vary depending on greens used). Season to taste with salt and pepper. Toss greens and arrange over nectarine mixture. Leafy No-Lettuce Salad has 224.0 calories. Leafy No-Lettuce Salad has 11.0 grams of protein. Leafy No-Lettuce Salad has 16.0 grams of fat. Leafy No-Lettuce Salad has 343.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Leafy No-Lettuce Salad are 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 tablespoons minced shallot, Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, 1/4 cup (or more) olive oil, 3 nectarines, plums, or peaches, halved, pitted, thinly sliced, 1 cup (scant) almonds, pistachios, or hazelnuts (about 4 ounces), toasted, chopped, 1 cup crumbled blue cheese (about 4 ounces), 8 cups mixed leafy greens (such as purslane, mâche, baby kale, watercress, and tatsoi), 1 1/2 cups chervil sprigs, 1 cup (packed) assorted tender herb leaves (such as anise hyssop, tarragon, and mint).There's so much more to leafy greens than lettuce. Here, they're tossed with a light vinaigrette and spooned over sliced stone fruit, nuts, and blue cheese.

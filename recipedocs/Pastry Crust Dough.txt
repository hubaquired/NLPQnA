Pastry Crust Dough recipe.  Mix first 4 ingredients in large bowl. Add butter; rub in with fingertips until mixture resembles coarse meal. Add yolks; mix with fork until dough begins to clump together. Shape dough into 6 balls for Walnut Tartlets, 2 balls for Apple Galette or 1 ball for Apricot Tart. Flatten balls) into disks); wrap in plastic. Chill 30 minutes. Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep chilled. Let dough soften slightly at room temperature before rolling out.)

Avocado Radish Salad with Lime Dressing recipe. In a bowl whisk together lime juice, oil, and salt and pepper to taste. Add avocado and toss gently with a rubber spatula to coat. Divide romaine between 2 salad plates, making a nest on each, and put avocado mixture in centers. Sprinkle salads with radishes. Avocado Radish Salad with Lime Dressing has 265.0 calories. Avocado Radish Salad with Lime Dressing has 3.0 grams of protein. Avocado Radish Salad with Lime Dressing has 25.0 grams of fat. Avocado Radish Salad with Lime Dressing has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Avocado Radish Salad with Lime Dressing are 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice, or to taste, 1 1/2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 firm-ripe avocado (preferably California), peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes, 3 cups shredded romaine, washed well and spun dry, 3 radishes, chopped fine.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

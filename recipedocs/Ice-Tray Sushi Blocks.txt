Ice-Tray Sushi Blocks recipe.  Combine all the ingredients except the rice in a bowl. To make the sushi-rice blocks, use an ice-cube tray as a mold small cups, mini-muffin tins, and tartlet pans will also work). Prepare the mold by sprinkling it lightly with water. Press the rice into the tray squares, then use your finger to push a hole 3/4 of the way into each square. Turn the mold upside down onto waxed paper, and tap until the squares fall out.

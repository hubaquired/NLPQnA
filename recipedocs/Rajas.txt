Rajas recipe. Heat oil in heavy small skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until very tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in chilies and cream. Simmer until mixture is thickened and flavors are blended, about 5 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Rajas has 498.0 calories. Rajas has 6.0 grams of protein. Rajas has 50.0 grams of fat. Rajas has 45.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rajas are 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, 1/2 onion, sliced, 1 garlic clove, sliced, 1 4-ounce can whole green chilies, sliced, 1/2 cup whipping cream.This quick and easy rendition of the classic is made with canned chilies.

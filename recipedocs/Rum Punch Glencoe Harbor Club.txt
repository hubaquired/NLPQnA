Rum Punch Glencoe Harbor Club recipe. In a pitcher combine the juices, the rums, the brandy, the grenadine syrup, the Triple Sec and the bitters and chill the punch, covered, until it is cold. Pour the punch into long-stemmed glasses filled with ice cubes and garnish each drink with an orange slice and a cherry. Rum Punch Glencoe Harbor Club has 238.0 calories. Rum Punch Glencoe Harbor Club has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rum Punch Glencoe Harbor Club are 3/4 cup orange juice, 3/4 cup pineapple juice, 3/4 cup Gosling's Black Seal rum, 3/4 cup golden rum, 1/3 cup peach or apricot brandy, 1/3 cup grenadine syrup, 2 tablespoons Triple Sec or Cointreau, 2 tablespoons Angostura bitters, Orange slices for garnish if desired, Maraschino cherries for garnish if desired.Tojo, the bartender at the Glencoe Harbor Club, in Paget, Bermuda, introduced us to this traditional island cocktail.

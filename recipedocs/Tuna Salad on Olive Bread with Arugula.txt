Tuna Salad on Olive Bread with Arugula recipe.  Mix tuna, 1/4 cup mayonnaise, and next 6 ingredients in medium bowl to blend. Season with salt and pepper. Divide tuna salad among 6 bread slices; top with arugula leaves. Spread remaining mayonnaise on remaining 6 bread slices; place atop arugula. Cut sandwiches in half; wrap each tightly in plastic wrap. Can be made 1 day ahead. Refrigerate.) Tuna Salad on Olive Bread with Arugula has 403.0 calories. Tuna Salad on Olive Bread with Arugula has 19.0 grams of protein. Tuna Salad on Olive Bread with Arugula has 25.0 grams of fat. Tuna Salad on Olive Bread with Arugula has 703.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tuna Salad on Olive Bread with Arugula are 2 6-ounce packages albacore tuna packed in water, drained, 3/4 cup mayonnaise, 1/2 cup chopped green onions, 1/4 cup diced seeded English hothouse cucumber, 1/4 cup minced fresh dill, 1/4 cup drained capers, 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons minced fresh tarragon, 12 1/3-inch-thick slices olive bread, 1 cup arugula.Complete this one with cherry tomatoes, potato chips, and lemonade.

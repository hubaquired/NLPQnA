Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Honey Glaze recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Arrange sweet potatoes in 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Stir butter, honey, and lemon juice in small saucepan over medium heat until butter melts. Pour butter mixture over sweet potatoes; toss to coat. Sprinkle generously with salt and pepper. Bake sweet potatoes until tender when pierced with skewer, stirring and turning occasionally, about 50 minutes. Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Honey Glaze has 335.0 calories. Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Honey Glaze has 3.0 grams of protein. Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Honey Glaze has 12.0 grams of fat. Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Honey Glaze has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Honey Glaze are 2 1/4 pounds red-skinned sweet potatoes (yams), peeled, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces (about 7 cups), 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) butter, 3 tablespoons honey, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice.

Crudites and Grilled Sausage with Sweet and Hot Chutneys recipe.  Arrange vegetables around edge of platter. Grill or panfry sausages until brown and heated through. Cut into 1-inch pieces. Arrange sausage pieces in center of platter, placing toothpick in each piece. Serve with chutneys. The ingredients of Crudites and Grilled Sausage with Sweet and Hot Chutneys are Vegetables to dip (such as carrot sticks, steamed broccoli florets, jicama and cauliflower), 3 pounds fully cooked sausages (such as bratwurst or kielbasa), Sweet Chutney, Hot Chutney.

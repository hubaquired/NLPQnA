Millionire recipe. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into a cocktail glass. The ingredients of Millionire are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 3/4 ounce Pernod, anisette, 1 egg white, 1/2 cup crushed ice. Gin Liqueur Alcoholic Cocktail Party Spirit Pernod House & Garden Drink.

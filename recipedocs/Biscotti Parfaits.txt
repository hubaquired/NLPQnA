Biscotti Parfaits recipe. Place biscotti in heavy sealable plastic bag. Crush biscotti coarsely with rolling pin. In each of 2 brandy snifters or tall wine goblets, layer 1/4 of crumbs, 2 teaspoons liqueur, 1/4 cup ice cream, 2 tablespoons diced fruit, 2 teaspoons liqueur, 1/4 cup ice cream, 1/4 crumbs and 2 teaspoons liqueur. Serve immediately or cover and freeze up to 1 hour. Biscotti Parfaits has 356.0 calories. Biscotti Parfaits has 4.0 grams of protein. Biscotti Parfaits has 11.0 grams of fat.

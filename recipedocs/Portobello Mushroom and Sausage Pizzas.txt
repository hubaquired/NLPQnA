Portobello Mushroom and Sausage Pizzas recipe.  Preheat oven to 350°F. Brush mushrooms with oil; arrange rounded side down on baking sheet. Bake mushrooms until almost tender, about 8 minutes. Maintain oven temperature. Sauté sausage in medium skillet over medium-high heat until brown and cooked through, breaking up sausage with back of spoon, about 6 minutes. Using slotted spoon, remove sausage from skillet. Divide sausage among mushrooms. Top each with crushed red pepper, then 2 tablespoons sauce and 2 tablespoons mozzarella cheese. Bake mushroom pizzas until heated through, about 10 minutes. Top each with basil leaf, if desired. Portobello Mushroom and Sausage Pizzas has 447.0 calories. Portobello Mushroom and Sausage Pizzas has 19.0 grams of protein. Portobello Mushroom and Sausage Pizzas has 39.0 grams of fat. Portobello Mushroom and Sausage Pizzas has 1015.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Portobello Mushroom and Sausage Pizzas are 8 4-inch-diameter portobello mushrooms, stemmed, dark gills scraped away, Olive oil, 3 sweet Italian sausages (about 10 ounces), casings removed, Dried crushed red pepper, 1 cup purchased marinara sauce, 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese, 8 fresh basil leaves (optional).This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

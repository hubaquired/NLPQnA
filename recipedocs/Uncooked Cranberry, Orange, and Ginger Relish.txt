Uncooked Cranberry, Orange, and Ginger Relish recipe. In a food processor chop fine the gingerroot and the orange, add the cranberries, and pulse the motor until the berries are chopped fine. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and stir in the sugar. Chill the relish, covered, for at least 30 minutes. The relish keeps, covered and chilled, for 2 weeks. Uncooked Cranberry, Orange, and Ginger Relish has 206.0 calories. Uncooked Cranberry, Orange, and Ginger Relish has 1.0 grams of protein. Uncooked Cranberry, Orange, and Ginger Relish has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Uncooked Cranberry, Orange, and Ginger Relish are 2 teaspoons chopped peeled fresh gingerroot, 1 large navel orange, including the rind, chopped, a 12-ounce bag of cranberries, picked over, 3/4 cup sugar, or to taste.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

Spinach-Pineapple-Mint Juice recipe.  Juice the lemon using a manual citrus juicer. Juice the remaining ingredients in a juicer, and then combine with the lemon juice. Store in an airtight glass container in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. Spinach-Pineapple-Mint Juice has 194.0 calories. Spinach-Pineapple-Mint Juice has 3.0 grams of protein. Spinach-Pineapple-Mint Juice has 1.0 grams of fat. Spinach-Pineapple-Mint Juice has 39.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spinach-Pineapple-Mint Juice are 1/2 lemon, 3 cups spinach, 2 Granny Smith apples, sliced, 2 cups cubed pineapple, 8 mint leaves.The mint in this juice ties together the sweet pineapple and tart green apple so well that you won't even notice the spinach.

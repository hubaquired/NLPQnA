Banana-Cinnamon French Toast recipe. Blend first 4 ingredients in processor until smooth. Transfer to 13x9-inch pan. Place bread in milk mixture and soak until all liquid is absorbed, turning bread occasionally, about 20 minutes. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add bread and cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side. Serve; pass syrup separately. Banana-Cinnamon French Toast has 505.0 calories. Banana-Cinnamon French Toast has 17.0 grams of protein. Banana-Cinnamon French Toast has 26.0 grams of fat. Banana-Cinnamon French Toast has 356.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Banana-Cinnamon French Toast are 1 large very ripe banana (about 8 ounces), 2 eggs, 1/2 cup milk, 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 4 slices whole wheat bread, 3 tablespoons butter, Maple syrup.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less, but requires additional unattended time.

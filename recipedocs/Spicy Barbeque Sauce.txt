Spicy Barbeque Sauce recipe.  Heat vegetable oil in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and celery and sauté until tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic, paprika and cayenne and stir 1 minute. Add ketchup, beer, vinegar and Worcestershire sauce. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer uncovered until flavors blend and sauce is slightly reduced, stirring occasionally, about 30 minutes. Can be prepared 1 week ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Spicy Barbeque Sauce has 199.0 calories. Spicy Barbeque Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Spicy Barbeque Sauce has 10.0 grams of fat. Spicy Barbeque Sauce has 617.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Barbeque Sauce are 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 medium onion, finely chopped, 1/3 cup chopped celery, 1 tablespoon minced garlic, 1 tablespoon paprika, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 3/4 cup ketchup, 1/2 cup beer, 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce.This is terrific brushed onto grilled chicken or ribs, or spread onto brisket or barbecued-pork sandwiches.

Croque Monsieur recipe. Spread dressing on both pieces of bread. Place two slices of cheese onto each side of bread. Put ham in center of bread. Close the sandwich. Spread evenly 1/2 teaspoon of lightly salted butter onto each side of the bread. Grill over moderate heat for approximately 3-4 minutes per side. Croque Monsieur has 819.0 calories. Croque Monsieur has 55.0 grams of protein. Croque Monsieur has 48.0 grams of fat. Croque Monsieur has 1751.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Croque Monsieur are 1 teaspoon thousand island dressing, 2 slices white bread, 4 slices Swiss cheese, 4 ounces of ham (5 slices), 1 teaspoon lightly salted butter.Ham and Thousand Island Dressing.

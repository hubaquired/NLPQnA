Hummus recipe. Put everything except the parsley in a food processor and begin to process; add the chickpea liquid or water as needed to allow the machine to produce a smooth puree. Taste and adjust the seasoning I often find I like to add much more lemon juice). Serve, drizzled with the olive oil and sprinkled with a bit more cumin or paprika and some parsley. Hummus has 205.0 calories. Hummus has 6.0 grams of protein. Hummus has 16.0 grams of fat. Hummus has 430.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hummus are 2 cups drained well-cooked or canned chickpeas, liquid reserved, 1/2 cup tahini (sesame paste), optional, with some of its oil, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, plus oil for drizzling, 2 cloves garlic, peeled, or to taste, Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste, 1 tablespoon ground cumin or paprika, or to taste, plus a sprinkling for garnish, Juice of 1 lemon, plus more as needed, Chopped fresh parsley leaves for garnish.Editor's note: The recipe below is from The Best Recipes in the World by Mark Bittman.

Seven Minute Frosting recipe. In large metal bowl, whisk together 1/3 cup water, sugar, egg whites, cream of tartar, and salt. Set bowl over pan of barely simmering water and mix with handheld electric mixer at low speed. Gradually increase speed to high, beating until mixture holds stiff peaks, about 5 minutes. Transfer bowl from pan to folded kitchen towel on counter and continue beating until mixture is cool and billowy, about 2 minutes more. Beat in vanilla. Frosting can be made 4 hours ahead and chilled, covered.) Seven Minute Frosting has 241.0 calories. Seven Minute Frosting has 3.0 grams of protein. Seven Minute Frosting has 115.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Seven Minute Frosting are 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar, 3 large egg whites, 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract.This frosting is fluffy and foolproof.

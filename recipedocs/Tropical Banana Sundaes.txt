Tropical Banana Sundaes recipe. Place 1 scoop vanilla ice cream in each of 4 dessert bowls. Place bowls in freezer. Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add bananas; sauté until just beginning to soften, about 3 minutes. Spoon bananas over ice cream. Top with chocolate sauce and coconut. The ingredients of Tropical Banana Sundaes are Vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt, 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 4 ripe but firm bananas, peeled, thickly sliced on diagonal, 1 cup purchased chocolate sauce, warmed, Toasted sweetened coconut.

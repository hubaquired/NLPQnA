Apple and Sausage Stuffing recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage; sauté until cooked through and brown, breaking into pieces with spoon, about 8 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer sausage to large bowl. Add celery and next 6 ingredients to drippings in skillet. Sauté over medium heat until vegetables are soft, about 5 minutes. Discard bay leaf. Add mixture to sausage. Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill. Reheat to lukewarm before continuing.) Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish. Add bread to sausage mixture. Whisk milk, broth, and butter in bowl to blend. Mix into stuffing; season stuffing with salt and pepper. Mix in eggs; transfer to prepared dish. Bake uncovered until cooked through and brown, about 50 minutes. Apple and Sausage Stuffing has 540.0 calories. Apple and Sausage Stuffing has 15.0 grams of protein. Apple and Sausage Stuffing has 38.0 grams of fat. Apple and Sausage Stuffing has 714.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apple and Sausage Stuffing are 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, 1 pound spicy pork bulk sausage, 1 cup diced celery, 1 cup diced onion, 1 cup diced peeled cored apple, 2 garlic cloves, minced, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, 2 teaspoons minced fresh sage, 1 bay leaf, 8 cups 1-inch cubes French bread with crusts (from 1-pound loaf), 1 cup whole milk, 1 cup low-salt chicken broth, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, melted, 3 large eggs, beaten to blend.This delicious stuffing is similar to a savory bread pudding.

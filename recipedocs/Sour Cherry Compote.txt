Sour Cherry Compote recipe.  Combine water, sugar and lemon juice in heavy medium saucepan. Scrape seeds from vanilla bean into saucepan; add bean. Bring to boil over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Boil until thin syrup forms, about 7 minutes. Reduce heat to medium; add cherries. Stir 1 minute. Cool compote. Cover and chill until cold. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Keep chilled.) Sour Cherry Compote has 150.0 calories. Sour Cherry Compote has 1.0 grams of protein. Sour Cherry Compote has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sour Cherry Compote are 2/3 cup water, 6 tablespoons sugar, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 vanilla bean, halved lengthwise, 2 1/2 cups (1 pound) cherries, pitted.

Balsamic-Roasted Acorn Squash with Hot Chiles and Honey recipe.  Preheat oven to 400°F. Place acorn squash halves, cut side up, on rimmed baking sheet. Brush both sides with 2 tablespoons oil. Whisk 4 tablespoons oil with remaining ingredients in bowl; divide glaze among hollows of squash halves. Brush glaze over cut surfaces, leaving excess glaze in squash hollows. Bake until squash are very tender and brown, brushing cut surfaces every 15 minutes with glaze in squash hollows, about 1 hour. The ingredients of Balsamic-Roasted Acorn Squash with Hot Chiles and Honey are 3 acorn squash, halved lengthwise, seeded, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 3 tablespoons honey, 2 tablespoons minced peperoncini, 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme, 1 teaspoon salt.Makes 6 servings.

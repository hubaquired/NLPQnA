Sausages Braised in Beer, Apples, and Onions recipe. Bring beer, onion, and apples to a bare simmer. Add sausages and cook 3 to 5 minutes or until heated through. Reserve beer mixture. Grill sausages until golden brown, about 2 minutes. Transfer beer mixture with a slotted spoon to a platter and top with grilled sausages. The ingredients of Sausages Braised in Beer, Apples, and Onions are 4 bottles lager-style beer, 1 large onion, thinly sliced, 2 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored and sliced, pre-cooked sausages such as weisswurst, knockwurst, and bratwurst.

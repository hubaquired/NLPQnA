Toasted Nori Mayonnaise recipe. Finely grind nori in a spice mill or a blender. Mix nori, mayonnaise, and lime juice in a small bowl; season with salt. Mayonnaise can be made 5 days ahead. Cover and chill. The ingredients of Toasted Nori Mayonnaise are 3 toasted nori sheets, chopped, 1 cup mayonnaise, 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice, Kosher salt.Try this as a dip, on crackers with smoked fish, or over hard-boiled eggs.

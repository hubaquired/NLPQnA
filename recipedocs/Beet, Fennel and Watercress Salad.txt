Beet, Fennel and Watercress Salad recipe.  Preheat oven to 375°F. Cut tops and root ends off beets. Wrap beets in aluminum foil and bake until tender, about 50 minutes. Cool slightly. Peel beets. Slice beets into rounds; stack rounds and cut into strips. Combine fresh lemon juice and honey in small bowl. Gradually mix in olive oil. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Dressing can be prepared 1 day ahead. Wrap beets tightly and refrigerate. Cover dressing and let stand at room temperature.) Place watercress in bowl and toss with enough dressing to coat. Arrange on platter. Top with fennel and then beets. Drizzle remaining dressing over. Beet, Fennel and Watercress Salad has 207.0 calories. Beet, Fennel and Watercress Salad has 2.0 grams of protein. Beet, Fennel and Watercress Salad has 18.0 grams of fat. Beet, Fennel and Watercress Salad has 66.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Beet, Fennel and Watercress Salad are 2 beet bunches with tops, tops reserved for Sunday Supper Macaroni and Cheese, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon honey, 1/2 cup olive oil, 2 watercress bunches, trimmed, 2 fennel bulbs, trimmed, cut into strips.

Fig Salad with Goat's Milk Yogurt and Pepper Cress recipe.  Whisk together first 5 ingredients in medium bowl. Season with salt and reserve. Sprinkle figs with fleur de sel; set in center of plate. Drizzle dressing on figs; scatter pepper cress and mint over. Season with olive oil and Indonesian pepper. The ingredients of Fig Salad with Goat's Milk Yogurt and Pepper Cress are 1/2 cup goat's milk yogurt, 1/2 cup soft fresh goat cheese, crumbled, 2 teaspoons honey, 1/2 teaspoon (scant) vanilla extract, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 24 ripe black Mission figs, halved lengthwise, Fleur de sel*, 2 bunches pepper cress or watercress, thick stems trimmed (about 4 cups), 1 cup (loosely packed) small mint leaves, Extra-virgin olive oil, 1 dried Indonesian long pepper**.Watercress, black pepper, and plain yogurt, which are easier to find, can be substituted here.

Chipotle Mayonnaise recipe. Combine the chipotle chile, tofu, mayonnaise, ketchup and Dijon mustard in a food processor and process until smooth. The ingredients of Chipotle Mayonnaise are 1 chipotle chile, 3 ounces silken tofu, 3 tablespoons nonfat mayonnaise, 3 tablespoons ketchup, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard.Editor's note: The recipe below is excerpted from Cal-a-Vie Living: Gourmet Spa Cuisine and is part of a healthy and delicious spa menu developed exclusively for Epicurious by Cal-a-Vie.

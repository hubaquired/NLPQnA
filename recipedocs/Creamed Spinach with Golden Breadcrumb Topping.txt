Creamed Spinach with Golden Breadcrumb Topping recipe. Melt butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs; sauté until golden, about 2 minutes. Set aside. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter two 8x8-inch glass baking dishes. Mix spinach, sour cream, milk, cheese, onion, and nutmeg in large bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Mix in eggs. Divide spinach mixture between dishes. Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover; chill.) Top with breadcrumbs. Bake until set, about 30 minutes. Creamed Spinach with Golden Breadcrumb Topping has 269.0 calories. Creamed Spinach with Golden Breadcrumb Topping has 10.0 grams of protein. Creamed Spinach with Golden Breadcrumb Topping has 19.0 grams of fat. Creamed Spinach with Golden Breadcrumb Topping has 288.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamed Spinach with Golden Breadcrumb Topping are 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, 2 cups fresh breadcrumbs (made from white bread), 6 10-ounce packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, squeezed dry, 4 cups sour cream, 2 cups whole milk, 2 cups (about 8 ounces) grated extra-sharp cheddar cheese, 1 cup chopped onion, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 2 large eggs, beaten to blend.Easy and delicious. Sautéed breadcrumbs top a mixture of spinach, cheese, and sour cream.

Cheddar Potato Soup with Bacon recipe.  Chop bacon and in a large heavy saucepan cook over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until crisp. Transfer bacon with a slotted spoon to paper towels to drain, reserving fat in pan. While bacon is cooking, chop onion. Peel potatoes and cut into 1/2-inch cubes. Cook onion in reserved fat over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until softened. In a measuring cup stir together flour and water and add to onion with potatoes and broth. Simmer mixture 5 minutes, or until potatoes are just tender. Add wine and simmer 1 minute. Reduce heat to low and gradually stir in Cheddar do not let boil). Divide soup among 4 bowls and sprinkle with bacon and chives. Cheddar Potato Soup with Bacon has 262.0 calories. Cheddar Potato Soup with Bacon has 12.0 grams of protein. Cheddar Potato Soup with Bacon has 16.0 grams of fat. Cheddar Potato Soup with Bacon has 349.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cheddar Potato Soup with Bacon are 4 bacon slices, 1 large onion, 1 pound boiling potatoes, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, 1 3/4 cups water, 1 1/2 cups chicken broth, 1/4 cup dry white wine, 2 cups grated sharp Cheddar (about 8 ounces), 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

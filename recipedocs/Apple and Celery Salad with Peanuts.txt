Apple and Celery Salad with Peanuts recipe. Whisk together lemon juice and mustard in a large bowl. Add oil in a stream, whisking until emulsified. Add apples, celery, and parsley, tossing to coat. Just before serving, sprinkle with peanuts. Apple and Celery Salad with Peanuts has 139.0 calories. Apple and Celery Salad with Peanuts has 2.0 grams of protein. Apple and Celery Salad with Peanuts has 10.0 grams of fat. Apple and Celery Salad with Peanuts has 42.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Apple and Celery Salad with Peanuts are 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 1/4 cup olive oil, 3 crisp red apples such as Fuji or Rome, cut into 1/4-inch-thick julienne, 2 tart green apples such as Granny Smith, cut into 1/4-inch-thick julienne, 4 large celery ribs, cut into 1/4-inch-thick julienne, 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, 1/3 cup dry-roasted peanuts, coarsely chopped.

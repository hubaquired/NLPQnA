Roasted Sweet Potato Purée with Lime recipe. Preheat oven to 400°F. Prick sweet potatoes and roast on a foil-lined baking sheet in middle of oven 1 hour, or until very soft. Cool potatoes just until they can be handled and scoop flesh into a food processor.Purée potatoes until smooth. Add lime juice and salt to taste and purée mixture until combined well. Sweet potato purée may be made 2 days ahead and chilled, its surface covered with plastic wrap. Roasted Sweet Potato Purée with Lime has 124.0 calories. Roasted Sweet Potato Purée with Lime has 2.0 grams of protein. Roasted Sweet Potato Purée with Lime has 78.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Sweet Potato Purée with Lime are four 5-ounce sweet potatoes, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice.

Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe recipe.  Heat oil in heavy small saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until beginning to color, about 1 minute. Remove from heat. Cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water until beginning to soften, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes. Add broccoli rabe and cook until pasta is just tender, but still firm to bite, about 3 minutes. Drain. Transfer pasta and broccoli rabe to large bowl. Pour garlic oil over. Sprinkle with cheeses and toss to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe has 365.0 calories. Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe has 15.0 grams of protein. Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe has 14.0 grams of fat. Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe has 229.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe are 1/4 cup olive oil, 4 garlic cloves, minced, 12 ounces orecchiette or shell pasta, 1 pound broccoli rabe, trimmed, chopped, 2/3 cup freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese (about 2 ounces), 1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese (about 1 ounce).In Apulia, women still make pasta at home. Their favorite pasta shape - and the symbol of the region - is arecchiette, or "little ears". Called recchie or recchietelle in the local dialect, orecchiette is eaten just about daily, but is prepared a different way in almost every village. The sauces for this whimsical pasta are straightforward and usually contain vegetables, beans or legumes. This recipe is one of the most basic.

Raspberry Lemonade recipe.  Puree thawed berries in processor. Strain into pitcher, pressing on solids in strainer to extract as much liquid as possible. Mix in lemon juice and sugar; stir until sugar dissolves. Mix in 2 cups water. Fill pitcher with ice. Let stand 5 minutes. Fill 4 to 6 tall glasses with ice; add lemonade. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh raspberries. Raspberry Lemonade has 199.0 calories. Raspberry Lemonade has 1.0 grams of protein. Raspberry Lemonade has 1.0 grams of fat. Raspberry Lemonade has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Raspberry Lemonade are 1 10-ounce package frozen raspberries in syrup, thawed, 1 1/4 cups fresh lemon juice, 3/4 cup sugar, 2 cups water, Ice cubes, Lemon slices (optional), Fresh raspberries (optional).Add a little fizz by substituting sparkling water or club soda for the two cups of water.

The ingredients of Sesame Eggplant with Green Onions are 1/2 cup olive oil, 5 large green onions; 4 coarsely chopped, 1 thinly sliced for garnish, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 teaspoons Asian sesame oil, 2 teaspoons sesame seeds, 2 eggplants (about 2 1/2 pounds), cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-thick slices.This simple side dish can be served warm or at room temperature.

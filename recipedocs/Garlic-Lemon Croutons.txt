Garlic-Lemon Croutons recipe.  Preheat oven to 425°F. Mix first 4 ingredients in small bowl. Spread over baguette slices; arrange on baking sheet. Bake croutons until crisp, about 5 minutes. The ingredients of Garlic-Lemon Croutons are 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon chopped garlic, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel, 12 thin baguette bread slices.

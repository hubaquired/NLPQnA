Armagnac Toffee Sauce recipe. In a heavy saucepan simmer cream with butter and brown sugar, stirring, 3 to 4 minutes, or until slightly thickened. Remove pan from heat and stir in Armagnac. Sauce keeps, covered and chilled, 1 week. Reheat sauce before serving. Armagnac Toffee Sauce has 520.0 calories. Armagnac Toffee Sauce has 1.0 grams of protein. Armagnac Toffee Sauce has 45.0 grams of fat. Armagnac Toffee Sauce has 26.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Armagnac Toffee Sauce are 1 1/2 cups heavy cream, 1 1/2 sticks (3/4 cup) unsalted butter, softened, 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar, 3 tablespoons Armagnac.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Mulled Grapefruit Juice recipe. In a saucepan, combine the first five ingredients. Stirring as it warms to dissolve the honey, bring the water to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Strain mixture through a fine sieve into another saucepan. Stir in the grapefruit juice and reheat slowly. Serve warm in mugs. Mulled Grapefruit Juice has 131.0 calories. Mulled Grapefruit Juice has 1.0 grams of protein. Mulled Grapefruit Juice has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mulled Grapefruit Juice are 2 cups water, 6 cardamom pods, 3 1/4-inch-thick rounds of peeled fresh ginger, 2 whole cloves, 3 tablespoons honey, 3 cups grapefruit juice.

Tequila Sunrise recipe.  In a large pitcher stir together well the tequila, the orange juice, and the grenadine. Divide the drink among 6 glasses filled with ice cubes. Garnish the drinks with the orange slices. Tequila Sunrise has 122.0 calories. Tequila Sunrise has 1.0 grams of protein. Tequila Sunrise has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tequila Sunrise are 2/3 cup tequila, 3 cups fresh orange juice, 1 tablespoon grenadine, or to taste, 6 orange slices for garnish.

Rhubarb Raspberry Sauce recipe. In a saucepan simmer sugar and water until sugar is dissolved. Add rhubarb and simmer, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Transfer mixture to a bowl to cool and stir in raspberries. Sauce may be made 1 day ahead and chilled, covered. Bring sauce to room temperature before serving. Rhubarb Raspberry Sauce has 174.0 calories. Rhubarb Raspberry Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Rhubarb Raspberry Sauce has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Rhubarb Raspberry Sauce are 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water, 3/4 pound fresh rhubarb, trimmed and cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices (2 cups total), 1 cup picked-over fresh raspberries, quartered.This recipe was created to accompany Rhubarb Raspberry Meringue Cake .

Spicy Tamarind Skirt Steak recipe.  Whisk chiles, tamarind, brown sugar, salt, and 1 tablespoon oil in a shallow baking dish until sugar is dissolved. Add steak and turn to coat. Cover and chill at least 4 hours. Heat 2 teaspoons oil in a large skillet over high heat. Working in 2 batches and adding remaining 2 teaspoons oil between batches, cook steak until deeply browned, 2–4 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer steak to a cutting board; let rest at least 5 minutes before slicing. DO AHEAD: Steak can be marinated 1 day ahead. Keep chilled. Spicy Tamarind Skirt Steak has 452.0 calories. Spicy Tamarind Skirt Steak has 35.0 grams of protein. Spicy Tamarind Skirt Steak has 30.0 grams of fat. Spicy Tamarind Skirt Steak has 1547.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Tamarind Skirt Steak are 3 Thai or serrano chiles, with seeds, thinly sliced into rounds, 1/4 cup tamarind concentrate, 3 tablespoons light brown sugar, 1 tablespoon kosher salt, 1 tablespoon plus 4 teaspoons vegetable oil, 1 1/2 pounds skirt steak, cut into 4 pieces, Ingredient info: Tamarind concentrate, often labeled "concentrate cooking tamarind," is available at Asian markets..You'll see fibers running through the cooked steak; make sure to cut across them for tender slices.

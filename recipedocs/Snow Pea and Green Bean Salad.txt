Snow Pea and Green Bean Salad recipe. In a small bowl whisk together the vinegar, the oil, and salt and pepper to taste. In a large saucepan of boiling salted water cook the green beans for 4 minutes, add the snow peas, and cook the vegetables for 30 seconds. Drain the vegetables, transfer them to a bowl of ice and cold water to stop the cooking, and drain them. Pat the vegetables dry between several thicknesses of paper towels and in a bowl toss them with the sunflower seeds and the dressing. Snow Pea and Green Bean Salad has 291.0 calories. Snow Pea and Green Bean Salad has 6.0 grams of protein. Snow Pea and Green Bean Salad has 25.0 grams of fat. Snow Pea and Green Bean Salad has 10.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Snow Pea and Green Bean Salad are 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar, 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 pound green beans, trimmed, 1/4 pound snow peas, trimmed and strings discarded, 2 tablespoons shelled sunflower seeds, toasted lightly.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

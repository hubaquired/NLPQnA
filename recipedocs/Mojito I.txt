Mojito I recipe. Pour rum into a 10-ounce highball glass, squeeze in the lime juice, drop in the peel. Add sugar and enough crushed ice to fill the glass two-thirds full. Fill up with club soda, stir gently and garnish with mint leaves. Mojito I has 147.0 calories. Mojito I has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mojito I are 2 ounces light rum, 1/2 lime, 1 teaspoon superfine sugar, Crushed ice, Chilled club soda, Mint leaves. Rum Alcoholic Cocktail Lime Mint Summer House & Garden Drink.

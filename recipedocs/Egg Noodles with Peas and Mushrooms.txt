Egg Noodles with Peas and Mushrooms recipe. Cook shallots in butter in a 10-inch heavy skillet over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until beginning to brown, 5 to 7 minutes. Add mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid mushrooms give off is evaporated and mushrooms are browned and tender, 7 to 8 minutes. Stir in peas, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring frequently, until heated through, 1 to 2 minutes. Cook noodles in a 4- to 6-quart pot of boiling salted water until tender, 7 to 8 minutes. Drain well in a colander, then immediately add to mushroom mixture, tossing to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Egg Noodles with Peas and Mushrooms has 377.0 calories. Egg Noodles with Peas and Mushrooms has 14.0 grams of protein. Egg Noodles with Peas and Mushrooms has 12.0 grams of fat. Egg Noodles with Peas and Mushrooms has 359.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Egg Noodles with Peas and Mushrooms are 2/3 cup sliced shallots (2 large), 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, 10 oz mushrooms, trimmed and quartered lengthwise (4 cups), 1 (10-oz) package frozen baby peas, thawed, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon black pepper, 1/2 lb medium or wide egg noodles.

Whole Wheat Pancake and Waffle Mix recipe. Mix all ingredients together and store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Whisk the egg, buttermilk and oil in a large bowl. Whisk in the pancake mix until just combined. Heat a large pan or griddle over medium heat and grease with butter or oil. Pour about 1 tablespoon of the pancake mixture onto the griddle, making as many pancakes as will fit and cook for 2 minutes. Flip the pancakes and cook for one minute longer and serve. The ingredients of Whole Wheat Pancake and Waffle Mix are 6 cups white whole wheat flour, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons baking powder, 1 tablespoon baking soda, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 large egg, whisked, 1 cup buttermilk, 1 tablespoon vegetable or canola oil, 1 cup pancake mix (above).

Pendennis Eggnog recipe. Pour the bourbon into a bowl and stir in the sugar. Set aside for 2 hours. Beat the egg yolks until light and slowly beat in the bourbon. Set aside for another 2 hours. When ready to serve beat the cream until stiff; beat the egg whites until they stand in peaks. Pour the bourbon mixture into a punch bowl and fold in the cream then the egg whites. Grate a little nutmeg on top. Serve in 4-ounce punch glasses. The ingredients of Pendennis Eggnog are 1 bottle bourbon, 1 pound superfine sugar, 12 eggs, separated, 2 quarts heavy cream, Nutmeg.

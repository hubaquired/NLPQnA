Crostini with Burrata Cheese, Pink Pepper, and Arugula recipe. Preheat broiler. Place bread on baking sheet. Brush with oil; rub with garlic. Broil until golden, about 1 1/2 minutes per side. Transfer toasts to platter. Top with arugula and cheese. Drizzle with oil. Sprinkle with pepper and fleur de sel. *A type of sea salt; available at some supermarkets and specialty foods stores. The ingredients of Crostini with Burrata Cheese, Pink Pepper, and Arugula are 16 1/4-inch-thick baguette slices, Extra-virgin olive oil, 1/2 garlic clove, Arugula leaves (about 3 ounces), 16 tablespoons burrata cheese (about 12 ounces), 2 teaspoons pink peppercorns, Fleur de sel*.Burrata, an ultra-creamy fresh mozzarella, is available at specialty cheese shops.

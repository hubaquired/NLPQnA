Baby Peas with Mustard Horseradish Butter recipe.  Cook peas with water and salt in a 2-quart saucepan, covered, over moderate heat, until tender, 5 to 8 minutes. Meanwhile, mash together remaining ingredients and salt and pepper to taste in a small bowl. Drain peas in a colander and return to pan, then stir in butter mixture. Baby Peas with Mustard Horseradish Butter has 116.0 calories. Baby Peas with Mustard Horseradish Butter has 3.0 grams of protein. Baby Peas with Mustard Horseradish Butter has 8.0 grams of fat. Baby Peas with Mustard Horseradish Butter has 365.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baby Peas with Mustard Horseradish Butter are 1 (10-ounce) package frozen baby peas, 1/4 cup water, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 2 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened, 1 tablespoon finely chopped shallot, 2 teaspoons bottled horseradish (not drained), 1 teaspoon grainy mustard.

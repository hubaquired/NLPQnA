Cauliflower with Horseradish Sauce recipe. Bring 1 inch of water to a boil in a 5-quart wide heavy pot. Steam cauliflower in a steamer rack set over boiling water in pot, covered, until just tender, about 12 minutes. Meanwhile, whisk together remaining ingredients in a bowl. Serve cauliflower topped with sauce. Cauliflower with Horseradish Sauce has 157.0 calories. Cauliflower with Horseradish Sauce has 7.0 grams of protein. Cauliflower with Horseradish Sauce has 9.0 grams of fat. Cauliflower with Horseradish Sauce has 436.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cauliflower with Horseradish Sauce are 1 (2 1/2- to 3-lb) head of cauliflower, cut lengthwise into 8 wedges, 2 tablespoons bottled horseradish (not drained), 1/4 cup water, 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill, 2/3 cup sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon salt.Visually arresting and ready in a snap, these steamed cauliflower wedges are the side dish of your dreams.

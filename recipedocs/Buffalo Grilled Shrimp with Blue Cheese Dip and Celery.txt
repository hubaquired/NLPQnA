The ingredients of Buffalo Grilled Shrimp with Blue Cheese Dip and Celery are 1/2 cup sour cream, 1/4 cup finely chopped scallions, 2 tablespoons finely chopped flat-leaf parsley, 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese (2 ounces), 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 teaspoon milk, 18 jumbo shrimp (1 1/2 to 2 pounds), peeled, leaving last shell segment and tail fan attached, then deveined, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 stick unsalted butter, melted, 1/4 cup hot sauce such as Frank's RedHot, 1/2 bunch celery, cut into 4-inch sticks.Hot-wing aficionados will flip for these grilled shrimp, seasoned with bottled hot sauce and melted butter.

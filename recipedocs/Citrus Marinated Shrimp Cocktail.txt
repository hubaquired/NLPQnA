Citrus Marinated Shrimp Cocktail recipe. Combine juices, ketchup, vodka and hot pepper sauce in large bowl. Whisk in oil. Add shrimp, onion and cilantro and mix well. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours and up to 6 hours. Drain before serving. Citrus Marinated Shrimp Cocktail has 262.0 calories. Citrus Marinated Shrimp Cocktail has 17.0 grams of protein. Citrus Marinated Shrimp Cocktail has 10.0 grams of fat. Citrus Marinated Shrimp Cocktail has 925.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Citrus Marinated Shrimp Cocktail are 1 cup orange juice, 1 cup fresh lemon juice, 3/4 cup ketchup, 1/3 cup vodka, 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 1/2 pounds cooked peeled large shrimp, 1 small red onion, thinly sliced (about 1 3/4 cups), 1 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro."I'm originally from Ecuador, and I'll never forget the wonderful food there," Maria Budde of Orange, California. "I enjoy re-creating dishes from my home country, and this flavorful starter is typical of my native cuisine. It's simple to make and delicious.".

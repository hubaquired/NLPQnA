Pickled Corn recipe.  Combine 2 small dried chiles, 1 seeded thinly sliced jalapeño, 1/4 thinly sliced medium red onion, 2 cups corn kernels from about 2 ears), 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves with tender stems, 1/4 cup fresh lime juice, and 1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper in a heatproof 1-quart jar. Bring 1/2 cup white wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar, 2 teaspoons kosher salt, 1 teaspoon sugar, and 3/4 cup water to a boil; pour over corn. Add water to cover, if needed.) Seal jar. Let cool; chill. Will keep up to 1 month unopened. Pickled Corn has 111.0 calories. Pickled Corn has 3.0 grams of protein. Pickled Corn has 1.0 grams of fat. Pickled Corn has 960.0 grams of sodium.

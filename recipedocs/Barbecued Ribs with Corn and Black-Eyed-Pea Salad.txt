Barbecued Ribs with Corn and Black-Eyed-Pea Salad recipe.  Combine salsa, corn, black-eyed peas, bell pepper, cilantro, and jalapeño in medium bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Grill ribs until heated through, about 10 minutes. Cut racks into 4-rib sections or cut into individual ribs. Serve with corn and black-eyed-pea salad.

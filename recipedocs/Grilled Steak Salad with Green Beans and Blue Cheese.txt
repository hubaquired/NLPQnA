Grilled Steak Salad with Green Beans and Blue Cheese recipe. Cook green beans in pot of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Drain. Transfer to bowl of ice water and cool. Drain. Prepare barbecue medium-high heat). Combine beans, arugula, tomatoes, and olives in large bowl. Whisk 1/2 cup oil and vinegar in small bowl. Brush steaks with remaining 1 tablespoon oil; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Grill to desired doneness, about 4 minutes per side for medium-rare. Cut steaks crosswise into strips. Toss salad with enough dressing to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Divide among 6 plates. Top with steak strips. Sprinkle cheese over. The ingredients of Grilled Steak Salad with Green Beans and Blue Cheese are 1 pound slender green beans, trimmed, 6 cups arugula (about 6 ounces), 4 cups cherry tomatoes, halved, 1 1/4 cups pitted Kalamata olives or other brine-cured black olives, halved, 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon olive oil, 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 3 8- to 9-ounce New York steaks, 1 cup crumbled blue cheese.When buying green beans, look for the skinniest ones—they have the best texture.

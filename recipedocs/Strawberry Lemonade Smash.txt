Strawberry Lemonade Smash recipe.  Muddle 3 hulled strawberries in a cocktail shaker. Add 2 ounces white rum, 1 ounce fresh lemon juice, and 1 ounce simple syrup. Fill with ice, cover, and shake until outside of shaker is frosty, about 30 seconds. Fill a highball glass with crushed ice. Strain strawberry mixture into glass. Garnish with a strawberry, and serve with a straw. Strawberry Lemonade Smash has 114.0 calories. Strawberry Lemonade Smash has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Strawberry Lemonade Smash are 3 hulled strawberries, 2 ounces white rum, 1 ounce fresh lemon juice, 1 ounce simple syrup, 1 strawberry.It goes without saying, but the sweeter and riper your strawberries, the better this adult slushie will taste (and look).

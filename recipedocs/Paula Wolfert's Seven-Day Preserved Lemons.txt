Paula Wolfert's Seven-Day Preserved Lemons recipe. Dry lemons well and cut each into 8 wedges. In a bowl toss wedges with salt and transfer to a glass jar about 6-cup capacity). Add lemon juice and cover jar with a tight-fitting glass lid or plastic-coated lid. Let lemons stand at room temperature 7 days, shaking jar each day to redistribute salt and juice. Add oil to cover lemons and store, covered and chilled, up to 6 months. Paula Wolfert's Seven-Day Preserved Lemons has 19.0 calories. Paula Wolfert's Seven-Day Preserved Lemons has 1.0 grams of fat.

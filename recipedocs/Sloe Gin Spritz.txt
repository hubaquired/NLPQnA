Sloe Gin Spritz recipe.  Pour 4 ounces dry Prosecco or other sparkling white wine into an ice-filled large wine or rocks glass. Add 1 ounce sloe gin and top off with club soda. Gently stir together; garnish with blueberries and a sprig of mint. Sloe Gin Spritz has 181.0 calories. Sloe Gin Spritz has 9.0 grams of sodium.

Vanilla Butter Cream Frosting recipe. In a large bowl, cream the butter until smooth. Add the powdered sugar, salt, milk, and vanilla and mix until smooth and creamy. Vanilla Butter Cream Frosting has 272.0 calories. Vanilla Butter Cream Frosting has 9.0 grams of fat. Vanilla Butter Cream Frosting has 122.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Vanilla Butter Cream Frosting are 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened, 4 cups powdered sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/3 cup whole milk, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. Dessert Vanilla.

Mashed Sweet Potatoes with Maple Syrup recipe. Cook potatoes in pot of boiling salted water until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain. Return potatoes to pot; mash. Mix in butter, syrup and cardamom. Season with salt and pepper. Mashed Sweet Potatoes with Maple Syrup has 348.0 calories. Mashed Sweet Potatoes with Maple Syrup has 4.0 grams of protein. Mashed Sweet Potatoes with Maple Syrup has 12.0 grams of fat. Mashed Sweet Potatoes with Maple Syrup has 143.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mashed Sweet Potatoes with Maple Syrup are 2 1/4 pounds red-skinned sweet potatoes (yams), peeled, cut into 1-inch pieces, 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup, 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom.

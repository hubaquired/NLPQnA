Grilled Asparagus recipe. Prepare grill. Divide asparagus into 4 bunches. Align each bunch in a flat row and thread 2 skewers crosswise through each bunch see photo, above). Brush asparagus with oil and season with kosher salt. Grill asparagus on an oiled rack set 5 to 6 inches over glowing coals until tender, 3 to 5 minutes on each side. The ingredients of Grilled Asparagus are 1 1/2 lb medium asparagus (24), trimmed, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, Kosher salt, Special equipment: 8 (6-inch) wooden skewers, soaked in warm water 10 minutes.Asparagus is a star of the season, as a side dish to any main course — chicken, fish, pork, beef, or lamb — or as a salad topped with a squeeze of lemon juice.

Fresno Chile Hot Sauce recipe.  Cook 1/2 pound stemmed Fresno chiles 8–10) and 6 peeled garlic cloves in a large saucepan of boiling salted water until chiles turn bright red, about 2 minutes; drain. Blend chiles, garlic, and 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar in a blender until almost smooth; season with salt. Let cool. Fresno Chile Hot Sauce has 63.0 calories.

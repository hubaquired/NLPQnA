Whipped Parsnips recipe. Combine parsnips, 3/4 cup milk and whipping cream in heavy large saucepan. Cover and simmer over medium-low heat until parsnips are tender and most of liquid has evaporated, about 30 minutes. Transfer mixture to blender. Puree until smooth, thinning with more milk if necessary. Transfer to bowl. Season parsnips to taste with salt and pepper and serve. Whipped Parsnips has 288.0 calories. Whipped Parsnips has 6.0 grams of protein. Whipped Parsnips has 19.0 grams of fat. Whipped Parsnips has 64.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Whipped Parsnips are 1 pound parsnips, peeled, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1 1/2 cups (about) whole milk, 1/3 cup whipping cream.

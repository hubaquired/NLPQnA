The Ladybug (Watermelon Drink) recipe.  Combine all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. The ingredients of The Ladybug (Watermelon Drink) are 4 cups half-frozen watermelon cut into 1-inch chunks (seeds and all), 1/4 cup vodka, 1/4 cup lime sugar, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice.

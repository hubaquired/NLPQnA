The ingredients of Roasted Red Pepper Almond Dip are 1/2 inch thick slice crusty bread (about 1/2 cup roughly chopped), 2 roasted red bell peppers, or 1 (12-ounce) jar, 1 cup toasted slivered almonds, 1/4 cup parsley leaves, 2 garlic cloves, 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika, 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt.

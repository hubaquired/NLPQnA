Bourbon Sloe Gin Cocktail recipe. In a blender, blend cracked ice, bourbon, lemon juice, sloe gin and sugar for 20 seconds. Pour into a chilled 6-ounce stemmed glass. Bourbon Sloe Gin Cocktail has 188.0 calories. Bourbon Sloe Gin Cocktail has 6.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Bourbon Sloe Gin Cocktail are 1/2 cup cracked ice, 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) bourbon, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1/2 pony (1/2 ounce) sloe gin, 2 teaspoons superfine granulated sugar.

Lemon-Lime Gelato recipe. In a saucepan, stir together a quarter cup 60ml) freshly squeezed lemon juice and its zest, a quarter cup freshly squeezed lime juice and its zest, and a half cup 100g) sugar. Simmer for a few minutes, until the sugar has dissolved. Taste and add more sugar if you'd like. Strain and let cool down in the fridge. Stir in half a cup 125ml) heavy double) cream and pour the mixture into an ice cube tray. When it's frozen, spoon several cubes out into one big refreshing cup. Lemon-Lime Gelato has 622.0 calories.

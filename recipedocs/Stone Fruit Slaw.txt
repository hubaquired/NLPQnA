Stone Fruit Slaw recipe. Whisk first 6 ingredients in a medium bowl. Add fruit and scallions; toss gently to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Stone Fruit Slaw has 100.0 calories. Stone Fruit Slaw has 2.0 grams of protein. Stone Fruit Slaw has 4.0 grams of fat. Stone Fruit Slaw has 29.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Stone Fruit Slaw are 1 tablespoon grated peeled fresh ginger, 1 tablespoon unseasoned rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 2 teaspoons (packed) light brown sugar, 1/4 teaspoon curry powder, 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, 1 1/2 pound assorted firm stone fruit (about 5; such as plums, nectarines, peaches, or apricots), julienned, 2 scallions, thinly sliced diagonally, Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper.Serve this succulent slaw as a side or condiment for grilled chicken or pork. Use slightly underripe fruits, which julienne better than soft, juicy ones.

Double-Raspberry Malt for Two recipe.  Set aside 4 raspberries for garnish. Combine remaining raspberries, milk, malted milk powder, and sugar in blender; blend until smooth. Add ice cream; blend until smooth, stopping once or twice to scrape down sides of blender, if necessary. Pour malted shake into fluted soda-fountain style glass. Top with sorbet. Garnish malt with reserved berries. Double-Raspberry Malt for Two has 293.0 calories. Double-Raspberry Malt for Two has 6.0 grams of protein. Double-Raspberry Malt for Two has 10.0 grams of fat. Double-Raspberry Malt for Two has 95.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Double-Raspberry Malt for Two are 6-ounce basket fresh raspberries, 1/4 cup whole milk, 1/4 cup malted milk powder, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 cup vanilla ice cream, 1 small scoop raspberry sorbet.Offer chocolate truffles alongside or afterwards, with coffee or Champagne.

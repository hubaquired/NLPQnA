Deviled Eggs with Capers and Tarragon recipe. Shell eggs, then cut in half lengthwise. Transfer yolks to small bowl and mash with fork. Mix in oil, mayonnaise, and mustard. Stir in minced celery, tarragon, capers, and shallot. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon yolk mixture into whites. Garnish each with celery slice. Can be made 4 hours ahead. The ingredients of Deviled Eggs with Capers and Tarragon are 6 hard-boiled eggs, 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon mayonnaise, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons minced celery, 4 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon, 1 tablespoon minced drained capers, 2 teaspoons minced shallot, Sliced celery.

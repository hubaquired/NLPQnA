Iced Cucumber Soup with Mint, Watercress, and Feta Cheese recipe.  Puree cucumbers, broth, feta, onion, mint, watercress, oregano, garlic, and salt in blender until smooth. Pour soup into large bowl; whisk in yogurt. Refrigerate until cold, about 2 hours. DO AHEAD: Soup can be made 1 day ahead. Cover; chill. Before serving, put soup in freezer 30 minutes. Pour into bowls; top with chopped egg and chives. Iced Cucumber Soup with Mint, Watercress, and Feta Cheese has 162.0 calories. The ingredients of Iced Cucumber Soup with Mint, Watercress, and Feta Cheese are 1 pound Persian or Japanese cucumbers, peeled, halved, seeded, cut crosswise into 1-inch-thick slices (about 2 1/2 cups), 2 cups low-salt chicken broth or vegetable broth, 4 ounces finely crumbled feta cheese (about 3/4 cup), 3/4 cup chopped white onion, 1/2 cup loosely packed fresh mint leaves, 1/2 cup loosely packed watercress leaves, 1 tablespoon fresh oregano leaves, 1 small garlic clove, peeled, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 17.6-ounce container whole-milk or nonfat yogurt (preferably Greek; about 2 cups), 2 hard-boiled eggs, chilled, coarsely chopped, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives.This refreshing soup makes a great first course for a summer dinner party.

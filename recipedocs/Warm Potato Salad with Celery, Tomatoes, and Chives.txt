Warm Potato Salad with Celery, Tomatoes, and Chives recipe. Cover potatoes with salted cold water by 1 inch in a saucepan, then simmer until just tender, about 20 minutes. While potatoes are simmering, whisk together mayonnaise, oil, vinegar, garlic, chives, and salt and pepper to taste in a large bowl. Drain potatoes in a colander and cool 5 minutes. Add to dressing along with celery and tomatoes. Toss salad gently and season with salt and pepper The ingredients of Warm Potato Salad with Celery, Tomatoes, and Chives are 2 1/2 lb small (1 1/2- to 2-inch) boiling potatoes (preferably Yukon Gold), quartered, 1/3 cup mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 2 teaspoons cider vinegar, 1/4 teaspoon minced garlic, 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh chives, 4 celery ribs, thinly sliced diagonally, 1 1/2 cups grape or cherry tomatoes, halved lengthwise.Active time: 15 min Start to finish: 30 min.

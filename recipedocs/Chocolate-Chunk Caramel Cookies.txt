Chocolate-Chunk Caramel Cookies recipe. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. In a bowl whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Stir chocolate, caramels, and pecans into dough and drop rounded tablespoons about 2 inches apart onto baking sheets. Bake cookies in batches in middle of oven 12 to 15 minutes, or until golden.

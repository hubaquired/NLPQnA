Dried Chile Salsa recipe. Toast chiles in a dry medium skillet over high heat, turning often, until fragrant, about 3 minutes. Let cool slightly, then remove stems and seeds. Place chiles, onion, garlic, salt, and 1 1/2 cups water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until very soft, 15–20 minutes. Purée chile mixture in a blender until smooth. Strain into a medium bowl and mix in vinegar. DO AHEAD: Salsa can be made 4 days ahead. Cover and chill. Dried Chile Salsa has 42.0 calories. Dried Chile Salsa has 1.0 grams of protein. Dried Chile Salsa has 1.0 grams of fat. Dried Chile Salsa has 724.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Dried Chile Salsa are 1 1/2 ounces dried chiles de árbol, 1 1/2 ounces ancho chiles, 1 medium white onion, chopped, 4 garlic cloves, peeled, 1 tablespoon kosher salt, 1/3 cup distilled white vinegar.This smoky, fiery concoction is inspired by Bar Amá's "Bus Driver" salsa.

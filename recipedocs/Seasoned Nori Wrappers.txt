Seasoned Nori Wrappers recipe. Brush both sides of nori with sesame oil. Sprinkle nori lightly with salt. Grill nori until edges begin to curl, about 5 seconds per side. Pile sheets on work surface. Using scissors, cut nori lengthwise in half. Cut each half crosswise into 4 rectangles.

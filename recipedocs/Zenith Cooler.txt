Zenith Cooler recipe.  Put the ice cubes in a 10-ounce highball glass and pour in the gin and pineapple juice. Fill up with club soda and stir. Decorate with pineapple stick. The ingredients of Zenith Cooler are 3 or 4 ice cubes, 2-3 ounces gin, 1 tablespoon pineapple juice, Chilled club soda, Pineapple stick.

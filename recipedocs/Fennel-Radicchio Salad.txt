Fennel-Radicchio Salad recipe.  Whisk 3 tablespoons olive oil, lemon juice and minced shallot to blend in small bowl. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. Toss fennel and radicchio with dressing in medium bowl. Divide salad between 2 plates. Garnish with sliced olives and serve. Fennel-Radicchio Salad has 259.0 calories. Fennel-Radicchio Salad has 3.0 grams of protein. Fennel-Radicchio Salad has 22.0 grams of fat. Fennel-Radicchio Salad has 164.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fennel-Radicchio Salad are 3 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 shallot, minced, 1 small fresh fennel bulb, trimmed, cut vertically into thin slices, 1 1/2 cups bite-size pieces radicchio, 3 tablespoons sliced pitted black olives.A great accompaniment to baked fish or sausage calzones.

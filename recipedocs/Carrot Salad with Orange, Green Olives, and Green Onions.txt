Carrot Salad with Orange, Green Olives, and Green Onions recipe.  Cook carrots in large pot of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 7 minutes. Drain well. Whisk oil, lemon juice, orange peel, and coriander in large bowl to blend. Add hot carrots and toss to coat. Cool, tossing occasionally. Season with salt and pepper. Drizzle with honey, if desired. Stir in olives and green onions. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours. Can be made 1 day ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Stir salad to redistribute dressing. Garnish edges of platter with orange slices and parsley. Mound carrots in center. Carrot Salad with Orange, Green Olives, and Green Onions has 214.0 calories. Carrot Salad with Orange, Green Olives, and Green Onions has 3.0 grams of protein. Carrot Salad with Orange, Green Olives, and Green Onions has 13.0 grams of fat. Carrot Salad with Orange, Green Olives, and Green Onions has 471.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Carrot Salad with Orange, Green Olives, and Green Onions are 3 pounds carrots, peeled, cut on diagonal into 1/4-inch-thick slices, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon grated orange peel, 1 tablespoon ground coriander, Drizzle of honey (optional), 1 cup drained chopped pitted brine-cured green olives, 1 cup chopped green onions, Orange slices, Fresh Italian parsley sprigs.The honey is optional because some carrots are sweeter than others. Taste the salad, then decide whether to drizzle.

Annabel's Baby Back Ribs recipe. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Place ribs, meat-side up, on 2 baking sheets. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake 30 minutes. Brush 1/4 cup barbecue sauce over each rack of ribs; bake for 20 minutes. Brush another 1/4 cup sauce evenly among all 3 racks; bake 20 minutes more. Repeat, using 1/4 cup sauce among all 3 racks; bake for 30 minutes. To serve, cut each rack into thirds and arrange on a large decorative platter. Moisten with a little warm barbecue sauce. Sprinkle with chopped parsley to garnish. The ingredients of Annabel's Baby Back Ribs are 3 racks of baby back ribs, 2 to 2 1/2 pounds each, Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste, 1 1/2 to 2 cupsParty Barbecue Sauce, 2 to 3 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley.Provide knives so your guests can slice the sections into individual ribs.

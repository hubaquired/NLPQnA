Cantaloupe Basil Salsa recipe. Toss together all ingredients in a bowl and let stand, covered and chilled, 10 minutes. Cantaloupe Basil Salsa has 93.0 calories. Cantaloupe Basil Salsa has 2.0 grams of protein. Cantaloupe Basil Salsa has 1.0 grams of fat. Cantaloupe Basil Salsa has 188.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cantaloupe Basil Salsa are 2 cups diced (1/4 inch) cantaloupe (from a 2 1/4-lb piece), 1/4 cup finely chopped sweet onion such as Vidalia, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh basil, 1 (2-inch-long) fresh hot red or green chile such as serrano or Thai, minced (including some seeds), 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1/4 teaspoon salt.Juicy cantaloupe makes a refreshing salsa for grilled chicken or fish. For a milder salsa, discard all the chile seeds.  Condiment Spread Sauce Fruit Herb No-Cook Picnic Quick & Easy Cantaloupe Basil Summer Gourmet.

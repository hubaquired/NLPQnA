Gingered Apple Slaw recipe. Stir together mayonnaise, ginger, and vinegar. Add celery and apples and gently toss to coat. Gingered Apple Slaw has 299.0 calories. Gingered Apple Slaw has 1.0 grams of protein. Gingered Apple Slaw has 23.0 grams of fat. Gingered Apple Slaw has 185.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Gingered Apple Slaw are 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated peeled fresh ginger, 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar, 2 celery ribs, cut into matchsticks, 4 apples, of different varieties, cored and cut into matchsticks.Active time: 20 min Start to finish: 20 min.

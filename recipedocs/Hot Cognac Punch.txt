Hot Cognac Punch recipe.  Dissolve the sugar in the hot tea. Stir in the strained orange and lemon juice and add the Cognac. Heat the punch but do not let it boil. Serve in punch glasses. Hot Cognac Punch has 133.0 calories. Hot Cognac Punch has 1.0 grams of protein. Hot Cognac Punch has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Cognac Punch are 1 cup sugar, 4 cups freshly brewed hot tea, Strained juice of 8 oranges and 3 lemons, 1 1/2 cups Cognac.

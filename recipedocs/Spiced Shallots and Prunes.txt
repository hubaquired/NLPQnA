Spiced Shallots and Prunes recipe.  Blanch shallots not peeled) in boiling water 2 minutes, then drain in a colander and rinse under cold water to cool. Peel shallots, then combine with remaining ingredients in a 10-inch heavy skillet. Simmer, uncovered, over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until shallots are tender, 20 to 25 minutes. Transfer to a bowl and cool. Chill, covered, at least 8 hours for flavors to meld. Discard whole spices. Serve at room temperature. Spiced Shallots and Prunes has 317.0 calories. Spiced Shallots and Prunes has 5.0 grams of protein. Spiced Shallots and Prunes has 25.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spiced Shallots and Prunes are 1 1/4 pound small shallots (about 24), 1/2 pound pitted prunes (1 1/2 cups), 1 1/2 cups ruby Port, 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 1 (3-inch) cinnamon stick, 1/2 teaspoon whole black peppercorns, 1/4 teaspoon whole allspice.

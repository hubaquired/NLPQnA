Sausage and Bean Soup recipe.  Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add sausage and onion; Sauté until onion is tender, about 6 minutes. Add broth and kale; bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer uncovered 10 minutes. Add beans and wine. Cook until kale is tender, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Sausage and Bean Soup has 720.0 calories.

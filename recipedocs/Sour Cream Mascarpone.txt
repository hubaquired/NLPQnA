Sour Cream Mascarpone recipe. In a bowl whisk together sour cream, mascarpone, and confectioners' sugar. Mixture may be made 1 day ahead and chilled, covered.) The ingredients of Sour Cream Mascarpone are 1/2 cup sour cream, 1/2 cup mascarpone*, 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar, or to taste, available at specialty foods shops and some supermarkets..

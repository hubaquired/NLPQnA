Silver Sunset recipe. Combine vodka, apricot brandy, Campari, lemon and orange juices and egg white with ice cubes in a cocktail shaker. Shake well. Strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with the cherry and orange slice. The ingredients of Silver Sunset are 1 1/2 ounces vodka, 1/2 ounce apricot brandy, 1/2 ounce Campari, 1/2 ounce lemon juice, 2 ounces orange juice, 1 egg white, Maraschino cherry, Orange slice, 3 or 4 ice cubes.

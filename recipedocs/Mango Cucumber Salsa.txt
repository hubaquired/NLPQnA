Mango Cucumber Salsa recipe. Peel mango and cut flesh from pit. Cut mango into 1/4-inch dice and transfer to a small bowl. Halve and seed cucumber. Cut cucumber into 1/4-inch dice and add to mango. Stir in remaining ingredients until combined well and season with salt and pepper. Mango Cucumber Salsa has 103.0 calories. Mango Cucumber Salsa has 2.0 grams of protein. Mango Cucumber Salsa has 2.0 grams of fat. Mango Cucumber Salsa has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mango Cucumber Salsa are 1 firm-ripe small mango, 1/3 English cucumber, 2 tablespoons finely chopped scallion, 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice, 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice, 1/2 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil.This recipe was created to accompany Seared Rosemary Scallops .

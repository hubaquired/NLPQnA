Merry Sparkler recipe.  Into a chilled Champagne glass, pour orange juice. Add enough Burgundy to fill the glass and stir lightly to blend. Garnish with orange peel. Serve. The ingredients of Merry Sparkler are 2 ounces orange juice, strained and chilled, Sparkling Burgundy, chilled, Garnish: spiral of orange peel.

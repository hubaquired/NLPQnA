The ingredients of Pineapple-Apricot Salsa are 1 cup finely chopped peeled cored fresh pineapple, 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion, 1/2 cup apricot-pineapple preserves, 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 1/2 tablespoons minced seeded jalapeño chili.

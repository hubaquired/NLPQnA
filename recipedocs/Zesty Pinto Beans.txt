Zesty Pinto Beans recipe.  Place beans in large pot. Add enough water to cover beans by 3 inches. Soak beans overnight. Drain beans and return to same pot. Add enough water to pot to cover beans by 4 inches. Mix in remaining ingredients. Simmer over medium heat until beans are tender and creamy, stirring occasionally, about 1 1/2 hours. Season with salt and serve. Zesty Pinto Beans has 355.0 calories. Zesty Pinto Beans has 19.0 grams of protein. Zesty Pinto Beans has 9.0 grams of fat. Zesty Pinto Beans has 138.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Zesty Pinto Beans are 1 pound dried pinto beans, 1 onion, chopped, 4 bacon slices, chopped, 4 garlic cloves, sliced, 1 jalapeño chili, halved.

Pears with Almond Cream recipe. Toast the almonds in a dry skillet over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until golden brown and fragrant, about 2 minutes. Whip the cream with an electric beater until it is thickened. Add the sugar and continue beating until soft peaks are formed. Gently fold in the yogurt and almond extract. The cream will keep in the refrigerator, covered, for 2 days. Core and slice the pears and arrange slices of half a pear on each serving plate. Top each with 3 tablespoons of the cream and sprinkle with almonds. The ingredients of Pears with Almond Cream are 1/4 cup sliced almonds, 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream, 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar, 1/2 cup plain Greek-style nonfat yogurt, 3/4 teaspoon almond extract, 4 firm ripe pears.This rich, subtly tangy cream spiked with almond essence gives ripe, sliced pears just the embellishment they need to go from fruit to fabulous.

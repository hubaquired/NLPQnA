Frozen Mint Lemonade recipe. Blend lemon juice, sugar, and mint leaves in a blender until leaves are finely chopped and sugar is dissolved. Add water and enough ice cubes to fill blender, then blend until smooth. Serve immediately. Frozen Mint Lemonade has 107.0 calories. Frozen Mint Lemonade has 15.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Frozen Mint Lemonade are 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup packed fresh mint leaves, 2 cups water, About 3 cups ice cubes, cracked if large, Garnish: 4 fresh mint sprigs.

Lemon-Cassis Martini recipe. Mix first 4 ingredients in glass measuring cup. Let stand 10 minutes. Strain into martini shaker or another cup. Mix in cassis. Add ice; shake or stir until very cold. Strain into glasses. Garnish with lemon. The ingredients of Lemon-Cassis Martini are 3/4 cup vodka, 4 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 2 teaspoons sugar, 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel, 1 tablespoon crème de cassis, Ice cubes, Lemon peel twists.Just a touch of cassis adds sweetness and berry flavor to this lemony drink.

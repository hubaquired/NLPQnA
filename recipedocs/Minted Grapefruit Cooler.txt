Minted Grapefruit Cooler recipe. In a pitcher combine the grapefruit juice and the lemon juice and stir in the ginger ale. Into each of 4 tall glasses spoon 1 teaspoon of the sugar and add 1 of the mint sprigs. With the back of the spoon press the sugar into the mint until the mint gives off a fragrance. Fill the glasses with ice cubes and divide the grapefruit mixture among them. Minted Grapefruit Cooler has 133.0 calories. Minted Grapefruit Cooler has 1.0 grams of protein. Minted Grapefruit Cooler has 13.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Minted Grapefruit Cooler are 2 1/2 cups fresh grapefruit juice, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 1/2 cups ginger ale, 4 teaspoons sugar, or to taste, 4 mint sprigs, or to taste.

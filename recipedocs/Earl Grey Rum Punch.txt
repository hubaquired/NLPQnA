Earl Grey Rum Punch recipe. Mix rum and tea in small bowl; let stand 1 hour. Strain mixture into 2-cup glass measuring cup. Mix in pineapple juice and next 5 ingredients. Fill 2 highball glasses with ice. Pour punch over. Garnish with fresh apricot wedges, if desired. Earl Grey Rum Punch has 231.0 calories. Earl Grey Rum Punch has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Earl Grey Rum Punch are 1/2 cup white rum, 1 1/2 teaspoons loose Earl Grey tea, 1/4 cup pineapple juice, 2 tablespoons apricot liqueur, 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 2 teaspoons Simple Syrup, 2 dashes of angostura bitters, Ice cubes, Fresh apricot wedges (optional).A colonial twist on the ubiquitous — and potent — rum punch.

Marshmallow Frosting recipe. In a small saucepan over medium heat, bring the agave nectar to a boil, stirring frequently. Decrease the heat to low and simmer for 4 to 8 minutes, watching constantly and stirring occasionally, until the agave nectar darkens slightly from its original amber color. In a large bowl, using a handheld mixer, whip the egg whites to stiff peaks. Drizzle the agave nectar slowly into the egg whites, whisking continuously until blended. Use to frost cakes or cupcakes, or as a filling between cookies. Store in a glass Mason jar in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. Marshmallow Frosting has 123.0 calories. Marshmallow Frosting has 1.0 grams of protein. Marshmallow Frosting has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Marshmallow Frosting are 1/2 cup agave nectar, 2 egg whites.This marshmallow frosting recipe was inspired by one of my favorite cookbook authors, the late Elaine Gottschall. It works especially well with S'mores and is divine frosted over Chocolate Cake or sandwiched between Chewy Chocolate Cookies.

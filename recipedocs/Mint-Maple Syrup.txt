Mint-Maple Syrup recipe. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil. Remove from the heat. Add the mint and the maple syrup and let the mint steep for 15 minutes, until cool. Strain and pour the syrup over the fruit or store for up to 1 month in the refrigerator. Mint-Maple Syrup has 122.0 calories. Mint-Maple Syrup has 2.0 grams of protein. Mint-Maple Syrup has 18.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mint-Maple Syrup are 1 cup fresh mint leaves, 1/4 cup grade A or B maple syrup..

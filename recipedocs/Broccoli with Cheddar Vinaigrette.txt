Broccoli with Cheddar Vinaigrette recipe.  In a blender blend together the oil, the vinegar, the water, the Tabasco, the Cheddar, and salt and pepper to taste until the vinaigrette is smooth. In a small saucepan bring 1 inch of water to a boil and in it cook the broccoli, covered, over high heat for 4 to 5 minutes, or until it is crisp-tender. Drain the broccoli well and serve it topped with the vinaigrette. Broccoli with Cheddar Vinaigrette has 216.0 calories. Broccoli with Cheddar Vinaigrette has 10.0 grams of protein. Broccoli with Cheddar Vinaigrette has 17.0 grams of fat. Broccoli with Cheddar Vinaigrette has 223.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Broccoli with Cheddar Vinaigrette are 1 tablespoon olive or vegetable oil, 1 1/2 teaspoons white-wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon water, 1/4 teaspoon Tabasco, 1/2 cup coarsely grated Cheddar, 1/2 pound broccoli, cut into long flowerets.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Sliced Tomatoes and Mozzarella with Three-Herb Pesto recipe.  On a large round platter alternate tomato and mozzarella slices, overlapping slightly, and spoon pesto in a ring around center of slices. Sprinkle tomatoes and mozzarella with pepper and garnish with herbs. Sliced Tomatoes and Mozzarella with Three-Herb Pesto has 212.0 calories. The ingredients of Sliced Tomatoes and Mozzarella with Three-Herb Pesto are 3 large vine-ripened tomatoes (about 1 1/2 pounds), sliced, 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese (preferably fresh), sliced 1/4 inch thick, 1/2 cup Three-Herb Pesto, freshly ground black pepper to taste, basil, mint, and/or parsley sprigs for garnish if desired.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

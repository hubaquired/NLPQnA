Winter Tomato Sauce recipe.  Using fingers, split open tomatoes; remove and discard seeds. Chop tomatoes; reserve juice. Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add chopped tomatoes and reserved juice and all remaining ingredients; stir to combine. Bring to boil; reduce heat to medium and simmer uncovered until sauce is reduced to 4 cups, stirring often, about 1 hour. Remove bay leaf and any stems from herbs. Pass sauce through food mill fitted with large holes. Alternately, transfer to processor. Using on/off turns, process to coarse puree. Winter Tomato Sauce has 118.0 calories. Winter Tomato Sauce has 3.0 grams of protein. Winter Tomato Sauce has 7.0 grams of fat. Winter Tomato Sauce has 488.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Winter Tomato Sauce are 2 28-ounce cans whole Italian-style tomatoes, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 cups chopped onions, 8 large fresh thyme sprigs, 6 garlic cloves, peeled, quartered, 4 4-inch-long fresh rosemary sprigs, 4 large fresh basil sprigs, 1 bay leaf, 1 oil-packed anchovy fillet, 1 teaspoon coarse kosher salt, 1 teaspoon Hungarian or Spanish sweet paprika.This is also a wonderful sauce for hearty pasta dishes.

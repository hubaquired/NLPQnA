Fingerling Potatoes with Butter and Thyme recipe.  Place potatoes in medium saucepan; pour enough cold water over to cover. Add thyme sprigs, butter, and salt. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and simmer until potatoes are just tender when pierced with skewer, about 10 minutes. Drain. DO AHEAD_Can be made 2 hours ahead. Transfer to baking sheet; cover loosely with foil and let stand at room temperature. Before serving, rewarm in 400°F oven until heated through, about 7 minutes._ Fingerling Potatoes with Butter and Thyme has 62.0 calories. Fingerling Potatoes with Butter and Thyme has 1.0 grams of protein. Fingerling Potatoes with Butter and Thyme has 2.0 grams of fat. Fingerling Potatoes with Butter and Thyme has 309.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fingerling Potatoes with Butter and Thyme are 12 ounces unpeeled fingerling potatoes or baby potatoes, 2 large fresh thyme sprigs, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon salt.

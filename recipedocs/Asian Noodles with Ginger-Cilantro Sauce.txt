Asian Noodles with Ginger-Cilantro Sauce recipe.  Cook noodles in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender but still firm to bite. Drain noodles. Rinse with cold water; drain well. Transfer to large bowl. Toss with 1 tablespoon sesame oil. With processor running, drop ginger and chili through feed tube and mince. Add cilantro, soy sauce, vinegar, peanut butter, 3 tablespoons broth and remaining 2 tablespoons sesame oil. Process until mixture is almost smooth, adding more broth if necessary. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Add sauce to noodles and toss. Asian Noodles with Ginger-Cilantro Sauce has 438.0 calories. Asian Noodles with Ginger-Cilantro Sauce has 13.0 grams of protein. Asian Noodles with Ginger-Cilantro Sauce has 14.0 grams of fat. Asian Noodles with Ginger-Cilantro Sauce has 272.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Asian Noodles with Ginger-Cilantro Sauce are 1 12-ounce package fresh oriental-style water noodles or linguine, 3 tablespoons oriental sesame oil, 2 1/2 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger, 1 small jalapeño chili, seeded, 1 cup (packed) fresh cilantro leaves, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 tablespoon rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon creamy peanut butter, 3 tablespoons (or more) canned chicken broth.Add chicken or pork to turn this side dish into a satisfying main course.

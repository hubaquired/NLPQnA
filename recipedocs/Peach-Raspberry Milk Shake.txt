Peach-Raspberry Milk Shake recipe.  In a blender purée 1 cup of the raspberries with the milk, strain the purée through a coarse sieve into a glass measure, pressing hard on the solids, and return it to the blender, cleaned. Add the ice cream, the peaches and the sugar, blend the mixture until it is smooth but still thick, and serve the milk shakes garnished with the additional raspberries. Peach-Raspberry Milk Shake has 480.0 calories. Peach-Raspberry Milk Shake has 9.0 grams of protein. Peach-Raspberry Milk Shake has 17.0 grams of fat. Peach-Raspberry Milk Shake has 133.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peach-Raspberry Milk Shake are 1 cup picked-over raspberries plus additional for garnish, 1/2 cup milk, 1 pint vanilla ice cream, softened slightly, 1 pound (about 6 small) peaches, peeled, pitted, and chopped, 2 tablespoons sugar, or to taste.

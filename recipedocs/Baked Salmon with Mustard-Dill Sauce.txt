Baked Salmon with Mustard-Dill Sauce recipe. Whisk sour cream, dill, onion and mustard in small bowl to blend. Season sauce to taste with salt and pepper. Let stand at room temperature 1 hour. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly oil baking sheet. Place salmon, skin side down, on prepared sheet. Sprinkle with garlic, salt and pepper; spread with 1/3 cup sauce. Bake salmon until just opaque in center, about 20 minutes. Serve with remaining sauce. Baked Salmon with Mustard-Dill Sauce has 475.0 calories. Baked Salmon with Mustard-Dill Sauce has 36.0 grams of protein. Baked Salmon with Mustard-Dill Sauce has 34.0 grams of fat. Baked Salmon with Mustard-Dill Sauce has 211.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Baked Salmon with Mustard-Dill Sauce are 1 cup sour cream, 1/3 cup chopped fresh dill, 3 tablespoons finely chopped onion, 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 1 1 1/2-pound center-cut salmon fillet with skin, 2 teaspoons minced garlic.

Minted Pineapple Gin Blossom recipe. In a tall glass filled with ice, combine gin and pineapple juice and fill the glass with the seltzer. Bruise 1 or 2 mint leaves from the sprig of mint; stir them into the drink. Garnish with the remaining mint sprig. Minted Pineapple Gin Blossom has 178.0 calories. Minted Pineapple Gin Blossom has 1.0 grams of protein. Minted Pineapple Gin Blossom has 33.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Minted Pineapple Gin Blossom are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) gin, 1/2 cup unsweetened pineapple juice, 1/2 to 2/3 cup chilled seltzer, Sprig of mint.

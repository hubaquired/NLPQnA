Apple Cider Vinaigrette recipe.  In a jar with tight fitting lid, combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Remove cinnamon stick. Can be stored in refrigerator for several weeks. The ingredients of Apple Cider Vinaigrette are 2 cups apple cider, 1/4 cup cider vinegar, 1/4 cup honey, 1 cinnamon stick, 1 teaspoon salt.Editor's note: The recipe below is part of a healthy and delicious spa menu developed exclusively for Epicurious by Canyon Ranch.

Chipotle-Honey-Glazed Pork recipe.  Preheat oven to 450°F. Purée chipotles in adobo with garlic and honey in a blender until smooth. Pat pork dry and season with salt. Put pork on a foil-lined baking sheet and spread with chile glaze to coat well. Roast in middle of oven until an instant-read thermometer registers 155°F, about 20 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes on a cutting board before cutting into thin slices. Chipotle-Honey-Glazed Pork has 257.0 calories. Chipotle-Honey-Glazed Pork has 35.0 grams of protein. Chipotle-Honey-Glazed Pork has 6.0 grams of fat. Chipotle-Honey-Glazed Pork has 189.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chipotle-Honey-Glazed Pork are 3 canned chipotle chiles in adobo (1/4 cup total), 3 garlic cloves, coarsely chopped, 3 tablespoons honey, 1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin.

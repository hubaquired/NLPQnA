Coriander Lime Pesto recipe. In a food processor blend together all ingredients with salt and pepper to taste until smooth. Pesto keeps, surface covered with plastic wrap, chilled, 1 week.) Coriander Lime Pesto has 653.0 calories. Coriander Lime Pesto has 14.0 grams of protein. Coriander Lime Pesto has 66.0 grams of fat. Coriander Lime Pesto has 469.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coriander Lime Pesto are 1 cup packed fresh coriander sprigs, washed well and spun dry, 1/2 cup packed fresh flat-leafed parsley leaves, washed well and spun dry, 1/4 cup walnuts, toasted brown and cooled, 1/3 cup olive oil, 3 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan, 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated lime zest.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

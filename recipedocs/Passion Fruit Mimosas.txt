Passion Fruit Mimosas recipe.  Divide fruit juice between 2 Champagne flutes. Slowly pour Champagne into flutes, dividing equally. Add 1 tablespoon Grand Marnier to each flute; stir gently. Garnish with orange peel twists, if desired. Passion Fruit Mimosas has 116.0 calories. Passion Fruit Mimosas has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Passion Fruit Mimosas are 6 tablespoons fresh passion fruit juice or passion fruit blend nectar, 1 cup chilled brut Champagne (about 2/3 of 375-ml bottle), 2 tablespoons Grand Marnier, Orange peel twists (optional).

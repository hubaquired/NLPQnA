Pomegranate Manhattan recipe. Fill a cocktail shaker halfway with ice cubes, then add all drink ingredients and shake well to dissolve sugar. Strain into a Martini glass. Pomegranate Manhattan has 142.0 calories. Pomegranate Manhattan has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pomegranate Manhattan are 1 1/2 oz (3 tablespoons) bourbon or blended whiskey, 1 1/2 oz (3 tablespoons) pure pomegranate juice such as POM Wonderful, 1/2 teaspoon superfine granulated sugar, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, Garnish: fresh pomegranate seeds or a maraschino cherry.

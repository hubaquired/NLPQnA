Pickling Juice recipe. Combine all of the ingredients in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over low heat, allowing the sugar and salt to dissolve. Cool and strain through a fine-mesh sieve. Use immediately, or refrigerate for up to 1 month. Pickling Juice has 100.0 calories. Pickling Juice has 2866.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pickling Juice are 1 cup water, 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar, 1/3 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons kosher salt, 1 whole clove, 1 teaspoon mustard seeds, 1 teaspoon black peppercorns, 1 teaspoon peeled and chopped fresh ginger, 1/2 jalapeño, seeded and chopped. Marinate Vinegar Jalapeño Seed.

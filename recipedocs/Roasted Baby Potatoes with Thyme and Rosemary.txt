Roasted Baby Potatoes with Thyme and Rosemary recipe.  Cut the potatoes in half and toss them in a little chopped fresh thyme and rosemary, some chopped garlic, and a little olive oil. Roast on a baking sheet in a 450°F oven for about 30 minutes. The ingredients of Roasted Baby Potatoes with Thyme and Rosemary are Baby potatoes, Fresh thyme, chopped, Fresh rosemary, chopped, Garlic, chopped, Olive oil.For an extra special look, pick up a mix of white, red, and purple baby potatoes.

Smoked Paprika and Roasted Red Pepper Butter recipe. Mix all ingredients in small bowl to blend. Place on sheet of plastic wrap. Using wrap as aid, form into 1 1/4- to 1 1/2-inch-diameter log. Wrap plastic tightly around log. Chill until firm, about 2 hours. *Smoked paprika from Spain is available at specialty foods stores. Smoked Paprika and Roasted Red Pepper Butter has 209.0 calories. Smoked Paprika and Roasted Red Pepper Butter has 23.0 grams of fat. Smoked Paprika and Roasted Red Pepper Butter has 195.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Smoked Paprika and Roasted Red Pepper Butter are 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature, 3 tablespoons (packed) finely chopped drained roasted red peppers from jar, patted dry, 1 1/2 teaspoons smoked hot paprika* (Pimentón de la Vera), 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper.This zesty butter can be kept in the refrigerator for a week or frozen for a month.

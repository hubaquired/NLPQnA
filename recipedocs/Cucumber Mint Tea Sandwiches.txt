Cucumber Mint Tea Sandwiches recipe.  In a small bowl combine the mint, the butter, and the cream cheese and stir the mixture until it is combined well. Spread the bread slices with the butter mixture, top 3 of them with the cucumber, distributing the cucumber evenly and seasoning it with salt, and top the cucumber with the remaining bread slices. Cut off and discard the crusts and cut each sandwich diagonally into quarters. Cucumber Mint Tea Sandwiches has 59.0 calories. Cucumber Mint Tea Sandwiches has 2.0 grams of protein. Cucumber Mint Tea Sandwiches has 3.0 grams of fat. Cucumber Mint Tea Sandwiches has 71.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cucumber Mint Tea Sandwiches are 1/4 cup loosely packed fresh mint leaves, rinsed, spun dry, and chopped fine, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened, 2 tablespoons cream cheese, 6 slices of whole-wheat bread, a 3-inch length of seedless cucumber, cut into thin slices.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

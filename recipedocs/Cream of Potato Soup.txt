Cream of Potato Soup recipe. Place ingredients in electric blender in order indicated. Cover container and turn on blender. Mix about 1 minute. Pour into saucepan or double boiler and heat thoroughly over moderate heat, stirring frequently. Serve immediately in soup cups, sprinkled with chives. For a richer soup, stir in 1/2 cup light cream. Cream of Potato Soup has 116.0 calories. Cream of Potato Soup has 5.0 grams of protein. Cream of Potato Soup has 5.0 grams of fat. Cream of Potato Soup has 585.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cream of Potato Soup are 1 cup diced hot cooked potatoes, 2 cups milk, 1 tablespoon chopped onion, 1 chicken bouillon cube, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon pepper, 2 sprigs parsley, 1 teaspoon butter, Chopped chives.

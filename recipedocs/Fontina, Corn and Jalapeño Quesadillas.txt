Fontina, Corn and Jalapeño Quesadillas recipe.  Heat 2 teaspoons oil in heavy medium skillet over medium heat. Add corn, chilies and oregano and sauté 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Heat clean heavy medium skillet over medium heat. Brush with oil. Add 1 tortilla. Cover with 1/2 cup cheese and 1/4 of corn mixture. Top with another tortilla. Cook until tortillas are brown and cheese melts, about 2 minutes per side. Transfer to work surface. Repeat with remaining ingredients, forming 4 quesadillas in total. Cut each into 8 wedges. Fontina, Corn and Jalapeño Quesadillas has 640.0 calories. Fontina, Corn and Jalapeño Quesadillas has 24.0 grams of protein. Fontina, Corn and Jalapeño Quesadillas has 35.0 grams of fat. Fontina, Corn and Jalapeño Quesadillas has 1183.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fontina, Corn and Jalapeño Quesadillas are 2 teaspoons olive oil, 1 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed, 2 jalapeño chilies, seeded, minced, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crumbled, Olive oil, 8 7-inch flour tortillas, 2 cups grated Fontina cheese (about 8 ounces).

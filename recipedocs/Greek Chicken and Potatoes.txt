Greek Chicken and Potatoes recipe. Preheat oven to 375°F. Arrange chicken, potatoes and garlic in large roasting pan. Season with salt and pepper. Pour broth over. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice and oregano to combine. Pour evenly over chicken and potatoes. Bake until chicken is cooked through and golden brown and potatoes are tender, basting occasionally with pan juices, about 1 hour 15 minutes. Greek Chicken and Potatoes has 1526.0 calories. Greek Chicken and Potatoes has 83.0 grams of protein. Greek Chicken and Potatoes has 101.0 grams of fat. Greek Chicken and Potatoes has 312.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Greek Chicken and Potatoes are 1 3 1/2-pound chicken, quartered, 6 russet potatoes (about 3 1/4 pounds), peeled, quartered, lengthwise, 4 large garlic cloves, halved, 3/4 cup canned low-salt chicken broth, 3/4 cup olive oil, 2/3 cup fresh lemon juice, 2 teaspoons dried oregano, crumbled.

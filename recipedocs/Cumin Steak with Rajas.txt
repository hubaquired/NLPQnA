Cumin Steak with Rajas recipe.  Preheat broiler. Season steak generously with salt and pepper. Rub both sides of steak with cumin and cayenne pepper. Broil to desired doneness, about 3 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer to platter and serve with Rajas. Cumin Steak with Rajas has 311.0 calories.

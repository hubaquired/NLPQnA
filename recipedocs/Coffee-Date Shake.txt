Coffee-Date Shake recipe.  Combine dates, milk and corn syrup in blender and blend until smooth. Add ice cubes, then ice cream; blend until thick and smooth. Pour into 2 chilled glasses and serve immediately. Coffee-Date Shake has 485.0 calories. Coffee-Date Shake has 8.0 grams of protein. Coffee-Date Shake has 17.0 grams of fat. Coffee-Date Shake has 143.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Coffee-Date Shake are 1/2 cup lightly packed pitted dates, minced to paste, 1/2 cup whole milk, 2 teaspoons dark corn syrup, 4 ice cubes, 1 pint coffee ice cream.

Jack Rose recipe. In cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine applejack, lemon juice, and grenadine. Shake vigorously, then strain into a 6-ounce cocktail glass. Garnish with apple slice. Jack Rose has 168.0 calories. Jack Rose has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Jack Rose are 2 ounces applejack, 3/4 ounce fresh lemon juice, 1/2 ounce homemade grenadine (3/4 ounce if using bonded applejack), Thinly sliced apple, for garnish (optional).This drink was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.

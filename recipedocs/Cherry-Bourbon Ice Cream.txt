Cherry-Bourbon Ice Cream recipe. Cook 1 1/2 cups halved pitted cherries, 2 tablespoons sugar, and 1 tablespoon water in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until syrupy, 8-10 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in 1 tablespoon bourbon; let cool. Process custard and fold in cherry mixture just before transferring to freezer. Cherry-Bourbon Ice Cream has 132.0 calories. Cherry-Bourbon Ice Cream has 1.0 grams of protein. The ingredients of Cherry-Bourbon Ice Cream are 1 1/2 cups halved pitted cherries, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 tablespoon bourbon.

Minted Cucumber Raita recipe.  Peel cucumber. Cut in half lengthwise. Using spoon, remove seeds and pulp. Thinly slice cucumber. Combine cucumber with remaining ingredients. Season to taste with salt and pepper; serve. The ingredients of Minted Cucumber Raita are 1 cucumber, well chilled, 1 cup plain nonfat yogurt, 2 tablespoons minced fresh mint, 1 jalapeño chili, seeded, minced.Serve this Indian condiment with grilled chicken, curries or sliced tomatoes.

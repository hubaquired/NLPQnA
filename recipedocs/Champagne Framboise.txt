Champagne Framboise recipe.  Fill 8 champagne flutes with ice cubes. Pour 1 1/2 tablespoons framboise into each flute. Add 1 teaspoon lemon juice to each. Fill flutes with champagne. Garnish with raspberries and serve. The ingredients of Champagne Framboise are Ice cubes, 12 tablespoons framboise, 8 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 750-ml bottles (about) chilled Brut champagne, Garnish: 3/4 cup fresh raspberries (about 24).A simple drink for any kind of celebration.

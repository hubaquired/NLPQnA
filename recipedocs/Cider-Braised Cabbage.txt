Cider-Braised Cabbage recipe. Melt butter in large pot over medium-high heat. Add onion; sauté until beginning to soften, about 5 minutes. Add cabbage; saut;eacute; until slightly wilted, tossing frequently, about 6 minutes. Stir in apple cider. Reduce heat to medium; cover and cook until cabbage is tender, stirring occasionally, about 6 minutes. Uncover; simmer until almost all liquid in pot evaporates, about 3 minutes. Stir in vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Cider-Braised Cabbage has 136.0 calories. Cider-Braised Cabbage has 3.0 grams of protein. Cider-Braised Cabbage has 6.0 grams of fat. Cider-Braised Cabbage has 44.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cider-Braised Cabbage are 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 1 medium onion, thinly sliced, 1 2-pound head green cabbage, quartered, cored, thinly sliced, 1/2 cup apple cider, 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar.Braising the cabbage in cider gives it a light but distinct apple flavor.

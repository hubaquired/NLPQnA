Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste recipe. Combine all ingredients in processor and blend until very finely chopped. Can be prepared 1 week ahead. Refrigerate in covered container.) Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste has 138.0 calories. Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste has 1.0 grams of protein. Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste has 14.0 grams of fat. Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste has 339.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste are 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro, 1 cup chopped fresh parsley, 1/4 cup chopped seeded fresh red serrano or red jalapeño chilies, 1/4 cup water, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 1/2 tablespoons minced garlic, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon ground cumin.This recipe was created to accompany Grilled Ground Lamb Kebabs with Fresh Hot-Pepper Paste.

Ice Cream Sundaes with Chocolate-Coffee Sauce recipe. Combine chocolate, liqueur, and whipping cream in heavy small saucepan. Whisk over medium heat until melted and smooth. Scoop ice cream into dessert dishes or goblets. Spoon warm sauce over. Serve, passing additional liqueur. The ingredients of Ice Cream Sundaes with Chocolate-Coffee Sauce are 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped, 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons Homemade Coffee-Flavored Liqueur or Kahlúa plus additional for passing, 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream, 1/2 gallon purchased vanilla ice cream.Kate makes the chocolate sauce with homemade liqueur.

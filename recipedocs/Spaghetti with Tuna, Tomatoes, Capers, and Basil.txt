Spaghetti with Tuna, Tomatoes, Capers, and Basil recipe. Gently mix first 6 ingredients in large bowl. Season tuna-tomato mixture to taste with salt and pepper. Let stand 1 hour at room temperature. Cook spaghetti in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender but still firm to bite, stirring occasionally. Drain; return to pot. Add tuna-tomato mixture and basil. Toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to bowl and serve. Spaghetti with Tuna, Tomatoes, Capers, and Basil has 659.0 calories. The ingredients of Spaghetti with Tuna, Tomatoes, Capers, and Basil are 2 pounds tomatoes, halved, seeded, very thinly sliced, 2 6-ounce cans solid white tuna packed in oil, drained, separated into chunks, 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 4 anchovy fillets, minced, 2 small garlic cloves, minced, 1/4 cup chopped drained capers, 1 1/4 pounds spaghetti, 2 cups coarsely chopped fresh basil (from 3 large bunches).Serve this simple-to-prepare pasta with crusty bread and a glass of Italian rosé (called rosato) or Pinot Grigio.

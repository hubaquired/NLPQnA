Arugula, Blood Orange, and Blue Cheese Salad recipe. Combine arugula and cheese in large bowl. Remove peel and white pith from oranges. Cut between orange membranes to release segments into bowl with arugula and cheese. Pour oil and lemon juice over; toss to blend. Season with salt and pepper. Arugula, Blood Orange, and Blue Cheese Salad has 157.0 calories. Arugula, Blood Orange, and Blue Cheese Salad has 4.0 grams of protein. Arugula, Blood Orange, and Blue Cheese Salad has 13.0 grams of fat. Arugula, Blood Orange, and Blue Cheese Salad has 137.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Arugula, Blood Orange, and Blue Cheese Salad are 10 cups (packed) arugula (about 6 ounces), 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese, 2 blood oranges, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice.Pink grapefruit can be used if you can't find the blood oranges.

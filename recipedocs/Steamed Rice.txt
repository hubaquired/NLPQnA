Steamed Rice recipe.  Wash the rice. Put rice and salt into a heavy skillet lightly greased with butter. Pour on enough water or stock to come 1 1/2" above the rice. Cover tightly and bring to a rapid boil very quickly. Lower the heat and let the rice cook very slowly until all the liquid is absorbed. This should take about 15 to 20 minutes. Do not remove cover during cooking time. Fluff up rice with a fork and serve. Steamed Rice has 201.0 calories. Steamed Rice has 3.0 grams of protein. Steamed Rice has 3.0 grams of fat. Steamed Rice has 268.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Steamed Rice are 1 cup rice, 1/2 teaspoon salt, Butter, Water or stock.

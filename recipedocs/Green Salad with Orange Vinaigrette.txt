Green Salad with Orange Vinaigrette recipe.  Combine spinach leaves, endive and radishes in large bowl. Place vinegar in small bowl. Gradually mix in oil. Add sugar and orange peel. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Pour dressing over salad and toss. Mound salad on large platter. Top with orange and avocados slices. Green Salad with Orange Vinaigrette has 252.0 calories. Green Salad with Orange Vinaigrette has 3.0 grams of protein. Green Salad with Orange Vinaigrette has 24.0 grams of fat. Green Salad with Orange Vinaigrette has 42.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Salad with Orange Vinaigrette are 3/4 10-ounce package ready-to-use fresh spinach leaves, 2 large heads Belgian endive, cut into 1/2-inch-wide slices, 1 bunch radishes, trimmed, thinly sliced, 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar or red wine vinegar, 6 tablespoons olive oil, 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons grated orange peel, 2 oranges, peel and white pith removed, sliced into rounds, slices halved, 2 avocados, peeled, pitted, sliced.Avocado, Belgian endive and spinach are combined with oranges and radishes in this gorgeous seasonal salad.

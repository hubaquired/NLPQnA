Creamy Zucchini Soup recipe.  Bring zucchini and broth to boil in heavy medium saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until zucchini is very tender, about 15 minutes. Working in batches, puree soup in blender until almost smooth. Return soup to same saucepan. Add cream, nutmeg and cheese; stir over medium heat until warm. Season with salt and pepper. Creamy Zucchini Soup has 80.0 calories. Creamy Zucchini Soup has 5.0 grams of protein. Creamy Zucchini Soup has 4.0 grams of fat. Creamy Zucchini Soup has 92.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Zucchini Soup are 1 1/2 pounds zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 1 1/2 cups canned low-salt chicken broth or water, 1/4 cup whipping cream, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese.Nutmeg adds nice flavor to this soup.

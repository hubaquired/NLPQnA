Sauteed Radishes with Chives recipe. Halve radishes lengthwise and thinly slice them. In a heavy skillet heat butter over moderately high heat until foam subsides and sauté radishes, stirring, until radishes are crisp-tender, about 3 minutes. Stir in chives, pepper, and salt to taste. Sauteed Radishes with Chives has 24.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sauteed Radishes with Chives are 1 bunch radishes (about 6), 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh chives, freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Ginger Tea recipe. In a saucepan simmer ginger and water 20 minutes, or more for stronger tea. Add honey or sugar and strain tea through a sieve set over a teapot. Serve tea garnished with lemon wedges. Ginger Tea has 27.0 calories. Ginger Tea has 17.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Ginger Tea are 1/2 cup thinly sliced fresh ginger, 6 cups water, 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar, lemon wedges for garnish if desired.

Spicy Oven-Roasted Potatoes recipe. Preheat oven to 450°F. Toss potato wedges, oil, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper on a foil-lined large rimmed baking sheet to evenly coat; season potatoes with salt and pepper.

Brandy Cobbler recipe. Fill a tumbler three-quarters full with crushed ice. Add brandy, curaçao, and sugar. Stir. Decorate with the orange slice and pineapple stick. The ingredients of Brandy Cobbler are Crushed ice, 2 or 3 ounces brandy, 1 teaspoon curaçao, 1/2 teaspoon superfine sugar, Slice orange, Small pineapple stick.

Christmas Tonic recipe. Fill a double Old-Fashioned glass one-third full with cracked ice and pour over it the bitters and orange liqueur. Fill the glass with tonic and stir to blend. Serve. The ingredients of Christmas Tonic are Cracked ice, 2 ounces Campari, 1 ounce orange liqueur, Tonic water.

Pineapple Sangria recipe. In a pitcher, combine the juice, wine, brandy, pineapple, and soda with ice and stir. Pour into large wineglasses and garnish with the mint and orange. Pineapple Sangria has 204.0 calories. Pineapple Sangria has 1.0 grams of protein. Pineapple Sangria has 14.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Pineapple Sangria are 3 cups pineapple juice, 1 bottle dry white wine, such as Chardonnay or Sauvignon Blanc, 1 cup brandy (pear brandy is nice but not required), 1 ripe pineapple, cut into 1-inch chunks, 1 cup pineapple soda, 1 bunch fresh mint, roughly chopped, 1 orange, sliced.

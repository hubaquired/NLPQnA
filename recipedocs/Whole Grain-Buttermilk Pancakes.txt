Whole Grain-Buttermilk Pancakes recipe. Mix first 7 ingredients in medium bowl to blend. Whisk buttermilk, eggs, 2 tablespoons melted butter and molasses in large bowl to blend. Add dry ingredients; mix just until blended. Heat griddle or heavy large skillet over medium heat. Brush with butter. Working in batches, drop scant 1/4 cup batter onto griddle for each pancake. Cook until brown, about 3 minutes per side, brushing griddle with more butter as needed. Serve with syrup. Whole Grain-Buttermilk Pancakes has 91.0 calories. Whole Grain-Buttermilk Pancakes has 3.0 grams of protein. Whole Grain-Buttermilk Pancakes has 3.0 grams of fat. Whole Grain-Buttermilk Pancakes has 136.0 grams of sodium.

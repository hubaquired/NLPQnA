Salmon Burgers with Ginger Mustard Mayonnaise recipe.  In a small bowl whisk together mayonnaise ingredients. Discard any bones in salmon and finely chop salmon by hand. In a bowl stir together salmon, mustard, gingerroot, soy sauce, and salt and pepper to taste and form into two 3-inch patties. In a non-stick skillet heat oil over moderate heat until hot but not smoking and sauté patties 4 minutes on each side, or until just cooked through. Transfer salmon burgers to buns and top with mayonnaise, onion, and tomato. Salmon Burgers with Ginger Mustard Mayonnaise has 632.0 calories. The ingredients of Salmon Burgers with Ginger Mustard Mayonnaise are 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 3/4 teaspoon finely grated peeled fresh gingerroot, 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce, 3/4 pound salmon fillet, skin discarded, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, 2 teaspoons finely grated peeled fresh gingerroot, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, 2 hamburgers buns, cut sides toasted lightly, 2 thin slices sweet onion, 2 slices vine-ripened tomato.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

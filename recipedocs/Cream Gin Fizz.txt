Cream Gin Fizz recipe.  Combine all ingredients except the crushed ice and soda in a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Put the crushed ice in a highball glass, and strain in the mixture. Cream Gin Fizz has 25.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cream Gin Fizz are 1 1/2 ounces gin, 1 1/2 ounces milk, 3 teaspoons sugar, 4 tablespoons lime juice, 3 or 4 ice cubes, 1/2 cup crushed ice, Club soda.

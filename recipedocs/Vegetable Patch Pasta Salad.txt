Vegetable Patch Pasta Salad recipe. Cook pasta in medium pot of boiling salted water until just tender but still firm to bite. Drain pasta; rinse under cold water and drain well. Grate enough lemon peel to measure 1/2 teaspoon. Squeeze enough juice from lemon to measure 1 1/2 tablespoons. Place lemon peel and juice in medium bowl. Whisk in oil. Mix in carrot, radishes and chives, then pasta. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Let stand 10 minutes to blend flavors. Vegetable Patch Pasta Salad has 359.0 calories. Vegetable Patch Pasta Salad has 9.0 grams of protein. Vegetable Patch Pasta Salad has 15.0 grams of fat. Vegetable Patch Pasta Salad has 35.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Vegetable Patch Pasta Salad are 1 cup (about 4 ounces) small pasta shells or orzo (rice-shaped pasta), 1 large lemon, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 carrot, grated, 4 radishes, trimmed, thinly sliced, 1/3 cup chopped fresh chives or green onions.A simple lemon and oil dressing-which works with just about any fresh vegetable-turns some of your garden's finest into a light summer pasta salad.

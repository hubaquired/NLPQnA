Potato, Cucumber and Dill Salad recipe.  Cook potatoes in pot of boiling water until just tender. Drain. Transfer to large bowl. Add 2 tablespoons vinegar to hot potatoes and stir gently. Combine remaining 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar and mustard in small bowl. Gradually mix in oil. Add chopped dill. Mix into potatoes. Season with salt and pepper. Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover; chill. Bring to room temperature before serving.) Mix in cucumbers. Garnish with dill sprigs. Potato, Cucumber and Dill Salad has 365.0 calories. Potato, Cucumber and Dill Salad has 5.0 grams of protein. Potato, Cucumber and Dill Salad has 22.0 grams of fat. Potato, Cucumber and Dill Salad has 109.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Potato, Cucumber and Dill Salad are 2 pounds baby red potatoes, sliced, 3 1/2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar, 1 1/2 tablespoons country-style Dijon mustard, 6 tablespoons canola oil, 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill, 3/4 pound pickling cucumbers, sliced, Fresh dill sprigs.A quick low-fat, no-cholesterol side dish.

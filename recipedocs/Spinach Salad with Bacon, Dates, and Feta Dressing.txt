Spinach Salad with Bacon, Dates, and Feta Dressing recipe. In a blender blend together feta, lemon juice, water, and mayonnaise until smooth. With motor running add oil and blend until emulsified. In a small skillet cook bacon over moderate heat, stirring, until crisp and transfer with a slotted spoon to paper towels to drain. In a large bowl toss together spinach, bacon, onion, and dates with feta dressing. Spinach Salad with Bacon, Dates, and Feta Dressing has 291.0 calories. Spinach Salad with Bacon, Dates, and Feta Dressing has 8.0 grams of protein. Spinach Salad with Bacon, Dates, and Feta Dressing has 22.0 grams of fat. Spinach Salad with Bacon, Dates, and Feta Dressing has 365.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spinach Salad with Bacon, Dates, and Feta Dressing are 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese (about 2 ounces), 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 2 1/2 tablespoons water, 1 tablespoon mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 3 slices bacon, chopped, 1 bunch fresh spinach (about 3/4 pound), stems discarded and leaves washed well, spun dry, and torn into bite-size pieces, 1/2 medium red onion, sliced thin, 1/3 cup pitted dates, chopped.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Fennel and Carrot Confit recipe. Shave carrots with a vegetable peeler into very thin, wide ribbons. Quarter fennel bulb lengthwise, then very thinly slice lengthwise. Heat oil with cayenne, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a 1-quart heavy saucepan over low heat until warm. Add carrots, fennel, and zest and cook gently, without simmering, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are just tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Drain oil into a bowl see cooks' note, below) and transfer vegetables to another bowl to cool to room temperature. Fennel and Carrot Confit has 623.0 calories. Fennel and Carrot Confit has 1.0 grams of protein. Fennel and Carrot Confit has 68.0 grams of fat. Fennel and Carrot Confit has 44.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fennel and Carrot Confit are 2 medium carrots, 1 small fennel bulb, stalks discarded, 1 1/4 cups olive oil, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne, 2 (3-by 1-inch) strips lemon zest, very thinly sliced.Cooking anything very gently in oil produces a deliciously moist result (duck confit, for example), and this is no exception. The soft, tangled ribbons of carrot and fennel turn velvety and are balanced by slivers of lemon zest and the warm spark of cayenne.

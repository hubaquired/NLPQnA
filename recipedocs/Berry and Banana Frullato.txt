Berry and Banana Frullato recipe. Combine first 4 ingredients in blender. Add 1 tablespoon maple syrup and blend until smooth, adding more maple syrup to taste, if desired. Pour smoothie into 2 glasses. Top each smoothie with berries and serve. The ingredients of Berry and Banana Frullato are 1 cup fresh or frozen unsweetened berries (such as raspberries, blueberries, blackberries, or diced strawberries), 1 cup low-fat (1%) milk, 1 cup ice cubes, 1/2 large banana, peeled, sliced, 1 tablespoon (or more) maple syrup, Fresh raspberries, blackberries, or strawberries (for garnish).Inspired by frullato di frutta, the frothy Italian fruit drink, this smoothie can be made with any type of fresh or frozen berries.

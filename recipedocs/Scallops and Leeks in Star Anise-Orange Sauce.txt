Scallops and Leeks in Star Anise-Orange Sauce recipe.  Boil juice and star anise in saucepan until juice is reduced to 2/3 cup, about 4 minutes. Cool; remove star anise and reserve. Whisk sugar and cornstarch into juice. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add orange peel strips and reserved star anise to skillet and sauté 1 minute. Add leeks; sauté until tender, about 6 minutes. Add scallops; sauté until opaque in center, turning once, about 3 minutes. Add juice mixture; boil until sauce thickens slightly, about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Scallops and Leeks in Star Anise-Orange Sauce are 1 cup fresh orange juice, 4 whole star anise, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 teaspoon cornstarch, 2 tablespoons peanut oil, 1 tablespoon 1/2 x 1/8-inch orange peel strips (orange part only), 4 medium leeks (white and pale green parts only), cut crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces, 16 sea scallops.Star anise has been showing up all over restaurant menus lately, even in seafood dishes. This light and lively first course was inspired by such recipes.  Citrus Shellfish Orange Scallop Leek Anise Bon Appétit.

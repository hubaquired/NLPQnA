Applejack Spritzer recipe. In a tall glass combine the applejack, the apple juice, the lemon juice, and 4 ice cubes. Fill the glass with the seltzer and stir the drink. Applejack Spritzer has 138.0 calories. Applejack Spritzer has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Applejack Spritzer are 1 1/2 ounces (1 jigger) applejack, 3 ounces (2 jiggers) apple juice, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice, Chilled seltzer or club soda.

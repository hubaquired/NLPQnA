Grilled Lemon Pepper Chicken Breasts with Thyme Gremolata recipe. In a bowl whisk together lemon juice, pepper, and salt to taste and add oil in a stream, whisking until marinade is emulsified. With a rolling pin or smooth side of a meat pounder flatten chicken 1/4 inch thick between sheets of plastic wrap. In a large resealable plastic bag marinate chicken in marinade, chilled, 30 minutes. Prepare grill. Grill chicken on an oiled rack set 5 to 6 inches over glowing coals until just cooked through, about 2 minutes on each side. Serve chicken sprinkled with gremolata and garnished with lemon and thyme. In a small bowl stir together gremolata ingredients. The ingredients of Grilled Lemon Pepper Chicken Breasts with Thyme Gremolata are 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper, 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, 3 whole boneless skinless chicken breasts (about 2 1/4 pounds), halved, 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves, 2 large garlic cloves, minced, 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest, lemon slices, thyme sprigs.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less but requires additional unattended time.

In a medium bowl, whisk together the oil, egg, nectar or buttermilk, and extracts. Make a well in the middle of the dry ingredients and pour in the oil-egg mixture. Whisk or stir just to blend well; don't over mix. Scrape the batter into the prepared pans), filling them about two-thirds full. Bake 60 to 65 minutes for a large loaf, 40 to 45 minutes for small loaves or for the time indicated for your altitude in the chart), or until the bread is golden brown and a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean.

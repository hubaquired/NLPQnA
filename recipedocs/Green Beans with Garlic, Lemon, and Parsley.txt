Green Beans with Garlic, Lemon, and Parsley recipe. Cook beans in large pot of boiling salted water until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Drain. Place beans in bowl of ice water to cool. Drain well. Melt butter with oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic; stir 30 seconds. Add beans; sauté until heated through, about 5 minutes. Stir in parsley and lemon peel. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to platter. Green Beans with Garlic, Lemon, and Parsley has 112.0 calories. Green Beans with Garlic, Lemon, and Parsley has 2.0 grams of protein. Green Beans with Garlic, Lemon, and Parsley has 9.0 grams of fat. Green Beans with Garlic, Lemon, and Parsley has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Green Beans with Garlic, Lemon, and Parsley are 1 1/2 pounds green beans, trimmed, 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 large garlic cloves, minced, 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, 1 tablespoon grated lemon peel.

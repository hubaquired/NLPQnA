Berry Walnut Cereal recipe.  In a bowl, combine all ingredients. The ingredients of Berry Walnut Cereal are 3/4 cup cooked bulgur wheat, 1/2 cup blueberries, 1/2 cup 1 percent milk, 2 tablespoons chopped walnuts, 1 teaspoon maple syrup, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon.

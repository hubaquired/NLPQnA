Hot Cranberry-Rum Toddy recipe.  Bring cranberry juice, sugar, spices, and ginger to a boil, stirring until sugar is dissolved, then simmer, covered, 10 minutes. Stir in rum and serve hot. Hot Cranberry-Rum Toddy has 236.0 calories. Hot Cranberry-Rum Toddy has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Hot Cranberry-Rum Toddy are 3 qt cranberry juice cocktail, 3/4 cup sugar, 3 (3 1/2-inch) cinnamon sticks, 4 whole star anise, 1 (3-inch) piece gingerroot, peeled and thinly sliced, 2 cups amber or dark rum.

Chick-Pea, Garlic, and Parsley Dip recipe.  In a food processor blend all ingredients except oil until smooth. With motor running add oil in a slow stream. Season dip with salt. Serve dip with toasts. Chick-Pea, Garlic, and Parsley Dip has 344.0 calories. Chick-Pea, Garlic, and Parsley Dip has 11.0 grams of protein. Chick-Pea, Garlic, and Parsley Dip has 18.0 grams of fat. Chick-Pea, Garlic, and Parsley Dip has 388.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chick-Pea, Garlic, and Parsley Dip are a 19-ounce can chick-peas, rinsed and drained (about 2 cups), 2 garlic cloves, chopped and mashed to a paste with 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup packed fresh parsley leaves, washed well and spun dry, 1/4 cup water, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, Accompaniment: toasted pita wedges or toasted French bread slices.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

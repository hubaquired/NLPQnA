Chinese Celery Strips recipe. Trim celery, reserving leaves. Cut ribs crosswise into 2-inch pieces, then cut each piece lengthwise into 1/8-inch-thick strips. Toss with celery leaves and remaining ingredients until combined well. Serve chilled or at room temperature. Chinese Celery Strips has 47.0 calories. Chinese Celery Strips has 2.0 grams of protein. Chinese Celery Strips has 1.0 grams of fat. Chinese Celery Strips has 473.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chinese Celery Strips are 1 bunch celery (2 1/4 lb), 1 scallion, minced, 1 tablespoon oyster sauce, 2 teaspoons soy sauce, 1/8 teaspoon Asian sesame oil.

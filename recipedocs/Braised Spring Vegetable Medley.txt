Braised Spring Vegetable Medley recipe.  Bring broth to simmer in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat. Add potatoes; cover pan and cook potatoes until beginning to soften, about 5 minutes. Add lima beans, asparagus and wine. Cook, uncovered, until all vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Add peas and cook 1 minute. Season stew to taste with salt and pepper. Ladle stew into deep bowls. Top with pesto sauce and serve. Braised Spring Vegetable Medley has 478.0 calories. Braised Spring Vegetable Medley has 18.0 grams of protein. Braised Spring Vegetable Medley has 18.0 grams of fat. Braised Spring Vegetable Medley has 1224.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Braised Spring Vegetable Medley are 1 14 1/2-ounce can vegetable broth, 8 ounces red-skinned potatoes, cut into 1/2-inch cubes (about 1 3/4 cups), 8 ounces frozen lima beans (about 1 1/2 cups), thawed, 6 ounces asparagus spears, trimmed, cut into 1-inch lengths, 1/2 cup dry white wine, 3/4 cup frozen peas, 2 tablespoons prepared pesto sauce.

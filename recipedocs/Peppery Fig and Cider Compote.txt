Peppery Fig and Cider Compote recipe.  Cut figs into 1-inch pieces. Boil cider with figs and remaining ingredients, uncovered, until it is slightly syrupy and figs are plump, about 20 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature. Peppery Fig and Cider Compote has 137.0 calories. Peppery Fig and Cider Compote has 1.0 grams of protein. Peppery Fig and Cider Compote has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Peppery Fig and Cider Compote are 8 ounces dried Mission figs, stemmed, 1 1/2 cups unfiltered apple cider, 1/4 cup sugar, 3 (2-inch) strips orange zest, 1 Turkish or 1/2 California bay leaf, 1 teaspoon black peppercorns, crushed, Accompaniment: Fresh Ricotta.

Spicy Butternut Squash with Bacon and Lime recipe.  Steam squash on a streamer rack set over boiling water, covered, until tender, about 5 to 8 minutes. In a skillet cook bacon over moderate heat until crisp, reserving 2 teaspoons bacon fat. Crumble bacon into a bowl and toss with squash, juice, Tabasco, reserved bacon fat, and salt and pepper to taste. Spicy Butternut Squash with Bacon and Lime has 363.0 calories. Spicy Butternut Squash with Bacon and Lime has 10.0 grams of protein. Spicy Butternut Squash with Bacon and Lime has 18.0 grams of fat. Spicy Butternut Squash with Bacon and Lime has 312.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Spicy Butternut Squash with Bacon and Lime are 1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1/2-inch cubes, 3 slices bacon, 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice, 1/2 teaspoon Tabasco, or to taste.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Cranberry and Horseradish Relish recipe. Combine cranberries, onion, sugar and salt in processor. Chop coarsely, using on/off turns. Transfer mixture to medium bowl. Mix in sour cream and horseradish. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour. Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and keep refrigerated.) Cranberry and Horseradish Relish has 219.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry and Horseradish Relish are 1 12-ounce bag fresh or unthawed frozen cranberries, 1 small onion, quartered, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup light sour cream or plain low-fat yogurt, 3 tablespoons drained prepared horseradish.Here's a tangy condiment that's great with roast turkey, beef or leg of lamb.

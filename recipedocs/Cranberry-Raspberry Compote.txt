Cranberry-Raspberry Compote recipe. Combine all ingredients except vanilla in heavy medium saucepan. Simmer over medium heat until cranberries burst and mixture thickens, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat. Cool slightly. Stir in vanilla. Cool to room temperature. Cover and refrigerate until cold, at least 6 hours. Can be prepared 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.) Cranberry-Raspberry Compote has 171.0 calories. Cranberry-Raspberry Compote has 1.0 grams of protein. Cranberry-Raspberry Compote has 1.0 grams of fat. Cranberry-Raspberry Compote has 9.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cranberry-Raspberry Compote are 1 12-ounce package frozen unsweetened raspberries, thawed, 1 cup fresh cranberries or frozen, thawed, 3/4 cup (packed) golden brown sugar, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1 tablespoon vanilla extract.This recipe originally accompanied Cranberry Swirl Cheesecake with Cranberry Raspberry Compote.

Mike Romanoff recipe. In cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine vodka, Cointreau, apricot liqueur, and lime juice. Cover, shake vigorously, and strain into cocktail glass. Mike Romanoff has 166.0 calories. Mike Romanoff has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Mike Romanoff are 2 ounces vodka, 1 teaspoon Cointreau, 1 teaspoon apricot liqueur, 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice.This drink was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.

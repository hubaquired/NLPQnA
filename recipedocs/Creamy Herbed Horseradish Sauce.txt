Creamy Herbed Horseradish Sauce recipe.  Melt butter in heavy small saucepan over medium heat. Add shallots and garlic; sauté until soft and translucent, about 1 minute. Add wine; simmer until mixture is reduced to 2 tablespoons, about 8 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Whisk remaining ingredients into shallot mixture. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving. Can be prepared 2 days ahead.) Creamy Herbed Horseradish Sauce has 246.0 calories. Creamy Herbed Horseradish Sauce has 2.0 grams of protein. Creamy Herbed Horseradish Sauce has 19.0 grams of fat. Creamy Herbed Horseradish Sauce has 205.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Creamy Herbed Horseradish Sauce are 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon minced shallots, 1 teaspoon minced garlic, 1 cup dry white wine, 1 cup sour cream, 1/2 cup prepared horseradish, drained, 1 tablespoon minced fresh chives or green onions, 1 tablespoon minced fresh thyme or 1 teaspoon dried, 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice.

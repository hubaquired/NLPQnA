Quick Anchovy Mayonnaise for Asparagus recipe.  In a blender or food processor blend the egg, the lemon juice, the mustard, the anchovies, the salt, and the white pepper and with the motor running add the oil in a stream. Thin the mayonnaise with water if desired and serve it over asparagus. Quick Anchovy Mayonnaise for Asparagus has 375.0 calories. Quick Anchovy Mayonnaise for Asparagus has 1.0 grams of protein. Quick Anchovy Mayonnaise for Asparagus has 42.0 grams of fat. Quick Anchovy Mayonnaise for Asparagus has 144.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Quick Anchovy Mayonnaise for Asparagus are 1 large egg at room temperature, 5 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard, 3 flat anchovy fillets, rinsed, drained, and patted dry, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon white pepper, 3/4 cup olive oil, vegetable oil, or a combination of both.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

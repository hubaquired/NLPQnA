Celery Seed Dressing recipe.  Put the sugar, mustard, salt, onion juice, and vinegar into a pint jar and shake well. Add the oil and continue shaking vigorously until all are well blended. Add the paprika and celery seed and give the dressing a final shaking. Celery Seed Dressing has 184.0 calories. Celery Seed Dressing has 18.0 grams of fat. Celery Seed Dressing has 161.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Celery Seed Dressing are 1/3 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon dry mustard, 1 teaspoon salt, 2 teaspoons onion juice, 1/3 cup vinegar, 1 cup olive oil, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1 1/2 teaspoons celery seed.

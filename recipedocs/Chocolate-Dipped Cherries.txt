Chocolate-Dipped Cherries recipe. Line baking sheet with waxed paper. Place chocolate in top of double boiler set over barely simmering water do not allow bottom of pan to touch water); stir until melted and smooth. Remove chocolate from over water. Holding cherry stem, dip 1 cherry halfway into chocolate. Place cherry, chocolate side down, on prepared sheet. Repeat with remaining cherries and chocolate. Chill until chocolate is firm, at least 15 minutes. Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep chilled.) Chocolate-Dipped Cherries has 41.0 calories. Chocolate-Dipped Cherries has 2.0 grams of fat. Chocolate-Dipped Cherries has 1.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chocolate-Dipped Cherries are 6 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) or semisweet chocolate, chopped, 24 large fresh cherries with stems.This recipe originally accompanied Cherry-Fudge Brownies with Chocolate Sauce .

Champagne Black Raspberry Punch recipe.  In a punch bowl set in a hollowed-out block of ice, combine the champagne with the Chambord. Garnish the punch withthe raspberries and the lemon slices. Champagne Black Raspberry Punch has 551.0 calories. Champagne Black Raspberry Punch has 30.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Champagne Black Raspberry Punch are 3 bottles chilled dry champagne, 1/3 to 1/2 cup Chambord, or to taste, Fresh black raspberries, Garnish: thin lemon slices.

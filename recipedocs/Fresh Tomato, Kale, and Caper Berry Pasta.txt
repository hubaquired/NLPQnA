Fresh Tomato, Kale, and Caper Berry Pasta recipe.  Place the tomatoes, red wine vinegar, oil, salt and pepper in a large bowl and, using a fork, gently crush the tomatoes. Set aside to marinate for 10 minutes. While the tomato is marinating, cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water for 8–10 minutes or until al dente. Drain the pasta and place in a large bowl, add the pesto and toss to coat. Top with the tomato mixture, kale, caper berries, ricotta, salt and pepper to serve. The ingredients of Fresh Tomato, Kale, and Caper Berry Pasta are 1 3/4 pounds cherry tomatoes, quartered, 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar, 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, Sea salt and cracked black pepper, 1 pound spaghetti, 1/3 cup store-bought pesto, 2 cups baby kale leaves, 1/3 cup caper berries, 1 cup fresh ricotta.The sweet and juicy tomatoes perfectly contrast with the salty caper berries and creamy ricotta in this super-simple spaghetti—it's my go-to when I need a quick meat-free weeknight dish.

Brandied Dried Cranberries and Cherries recipe.  In a small saucepan simmer all ingredients except brandy, stirring, minutes, and remove pan from heat. Stir in brandy and cool. Brandied fruits may be made 2 days ahead and chilled, covered. Bring brandied fruits to room temperature before serving. Brandied Dried Cranberries and Cherries has 251.0 calories. Brandied Dried Cranberries and Cherries has 1.0 grams of fat. Brandied Dried Cranberries and Cherries has 5.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Brandied Dried Cranberries and Cherries are 1/4 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup dried cranberries (about 2 1/2 ounces), 1/2 cup dried sour cherries (about 2 1/2 ounces), 2 tablespoons brandy such as Cognac.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less. To accompany Ice-Cream Bombes with Brandied Dried Cranberries and Cherries.

Asparagus with Tomato Vinaigrette recipe.  Cook asparagus in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender, about 5 minutes. Drain. Refresh under cold running water and drain thoroughly. Arrange asparagus on large platter. Cover and refrigerate until cold. Whisk oil and vinegar to blend in small bowl. Add tomato, bell pepper, green onion and parsley. Season with salt and pepper. Can be prepared 3 hours ahead. Let stand at room temperature.) Spoon half of vinaigrette over asparagus. Garnish with egg. Pass remaining dressing separately. Asparagus with Tomato Vinaigrette has 227.0 calories. Asparagus with Tomato Vinaigrette has 4.0 grams of protein. Asparagus with Tomato Vinaigrette has 22.0 grams of fat. Asparagus with Tomato Vinaigrette has 19.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Asparagus with Tomato Vinaigrette are 1 pound large asparagus spears, trimmed, 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, 1 small tomato, finely chopped, 1/4 green bell pepper, finely chopped, 1 green onion, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, 1 hard-boiled egg, finely chopped.

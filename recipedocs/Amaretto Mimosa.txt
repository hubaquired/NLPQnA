Amaretto Mimosa recipe. In a pitcher stir together orange juice and liqueur. Fill each of 6 champagne flutes halfway with some juice mixture and top off with sparkling wine. Amaretto Mimosa has 98.0 calories. Amaretto Mimosa has 4.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Amaretto Mimosa are 3 cups fresh orange juice, strained, 1/4 cup almond-flavored liqueur such as Amaretto di Saronno, or to taste, A 750-ml. bottle chilled sparkling wine such as Freixenet.

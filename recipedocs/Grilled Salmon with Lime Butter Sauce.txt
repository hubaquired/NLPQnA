Grilled Salmon with Lime Butter Sauce recipe. Prepare grill for cooking over medium-hot charcoal moderate heat for gas). Season salmon all over with salt and pepper, then grill, flesh sides down, on lightly oiled grill rack covered only if using gas grill) 4 minutes. Turn fillets over and grill covered only if using gas grill) until just cooked through, 4 to 6 minutes more. Sprinkle fillets with zest and top each with 1 tablespoon lime butter sauce. The ingredients of Grilled Salmon with Lime Butter Sauce are 6 (6-oz) pieces center-cut salmon fillet (about 1 inch thick) with skin, 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated fresh lime zest, 6 tablespoons lime butter sauce.Just a sprinkle of zest and a dab of lime butter sauce beautifully highlight the flavor of grilled salmon.

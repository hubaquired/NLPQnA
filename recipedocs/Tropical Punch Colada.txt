Tropical Punch Colada recipe. Mix first 4 ingredients in pitcher. Fill 2 tall glasses with ice. Pour drink over. *Cream of coconut is available in the liquor section of most supermarkets. Tropical Punch Colada has 498.0 calories. Tropical Punch Colada has 1.0 grams of protein. Tropical Punch Colada has 12.0 grams of fat. Tropical Punch Colada has 43.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Tropical Punch Colada are 1 cup pineapple juice, 1/2 cup canned guava nectar, 1/2 cup cream of coconut (such as Coco López)*, 1/2 cup light rum, 2 cups ice cubes.

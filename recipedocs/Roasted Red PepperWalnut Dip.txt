Roasted Red PepperWalnut Dip recipe. Blend together walnuts, roasted peppers, bread pieces, garlic paste, lemon juice, cumin, and cayenne in a food processor until as smooth as possible, about 1 minute. With motor running, add oil in a slow stream and blend until combined well. Season to taste with salt and additional lemon juice, as well as additional cayenne and minced garlic. Roasted Red PepperWalnut Dip has 428.0 calories. Roasted Red PepperWalnut Dip has 2.0 grams of protein. Roasted Red PepperWalnut Dip has 43.0 grams of fat. Roasted Red PepperWalnut Dip has 689.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Roasted Red PepperWalnut Dip are 1/4 cup chopped walnuts, 1/2 cup chopped drained roasted red peppers (from a 7-ounce jar), 1 slice firm white sandwich bread, torn into pieces, 1 medium garlic clove, or to taste, minced and mashed to a paste with 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, or to taste, 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste, 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, Salt.Transform jarred roasted red peppers into a fresh and flavorful dip seasoned with cumin and a pinch of cayenne.

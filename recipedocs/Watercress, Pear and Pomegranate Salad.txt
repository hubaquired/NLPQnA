Watercress, Pear and Pomegranate Salad recipe.  Whisk first 5 ingredients in small bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Combine watercress and lettuce in bowl. Pour all but 2 tablespoons dressing over; toss to coat. Divide greens among 6 plates. Arrange 4 pear wedges in spoke pattern on each plate. Drizzle 2 tablespoons dressing over pears. Sprinkle with cheese and pomegranate kernels. Watercress, Pear and Pomegranate Salad has 184.0 calories. Watercress, Pear and Pomegranate Salad has 4.0 grams of protein. Watercress, Pear and Pomegranate Salad has 17.0 grams of fat. Watercress, Pear and Pomegranate Salad has 86.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Watercress, Pear and Pomegranate Salad are 1/4 cup olive oil, 3 tablespoons pure pomegranate juice, 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar, 2 tablespoons walnut oil, 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard, 2 large bunches watercress, thick stems trimmed, 1 head butter lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces, 2 firm but ripe pears, halved, cored, each half cut into 6 wedges, 1/2 cup crumbled ricotta salata cheese or feta cheese, 1/2 cup fresh pomegranate kernels.

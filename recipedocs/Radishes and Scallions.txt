Radishes and Scallions recipe. In a non-stick skillet cook the radishes and the scallions in the butter over moderate heat for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the radishes are tender. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the parsley, the zest, and salt and pepper to taste. Radishes and Scallions has 75.0 calories. Radishes and Scallions has 1.0 grams of protein. Radishes and Scallions has 6.0 grams of fat. Radishes and Scallions has 51.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Radishes and Scallions are 20 radishes, trimmed and sliced very thin (about 2 cups), 2 scallions, minced, 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, 2 teaspoons minced fresh parsley leaves, a pinch of freshly grated lemon zest.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

Rice Pilaf with Spinach and Caramelized Onions recipe. Heat oil in heavy large wide pot over medium heat. Add onions; sauté until tender and deep brown, about 20 minutes. Transfer 1/2 cup onion slices to small bowl and reserve. Add carrot, cumin, and garlic to onions in pot; stir 1 minute. Stir in rice. Add broth; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer until rice is tender and most liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes. Stir spinach into rice mixture; cover and cook until spinach wilts, about 2 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Transfer pilaf to bowl; top with reserved 1/2 cup onion slices. The ingredients of Rice Pilaf with Spinach and Caramelized Onions are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 very large onions, halved, sliced (about 4 cups), 1 large carrot, coarsely grated, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 garlic clove, minced, 1 1/2 cups arborio rice or medium-grain white rice, 4 cups canned low-salt chicken broth or vegetable broth, 1 6-ounce bag fresh baby spinach.

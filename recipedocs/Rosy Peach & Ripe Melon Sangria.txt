Rosy Peach & Ripe Melon Sangria recipe.  Place all the fruit in a large pitcher. Pour in wine, juice and Cointreau. Let rest at room temperature for 1 to 2 hours to blend flavors. Before serving, add the sparkling water. Serve in wine glasses over ice and garnish with mint. The ingredients of Rosy Peach & Ripe Melon Sangria are 8 peeled, diced (1/4 inch) ripe white or golden peaches, 4 cups diced (1/4 inch) ripe cantaloupe, 2 cups diced (1/4 inch) ripe strawberries, 2 bottles rosé or dry white wine, 1/2 cup fresh grapefruit juice, 1/4 cup Cointreau liqueur, 4 cups sparkling water, 16 mint sprigs, for garnish.Rosé wine with fresh fruit adds the right touch to this spiced-up menu.

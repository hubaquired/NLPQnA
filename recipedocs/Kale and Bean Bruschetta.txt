Kale and Bean Bruschetta recipe.  Rub cut side of garlic on toasts. Top with bean mixture and cheese shavings. The ingredients of Kale and Bean Bruschetta are 1 garlic clove, cut in half, 12 1/2-inch-thick, 3-inch-long slices baguette, toasted, 1 1/2 cups Cannellini Beans with Kale , warmed, Parmesan cheese shavings.

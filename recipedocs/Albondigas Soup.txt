Albondigas Soup recipe. Heat oil in heavy medium saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and three garlic clover and sauté until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in 1 tablespoon chili powder and cook 1 minute longer. Add tomatoes with their juices and beef broth. Cover and simmer 5 minutes. Puree mixture in blender or processor in batches; return to saucepan. Season beef with salt and pepper. Mix in remaining garlic clove, 1/2 tablespoon chili powder and oregano. Form beef into 1-inch meatballs. Return soup to gentle simmer. Add meatballs to soup and simmer until meatballs are just cooked through, about 5 minutes. Ladle soup into bowls. Garnish soup with chopped fresh cilantro and serve with sour cream if desired. Albondigas Soup has 302.0 calories. Albondigas Soup has 27.0 grams of protein. Albondigas Soup has 16.0 grams of fat. Albondigas Soup has 555.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Albondigas Soup are 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 large onion, chopped, 4 large garlic cloves, minced, 1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder, 1 28-ounce can plum tomatoes, 1 14 1/2-ounce can beef broth, 1 pound lean ground beef, 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, crumbled, Chopped fresh cilantro, Sour cream (optional).A different, easy way to enjoy ground beef.

Steak and Onion Sandwiches with Horseradish Mayonnaise recipe. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion; sauté until soft and lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Transfer to plate. Add remaining 1 tablespoon butter to skillet and increase heat to medium-high. Season steak on both sides with salt and pepper. Add to skillet and cook to desired doneness, about 2 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer steak to work surface; cover and let stand 5 minutes. Meanwhile, mix mayonnaise and horseradish in small bowl. Spread mixture over cut sides of rolls. Thinly slice steak. Place steak slices on roll bottoms. Top with onions and arugula, then roll tops, and serve. Steak and Onion Sandwiches with Horseradish Mayonnaise has 982.0 calories. The ingredients of Steak and Onion Sandwiches with Horseradish Mayonnaise are 3 tablespoons butter, divided, 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced into rounds, 1 12-ounce rib-eye steak, about 3/4 inch thick, 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish, 2 kaiser rolls, halved horizontally, lightly toasted, 1 cup arugula.Serve with: French fries or potato chips, and steamed broccoli.

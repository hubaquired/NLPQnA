Chipotle Mayonnaise with Lime recipe. Stir together chipotles and mayonnaise. Squeeze lime juice onto cooked corn, then spread chipotle mayonnaise onto corn. Chipotle Mayonnaise with Lime has 201.0 calories. Chipotle Mayonnaise with Lime has 22.0 grams of fat. Chipotle Mayonnaise with Lime has 222.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Chipotle Mayonnaise with Lime are 4 teaspoons minced seeded canned chipotle chiles in adobo, including sauce, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, Lime wedges.This flavorful topping brings out the flavor of grilled or boiled corn on the cob.

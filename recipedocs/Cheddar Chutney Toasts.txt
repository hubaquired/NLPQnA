Cheddar Chutney Toasts recipe.  Preheat broiler. On a baking sheet arrange bread slices in one layer and broil about 4 inches from heat until golden, 1 to 2 minutes on each side. In a small bowl stir together chutney and mustard and spread on one side of each toast. Divide Cheddar evenly among toasts and broil until cheese is melted and golden, 2 to 3 minutes. Serve toasts immediately. Cheddar Chutney Toasts has 162.0 calories. Cheddar Chutney Toasts has 6.0 grams of protein. Cheddar Chutney Toasts has 6.0 grams of fat. Cheddar Chutney Toasts has 289.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Cheddar Chutney Toasts are six 3/4-inch-thick diagonal slices Italian or French bread, 2 tablespoons Major Grey's chutney, any large pieces chopped, 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 3/4 cup grated extra-sharp Cheddar cheese.Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

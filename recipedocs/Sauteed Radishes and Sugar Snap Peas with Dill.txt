Sauteed Radishes and Sugar Snap Peas with Dill recipe.  This side dish would pair beautifully with roast lamb or salmon. To remove strings from fresh peas, just snap off the stem end and pull string lengthwise down each pod. Melt butter with oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add shallots and sauté until golden, about 5 minutes. Add sugar snap peas and radishes; sauté until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Add orange juice and dill seeds; stir 1 minute. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in chopped dill. Transfer to bowl; serve. Sauteed Radishes and Sugar Snap Peas with Dill has 82.0 calories. Sauteed Radishes and Sugar Snap Peas with Dill has 2.0 grams of protein. Sauteed Radishes and Sugar Snap Peas with Dill has 4.0 grams of fat. Sauteed Radishes and Sugar Snap Peas with Dill has 20.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sauteed Radishes and Sugar Snap Peas with Dill are 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1/2 cup thinly sliced shallots, 12 ounces sugar snap peas, trimmed, strings removed, 2 cups thinly sliced radishes (about 1 large bunch), 1/4 cup orange juice, 1 teaspoon dill seeds, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill.

Chipotle-Lime Salt recipe. Toss 2 tablespoons flaky sea salt and 1 teaspoon chipotle chili powder on a baking sheet. Finely zest 2 limes over. Bake in a 350°F oven, stirring occasionally, until lime zest is dry, about 5 minutes. Let cool. Chipotle-Lime Salt has 7.0 calories. Chipotle-Lime Salt has 2883.0 grams of sodium.

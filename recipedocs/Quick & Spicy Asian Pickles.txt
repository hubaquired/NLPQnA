Quick & Spicy Asian Pickles recipe.  Whisk rice vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, fish sauce, and crushed red pepper in large bowl. DO AHEAD Can be made 1 day ahed. Cover and Chill. Slice cucumbers crosswise into 1/4-inch-thick rounds. Add cucumber slices to vinegar mixture; toss. Cover and chill ten minutes to 1 hour, tossing occassionally. Quick & Spicy Asian Pickles has 23.0 calories. Quick & Spicy Asian Pickles has 1.0 grams of protein. Quick & Spicy Asian Pickles has 128.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Quick & Spicy Asian Pickles are 1/4 cup rice vinegar, 4 teaspoons sugar, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 1 teaspoon fish sauce, 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper, 2 small English hothouse cucumbers.The flavors get stronger the longer the cucumbers marinate.

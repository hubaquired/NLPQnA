Broccoli with Garlic and Parmesan Cheese recipe.  Heat oil in large skillet over high heat. Add garlic and lemon peel and stir 30 seconds. Add broccoli and toss to coat. Add 1/2 cup water. Cover skillet and cook until broccoli is crisp-tender and water has cooked away, about 5 minutes. Add cheese and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. The ingredients of Broccoli with Garlic and Parmesan Cheese are 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons chopped garlic, 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel, 2 pounds broccoli, stalks discarded, crowns cut into florets, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (about 11/2 ounces).For this recipe, Lisa uses broccoli rabe, a type of bitter Italian green. But the everyday supermarket broccoli works fine, too.

Apple and Celery Salad recipe.  In a small bowl whisk together mayonnaise, cilantro, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste until combined. Cut celery and apple into 1 1/2-inch-long thin julienne strips. Toss together celery, apple, dressing, and salt and pepper to taste. Apple and Celery Salad has 126.0 calories. The ingredients of Apple and Celery Salad are 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro leaves, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 celery ribs, 1 crisp red apple.This recipe can be prepared in 45 minutes or less.

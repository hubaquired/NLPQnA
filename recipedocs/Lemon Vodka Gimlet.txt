Lemon Vodka Gimlet recipe.  In an old-fashioned glass, stir together vodka, fresh lime juice and Rose's lime juice. Fill the glass with ice cubes, and garnish the drink with the rind. Lemon Vodka Gimlet has 113.0 calories. Lemon Vodka Gimlet has 2.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Lemon Vodka Gimlet are 1 jigger (1 1/2 ounces) lemon-flavored vodka, 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice, 1 tablespoon Rose's lime juice, Garnish: a twist of lime rind.

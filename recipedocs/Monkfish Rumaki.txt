Monkfish Rumaki recipe.  Sauté bacon in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat until crisp, about 6 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer bacon to paper towels. Pour off all but 1 tablespoon pan drippings. Add monkfish to skillet and sauté until cooked through, about 2 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer fish to plate. Tent with foil. Return bacon to skillet. Add radicchio and 1/4 cup Honey Mustard Sauce. Sauté until radicchio wilts, about 2 minutes. Spoon radicchio mixture onto center of plates. Arrange monkfish around radicchio. Drizzle remaining Honey Mustard Sauce atop fish. The ingredients of Monkfish Rumaki are 4 bacon slices, cut crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces, 1 1/2 pounds monkfish fillets, membranes removed, cut crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces, 1 large head radicchio, thinly sliced (about 4 cups), Honey Mustard Sauce.The most popular dish on the menu at The Atlantic Inn. It is served with sautéed bacon and radicchio and Honey Mustard Sauce .  Fish Leafy Green Pork Vegetable Appetizer Sauté Bacon Summer Bon Appétit.

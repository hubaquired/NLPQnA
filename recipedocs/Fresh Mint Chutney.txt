Fresh Mint Chutney recipe.  Coarsely purée all ingredients in a food processor. Fresh Mint Chutney has 59.0 calories. Fresh Mint Chutney has 2.0 grams of protein. Fresh Mint Chutney has 1.0 grams of fat. Fresh Mint Chutney has 655.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Fresh Mint Chutney are 1 cup packed fresh mint leaves, 4 scallions, coarsely chopped, 1 small fresh green serrano chile, coarsely chopped (1 to 1 1/2 teaspoons), including seeds, 1 large garlic clove, chopped, 1/4 cup fresh lime juice, 2 tablespoons water, 2 teaspoons sugar, 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin, 3/4 teaspoon salt.This chutney is great with sautéed scallops, grilled shrimp, or grilled lamb.

Sea Breeze Sparklers recipe. Stir juices together in a large pitcher. Just before serving, stir in sparkling water. Serve over ice with lime wedges. Sea Breeze Sparklers has 95.0 calories. Sea Breeze Sparklers has 1.0 grams of protein. Sea Breeze Sparklers has 13.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Sea Breeze Sparklers are 2 1/2 cups fresh grapefruit juice, 3 1/2 cups chilled cranberry juice cocktail, 2 1/2 cups chilled sparkling water, 1 lime, cut into wedges.

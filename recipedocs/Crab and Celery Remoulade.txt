Crab and Celery Remoulade recipe.  Whisk first 3 ingredients in medium bowl to blend. Stir in crabmeat, celery, 3 tablespoons chopped chives, and lemon juice. Season with pepper. DO AHEAD: Can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill. Transfer remoulade to serving bowl; surround with pita chips. Garnish with additional chives and serve. The ingredients of Crab and Celery Remoulade are 1/3 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup sour cream, 1 tablespoon drained capers, 8 ounces fresh crabmeat, picked over, 1/2 cup finely chopped celery, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives plus additional for garnish, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, Purchased baked pita chips.

Autumn Pear Chip recipe.  Swirl poire Williams in 2 glasses to coat; discard poire Williams. Using muddler or handle of wooden spoon, mash ginger in cocktail shaker. Add Calvados, pear juice, lemon juice, and ice; shake well. Strain into glasses. Garnish each with pear crisp. The ingredients of Autumn Pear Chip are Poire Williams (clear pear brandy), 6 quarter-size rounds of ginger, 6 tablespoons Calvados (apple brandy), 6 tablespoons pear juice, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1 cup ice cubes, 2 Dried Pear Crisps.Serve additional pear crisps as snacks.

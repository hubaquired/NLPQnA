Dry Manhattan recipe.  In mixing glass or cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine whiskey, vermouth, and bitters. Stir well, about 20 seconds, then strain into cocktail glass. Twist lemon peel directly over drink to release essential oils, drop into drink, and serve. Dry Manhattan has 218.0 calories. Dry Manhattan has 6.0 grams of protein. Dry Manhattan has 1.0 grams of fat. Dry Manhattan has 3.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Dry Manhattan are 2 ounces rye or bourbon whiskey, 1 ounce dry vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura or orange bitters, Small piece lemon peel for garnish.This drink was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.

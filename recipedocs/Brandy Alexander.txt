Brandy Alexander recipe.  Combine brandy, crème de cacao, and heavy cream in a mixing glass with ice and shake vigorously. Strain into a 6-ounce cocktail glass and grate nutmeg over top, to taste. Brandy Alexander has 234.0 calories. Brandy Alexander has 6.0 grams of fat. Brandy Alexander has 7.0 grams of sodium. The ingredients of Brandy Alexander are 2 ounces brandy, 1/2 ounce dark crème de cacao, 1/2 ounce heavy cream, Whole nutmeg.This drink was featured as a Cocktail of the Month.
